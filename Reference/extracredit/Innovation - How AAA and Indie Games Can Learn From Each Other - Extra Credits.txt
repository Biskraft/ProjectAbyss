Bringing these two sides of the industry

Number One:
One of the biggest drawbacks

Sure, indie developers have accomplished some

Having the distribution

Number Two:
Innovation's a big risk for AAA studios

Having an indie publishing arm would allow AAA

Number Three:

1. The most important part of making this a viable business model

AAA publishers, make sure you are
