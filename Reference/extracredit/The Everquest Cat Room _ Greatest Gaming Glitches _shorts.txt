Greatest gaming glitches. Once upon a
time in the EverQuest beta, you could be
walking through Befallen or Freeport,
then suddenly fall through the world and
end up in a box covered in cat pictures.
The perfect trap, perhaps? Well, not
quite. See, Jeff tells me back in the
'90s when he was working on EverQuest,
players would occasionally walk across
polygons that didn't quite line up
perfectly, clip through the ground, and
end up in a submap void falling forever.
Or, you know, until you reached a
support ramp who could teleport you back
to solid ground. So, as a quick fix,
Jeff hacked together a teleporter to put
below each zone set to move anyone
falling into it to a safe room. But
then, a colleague of his, Kevin, decided
that his zone safe room could be more
clawsome by plastering images of his cat
mittens all over it. A well, it didn't
happen often. Enough players found it,
took screenshots, and the catbox
EverQuest lore was born. Because, let's
be honest, cats just make everything
better. Which is why I've modded Zoe
into Fallout 4 for this year's Best
Friends Animal Society crowd control
charity stream. The Apocat Lips begins
on May 16th at 11:00 a.m. and runs all
day. So, bookmark the link here to be
notified when we go live and then come
mess with my game and help out our furry
friends at the same time because really
they do tend to make the best.
