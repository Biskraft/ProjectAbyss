YOKU'S ISLAND EXPRESS
Yoku's Island Express.
This game is a work 
of pure whimsy,
that captures the unabashed joy
of the old SNES era Nintendo games.
In it you're a tiny bug postman 
out to deliver the mail on a tropical island.
The area's lovely and the characters
are memorable and quirky,
but it's the gameplay
that sets it apart.
It's a pinball-platforming-Metroidvania.
And they make the combination of pinball mechanics and Metroidvania progression really work.
It's the logical extension of the vastly 
under recognised Sonic Spinball.
It may not change your life,
but you'll certainly get
to experience something new,
and have a delightful time
while doing it.

The game is about slackers in a small town

The metaphors

But it's earnest,
