slop, and I mean beyond the AI kind. I'm
talking friend slop, a colloquialism for
a style of game built upon mechanics
that promote chaotic emergent gameplay
in a small group multiplayer
environment. You know, think Overcooked
Lethal Company, or heck, even Hell
Divers, too. Just don't tell Super I
said that. But really, why are we
calling it slop? My theory is it's easy
to confuse the chaotic fun and wacky hy
jinks of something like Repo or
Headliners with a lack of polish or
something hastily thrown together. But
when done right, there is nothing sloppy
about cultivating a fun experience.
That's what games do. These are simply
titles whose main purpose goes beyond
winning and losing. While it can be fun
to meet your quota or make it out of a
haunted house, the real magic of these
games comes from the wacky performative
failures we all do with our friends
along the way. So, I don't want to call
them slop. What should we call these
