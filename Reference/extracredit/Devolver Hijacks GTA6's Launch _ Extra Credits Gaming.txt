Deolver Digital has a long history of
doing some truly absurd marketing
campaigns for their games. They've
invented fake mascots, sold one of said
mascots VHS tapes as a collectible, ran
an entire virtual game festival, made
another one as a game itself, and so
much other wacky stuff. So, when they
say they've got a game that they're
determined to release on November 19th,
2026, aka the exact same day Grand Theft
Auto 6 is coming out, I have every
reason to believe them. And this might
make sense as more than a marketing
stunt, too, because it seems like
everyone else is moving out of the way
of GTA 6's release. Sure, tons of people
will be buying that game and causing a
lot of virtual crime, even if Rockstar's
real crime of union busting continues up
to the day of launch, but there will be
other people who just aren't interested.
And for them, Deolver will be there with
this mystery off-the-wall offering.
Plus, since everyone else will have
shifted their releases away from
November 19th, the cleared out launch
window means that there is a pretty big
opportunity here if you're bold enough
