So you can think about the cards are –

very similar to the old-school sprites.
Not that "card" is necessarily a
standardized term, but a card or billboard
it's effectively a 3D plane that starts
orienting towards the camera.
Traditionally, what happens is that,
in order to give that tree
that density and fullness, you
insert a lot of cards –
but you place the cards in such a way that,

at it side-on, basically the card disappears.
What we actually do is, every card in
the tree orients towards the player.
So, even when you rotate around, every card

So it's always facing that way.
And then, because we're a stylistic game,
we're able to draw

it's not obvious to the player but it also
sort of blends in.
And, again, if you have enough cards

fullness that you're looking for

of cards we actually have to draw.
