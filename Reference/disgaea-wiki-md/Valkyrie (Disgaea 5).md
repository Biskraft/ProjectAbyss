# Valkyrie (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Valkyrie*

The **Valkyrie** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Valkyrie is a standard physical attacker with solid physical stats and accuracy but bad magical defense and evasion. She excels at hit-and-run tactics thanks to her great mobility and proficiency in long-range weapons.

## Personality types

- Energetic
- Aiming For Ace
- Effort
