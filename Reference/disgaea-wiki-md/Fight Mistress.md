# Fight Mistress

200px|thumb|Artwork of the Fight Mistress for *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.
The **Fight Mistress** (also known as the **Martial Artist**) is a recurring class in the *Disgaea* series. 

## Appearance


## Characteristics
The Fight Mistresses hail from the Village of Beasts, a town that was originally a place for marriage training, but over the years it became a training ground. The strongest of the Fight Mistresses, known as Nirvam, are well known by martial artists across the Netherworlds.

## Gameplay


## Appearances
### *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
*Main article: Female Martial Artist (Disgaea)*
Fight Mistresses first appeared as one of the starting classes in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*. A level 20 Female Martial Artist is required to unlock the Samurai class.

### *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
*Main article: Fight Mistress (Disgaea 3)*
While absent from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Fight Mistress returns in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice* as one of the starting classes.

### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Fight Mistress (Disgaea D2)*
The Fight Mistress makes an appearance as one of the initial jobs available at the start.

### *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
*Main article: Fight Mistress (Disgaea 5)*
The Fight Mistress returns in *Disgaea 5*
