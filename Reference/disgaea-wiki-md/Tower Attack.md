# Tower Attack

thumb|A Tower Attack being performed by a tower of four in *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days*.
A **Tower Attack** is a type of attack performed by a tower.

## Description
First introduced in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, it refers to a tower's ability to use normal attacks. The damage of the attack is simply the combined damage of every unit in the tower using a normal attack, applying any ailment/debuff associated to them. The range of the attack is 2(in any direction), increased to 3 if there's at least 5 units in the tower. The EXP and Mana earned from killing an enemy will be evenly split among the members of the tower.

Starting in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*, humanoid characters are able to learn special skills that can only be used while they're the base of a tower. Like with normal attacks, the damage is the sum of everyone using the skill individually(applying weapon debuffs when it corresponds), and range depends on the amount of units involved.

In *Disgaea 4*, tower skills are learned by paying Mana at the Evility Shop. In *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness* and *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*, tower skills are automatically learned by doing actions while on a tower.

## Special Skills
Attack skill range is the same as a normal Tower Attack. Move and Lift range is 1 for every 2 units in the tower(straight line only).
