# Dark Knight (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Living Armor*
The **Dark Knight** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 5 onwards.

## Quotes
> Unlike ordinary demons, Dark knights have a strong sense of chivalry and prefer a fair fight. Very few demons in the Netherworld can compete against the Dark Knights. In fact, with their sword skills, they can slay a dragon with a single slash|Disgaea DS Collection Book
