# Celestial Host (Disgaea 2)

*Main article: Celestial Host*

|  |

The **Celestial Host** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days*.

## Overview
The Celestial Host is a unit with high magical power. Their stats are higher than those of other caster and healer classes, but they need to be taught stronger spells to properly function.

## Skills
### Passive
- Reduce non-elemental damage to adjacent allies by 50%(+1% per Lover).
