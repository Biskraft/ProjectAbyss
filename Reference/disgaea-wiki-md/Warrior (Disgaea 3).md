# Warrior (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Warrior*
The **Warrior** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Retaining his design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, he appears as a shirtless young male demon with a bandana and iron gauntlets. He is the main offensive force in the game and is comfortable with most physically offensive weapons (Sword, Spear and Axe). However, he truly shines when wielding an Axe in his hands. His native Evility, **Wild Cry**,** **increases his stats by 20% for every adjacent enemy which fits his close combat style perfectly.

When the player starts a New Game, Geoffrey gives them a set of "Starter Characters", one of which is a Warrior. His name is randomly generated.

The character  Muscle Preacher Bo-Tie is a recolor of the Warrior class.
