# Paralysis

- Paralysis** is is one of the Status Ailments that can be inflicted upon the characters.

## Description
It's represented by white lightning bolts with black outlines above the character's head. Units affected by it will be unable to move and(from *Disgaea 2* onwards) their SPD will be reduced to 1.

It's typically caused by attacks of electrical nature.

## Specialists
Two Specialists deal with the Paralysis ailment:

- Having a **Medicine Man** on any equipment increases a character's resistance to the Paralysis status by 1% per level. (Note: Due to a possible reference error in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, the Social Worker increases Paralysis resistance in that game instead)
- Having a **Witch Doctor** on any equipment gives a character's normal attacks a chance to inflict Paralysis by 1% per level.
