# Quick-Draw Overlord

- Quick-Draw Overlord** (briefly **<abbr title="During Chapter 7">Silly Billy Overlord</abbr>** and **<abbr title="After starting Chapter 8">Former Lost Demon</abbr>**, and finally **<abbr title="After clearing Stage 8-4">Nether Heart Overlord**) is Zeroken's exclusive class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Description

:*"A kid who uses the same skills as the Great Demon Fist, Goldion. He considers Killia to be a thief. He's a small fry."* <sub>(During Chapter 6)</sub>

:*"A frivolous kid who idolizes Killia as his apprentice brother. He is bothered by his cowardice."* <sub>(From Chapter 7 onward)</sub>
