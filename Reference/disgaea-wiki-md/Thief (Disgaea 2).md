# Thief (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Thief*
The **Thief** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Thief is an agile support specialized in Stealing. Not fit for direct combat, they help the party by disarming and incapacitating the enemy.
