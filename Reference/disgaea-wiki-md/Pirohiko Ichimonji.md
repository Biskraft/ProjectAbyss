  - Pirohiko Ichimonji** a character from the PSP game *Zettai Hero Project: Unlosing Ranger VS. Darkdeath Evilman*.

## Zettai Hero Project
Pirohiko was the Unlosing Ranger at the beginning of the game. He was running late to a battle with the demon general Darkdeath Evilman when he got ran over by a car. As he was laying there dying, the Main Character came along and found him. Pirohiko gave the Main Character the Unlosing Ranger Morpher and asked him to fight Darkdeath in his place. When the Main Character was unable to do so, Pirohiko's dead spirit came to act as a guide and mentor to the Main Character. He took the Main Character to Bizzaro Earth in order to train him and make him strong enough to beat Darkdeath. Pirohiko has a rivalry with fellow hero Dangerama and trained under the former villain, Noa Daikokudo.

## Other Appearances
Pirohiko appears in the Playstation Vita game *Disgaea 3: Absence of Detention* as part of the Tera Fire spell.

He also appears as a DLC character in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*. Like the other DLC characters, Pirohiko is applying to join up with the Hades Party. While Fenrich is against him joining since the Netherworld doesn't need heroes, Valvatorez is willing to give him a chance (while also expressing interest in Pirohiko's hero suit). After the fight, Pirohiko refuses to give up fighting and receives a power boost through "Cheer Energy". Impressed with this, Valvatorez decides to let him join up.

Pirohiko is unusual compared to other DLC characters because he only comes with one special attack (Fist of Love). His innate evility "Unlosing Mode" (which increases his stats by 50% for three turns) is also unique since the players can activate this at any time from the menu before letting Pirohiko do any actions.
