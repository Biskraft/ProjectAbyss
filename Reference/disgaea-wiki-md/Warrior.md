# Warrior

thumb|Artwork of the Warrior for *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.
The **Warrior** (also known as the **Fighter**) is a recurring humanoid class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*. They are essentially the male variant of the Valkyrie class. 

## Appearance

Warriors are buff, shirtless demons with light clothes, gauntlets and headbands that trail in the wind.
## Characteristics
Warriors are physical battlers who use weapons like axes and swords to take on enemies. Most males in the Netherworld can become Warriors, though one must make a pact with the God of War Grandell to take on the title. Warriors wear light armor, but can harden their bodies instantly as a defensive measure. They are aggressive wandering soldiers with a low capacity for friendship, but respect those they deem worthy.

## Gameplay
Warriors are the generic physical attack class, who are easily trained to use a sword or axe.

## Appearances
### *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
*Main article: Male Warrior (Disgaea)*

The Warrior excels at the use of Swords and Axes. They are available for creation from the beginning, along with their female counterpart.

### *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*
*Main article: Male Fighter (Disgaea 2)*

The Fighter in *Disgaea 2* still retains the high mastery of Swords and Axes, while also being able to effectively use Spears. Their special ability raises the amount of damage when scoring a 'critical' if the Male Fighter has 1/4 remaining HP or less. They are again recruitable from the start of the game, along with their female counterpart.

### *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
*Main article: Warrior (Disgaea 3)*

The Warrior in *Disgaea 3* is like his *Disgaea 2* counterpart, excelling in the use of Swords and Axes, and being available for creation from the beginning of the game, as well as their female counterpart.

### *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
*Main article: Warrior (Disgaea 4)*

The Warrior is again a starting class, along with their female counterpart. They is best at using Swords and Spears.

### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Warrior (Disgaea D2)*

The Warrior is one of the initial classes made available for creation, along with their female counterpart. They use Swords and Axes best.

### *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
*Main article: Warrior (Disgaea 5)*

The Warrior returns in *Disgaea 5*, again as a starting class.

## Other Appearances
### *Phantom Brave*
The Fighter in *Phantom Brave* is most proficient with Physical and Energy attacks, making the Fighter most proficient with Swords, Spears, Daggers, and Axes, but also with items such as rocks or pots.

### *Makai Kingdom*

The Fighter in *Makai Kingdom* is proficient with the Morning Star, Axe, Spear, and Sword weapons. His special abilities also add to his HP, Attack, and Defense stats.

He is also used to unlock many other classes, such as the Male Swordsman.
