# Coliseum (Disgaea 2)

- Coliseum** is the sixth episode of *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Story
After rescuing Taro from the Cavern of Evil, the party heads back to Holt Village, only to find a girl called Yukimaru who asks them the directions to the Coliseum. Hanako helpfully gives the information, then Yukimaru told Adell and the party the reward for participating and winning the tournament... a chance to meet Zenon! Adell and Rozalin went into action of winning the tournament.
