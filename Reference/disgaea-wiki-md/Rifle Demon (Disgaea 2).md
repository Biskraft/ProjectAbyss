# Rifle Demon (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Rifle Demon*

The **Rifle Demon** is a recurring monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Rifle Demon is an offensive class specialized in Star damage. With perfect accuracy and good defenses, their only major drawback is poor movement.

## Skills
### Passive

- Your attack always have 100% accuracy.
- Lovers increase stats by 1.25% instead of the usual 1%.
