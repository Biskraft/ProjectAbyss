# Brute Warrior

|  |


The **Brute Warrior** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. It originally appeared in *Makai Kingdom*.

He resembles a demonic humanoid with bizarre clothing. He also has a large amount of bandages and stitches from all his previous fights (even on his tongue). The Brute Warrior is known for his high attack power, high defense and excellent speed. As such, he is proficient with Swords and Axes as weapons. His native evility Madness boosts his Attack Power by 50% when his HP is down to 25%.

To unlock, the player must pass the "Make a Brute Warrior" bill in the Classroom Assembly which requires a Level 40 Armor Knight and a Level 40 Beastmaster
