# Angel (Flonne)

- Angel** is Flonne's exclusive class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Quotes
> Lord, give me strength. / Sorry, I won't hold back!|Flonne

> Flonne is an angel trainee who respects Seraph Lamington, and came to the Netherworld on a mission he assigned to her. She is a love zealot who believes that "even demons can understand love." She joins Laharl's group in order to teach him about love. She loves superheroes, and swears by the rule that people should "not attack when the hero is transforming."|The World of Disgaea
