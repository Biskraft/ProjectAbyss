# Mass Produced Desco

|  |

  - Mass Produced Desco** (also known as Factory Desco) is a creatable class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Detention*. They become available in the postgame after passing a bill that is unlocked when Desco has been defeated and becomes a playable character.

## Class Overview
:*"Created by Mao's burning pathos. Usatako absorbs her defeated enemies."* - Unit Help

The Mass Produced Descos were created by Mao when he did some experiments on Desco and found a way to use Desco's DNA and create clones of her. Fuka was initially against the idea but Desco approved since she felt the Desco clones could help with their plans. The Mass Produced Descos look exactly the same as Desco (apart from their colors) and have almost identical minds to the original Desco. However, their abilities are different than the original. Their native Evility, Usatako Charge, allows them to recover 10% of their HP and SP per unit they've KO'd in a single strike.

Along with the original (Desco), Mass Produced Desco Magichanges into an Axe.
