# Flora Beast

thumb|A Flora Beast in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.
The **Flora Beast** is a recurring monster class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Appearance
They are plants with humanoid bodies, arms, and human-like hair with plant-like bulbs. The point at which the legs start is covered up by a ring of leaves and below the ring a spring like tail is seen.

In *Disgaea 2* and *Disgaea 3*, they have a more androgynous body, and their chests are plain, where in *Disgaea 4* and *Disgaea 5*, they have leaves on the edges of their previously not present breasts.

In their default coloration, they have green bodies, with the spring like tail and leaf on their chests appearing with a darker green coloring. They have yellow hair with green and red flower bulbs growing from them. The ring of leaves is red.

## Characteristics
Flora Beasts are forest spirits grown from seeds and Netherworld plants that suck up Mana around them, and by sucking up Dark Mana they become demons. The Flora Beast's cute appearance and sweet smell are used to deceive its enemies. Those who approach end up being strangled and/or sliced and diced by vines. Despite their reputation of being savage, they are in actuality incredibly shy, and there are some so shy they will close themselves up in their petals and refuse to show their faces to anyone.

They apparently have the ability to rest on their petals and spring like tail.

Though initially thought to be an all female species, with some games outright claiming that they are a female only species in their glossaries, some titles such as *Disgaea 2* and *Prinny 2* show that the Flora Beast can be born male. This could stem from the biological fact that plants are born with both male and female organs.

## Gameplay
Despite some games showing off male Flora Beast characters, in the game series they are treated as female characters.


## Appearances
### *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*
*Main article: Flora Beast (Disgaea 2)*
In *Disgaea 2*, Flora Beast appear on the first map of chapter 4. Their innate ability gives them magical resistance. Their specials are largely divvied up between attack-based, intelligence based and resistance based. Noticeably, they have a high resistance stat, which effectively makes them an offensive/healing class, much like Holy Dragons and Succubi. There is an NPC Flora Beast named Bridget, who is confirmed to be male.

### *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
*Main article: Flora Beast (Disgaea 3)*
In *Disgaea 3*, their evilities allow them to heal allied characters as well as boost their elemental resistance; catch is, the ally has to be standing close to them. Their magichange transforms them into a spear; this was carried over into *Dark Hero Days*.

### *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
*Main article: Flora Beast (Disgaea 4)*

In *Disgaea 4*, Their evility allows them to heal allied characters once again. Different from *Disgaea 3* is that they Magichange into a staff.
