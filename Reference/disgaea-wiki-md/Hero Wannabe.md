# Hero Wannabe

- "Hero Wannabe with a decent name. As you can see, he's a loser."* -- Unit Help.

  - Hero Wannabe** (later known as **Demon in Training**, **Demon Flavored**, **Cosplay Demon**, **Soft-boiled Demon**, **Demon Lover**, **Demon Maniac**, **Mostly Demon**, **Hero** and ultimately **Newly Married Hero**) is Almaz's class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. He can be considered the tank of the main characters, with his high Defense aptitude and base stats. He excels in physical combat.
