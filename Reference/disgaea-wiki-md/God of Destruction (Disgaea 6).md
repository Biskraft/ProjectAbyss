# God of Destruction (Disgaea 6)

> The emergence of a new God of Destruction, the strongest ever recorded, carries death and disaster in its wake. It appears out of nowhere with no discernable pattern or reason for its atrocities. Countless worlds and innumerable demons have been reduced to nothingness by its sheer power. None can oppose this God of Destruction. Nothing exists that it cannot destroy.|Narrator from Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny
The **God of Destruction** is a character in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*. It threatens Zed and Bieko and appears to be the main antagonist of the game. It is simply named "God of Destruction" and it is currently unknown if it has a real name or any relation to previous Gods of Destruction that have appeared in the series.
