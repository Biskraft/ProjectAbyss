# Dragon (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Dragon*
The **Dragon** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

They specialize in multi-target elemental attacks.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 4 onwards.

## Quotes
> The most powerful clan of monsters, dragons are known throughout Celestia and the Human World. However, a lot of research has been done on dragons for the purpose of summoning and controlling them. Low ranking dragons are often summoned by angels and humans to help out in a pinch.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
