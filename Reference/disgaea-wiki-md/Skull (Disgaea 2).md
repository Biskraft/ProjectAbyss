# Skull (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Skull*
The **Skull** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.
## Overview
The Skull is a standard caster class, with good magical attack but poor physical defense. Throughout their different Tiers they learn all elemental spells, as well as some debuffs.

## Skills
### Passive

- Increase Spell EXP gain by 5(+1 per 2 Lovers) per use.

### Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.
