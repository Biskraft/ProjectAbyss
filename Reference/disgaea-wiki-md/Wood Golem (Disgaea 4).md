# Wood Golem (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Wood Golem*

The **Wood Golem** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Wood Golem is a frontliner with excellent attack power and health. Their self-regeneration ability keeps them in battle a long time.
