# Mage (Disgaea D2)

|  |


*Main article: Mage*

The **Mage** (also known as the **Witch**) is a humanoid class in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.

## Class Overview
The Mage is one of the classes made available from the start.

Mages are almost identical to their counter-parts, the *Skulls*, albeit that their evilities are more focused on preserving and effectively managing their SP.

Stat-wise, the Mage is very frail, with less HP and SPD, but having higher DEF and SP as a trade-off. This makes the Mage slightly better at taking a physical hit, but having less HP to survive it with. 

Both genders have pitiful ATK, making physical weapons ineffective. However, later tiers can eventually accomodate Gun/Bow-toting, despite Staves and Books being this class' strongest weapons.

As stated previously, Evilities are aimed at the preservation of SP, ranging from a 50% decrease in casting costs to using HP as a replacement when SP is drained.

## Elemental Resistance
The Mage has 25% resistance to all elements, but the player must choose one to go up by 50% and in turn another to go down by 50% upon creating her.
