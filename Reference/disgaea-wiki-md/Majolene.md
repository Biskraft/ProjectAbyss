  - Majolene** is a playable character in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*. She is a great witch who is also the principal of the Magimage School of Magic. She uses the legendary Magical Wand of Transformation to become **Magical Girl ☆ Magnificent Majolene** and kick butt!

## Profile
### Appearance
The behind the seams artbook describe her wings as "fleshy" and always facing down, they come out of her lats and are tucked under her skirt. She wears a winged beret with much of her outfit being held up by the bat on her chest. She has similar bats on her shoes they appear to be holding her ankles.

Her elderly design is a simpler pink dress with a white over coat.

### Personality
She is a very strict but kind and caring woman who tries to aid her friends in battle as much as possible. She would originally do whatever was considered "perfect" not doing anything for herself to be accepted but now follows her own wishes. She can be very stubborn and thick-headed as when she went to the past to stop the lesson that would kill Lemisera her old self refused without hesitation, she loves kids, being very attached to her students and always striving to better them.

## Story

Majolene was born into a very strict family, constantly getting high grades and going to a top school to please them, even becoming the headmaster of the Magimage School of Magic for such reason, she was originally an older women who was cursed, said curse transformed her body into a young magi-girl, she goes back in time to try and save her previous student lemisera, a young mage who had died to her own summoned dragon which put her under much grief.
