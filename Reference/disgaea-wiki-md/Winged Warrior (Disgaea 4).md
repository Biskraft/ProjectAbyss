# Winged Warrior (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Winged Warrior*
The **Winged Warrior** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Winged Warrior is a highly mobile unit who stays safe in battle through their high evasion and ailment-inflicting attacks.
