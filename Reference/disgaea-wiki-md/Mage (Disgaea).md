# Mage (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Mage*

The **Mage** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Mage is a standard caster class, with high magical stats but poor physical stats and health. Throughout their different Tiers they learn all elemental spells, as well as some buffs.


## Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.


## Quotes
> "Magic" is the term used to describe the power behind the "Laws of the Seven Spiritual Elements." This law was proposed by the father of alchemy and Netherworld geology, Vryamid, who also created the Geo Effect. More precisely, magic is a chain reaction caused by consuming spiritual energy and releasing the spiritual elements within. The title of a mage, Troyra, comes from Troylab, meaning "self-sacrifice" in the ancient language.|The World of Disgaea
