# Angels, Demons, and Humans

- Angels, Demons, and Humans** is the tenth episode in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and its remakes.

## Story
Although Captain Gordon still bemoans his fate as Laharl's servant in the Netherworld, Jennifer and Thursday have settled right in. Along with Flonne and Etna, they decide to visit Sea of Gehenna for a picnic, against the wishes of the reluctant Laharl.

Upon arriving, though, their picnic basket is stolen by none other than Mid-Boss. Determined to reclaim their stolen lunches, Laharl and his friends chase after Mid-Boss, eventually defeating him yet again. Unfortunately, Mid-Boss consumes the lunchboxes along the way. The episode ends with Flonne reflecting on her experiences and feelings.
