# Male Samurai (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Male Samurai*
  - Male Samurai** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Male Samurai is an offensive class specialized in using Swords. They disregard defense, focusing on dealing deadly and precise attacks.

## Skills
### Passive
- Your normal attacks have a 20% chance (+1% per Lover) to insta-kill if your HP is below 25%.
