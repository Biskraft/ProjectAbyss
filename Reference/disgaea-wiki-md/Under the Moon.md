# Under the Moon

- Under the Moon** is the fourth and final stage in Episode 8: Reincarnation in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Story
They see white lights floating up into the sky and Flonne asks what they are. Big Sis Prinny explaines that these are souls of prinnies who have atoned for their sins. The red moon purifies their souls so they can be reborn. Flonne is touched by this, but Laharl is not about to let his vassals leave.

After the battle, Death asks why Laharl is interfering with their work. Big Sis Prinny tries to reason with Laharl, telling him that if he values his vassals, he should do what is best for them. Laharl finally agrees to let them go. Big Sis Prinny then leaves as well, having atoned for her sin as well. They ask about her sin, and she says she commited the greatest sin of all - she took her own life... to save her son's life....
