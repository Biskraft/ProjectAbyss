# Lord

- Lord** is a distinguishing title used throughout the series for various characters, often with additional descriptors. It may refer to:
- Demon Lord (Etna, Adell, 20 of these were defeated by Priere)
- Lord: The highest Tier in the Strider class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, who can manipulate Geo Change and summon cannons.
- Lord Laharl (referred to as such in his hymn).
- Lord of Terror (Baal, and any who defeat him)
- Overlords (various)
- The Lords' Party: the first map in The Truth of Disgaea 2's Dark World
