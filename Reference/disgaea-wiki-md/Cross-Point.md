  - Cross-Point** is the fourth stage in Episode 9: Captain Gordon, Defender of Earth in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.
## Story
Jennifer asks about Laharl's plan to invade Earth. But Laharl makes it clear he has no interest in that 'rotten planet'. This puts Jennifer in an awkward postion. Because if Laharl isn't lying about this, then the Earthlings are? They have to find Gordon and find out the truth, or a war might break out between the Netherworld and the Human World for no reason.
## Additional Strategies
If you happen to have a strong enough character in your Base Panel, you can potentially exploit this map: The combined enemies here carry 44 Moon Orbs, 9 Star Orbs and 2 King Orbs. These can sell for a lot. If you capture all 11 enemies here, you can up with a great amount of HL.

Plus, you might get Legendary or Rare King/Moon orbs, potentially making this map one of the greatest orbs spots of the game.
