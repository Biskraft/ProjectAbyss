# 1st Defender

- 1st Defender** is an enemy-only class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and its remakes, exclusive to Don Joaquin. It is immune to status effects.

## Quotes
> The first Defender of Earth to enter the Netherworld to defeat the Overlord. He died before fighting the Overlord, and now his spirit wanders aimlessly. Over the centuries, he has befriended some of the local demons.|The World of Disgaea
