# Disgaea 4 Complete+

*(This article is a stub)*
    - Disgaea 4 Complete+*** (魔界戦記ディスガイア4 Return* *Makai Senki Disugaia Fō Return*) *is a port of* **Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten ***for the Playstation 4, Nintendo Switch and Microsoft Windows*. *

## Changes from *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
- All previously added content from *A Promise Revisited* is included.
- Characters may be repositioned while viewing the target areas of their skills.
- Skill speed options have been added, and can be adjusted between x2, x3, and x4.
- You can preview the Magichange weapon that your monster class becomes by viewing it in the Status screen.
- The game will automatically save upon completing a battle. This can be turned on or off in the settings menu.
