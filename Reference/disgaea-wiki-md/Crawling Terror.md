  - Crawling Terror** is the third stage in Episode 11: Hero's Will, Overlord's Way in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Story
The damage done to Thursday by Don Joaquin's attack is extensive and Jennifer cannot repear it without the proper tools. It seems the memory circuit took the brunt of it, and if Thursday were to overheat, his entire memory will be lost. Still, fully aware of the danger, Thursday decides to fight along his friends.
