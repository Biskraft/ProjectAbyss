# Undead (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Undead*
The **Undead** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 5 onwards.

## Quotes
> They are categorized as a species, but since they are monsters that have come back to life, they aren't technically a species. Because of their smell, they're generally hated, but they do have a cult following. Most undead are weak, but those of the King variety are known to be as strong as an Overlord.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
