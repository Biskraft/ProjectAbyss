# Strider

|  |

The **Strider** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Strider is an explorer who prefers to use his good mobility and Gun proficiency to fight from a distance. They stand out thanks to their unique skills, which facilitate exploration of the Item World.

### Notes on Dark Cannon

- The Dark Cannon is an object that can be summoned into an empty panel. When first summoned, a cursor will appear and the player will have 10 seconds to shoot as much as possible at any targets.
- In-between turns, the Cannon will lock on to the closest unit(ally or enemy) and fire 5 shots at them.
- Shots have infinite range, but they fly to the target in a straight line and can't pass through terrain. They cause splash damage, hitting other units next to a hit target and making Prinnies explode. Shots will always do a minimum of 1 damage.
- The range of the skill follows the same rules as Spells, growing with skill level and Staff Mastery. The level of the Dark Cannon(and consequently, its stats) is **1 + SkillLevel + StriderLevel/2**. Increasing skill level also increases the time available to fire the Cannon when summoned.
- Units killed by the Dark Cannon do not give any EXP, Mana or HL.
