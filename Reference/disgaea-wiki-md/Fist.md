# Fist

The **Fist** is a weapon type in the *Disgaea* series. Their damage scales with the average of ATK and SPD, and they also increase the user's Counter Attack limit. Their skills tend to change the target's position.

## *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*


In this game their damage depends only on the ATK stat, and they also increase the user's HIT. While they don't increase the Counter stat, their users tend to have a good Counter value already. Jennifer specializes in Fists, as well as the Male Martial Artist, Female Martial Artist, and Ninja classes. 

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Fists, please see List of Disgaea 1 Weapons#Fist.

## *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*


This game sets the standard for Fists increasing SPD and Counters. Adell specializes in Fists, as well as the Sinner class.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Fists, please see List of Disgaea 2 Weapons#Fist.

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*


Mr. Champloo specializes in Fists, as well as the Martial Artist and Fight Mistress classes.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Fists, please see List of Disgaea 3 Weapons#Fist.

## *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*


Fenrich specializes in Fists, as well as the Bouncer class.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Fists, please see List of Disgaea 4 Weapons#Fist.

## *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*


The Counter bonus has been lowered to 1, but their function remains the same. Killia and Zeroken specialize in Fists, as well as the Martial Artist, Fight Mistress and Wrestler classes.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Fists, please see List of Disgaea 5 Weapons#Fist.
