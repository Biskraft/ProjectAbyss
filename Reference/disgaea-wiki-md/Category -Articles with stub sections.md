# Category:Articles with stub sections

This category lists **pages with stub sections** on this wiki.

A stub is a short article that can be expanded with more information on a subject by any user. Stub sections are sections with very little or no information. If a section is not relevant to the article, the section may safely be removed. To mark a section of an article as a "stub" add the code  to the bottom of any section on an article.
