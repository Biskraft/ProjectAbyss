# Sicily (disambiguation)

Sicily is a female character in the *Disgaea* series.

Due to appearing in multiple *Disgaea* games as a playable character, she has multiple classes in the series.

- For her class in *Disgaea D2*, see Laharl's Sister (Disgaea D2)
- For her class in *Disgaea 5*, see Laharl's Sister (Disgaea 5)
