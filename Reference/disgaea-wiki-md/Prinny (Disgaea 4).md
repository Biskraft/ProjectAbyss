# Prinny (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Prinny (Main)*

The **Prinny** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Prinny is a relatively weak class, with average stats and low combat prowess. They serve mostly as cannonfodder, being easy to sacrifice thanks to their low maintenance cost and use as portable bombs.
