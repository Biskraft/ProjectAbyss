# Thief (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Thief*
The **Thief** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Retaining her design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, she is a young girl dressed in sly, feline-themed clothing. She wears a long sleeved short top and denim hot-pants, showing her stomach area. She also wears striped stockings and has her hair in a ponytail. Like the original Thief from *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, she isn't built to be too overly powerful, but can Steal items with a higher chance than any other class, capping at 99%. This is due to her native Evility, **Thief Skills**, which doubles her chances of stealing. Because of her high Speed and Hit Stats and Aptitudes, players often equip Thieves with Bows or Guns.

The character Classroom Queen Breezy Santina is a recolor of the Thief class.
