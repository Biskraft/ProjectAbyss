# Rifle Demon (Disgaea 1 Complete)

- Rifle Demon** is a monster class featured in the *Complete* release of *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, replacing the Galactic Demon class from previous versions. Apart from visual changes, this new class is unchanged from the class it has replaced.

## Collection Book
- "High-ranked demons that favor destruction and chaos. They destroyed a Human World and learned the technologies that they later implanted onto themselves. They are a group of elites who are as strong as Demon Lords."*
