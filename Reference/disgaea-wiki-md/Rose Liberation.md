# Rose Liberation

Rose Liberation is the third special attack of Rozalin in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* and *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days*. It is also known as Dark Liberation when Rozalin's seal has been broken in the story. Dark Liberation can only be used in one battle, which is the fight against Overlord Laharl in the main story.
