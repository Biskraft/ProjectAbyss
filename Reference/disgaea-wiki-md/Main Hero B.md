  - Main Hero B** (called **Main Character B** in the Japanese version) is a playable character in *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days*. Main Hero B is not his real name, it is his stage name (as he believes that living under an alias is exciting). Axel first meets him in the Dinner Theatre. Main Hero B is masochistic, as Pink notices right away. Initially he starts off as a neutral NPC character who basically stands around and gets beat up. Later on in the stages he joins Axel's party.

Though Main Hero B is a man, he uses a recoloured version of the Celestial Host (later named the Female Angel/Celestial Hostess) class, and has a feminine figure with visible breasts in his Disgaea 4 cut-in image. While his voice is masculine in Japanese, he was cast a much more feminine voice in English. His gender is not discussed at all by Axel or his allies in Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days, although it is confirmed by his vulnerability to male character-targeting effects (as well as being boosted by Rozalin's innate.) In Disgaea 4 he/him pronouns are used for him by the entire Hades party, thus confirming his gender outside of mechanics.

## Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days
Like the class he unlocks, Main Hero B starts with Fire, Wind, Ice and Heal spells, and doesn't gain any abilities during level grinding. 

Similarly to Pink and Actress, he is only playable in Axel mode in Disgaea 2. Access to him is lost after the player completes the mode, and they will have to wait another cycle in Axel mode to re-obtain him in the Dinner Theater again. However, if the player has completed Axel Mode, is in Adell Mode (with Adell as a Senator), and has the Land of Carnage unlocked, Main Hero B can be playable again provided the player passes the recruitment bill. 

Main Hero B is met in Chapter 3: Dinner Theater, at the theater in question. He is one of the "main characters" on the stage with Axel, explaining to him that he isn't there to be a performer, but to be served as part of a meal to the diners. Telling him that everyone was tricked, Main Hero B is questioned on why he seems so happy, to which he responds with "Because I love being hurt ♪" This prompts Pink to question him on his presence in the Netherworld, and Actress to comment that angels "will offer their left arm if their right arm gets eaten," with Axel nodding along about angels being into "that kind of stuff." Main Hero B corrects that he's a special case among angels, and that truths always get twisted. In the first battle stages at the theater, Main Hero B is a passive NPC. He joins the party after deciding that he and Axel are similar, and he can "learn from him."

In battle, Main Hero B's stats are rather balanced; though he has a 120% INT Aptitude, hinting at a Mage-like character. However, in the end, how Main Hero B fights depends on the player's preferences, for he can fulfil any combat role rather well.

## Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten
Main Hero B also appears in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten* as DLC. He comes to the Netherworld as a Hades Party applicant, stating that he's looking for more excitement. After being defeated, he is excited by how powerful Valvatorez and the others are, looking forward to receiving similar treatment while fighting alongside them. Purchasing Main Hero B unlocks the Celestial Host class for character creation.

If he is recruited after Axel, there is a special scene with the two in which Axel reveals that he knew Main Hero B before the events of Disgaea 4. The two also appear together in the Netherbattle Tournament on a team alongside Evil Ranger Pink. In the prelude to their battle, Adell encounters them and challenges Axel to a fight, but Axel uses Main Hero B to protect himself in his "Special Move" "Angel Shield". Adell is not only horrified at Axel's willingness to use his ally as a shield, but is also confused when he sees that Main Hero B enjoys taking his blows directly.
