# Sealed Room

- Sealed Room** is the fourth and final map in Rozalin the Liar.


## Story

### Fight

The most noteworthy enemy in this stage is Axel. 

### Dark World

The Dark World version of this stage contains a number of Iron Knights who, after a fixed number of turns, will all sacrifice themselves to the Dark Sun to level up a single Majin. Allowing this to happen is probably not a good idea.
