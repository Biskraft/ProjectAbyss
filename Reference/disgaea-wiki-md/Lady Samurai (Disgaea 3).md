# Lady Samurai (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Lady Samurai*
The **Lady Samurai** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Returning from the original *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, the Lady Samurai is a young demon girl in clothing similar to that of a Japanese shrine maiden. Her hair is done up in a ponytail. Like her male counterpart, the Lady Samurai is a master of the Sword. Unlike her male counterpart, she isn't very good with Spears but is instead decent with Bows. Her native Evility, **Calm Waters**, increases her attack power when she's fighting one-on-one.
