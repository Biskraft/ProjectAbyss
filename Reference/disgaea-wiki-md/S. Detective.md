# S. Detective

The **S. Detective** is Flonne's enemy-exclusive class in the Disgaea: Hour of Darkness remakes' Etna Mode.

The difference here with this class from Flonne's original Angel class is that she learns all her abilities at level 1.

## Encounters
- River of Lava
- Searing Tyranny
