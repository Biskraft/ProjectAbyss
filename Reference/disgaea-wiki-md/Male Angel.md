# Male Angel

thumb|Artwork of the Male Angel for *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.
The **Male Angel** (also known as the **Celestial Host**) is a recurring class in the *Disgaea* series, and is the only class used exclusively by the male angels of Celestia. 

## Appearance

## Characteristics
Unlike their female counterparts, they are less prejudiced and even train in the Netherword to increase their spiritual power. They work together to protect the peace of Celestia and if they identify an enemy, they will do whatever it takes to vanquish them.

## Gameplay

## Appearances
### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Male Angel (Disgaea D2)*
The Male Angel first appeared in *Disgaea D2* and is unlocked by either completing Chapter 8, or by having a Priest and a Magic Knight both at level 100.

### *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
*Main article: Celestial Host (Disgaea 5)*
The Male Angel is unlocked by completing Chapter 12, having a Cleric/Clergy at rank 3 and a Magic Knight at rank 2, and then completing the associated quest.
