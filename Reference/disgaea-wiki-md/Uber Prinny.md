  - Uber Prinny** is one of Baal's exclusive classes in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

He appears in this form from his second battle onwards. When first encountered he has two unique items equipped: the Nemesis Mk-II, and the Prinny Costume.

This class is immune to status ailments.

He's fought in the extra stage Lord of Terror.
