### Staff
*Main article: Staff#Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*

A weapon specialized in increasing INT, although weapon damage is still based on ATK. Staff Mastery does not grant weapon skills, instead improving the power of magical skills and the range and AoE of spells.


### Monster
*Main article: Monster weapon#Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*

These weapons can only be equipped by monster units. They increase ATK and have no skills associated to them.


There's two unique monster weapons that are listed as ranks 41 and 42, going beyond the usual 40 rank barrier. The two items can only be stolen from Baal or Rozalin and Prinny Baal, respectively.
