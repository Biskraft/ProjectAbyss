# Lady Samurai (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Lady Samurai*

The **Lady Samurai** is a Humanoid class from *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Lady Samurai is a Sword-user with high attack power and accuracy but low magical defense and evasion. She's a duelist who becomes drastically stronger during one-on-one fights.

## Personality types

- Bushido
- Pink
- Problem Solver
