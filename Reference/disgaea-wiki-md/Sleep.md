# Sleep

- Sleep** is one of the Status Ailments that can be inflicted upon units.

## Description
It's represented by several yellow "Z"s floating above the character's head. Units affected by it are completely unable to act or move, but the status is removed upon receiving an attack. Attacks against a sleeping target have their attacking stat increased by 50% during the damage calculation; in *Disgaea 5*, the attack results in a Critical Hit instead.

## Specialists
Two Specialists deal with the Sleep ailment:

- Having a **Coffee Maker** on any equipment increases a character's resistance to the Sleep status by 1% per level.
- Having a **Hypnotist** on any equipment gives a character's normal attacks a chance to inflict Sleep by 1% per level.
