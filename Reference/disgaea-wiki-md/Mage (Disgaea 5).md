# Mage (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Mage*

The **Mage** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Mage is a standard caster class, with good magical stats but low physical ones. They use elemental spells to hit the enemy's weakness from a distance. The Mage's magic is very SP-efficient, letting her use spells for a long time.

Note that when creating one you'll have to choose an element, determining what spells will be learned. This will also give +50% to that resistance, but -50% to another(Fire reduces Ice, Ice reduces Wind, Wind reduces Fire).

## Personality types
- Airhead
- Naive
- Wicked


## Skills
### Spells
Mages will learn the spells matching their chosen element, plus the spells in the Common tab.

Spell range and AoE depends on skill level and boosts.
