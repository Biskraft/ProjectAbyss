# Celestial Host (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Celestial Host*
The **Celestial Host** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Retaining her original design from Disgaea: Hour of Darkness she is a young Angel from Celestia. She is dressed in an extravagant and elegant robe and has her hair done up in a braided Ponytail. As an Angel she doesn't like fighting but should she have to, she will fight back using a Bow. She can also use a Staff, allowing her to use her Magical Abilities. The Celestial Host is only available to the player once they have successfully cleared the Item World Command Attack Online Challenge in under 145 moves on any difficulty setting. Please note; you must be connected to the internet or you will not unlock this class.

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Detention*
In the Vita port, the Celestial Host gains access to 2 class based skills. Carol affects a 5 square area directly in front of the caster in a cross formation. It heals at D power and cures status ailments. Miracle of Love sacrifices the Celestial Host and returns a character that is already dead to the base panel.
