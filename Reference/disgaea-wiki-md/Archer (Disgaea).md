# Archer (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Archer*
The **Archer** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Archer is a unit specialized in long-range combat with a Bow, supporting the offensive safely from the backlines. They also help by incapacitating enemies, as Bow is the only weapon whose skills can inflict status ailments.


## Quotes
> Archers come from the all-female society of Badre, an ice mountain village. They have a unique social system led by the Sevria. Archers can listen to the wind and deduce its strength and direction.
