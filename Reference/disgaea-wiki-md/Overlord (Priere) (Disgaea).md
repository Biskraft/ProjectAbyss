# Overlord (Priere) (Disgaea)

- Overlord** is Priere's exclusive class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

It should be noted that Priere comes with both a Lucifer Force(Rank 39 Monster Weapon) and an Arcadia(Rank 40 Emblem).
