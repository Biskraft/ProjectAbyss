# Warrior (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Warrior*

The **Warrior** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview

The Warrior is a standard frontliner, with solid HP and physical stats but bad magical defense and evasion. His Evilities give him the extra power and bulk needed to stand toe-to-toe against multiple enemies.

## Personality types

- Cool
- Quietly Burning
- Muscly
