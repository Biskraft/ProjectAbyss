- "A spoiled princess who came from Veldime, another Netherworld."*

  - Overlord Incarnate** (**Overlord's Child **in* Disgaea 3: Absence of Detention*) is Rozalin's exclusive class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Rozalin's aptitudes make her suited for using a Gun coupled with her Gun Forte up to the 6th skill. She has lower than average ATK and DEF stats making her best suited staying at the back rather than the frontlines.

## Skills
### Weapon
- Gun: Up to 3rd skill (Up to 6th in Absence of Detention)
- Magic: Ice only, up to Giga Level (Tera in AoD)
