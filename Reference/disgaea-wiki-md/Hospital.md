# Hospital

The **Hospital** is a recurring feature in the *Disgaea* series.

The player can heal and/or revive any of their characters, for a fee. The fees grow higher the more HP and SP must be restored. Prinnies (including Prinny Kurtis) are a notable exception to this, costing 1 HL to revive/heal no matter how much HP and SP they're missing. Starting in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*, the party members are restored fully automatically after battles outside the Item World, and this functionality of the Hospital in the main base was removed. It must still be manually payed for in the Item World, however.

In all games, the Hospital contains a number of counters which measure the amount of HP and SP restored, and characters revived. When the counters reach certain levels, the Hospital will allow the player to redeem rare items by selecting "Claim Prize". If you leave the Hospital without claiming your prize or choose to reenter the healing menu, you can reenter the Prize menu repeatedly until a Rare version is generated. These items will not be generated as Legendary in rank. Exceptions to this rule are *Disgaea 3* when someone is assigned to the Nursing Club, and *Disgaea 5*.
