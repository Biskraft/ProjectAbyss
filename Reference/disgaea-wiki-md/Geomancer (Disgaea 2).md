# Geomancer (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Geomancer*
The **Geomancer** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Geomancer is a caster class specialized in Star damage. They are skilled at manipulating Geo Effects and traversing the Item World.


## Skills
### Passive
- Nullify damage taken from Geo Effects and Geo Chains.
- Increase range of Geo Warp by 1 per 5 Lovers.

### Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.
