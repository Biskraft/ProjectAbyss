  - Laharl's Sister** is Sicily's class in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.

Sicily has excellent ATK, INT and SP making her good with Staffs, and Books. Just like a Priest, her Weapon Mastery fortes lie in Books and Staffs; though she can wield a Spear as well.
