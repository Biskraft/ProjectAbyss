  - Alternate World** is the first map of the Alternate Netherworld bonus stage in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Strategy
This map consists of several small islands, each carrying a few enemies.

The easiest way to clear this stage is to use a well trained magic user. All enemies, except one, can be eliminated with spells from the starting island. Once you've cleared out all but that last one, throw your magic user to one of the adjacent islands, reducing the distance. And then take out the final enemy.

If you don't have a well trained magic user and only one or two decent fighters, you can still clear this stage with a bit of patience. Throw your fighter to one of the islands and kill all enemies. Once the island is cleared, you can throw a weaker character to the now cleared island and have them throw your fighter back to the starting island. Repeat with all the islands until the stage is cleared.
