# Celestial Hostess

|  |

*Main article: Celestial Host*

The **Celestial Hostess** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Celestial Hostess is a healer class who aims to help the party through their healing/buff spells and various support Evilities. Contrary to their male counterpart, they have better INT than ATK.

## Personality types

- Pious
- Peace Seeking
- Pure
