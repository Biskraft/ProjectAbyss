  - Piyori Nijino** is a playable characters in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*. She is the leader of the Ultra Chroma Power Squad Prism Rangers. She is a hero who fights evil in the TV World and aims for high ratings as the first female lead.

## Profile
### Appearance
Piyori is a young, human female with a sizable bosom. She has blue eyes. She has short, orange hair. She wears a red armored bodysuit that covers up her entire body from her head to her feet. The bodysuit has a rainbow emblem at her waist and a skirt design. She wears grey gloves and a rainbow bracelet on her left wrist.
