# Happily Never After

- Happily Never After** is the 3rd episode of Act 1 of *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*, and the 3rd episode overall.

## Summary

## Story Impact
- Melodia is introduced.
- Bieko is first mentioned.
- Zed and Cerberus continue their tale for Ivar and the Darkest Assembly.
  - Zed's party arrives at the Musical World and meets Melodia.
  - Melodia declares Zed her prince and forcefully joins the party.
  - Cerberus mentions Bieko to the group.
  - Zed is killed by the God of Destruction.

## Game Impact
- Melodia learns Evility, Power of (Fake) Love.
