# Horseman

thumb|A Horseman in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengance*.
The **Horseman** is a recurring monster class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Appearance


## Characteristics
Horsemen are very advanced people that can ride Bone Dragons. The Horsemen are skilled in the art of swords, and are feared by other monster races. Unlike most demons they are chivalrous in combat.
