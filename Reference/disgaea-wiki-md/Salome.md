  - Salome**, also known as **Lady Salome**, is a character from the game *Makai Kingdom*. She is an Overlord of an unnamed Netherworld and Zetta's love interest. She was Zetta's former apprentice and has the potential to become the strongest Overlord. In the PSP version of the game, it is revealed that she has a daughter with Zetta, named Petta.
