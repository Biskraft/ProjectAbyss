# Valkyrie (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Valkyrie*

The **Valkyrie** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Valkyrie is a standard frontliner class, with good HP and physical stats. She excels at running through the battlefield, favoring good use of movement and range.

## Personality types

- Cool and cute
- Beefcake
