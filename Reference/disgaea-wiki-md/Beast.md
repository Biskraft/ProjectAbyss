# Beast

|  |

The **Beast** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 4 onwards.

In *Disgaea 1 Complete* they are replaced by the Dragon Zombie class, though the change is purely aesthetic and they function the exact same way.

## Quotes
> Beasts have a lion's body, a monkey's face, a scorpion's tail, and bat wings. They are extremely proud about being at the top of the Netherworld food chain. It is very rare to find any beast serving another demon. There's nothing they love more than the thrill of the chase.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
