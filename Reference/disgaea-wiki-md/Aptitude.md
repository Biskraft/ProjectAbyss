# Aptitude

- Aptitude** is one of the stat modifiers that characters have, with every class having their own Aptitudes for each of the 8 main stats. It's represented as a percentage, and it acts as a multiplier to the stats gained from all equipment (increasing them if above 100%, decreasing them if below).

Aptitudes are not something that grows along with the character, and few methods exist to increase them:

- From *Disgaea 3* onwards, reincarnating a Unique character (Generics too from *Disgaea D2* onwards) increases all Aptitudes permanently by 5%(stacking up to 25% bonus).
- Weapon Scrolls (in *Disgaea 2*) modify some Aptitudes while equipped.
- Certain Evilities can increase Aptitudes.
- Class World (in *Disgaea 3*) lets you increase each Aptitude permanently by up to 100%, but they're capped at 255%.
- Chara World (in *Disgaea 4* and *5*) and Devil Dojo (in *Disgaea D2*) let you increase Aptitudes permanently (no limit to how many times), but they are capped at 300%.
