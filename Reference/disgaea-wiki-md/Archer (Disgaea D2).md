# Archer (Disgaea D2)

|  |


*Main article: Archer*

The **Archer** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.

## Class Overview
The Archer is a class designed around the use of the Bow, with ATK and HIT being the best. HIT in particular is the Archer's strongest statistic, and if given a Bow, ensures high critical hit chance.

The Archer's defense, to further augment her long-range style, is more prevalent against magical attacks. A high RES coupled with starting resistances to Fire and Wind attacks makes her excellent against magic-casters. Their RES also makes Archers excellent characters to teach Healing spells, despite Magic range being an issue.

Later tiers of the Archer can also find use in the Spear and the Gun, but mediocre DEF and horrible SPD makes both of these weapons relatively ineffective.

The Archer's Evilities focus on increasing her Attacking power under certain circumstances. Evilities range from a 5% increase per panel between her and her target, to a 100% increase to damage when attacking units with Flying-type movement such as Mothmen and Masked Heroes.
