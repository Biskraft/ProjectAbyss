# The Disgaea Triple Play Collection

- The Disgaea Triple Play Collection ***is a compilation of the three *Disgaea *games released on the Playstation 3*, *which are *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*, *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*, and *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness**. *All three games are included in one disc but do not come with any of their DLC bundled.

Each game in the collection is unchanged from their original standalone release. For *Disgaea 3* and *Disgaea 4*, none of the content from their Playstation Vita versions is included.

## Trivia
- This is the first *Disgaea *compilation ever released.
- In addition, this is the first *Disgaea *game that has been released first outside of Japan. There are currently no plans for a release in Japan.
