# Skill Level

Each skill has its own **Skill Level** that increases as it keeps being used. Skill EXP gained with each skill use is a fixed amount, increased only by having a Mentor Specialist equipped. 

The effect from increasing Skill Level changes with each game:

  - Disgaea 1* and *2:* As they level up, all skills get increased SP cost, and attack skills get increased damage. Spells also get an increase to range and AoE.
  - Disgaea 3* and *4:* Skill Level seems to do nothing, its effects being replaced by the boosts from the Evility Shop.
  - Disgaea 5* and *6:* Skill Level does not modify damage but now it reduces SP cost. The reduction is far more obvious in *Disgaea 6* due to the increased base SP costs of characters.

When a character Reincarnates they will lose part of each skills total EXP(depending on the Inheritance Rate of the Reincarnation), reducing the level of their skills. In *Disgaea 1* and *2*, if a skill would lose enough EXP to return to level zero because of this then the character will forget that skill (though it can still be relearned).
