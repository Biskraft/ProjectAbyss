# Skull (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Skull*

The **Skull** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Skull is a standard caster class, with high magical stats but low physical ones. They use elemental spells to hit the enemy's weakness from a distance. The Skull's magic is powerful, but burns through SP quickly.

Red Skull, Green Skull and Blue Skull are alternate versions of Tier 1, and can't be promoted into each other.

## Personality types
- Wholehearted
- Challenging
- Anxious
