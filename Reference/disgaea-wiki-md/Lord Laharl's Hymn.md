# Lord Laharl's Hymn

- Lord Laharl's Hymn** is Laharl's leitmotif and also the ending song for the Human Conqueror ending in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*. A remix of this track called *Lord Laharl's....Hymn? *appears in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* and on the *Disgaea Custom Soundtrack*. The main theme to *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*, *Demon Academy*, is also a remix of this track.

### English Translation
<poem>
Even the dead will wake up
If they hear his name.
He's a evil incarnation
Who doesn't shed blood or tears.
No matter what kind of demon they are,
They run around nude trying to escape.

That name is also Lord Laharl.

He is a royal youth who rules hell,
He is God's adversary,
People kneel in front of him,
Begging for their lives.

"Line up, People!"

It's a hair raising diabolical march,
No one can stop him.
He walks the path of darkness in purity and righteousness.
Evil spirits abound and rampant,
The wondrous road of wickedness.
Become eternity!

He's an evil elite that everyone admires,
He doesn't wash his hands in the bathroom;
He's everyone's role model,
His hobbies are staying up late playing with fire and laughing loudly.

That name is also Lord Laharl.

You can't count the number of wicked deeds he has done,
He is Hell's Number One!
His favorite words are treachery, unjust, and arrogance.

He's a good kid's idol.

It's a hair raising diabolical march,
No one can stop him.
He walks the path of darkness in purity and righteousness.
Evil spirits abound and rampant,
The wondrous road of wickedness.

Stain this whole world with darkness,
No one can disobey him.
He changes dreams and hopes into nightmares,
A Dark Hero that crushes the defenders of justice.
An evil way of life. 
Become eternity!
</poem>
