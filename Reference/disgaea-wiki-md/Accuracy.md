# Accuracy

- Accuracy** refers to the chance for an attack to hit its target. It depends on the involved characters' stats and positioning, following the next formula:

  - Accuracy = (attackerHIT/defenderSPD)*DirectionMultiplier*(1 + StationaryBonus)**

- DirectionMultiplier is 1 for attacks from the front, 5/4 from the side, and 5/3 from the back.
- StationaryBonus is the boost you gain as you keep doing actions without moving.

Accuracy has a minimum cap of 40% when attacking from the side, and 80% when attacking from the back. The DirectionMultiplier and minimum caps were removed in *Disgaea 5*. Note that skills in the first *Disgaea*(and rereleases) ignore the formula and can't be normally dodged.

As for effects that increase Evasion, there's 2 types:

- Most of them are applied after factoring the max accuracy cap of 100%, and each of them applies a multiplier of **(1 - EvasionBonus)** to the formula.
- A few of them however are applied before the accuracy cap, and their value is simply substracted from the formula.
