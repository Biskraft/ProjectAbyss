## Titles
Adell can also obtain five other titles, but they overwrite the previous one:

- Archer Guards - Obtained when Adell is charmed by Legendary Senator Venus while attending a proposal in the Dark Assembly.
- Lord Master - Obtained by killing the Prinny Baal Pirate.
- Demon Lord - Obtained by defeating Etna in Episode 3, but it'll disappear right after, when you're forced to begin a New Game +.
- Prism Black - Obtained by clearing Extra Stage 02, "Rangers' Return".
- Badass Overlord - Obtained by defeating Overlord Zetta.
