# Sidereal Cliff

- Sidereal Cliff** is the final stage in Episode 12: War of the Netherworld, Part 1 in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.
## Story
Flonne notices Jennifer has been really depressed lately. Etna points out it's Gordon's fault, that he should apologize. Before he has the chance to do so, Jennifer starts apologizing.

Laharl senses a presense and Kurtis shows himself. Intrigued by the other, the two decide to fight each other.

After the battle, a ship appears and Kurtis tells them they will launch a full-scale attack. But first... he separates Jennifer from the group, intending to take her back to her foster father, General Carter. Jennifer is torn but does not fight it. Before they dissappear, Kurtis tells them to find their way aboard the Gargantua, if they want Jennifer back.
