# Male Samurai (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Male Samurai*
The **Male Samurai** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Retaining his design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Male Samurai is a young demon donned in traditional, Japanese-style clothing as well as a long coat. Masters of the Sword, Samurais are deadly on the battlefield and bring quick death to their enemies. They can also use Spears. However, while Male Samurais are deadly when it comes to battle, they are, in fact, quite shy when it comes to dealing with the opposite sex. The Samurai's native Evility, **Chivalry**, increases the damage done when facing the opponent from the front.
