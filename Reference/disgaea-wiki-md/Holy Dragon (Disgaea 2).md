# Holy Dragon (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Holy Dragon*

The **Holy Dragon** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Holy Dragon is a magic user specialized in Ice damage with decent healing capabilities. While they have low defense, their passive immunity protects them from many dangerous skills.

## Skills
### Passive
- Nulls non-elemental skill damage received. Absorbs 1% of that damage per Lover.
