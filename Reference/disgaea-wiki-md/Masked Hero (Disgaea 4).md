# Masked Hero (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Masked Hero* 

The **Masked Hero** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*. 

## Overview
The Masked Hero is a speedy unit with a combination of high evasion and mobility. He's a coward who prefers to run away from combat, fighting only once he knows the enemy is weaker than him.
