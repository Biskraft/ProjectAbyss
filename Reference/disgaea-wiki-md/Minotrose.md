# Minotrose

<br />

  - Minotrose** is a minor character in Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten. He appears exclusively in the [Flashback Episode](https://disgaea.fandom.com/wiki/DLC?so=search#Modes_3) DLC.

## Personality
Minotrose is, like most demons are, ambitious and greedy. He is also shown to be arrogant and sarcastic.
