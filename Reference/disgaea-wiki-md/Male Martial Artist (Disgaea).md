# Male Martial Artist (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Martial Artist*
The **Male Martial Artist** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Male Martial Artist is an agile unit with good evasion and movement but low defenses. He's a short-range fighter with proficiency in Fists and a high Counter stat.
