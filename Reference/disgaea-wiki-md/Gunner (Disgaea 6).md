# Gunner (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Gunner*
The **Gunner** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Netherworld hunters who are adept with firearms. Their eagle eyes allow them to pinpoint the location and weaknesses of their targets, as well as share that info with allies. Their skills are highly regarded, but they speak little and prefer solitude, presumably to rest their bodies from concentrating so hard.

### Unlock Requirements
The Gunner is unlocked by completing the *Picking a Partner* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after the completion of the *Steeling to Steal* quest, which unlocks the Thief, and requires Rank 1 or higher class proficiency for both Warrior and Clergy to complete.
