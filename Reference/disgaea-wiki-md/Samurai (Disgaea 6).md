# Samurai (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Lady Samurai*
The **Samurai** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
As sworn guardians, Samurai hone their skills protecting the Celestial Gate. Since their clan is lenient on the path its members take, some forgo their duty to travel the Netherworlds as sword masters for hire. They follow a strict code called Bushi Road, and are known for their firm pride and resolve.

### Unlock Requirements
The Samurai is unlocked by completing the *Loyal Blade* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after the completion of the *On the Hunt* quest, unlocks the Archer, and requires Rank 1 or higher class proficiency for Archer and Rank 2 or higher class proficiency for Warrior to complete.

### Personality Types
- Bushido
- Flashy
- Problem Solver
