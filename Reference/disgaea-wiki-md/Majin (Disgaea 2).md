# Majin (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Majin*
The **Majin** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Majin is the most powerful humanoid class, having great stats all around and being highly proficient in all weapons. In dire situations they become almost unstoppable.


## Skills
### Passive
- Increase stats by 100% when you're the only ally unit on the map and no more can be dispatched.
