# Angel of Avarice (Disgaea 5)

|  |


  - Angel of Avarice**(or **Nun**, as a human) is Artina's exclusive class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

You can change between her Angel and Human forms by passing a Bill at the Assembly. Each form has its own Unique skills.

## Description

:*"An angel tasked with collecting money that should go to Celestia. She is known by demons as the Angel of Avarice."* - Angel of Avarice

:*"Artina when she was still a human. She tends to the injured soldiers."* - Nun
