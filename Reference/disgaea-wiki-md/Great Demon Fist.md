# Great Demon Fist

- Great Demon Fist**(or **Demon General** as an enemy) is Bloodis' exclusive class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.
## Description

:*"A Demon General serving under Demon Emperor Void Dark. His combat skills put him at the top of the Lost."* - Demon General

:*"The founder of the Ultimate Demon Technique. He is Killia and Zeroken's master, and Liezerota and Void's father."* - Great Demon Fist
