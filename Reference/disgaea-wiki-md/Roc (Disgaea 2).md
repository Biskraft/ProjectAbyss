# Roc (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Roc*

The **Roc** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Roc is an offensive support with mediocre stats except for their attack. They compensate for their low stats by weakening enemies through their passive debuff and ailment-inducing attacks.

## Skills

### Passive

- Decrease stats of adjacent enemies by 20%(+1% per Lover).
