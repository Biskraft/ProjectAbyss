# Drain

- Drain** is a secondary effect that allows the user to regain HP based on damage dealt to enemies introduced in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.
Skills that have the *Drain* effect, heal the user for the same amount of damage done with the skill. Some skills will only heal a partial of amount for the damage dealt with the skill. Some Evilities also have a drain effect on enemies

Skills with the drain effect in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*:

- Hip Attack (100% of Damage dealt) - Succubus

- Soul Eater (100% of Damage dealt) - Gargoyle

- Tail Attack (100% of Damage dealt) - Beast
Skills with the drain effect in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*:
- Impaler Prince (5% of Damage dealt) - Valvatorez
- Bloody Hole (10% of Damage dealt) - Valvatorez
- Tyrant Sweep (5% of Damage dealt) - Tyrant Valvatorez
- Blood Sting (10% of Damage dealt) - Tyrant Valvatorez
