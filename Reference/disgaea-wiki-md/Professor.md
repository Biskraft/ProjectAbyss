# Professor

thumb|Artwork of the Professor for *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.
The **Professor** is a recurring humanoid class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in Makai Kingdom, then in Disgaea proper in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*. 


## Appearance
They appear like female scholars with a striped shirt and a long coat.


## Characteristics
Professors are demons who have taken an interest in human science and technology and are now studying in the field of Netherscience. Though the profession is rare at the moment, Netherscience is a constantly expanding field with many unproven areas. Professors are eternal searchers of truth, making them comrades with the Magic Knights. They are actually quite skilled in the art of modifying the abilities of others.

## Gameplay


## Appearances
    - <big>Makai Kingdom</big>***

The professor makes its debut appearance in the game, Makai Kingdom, where it is unlocked by completing chapter 3 and starting chapter 4. In the hub world, it acts as the npc which levels up mech units which have filled the exp meter in exchange for MT.

### *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
*Main article: Professor (Disgaea 4)*
The Professor made its first appearance in the *Disgaea* series in *Disgaea 4*, where she can be unlocked after having a level 20 Healer and Onmyo Monk

### *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
*Main article: Professor (Disgaea 5)*
The Professor returns in *Disgaea 5*, where she can be unlocked by having a Gunner and Maid both at rank 2, then completing the associated quest.
