# Disgaea 5 Complete

*}}

    - Disgaea 5 Complete*** is a port of *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance* for the Nintendo Switch and Microsoft Windows.

## Changes from *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*

- The DLC Shop has been renamed to the Special Content shop.
- The fast forward feature applies to dice rolls and events in the Chara World, potentially shortening the amount of time to complete instances.
- Innocents can be combined in an item from the Innocent Farm menu.<br /> <br />
