# Spirit (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Spirit*
The **Spirit** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

They are a weak unit specialized in magical attacks.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 5 onwards.

## Quotes
> Spirits are the most mysterious type of demons in the Netherworld. Demon scholars now believe that they are spirits that failed to become prinnies, and exist in the form of wandering ghosts. They are known to be the most common type of monster, according to the last Netherworld Census.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
