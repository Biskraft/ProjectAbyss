# Monster weapon

- Monster Weapons** are a type of weapon in the *Disgaea* series. As the name suggests, they can only be equipped by Monsters. They come in 2 types: the Physical Monster Weapon, which increases ATK and HIT, and the Magical Monster Weapon, which increases INT and ATK. There's no weapon skills for Monster Weapons, and they lack any secondary effects. Unlike most weapons, they are not visible in the wielder's sprite.

- Disgaea 6* removed Monster Weapons, letting monsters equip Humanoid weapons instead.

## *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
This game featured only one type of Monster Weapon which only increased ATK, severely limiting Monster classes' options.

There's two unique monster weapons that were listed as ranks 41 and 42, going beyond the usual 40 rank barrier. The two items could only be stolen from Baal and Prinny Baal, respectively.

To see the charts of weapon stats for Monster weapons, please see List of Disgaea 1 Weapons#Monster. 

## *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*
This game introduced Magical Monster Weapons, as well as making the Physical ones also increase HIT.

To see the charts of weapon stats for Monster weapons, please see List of Disgaea 2 Weapons#Monster (Physical) or List of Disgaea 2 Weapons#Monster (Magical).

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
Monster Weapons remain unchanged, although from this game onwards equipping a Magical Monster Weapon increases Spell range by 1.

To see the charts of weapon stats for Monster weapons, please see List of Disgaea 3 Weapons#Monster (Physical) or List of Disgaea 3 Weapons#Monster (Magical).

## *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
Monster Weapons remain unchanged.

To see the charts of weapon stats for Monster weapons, please see List of Disgaea 4 Weapons#Monster (Physical) or List of Disgaea 4 Weapons#Monster (Magical).

## *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
This game reworks Magical Monster Weapons, making them increase RES instead of ATK.

To see the charts of weapon stats for Monster weapons, please see List of Disgaea 5 Weapons#Monster (Physical) or List of Disgaea 5 Weapons#Monster (Magical).
