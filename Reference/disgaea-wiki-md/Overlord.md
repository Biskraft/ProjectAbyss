# Overlord?

> I can't stand how big my butt and boobs are!!!|Laharl, *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*

  - Overlord?** is a class exclusive to Laharl in his female incarnation in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*. She is only avalible for the duration of episode 4 and switches back to Overlord afterwards.

Upon beating the main storyline, the female form can be re-acquired by passing the "I Wanna Change!" bill in the Dark Assembly for 100 Mana.

The Overlord? class possesses the exact same stats Laharl had prior to changing, but has voice clips unique to the form as well as a modified Evility called Overlord's Pride, which raises Laharl's stats by 25% per equal-or-higher levelled unit she defeats.
