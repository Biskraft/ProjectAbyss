# Skull (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Skull*
The **Skull** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Skull is a standard caster class, with high magical stats but poor physical stats and health. Throughout their different Tiers they learn all elemental spells, as well as some buffs.


## Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.
