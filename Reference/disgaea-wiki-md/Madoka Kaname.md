  - Madoka Kaname**, commonly known as **Madoka**, is the titular main character of the anime series *Puella Magi Madoka Magica*. She appears in *Disgaea RPG* as an obtainable character through the *Puella Magi Madoka Magica* alongside Sayaka Miki and Mami Tomoe. Madoka is a magical girl who wishes to erase all witches.

## Profile
### Appearance
Madoka retains her magical girl appearance from the series. She has long pink hair tied with red ribbons into two pigtails. She has pink eyes. She wears a ribbon choker. She wears a pink and white, frilly dress with a poofy skirt accented with ribbons. She wears white gloves tied with string at the wrist. She wears white socks and red shoes.

### Personality
Madoka retains her personality from the series.
