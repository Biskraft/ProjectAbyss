# Priest

|  |


*Main article: Male Healer*
The **Priest** is a humanoid class introduced in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*. They are the male counterpart to the Healer class.

## Class Overview
The Priest is one of the initial classes made available from the start. The sole defining factor that separates the Priest from the Healer are their differing stat make-ups.Both genders have high SP and RES, but that's where the similarities end.

The Priest has sub-par HIT, SPD, and ATK, having much better INT and DEF outputs than the Healer to compensate. Priests are naturally aligned towards Books and Staves.This gives him better Magical ability at the cost of reduced Weapon options. (This makes him a good character for learning spells)

The Priest's Evilities vary in effect, ranging from healing the caster and target with one spell, to giving equipped Book weapons the range properties of a Staff.
