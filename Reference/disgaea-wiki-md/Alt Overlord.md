# Alt Overlord

|  |

  - Alt Overlord** is the exclusive class of the Alternate Overlord, also known as the **True Overlord of Other Worlds**, in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and its remakes.

His appearance and abilities are the same of the Efreet, the first tier of the Great Wyrm (Disgaea) class.

The Alt Overlord can be fought twice. The first time is unavoidable, as it happens in the main storyline. The second time is in the bonus content Demonhall Mirror, only available for the remakes Afternoon of Darkness (PSP) and Disgaea DS. For each encounter, he has a slight variation for his class: *Alt. Overlord* & *(True) Overlord*. In the first variation, he is immune to all status effects.
