  - Mami Tomoe**, commonly known as **Mami**, is the one of the main characters of the anime series *Puella Magi Madoka Magica*. She appears in *Disgaea RPG* as an obtainable character through the *Puella Magi Madoka Magica* alongside Madoka Kaname and Mami Tomoe. Mami is a veteran magical girl who helps Madoka and Sayaka out like an older sister.
