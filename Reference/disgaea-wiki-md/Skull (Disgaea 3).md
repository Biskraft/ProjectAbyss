# Skull (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Skull*
The **Skull** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Retaining his design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, he appears as a young version of the original Skull from *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*. Unlike the older Skulls, he doesn't wear his hood over his face and instead has it sitting above his forehead. His native Evility, **Magic Bundle**, increases the power of his Special Attacks but also increases the SP needed to use them. Like his female counterpart, his first three tiers are unlocked at the start, each using a different element of magic. According to the official website, the Skulls attend an advanced school of magic.


## Skills
Red, Green, Blue, and Star Skulls are capable of learning up to Omega Fire/Wind/Ice/Star (Tera in AoD) as well as Slumber/Blank Out/Poison/Stun respectively. Prism Skulls are capable of learning up to Giga Fire/Wind/Ice, and Galaxy Skulls can learn up to Giga Fire/Wind/Ice/Star, with neither having access to any status spells. Because of this, making a Prism or Galaxy Skull from scratch will result in a weaker unit, with it being preferable in the long term to reincarnate them through the first four tiers and then into the fifth or sixth instead.
