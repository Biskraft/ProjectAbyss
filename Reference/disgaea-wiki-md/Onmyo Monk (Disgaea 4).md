# Onmyo Monk (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Onmyo Monk*

The **Onmyo Monk** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten.*

## Overview
The Onmyo Monk is a caster class specialized in Star damage. They are skilled at manipulating Geo Effects and traversing the Item World.
