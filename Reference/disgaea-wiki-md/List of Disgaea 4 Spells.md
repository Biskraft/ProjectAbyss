# List of Disgaea 4 Spells

## Range and AoE
Boosting spells in the Evility shop not only strengthens their effect but also their range and area of effect. Note that these rules(and other Spell modifiers) also apply to Humanoid skills with variable range.

All spells start with a range of 3(free), increasing by 1 for every 3 boost levels. Range also increases by 1 while a Staff or Magical Monster Weapon is equipped.

Spells start as single target only but gain an additional, bigger area of effect every boost until level 8.
