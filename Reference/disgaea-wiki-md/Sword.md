# Sword

The **Sword** is a weapon type in the *Disgaea* series. A straightforward weapon that only increases ATK and lacks special characteristics, making it the easiest to use. Its skills don't have secondary effects, consisting mostly of various AoE attacks.

## *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
 

The Sword is presented as Laharl's main weapon, though he's equally proficient at many others. The Male Warrior, Female Warrior, Knight, and Samurai classes are also notably proficient or specialized in Swords.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Swords, please see List of Disgaea 1 Weapons#Sword.

## *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*
 

Yukimaru specializes in Swords, as well as the Magic Knight, Male Samurai, and Lady Samurai classes.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Swords, please see List of Disgaea 2 Weapons#Sword.

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
 

Almaz specializes in Swords, as well as Mao, Master Big Star, the Magic Knight, Male Samurai, and Lady Samurai classes. 

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Swords, please see List of Disgaea 3 Weapons#Swords.

## *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
 

Valvatorez specializes in Swords, as well as the Magic Knight and Lady Samurai classes.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Swords, please see List of Disgaea 4 Weapons#Swords.

## *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*


This game adds some defensive utility to the Sword through its new Parry mechanic, giving the wielder a chance to reduce damage taken.

While Fists are presented as Killia's main weapon, he's equally good with Swords. The Lady Samurai and Magic Knight classes also specialize in them.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Swords, please see List of Disgaea 5 Weapons#Sword.
