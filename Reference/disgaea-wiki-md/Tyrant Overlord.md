# Tyrant Overlord

- Tyrant Overlord** is Baal's class in Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten. Though not available originally, he was added later as DLC.

He's fought in the DLC Stage, "Tyrant... Overlord". Defeating him rewards you with the strongest weapon in the game, the Great Baal Horn.

## Description

:*"A legendary, invincible Overlord. He's being stalked by the hardcore users. Your adventure continues!"*
