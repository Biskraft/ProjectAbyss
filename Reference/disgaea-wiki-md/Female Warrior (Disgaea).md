# Female Warrior (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Valkyrie*
The **Female Warrior** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Female Warrior is a standard frontliner unit, having good physical stats but poor magical ones. She's proficient in using both Swords and Spears.


## Quotes
> Since those with the potential to become female warriors are rare, the qualified few are adopted by a master and spend their lives training under them. Their masters are usually retired female warriors. The students are called the Liebe, and they are taught battle techniques and methods of survival.|Disgaea DS Collection book
