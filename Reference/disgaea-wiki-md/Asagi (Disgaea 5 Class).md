# Asagi (Disgaea 5 Class)

|  |

  - Asagi** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
As copies of **Asagi**, this class' stats are geared towards the use of Guns. Their initial stats are not great, but grow pretty well with each tier. They're an unpredictable being, with all sorts of risky and double-edged Evilities.
