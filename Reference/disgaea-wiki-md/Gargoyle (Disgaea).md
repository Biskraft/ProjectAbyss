# Gargoyle (Disgaea)

|  |


The **Gargoyle** is a Monster class in Disgaea: Hour of Darkness.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 4 onwards.

## Quotes
> Unlike most demonic monsters, Gargoyles were created to be guardians by ancient wizards. Even after their master's death, they continue to protect lost treasures. Over the years, some Gargoyles have gained their own consciousness.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
