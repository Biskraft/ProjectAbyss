# Masked Hero (Disgaea D2)

|  |

*Main article: Masked Hero*
The **Masked Hero** is a Humanoid unit in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness.*

The Masked Hero is not a class built for damage-dealing. ATK, INT, and HIT are mediocre across the board while HP and DEF are extremely low. Later tiers also have excellent RES growth. The Hero's sole saving grace however is his extremely high SPD. In fact, he has the highest SPD Aptitude of any creatable class in the game.

Unfortunately, low offensive stats mean low damage. This is made worse by the Hero's lack of Weapon Mastery in all but his two native weapons: Fists and Guns, both of which rely partly on his amazing SPD. If the player intends to improve their damage, Belts, (If using Fists) Glasses, (If using Guns) and Demon Dojo training are heavily recommended. Their high SPD also makes them excellent evaders. However, the Hero's HP is too low for this to be continously relied upon.

The Hero's greatest asset is his innate Flying-type movement, which lets them move past obstacles/enemies other characters would have to lift out of the way. This makes them invaluable in big, sprawling maps or in the Item Sea, when the player is rushing to Mystery Anchors. Shoes are by far the best accessory a Hero can be given. The Hero's Evilities all focus towards his strengths. These range from an Evasion boost per panels moved, to a permanent 5-MV increase at the cost of permanent Deathblow status.

## *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
Mysterious, self-proclaimed heroes who live to protect the weak and crush the strong. They fly all over the Netherworld in their homemade costumes looking for people to save. However, if they find evil demons, they're the first to run away. Other demons look down on them and even shun those who interact with them.
