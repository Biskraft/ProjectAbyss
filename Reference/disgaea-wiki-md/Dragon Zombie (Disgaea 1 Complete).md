# Dragon Zombie (Disgaea 1 Complete)

|  |

  - Dragon Zombie** is a monster class featured in the *Complete* release of *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, replacing the Beast class from previous versions. Apart from visual changes, this new class is unchanged from the class it has replaced.

## Collection Book
"High-level dragons sometimes live on as bones and souls, even after their deaths. They're faster and stronger, but less intelligent compared to when they were alive."
