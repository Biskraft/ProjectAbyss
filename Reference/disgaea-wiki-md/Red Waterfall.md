  - Red Waterfall** is the third mission of the fourth episode in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.  It is a moderately sized map containing several nekomata enemies and a beastmaster enemy.  All of the enemies on this map are standing on Enemy Level +10% panels.  This map is the place used for the infamous Nekomata trick on the PS2 version of the game.
