  - Overlord** is Zenon's exclusive class in Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories. He has 2 forms, with the second one activating after his HP drops below 50%. His stats are practically the same, though the 2nd form has a slightly lower HIT and SPD. Zenon is flying, so he can pass through units on battlefield while moving. He has a 5 spaces-long range attack.

Special (1st form): Takes less damage from elemental attacks.

Special (2nd form): Attack up when in danger.

Boss HP Modifier: 2

Location: Stage 13-5, "Viewing Room".
