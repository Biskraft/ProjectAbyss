- 'Hero from this one action game. 'I've mastered the Art of Dying, dood!"*

  - Hero Prinny** is the Hero Prinny's exclusive DLC class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice. *It Magichanges into a Gun.


He has all-around aptitudes, with his top three being SP, HIT, and SPD. His native Evility is Red Scarf, which revives him at the Base Panel with 1HP when he is defeated, but reduces the amount of characters than can be dispatched by 1.
