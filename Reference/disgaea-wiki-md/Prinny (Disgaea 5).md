# Prinny (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Prinny (Main)*
The **Prinny** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Prinny is the Netherworld's most basic soldier, with unimpressive stats all around. They're quite useful as cannonfodder, being easy to sacrifice thanks to their low maintenance cost and their use as walking bombs.
