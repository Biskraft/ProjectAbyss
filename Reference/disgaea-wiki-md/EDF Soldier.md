# EDF Soldier

|  |

The **EDF Soldier** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The EDF Soldier is a unit capable of both close-range and long-range combat. While they are the best with Guns, their high defense and proficiency in Fists and Axes make them also great at direct confrontations.


## Quotes
> The EDF soldiers protect the Earth with their mighty technology. EDF soldiers are almost as popular with Earth children as Defenders of the Earth. Their skill with axes and guns is matched by their skill in hand-to-hand combat. Since science beat out magic on Earth, many soldiers can't use a staff.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
