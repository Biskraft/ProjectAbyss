# God

- God** may refer to:
- The deity, mentioned in both *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*, but never seen.
- "God" (the role Vulcanus aspires to take, and impersonates in Etna Mode)
- God of Destruction
- God of Overlords
- Item God
- Prinny God - the name of a high tier of of the Prinny classes in any given *Disgaea* game
