# No. 1 Delinquent (Disgaea 3)

- "This game's heroine and main rival. No tardies, no absences. A rule breaker."*

  - No. 1 Delinquent **(later known as **Valedictorian** and **Delinquent Teacher** in Raspberyl Mode) is Raspberyl's class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. She is the spellcaster of the main characters, but only learning Fire elemental spells, and even then not even mastering them completely. This is adverted in *Absence of Detention* where she learns Fire up until Tera Level by level 150. Much Magic inheritance from the Class World is needed to unlock her full potential.

She is able to Magichange into a Sword.
