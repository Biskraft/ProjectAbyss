# Spirit (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Spirit*
The **Spirit** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Spirit is a glass cannon, with great magical attack but terrible physical defense. Their SP regeneration means they can keep using skills throughout the whole battle.

## Skills
### Passive
- Restore 10%(+1% per Lover) of your max SP each turn.
