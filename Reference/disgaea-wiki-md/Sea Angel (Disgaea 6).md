# Sea Angel (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Sea Angel*
The **Sea Angel** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Microscopic sea creatures turned demons by polluted Netherworld waters. Shy and cowardly, they like to travel in groups and typically stay underwater, except to feed. They eat by releasing buccal cones from their head, submerging prey to absorb its nutrients. They can live on land if a body of water is nearby.

### Unlock Requirements
The Sea Angel is unlocked by completing the *Living Conditions* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after clearing Episode 5, The Magical Wand of Transformation, and requires the submission of one rare single use item.

### Personality Types
- Quiet
- Unique
- Monstrous
