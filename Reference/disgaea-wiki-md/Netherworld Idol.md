  - Netherworld Idol** is Lanzarote's class in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.

Lanzarote is a recolor of the Archer class, possessing elements from both the Archer and Sea Angel classes. Like an Archer, Lanzarote starts with an "S" Mastery level with Bows, and possessing an extraordinary HIT stat. Like a Sea Angel, Lanzarote possesses unique Support Spells usually relegated to that class, such as "Move!," which increase auxiliary stats such as Counters and Throwing range.

Stat-wise, Lanzarote is misleading. Despite her high Bow Mastery, she possesses a very low ATK Aptitude compared to the class she is emulating, while also sporting a decent SPD stat. This makes her more attuned to the Gun as opposed to her native weapon, and expands her combat options. Her defense is also lackluster, with low HP and DEF Aptitudes.

Regardless of her weapon, Lanzarote is designed as a support character first and foremost, and makes an excellent back-up character.

Her Evility **Vocal Mind Control** increasea the stats of all ally units (including herself) by 4% and decreases the stats of all enemy units by 4%.
