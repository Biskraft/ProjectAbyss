# Galactic Demon

|  |

The **Galactic Demon** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

The top defensive class of the game, and the only generic Monster with Aptitudes above 100%.

This class is immune to ailments.

In *Disgaea 1 Complete* they are replaced by the Rifle Demon class, though the change is purely aesthetic and they function the exact same way.

## Quotes
> Born from the eternal blackness of the galactic abyss, these demons are slow-witted and sluggish. They were first discovered in the Stellar Graveyard.
