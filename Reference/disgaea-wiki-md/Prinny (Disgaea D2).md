# Prinny (Disgaea D2)

|  |

## Quotes
> They are different from other demons, down to their core. They have random and irresponsible personalities. They work for demons in power in order to pay for their sins, but it actually looks pretty fishy for them to be doing any work. Once they pay for their sins, they are led to the Red Moon to reincarnate. They are very susceptible to impact, so handle them with care.|Character List
