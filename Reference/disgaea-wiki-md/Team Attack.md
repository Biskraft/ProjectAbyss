# Team Attack

The **Team Attack** (called **Chain Attack** in *Disgaea 2*) is a stronger version of a normal attack with the help of close teammates.

When a unit uses a normal attack against an adjacent enemy, that unit's adjacent allies have a chance to join in, increasing the damage dealt and playing a special animation. The initiator will still do normal attack damage(100% base power), while each helper will add a weakened normal attack(75% base power) on top of it. The EXP/Mana earned from killing a unit will be split amonst all participants, making Team Attacks a good way to share experience to weaker units.

In *Disgaea 6*, any ally within 2 spaces from the initiator can join in, and up to ten units can attack the same target at once.

It's important to note that joining in to a Team Attack doesn't use up the helpers' action for the turn, and those who already spent their action can still join in to them.

Every pair of classes in a game has their own base chance of joining each other for a Team Attack, and various factors can increase/decrease the chance for a unit to join in:

- The weapons on the initiator and helper(different modifier for each pair of weapon types)
- Helper already moved this turn(-10%)
- Helper already acted this turn(-10%)
- (*Disgaea 1*, *2* and *D2*) Both units are associated by Extra Gain
- (*Disgaea 4*) Both units are adjacent in the Cam-Pain HQ(+10%)
