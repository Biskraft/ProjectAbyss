# Male Fighter (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Warrior*
The **Warrior** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Warrior is a standard fighter class, with good physical attack and defense but low magic resistance. He favors vitality and short-range weapons.


## Skills
### Passive

- Increase Critical damage bonus by 50%(+1% per Lover) while HP ≤ 25%.
