# Rozalin's mother

thumb|left
thumb|right
The **mother of Rozalin** is a character who was originally intended to appear in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, but was cut and is not in any version of *Disgaea 2*. It is known that she has concept art. In *Disgaea 3: Absence of Detention*, her artwork was used as the basis of the portrait used for the Tera Ice spell.
