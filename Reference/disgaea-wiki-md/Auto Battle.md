# Auto Battle

- Auto Battle** is a gameplay feature that allows the AI to control player controlled characters for the player in battle, first introduced in the iOS and Android versions of *Disgaea 1 Complete*. In *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*, the system allows for further customization with the Demonic Intelligence system with regard to what actions the AI will execute under different circumstances.

## Appearances
### *Disgaea 1 Complete*
Auto Battle appears as mechanic exclusive to the [iOS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IOS) and [Android](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Android_(operating_system)) releases of *Disgaea 1 Complete*.

### *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*
The script that characters use during Auto-Battle can be configured with the Demonic Intelligence system.
