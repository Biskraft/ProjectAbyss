# List of Disgaea 5 Spells

## Range and AoE
Each Spell starts with a free range of 1, and it's increased by 1 upon reaching Skill Levels 2, 6, 11, 21, 41, 61, 81, and 91. Range is also increased by 1 while a Staff or Magical Monster Weapon is equipped as either main or sub weapon.

Each Spell starts targetting a single panel only, and it's increased by purchasing area upgrades(up to 8 increases) at the Skill Shop.

These rules also apply to Unique skills marked as having variable range.
