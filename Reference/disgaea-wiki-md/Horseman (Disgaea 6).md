# Horseman (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Horseman*
The **Horseman** is a Giant Monster class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Only the finest knights are allowed to ride Bone Dragons. They are skilled in swordsmanship, and can slay even dragons in a single stroke. Other monster races fear them as beings of dread. They are chivalrous in combat, a rarity among demons. They get along well with the Samurai, due to their similar ideals.

### Unlock Requirements
The Horseman is unlocked by completing the *Promised Spear* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after clearing Episode 10, The Final Battle, and requires the submission of one legendary spear to complete.

### Personality Types
- General
- Cold-Blooded
- Bullied
