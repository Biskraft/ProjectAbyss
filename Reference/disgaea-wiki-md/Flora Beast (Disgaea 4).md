# Flora Beast (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Flora Beast*

The **Flora Beast** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Flora Beast is a healer/support that learns all the healing and buff spells. Their allies are kept healthy just by standing near them.
