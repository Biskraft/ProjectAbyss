# Hero's Tomb

- Hero's Tomb** is the final stage in Episode 11: Hero's Will, Overlord's Way in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Story
Don Joaquin awaits them and challenges the overlord to battle. Laharl agrees.

After the battle, Flonne is very dissappointed in Laharl, because he didn't let the old Defender win. But Don Joaquin seems satisfied anyway. He explains that this was more about the battle itself than about winning. He has no regrets and can finally rest in peace. As a parting gift, he fixes Thursday.
