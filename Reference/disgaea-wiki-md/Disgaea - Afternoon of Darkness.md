# Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness

*}}


    - Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness*** is a remake of the original PlayStation 2 game *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* for the PlayStation Portable handheld system. *Afternoon of Darkness* was later ported to the DS as *Disgaea DS* and later still to the PC as *Disgaea PC* via Steam.

The most notable changes from *Hour of Darkness* include hidden content revolving around Laharl's primary vassal, Etna, as well as Ad-Hoc WiFi Multiplayer support.

## Other changes
- Battles against: Adell and Rozalin from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* and Zetta from *Makai Kingdom*.
- Etna Mode: A story relating what would've happened if Etna killed Laharl at the beginning of the game.

### Multiplayer support
    - Item Sales** - Allows you to set the price of items you have built & leveled for another player to buy
  - *Note 1: You do NOT lose the item put up for sale*
  - *Note 2: You do NOT receive HL from these Transactions*

    - Head-to-Head Battles** - Face-off with a friend (or rival) in multiplayer combat!
  - Extra mulitplayer features, modes, and options unlock with more play

The UMD for *Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness* also includes PSP firmware update v3.51, the minimum required to play this game.

## Comparison to *Hour of Darkness*
- Etna's NA version voice actor has been changed to Michelle Ruff.
- A few exclusive songs were removed.
- Has an option to turn off animation.
- Has the Music Shop
- Has Multiplayer Mode
- Has Etna Mode
- Adell, Rozalin and Zetta are new optional bosses.
- In the last stage in Cave of Ordeal, the Demonhall Mirror is present.
- Purchased songs can be selected for the Item World.
- Counter glitch present
- Glitched items present
- Record Keeper present
- Good graphics quality
- Good audio quality
