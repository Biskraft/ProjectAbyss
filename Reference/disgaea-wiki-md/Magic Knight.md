# Magic Knight

thumb|Artwork of the Magic Knight.
The **Magic Knight** (also referred to as simply **Knight**) is a recurring humanoid class in the *Disgaea* series, first introduced in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*. 

## Appearance
Magic Knights are female armor wearing demons noted for having very large busts and short bob hair of varying colors.

The default coloration of Magic Knights present them as blond haired tan skinned women.


## Characteristics
Members of the class are chivalrous female warriors with skills both as swordswomen and magicians and are said to be able to see into the future using a power called "Zone". They also seek the universal truth and they frequently communicate with Professors due to their shared interest. Magic Knights all follow "the Sword's Oath", and breaking the Oath is punishable by death.

## Gameplay
Their preferred weapons are swords and staves, as they are able to effectively use both physical attacks and elemental magic. They also have a innate ability which allows them to cast a random spell after performing a physical attack.

## Appearances
### *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
*Main article: Knight*
The Knight class is introduced in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, and is obtained by having a level 10 Female Warrior and Mage.

### *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*
*Main article: Magic Knight (Disgaea 2)*
In *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* the Knight class was renamed Magic Knight, presumably to make them distinct from the Heavy Knight class. They also gain additional abilities to make them more than just a Valkyrie who can cast magic. The class is obtained by having a Level 3 mastery in both swords and staves then passing the "Make a Busty Beauty" bill in the Dark Assembly.

### *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
*Main article: Magic Knight (Disgaea 3)*
The Magic Knight is obtained by having both a Skull/Mage and a Warrior/Valkyrie at level 25, and then passing a bill in the Dark Assembly.

The Magic Knight uses all the types of magic: fire, ice and wind.

### *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
*Main article: Magic Knight (Disgaea 4)*
The Magic Knight is obtained by having a level 30 Warrior/Valkyrie and a Skull/Mage.

### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Magic Knight (Disgaea D2)*
The Magic Knight is obtained after the player has a level 20 Warrior/Valkyrie and Skull/Mage.

### *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
*Main article: Magic Knight (Disgaea 5)*
The Magic Knight is obtained by having a both Warrior/Valkyrie and Skull/Mage at rank 3, then completing the associated quest.

### *Prinny: Can I Really Be the Hero?*
Two Magic Knights appear in the game, twins by the name of Kim and Chi. They are the bosses of the High Tomb Forest.
