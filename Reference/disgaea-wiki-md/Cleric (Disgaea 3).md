# Cleric (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Female Healer*
The **Cleric** is a class from *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Retaining her design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Cleric is a young female demon with long blond hair and a black dress. She also wears giant handcuffs on her wrists. Because the Cleric is meant to heal allies, she isn't really built for close ranged combat like some other classes. Should she need to, she can fight using a Bow. Her native Evility, **Light's Blessing**, decreases the damage that adjacent allies receive from magic attacks.

When the player starts a New Game, Geoffrey will provide them with several "Starter Characters", one of which is a Female Healer. Her name is randomly generated.

## Skills
Every tier of the Female Healer begins with Heal (unless reincarnated from another class) and is capable of learning Twirly Attack, Attack Punisher, Skewer Piercer, Mega Heal, Espoir, Shield, Magic Wall, Speed Boost, Enfeeble, Mind Break, and Blind. Tier 2 and onward can also learn Giga Heal, and Tier 3 and onward can also learn Omega Heal.
