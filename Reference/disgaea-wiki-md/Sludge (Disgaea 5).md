# Sludge (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Sludge*
The **Sludge** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Sludge is a creature that has unparalleled physical defense, but is easily damaged by magical or elemental attacks. While their attacks are not particularly strong, they're compensate by inflicting Poison on their targets.

## Personality types
- Slimy
- Jelly
- Weak Willed
