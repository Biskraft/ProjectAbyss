  - Mom** is a character in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* who is the mother of Taro and Hanako, as well as Adell's adoptive mother. "Mom" is in fact her real name.

She was a skilled summoner in her younger days, and still retains many of her skills now. Zenon's curse appears to have taken away many of her morals: she in fact emphasizes the fact that she would use her own family as ritual ingredients shows she has no conscience, unless she wasn't putting them at risk and was just joking.
