# Thief (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Thief*

The **Thief** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Thief is a support class specialized in stealing, helping the party by getting rare items or disarming the enemy. They're not suited for direct combat as their stats and weapon proficiencies are overall terrible, with Guns being their best option.

Passive skill: This class uses an improved stealing formula, and has a higher maximum steal chance.

Note that in *Disgaea 1 Complete* they are replaced by their female counterparts, though the change is purely visual and they function the exact same way.
