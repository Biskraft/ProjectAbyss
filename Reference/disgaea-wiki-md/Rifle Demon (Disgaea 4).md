# Rifle Demon (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Rifle Demon*

The **Rifle Demon** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Rifle Demon is a slow but bulky attacker that prefers shooting at the enemy from where they stand rather than chasing them.
