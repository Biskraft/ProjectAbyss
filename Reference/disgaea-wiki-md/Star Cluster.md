# Star Cluster

- Star Cluster** is the third stage in Episode 12: War of the Netherworld, Part 1 in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Story
Vulcanus reveals his evil plans: getting rid of Flonne, the overlord and then the Seraph. Flonne picks up on the evil vibe but doesn't elaborate about it to her companions.
