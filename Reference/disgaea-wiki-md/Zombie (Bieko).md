## Overview
> Zed's little sister. Has an unusually kind heart for a demon. Wants to be a doctor when she grows up.|Unit Info
A young zombie girl. She affectionately refers to Zed as her Bubby. Bieko is unusually kindhearted for a demon, and hopes to become a doctor in the future. Though unlicensed, she managed to save Cerberus from death via brain transplant. (WARNING: Performing unlicensed brain transplants is prohibited by law.)
