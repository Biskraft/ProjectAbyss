The **Director** is a character from the game *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*. He is a non-playable character and makes his first appearance in the first chapter. The Director usually follows Axel around attempting to be a voice of reason when Axel gets crazy schemes in his head, however he is more than happy to be drawn in by the schemes after a conspiracy causes the world to believe them dead "killed by a wild tribesman (Adell)". The Director never fights and never joins the team but he regularly appears in scenes with Axel.
