# Giant Unit

- Giant Units** is a game system introduced in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*. The effects and conditions to use these units varies per game.

## *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
thumb|The result of a Monster Fusion.
Monster Fusion allows a monster to become Giant. A Giant Monster occupies 5 panels (cross-shaped area) on the map, has increased attack range and their skills generally gain a larger area of effect.

## *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
  - Giant** is a buff that can be applied to any unit, doubling their current and max HP and SP, and increasing stats by 30%. A Giant unit won't be able to use Unique or Weapon skills, but they'll gain the exclusive Giant Press skill.


## *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*
thumb|A screenshot featuring the Clawed Shell unit, a Giant Unit.
Some generic monster classes are larger than normal and take up 5 panels! They have higher stats that other characters, and can use their massive size to block enemies from passing by them. In addition, their normal attacks have farther reach, and they can overpower weaker foes without using skills.
