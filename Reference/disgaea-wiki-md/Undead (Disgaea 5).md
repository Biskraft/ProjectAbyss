# Undead (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Undead*
The **Undead** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Undead is a sluggish attacker with average-to-low stats except for their HP. Despite their rotten bodies they're quite resilient, thanks to their self-healing capabilities.

## Personality types
- Rotting Away
- Cheerful
- Always Down
