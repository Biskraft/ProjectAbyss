# Female Healer (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Female Healer*
The **Female Healer** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Female Healer, as suggested by their name, is a support class specialized in healing. They learn all of the healing spells along with defensive buffs in order to keep the party healthy and safe.


## Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.
