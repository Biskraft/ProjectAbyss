# Armor Knight (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Armor Knight*
The **Armor Knight** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Graduates of an academy that trains knights in combat and chivalry. Top students are appointed to Netherworld Nobles and granted a set of obsidian armor. Their gallantry and devotion is much admired by children. Recently, female knights have been so on the rise that they've begun to outclass the men.

### Unlock Requirements
The Armor Knight is unlocked by completing the *Worthy of Guarding* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after the completion of the *Steeling to Steal* quest, which unlocks the Thief, and requires Rank 2 or higher class proficiency for both Warrior and Martial Artist to complete.

### Personality Types
- Protective
- Sadist
- Masochist
