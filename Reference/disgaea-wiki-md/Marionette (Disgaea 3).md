# Marionette (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Marionette*
The **Marionette** is a Monster Class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. It is an old toy given sentient life.

Retaining its design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Marionette is a puppet that, through some means, acquired a life of its own, letting it move around freely. Since being given life, the Marionette has removed the strings tying him to his control rods, but has kept his control rods as well as modified them into two Battle Tonfas. The Marionette can, oddly enough, spin its body with the arms stretched out, creating a helicopter motion and actually allowing it to go up in the air. Its body can also come apart and, due to this, it has a tendency to show off a lot (by standing on one hand and balancing its rotating head on its foot). Due to the Marionette's body being made of inorganic materials, it is immune to things that would cause normal living creatures discomfort (as shown with its native Evility, Soulless Offset, which allows it to be immune to Status Conditions).
