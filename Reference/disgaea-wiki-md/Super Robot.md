# Super Robot

- Super Robot** is Thursday's exclusive class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

This class cannot Transmigrate, but it has the ability to steal stats. This class is immune to status ailments.

Team Attack Chance is always 100% between Gordon, Jennifer, and Thursday.

## Quotes
> A super robot made by Jennifer, Thursday can detect life forms, detect smells and do all sorts of cool stuff. He always makes awesome old-school computer sounds and threatens to self-destruct.|The World of Disgaea
