# Overlord Spawn

- Overlord Spawn** (later known as **Scrub Boss**, **Hero**, **Baby**, **Honor Student**, and ultimately **Overlord**, and **Delinquent Prince** and **Raspberyl's Rival** in Raspberyl Mode) is Mao's class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Like most main character classes, Mao focuses on physical weapon use, but can also learn Ice magic.

## Skills
### Weapon
- Sword: Up to 3rd skill (Up to 6th in Absence of Detention)
- Spear: Up to 3rd skill (Up to 6th in AoD)
- Axe: Up to 3rd skill (Up to 6th in AoD)
- Magic: Ice only, up to Giga Level (Tera in AoD)
