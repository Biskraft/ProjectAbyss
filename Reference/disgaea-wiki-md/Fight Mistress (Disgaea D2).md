The **Fight Mistress** is a humanoid unit in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.

## Class Overview
The Fight Mistress is one of the initial classes made available from the start.

The Fight Mistress has higher SPD and HIT, but lower HP and ATK than her male counterpart, but that's the extent of their stat differences.

The Fight Mistress is a physical attacking class in a vein similar to the Warrior and Valkyrie, but is more simplistic in application. Fists are the main weapon of this class, with high ATK and SPD to accomodate. DEF is rather low for a physical attacking class, with SPD being stressed to compensate. With a higher SPD than most starting classes, the Mistress is an excellent character to evade attacks with. Like other physical classes, RES and INT are low, making them vulnerable to Magic. Later tiers of the Fight Mistress also see an increase in Sword/Spear/Bow Mastery, allowing them to branch into other fighting methods later on, though Fists best augment their best stats.

Evilities are all centered around constant attack. These range from attacking twice with a single Counterattack to being able to evade any Counters aimed at her.
