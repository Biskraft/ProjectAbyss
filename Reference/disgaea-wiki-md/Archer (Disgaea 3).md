# Archer (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Archer*
The **Archer** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.

Retaining her design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Archer is a young girl wearing a dress. She also has blond hair in "drill pigtails". All Archers are upbeat and cheerful, as opposed to their more cold and serious counterparts, the Rangers. The Archers and the Rangers both hate each other immensely due to a war many years ago and they have yet to fully resolve the tension. The Archers are also known for being flat chested, having developed that way due to larger breasts causing their ancestors difficulty while trying to operate their bows. Her native Evility, **Accel Shot**, increases her attack power by 5% for every space she has between herself and the enemy.
