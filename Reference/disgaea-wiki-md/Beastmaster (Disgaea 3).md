# Beastmaster (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Beastmaster*
The **Beastmaster** is a Humanoid class from *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.

Retaining her design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, she appears as an extremely wild girl with long and unruly hair. She also has paws for shoulder pads and a tail (but this may just be part of the outfit). Her clothing is also very strange, as it consists of a Blueish/Green top that stretches out into suspenders to hold her pant legs up. She doesn't wear any clothing in between, meaning her undergarments are on full display.

She is an essential character to have for players who are heavy users of monster characters, because her native Evility, Dark Tamer, increases the stats of adjacent ally Monsters. She also has unique specials, Wild Groom and Dominate, which boost the effectiveness of Monsters. She is good with Spears, Axes and Bows (although Axes are not a very good choice, due to her need to stand adjacent to her monsters).


## Skills
In addition to her weapon skills, the Beastmaster also learns the class-specific skills Wild Groom and Dominate by level  up.
