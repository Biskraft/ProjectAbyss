# Red Moon

- Red Moon** (赤い月 *Akai Tsuki*) is a song from *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*. It is played during Chapter 8: Reincarnation of the game.

The song can also be briefly heard in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* when choosing the option "Reincarnate to Atone for Sins" in the Dark Assembly.
