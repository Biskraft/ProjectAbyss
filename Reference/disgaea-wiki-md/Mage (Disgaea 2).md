# Mage (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Mage*
The **Mage** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Mage is a standard caster class, with good magical attack but poor physical defense. Throughout their different Tiers they learn all elemental spells, as well as some debuffs.

## Skills
### Passive
- Decrease SP cost of spells by 25%(+1% per Lover).

### Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.
