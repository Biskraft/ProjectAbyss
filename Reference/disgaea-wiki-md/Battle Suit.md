# Battle Suit

|  |


The **Battle Suit** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Battle Suit is an offensive class with high physical power. The suit offers many offensive and defensive boosts, though some of them might put too much strain on the wearer.
