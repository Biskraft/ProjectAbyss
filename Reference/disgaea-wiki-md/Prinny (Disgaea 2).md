# Prinny (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Prinny*

The **Prinny** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
Prinnies are weak units with unimpressive stats all around. They're mostly useful as cannonfodder, being easy to sacrifice thanks to their low maintenance cost and their use as walking bombs.
## Skills

### Passive

- Explode when thrown or when within 3 spaces from an exploding Prinny. Explosion damage = 50%(+1% per Lover) of your remaining HP(ignores all defense and evasion). Hits radius-2 circle.
- Revive/heal cost at the hospital is always 1 HL.
