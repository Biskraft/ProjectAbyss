# Living Geo Symbol

- Living Geo Symbols** are a special form of Geo Symbol that were first introduced in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*. They are commonly found in the Item World and the Dark Sun World. Unlike normal Geo Symbols, they are able to move and carry their effects to whichever color Geo Panel they land on. Some Living Geo Symbols have set movement patterns, while others (such as those in the Item World) move randomly across the screen.
