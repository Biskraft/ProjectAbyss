# Flora Beast (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Flora Beast*
The **Flora Beast** is a Monster class from *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Flora Beast is a support class with stats favoring only magical defense and healing. Their Evilities provide them with auras that keep nearby allies healthy and protected.
