# Prisoner

In *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*, captured units become **prisoners** of the rebellion army. They are kept in the Interrogation Room, where they can be interrogated, where they are weakened by preforming actions such as starving them, verbal misconduct, and physical abuse. When weakened, they can then be solicited into the party, or have their power extracted and turned into an extract that can be used to boost a character's stats. Any prisoner can be released and turned into a citizen of Seraphina's pocket Netherworld or used to support a rebellion squad.
