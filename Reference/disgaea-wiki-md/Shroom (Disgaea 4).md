# Shroom (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Shroom*

The **Shroom** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Shroom is an offensive support unit who inflicts ailments with their attacks. Their spores weaken nearby female units.
