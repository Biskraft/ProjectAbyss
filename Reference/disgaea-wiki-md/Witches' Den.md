  - Witches' Den** is the third map of Episode 6: Laharl's Challenge in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Story
Laharl-tachi come upon the Prinnies, who say it can't hurt for them to try to gain the title of Overlord. Their plan is... baseball. Laharl and Etna are ... not amused.

## Strategy
This is not a 'Prinny Bowling' map, in that you can throw one Prinny and make them *all* explode. You can, however, throw them in smaller sequences. Exploding Prinnies is fun. Do it while you can, because upon repeat, the Prinnies are replaced by a set of higher-level Imps.
