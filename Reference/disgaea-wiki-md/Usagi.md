# Usagi

- Usagi** (Japanese: ウサギ *Usagi*) is Pleinair's doll. He appears in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* between chapters with Pleinair. He also appears in *Disgaea DS* in Pleinair's unique special, *Usagi Drop.*

## Trivia
- Usagi appears as the news anchor for SatilTV in Disgaea 2.
- His name is Japanese for "rabbit."
