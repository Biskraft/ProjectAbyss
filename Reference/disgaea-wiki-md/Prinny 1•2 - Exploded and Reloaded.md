# Prinny 1•2: Exploded and Reloaded

*(This article is a stub)*
    - Prinny 1•2: Exploded and Reloaded*** is a compilation of the PlayStation Portable games *Prinny: Can I Really Be the Hero?* and *Prinny 2: Dawn of Operation Panties, Dood!* for the Nintendo Switch. It was announced on March 5, 2020, and released as a physical compilation on October 13, 2020. However, the digital versions of the two games are sold separately, and not as a compilation.
