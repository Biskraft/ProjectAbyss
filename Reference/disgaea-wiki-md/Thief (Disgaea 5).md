# Thief (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Thief*

The **Thief** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Thief is a class specialized in stealing, helping the party by disarming the enemy and getting rare loot. They have low stats overall except for movement and evasion so they prefer to fight from a long distance if necessary.
