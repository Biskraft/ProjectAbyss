# Marionette (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Marionette*

The **Marionette** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Marionette is a resilient unit with above average defensive stats coupled with ailment immunity. Their attacks are innacurate but lower the enemy's evasion in return.
## Skills

### Passive

- You're immune to Status Ailments.
- Lovers increase stats by 1.25% instead of the usual 1%.
