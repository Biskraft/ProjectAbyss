# Holt Village

thumb|Holt Village and background for some talking scenes.
  - Holt Village** (aka "town") is the base area in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*. Adell, the main character of the game, was raised here by Mom and Dad who unlike him turned from human into monster 15 years ago, later spawning Hanako and Taro, and lives peacefully with the other villagers, including Mario Boy, Moocho, Friday, and Bridget.

The Dimension Guide is a girl in town. There is also Moocho and Solei on the bridge wall.

## Secrets
- There is a chest which can be opened containing 200HL
- There is a button which you can push, it doesn't explain what it does.
