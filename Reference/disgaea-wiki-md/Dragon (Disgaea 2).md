# Dragon (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Dragon*

The **Dragon** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Dragon is a strong and bulky unit specialized in Fire damage. Their power is limited only by their low accuracy and movement.
## Skills

### Passive

- Nulls Fire damage received. Absorb 1% of that damage per Lover.
