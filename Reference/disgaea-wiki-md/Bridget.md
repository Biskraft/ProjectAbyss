# Bridget

thumb
thumb
  - Bridget** is someone Hanako mentions at the start of *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

Who this is becomes clarified later in town, it is an Alraune who asks Adell "why aren't you looking at me?"

Although Bridget is a girl's name and the character looks effeminate, Bridget quips "it's okay, I'm a guy" indicating maleness.

This may be a joke in respect to the human character Bridget from the 2002 game "Guilty Gear XX" who is a boy who resembles a girl.
