# Axel's Scheme

- Axel's Scheme** is the fourth episode of *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*
### Story
After the gang loses to Etna, Adell's mother tells him that Hanako and Taro have been kidnapped by Axel.
By crossing the maps, Adell reveals that his real parents disappeared a long time ago.
In the end, Hanako joins the party and Taro is drowned by the river.
