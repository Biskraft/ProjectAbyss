  - Student Chaos** is the second map in Ch 3: The Freshman Leader! in  *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.

There is no story associated with this map.

## Strategy
There's a break in the middle of the map, all characters will have to be thrown across. There are plenty of Slimes, don't forget that melee attacks are not very effective on them; they're very weak to Fire anyway, so a Fire Mage or Skull that you've been using the whole game anyway will be strong enough to nuke them one at a time. Bring out the ranged characters to take care of those Gunslingers before they and their +50% buff take care of you.
