# Charm

- Charm** is one of the Status Ailments that can be inflicted upon the characters. It was introduced in *Disgaea 5*.

## Description
It's represented by pink hearts above the character's head. Units affected by it can't be controlled and will attack their own teammates. A unit can't inflict Charm on their allies.

## Specialists
Two Specialists deal with the Charm ailment:

- Having a **Pastor** on any equipment increases a character's resistance to the Charm status by 1% per level.

- Having a **Charmer** on any equipment gives a character's normal attacks a chance to inflict Charm by 1% per level.

## Methods to inflict Charm
### Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance
- Charm (Spell)
- Dazzling Eyes (Seraphina skill)
- Malicious Eye (Seraphina combo skill)
- Explosive Elegance (Seraphina combo skill)
- Heart Pounder (Felynn Dual Magichange skill)
- Hip Attack (Succubus skill)
- Dazzling Stage (Succubus skill)
- Temptation (Succubus Evility)
- Ice Dancer (Sea Angel Magichange skill)
