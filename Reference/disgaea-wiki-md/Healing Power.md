# Healing Power

The amount of HP restored by healing skills follows a specific formula, which changes throughout the franchise.

### *<u>Disgaea</u>*
  - Healing = StatScaling*SkillPower*MasteryBonus**

- StatScaling changes depending on your INT:
      - (25 + INT/4)** from 1 to 400 INT
      - (75 + INT/8)** from 401 to 900 INT
      - (142,5 + INT/20)** from 901 INT onwards
- MasteryBonus is **(1 + 0.03*StaffMasteryLevel)**, with a max value of 2.

### *<u>Disgaea 2</u>*
  - Healing = StatScaling*(SkillPower + MasteryBonus)*SecondaryMult**

- StatScaling changes depending on your RES:
      - (80 + RES*0.8)** from 1 to 400 RES
      - (240 + RES*0.4)** from 401 to 900 RES
      - (456 + RES*0.16)** from 901 RES onward
- MasteryBonus is **(0.03*StaffMasteryLevel)**, with a max value of 1. Only applies to spells.
- SecondaryMult is a second power multiplier all skills have in this game.
- Flonne's passive applies another multiplier of **(1.5 + FlonneLovers/100)**

### <u>*Disgaea 3* onwards</u>
  - Healing = RES*SkillPower*BoostMultiplier**

- BoostMultiplier is the increase from enhancements at the Evility Shop
- Note that various Evilities may add extra modifiers to this formula
