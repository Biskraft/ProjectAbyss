# Skull (Disgaea D2)

|  |

*Main article: Skull*
The **Skull** (also known as the **Magician**) is a humanoid class in  *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.

## Quotes
> An earnest magician boy. Can use very powerful skills and magic.|Job Description, *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*

> Many boys who manifest mysterious powers attend a magic school. Those at the tops of their class earn the right to be Magicians. They receive traditional clothing and glasses that increase their magic powers. They study hard, even after graduating, and are always looking to improve. Because they live in harsher environments, they are more earnest and hardworking than Witches.|*Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*

## Class Overview
The Skull is one of the initial classes made available from the start.

Skulls are almost identical to their counter-parts, the Witches, albeit that their evilities are more focused on dealing more damage with their spells and special skills, at the cost of using more of their SP. Stat-wise, the Skull is much more frail than the Witch, with less DEF SP, and HIT, but having higher HP, SPD, and RES as a trade-off.

Both genders have pitiful ATK, making physical weapons ineffective. However, later tiers can eventually accommodate Spears, Swords, and Guns, despite Staves and Books being this class' strongest weapons. As stated previously, Evilities are aimed at dealing out more damage with more SP. Evilities range from a 30% increase in special skill power at the cost of adding 50% SP to the cost, to a 50% increase in power when SP is below half.
