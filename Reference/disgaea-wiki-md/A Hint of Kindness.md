# A Hint of Kindness

- A Hint of Kindness** is the third episode of *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, following Enter Flonne.

## Story
It's payday for the Prinnies, and Laharl needs to pay up. So what's a demon to do? Why, find the richest demon living nearby and loot his mansion for the prinnies' salary! After bypassing the mansion's security system with a level 10 Item, Laharl, Etna and Flonne enter the mansion and defeat all the enemies inside. They also find a painting of Laharl's father, King Krichevskoy, and a number of other items looted from Laharl's Castle.

In the final room, they encounter Hoggmeiser, a former vassal of King Krichevskoy. Angered by Hoggmeiser's actions and behaviour, Laharl defeats the demon. But before he can land the finishing strike, Hoggmeiser's young son Porkmeiser stands between them and stops Laharl. Faced with this display of bravery, Laharl decides to allow Hoggmeiser his life and some of his riches. Out of gratitude, Hoggmeiser then joins the party.

## Endings
If the player has racked up 50 or more Ally Kills, they will be given the choice of whether or not to kill Hoggmeiser. If the player chooses to kill Hoggmeiser, the Flonne Tragedy ending will be triggered and the game will end. If the player chooses not to kill Hoggmeiser, the game will proceed as normal.
