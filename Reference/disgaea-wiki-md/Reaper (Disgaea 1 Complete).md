# Reaper (Disgaea 1 Complete)

- Reaper** is a monster class featured in the *Complete* release of *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, replacing the Shadow class from previous versions. Apart from visual changes, this new class is unchanged from the class it has replaced.

## Collection Book
- "Known as dark gods, they are hated by others in the Netherworld. Rumor has it, an upper-class Shadow has control over the lost souls. Their magic powers are second-to-none in the Netherworld, and they are especially good at using fire and ice magic."*
