# Marionette

thumb|A Marionette in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.
The **Marionette** is a recurring monster class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Appearance
In its default coloration, it is white with green clothing.

## Characteristics
The Marionette is a moving puppet. They are neglected toys of children who have grown out of them. The Marionette has removed the strings tying it to its control rods, but has kept the control rods as well as modified them into two Battle Tonfas.
