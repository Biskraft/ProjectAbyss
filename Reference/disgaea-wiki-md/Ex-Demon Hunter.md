- "Hero who saved the World of Veldime. Hot-blooded, righteous, and honest."*

  - Ex-Demon Hunter** is Adell's exclusive DLC class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. He is the usual brawler class that gives a lot of damage but runs out his reserves of energy pretty quick.

Because of his unique Evility, Mercy, Adell is actually quite useful for leveling up weaker teammates since it leaves the enemy with 1hp, easy pickings for weak chars to power level.
