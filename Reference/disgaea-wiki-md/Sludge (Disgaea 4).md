# Sludge (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Sludge*

The **Sludge** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Sludge is a unit with superb physical defense but low resistance to magical attacks. Their attacks are not too powerful, but compensate by inflicting Poison.

## Personality types

- Slimy
