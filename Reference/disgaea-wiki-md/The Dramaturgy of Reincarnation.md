# The Dramaturgy of Reincarnation

- The Dramaturgy of Reincarnation** is the 1st episode of Act 1 of *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*, and the 1st episode overall.

## Summary

## Story Impact

- Zed, Cerberus, Ivar and the God of Destruction are introduced.
- Ivar convenes the Darkest Assembly to discuss the God of Destruction problem.
- Zed and Cerberus crash the Darkest Assembly and reveal that Zed has already defeated the God of Destruction.
- Zed and Cerberus recount their defeat of the Darkest Assembly.
  - Zed and Cerberus arrive at the Prinny World.
  - Zed gains a Prinny vassal.
  - Zed is killed by the God of Destruction.
- Zed reveals his special power, Super Reincarnation.

## Game Impact

- The following tutorials were introduced:
  - Basics of Battle 1 (Moving, Attacking)
  - Basics of Battle 2 (Team Attacks, Combos)
  - Basics of Battle 3 (Lifting, Throwing)
  - Basics of Battle 4 (Geo Effects)
- Zed learns Evility, Defy the Strong.
