# Super Reincarnation

> It's a powerful spell that allows someone to live their life over again until they've seen their wish through. It's a system that'll seek out the best avenue and resurrect the contractor, no matter how many times they've died. After someone's been Super Reincarnated once, they become stuck within the cycle, which is why it's forbidden.|Cerberus
  - Super Reincarnation** is a modified reincarnation system introduced in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*. It is also a major plotline element in the game's story.

## Gameplay
Similar to regular reincarnation, Super Reincarnation returns a character to Level 1. Using built up Karma obtained from redeeming D-Merit objectives and by gaining large amounts of levels, the base stats of a character can be raised, making their stats raise higher per level up. Unlike prior games, the Karma cost to upgrade a base stat increases proportionally as the base stat is upgraded. Karma is also used to purchase upgrades such as counter amount boosts, damage amount increases, and movement speed increases, which can be done during a Super Reincarnation.

When a chapter is completed for the first time, the reincarnation count of every character in the party at the time increases by one internally, though this does not affect anything not directly related to the number, such as quests. This only occurs during the initial playthrough on the save, repeating a chapter will not award the reincarnation point. In either case, the party's level is not reset during a chapter's completion. A character's level is only reset during a manual reincarnation using the Dark Assembly.

## Storyline
Through the forbidden magic "Super Reincarnation" taught by Cerberus, Zed can transcend death and reincarnate in another world when defeated by the God of Destruction. However, doing this will lock the user of Super Reincarnation in a cycle. This element of Super Reincarnation forms the progression framework of the plotline of *Disgaea 6*. Generally, Zed gets killed with massive bursts of 999,999,999 damage by the God of Destruction and is reincarnated using Super Reincarnation, which causes him to be revived on a different Netherworld, which is determined by the Super Reincarnation system.

## Trivia
- The nature of Super Reincarnation (being reset to Level 1, but having certain stats carry over) is similar to the KO system from *Zettai Hero Project: Unlosing Ranger VS. Darkdeath Evilman*.
