# Lady Samurai

thumb|Artwork of the Lady Samurai for *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.
The **Lady Samurai** (also known as **Shrine Maiden** or simply **Samurai**) is a recurring humanoid class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Appearance


## Characteristics
For centuries, the Lady Samurai have protected the holy roads that connect the Netherworld to parallel worlds, but since most of these holy grounds have been destroyed by cataclysm, most now serve as professional swordsmen. Despite this, purity, loyalty, and pride remain strong in their hearts. They have a unique belief system called "Bushi Road".

## Gameplay


## Appearance
### *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
*Main article: Samurai (Disgaea)*
Samurai are proficient with primarily swords, axes, and spears. They are unlocked by having a Female Warrior and a Female Martial Artist with a total level of ten or higher.

### *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days*
*Main article: Lady Samurai (Disgaea 2)*
While missing from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Lady Samurai return in *Dark Hero Days*, unlockable by having save data from *Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness*.

### *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
*Main article: Lady Samurai (Disgaea 3)*
The Lady Samurai can be unlocked by leveling both a Valkyrie and an Archer to level 35.

### *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
*Main article: Lady Samurai (Disgaea 4)*
The Lady Samurai can be unlocked by leveling both a Valkyrie and an Archer to level 30.

### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Lady Samurai (Disgaea D2)*
The Lady Samurai reappears in *Disgaea D2*.

### *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
*Main article: Lady Samurai (Disgaea 5)*
The Lady Samurai can be unlocked by leveling a Warrior/Valkyrie to rank 3 and an Archer to rank 2, then completing the associated quest.

### *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*
*Main article: Samurai (Disgaea 6)*
The Samurai returns in *Disgaea 6* and is unlocked by completing the *Loyal Blade* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after the completion of the *On the Hunt* quest, which unlocks the Archer, and requires Rank 1 or higher class proficiency for Archer and Rank 2 or higher class proficiency for Warrior to complete.

## Other Appearances
### *Makai Kingdom*
Samurai can be either male or female, each having proficiency with katanas and spears.
