  - Frigid Garden** is the second stage in Episode 8: Reincarnation in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.
## Story
Laharl asks Flonne if she knows anything about the prinnies, because she has been acting strange all morning. Flonne refuses to answer, instead pointing out that Laharl should use her name when adressing her. He hasn't done so since they met and she wants him to know it's inconsiderate. His attitude will prevent him from becoming a respecting overlord. Laharl stops talking to Flonne in reaction to her comments.
