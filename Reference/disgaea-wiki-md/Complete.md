# Complete

The **Complete** series is remastered games:
- Disgaea 1 Complete
- Disgaea 4 Complete+
- Disgaea 5 Complete

The purpose of the *Complete* games is to provide a definitive version of the game they are based on. This includes providing any DLC within the game itself, and updating mechanical features to make the games more in-line with one another. This includes updating Class designs (such as the Skulls) and providing quality of life improvements.

## Trivia
- Currently, *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* and *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice* are the only mainline games to not have *Complete* versions.
