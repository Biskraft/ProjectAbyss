# Dragon

thumb|The Dragon in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.
The **Dragon** is a recurring monster class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Appearance
They are based on the western dragon. They stand up and are roughly humanoid in appearance. They have large bat like wings on their back. Dragons have a long tail, roughly humanoid arms, and two clawed legs. Their heads have two horns and two black spikes on them.

In their default coloration, they are green.

## Characteristics
They are described as the most powerful clan of monsters and are known throughout Celestia and the Human World. Research was conducted on dragons in order to better know how to summon and control them in battle. Lower ranking dragons are summoned to help out in critical situations by angels and humans.
