# Zommie III

- Zommie III** is a 3rd Zombie in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* found in Holt, possibly related to the Zommie who was one of Krichevskoy's Vassals in the 1st game.

He has a 50% affinity to fire and -50% to wind.

He explains the Demons' Society functioning on contracts, prompting Rozalin to comment on how they're a "nuissance". (it's actually meant to be spelled with one S, this is probably a typo)
