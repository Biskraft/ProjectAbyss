# Axe

The **Axe** is a weapon type in the *Disgaea* series. This weapon specializes in raw power: it gives the highest ATK and Critical Hit rate bonuses, and their attacks and skills reduce the target's DEF. In exchange, Axes reduce the wielder's HIT and/or SPD (depending on the game), and their skills have short range or small areas of effect.

## *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*


In this game, Axes reduce the wielder's HIT. Gordon specializes in Axes, as well as the Male Warrior class.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Axes, please see List of Disgaea 1 Weapons#Axe.

## *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*


As in the previous game, Axes reduce the wielder's HIT. Only Fubuki has the Axe as their main weapon, but he is only available in the Land of Carnage after beating the third stage of the last chapter in Adell Mode. 

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Axes, please see List of Disgaea 2 Weapons#Axe.

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*


From this game onwards, Axes reduce the wielder's SPD. Mao, the Warrior and Brute Warrior classes specialize in Axes.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Axes, please see List of Disgaea 3 Weapons#Axe.

## *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*


As in the previous game, Axes reduce the wielder's SPD. Fuka specializes in Axes, as well as the Warrior and Battle Suit classes.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Axes, please see List of Disgaea 4 Weapons#Axe.

## *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*


In this game, Axes reduce both the wielder's HIT and SPD(the latter by a larger amount). Red Magnus specializes in Axes, as well as the Warrior class.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Axes, please see List of Disgaea 5 Weapons#Axe.
