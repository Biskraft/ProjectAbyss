# Armor Knight (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Armor Knight*

The **Armor Knight** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Armor Knight is a tank class with excellent defensive stats all around. They are a Spear-user, specialized in taking damage in place of their allies.

## Personality types

- Protective
- Sadist
- Shy
