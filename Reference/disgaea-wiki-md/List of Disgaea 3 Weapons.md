# List of Disgaea 3 Weapons

This is a list of **Disgaea 3 Weapons**.

## Information
In *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*, items of rank 30 and below can be bought from the store, while ranked 31-34 items can only obtained in the Item World. Items ranked 35-39 can be obtained in the Land of Carnage while a rank 40 items can only be obtained by stealing from the Item God 2 in a rank 39 item in the Land of Carnage. The rank 40 monster weapon Baal Body can also be obtained by stealing it from Baal in his recruiting mission, while the Rank 40 Usagi Staff can only be obtained by stealing it from Plenair in her recruiting mission.

### Sword
*Main article: Sword#Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*


The Sword returns as a weapon type in *Disgaea 3*. Again, it is a commonly-used weapon that tends to only boost attack. Some Swords can also boost INT greatly, such as the Baal Sword, Baal Horn, or the Sunflower. Both Mao and Almaz use swords.

### Axe
*Main article: Axe#Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*


The Axe returns in *Disgaea 3* as a weapon type, but instead of lowering Hit, it lowers Speed. This alteration makes Axe users better at hitting targets, but also makes them slower targets, thus making *them* easier to hit. No noteworthy characters use Axes.


<br />
### Staff
*Main article: Staff#Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*


The Staff returns as a weapon type in Disgaea 3. Like always, they have no set skills of their own, and basically are only usable in a mage's hands, and perhaps a healer. No noteworthy characters use Staves in this game. Staves increase Magic range by 1.
