# Faery

|  |

The **Faery** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

They are pranksters whose skills mess with the enemy.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 5 onwards.

## Quotes
> Faeries are demons that even appear in the Human World. In the Netherworld, their magic powers get dispersed, so they wear a special cape to prevent it. When it gets angry, a Faery swallows its enemy with its huge mouth. Unlike other demons, faeries have strong bonds within their species.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
