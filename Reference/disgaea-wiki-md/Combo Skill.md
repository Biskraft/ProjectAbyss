# Combo Skill

- Combination Skills** are a new type of skill introduced in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*. They are all unlocked at the beginning of Episode 9, having no level requirement to be learned.

Each Combo Skill has a designated companion, and they can only be used if said companion is in one of the 8 panels surrounding the owner of the skill. The strength of the effect depends only on the stats of the owner, but EXP/Mana gained will still be split amongst all participants. Like with Team Attacks, enabling a Combo Skill doesn't spend the turn of the helper unit.
