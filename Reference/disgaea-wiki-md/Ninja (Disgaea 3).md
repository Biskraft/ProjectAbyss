# Ninja (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Ninja*
The **Ninja** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Retaining his design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Ninja is dressed in the stereotypical "black ninja uniform" and "ninja mask". He also wears a scarf around his neck. Ninjas are masters of speed and stealth and as such, their Speed Aptitudes are high. With their long movement range, they are best used to scout out areas and pick off the weaker adversaries or to quickly reach a certain point in a map (such as where an important Geo Block is located). The Ninja truly shines when using Fist weapons. The Ninja's native Evility, **Misty Haze**, gives him a 50% chance of evading attacks coming from the front.

## Skills
The spells available to Ninjas are Poison and Stun, both of which can be learned at any tier.
