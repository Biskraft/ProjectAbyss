# Gunslinger

|  |

The **Gunslinger** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. The Gunslinger appears as a large chested cowgirl with very skimpy clothes, glasses, and a bullet holed hat. Her design is based off of the Merchants from *Phantom Brave* (and thus, she has Evilities that revolve around making money). Like her male counterpart, she can use Guns with extraordinary results. Her native Evility, **Big Chance**, increases the Bonus Gauge if she takes part in a Combo, effectively netting the player more Hell, items and Bonus EXP.
