# List of Disgaea 4 Accessories

## Information
Most Armor can be bought from the standard shop. Ranks 31+ must be obtained in the Item World, ranks 35+ only while in Land of Carnage.

Most other Accessories can be bought from the Specialty Shop from the Rosen Queen Evil Symbol.
