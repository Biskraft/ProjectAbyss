# Cave of Ordeal

thumb|300px|Cave of Ordeals Background
The **Cave of Ordeal**, also called Cave of Ordeals, is a recurring battlefield in the Disgaea Universe. The Cave of Ordeal has different challenges leading up to one final big reward for finishing it, and also often contains the best leveling spot in the game. This area is unlocked by passing the appropriate bill in the Dark Assembly, which adds the Ordeals to the Dimension Guide's list of battles.

## *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* Maps
- Cave of Ordeal 1
- Cave of Ordeal 2
- Cave of Ordeal 3
- Cave of Ordeal 4
- Cave of Ordeal 5
- Demonhall Mirror - only in the remakes *Disgaea DS*, *Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness* (PSP),  *Disgaea PC*. and *Disgaea 1 Complete*.

## *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* Maps

- Ordeal 2
- Ordeal 3
- Ordeal 4
- Ordeal 5
