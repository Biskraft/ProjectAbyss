  - Neo Eden** is the second map of the Human World series. It has no geo symbols or panels.

## Strategy
Basically, pretty much the same strategy applies here as on the previous map. Most of the enemies are again gun users, except for the three Cyborg, who use axes.

The enemies are mostly grouped together so take advantage of that. Use magic or area of effect skills to quickly reduce their numbers. EDF Soldiers have a high Defense but also fairly low Resistance. So if you're heaving some trouble, using magic over melee might just help you tip the scales.
