# Rifle Demon

thumb|A Rifle Demon in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.
The **Rifle Demon** is a recurring monster class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Appearance
They are large black draconic creatures. They have three eyes, curved horns, and a boxy mouth with lots of teeth. One of their bulky arms has a laser cannon while the other ends in a hand with three fingers. They have small bat wings compared to the size of their body.

## Characteristics
During Dark Assembly meetings, they commonly show up drunk.
