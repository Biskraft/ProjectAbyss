# Archer (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Archer*
The **Archer** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Members of Badre, an all-women, ice mountain village. They can listen to the wind to deduce its force and direction. Archers strictly adhere to the rules set forth by their leader, Sevria, and hate any weapons besides bows. Their breasts slowly got smaller over the years, so as not to hinder their use of the bow.

### Unlock Requirements
The Archer is unlocked by completing the *On the Hunt* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after the completion of the *Steeling to Steal* quest, which unlocks the Thief, and requires Rank 1 or higher class profiency for both Warrior and Clergy to complete.

The completion of the *On the Hunt* quest is required to unlock the Samurai recruitment quest, *Loyal Blade*, and the Mecha Girl recruitment quest, *Robot Army*.

### Personality Types
- Serious
- Cold
- Regional
