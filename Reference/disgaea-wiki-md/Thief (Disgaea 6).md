# Thief (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Thief*
The **Thief** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Stealing is a traditional skill passed down since ancient times. The Thieves Guild is the largest trade union in the Netherworlds, and can be hired to steal anything. In recent years, they have even found ways to pilfer the emotions and abilities of people. Only time will tell where things develop from here.

### Unlock Requirements
The Thief is unlocked by completing the *Steeling to Steal* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after clearing Stage 2-1, *Throne Room*, and requires the creation of a Martial Artist.

The completion of the *Steeling to Steal* quest is required to unlock the Archer recruitment quest, *On the Hunt*, Gunner recruitment quest, *Picking a Partner*, Ninja recruitment quest, *Ninja Nite*, the Armor Knight recruitment quest, *Worthy of Guarding*, and the Magic Knight recruitment quest, *Magical Warrior*.
