# Mage (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Mage*

The **Mage** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Mage is a standard caster class, with high magical stats but low physical ones. They use elemental spells to hit the enemy's weakness from a distance. The Mage's magic is very SP-efficient, letting her use spells for a long time.

Red Mage, Green Mage and Blue Mage are alternate versions of Tier 1, and can't be promoted into each other.

- Laid-back
- Daring
