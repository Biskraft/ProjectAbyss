# Ranger

|  |

The **Ranger**, also known as simply the **Male Archer**, is a class introduced in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.

The Ranger's design is that of a young, tall male demon with glasses. He also wears a long coat with large buttons on it. The Rangers once lived alongside the  Archers, until a war broke out due to the Archers opposing the cold-hearted nature of the Rangers. The war only ended after several Overlords stepped in to mediate, and, presumably, the Rangers were banished. Modern day Rangers serve primarily as mercenaries, doing jobs that keep up the pay. His native Evility, **Positional Energy**, increases his attack power when he is at a higher height than his foe.

## Disgaea 3: Absence of Detention
In the Vita port, the Ranger gains the unique class skill "Floater Shot", which is effective against flying enemies like the Winged Warrior.
