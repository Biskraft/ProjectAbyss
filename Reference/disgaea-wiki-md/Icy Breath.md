  - Icy Breath** is the second map of the second episode, Enter Flonne, in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Story
Laharl and Etna catch up to Flonne. Flonne summons some help, and says Laharl and company should run if it gets too dangerous.

There is a scene in Celestia where Lamington is speaking with an unidentified, off-screen person; the person inquires if Flonne met Laharl safely, and Lamington replies that Flonne is managing in her own way.

## Map Strategy
Many Undead, left side. Also right side. They're pretty spread out, and shouldn't be much trouble.
