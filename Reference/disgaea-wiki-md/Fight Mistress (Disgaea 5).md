# Fight Mistress (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Fight Mistress*
The **Fight Mistress** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Fight Mistress is a Fist-user with good physical power and evasion but low defenses. She specializes in dodging and responding with counter attacks.

- Tomboy
- Hungry
