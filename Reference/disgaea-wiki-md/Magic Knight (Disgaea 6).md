# Magic Knight (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Magic Knight*
The **Magic Knight** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Wielders of swords and magic, of which few exist. Their contract with fairies lets them unleash spells and physical attacks at once. Uncharacteristically chivalrous and loyal for demons, they consider failing to uphold the Sword's Oath punishable by death, and can see the future with a power called the Zone.

### Unlock Requirements
The Magic Knight is unlocked by completing the *Magical Warrior* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after the completion of the *Steeling to Steal* quest, which unlocks the Thief, and requires Rank 2 or higher class proficiency for both Warrior and Witch to complete.

### Personality Types
- Calm
- Sisterly
- Heroine
