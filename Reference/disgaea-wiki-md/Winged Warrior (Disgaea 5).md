# Winged Warrior (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Winged Warrior*
The **Winged Warrior** is a Monster class from *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Winged Warrior is a support class who prefers to incapacitate the enemy rather than fight directly. Their excellent movement but poor defenses favor hit-and-run tactics.

## Personality types
- Airborne
- Selfish
- UFO
