# Archer (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Archer* 
The **Archer** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Archer is a long-range attacker specialized in using Bows. While not too powerful, their long range allows them to fight without fear of being hit back.

## Skills
### Passive
- Increase normal attack range by 1(+1 per 10 Lovers) while a Bow is equipped.
