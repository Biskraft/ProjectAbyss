# Weapon

- Weapons** are a category of equipment in the *Disgaea* series. There are various types of weapon, and each unit can only equip one at a time. Their main function is to increase attack power, though most of them come with other bonuses as well.

Like any other item, they can be strengthened by adding Specialists or going through its Item World.
