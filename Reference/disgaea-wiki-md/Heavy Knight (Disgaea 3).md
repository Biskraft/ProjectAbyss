# Heavy Knight (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Heavy Knight*
> The END!|Heavy Knight, *Disgaea 3* 
The **Heavy Knight** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Retaining his design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, he is exactly what his name implies, a gigantic behemoth in heavy armor. As such, his movement is poor but his defensive stats are off the charts, meaning he can take the bulk of most physical attacks with little harm. Players often equip Heavy Knights with Spears to increase their Defense and their attack range (which helps with their low movement). Heavy Knights are also decent with Axes. The Heavy Knight's native Evility, **Aegis**, decreases the damage received from the front.

## Trivia
- When talking to him in class, he may sometimes mention a joke about him unleashing super powers when his armor is removed. This is a reference to several stories in fiction where someone wears something (like armor or a belt) to limit their powers but should that be removed, they unleash their full powers, such as Machoke from Pokemon, Bui from Yuyu Hakusho, and Gearfried the Iron Knight from Yu-Gi-Oh.
- The character Gold Knuckle is a gold recolor of the Heavy Knight Class.
