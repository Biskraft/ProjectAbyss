    - Disgaea RPG*** (魔界戦記ディスガイア*RPG*, *Makai Senki Disgaea RPG*) is a spin-off title of the *Disgaea* series released for iOS and Android devices in Japan. Since its initial release on March 19, 2019, the service had faced multiple reported issues and underwent maintenance for several months, relaunching in November 2019.

In December 2020, it was announced that the game will have an English version release during the middle of 2021.
