# Tyrant (Term)

- Tyrant** is a recurring title in the series, given to the most powerful of Demons. It is unknown if a Tyrant is a variant of Overlord, or just a powerful demon, though three of the five known title holders are Overlords.

## Known Tyrants
- Baal
- Eryndum
- Valvatorez
- Laharl (if Baal is defeated in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*)
- Killia
