# Cooking Corridor

- Cooking Corridor** is the third map in Ch.2: Mao's Heart in  *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.

Due to the large amount of story attached to the previous map, there is no story that happens upon selecting this map.

## Strategy

You can attack through the walls. So can the mobs. So can Beryl-tachi. 

Throwing the central Red Block will remove a large section of the wall, allowing access to at least some of the mobs.
