# Prinny (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Prinny (Main)*
The **Prinny** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. They are the souls of humans (or demons) who have committed a Sin and must pay for what they did. They are usually employed as cheap labor in the Netherworld.

Just like in the previous 2 games, the Prinnies are medium-sized, blue birds, similar to penguins. They have wide, circular eyes, small demon wings, peg legs, and their signature fanny pack, or "Pouch of Holding", which contains their various personal items. They also have a stitch just under their beak. Prinnies have relatively decent ATK and DEF strength and are fairly accurate with their strikes. Unfortunately, for some unexplainable reason, throwing them causes them to explode (As shown in their native Evility, Explosive Nature, which does just that). Many players use this to their advantage and often use Prinnies as living grenades since the explosion will damage all units within a certain radius.
