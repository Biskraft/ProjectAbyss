# Beastmaster (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Beastmaster*
The **Beastmaster** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Beastmaster is a unique support specialized in strengthening Monster units and fighting in tandem with them. They not only increase their power, but also speed up their growth.
