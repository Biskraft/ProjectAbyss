  - Seraph** is the exclusive class of Lamington, the final boss of *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and its remakes. Seraph is the highest rank of Angel, and are considered Celestia's equivalent of the Netherworld Overlords.
He is immune to all status ailments.
