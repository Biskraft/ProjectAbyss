# Thief (Disgaea 1 Complete)

|  |

*Main article: Thief*
- For the original class, see Thief (Disgaea)*

The female **Thief** is an unlockable class in the *Complete *release of *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, replacing the male class of the same name from previous versions. Apart from gender and visual changes, the class is unchanged from the original.

## Collection Book
- "Stealing is officially recognized as an advanced technique. The Thieves' Guild is the largest organization of the Netherworld. Thieves will steal anything by request, and anyone can join the Thieves Guild as long as they have the skill. The most legendary of them all is known as the "King of Thieves"."*
