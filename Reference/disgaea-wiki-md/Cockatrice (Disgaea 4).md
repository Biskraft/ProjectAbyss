# Cockatrice (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Roc*

The **Cockatrice** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Cockatrice is an offensive support that inflicts ailments with their attacks and supresses nearby enemies with their Evilities.
