# Defying God (B)

**Defying God** is the last story stage in Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten. If you have beaten the previous stage, Boiling Point, by using Valvatorez only you will fight 4 Parts of God as your final boss.  Beating this stage will **automatically start ** a New Cycle (story restarts at Chapter 1, no epilogues are viewed, and the player is not given the opportunity to enter the Post-Game). |

## Strategy
You have to fight four extremely powerful, modified, fused Eryngi.  Each enemy has HP in the range of 100,000 and can hit multiple friendly units for damage in the tens of thousands.  There is a huge increase in difficulty compared to previous stages; jumping from level 100 to level 1000. You can either power level to stand a chance against these high level foes or use the dupe trick.

The best course of strategy is to focus one unit at a time with the Heart Cannon strategy. You can't use the Lift-Sacrifice Strategy since all the enemy units are fused.
