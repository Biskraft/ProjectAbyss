# Android

|  |


The **Android** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Android is the final Humanoid class, with balanced stats and Fortes. While they may start weak, they become devastating when their unique traits are properly used.

## Personality Types
- The Mechanic
- The Murdercator
- The Little Girl

## Trivia
- With its unlocking method, stats, aptitudes and native Evility, the Android has taken the place of the Majin class.
- The Android's "Murdercator" personality type makes the Android say various Terminator quotes when talked to at the Cam-Pain HQ. The Android will also say "Hasta la vista, baby" occasionally when attacking, leveling up, and so on.
- Contrary to their names, the player Androids are actually cyborgs, as their creation intro shows a Valkyrie getting kidnapped and altered into an Android.
