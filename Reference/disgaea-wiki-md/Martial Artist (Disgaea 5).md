# Martial Artist (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Martial Artist*

The **Martial Artist** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Martial Artist is a Fist-user with good physical power and evasion but low defenses. He's a short-ranged brawler who specializes in dealing powerful counter attacks.

## Personality types
- Fists Do the Talking
- Martial Arts Master
- Innocent Girl's Heart
