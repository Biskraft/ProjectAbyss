# Combos

thumb|left|A combo of two hits in *Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness*.

thumb|Adell explaining Combos and Chains in the sequel.
  - Combos**, or **Combo Attacks**, are large strings of attacks on the same enemy.

When the player chooses to Execute the assigned commands, if multiple attacks in a row target the same single enemy it'll initiate a Combo, increasing damage dealt by 10% for each attack that has been executed during it.

Prior to *Disgaea 5*, switching target, attacking multiple enemies or using non-damaging skills will cut the Combo short. Since *Disgaea 5*, however, the Combo count will increase based on all the attacks that happen during a same Execute, and the counter will not reset by hitting other units or using non-damaging skills(the latter will simply not modify the counter at all).

In addition to the increased damage, making Combos causes the Bonus Gauge to fill up, with longer Combos filling it by a larger amount.
