# Sea Angel

thumb|A Sea Angel as it appears in *Disgaea D2*.
The **Sea Angel** is a recurring monster class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.

## Appearance


## Characteristics
Originating from microscopic sea animals, the first Sea Angels were generated due to polluted waters. Their bodies are semi-transparent. They are shy and cowardly, and usually travel in large groups. They usually stay underwater except to feed. To eat, they release buccal tentacles from their heads, and either grab their food and slowly pull it back into the water or absorb the nutrients from the food itself.

## Trivia
- The names used for the Sea Angel's ranks are based on the Muses from Greek mythology.
  - Clio, the muse of history.
  - Euterpe, the muse of flutes and music.
  - Thalia, the muse of comedy and pastoral poetry.
  - Urania, the muse of astronomy.
  - Polyhymnia, the muse of sacred poetry.
  - Erato, the muse of love poetry and lyric poetry.
