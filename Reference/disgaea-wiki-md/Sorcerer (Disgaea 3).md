# Sorcerer (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Sorcerer*
The **Sorcerer** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*, appearing for the first time in the series. She is a young girl with a skull on her head. Sorcerers are supporting spell casters able to reduce enemy abilities and inflict status ailments. They are proficient with spear and staff weapons.


## Skills
Every tier of the Sorcerer is capable of learning Impaler Drop, Pole Charge, Piercing Spears, Enfeeble, Armor Break, Mind Break, Resist Break, Slow, Blind, Poison, Slumber, Stun, Blank Out, and Charm. Tier 2 and onward can also learn Divine Storm.

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Detention*
The Vita port granted Sorcerers with two new unique abilities: Aptitude Down, which drastically lowers targets aptitudes in the targeted area lasting for three turns, and Curse Storm, which targets all foes for a high chance to inflict them with all negative status ailments.
