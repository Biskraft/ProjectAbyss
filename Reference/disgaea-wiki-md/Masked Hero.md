# Masked Hero

| thumb|Artwork of the Masked Hero for *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. |
The **Masked Hero** is a recurring class in the series first introduced in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.

## Appearance


## Characteristics
Masked Heroes are self proclaimed heroes who admire Prinny Mask and patrol the streets in self made costumes. With their motto of "help the weak, beat the strong", they look for people to protect from danger, only to flee once they realize their opponent's strength. They are unpopular due to other demons thinking they are strange, though their outfits make them popular with Prinnies. They are the faster than anyone else at running away.

## Gameplay
Rather that fighting head-on, the Masked Hero is a coward who prefers to avoid battles. Notable for their high SPD and movement range, as well as being one of the few Human classes with natural Flying type movement. 

Their main weapons are Fists and Guns, though their terrible offensive and defensive stats means they don't want to participate in battle unless necessary.

Their Evilities center around increasing their evasion, and giving them combat boosts only against enemies that are already weaker than them anyway.

## Appearances
### *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
*Main article: Masked Hero (Disgaea 3)*
The Masked Hero is unlocked by having a level 45 Gunner/Gunslinger and a Level 45 Thief.

### *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
*Main article: Masked Hero (Disgaea 4)*
The Masked Hero is unlocked by having a level 25 Thief and a level 25 Warrior.

### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Masked Hero (Disgaea D2)*
The Masked Hero is unlocked by having a level 25 Ninja and either a level 25 Martial Artist or Fight Mistress.
