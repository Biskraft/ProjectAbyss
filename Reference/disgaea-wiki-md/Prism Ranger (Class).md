# Prism Ranger (Class)

|  |


The **Prism Ranger** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten.* The class consists of the various members of the reoccurring group the Prism Rangers.

## Overview
The Prism Ranger class is rather unusual as, unlike most classes, each tier is not a more advanced version of the last. Instead, each tier acts as a different ranger in the group and has different stats, aptitudes, weapon fortes and personality:

- Blue specializes in SPD and is a Gun user.
- Yellow specializes in DEF and is a Spear user.
- Green specializes in INT and is a magic user.
- Purple specializes in RES and is an Axe user.
- Orange specializes in HIT and is a Bow user.
- Aqua has balanced stats and is a Fist user.

Prism Red of the exclusive Prism Ranger class is the team leader, who specializes in ATK and is a Sword user.

What the tiers have in common is their unique skills and Evilities which, true to their character, center around teamwork and gaining friends.
