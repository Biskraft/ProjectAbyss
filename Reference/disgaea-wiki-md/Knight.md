# Knight

|  |

*Main article: Magic Knight*

The **Knight** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Knight is a mixed attacker, proficient at the use of Swords as well as Spells(offensive and support). Their overall stats are below average to compensate for the dual specialty.

Passive skill: Upon using a normal attack, there's a chance to follow up with a random elemental spell.
## Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.

Knights will learn elemental spells matching their highest natural Elemental Affinity, as well as all buff spells.
