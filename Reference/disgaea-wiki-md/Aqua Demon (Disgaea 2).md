# Aqua Demon (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Aqua Demon*

The **Aqua Demon** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Aqua Demon is an offensive class specialized in multi-target magic attacks. Their defenses are not bad for an magic attacker, but their evasion is terrible.

## Skills

### Passive

- Nulls Ice damage received. Absorb 1% of that damage per Lover.
