# Winged Warrior (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Winged Warrior*
The **Winged Warrior** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Low-ranking demons capable of flight. These hard workers are often hired as postmen, and their willingness to go to the most dangerous Netherworlds is valued by the populace. Attacking them on duty is a serious crime. Cowards by nature, they will scatter dust and flee at rapid speeds when threatened.

### Unlock Requirements
The Winged Warrior is unlocked by completing the *No Normal Bug!* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after clearing Episode 4, The Meaning of Justice, and requires the defeat of five Archer to complete.

### Personality Types
- Airborne
- Selfish
- Pervpillion☆
