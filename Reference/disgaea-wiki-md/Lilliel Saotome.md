  - Lilliel Saotome** is an Angel of Celestia who assists God in altering the stark realities of the people who pray to him in *The Guided Fate Paradox*.

## Profile
### Appearance
Lilliel has long black hair and blue eyes. She wears a maid outfit with a pink ribbon by her neck, white angelic hairclips on her hair, white stockings trimmed with blue garters, and blue shoes. Her outfit is backless and reveals a large white ribbon behind it.

### Personality

## Story
### *The Guided Fate Paradox*
Lilliel first meets Renya at the mall. She issues him a chance to participate in a lottery where he ultimately wins and becomes God.

Throughout Renya's ventures in the Fate Revolution Circuit, it revealed that Lilliel's family was annihilated in the war between the angels and the demons. She was the only angel from her home to escape the travesties although she later bore deep scars from the event. She relies on her manual as a form of reassurance, relying it on precisely so as to instill within herself hope for the future. Following certain events within the Fate Revolution Circuit, she leaves Renya for a time, greatly bewildered by the fact that demons come from angels.

## Other appearances
### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
In *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*, Lilliel appears as a DLC character.
