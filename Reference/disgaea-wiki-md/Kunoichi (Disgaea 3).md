# Kunoichi (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Kunoichi*
The **Kunoichi** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. Retaining her design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Kunoichi is a female demon dressed in a skimpy ninja costume with a mask, pants that show off her thighs and a top that doesn't cover much of her ample chest area. She also has her hair tied up in a ponytail. Like her male counterpart, she is incredibly fast. She also fights best when using Fist weapons. She can also use Status Condition inducing skills (such as Poison or Charm) to cripple her enemies. Her native Evility, **Flame Haze**, gives her a 50% chance of dodging ranged and area of effect Special Skills (unfortunately, this includes healing and stat-boosting spells from her own allies as well).

## Skills
The spells available to Kunoichi are Slumber and Charm, both of which can be learned at any tier.
