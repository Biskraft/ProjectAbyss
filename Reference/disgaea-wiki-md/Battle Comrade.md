## Trivia
- Battle Comrade plays on the map Divine Prison the first time in Disgaea: Hour of Darkness, if you set the audio to 'Japanese' before entering the map.
- It also plays when Kurtis is summoned via Cell Phone in Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories.
- The song is also heard in Disgaea 3 when Prinny Kurtis is faced after downloading him via DLC.
- The Disgaea Custom Soundtrack refers to this track as *My Comrade*, which is a slightly more accurate translation of the original Japanese title, *Senyuu Tomoyo*. This version of the song also plays during Kurtis' appearance in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten**.*
