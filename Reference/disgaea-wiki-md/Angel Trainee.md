# Angel Trainee

The **Angel Trainee** is Dark Flonne's enemy-exclusive class in the Disgaea: Hour of Darkness remakes' Etna Mode.

The Angel Trainee is almost completely separate from both the Angel and S. Detective titles that Flonne has taken previously. Not only have her specials changed, but she also gained 3 counters, AND she is completely immune to Status Ailments and Stat changes (such as lowering defense).

However, because of this, she is also worth more Experience and Hell when she is defeated.
