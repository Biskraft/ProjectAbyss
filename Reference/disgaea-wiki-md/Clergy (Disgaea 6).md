# Clergy (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Male Healer*
The **Clergy** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Contrary to other demons, they hate to fight. Many hold no prejudices against humans or angels. Since demons consider healing a grave sin, they wear barbed attire as self-punishment. They love to learn, and will often visit the Nether Central Lie-brary. One of their biggest pet peeves is being mistaken for girls.

### Personality Types
- Naughty
- Lazy
- Optimistic
