# The King and The Hero

- The King and The Hero** is the 2nd episode of Act 1 of *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*, and the 2nd episode overall.

## Summary

## Story Impact
- Misedor and Hero Yarmada are introduced.
- Zed and Cerberus continue their tale for Ivar and the Darkest Assembly.
  - Zed's party arrives at the Human World and meets Misedor.
  - Misedor convinces Zed to allow him to join his party.
  - Hero Yarmada attacks the party to stop Misedor from leaving but is defeated.
  - Zed is killed by the God of Destruction.

## Game Impact
- Misedor learns Evility, Almighty Wallet.
