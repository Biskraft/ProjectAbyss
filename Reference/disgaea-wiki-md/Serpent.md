# Serpent

thumb|A Serpent as seen in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.
The **Serpent** is a recurring monster class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Appearance
Serpents are bulky shark-looking dragons. Their head have coral reef looking horns. A large shark fin is on their back.

In their default coloration, they are blue with white underbellies. Their horns and claws are pink.

## Characteristics
Despite their name, Serpents have do not resemble snakes or serpents, physically or otherwise. Instead, they are a subspecies of aquatic dragon that have a major resemblance to sharks, both in appearance, and their behavior, mimicking an intelligent shark. They single mindedly hunt prey using the water as cover.
