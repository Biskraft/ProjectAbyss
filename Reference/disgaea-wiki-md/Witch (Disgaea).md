# Witch (Disgaea)

- Witch** is Marjoly's exclusive class in Disgaea: Hour of Darkness.

She comes fully equipped with unique Rank 40 items: the Nyanko Soul, Gao's Guts, Crowdia's Beauty, and Myao's Cat Ears.
