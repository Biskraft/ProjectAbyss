# List of Disgaea Spells

This is a list of Disgaea spells.

## Range and AoE
Spells start with a range of 2(in any direction) and 1 area of effect, but these can be increased by 2 methods:

- Each Spell will gain +1 range when it reaches levels 3, 5, 7 and 10, and a new area of effect every level until 9.

- While you have a Staff equipped, all your Spells will gain bonuses depending on Staff Mastery level: +1 range at levels 2, 6, 10 and 15, and a new area of effect at levels 3, 7, 11 and 16.

Even with both methods in play, the range increase has a limit of +5 and there's only 9 areas of effect.
