# Bow

The **Bow** is a weapon type in the *Disgaea* games. Their damage depends on the average of ATK and HIT, and they have very long attack range(slightly lower than Guns, but are not restricted to straight lines). While in the first two games their skills can inflict all 5 status ailments, from the third game onwards their skills are simply long range attacks with various areas of effect.
## *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
 

Since ailments Spells don't exist yet, Bow skills are the only way for Humanoid classes to inflict status ailments. Only the Archer class specializes in Bows.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Bows, please see List of Disgaea 1 Weapons#Bow. 

## *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*


This game gives Bows a 30% chance to turn an enemy into a Treasure Chest if the attack kills it. The Chest is usually at the attacker's level, its contents affected by the map's Bonus Rank. Only the Archer class specializes in Bows.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Bows, please see List of Disgaea 2 Weapons#Bow. 

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*


From this game onwards, Bows don't specialize in inflicting ailments. The Ranger and Archer classes specialize in Bows.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Bows, please see List of Disgaea 3 Weapons#Bow.

## *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*


Only the Archer class specializes in Bows.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Bows, please see List of Disgaea 4 Weapons#Bow.

## *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*


Christo specializes in Bows, as well as the Archer class.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Bows, please see List of Disgaea 5 Weapons#Bow.
