  - Main Corridor 2** is the third stage in Episode 13: War of the Netherworld, Part 2 in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*. This stage is only available in this chapter.

## Story
Gordon receives a message from General Carter. After some bickering, the General explains the invasion is for the sake of Earth. The planet is facing a crisis, and taking over the Netherworld is an economical solution to many of their problems. Laharl praises Carter for his ambition, saying he would make a great demon. But Earth's problems are of their one creation and he has but one message: "Clean up your own damn mess!!"

## Strategy
Same strategy as the previous map. Take advantage of the low resistance of the EDF Soldiers. Area spells will reduce their numbers quickly.
