# Clergy (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Male Healer*
The **Clergy** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. The male Healer class was redesigned from his *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* counterpart and has long white hair, is shirtless and has giant handcuffs on each wrist. He has a similar appearance to the Sinner from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*. Unlike the Clerics, Clergy are slightly better offensively and can use Spears as well as Staves. His native Evility, **Spirit's Blessing**, decreases the damage that adjacent allies receive from elemental attacks.

## Skills
Every tier of the Male Healer begins with Heal (unless reincarnated from another class) and is capable of learning Impaler Drop, Pole Charge, Piercing Spears, Mega Heal, Espoir, Brave Heart, Magic Boost, Target Lock, Armor Break, Resist Break, and Slow. Tier 2 and onward can also learn Giga Heal, and Tier 3 and onward can also learn Omega Heal.
