# Deathsaber (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Deathsaber*

The **Deathsaber** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days*.

## Overview
The Deathsaber is a short-ranged physical attacker with great speed. They favor good positioning and chasing faraway targets.

## Skills

### Passive

- Increase damage by 10%(+1% per 10 Lovers) per panel moved this turn.
