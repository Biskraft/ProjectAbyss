  - Foreboding Seal** is the first map of the Beauty Castle bonus stage in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Strategy
The Chimeras have a good amount of HP, but their defense is fairly low. This makes them excellent candidates to eliminate first. Focus your attention on one Manticore at the time. The faster you take out the manticores, the better your chances.

The Flamberg is a lot sturdier. He has even more HP, a better defense and a higher attack to boot. The Great Wyrm monster class has access to high damage dealing area of effect attacks. So avoid grouping your characters together.
