# Bouncer

|  |


The **Bouncer** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Bouncer is short-range brawler specialized in using Fists. They are protectors who specialize in reducing damage to their allies.

- Protective
- Manly
- Innocent
