# Samurai (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Lady Samurai*
The **Samurai** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Samurai is a powerful offensive class, with good physical stats but poor magical ones. She's an advanced version of the Warrior classes, with improved offensive stats and excellent proficiency in Swords, Spears and Axes.


## Quotes
> As sworn guardians, the samurai have protected Netherworld holy lands for centuries. Since those holy lands were lost in a great cataclysm, many samurai have become professional swordsmen. Though no longer guardians, their purity and pride remain strong in their hearts. Their beliefs are called "Bushi Road."|Disgaea DS Collection book
