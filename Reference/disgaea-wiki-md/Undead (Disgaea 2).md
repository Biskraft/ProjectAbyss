# Undead (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Undead*

The **Undead** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*. 

## Overview
The Undead is a short-ranged physical attacker, with average attack and defenses. Their good HP and self-regeneration can keep them in battle for a long time.

## Skills

### Passive

- Fully heal HP and SP when killing a unit with a normal attack.
- Lovers increase stats by 1.25% instead of the usual 1%.
