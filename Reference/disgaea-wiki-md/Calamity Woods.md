# Calamity Woods

- Calamity Woods** is the first map of Episode 6: Laharl's Challenge in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Story
Laharl marvels at how well his letter brought the Overlord contenders to the yard. There are so many demons waiting to fight him that there's not even any room to walk.

## Strategy
This map has a large amount of adversaries. It isn't really that hard to win; with a few wide range spells and attacks, all the player may have to worry about is their levels.
