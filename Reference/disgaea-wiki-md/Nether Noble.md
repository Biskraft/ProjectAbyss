# Nether Noble

thumb|Artwork of the Nether Noble.
The **Nether Noble** (also known as the **Minotaur**) is a recurring monster class in the series first introduced in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Appearance
They are bulky, incredibly muscular demons with porcine and bovine features. They wear royalty clothing and a crown. They wield an axe as a weapon, however during gameplay they still equip monster weapons like other monsters.

## Characteristics
Generally, they order their servants to fight for them. However, when they fight on their own volition, they can deal lots of damage. In Disgaea 5, they use their wealth in battle to boost their strength and stamina.

## Gameplay
Some of their class names refer to them as boars or minotaurs.

## Appearances
### *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
*Main article: Nether Noble (Disgaea)*
The Nether Noble is unlocked by defeating one in battle. Hoggmeiser's Rich Demon class is a minor alteration of the first Nether Noble tier.

### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Minotaur*
The Nether Noble returns in *Disgaea D2*. Like his father before him, Porkmeiser's Hoggmeiser's Son class is a minor alteration of the first Nether Noble tier.

### *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
*Main article: Minotaur (Disgaea 5)*
In Disgaea 5, they use their wealth in battle to boost their strength and stamina.
