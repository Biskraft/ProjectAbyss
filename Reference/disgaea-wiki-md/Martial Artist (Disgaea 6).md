# Martial Artist (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Martial Artist*

The **Martial Artist** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Lone fighters who push themselves to their limits. They can stand on par against swords and guns with fists alone by using their ki. Their unique, traditional attire honors the original master of this martial art, a legendary street fighter from the Human World. To them, power is both justice and truth.

### Personality Types
- Fists Do the Talking
- Martial Arts Master
- Chinese Food Fan
