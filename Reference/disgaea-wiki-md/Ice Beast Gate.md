# Ice Beast Gate

**Ice Beast Gate** is the fifth map in Episode 4: Chronicles of Etnarnia (Cont.), the final episode of Etna Mode, a bonus feature found in the remakes Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness and Disgaea DS.

This stage can only be accessed in Etna Mode.

## Etna Mode Story
Etna has a few flashbacks about her pasts, which is strange because she hasn't gotten her memories back from Maderas. She feels like she's forgetting something important...

## Etna Mode Strategy
There are no Geo Effects to worry about. The enemies are in small groups, spread out over the map. Using area of effect skills or magic will help reduce the number of enemies more quickly. All the enemies can inflict status ailments, so keep a healer or someone else with the Espoir skill at the ready to deal with those as quickly as possible.
