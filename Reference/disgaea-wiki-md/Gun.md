# Gun

The **Guns** is a weapon type in the *Disgaea* series. They have the longest attack range of any weapon, but can only target units on a straight line. Their damage scales with the average of HIT and SPD(only HIT in the first two games), and their attacks and skills reduce the target's SPD.

## *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
 

Unlike the rest of the franchise, in this game Guns have unrestricted range and depend only on the HIT stat. To compensate for this, they have a 25% reduction to all damage done. Their skills have good power but only hit single target. Gordon is proficient with Guns, as well as the Thief, Strider and EDF Soldier classes.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Guns, please see List of Disgaea 1 Weapons#Gun. 

## *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*
 

This game introduces their straight line-only range restriction, as well as the SPD debuff on their attacks. Rozalin specializes in Guns, as well as the Thief and Gunner class. 

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Guns, please see List of Disgaea 2 Weapons#Gun. 

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
 

From this game onwards, Gun damage depends on the average of the user's HIT and SPD. While this makes it harder to increase their damage, it also means Gun users now gain extra SPD. The Thief, Gunner and Gunslinger classes specialize in Guns. 

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Guns, please see List of Disgaea 3 Weapons#Gun. 

## *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
 

Vulcanus specializes in Guns, as well as the Thief, Gunner and Professor classes.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Guns, please see List of Disgaea 4 Weapons#Gun.

## *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
 

Seraphina specializes in Guns, as well as the Thief, Gunner, Professor and Pirate classes.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Guns, please see List of Disgaea 5 Weapons#Gun.
