# Nekomata Trick

In *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the **Nekomata Trick** is a trick to get high level units quickly. The name of the trick refers to the type of monster gained by using the trick.

## Explanation
In the third stage of the fourth episode, there are a group of Nekomata which are standing on Geo Panels with the effect of "Enemy Level Up 10%". Since it is possible to capture a monster enemy roughly twice the level of the highest level ally in the Base Panel, it is possible to put a cycle in until one gets a level 9999 Nekomata.

In *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days* and ports derived from this version such as *Disgaea 2 PC*, a limit has been added that prevents players from capturing characters who are higher level than Adell, or Axel in Axel Mode, preventing the exploit from working.
