# Winged Warrior (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Winged Warrior*
The **Winged Warrior** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Winged Warrior is a support class specialized in weakening enemies through debuffs and ailments. They have terrible defense but high evasion and movement, favoring the use of hit-and-run tactics.

## Skills
### Passive
- Increase movement range by 1(+1 per 10 Lovers) when HP is below 25%.
