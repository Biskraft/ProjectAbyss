# Great Wyrm (Disgaea 2)

|  |


*Main article: Great Wyrm*

  - Great Wyrm**(also known as **Entei**)is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days.*

## Overview
The Great Wyrm is a powerful offensive class specialized in Fire damage. Because of their passive effect, large groups of them are extremely dangerous.

## Skills

### Passive

- Increase stats by 10%(+1% per 5 Lovers) for each Great Wyrm dispatched(including yourself).
