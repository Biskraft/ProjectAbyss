    - Makai Wars*** (魔界ウォーズ, *Makai Wōzu*) is a mobile game starring various characters from the *Disgaea* series and characters from *Yuru Dorashiru*, otherwise known as *Ragna Quest* globally. The game tells the story of Asagi, a young woman who wants her own game and is a recurring character from the *Disgaea* series.

## Plot
Asagi has a dream about being a capital-H Hero who fights the Final Boss/Desco and encounters her evil twin.

She wakes up, decides that obviously that would never happen to a Normal High School Girl™ like her.

Then she is immediately summoned by Freya from *Ragna Quest* who tells Asagi that she's secretly a Goddess who must save the multiverse by gathering heroes to fight for Justice. And also because Freya's too lazy to do it herself.

## Characters
thumb|right|170px|Main Characters of *Makai Wars*.
*Main article: List of Makai Wars characters*
Although primarily a crossover between *Disgaea* and *Ragna Quest*, *Makai Wars* has featured several collaborations with other games, primarily other Nippon Ichi titles.

### Collab Characters (Nippon Ichi)
- Zetta (both normal and Book forms), Petta, Salome, Pram, Alexander, Valvoga, Trenia, and King Drake the Third from *Makai Kingdom*.
- Marona and Ash (both together and as seperate units) from *Phantom Brave*.
- Metallia, Hundred Knight, Malia, Visco, and Nezaria from *The Witch and The Hundred Knight*.
- Priere (both Demon and Human forms) from *La Pucelle: Tactics*.
- Cornet from the *Marl Kingdom* series.
- Danette, Gig, and Feinne from *Soul Nomad and the World Eaters*.
- Kogure from the *Hayarigami* series.
- Yui and Haru (as a single unit) from the *Yomawari* series.
- Princess Wolf and Prince (as a single unit) from *The Liar Princess and the Blind Prince*.
- Dronya from *Labyrinth of Refrain: Coven of Dusk*.

### Collab Characters (Non-Nippon Ichi)
- Terry Bogard and Mai Shiranui from the *Fatal Fury* series (SNK).
