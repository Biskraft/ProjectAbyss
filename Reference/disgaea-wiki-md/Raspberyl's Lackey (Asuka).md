# Raspberyl's Lackey (Asuka)

- Raspberyl's Lackey**, (also known as **Alumni**) is Asuka's class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.  She complements Raspberyl with her extraordinary Samurai skills and has unique special skills.

## Descripton of Asuka
: "*A gentle Samurai who adores Raspberyl.  Her hobby is folding paper cranes." - *Unit Help in Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.*
