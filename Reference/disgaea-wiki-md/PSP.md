# PSP

Several notable games related to the Disgaea universe have been produced for the **PSP** (**PlayStation Portable**) system. So far, besides Prinny and Disgaea Infinite, they are all remakes of previous PS2 games. They include:
- Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness, the remake of Disgaea: Hour of Darkness with additional features.
- Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days, a remake of Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories with additional features and downloadable content.
- La Pucelle Ragnarok, a remake of La Pucelle: Tactics
- Prinny: Can I Really Be the Hero?, an original game
- Disgaea Infinite, a Disgaea visual novel spin-off.
- Phantom Brave the Hermuda Triangle, a remake of Phantom Brave with additional features and characters.
