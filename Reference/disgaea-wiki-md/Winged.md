# Winged

|  |


The **Winged** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 5 onwards.

In *Disgaea 1 Complete* they are replaced by the Imp class, though the change is purely aesthetic and they function the exact same way.

## Quotes
> Winged have the greatest mobility in the Netherworld, and are also used as couriers.|The World of Disgaea

> Little is known about the Winged, other than their natural habitat. They travel in groups, but once battle begins, they'll attack anyone. Because of this, they are believed to have little bond between their kind. Thanks to their wings, they have some of the best movement in the Netherworld.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
