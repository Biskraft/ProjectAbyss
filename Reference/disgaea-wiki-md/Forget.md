# Forget

- Forget** (also called **Amnesia**) is one of the Status Ailments that can be inflicted upon the characters.

## Description
It's represented by large red question marks above the character's head. Units affected by it will be unable to use special skills of any kind.

## Specialists
Two Specialists deal with the Forget ailment:

- Having a **Psychologist** on any equipment increases a character's resistance to the Forget status by 1% per level.
- Having an **Amnesiac** on any equipment gives a character's normal attacks a chance to inflict Forget by 1% per level.
