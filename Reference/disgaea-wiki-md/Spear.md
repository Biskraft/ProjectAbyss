# Spear

The **Spear** is a weapon type in the *Disgaea* series. While they don't increase ATK as much as other weapons they present a good amount of defensive utility, with a normal attack range of 2, increased DEF, and skills that hit all around the user. Most of their skills also move the user, facilitating getting into and out of combat.

## *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*


Unlike the rest of the franchise, Spears don't increase the user's DEF. Etna specializes in Spears, as well as the Female Warrior class. 

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Spears, please see List of Disgaea 1 Weapons#Spear.

## *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*


From this game onwards, Spears increase the user's DEF. In this game they also have a block mechanic, reducing damage taken from the front by 30%. Etna specializes in Spears, as well as the Valkyrie and Heavy Knight classes. 

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Spears, please see List of Disgaea 2 Weapons#Spear.

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*


Only Mao, the Valkyrie, and the Heavy Knight classes specialize in Spears.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Spears, please see List of Disgaea 3 Weapons#Spear.

## *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*


Only the Valkyrie and Heavy Knight classes specialize in Spears.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Spears, please see List of Disgaea 4 Weapons#Spears.

## *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*


Their skills have been reworked: They no longer move the user, and now consist of big AoE attacks in front of them. They also gain a feature where normal attacks to a distant enemy will also damage other enemies in between. The Valkyrie and Armor Knight classes specialize in Spears.

To see the charts of weapon skills and weapon stats for Spears, please see List of Disgaea 5 Weapons#Spear.
