# List of Disgaea 2 Weapons

## Information
Weapon skills unlocked by attaining high enough Mastery in the corresponding weapon. Seventh skill only available in *Dark Hero Days.*

### Staff
Damage is based on INT(though normal attacks are still ATK-based). Staff Mastery does not grant weapon skills, instead improving the effect of spells.


### Monster (Physical)
Increases ATK and HIT. Has no skills or additional effects.


### Monster (Magical)
Increases ATK and INT. Has no skills or additional effects.
