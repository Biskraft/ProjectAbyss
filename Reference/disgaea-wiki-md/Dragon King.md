# Dragon King

|  |

*Main article: Great Wyrm*
The **Dragon King** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Dragon King is a powerful offensive class with an affinity for Fire-based attacks. They specialize in elemental damage, and can increase their own elemental power as well as lower the enemy's.
