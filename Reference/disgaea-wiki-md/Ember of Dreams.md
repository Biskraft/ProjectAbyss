# Ember of Dreams

- Ember of Dreams** is the fifth and final map of Episode 7: Of Being an Overlord in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.
## Story
Laharl is getting annoyed: "Zombies, zombies, zombies...!! I'm getting sick and tired of this..." he says. But Aramis warns him to stay alert. Because there is unique zombie here. Aramis calls him "his masterpiece". It is a zombie composed of different special parts. One of those parts even scares Laharl and the prince immediately orders everyone to keep their guard up.

After the battle, Aramis thanks Laharl and call's him "Your Highness", officially recognizing him as the new overlord. Laharl passed the test for the time being.

## Additional Strategies
If you happen to have a high-enough leveled rogue in your team, stealing the Mahogany's Brain, Hercule's Body and... Uh... The... You know. (**cough**Horse Wiener**cough**) is a MUST. You can also re-start this stage until you get rare or legendary versions of these items.
