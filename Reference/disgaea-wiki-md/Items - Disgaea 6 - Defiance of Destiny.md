# Items - Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny

There are a variety of items in Disgaea 6 which provide a range of bonuses for the player. All items can be levelled-up in the item world, increasing their effect or stat bonuses and innocents can be stored within them to further enhancing their effect. Each item has a different inherent rank which correlates to both the strength of the item as well as the difficulty of the associated item world.

## Weapons
There are 7 different types of weapons in Disgaea 6, Fists, Swords, Spears, Bows, Guns, Axes and Staves.
