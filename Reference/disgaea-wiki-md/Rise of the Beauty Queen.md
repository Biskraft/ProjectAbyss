# Rise of the Beauty Queen

- Rise of the Beauty Queen** is the third episode of *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Story
Adell, Rozalin and Tink go to the swamp to find Zenon, in yet another fake path created by Rozalin. In the end, Etna shows up trying to reach Zenon and claim the title "God of All Overlords". After an attempt to defeat Adell and Rozalin, she departs.

## Maps
- Poisonous Swamp
- Dangerous Refuge
- Corpse Garden
- Tragedyland (Requires at least one Felony)
