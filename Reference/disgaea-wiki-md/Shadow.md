# Shadow

|  |

The **Shadow** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

They specialize in magical attacks over big areas.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 5 onwards.

In *Disgaea 1 Complete* they are replaced by the Reaper class, though the change is purely aesthetic and they function the exact same way.
