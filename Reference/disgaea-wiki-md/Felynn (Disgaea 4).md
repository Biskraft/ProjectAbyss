# Felynn (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Felynn*

The **Felynn** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Felynn is an aggresive fighter with high attack and evasion but low defenses. She specializes in powerful counter attacks.
