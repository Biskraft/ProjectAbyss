# Beastmaster (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Beastmaster*
The **Beastmaster** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Beastmaster is a support class specialized in boosting monster units. They not only increase the power of monsters, but also speed up their growth.

## Skills
### Passive
- Increase stats of adjacent ally monster units by 20%(+1% per Lover).
- You gain double Bonus Gauge points.
