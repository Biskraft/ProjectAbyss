# Mystic Beast (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Mystic Beast*

The **Mystic Beast** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Mystic Beast is a very mobile class specialized in Wind damage. Their low defenses are compensated by their excellent evasion.

## Skills

### Passive

- Nulls Wind damage received. Absorbs 1% of that damage per Lover.
