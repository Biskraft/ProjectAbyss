# Undead (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Undead*
The **Undead** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Corpses revived by the miasma of the Netherworlds. They are not technically a race, despite being categorized as one. Zombie traits vary based on their living selves. Their rotten brains give them low smarts, but strong stenches. Most are weak, but some, known as Kings, are as strong as Demon Lords.

### Unlock Requirements
The Undead is unlocked by completing the *Hungry, Hungry Zombie* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after clearing Episode 3, Happily Never After, and requires the submission of one Charred Newt.
