# Heavy Knight (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Heavy Knight*

The **Heavy Knight** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Heavy Knight is a tank class, with excellent defensive stats all around. Their attack is not bad, but their poor mobility makes it hard for them to get into and out of combat.

They also have the highest Throwing power of all Humanoids. Making a few to toss other characters about is an option.


## Skills
### Passive

- Increase DEF by 50%(+1% per Lover) while HP ≤ 25%.
