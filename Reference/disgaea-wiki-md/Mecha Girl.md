# Mecha Girl

*(This article is a stub)*

|  |

  - Mecha Girl** is a Humanoid class introduced in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Robots built by anti-war engineers and researchers to end a conflict between two Netherworlds of science and magic. With an unlimited magical energy source and advanced scientific AI, they will take the ideal measure in any scenario. It is said that the response signals of some vanished after the war's end.

### Unlock Requirements
The Mecha Girl is unlocked by completing the *Robot Army* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after the completion of the *On the Hunt* and *Worthy of Guarding* quests that unlock Archer and Armor Knight, respectively, and requires Rank 2 or higher class proficiency for both Gunner and Armor Knight.

### Personality Types
- Emotionless
- Mecha
- Pilot
