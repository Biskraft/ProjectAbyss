# Majin (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Majin*
The **Majin** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The ultimate Humanoid class, possessing high stats and Aptitudes all around and excellent proficiency in all weapons.


## Limitations
While unlocking Majins effectively makes most other classes obsolete, they do lack a few abilities that other classes possess:

- They don't use the improved steal formula of Thieves.
- They lack the unique skills of Striders.
- They can't evade skills like Ninjas.
- They don't learn any spell naturally(though that can be fixed by Reincarnations and Pupils).
