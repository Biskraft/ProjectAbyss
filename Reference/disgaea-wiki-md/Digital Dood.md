# Digital Dood

- Digital Dood** is an "edition" of Disgaea PC and Disgaea 2 PC when they are each packaged with their Digital Art Books.

Buying all 4 products (both games and both art books) is collectively called the **Digital Doods Edition**.

The name is derived from the "Dood" slogan of Prinnies.
