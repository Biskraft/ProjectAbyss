# Vulcan Blaze

- Vulcan Blaze** is Adell's ultimate special move in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*, *Disgaea DS*, *Disgaea PSP*, *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days* and in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

This is a fire elemental attack based attack. Most people would go for max damage by using Kneel Before Me (Rozalin's 2nd attack gained), or Braveheart just so they can deal massive damage.

In *Disgaea 3* and *Disgaea 4*, this skill has a Power Level of S, making have very high damage potential especially on a target weak to fire. In *Disgaea 3*, unlike other skills, Vulcan Blaze boosted 9 times will have its damage multiplied by 660% instead of 640%. In *Disgaea D2*, Vulcan Blaze has a Power Level of SS, the highest skill level in the entire game and is also Fire based.

### Description
Adell obtains Vulcan Blaze at level 50. The special starts with Adell attacking furiously at the enemy (6 hits). Next, he smashes the enemy unit and kicks the enemy in the air successively (4 hits). Then, he punishes the enemy unit with a fierce uppercut, actually worth 2 hits. Finally, he finishes it by holding out his arm, then closing it, causing a massive explosion.

In *Disgaea 4*, this move's animation has noticeably changed. Adell uses a combo of punches to launch the enemy into the air. Then he smashes the enemy to the ground and and lands more punches before constantly stomping it with his feet. Finally, he uppercuts the enemy into the air, holds out his arm and closes it, creating a large explosion.

In *Disgaea D2*, Vulcan Blaze receives a new animation. First, Adell charges up with fire energy and lands a series of quick blows on his enemys. He launches his enemy to the sky and fires a series of blue energy blasts in mid-air. Adell lands 3 kicks on his enemy and flies high into the sky, before striking his enemy with a powerful kick. Adell lands on the ground and holds out his hand, before closing it and creating a large explosion at where the enemy is.
