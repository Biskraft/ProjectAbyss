# Dragon Zombie (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Dragon Zombie*

The **Dragon Zombie** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Dragon Zombie is a frontliner with high attack and excellent physical defense, but low mobility and terrible magical defense.
