# Wood Golem (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Wood Golem*

The **Wood Golem** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Wood Golem is a slow but powerful tank class. Their high HP and passive regeneration make them incredibly resilient.
## Skills

### Passive
