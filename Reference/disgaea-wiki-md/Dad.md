  - Dad** is a character in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* who is the father of Taro and Hanako, and the husband of Mom. "Dad" is in fact his real name.

Dad is a Zombie-type Demon, but doesn't have a rotten smell. He also has a strange face-like creature attached to his chest. The creature not only wishes he would be more aggressive but is very perverted, often spouting suggestive comments. It may be a reflection of his subconscious.
