## Unlocking Classes
### Unlocking Humanoid Classes
  - Unlocked by Default**

- Warrior
- Valkyrie
- Skull
- Mage
- Healer
- Thief

  - By Leveling**

Humanoid classes are unlocked by passing the corresponding bill after clearing a condition(usually involves leveling up other classes).

    - Level 15**
      - Beastmaster** (Warrior and Valkyrie)
      - Sorcerer** (Skull and Mage)
      - Archer** (Valkyrie and Healer)
      - Gunner** (Skull and Thief)

    - Level 20**
      - Armor Knight** (Warrior)
      - Professor** (Healer and Onmyo Monk)

    - Level 25**
      - Masked Hero** (Warrior and Thief)

    - Level 30**
      - Magic Knight** (Warrior or Valkyrie and Skull or Mage)
      - Ninja** (Skull and Thief)
      - Lady Samurai** (Valkyrie and Archer)
      - Onmyo Monk** (Sorcerer and Ninja)

    - Special Criteria**
      - Bouncer**: Complete Chapter 7 or have a Level 100 Armor Knight
      - Battle Suit**: Complete Chapter 8 and have a Lv. 30 Armor Knight and a Lv. 30 Warrior
      - Android**: Beat the game and start a new cycle.

  - Minimum Levels to Unlock All Classes**

- Level 100: Armor Knight (Optional)
- Level 30: Warrior, Valkyrie, Thief, Skull, Mage (Optional), Archer, Sorcerer, Ninja, Armor Knight
- Level 20: Onmyo Monk, Healer
- Level 15: Mage

### Unlocking Monster Classes
  - Unlocked by Default**

- Prinny
- Slumber Cat
- Orc

  - Unlock via Defeating Monsters in Battle**

Monster classes are unlocked by defeating a unit of that class in combat. Class pages will list the first time they appear in a story map, but it's always possible to find them before through use of the Item World.

    - Sludge**: Episode 2, Geo Tutorial 1
    - Shroom**: Episode 2, Geo Tutorial 3
    - Spirit**: Episode 2, Stage 1 "Rotten Sickbed"
    - Undead**: Episode 2, Stage 2 "Hypha Invasion"
    - Winged Warrior**: Episode 3, Stage 1 "Murder Labyrinth"
    - Gargoyle**: Episode 3, Stage 1 "Murder Labyrinth"
    - Mystic Beast**: Episode 4, Stage 1 "Ultra Security"
    - Flora Beast**: Episode 4, Stage 3 "Total Fabrication"
    - Dragon Zombie**: Episode 4, Stage 6 "Fall of the Zenith"
    - Felynn**: Episode 5, Stage 1 "Scream Park"
    - Cockatrice**: Episode 5, Stage 2 "Lost Dignity"
    - Succubus**: Episode 5, Stage 4 "Ruin Coaster"
    - Rifle Demon**: Episode 5, Stage 5 "Frantic Wheel"
    - Dragon**: Episode 6, Stage 3 "Ghoul Standard" (during replay)
    - Death**: Episode 7, Stage 1 "Rebel Invasion"
    - Wood Golem**: Episode 8, Stage 3 "Section D"
