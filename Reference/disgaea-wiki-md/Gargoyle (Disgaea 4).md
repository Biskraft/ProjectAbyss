# Gargoyle (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Gargoyle*

The **Gargoyle** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Gargoyle is a defensive class, with Evilities that further increase their defenses in exchange for minimizing their movement.

## Personality types

- Silent
- Hot-blooded
