  - Thurvean Sector** is the second stage in Episode 9: Captain Gordon, Defender of Earth in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.
## Story
While Jennifer and Thursday are looking for Gordon, Jennifer's immagination runs wild thinking of all the horrible creatures and painful deaths they could meet here in the Netherworld. She is most surprised when she sees Laharl and his companions, mistaking them for human children kidnapped by the ruthless overlord. While she fusses over them, Laharl is once again confronted with on of his weaknesses: a sexy body. Flonne interrupts, introcing the party. They decide to go look for Gordon together. Afterall, it is an overlord's duty to challenge Heroes.
