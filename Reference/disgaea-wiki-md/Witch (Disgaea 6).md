# Witch (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Mage*
The **Witch** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Female demons naturally gifted with arcane talent. The Magic Organization, founded in Magitopia, provides girls who become witches at a young age with a special ribbon that adjusts to its wearer's magic and relays them info from the Organization directly. Never having to study makes them quite optimistic.

### Personality Types
- Airhead
- Naive
- Mean

## Notable Witches
- Majolene - the Witch of the Abyss and the principal of the Magimage School of Magic.
- Lemisera - one of Majolene's students at the Magimage School of Magic.
- Releiza - the Legendary Witch of myth and creator of many magical items, including the Magical Wand of Transformation.
- PolPol - a DLC character based on Hololive Vtuber, Omaru Polka.
