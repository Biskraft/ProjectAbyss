# Speed

- Speed**, abbreviated as **SPD**, is a stat that increases evasion against attacks. It also increases damage dealt with Fists (from *Disgaea 2* onwards) and Guns (from *Disgaea 3* onwards). It's generally increased by equipping Fists (since *D2*), Guns (since *D3*) or Shoes, as well as any item with a Coach Specialist.


Category: Statistics
