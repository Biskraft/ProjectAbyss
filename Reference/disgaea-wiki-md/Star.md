# Star

- Star** (星 *hoshi*, *Star*) is one of the four basic elements in the series. In earlier *Disgaea* games it is not part of the Elemental Affinities. Star is in effect neutral or non-elemental, causing no extra damage to any character due to a weakness of it. Conversely, the damage can't be lowered due to increased resistance. In *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*, Star is treated much like the basic Fire, Ice, and Wind elements, as it now has resistance stats just like the others.

No Specialist will increase resistance to a star based attack, however, *Disgaea 3* onwards added an Evility that will.
