  - Sayaka Miki**, commonly known as **Sayaka**, is the one of the main characters of the anime series *Puella Magi Madoka Magica*. She appears in *Disgaea RPG* as an obtainable character through the *Puella Magi Madoka Magica* alongside Madoka Kaname and Mami Tomoe. Sayaka is a magical girl who helps her friends fight witches.
