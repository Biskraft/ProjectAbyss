# Evil Eye

|  |

The **Evil Eye** is a Monster class introduced in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Bovine monsters known for their downcast demeanors. Many are cowardly, and will run or hide upon encountering others. Despite this, their innate strength is tremendous, crushing foes with ease. Due to a lack of combat records, the true capabilities of their namesake Evil Eyes are shrouded in mystery.

### Unlock Requirements
The Evil Eye is unlocked by completing the *Evil Eye Elite* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after clearing Episode 7, For Whom the Belle Sings, and requires the submission of one legendary glasses to complete.

### Personality Types
- Meek
- Righteous Warrior
- Rampaging Cow

## Trivia
- The names used for the Evil Eye's ranks are based on figures from different mythologies or religions.
  - Catoblepas is a bull-like creature from Ethiopia, the name of which comes from the Greek word καταβλέπω (katablépō) which means "to look downwards".
  - Stonka is a bull from Bulgarian mythology with skin as tough as bronze and causes thunder when it stomps.
  - Humbaba is a monstrous giant from Ancient Mesopotamian religion sometimes depicted with horns of a wild bull.
  - Gugalanna is a mythical bull in Ancient Mesopotamian religion fought by the hero Gilgamesh.
  - Hathor is an Egyptian mother goddess often depicted as a cow.
  - Audumbla is a primeval cow in Norse mythology that nursed the Jötunn Ymir and freed the ancestor of the gods, Búri, from salty rime stones.
