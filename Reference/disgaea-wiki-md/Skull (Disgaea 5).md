# Skull (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Skull*

The **Skull** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance.*

## Overview
The Skull is a standard caster class, with good magical stats but poor physical ones. They use elemental spells to hit the enemy's weakness from a distance. The Skull's magic is powerful, but burns through SP quickly.

Note that when creating one you'll have to choose an element, determining what spells will be learned. This will also give +50% to that resistance, but -50% to another(Fire reduces Ice, Ice reduces Wind, Wind reduces Fire).

## Personality types

- Studious
- Brave
- Tactician


## Skills
### Spells
Skulls will learn the spells matching their chosen element, plus the spells in the Common tab.

Spell range and AoE depends on skill level and boosts.
