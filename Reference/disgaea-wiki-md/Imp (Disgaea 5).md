# Imp (Disgaea 5)

|  |

The **Imp** is a Monster class introduced in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Imp is an offensive class with unimpressive stats, prefering to fight from a distance or prey on the weak. They're natural pranksters who will just become stronger if the enemy tries to debilitate them.

## Personality types
- Villainous
- Mischievous
- Butler
