# Ninja (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Ninja*
The **Ninja** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Members of a group feared as assassins of darkness. They excel at the covert art of Ninpo and wear black garb that is both durable and light. Their cold resolve in their duties makes them good bodyguards, as well as feared by those in power. Ninjas follow a strict code, and their masters' words are absolute.

### Unlock Requirements
The Ninja is unlocked by completing the *Ninja Nite* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after the completion of the *Steeling to Steal* quest, which unlocks the Thief, and requires Rank 1 or higher class proficiency for Thief and Rank 2 or higher class proficiency for Martial Artist to complete.

### Personality Types
- Dry
- Showy
- Accented
