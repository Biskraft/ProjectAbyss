# The Invasion From Within

- The Invasion From Within** is a song by metal band "Tsunami Bomb". It was originally used during the North American release of *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* in-game and during a promotional commercial. One notable spot in-game where it was used was during the battle with Baal. However, when NIS America was releasing the PSP remake, *Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness*, they had to omit the song from the game due to rights to the song still being held by Atlus and having too many legal problems to get the rights from them. Another song called "One With The Stars" also had a similar issue due to it being added in by Atlus during the American release of the original game.
