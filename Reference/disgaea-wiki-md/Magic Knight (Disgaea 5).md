# Magic Knight (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Magic Knight*

The **Magic Knight** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Magic Knight is a mixed-attacker, who uses both Swords and Magic in order to strike the enemy where it hurts the most. To compensate for this double specialty, her stats are overall low except for the offensive ones.

## Personality types

- Calm
- Sisterly
- Mysterious


## Skills
### Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on skill level and boosts.

### Unique
Unique skills are learned by increasing Sword Mastery level, rather than the character's level.
