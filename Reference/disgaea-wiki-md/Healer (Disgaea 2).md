# Healer (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Female Healer*
The **Healer** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Healer, as suggested by their name, is a support class specialized in healing. They learn all of the healing and buff spells in order to keep the party healthy and strong.

## Skills
### Passive
- Decrease elemental damage received by 50%(+1% per Lover).

### Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.
