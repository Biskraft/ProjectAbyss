  - Theatre of Death** is the first stage in Episode 8: Reincarnation in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Map Strategy
The Enemy Boost panel in addition to Defense -50 can make this level a real challenge, so exploiting elemental weaknesses would be best. Unless Laharl is incredibly strong, it wouldn't be wise to have him take on more than one enemy at a time.
