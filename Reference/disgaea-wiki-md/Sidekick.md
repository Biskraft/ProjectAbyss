# Sidekick

- Sidekick** is Jennifer's exclusive class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

The player will lose the sidekick after completing Stage 12-4, but regain her at the beginning of Stage 13-5.

Team Attack Chance is always 100% between Gordon, Jennifer, and Thursday.
