# Super Robo Suit

The **Super Robo Suit** is a high-ranking Armor Item found in the *Disgaea* series.

It first appeared in La Pucelle: Tactics worn by Baal in the Dark Shrine. It greatly increases the character's stats (except SP and INT) and increases movement by 3. The player was intended to acquire the suit by defeating Baal, but if the inventory is full and Prier has the Demon Lord title (thus allowing her to Purify Baal), he will join the party equipped with the suit. This trick is repeatable, but may be removed in revised/rereleased versions.

In Hour of Darkness, Baal is equipped with the Super Robo Suit and the Nemesis monster weapon. It again increases all the stats significantly, increases movement by 3, and also increases jump by 30. By the postgame, it is not uncommon to have multiple Super Robo Suits equipped on all party members. There is a misconception, however, that it can be attained at the 100th floor of either a legendary Nirvana or Astro Suit.

The Super Robo Suit in *Hour of Darkness* and its remakes has 3000 DEF and 1500 in all other stats including its movement boosts and jumping power boosts. After stealing one from Baal, the player can obtain multiple copies by stealing it from the Item God 2. The Super Robo Suit has a special trophy icon like the Yoshitsuna and Hyperdrive.

## Other Appearances
### Disgaea 2
The Super Robo Suit returns in *Disgaea 2* as the rank 40 armor. It is obtained by going to the 100th floor of a legendary Infernal Armor in the Land of Carnage. It no longer has the movement and jump boosts, but it provides a 6300 boost in DEF, 1500 in all other stats and HP is increased by 3000 as its base stats. Like in *Hour of Darkness*, it has a unique trophy icon.

### Disgaea 3
In Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice, the Super Robo Suit is the rank 39 armor, making room for the Antimatter Field to be the Rank 40 armor. The Antimatter Field can be obtained from the 100th floor of a Legendary Super Robo Suit in the Land of Carnage.

### Makai Kingdom
In Makai Kingdom, the Super Robo Suit is a usable vehicle, second in power to the Yoshitsuna.
