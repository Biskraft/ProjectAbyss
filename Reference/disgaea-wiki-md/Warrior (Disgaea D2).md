# Warrior (Disgaea D2)

|  |

*Main article: Warrior*
The **Warrior** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness.*

The Warrior is one of the best physical attacking classes early on in the game. High ATK, DEF, and HP make him able to receive and deal damage efficiently. Like in many JRPGS, the Warrior has a practically non-existent casting ability, making spells a poor choice for him. The Warrior's biggest weakness is his lack of SP. Little SP prevents him from using Special Skills too often early on, forcing him to rely on regular attacks. Like his female counterpart, he has a weak RES growth, making him vulnerable to magic-users.

The Warrior's Evilities are all centered around interaction with the enemy. These range from a 15% increase in stats per enemy within a two-panel range to being able to survive with 1 HP from any attack if the its inflicted damage goes beyond his max HP.
