# Celestial Host

thumb|Artwork of the Celestial Host for *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.
The **Celestial Host** (also known as the **Female Angel** and **Celestial Hostess**) is a recurring class in the *Disgaea* series and is the only class used exclusively by the female angels of Celestia.

## Appearance


## Characteristics
Celestial Hosts are masters of both offensive and defensive combat and are comprised of angels with great skill in battle. They heal wounded allies. They believe in the absolute righteousness of the angels and will eliminate all those who oppose them. Their rank and wingspan are proportional to their spiritual power.

## Gameplay


## Appearances
### *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
*Main article: Celestial Host (Disgaea)*
Celestial Hosts were introduced in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and were unlocked when a Female Healer, a Knight, and an Archer all reached level 100. This class serves as an improved version of the Knight, also employing Healing spells, making this class good with any weapon or skill type, except for Guns.

### *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days*
*Main article: Celestial Host (Disgaea 2)*
While absent from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Celestial Hosts return in the PSP port *Dark Hero Days*. They are able to be created after Main Hero B, a palette swap of a Celestial Host, joins Axel's party.

### *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
*Main article: Celestial Host (Disgaea 3)*
The Celestial Host class only appears as an award granted to the top 100 players in one of three difficulties in the Command Action DLC.

### *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
*Main article: Celestial Host (Disgaea 4)*
The Celestial Host appears in this game as a DLC class unlocked by purchasing Main Hero B.

### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Female Angel*
The Celestial Host appears as a playable class in the game, unlocked either by clearing Chapter 8 or having both an Onmyo Monk and a Healer leveled to 100.

### *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
*Main article: Celestial Hostess*
The Celestial Host returns in *Disgaea 5*.
