# Psychic

*(This article is a stub)*

|  |

The **Psychic** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Young boys with psychic powers. Those with potential are gathered and trained at a young age. Many start to believe they are special, developing into huge edgelords (as you can see at the Base). Each Psychic receives a rank according to their capabilities, which helps to determine where they are stationed.

### Unlock Requirements
The Psychic is unlocked by completing the *Psychic Squadron* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after the completion of the *Steeling to Steal* quest, which unlocks the Thief, and requires Rank 2 or higher class proficiency for both Clergy and Witch to complete.

### Personality Types
- Pompus Brat
- Isekaied Officer
- Edgelord
