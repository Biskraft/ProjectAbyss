# Warrior (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Warrior*
The **Warrior** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Demons who choose to make a living by fighting on battlefields throughtout the Netherworlds. They are accepted by the God of War, Grandall, and are highly skilled in combat. Warriors wear light gear to enhance mobility and can harden their bodies. Though extremely hostile, they do respect those they deem worthy.

### Personality types
- Cool
- Serious
- Muscly
