The **Healer** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.

## Class Overview
The Healer is one of the initial classes made available from the start. 

The sole defining factor that separates the Healer from the Priest are their differing stat make-ups. Both genders have high SP and RES, but that's where the similarities end. The Healer is more physically-aligned than her male counterpart. Possessing a higher ATK, HIT, and HP when compared to the Priest, the Healer is almost tailor-made for basic physical weaponry (particularly with the bow), barring Fortes. 

As a price for her physical prowess however, she possesses a lower INT and DEF than the Priest, as well as possessing an even lower SPD. This makes her a less-than-stellar magic-user compared to most of the other classes. However, the Healer's balanced stat make-up is easier to play with as opposed to most of the classes available from the start of the game, making her the most customizable starting class.

The Healer's Evilities are all focused towards amplifying her already powerful healing ability. The Evilities range from increasing her healing spell power per adjacent ally to modifying a Book's normal attack to use RES for damage instead of ATK/INT.
