# Moocho

thumb
thumb
thumb
  - Moocho** is a Petite Orc encountered in the town of Holt at the start of *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

After the first time he claims to have looked up a girl's skirt (the "best kind of evil") Rozalin tells him to stay away from her, though she does not repeat this on repeat encounters with him.

He aspires to be a Dark Hero.
