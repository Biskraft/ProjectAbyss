# Fight Mistress (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Fight Mistress*
> Keep going!|Fight Mistress, *Disgaea 3* 

The **Fight Mistress** is a class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. She seems to appear as a Chinese martial artist as she says "Ni Hao" upon arrival, wearing a battle robe with white flame designs on the cuffs of the sleeves, hakama and shoes. Her hair is pink, similar to her counterpart from *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness,* but her pigtails are slightly different than the original's; having a bit of a ball or bun shape at the base of the pigtail. Her native Evility, **Feint**, can lower an enemy's amount of Counter Attacks.
