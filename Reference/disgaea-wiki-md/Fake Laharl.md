# Fake Laharl

- Fake Laharl** is a Prinny that has been given a pair of antenna by Etna. It appears only in Etna Mode.

## Information
Fake Laharl is the only character who follows Etna to her destinations, but never actually joins the battle party. He is most likely a one-shot character, since his only spoken lines are in the first episode of Etna Mode.
