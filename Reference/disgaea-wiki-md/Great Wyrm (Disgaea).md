# Great Wyrm (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Great Wyrm*

The **Great Wyrm** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

They specialize in dealing damage over large areas.

This class is immune to ailments.

## Quotes
> These are the strongest of all dragons. There are few of them left in the Netherworld, so little is known about these monsters, other than they have impervious skin and massive claws that can rip anything to shreds. Challenging them is literally suicide.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
