# Flora Beast (Disgaea 2)

---
|  |


*Main article: Flora Beast*

The **Flora Beast** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Flora Beast is a versatile class with physical damage, magical damage and healing. With their excellent magic resistance but poor physical defense, it's best for them to fight from the sidelines.

## Skills

### Passive

- Reduce magical damage received by 50%(+1% per Lover).
