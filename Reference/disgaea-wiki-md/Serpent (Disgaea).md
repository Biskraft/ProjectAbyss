# Serpent (Disgaea)

|  |

The **Serpent** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and its ports.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 4 onwards.

## Quotes
> A rare, shark-type dragon. One theory says that they are a foreign species that came from another Netherworld. They are extremely aggressive and seek their prey throughout the Netherworld.
