# Heavy Knight (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Heavy Knight*

The **Heavy Knight** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten.*

## Overview
The Heavy Knight is a standard defensive class, with high defenses and damage-decreasing effects but low mobility. His long lift/throw range is useful for protecting allies or repositioning them.

## Personality types

- Honest
- Extravagant
- Overwhelming
