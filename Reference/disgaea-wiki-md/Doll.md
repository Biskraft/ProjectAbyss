| 0}}**Doll** is the exclusive class of Prism Red in *Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness*. It is considered a monster class. Prism Red can be obtained by earning 100 battle points in the multiplayer arena, which requires winning 50 multiplayer battles. This class is notable for being absolutely terrible. It has pathetically low base stats, and horrendously low aptitudes of 20% all across the board, as well as only naturally learning a single skill, Swift Justice. This makes it easily the weakest class in the entire game, and perhaps even the entire series. Later ports of the first *Disgaea* would remove multiplayer battles, leaving Prism Red, and thus this class, unobtainable. |

In *Disgaea 1 Complete* Prism Red was given a new unlock condition, as well as the Prism Ranger class which has much better stats and aptitudes, but still only learns one skill naturally.
