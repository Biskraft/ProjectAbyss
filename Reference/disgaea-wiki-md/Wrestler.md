# Wrestler

|  |


  - Wrestler** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Wrestler is an offensive support class with below average stats except for great attack and health. Their specialty lies not in fighting but in lifting and throwing units, helping the team by improving the movement of your units or disrupting enemy positioning.

- Contender
- Heel
- Masseur
