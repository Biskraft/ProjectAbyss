# Nether Noble (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Nether Noble*

The **Nether Noble** is a Monster class in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

They specialize in multi-target physical attacks.

This class is immune to ailments from Tier 4 onwards.

## Quotes
> A prestigious class of demons. They don't fight their own battles. Instead, they hire servants to fight for them. But if necessary, they have been known to finish off adversaries by themselves. For all of their accumulated wealth, they are also known to be ridiculously stingy.|Disgaea DS Collection Book
