# Second Battle

- Second Battle** is, as its name implies, the second map in the Coliseum.

It is littered with Geo Panels of various effects. One of the Geo Symbols on the map is one with an Invinciblity effect which will protect the Geomancer at the center of the map.

To defeat the Geo Panel puzzle, destroy the Geo Symbol in between the Geomancer and the lot of four Geo Symbols. This will result in a chain reaction that will destroy all Geo Symbols and Geo Panels on the map.

It is possible to break the Geo Panel Puzzle by breaking one of the Geo Symbols other than the mentioned Geo Symbol which will set off a chain reaction that will result in all Geo Panels turning yellow and destroying Symbols other than the one which gives invincibility. The end result is that everyone is no longer able to take damage on the field.

However, it is still possible to beat the map in this way. It is still possible to attempt to capture an enemy on the stage which will eventually result in the Base Panel being destroyed, freeing a space for damaging enemies up as this spot does not have a Geo Panel. 

When revisiting the stage, all of the Geo Symbols except for the Ally Damage 20% and No Range symbols will be gone.
