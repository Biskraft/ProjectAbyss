# Ninja (Disgaea D2)

|  |

*Main article: Ninja*
The **Ninja** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness.*

The Ninja is the master of evasion.  High SPD and Evilities built around evasion make the Ninja hard to hit, which is good news because this class suffers in DEF.  A decent level of ATK, INT, and SP make this class versatile between special attacks and spells.

## Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness
Born from the darkness of the Netherworld, these warriors make very adept spies. Skilled in the art of Ninpo, they are renowned assassins. They were highly feared by those in power, but during King Krichevskoy's reign, these activities slowly dwindled. Now they are known for their loyalty to their masters, and are used as bodyguards.

The Ninja is unlocked once a Thief and a Martial Artist or Fight Mistress reach level 20 or higher.
