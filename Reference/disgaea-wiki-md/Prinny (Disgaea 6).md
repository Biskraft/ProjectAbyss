*Main article: Prinny*
The **Prinny** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
These plushie-looking penguins actually house the souls of Human criminals. Depending on the crimes they committed, they have to pay off their sins with hard labor and money in order to be reincarnated on the night of the Red Moon. Handle with care to prevent explosions.

### Unlock Conditions
The Prinny is unlocked by clearing Stage 1-3, Scorched Plains.

### Personality Types
- The Usual
- Confident
- Overworked
