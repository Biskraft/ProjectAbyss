# Dragon (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Dragon*
The **Dragon** is a Giant Monster class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Beings who have driven the prosperity of the Netherworlds since ages past. Famed as some of the strongest demons around, their might is even feared in Celestia and the Human World, and some are widely revered as divine legends. Their regal presence evokes admiration, hope, and envy from lesser demons.

### Unlock Requirements
The Dragon is unlocked by completing the *Treasure & Dragons* quest. The quest becomes available after clearing Episode 8, To See Justice Through!, and requires submission of one Emblem to complete.

### Personality Types
- Gallant
- Ruler
- Too Friendly
