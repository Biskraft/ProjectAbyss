# Shark King

- Shark King** is a warrior of the Dragon Gods who wanders around the Netherworlds in search of blood. Once he smells that trickle of blood, he will rip apart anyone. This behaviour gave him the title of the Shark King, or Brutalord. However he is troubled by the fact he has no friends.
