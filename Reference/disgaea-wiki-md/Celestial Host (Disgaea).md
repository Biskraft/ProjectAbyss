# Celestial Host (Disgaea)

|  |

*Main article: Celestial Host*

The **Celestial Host**, otherwise known as **Angel**, is one of the final Humanoid classes of *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Celestial Host is one of the strongest classes in the game, having overall high stats and Aptitudes all around. They are a versatile unit, being adept at physical damage, magical damage and healing.


## Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.

Celestial Hosts will learn elemental spells matching their highest natural Elemental Affinity, as well as the healing spells.
