# Prinny Land

- Prinny Land** is a special area in which prinnies do not explode when thrown.

It was first seen in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and its ports. It's notable for being the only place where the higher tiers of the Prinny class can be found. Prinny Land can be unlocked by passing the bill "Prinny Land" in the Dark Assembly. The bill only appears after completing Episode 8, and a character with a rank of 5 or higher is needed to propose the bill. It is only available during the main story, and cannot be entered if you are playing Etna Mode.

In *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*, Prinny Land makes a return, with a total of 6 maps to clear including a final boss. Unlike the previous game, Prinnies will explode when thrown. Instead, there are several Geo Effects which may hinder completion of the stages.
