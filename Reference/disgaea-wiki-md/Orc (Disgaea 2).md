# Orc (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Orc*

The **Orc** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Orc is a frontliner who charges straight at the enemy, crippling them with their blows. They have good physical attack and defense in exchange for low magical defense and evasion.
