# D-Merit

The **D-Merit** system is a rewards system introduced in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*. Each unit in the player's army has a separate D-Merit compilation. Characters can complete various missions which will grant rewards to the character, such as Karma, increased Weapon Mastery increasing rate, increased Karma limits, and more.
