# Innocent Town

The **Innocent Town** is a location in the Item World found in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* and onwards. Here you may heal your party, pass a single bill to improve the item you are traveling through, or return to HQ. The Innocent Town can be entered by clearing 10 floors in any Item World but it will not always show up, however by using mr. Gency's exit at floor x9 and saving the player can make sure it appears by loading save and trying again. Any Specialists the player subdued before hand will appear in the town. Occasionally, NPC travelers will appear as well, triggering various events and conversations. It will also contain multiple treasure chests for each visit.

The chance to enter Innocent Town can be increased in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice *by having characters be part of the Wednesday Team Club. The Medic heals your party, the Gatekeeper allows you to leave or stay in the Item World and the Item Assembly NPC allows you to pass bills to increase an Item's stats.

There is always a Gatekeeper and a Healer but the Item Assembly NPC will not always show up. NPC's also include the subdued innocents, some storyline characters such as Axel or the Diez Gentlemen. Events in Innocent Town may yield items, gained by talking to an NPC a certain amount of times or it may even unlock new bills. In *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten, *Prinny Kurtis cannot be unlocked until the player talks to him 5 times in Innocent Town.
