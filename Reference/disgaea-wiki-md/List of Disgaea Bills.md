## Promotion Exams
The list of Bills available to each character depends on their Demon Rank, which can be increased by taking Promotion Exams. Each exam simply consists of a battle using only the character taking the exam(no Base Panel is spawned) against various enemies, increasing their rank by 1 is they win.

A higher Demon Rank not only makes new Bills available, but also causes Senators with higher level and higher influence to appear. Do note that Transmigrating resets the unit's rank back to 0, so their exams will need to be passed again.

All exams take place in the Assembly meeting room, and the type of enemies battled is different for each one:

- Exam 1: Lvl 2 Zombie x3, Lvl 4 Ghoul
- Exam 2: Lvl 5 Ghoul x2, Lvl 8 Corpse
- Exam 3: Lvl 9 Hobbit x3, Lvl 12 Gremlin
- Exam 4: Lvl 20 Guardian x2
- Exam 5: Lvl 25 Nightmare x2, Lvl 30 Dark Knight
- Exam 6: Lvl 34 Wraith x3, Lvl 40 Ekim
- Exam 7: Lvl 42 Red Skull x2, Lvl 45 Male Centurion, Lvl 50 Majin
- Exam 8: Lvl 60 Decoy, Lvl 70 Serpent
- Exam 9: Lvl 80 Dragon x2, Lvl 100 Ahzi Dahaka
- Exam 10: Lvl 120 Lilim x3, Lvl 150 Neuntoter
- Exam 11: Lvl 200 Wight, Lvl 250 Shaitan
