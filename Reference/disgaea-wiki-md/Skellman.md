# Skellman

> Hehee, late, late, late for your fate, fate, fate! Trip and fall and miss your meeting. Beg and brawl and catch a beating. Prinny bombs for my Prinny friend. Prinny dood meets a Prinny end.|Skellman
  - Skellman** is a very strange character appearing in *Prinny: Can I Really Be the Hero?*. It resembles a comical-looking skull with a red nose, straw witch's hat and a stick for a body, holding a sign. He or she has no role in the game except for showing the player where to go, and only speaks during the opening.
