# Succubus (Disgaea 6)

|  |

*Main article: Succubus*
The **Succubus** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Succubi are a clan of females who feed by seducing men. Since they appear as their target's ideal woman, none have ever seen their true forms. They only go for those they've taken a liking to, though they're known to give up on ones who already have partners. Other female demons hate them, save for the Felynn.

### Unlock Requirements
The Succubus is unlocked by completing the *Seductive Demon* quest. The quest becomes available after clearing Episode 6, Lord of the Ka-Chings, and requires submission of one Emblem to complete.

### Personality Types
- Bewitching
- Teacher
- Old Little Sis
