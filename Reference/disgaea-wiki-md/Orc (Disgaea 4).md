# Orc (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Orc*

The **Orc** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Orc is a basic frontliner who charges straight at the enemy, crippling them with their blows. While weak on their own, they become dangerous when in a group.
