# Magic Knight (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Magic Knight*

The **Magic Knight** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten.*

## Overview
The Magic Knight is a mixed-attacker, who uses both Swords and Spells in order to strike the enemy where it hurts the most. To compensate for this double specialty her stats are low overall, except for the offensive ones.

## Personality types

- Peaceful
- Short-tempered
- Calm


## Skills
### Spells
Spell range and AoE depends on boost level.

### Unique
Range and AoE depends on boost level. Effect increased by 3% per boost.
