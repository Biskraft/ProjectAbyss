# Orc (Disgaea 5)

|  |

*Main article: Orc*
The **Orc** is a Monster class in *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

## Overview
The Orc is a brute who simply seeks to deal physical damage. While not too threatening on their own, they become fairly strong when part of a group.
