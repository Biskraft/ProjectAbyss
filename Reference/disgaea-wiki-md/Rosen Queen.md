# Rosen Queen

The **Rosen Queen** shop is a store that appears in several Nippon Ichi games.

## Origin
The Rosenqueen shop is first shown in *Rhapsody: A Musical Adventure* as the shop in Mothergreen owned by the family of Etolie Rosenqueen.

## *La Pucelle: Tactics*
By the period of *La Pucelle: Tactics*, which takes place in the same world as *Rhapsody*, The Rosenqueen store has expanded not only to the kingdom of Paprika, but also to the Dark World as well. In this game, the owner is a girl named Ellie that can actually be recruited in the *Ragnarok* version.

## *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
In *Disgaea*, the Rosenqueen shop is the normal item shop in Laharl's Castle that is split into two sections. An Archer mans the Weapons Shop while a Male Fighter mans the Armor Shop.
## *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*
In this installment, the shop is split into three sections, and its owners changed depending on your game mode. In the main story, a Male Fighter controls the Weapons Shop, a Female Fighter runs the Armor Shop, and a Female Mage runs the Item Shop.

In Dark Hero Days' Axel Mode, each of the three shops are run by Nekomatas.

## *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
Rosen Queen is again the default shop in the Evil Academy.

Provided by a Male Brawler at Weapons, a Female Brawler at Armor/Accessories, and a Female Gunner at General Items.

## *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
In this game, a Male Fighter runs the weapons shop, a Female Fighter runs the items shop, and a Star Skull runs the general store. There is also an Evil Symbol that you can place called the Rosen Queen that gives you a specialty shop where you can buy items based on the place that you put the shop on the Cam-Pain HQ.

## *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*
In *Disgaea 6*, the Rosen Queen shop is run by a single Warrior who can provide Weapons, Armors and several Accessories. The shop is located in the base, however it is also possible to access a special Rosen Queen Shop in resting areas in the Item World.
