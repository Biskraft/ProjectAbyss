# Orc (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Orc*
The **Orc** is a monster class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. They are quick-tempered creatures that live for battle.

Retaining its design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Orc is a small, Boar-like creature. They are extremely hotheaded and love to charge straight into battle. They also hate to be called small. Although Orcs love to fight, their true nature is that they fight best when they have others on their side (as shown with its native Evility Wild Gathering, which increases the Orc's ATK for each adjacent Ally).

The Vato Brothers are purple recolors of the normal Orcs.

## Trivia
- The Orc's Magichange skill "GoldAxe SilverAxe" is a parody of "The Honest Woodman" fable.

- The Flying V Strike skill is a reference to the Mecha Anime *Chodenji Voltes V.*
