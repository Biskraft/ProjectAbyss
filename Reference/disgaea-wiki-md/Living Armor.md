# Living Armor

thumb|Artwork of the Living Armor for *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.
The **Living Armor** (also known as the **Dark Knight**) is a recurring monster class in the *Disgaea* series.

## Appearance
The Living Armor is a being made out of three seperate pieces of armor, the top part of a breastplate, green gloves, and what appears to be a dress on the lower part. No humanoid body has been seen. Flames appear where the hole for the head is.

## Characteristics
Living Armors have a strong sense of chivalry and unparalleled skill with the sword, being able to slay even a Dragon with a single slash. The greatest of the Living Armors can ride Dragon Zombies and become Horsemen.

## Appearances
### *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*
*Main article: Dark Knight (Disgaea)*

### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Living Armor (Disgaea D2)*
Absent for most of series after *Disgaea*, the Living Armor returns as a monster class. It is recruited after the player defeats one in battle.
