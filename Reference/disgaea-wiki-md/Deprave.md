# Deprave

- Deprave** is one of the Status Ailments that can be inflicted upon the characters.

## Description
It's represented by a dark swirl above the character's head. Units affected by it will have their stats reduced by 20%(10% in the first *Disgaea* and its rereleases) and won't earn EXP/Mana.

Despite being caused by the spell called Charm, it's not related to the Charm ailment introduced in *Disgaea 5*.
## Specialists
Two Specialists deal with the Deprave ailment:

- Having a **Social Worker** on any equipment increases a character's resistance to the Deprave status by 1% per level. (Note: Due to a possible reference error in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, the Medicine Man increases deprave resistance instead in that game)

- Having a **Gangster** on any equipment gives a character's normal attacks a chance to inflict Deprave by 1% per level.
