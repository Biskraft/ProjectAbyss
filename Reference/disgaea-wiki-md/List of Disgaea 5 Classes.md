## Unlocking Classes
The classes available by default are Warrior, Valkyrie, Martial Artist, Fight Mistress, Magician, Witch, Clergy, Cleric, Maid, and Prinny. In a change from previous Disgaea games, unlocking classes is now entirely dependent upon the game's new Quest system. All classes, both humanoids and monsters, require you to complete a quest in order to unlock them.

### **Unlocking Humanoid Classes**
The quests to unlock Humanoid classes require you to reach certain ranks of Class Proficiency with other classes, with each quest(except for one) becoming available as soon as you have its relevant classes.

Here are the classes, followed by their quest's name and requirements:

    - Thief**: "Stealing Specialist" - Rank 2 Martial Artist or Fight Mistress
    - Gunner**: "Seeking Partner" - Rank 2 Warrior or Valkyrie, Rank 2 Magician or Witch
    - Archer**: "Hunter Intern" - Rank 2 Warrior or Valkyrie, Rank 2 Clergy or Cleric
    - Professor**: "Samples Wanted!" - Rank 2 Gunner, Rank 2 Maid
    - Ninja**: "Assassinations" - Rank 2 Thief, Rank 3 Martial Artist or Fight Mistress
    - Lady Samurai**: "Valuing Loyalty" - Rank 2 Archer, Rank 3 Warrior or Valkyrie
    - Armor Knight**: "Impregnable Guard" - Rank 3 Warrior or Valkyrie, Rank 3 Martial Artist or Fight Mistress
    - Magic Knight**: "Magical Warrior" - Rank 3 Warrior or Valkyrie, Rank 3 Magician or Witch
    - Wrestler**: "Star of the Ring!" - Rank 2 Armor Knight, Rank 3 Warrior or Valkyrie
    - Dark Knight**: "Black Knight" - Rank 2 Ninja, Rank 2 Lady Samurai, Rank 2 Wrestler
    - Pirate**: "Space Pirates" - Rank 2 Gunner, Rank 3 Archer, Rank 3 Maid
    - Sage**: "Clandestine Child" - Rank 2 Professor, Rank 4 Magic Knight
    - Celestial Host**: "Celestial Messenger" - Episode 11. Rank 2 Magic Knight, Rank 3 Clergy or Cleric
    - Fake Asagi**: "Asagi's Future" - Recruit the real Asagi. Raise Asagi's level to 500

Total minimum requirements to unlock all humanoid classes (except for Fake Asagi):

- Rank 2 Thief
- Rank 2 Gunner
- Rank 2 Ninja
- Rank 2 Lady Samurai
- Rank 2 Armor Knight
- Rank 2 Professor
- Rank 2 Wrestler
- Rank 3 Warrior or Valkyrie
- Rank 3 Martial Artist or Fight Mistress
- Rank 3 Magician or Witch
- Rank 3 Clergy or Cleric
- Rank 3 Archer
- Rank 3 Maid
- Rank 4 Magic Knight
- Complete Episode 11

### **Unlocking Monster Classes**
The quests to unlock Monster classes have all sorts of requirements(mostly item delivery), with each quest becoming available the Episode after  the one where you first fight that class.

Here are the classes, followed by their quest's name and requirements:

    - Orc**: "Orc Pride" - Episode 2. Deliver 1 item of any type
    - Imp**: "Pranking Footsteps" - Episode 2. Deliver 1 item of any type
    - Undead**: "Let's Dance" - Episode 3. Deliver 1 Physical Monster Weapon
    - Sludge**: "Secret to Jiggling" - Episode 3. Deliver 1 Unopened Soda
    - Winged Warrior**: "No Ordinary Bug!" - Episode 4. Defeat 1 Archer
    - Flora Beast**: "Opening Up" - Episode 4. Deliver 4 healing items
    - Slumber Cat**: "Cute Animal, Meow" - Episode 4. Deliver 1 Sardine
    - Nether Noble**: "Rules of the Nobles" - Episode 4. Deliver 1 Angel Cake or Gold Bar
    - Shroom**: "Eri Eri Poko Poko" - Episode 5. Destroy 3 'Mushroom' objects
    - Fairy**: "Small Friend" - Episode 5. Deliver 1 Fairy Dust
    - Rabbit**: "Serious Warrior" - Episode 5. Defeat 3 Imps
    - Sea Angel**: "Living Conditions" - Episode 6. Deliver 1 percentage-based healing item
    - Chimera**: "Chimera Problems" - Episode 6. Deliver 3 Magical Monster Weapons
    - Nine-Tails**: "Colla-tail-ral" - Episode 7. Deliver 1 Emblem
    - Bear**: "Are We Friends?" - Episode 8. Deliver 3 Muscles
    - Succubus**: "Seductive Demon" - Episode 9. Deliver 5 Strange Feelers
    - Felynn**: "I Wanna Fight!" - Episode 10- Deliver 5 Fists
    - Twin Dragon**: "Treasure & Dragons" - Episode 13. Deliver 1 Orb of RARE or LEGENDARY quality
    - Horseman**: "Promised Spear" - Episode 15. Deliver 1 Spear of LEGENDARY quality
    - Dragon King**: "Hard Boiled Heart" - Episode 16. Deliver 1 weapon with a Burner Innocent
