# Stellar Graveyard

- Stellar Graveyard** is a bonus area in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and its remakes. It is unlocked by passing the Stronger Enemies Bill 20 times, then talking to the Dimension Guide. This area only contains one map, and features Zetta as the boss. Completing this map unlocks Zetta as a playable character.
