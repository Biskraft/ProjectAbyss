  - Robust Princess** (later known as **Newly Married Queen**) is Sapphire's class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. She is a jack of most trades kind of character, being good at pretty much everything, Offensive, Range, Support, except for offensive magic, since she does not learn it naturally.


## Skills

### Weapon Forte
- Bow: Up to 3rd skill (Up to 6th in Absence of Detention)
- Axe: Up to 3rd skill (Up to 6th in AoD)
- Gun: Up to 3rd skill (Up to 6th in AoD)
- Staff
- Healing Magic: Up to Giga (Omega in AoD)
