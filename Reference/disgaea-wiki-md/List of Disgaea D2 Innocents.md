# List of Disgaea D2 Innocents

A List of All *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness* Innocents in the game. Most Innocents have returned from past games. The Weapon Mastery Innocents from *Disgaea 3 *and *Disgaea 4* are not present due to the fact that actual Weapon Mastery has returned, along with the bonus stats it provides to equipment (which is 3% per level, for up to a 765% increase in weapon stats).
