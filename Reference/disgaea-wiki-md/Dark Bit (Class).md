# Dark Bit (Class)

- Dark Bit** is a special unit that accompanies Liezerota • Dark, the final boss of *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.

There's 4 of them, each with different spells.

## Description

:*"A cluster of magical energy, born from an evil heart. Without a will of its own, it constantly attacks Killia and the others."*


## Skills
### Spells
Their spells have 7 panels of range, and 2 power boosts.
