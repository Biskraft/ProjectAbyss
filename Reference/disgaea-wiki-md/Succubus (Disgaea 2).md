# Succubus (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Succubus*

The **Succubus** is a monster class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Succubus is a mixed attacker with both physical and magical power. Despite their low defenses they can stay healthy thanks to their ability to drain the enemy's HP.
