  - Misedor** is a playable character in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*. He is the king of the Human World. Being the richest person in the entire universe, he believes that anything can be solved with money. He has a special ability of reviving heroes.

## Profile
### Appearance
He has a large head and a button-like besagew that keeps his outfit together. His cape can also split in the middle and can be worn as a jacket.

## Trivia
- In the French version of the game, his name is changed to "Pingredor", a play on words between "Pingre" (Greedy person) and "Or" (Gold).
- According to the *Behind the Seams* artbook:
  - The beard-like accessory wrapped around his neck is actually a neckpiece and not part of his actual beard.
  - He has piercing eyes similar to that of a dwarf king.
