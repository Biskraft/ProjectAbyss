# Roc (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Roc*
The **Roc** is a monster class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*. They are foul beings that are said to bring ill fortune.

Retaining its design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Roc is a large bird with various chicken-like features. It also has a snake for tail. The Roc is a creature that most people like to stay away from, due to it being rather unpleasant company. The major reason for this is that the Roc can emit a poisonous gas called Miasma which causes problems for those around it (as shown with its native Evility, Miasma of Silence, which makes it so adjacent enemies cannot use their special skills or magic). Its special skills can also inflict status conditions on its enemies.
