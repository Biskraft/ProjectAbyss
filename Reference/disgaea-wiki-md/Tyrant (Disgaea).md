  - Tyrant** is one of Baal's exclusive classes in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.

He only uses this class during your first encounter, later switching to the Uber Prinny class.

Laharl gains the title of Tyrant upon defeating him, drastically increasing the chances of his Assembly Bills being approved.

He's fought in extra stage Lord of Terror.
