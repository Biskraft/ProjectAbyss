# Female Fighter (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Valkyrie*
The **Valkyrie** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Valkyrie is a standard fighter class, with good physical attack and defense but low magic resistance. She favors agility and long-range weapons.


## Skills
### Passive

- Multiply Critical rate by 1.5(+0.01 per Lover) while HP ≤ 25%.

## Trivia
- The English names for the tiers and the Japanese names do not 'match up' well, as tier names seem to get moved around upon translation. They're all there, but the last four are not in the same order.
- The Japanese name for the 6th tier, 'Vanadis', is likely derived from [an alternate name for the Norse goddess Frejya](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_names_of_Freyja), associated with (among several other qualities) war. *Disgaea 2* predates the "Lord Marksman and the Vanadis" series of light novels by most of a decade.
