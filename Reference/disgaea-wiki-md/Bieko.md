  - Bieko** is a playable character in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*. She is Zed's litle sister, who has a kind and innocent heart. She adores Zed, and calls him "Bubby". Bieko inspires to be a doctor someday and is the reason Zed never gives up.

## Profile
### Appearance
From the *Behind the Seams* artbook, Bieko is described as having big puppy dog eyes, with her cuteness being something about her that makes everyone want to protect her. 

She has two skin tones being a zombie, her face and legs are light pink and her mid section and left arm and hand are light purple. She has stitches under her accessories which show sometimes, unlike Zeds which are always noticeable; except for the one on her right shoulder which are noticeable, almost like she was cut. Her zombie powers keep her in shape. 

Her bows alternate on her midsection from big to small, sort of like a "myriapoda". Lastly the book notes that her sandals have toe loops and a big bow shaped flower on the big toe.
