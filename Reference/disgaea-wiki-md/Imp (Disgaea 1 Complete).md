# Imp (Disgaea 1 Complete)

- Imp** is a monster class featured in the *Complete* release of *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, replacing the Winged class from previous versions. Apart from visual changes, this new class is unchanged from the class it has replaced.

## Collection Book
- "Little is known about them other than their natural habitat. They travel in groups, but once battle begins, they'll attack anyone. Because of this, they are believed to have little bond between their kind. Thanks to their wings, they have some of the best movement in the Netherworld."*
