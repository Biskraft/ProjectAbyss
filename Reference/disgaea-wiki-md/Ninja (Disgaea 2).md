# Ninja (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Ninja*
  - Ninja** is a humanoid class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Ninja is an agile offensive class. Their high movement and unmatched evasion makes it hard to take them down.
