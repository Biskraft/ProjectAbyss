# Majin (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Majin*

The **Majin** is a class from *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.

Retaining his design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*, the Majin appears as an overly muscular demon with wild hair, 3 blank eyes and an aura surrounding him. The in-game description for the Majin states "A warrior with power beyond man. All Stats are high, but...", referring to the Majin having very high base stats at the cost of his low growth aptitudes and inability to learn skills naturally.

The Majin's native Evility,  One Man Army, increases his stats by 100% if all of his allies have been killed and he is the only unit.

In *Absence of Detention*, the Majin class gains 2 unique skills. The first skill is Land Demise and it is F-Rank in Power. It is unique in the fact that it is the only skill that hits every enemy on the map, essentially having infinite range. Land Demise is not affected by height, and it will not target neutral objects such as Geo Blocks and Chests. The second attack is Black Hole, which is an SS-Rank in power, has a free range of 4. This skill can be hard to perform as it requires 8 spaces surrounding the target to be free in order for the skill to be used.
