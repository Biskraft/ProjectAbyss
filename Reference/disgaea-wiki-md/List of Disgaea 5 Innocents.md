# List of Disgaea 5 Innocents

## Innocent Farm
A feature available at the Innocent Shop, enabled by the Innocent Aid Squad. Innocents can be left in care of the farm to grow automatically, which can happen in 2 ways:

- After each battle there's a chance for Innocents at the farm to increase their current level by 1%(increased by leveling up the Squad).
- If there's at least 2 Innocents at the farm and at least 1 free slot, after each battle there's a chance for a new Innocent to be born. The new Innocent will be the same type as one of the parents(or a dual-stat Innocent if both parents are Stat-type), and its level will be 10% of the parents' combined unsubdued level.
