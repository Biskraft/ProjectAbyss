- Sphere VIII** is the third stage in Episode 9: Captain Gordon, Defender of Earth in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.
## Story
Being separated from Jennifer and Thursday, Gordon starts a search and rescue mission, for they might have fallen into the evil clutches of the overlord.
