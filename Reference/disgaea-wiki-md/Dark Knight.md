# Dark Knight

- Dark Knight** can refer to any of the following:

- The class from *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*.
  - The second tier of said class.
- The second tier of the Magic Knight from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.
- The second tier of the Magic Knight from *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.
- The second tier of the Magic Knight from *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.
- The second tier of the Magic Knight from *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*.
- The class from *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*.
