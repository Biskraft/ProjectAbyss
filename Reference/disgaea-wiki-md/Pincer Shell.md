# Pincer Shell

|  |

The **Pincer Shell** is a Giant Monster class introduced in *Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny*.

## Overview
Crabs that became demons by absorbing the Netherworld's Mana. Big, strong, and hard-shelled, their combat prowess is top-notch. Some hire them as clerks or attendants due to their gentlemanly manner. They tend to crabwalk, and are surprisingly quick on their feet, which pairs well with their high defenses.

### Unlock Requirements
The Pincer Shell is unlocked by completing the *Pincer Shells United* quest from the Quest Shop. The quest becomes available after clearing Episode 9, Tragedy's Refrain, and requires the submission of one Premium Crab Brains.

### Personality Types
- Gentlemanly
- Eccentric
- Sushi Chef
