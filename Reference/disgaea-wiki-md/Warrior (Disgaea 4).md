# Warrior (Disgaea 4)

|  |

*Main article: Warrior*

The **Warrior** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.

## Overview
The Warrior is a standard frontliner class, with good HP and physical stats. He has the extra power and bulk needed to stand toe-to-toe against multiple enemies.

## Personality types

- Passionate
- Opinionated
- Weak-willed
