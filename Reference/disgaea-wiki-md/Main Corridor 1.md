  - Main Corridor 1** is the second stage in Episode 13: War of the Netherworld, Part 2 in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*. This stage is only available in this chapter.

## Story
Jennifer has a bad dream. Kurtis asks about it but she isn't very forthcoming with information. Then she notices something in his hands that terrifies her. She questions his humanity and he reveals that he lost a lot in an accident, maybe that too. He advances and Jennifer screams, loud enough for Laharl and his companions to hear her. They hurry to find her but are intercepted by super-human Androids.

## Strategy
EDF Soldiers have good defense, and a good counter rate. Their resistance, on the other hand, is rather low. This makes them excellent targets for magic users. And because some of the soldiers are also grouped together, area spells are an excellent choice.
