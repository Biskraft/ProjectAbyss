# Gunner

thumb|Artwork of the Gunner.
The **Gunner** is a recurring humanoid class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Appearance


## Characteristics
Gunners are lone mercenaries who roam the Netherworld looking for jobs to take on. They dislike working in groups, but will place their trust in someone they find to be a dependable partner. Gunners use an "evil eye" to capture their targets from a great distance and have concentration abilities that rival that of a Skull's, ensuring that their chosen target will not escape.

## Appearances
### *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*
*Main article: Gunner (Disgaea 2)*
Gunners were introduced in *Disgaea 2* and are unlocked by having a unit with a level 5 Gun Mastery, then passing the associated bill in the Dark Assembly.

### *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*
*Main article: Gunner (Disgaea 3)*
The Gunner is unlocked by leveling both a Thief and Skull to level 15, then passing the associated bill in the Dark Assembly.

### *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*
*Main article: Gunner (Disgaea 4)*
The Gunner is unlocked by leveling both a Thief and Skull to level 15, then passing the associated bill in the Senate.

### *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness*
*Main article: Gunner (Disgaea D2)*
The Gunner is unlocked by both leveling a Thief to level 15 and having a unit with a level 5 Gun Mastery, then passing the associated bill in the Dark Assembly.

### *Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance*
*Main article: Gunner (Disgaea 5)*
The Gunner is unlocked by getting both a Warrior/Valkyrie and Mage/Skull to rank 2, then completing the associated quest.

### ***Disgaea 6: Defiance of Destiny***
*Main article: Gunner (Disgaea 6)*
The Gunner is unlocked by getting both a Warrior and Clergy to rank 1, then completing the associated quest.
