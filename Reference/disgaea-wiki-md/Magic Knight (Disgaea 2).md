# Magic Knight (Disgaea 2)

|  |

*Main article: Magic Knight*
The **Magic Knight** is a human class in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Overview
The Magic Knight is a mixed attacker, proficient at the use of Swords as well as elemental Spells. They possess the unique ability to manipulate the Elemental Affinity of units.

## Skills
### Passive
- Increase elemental damage done by 25%(+1% per Lover).

### Spells
Each Magic Knight will naturally learn only one set of spells, matching their highest Elemental Affinity upon creation.
Spell range and AoE depends on Staff mastery and skill level.
