# Magic Knight (Disgaea 3)

|  |

*Main article: Magic Knight*
> I feel a evil power in this class...We may cross blades in the future, but for now, I will fight for you.|Magic Knight immediately after creation, Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice

The **Magic Knight** is a Humanoid class in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice*.

Retaining her design from *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* the Magic Knight appears as a female demon. She has a tomboyish hairstyle, earrings, and an unusual outfit that has a large bow on the back of it. She can use Swords and Staffs very well due to her balance of physical and magical combat skills. Her native Evility **Spirit Benediction** increases the damage of elemental attacks by 20%. She can also use special skills that boost her elemental resistances.


## Skills
Every tier of the Magic Knight begins with Fire, Wind, and Ice (unless reincarnated into from another class) and is capable of learning Blade Rush, Hurricane Slash, and X-Level Flurry as well as the class-specific skills Fire Charge, Wind Charge, Ice Charge, Anti- Fire, Anti- Wind, and Anti- Ice by level up. Tier 2 and onward can also learn Slayer's Descent, Mega Fire, Mega Wind, and Mega Ice.

## Trivia
- All official art of the Magic Knight has her shown in her 4th tier form (Mega Knight). This is likely due to the fact that in the original *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness*, this color scheme was used for the first tier.
