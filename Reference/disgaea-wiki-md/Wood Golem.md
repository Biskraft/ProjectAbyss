# Wood Golem

thumb|A Wood Golem in *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten*.
The **Wood Golem** is a recurring monster class in the *Disgaea* series first introduced in *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories*.

## Appearance

The wood golem is a large, muscular golem with a wood core surface and a lighter colored armor-like covering of wood.
