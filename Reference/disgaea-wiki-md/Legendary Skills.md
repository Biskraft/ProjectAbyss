# Legendary Skills

The **Legendary Skills** are a set of skills in *Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice,* *Disgaea 4: A Promise Unforgotten *and *Disgaea D2: A Brighter Darkness *. These skills are extremely hard to come by but extremely powerful. Legendary Skills are found only in Class World (Disgaea 3) or Chara World (Disgaea 4) hosted by humanoid characters. They can be bought at Legendary Skill shops, which will only appear after clearing the 10th level of Geo Blocks and proceeding through the Mysterious Gate. Only one skill can be bought at a time, but characters can buy any skill, regardless of Weapon Mastery. Each weapon style has 2 Legendary Skills, the first one costs 2400 Mana and the second one costs 4800 Mana.

In *Disgaea D2*, Legendary Skills can be bought from a Prinny in a Mystery Island. Unlike *Disgaea 3 *and *Disgaea 4*, they are bought with HL and the player can buy as many Legendary Skills as they want for one character.

## Trivia
- All of the "Legendary Skills" in Disgaea 3 are actually skills that appeared in *Disgaea: Hour of Darkness* and *Disgaea 2: Cursed Memories* that were removed from the Skill Lists of each class in *Disgaea 3*.
- Some of the skills have had their damage output increased from the original *Disgaea* game they came from, most likely due to their now "Legendary Status".
- Each of the skills can be given to other characters through the Class World using Skill Inheritance.
- In addition, all Tier 8 skills in *Disgaea 4* are actually the Tier 7 skills in *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days. *Some of them are renamed such as Ultra Overlord being renamed to Super Crosslord or Brynhilda being renamed to Brynhildr.
- When using a Tier 8 skill in *Disgaea 4*, the user will perform a weapon animation before attacking, this is a reference to *Disgaea 2: Dark Hero Days *where characters perform a weapon animation before the skill is loaded by the PSP.
- In *Disgaea D2*, all Tier 7 Skills are modified/renamed versions of weapon skills from *Disgaea 3* with the exception of Sky Nine Slash which is a renamed Soaring Nonuple from *Disgaea 4* and Cat Tale since Books were only introduced in *Disgaea D2*.
