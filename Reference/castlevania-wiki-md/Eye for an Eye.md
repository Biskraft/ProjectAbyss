# Eye for an Eye

right|100px
  - Eye for an Eye** is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It allows the player to "share damage" received with enemies.

This means that any enemy that damages Charlotte will also be damaged by the same amount. The fully-charged version makes the affected enemies to be damaged as twice the amount that she receives.
