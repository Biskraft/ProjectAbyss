# Needle Lizard

The **Needle Lizard** is an enemy in *Super Castlevania IV*.

## Description
The Needle Lizard is a porcupine-like reptile that occasionally curls itself up and starts rolling back and forth at great speed a few times, damaging anything on its path with its sharp spikes.

Its behavior is somewhat similar to the She Worm in *Castlevania: The Adventure* and *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*.
