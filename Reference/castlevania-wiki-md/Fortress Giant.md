  - Fortress Giant** is an original music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula: Lords of Shadow*.

Based on the track's title, it is presumably the background music that plays during a segment where the Ogre is fought. This implication is due to his enormous size and the fortress in question might be the one where the Crow Witch Malphas resides.
