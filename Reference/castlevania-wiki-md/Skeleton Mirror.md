# Skeleton Mirror

The **Skeleton Mirror** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

## Description
The Skeleton Mirror is a red skeleton wearing a helmet and shield that lives inside of a mirror. It waits for a victim to walk by and then breaks through the mirror and starts to chase them by performing long lunges with its sword.
