# Brian Beacock

- Brian Keith Beacock** is a Californian voice actor. He provided the voice of the Devil Familiar and Sword Familiar in *The Dracula X Chronicles* version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.

Brian is best known for his roles as Byonko in *Zatch Bell!*, Takato Matsuki in *Digimon Tamers* and Yamato Delgado in *Battle B-Daman*.
