  - Return to the Castle** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It is identified as ambient music in some sections within Castlevania's corridors. This music is most notable when heard in the Bernhard's Wing because it is the first area in the castle visited by its lord since his awakening.
