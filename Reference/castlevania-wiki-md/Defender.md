# Defender

- Defender** is the enchanted soul of a Basilisk in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It decreases STR by 20% and increases CON by 20%.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, this soul is held by the Wakwak Tree. It decreases STR by 2 and increases CON by 4 for each soul collected; a total of 9 souls will yield a STR -18 decrease and CON + 36 increase to the player's stats.
