# Soundtracks

This page contains all the **soundtracks** officially released for *Castlevania* games.

## Original soundtracks
Original Soundtracks contains music from the different games of the series and some bonus tracks.


## Sampler and promo soundtracks
Sampler soundtracks are CDs you can get by pre-ordering a game through Konami's stores or another special website. Promo soundtracks are special soundtracks, released for an important event, such as an anniversary or a game release.


## Other albums
Other albums are official albums, made by Konami's composers or other persons who arrange in a specific genre music from the series. They shouldn't be confused with fan albums, like *Sonata of the Damned* or *Vampire Variations*, which are fan albums (see the Castlevania Fan Wiki).
