# Harmony of Despair Stage 5

- Song of the Unslakable Blade** is the fifth chapter in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It's an amalgamation of the Cursed Clock Tower and The Pinnacle from *Dawn of Sorrow*, and the Mechanical Tower and Forsaken Cloister from *Order of Ecclesia*; although Death's room is based off of the Skeleton Cave of the same game.

## Hidden Item
Vic Viper makes an appearance in this chapter. At the upper right corner of the map with the transporting mirror and blue chest, go down to the dark room to the right of the blue chest, the room right of the room with two harpies and a Dullahan. At the highest platform in that room, go to the corner and press up. Quickly jump back up by the chest, and jump to grab Vic Viper, which will zoom past the screen from the right.
