# Rusty Food Tin

The **Rusty Food Tin** is a rusty can of unknown food. It can be consumed, however, although it normally reduces one's health.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, it is one of three bad foods. It reduces HP by 100, which is more than the Rotten Meat (HP -29), but not as bad as Spoiled Milk (HP -300).
