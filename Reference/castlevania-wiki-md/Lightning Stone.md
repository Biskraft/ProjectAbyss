# Lightning Stone

The **Lightning Stone** is a material in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a stone made when lightning strikes a crystal by chance. Has massive energy and is shocking to the touch.

## Availability
- Stolen from Thunder Demons. They will randomly open their stealing window when attacked. Equip a weak weapon or unequip them altogether to avoid killing the demon, and proceed to attack it repeatedly until it opens its window.
- One can be obtained in the Infinite Corridor (2F).
