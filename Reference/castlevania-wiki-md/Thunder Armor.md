# Thunder Armor

The **Thunder Armor** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

## Description
The Thunder Armor can emit Electricity spheres from its sword which will track Nathan's position for a few seconds, after which they will travel in a straight line and out of the screen.
