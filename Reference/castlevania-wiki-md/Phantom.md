The **Phantom** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is a monster that dons the aura of darkness and cannot be seen by a naked eye.

## Description
Surrounded by a mist, it is said to have the "Aura of Darkness". Sometimes, the mist will disappear, making the skeleton invisible, but the mist rarely does so, and only for a second. Interestingly, the reflection of the Phantom on the floor is that of a regular skeleton with three eyes, making it easier to find it.
