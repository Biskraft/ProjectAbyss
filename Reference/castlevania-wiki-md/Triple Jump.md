# Triple Jump

The **Triple Jump** is an additional jump following a Double Jump. It is a rare ability, usually given to secondary playable characters who lack a High Jump in order to offer them access to high places on the map from the start of the game. Maria Renard, who was also the first character to have a Double Jump, was the first character to feature a Triple Jump. She had this ability in the Sega Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. Maxim Kischine also featured this move in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

While Julius Belmont technically does not have a triple jump, his Uppercut in *Dawn of Sorrow* functions as one.
