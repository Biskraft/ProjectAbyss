# Wheat Roll

A **Wheat Roll** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It is a type of "cake" that appears in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It can be obtained by dropping a $100 gold piece into the register at the City of Haze bakery, which is harder to get than a Mille-feuille ($50), but easier to get than a Tarte au Poire ($500). It can also be found in the 13th Street painting.
