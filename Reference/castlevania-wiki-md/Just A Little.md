# Just A Little

right


  - Just a Little** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is learned by the Proboscis Fairy evolution. This also has a short animation which consists of the Proboscis Fairy somewhat *hesitating* in healing Hector. Afterwards, a red spotlight will appear to where he stands for a few seconds. Most of the time, it only heals Hector for a jokingly 1 HP. To add insult to injury, this ability cannot be used (will not become available in the Command Menu) for the next 5 minutes.

However, just like the Lucky Slot ability, this is also a luck-based one. There is actually a small chance that this ability heals Hector for 573 HP.

## Trivia
- Konami is known for inserting the number *573* in their games, this is one of those examples in the *Castlevania* series. "Five" in Japanese is *go*, changed to the voiceless form *ko*; "7" in Japanese is *nana*, shortened to *na*; "3" in Japanese is *mittsu*, shortened to *mi*; "573" = *ko*-*na*-*mi*.
