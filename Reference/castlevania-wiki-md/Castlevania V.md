# Castlevania V

Despite the NES and SNES series being named *Castlevania*, *Castlevania II*, *Castlevania III* and *Super Castlevania IV*, there was never a game called ***Castlevania V***. However, several games could be considered to be "*Castlevania V*":

- *Akumajō Dracula* (Sharp X68000) was released in Japan only, and was a remake of the original game.
- *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* was also released only in Japan. This was the first game to move beyond the era of Simon Belmont, taking place in 1792.
- *Castlevania: Bloodlines* was the first proper *Castlevania* game released in North America after *Super Castlevania IV*, and thus could be considered "*Castlevania V*".
- *Castlevania: Dracula X* was a SNES port of *Rondo of Blood* which made it to North America and Europe. The name ***Castlevania V*** usually refers to *Dracula X*.
