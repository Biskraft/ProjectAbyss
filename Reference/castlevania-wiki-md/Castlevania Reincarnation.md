  - Castlevania Reincarnation** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is the background music that plays when Joachim Armster fights bosses in the alternate Joachim Mode, instead of the normal bosses' tunes from the main game.

This track contains arrangements of Black Banquet and Dracula's Castle, which both originated from *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.

## Trivia
- This song has a strange title, since *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* is the first chronological *Castlevania* game in the original timeline, yet both Castlevania and Dracula didn't exist at the time.
