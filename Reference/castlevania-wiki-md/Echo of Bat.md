# Echo of Bat

right|124px

  - Echo of Bat** is a bat-produced sound wave. It can be utilized by Alucard while in bat form in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* by pressing the back dash button when the Echo of Bat relic is activated. These sound waves are visible in the game and can allow him to navigate dark-lit rooms (which happen to be filled with spikes) by being reflected when they hit a wall, working as a sonar and letting Alucard know if there's an obstacle in front of him. Obtaining the **Force of Echo** (called **Crazy Echo** in the Japanese version) relic causes these sonar waves to deal damage to enemies.

The Echo of Bat ability can also be used by Soma Cruz either in human or while in bat form with a Bat soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. Short ranged circles of sound emit from Soma's body, which damage nearby foes.
