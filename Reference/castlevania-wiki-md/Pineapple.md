# Pineapple

- Pineapple** is a food item in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It's a tart southern fruit that recovers 8 HP. It is the third highest recovering fruit, giving one more HP than a Banana and one less than an Apple. It is obtained randomly by using a Meal Ticket.
