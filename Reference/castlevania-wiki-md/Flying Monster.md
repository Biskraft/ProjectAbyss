The **Flying Monster** is an enemy in the LCD watch version of *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.

## Description
A weaker monster dragon that flies in front and above the player. They take one hit to destroy and are worth 5 points.

## Trivia
- While this seemingly generic enemy was never officially given a species, other enemies in the series share some similarities to it, such as the Gargoyle. However, the Gargoyle that appears in the NES version of *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* —which this LCD game is based on— was more human-like and it wasn't until later titles where it started to be depicted more like a dragon.
