  - Phantom Palace** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the background theme that plays in the Inner Quarters area in Dracula's vacant castle.

A remixed arrangement of the theme called **Phantom Palace Fontaine** is later featured in its original soundtrack.
