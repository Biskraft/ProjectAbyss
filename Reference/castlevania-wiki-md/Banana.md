# Banana

The **Banana** is a food item that recovers health. It recovers 7 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is the fourth highest recovering fruit, giving one more HP than an Orange and one less than a Pineapple. It is (appropriately) dropped by the Skeleton Ape.
