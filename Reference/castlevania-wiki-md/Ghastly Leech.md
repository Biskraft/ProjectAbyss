The **Ghastly Leech** is an enemy in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.

## Description
Giant leeches that suddenly leap out from poisonous marshlands. They can be encountered in Belasco and Joma marshes. Their main purpose is to obstruct Simon's path while he's traversing a poisonous swamp and in a hurry to reach safe ground. They can often be found along with Mudmen.
