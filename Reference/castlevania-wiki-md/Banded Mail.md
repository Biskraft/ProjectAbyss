# Banded Mail

- Banded Mail** is armor with several metal plates, which makes it extremely heavy.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the Banded Mail can be created by adding Chain Mail and White Steel to a Half Plate. This increases its defense from 24 to 30. It cannot be further evolved and is surpassed by Plate Mail by 2 Defense when 2 White Steel are combined.
