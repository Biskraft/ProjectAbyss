  - Banquet of Madness** is a song composed by Yuzo Koshiro for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is identified as the boss theme that plays during the final boss fight against both Death and Dracula in addition to their fused form.

It features an organ as well as some vocalizations near the beginning that gradually increase, before featuring steady rising and falling of organ pitches and percussions in an ominous manner.
