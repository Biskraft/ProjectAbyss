# Resist Curse

- Resist Curse** is an ability that increases one's resistance to curses, which makes it less likely for attacks with a "Curse" attribute to actually inflict this status ailment on the player. In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, this soul will cure the status once equipped.

This ability can be gained by Soma Cruz by stealing the souls of enemies. In *Aria of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*, it is obtained with an Ectoplasm soul, while in *Dawn of Sorrow*, it is obtained with a Killer Doll soul.
