# Frostbite

- Frostbite** is a water spell that can be performed by Alucard in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*. It causes only inactive blocks to fall from an opponent's board for 10 (level 1) or 20 (level 2) seconds. It is less effective after multiple uses or against certain bosses.
