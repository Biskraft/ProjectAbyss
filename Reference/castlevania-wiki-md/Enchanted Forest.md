The **Enchanted Forest** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the first level of the second chapter.

## Trivia
- One of the Fallen Knight scrolls refers to an ancient, sacred tree known as "The Great Dekuh". It was said to be truly alive and that it used its magical power to protect the forest. This tree was supposedly cursed to die by the Lords of Shadow and is now a dead stump infested with Giant Spiders. This is an obvious reference to the Great Deku Tree, its role as the forest guardian, its fate dealt by the death curse placed on it, and the giant Skulltula spiders that dwell inside its body in *The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time*.
