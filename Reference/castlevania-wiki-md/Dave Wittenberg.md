# Dave Wittenberg

- David Richard Paul "Dave" Wittenberg** is a South African-born American voice actor who was raised in Boston. He is well known as the voice of Yazoo in *Final Fantasy VII: Advent Children* and Kakashi Hatake in *Naruto*.

Dave provided the voice of Leon Belmont in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*.
