# Piercing Silence

- Piercing Silence** (also known as **Ripped Silence**) is a music theme originally composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is the second boss battle theme. It plays when fighting against Dullahan, Mummy Man, The Creature, Medusa, Werewolf and Death. It features rapid-pace percussion beats, strings, as well as ominous vocalizations.

Arrangements later appeared in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and the fighting game *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate*.
