# Illyana Belmont

- Illyana Belmont** is a character in the graphic novel *Castlevania: The Belmont Legacy*, published by IDW. She is Christopher's wife and mother of his yet unborn child.

## Background
Known as **Illyana Riktor** prior to her marriage. She's able to fight pretty well herself, although she's kidnapped by Dracula and is the object of his desires. Once she's rescued and Dracula defeated, he hides his power inside of her womb.
