# God's Garb

- God's Garb** are the clothes that God or a god wears. It is the second most powerful armor in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and boosting resistance to all types of attack in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
God's Garb grants a Defense boost of +25 as well as a Constitution boost of +2, resulting in a full defense boost of +26. This is greater than the Alucard Mail (+20 DEF). From a defense view-point, it is the best armor in the game initially. Given enough exploration, however, the Walk Armor will become even more powerful in that regard. To add further insult to injury, God's Garb lacks the protection against Fire, Ice, and Thunder that the Alucard Mail offers.

God's Garb is dropped by Guardians, which are extremely difficult to kill without using a "broken" weapon (Crissaegrim, Shield Rod + Alucard Shield etc.).

There's also a popular **fake rumor** that if you have a clear game with 200.0% map coverage or more, you can start a new game and enter a new name called "MEPHISTO" on the Name Entry Screen to make it so these Guardians will always drop the God's Garb, or to even start the game with it. This has been tested and proven to be absolutely false.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
The God's Garb is a drop rare drop off chapter 5 and 6 boss chests, but is a much more common drop in hard chapter boss chests and golden chests in chapters 5 and 6.
