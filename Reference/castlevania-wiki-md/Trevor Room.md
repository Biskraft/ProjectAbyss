# Trevor Room

The **Trevor Room** is a room in the Overlook Tower. It connects to the Puppet Theatre and the Greenhouse. Young Trevor plays with his figures here and requires the Mirror of Fate from his father.

In the *Revelations* DLC, the collapsed doorway can be opened with the Timeless Vision, allowing access to the Guest House.
