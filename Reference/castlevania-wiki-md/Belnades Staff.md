# Belnades Staff

The **Belnades Staff**, also called the **Warakiya Staff**, is a mystical staff utilized by members of the Belnades Clan for centuries. In 2D games, it is a relatively quick overhead weapon which deals out a relatively low amount of damage. In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it recovers a small amount of health each time it strikes an enemy. It is not clear if it appeared in *Castlevania Judgment*, where the staff weapon is shown to have balls at either end.

It first appeared as Sypha Belnades' main weapon in *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* and perhaps later as her main weapon in *Castlevania Judgment*. Yoko Belnades is able to use the weapon in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
