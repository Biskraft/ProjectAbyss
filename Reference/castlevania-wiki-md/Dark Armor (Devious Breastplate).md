# Dark Armor (Devious Breastplate)

The **Dark Armor** (called **Devious Breastplate** in Japan) is an armor that offers protection against Dark based attacks in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*. It sometimes shares this characteristic with the Mojo Mail (also known as the Dark Armor in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* and the Dark Breastplate in Japanese) and the Demon's Mail. Alucard is able to use all three of these armor in one game or another.

In *Symphony of the Night*, the Dark Armor gives the same amount of Defense (+8) as the Gold Plate and two other elemental armors in this game, such as the Ice Mail and Lightning Mail. This is 4 more than the Fire Mail, but 3 less than the Holy Mail.
