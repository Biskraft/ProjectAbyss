# Witch (Lords of Shadow)

- Witches** are featured in two *Lords of Shadow* games: *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* and *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*.

## Appearances
### *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*
Crow Witch Malphas appears as a a boss. On the other hand, an old witch named Baba Yaga acts as a supporting character to Gabriel Belmont.

### *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*
Witches appear in Act 3, although they are not actually fought as enemies and only appear to release the Daemon Lord from imprisonment to challenge Dracula's rule.
