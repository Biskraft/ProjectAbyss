# Arthro Skeleton

The **Arthro Skeleton** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

## Description
The Arthro Skeleton appears as a hybrid between a human skeleton and a skeletal spider, similar to an Arachne. It is a rather large enemy, able to nearly reach the ceiling in the underground areas it resides in. The Arthro Skeleton's behavior is similar to that of the Skeleton Spider, as it walks back and forth often, although it can throw a stream of bones that makes it a larger threat.
