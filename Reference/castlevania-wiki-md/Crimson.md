# Crimson

right|200px

  - Crimson** is a Bird-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a Level 4 Innocent Devil and can learn Ignition Blow and Flame Breath. It can be obtained from a Wingosaurus Innocent Devil, using red, blue or white Evo Crystals.
