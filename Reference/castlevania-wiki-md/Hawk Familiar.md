# Hawk Familiar

right|148px
A **Hawk Familiar** can be summoned in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* by combining the Saturn with the Golem DSS cards.

It attacks by throwing itself at the enemies (telegraphed by glowing first), dashing a considerable distance and damaging all enemies both on its way out and also while returning to Nathan's side, all of this for the low cost of 3 MP per attack.

While non-active, its sprite will still deal damage, so it can destroy projectiles, candles and damage enemies if Nathan just passes through them. However, it will not a attack enemies behind him.
