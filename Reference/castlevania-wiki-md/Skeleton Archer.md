# Skeleton Archer

The **Skeleton Archer** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. They are skeletons armed with bows, which they use to fire arrows straight ahead. They are normally stationary but some varieties will move a bit and take aim at a different location.

## Appearances
### *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade*
Skeleton Archers set up positions in the Ruins of *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* and are also placed in many other areas. Only long-ranged weapons can destroy them.
