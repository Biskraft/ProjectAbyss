 (born November 10th, 1976) is a BEMANI artist who has been working for Konami since 1998.

## Biography
Fujimori got his start on the PlayStation game *Gungage*. He made his BEMANI debut in the 2001 PlayStation game *DanceDanceRevolution 5thMIX CS*. Originally a *DanceDanceRevolution* exclusive composer, working as a sound producer/director of several PlayStation 2 *DanceDanceRevolution* titles, since *beatmania IIDX 10th style* Sota has been one of the biggest names in *beatmania IIDX* games.

He is well-known for his complicated, energetic synth/trance songs; his earlier *beatmania IIDX* songs often featuring the robotic vocals of cyborg AKEMI, or other robotic-sounding vocals.

Sota is also a regular composer for *pop'n music* since *pop'n music 13*, *jubeat* since *jubeat ripples*, *GuitarFreaks & DrumMania* since *GuitarFreaksXG & DrumManiaXG*, and *Reflec Beat* since its beginning. He also still contributes occasionally to the *DanceDanceRevolution* series, although currently only for arcade games. He also did all the background music for the *Sound Voltex* series, *jubeat saucer fulfill* and *Reflec Beat groovin'!!*

He is also a regular contributor to Konami's non-BEMANI titles. His resume includes *Castlevania Chronicles*, *Contra: Shattered Soldier*, *Neo Contra*, the *Winning Eleven* series, both *Rumble Roses* titles, the *Quiz Magic Academy* series, and *Road Fighters*.
