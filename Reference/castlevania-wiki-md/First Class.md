# First Class

right|250px

  - First Class** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is obtained by gaining dominance over a Bone Ark's soul and allows Soma to summon a Bone Ark where he can sit upon and be carried around by skeleton priests.

This soul provides one of the few methods to get across the narrow, flooded and full of spikes path located in the Subterranean Hell.
