# Platinum Plate

- Platinum Plate** (also called **Platinum Mail** or **Platinum Armor**) and is a high grade armor made of platinum. It is generally stronger than a Gold Plate, but weaker than a Diamond Plate.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, Platinum Mail grants +10 Defense, which is two more than the Gold Plate, but 3 less than the Diamond Plate.

In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, Platinum Armor gives +150 Defense, which is more than Steel Armor (+120), but less than Diamond Armor (+210).

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, the Platinum Plate gives +45 Defense, which is the strongest suit of armor found in the game that also does not sacrifice mobility. This is more than a Gold Plate (+25).

In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* the Platinum Plate grants Defence +55, which is the strongest armor in the game that focuses on physical protection, compared to the Gold Plate's +40. It is made available at the shop by performing Eugen's **Work of the Finest Quality** quest by giving him 3 Gold Ores. This also makes available Gold Leggings, Gold Plate, and Platinum Leggings.
