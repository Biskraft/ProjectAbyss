# Crystal Necklet

The **Crystal Necklet** is one of Charlotte's necklaces in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* that increases Defense (+2) and Constitution (+2). It is more effective than the Forget Me Not (+1 DEF, +2 CON), but less effective than the Platinum Chain (+3 DEF, +2 CON).
