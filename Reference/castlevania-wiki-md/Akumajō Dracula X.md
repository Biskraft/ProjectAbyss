# Akumajō Dracula X

- Akumajo Dracula X** is a soundtrack released on November 3, 1993. It contains the original soundtracks to *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and *Castlevania: Bloodlines*, as well as a few songs from *Akumajō Dracula* (Sharp X68000). The music was composed by Konami Kukeiha Club.

## Additional information
- Disc 1: Rondo of Blood Original Soundtrack
- Disc 2:<br />Tracks 01~23: Bloodlines Original Soundtrack<br />Tracks 24~27: Akumajo Dracula X68000 Arranged Tracks
- Eric Lecarde's theme in *Castlevania Judgment* is "Iron-Blue Intention".
