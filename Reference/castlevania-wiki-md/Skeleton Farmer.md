# Skeleton Farmer

The  is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is a skeleton that sows haunted plants.

## Description
A unique skeleton dressed as a farmer who sows Une and Mandragora seeds in large numbers; these quickly grow into adult enemy plants which can hassle the player.

## Appearances
### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
The Skeleton Farmer's soul allows plant-based souls (Une and Alura Une) to do more damage. There is only one Skeleton Farmer in the game, so trying to get its soul could prove to be difficult.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Only two Skeleton Farmers appear in the entire game, both in the same room inside the Nest of Evil along with three Lesser Demons. A whip strike from weapons such as the Flame Whip or the fully powered Vampire Killer, is recommended to take care of the massive amounts of plants they summon.
