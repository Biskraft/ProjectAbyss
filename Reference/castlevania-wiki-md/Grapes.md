# Grapes

- Grapes** are a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. They recover 5 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is the lowest amount of any fruit or any food item, giving one less than an Orange.

Grapes also appear in *Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Portrait of Ruin*. It is always listed with a lower health than Strawberries. It is always one of the least HP giving food items in whatever game it appears in. Later games usually list a Meat Strip as the lowest HP item available, which gives considerably less HP. In *Aria of Sorrow*, the Persimmon is shown to be a fruit that gives less HP.

Grapes are dropped by various plant enemies, such as the Thornweed in *Symphony of the Night*, Wooden Golem in *Aria of Sorrow*, Alura Une in *Dawn of Sorrow*, and Wakwak Tree in *Portrait of Ruin*.
