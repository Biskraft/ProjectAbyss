# Edward (Lords of Shadow)

- Edward** is a mentioned character in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. He is the dearly departed son of one of the dead knights of the Brotherhood of Light. He was an eight year old boy who spent his days playing hide-and-seek and who liked to sing. A puppet in Castlevania similarly liked to spend its day playing hide-and-seek, made similar gestures as the knight's son Edward, and even whistled his favorite songs. The knight wondered if it was his son, before he died in the Toys Assembly Line.

## Trivia
- Edward could be a reference to Edward, a boy who was captured and brought to Dracula's Castle in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. He's one of six children that Henry Oldrey must find in his storyline.
- It's possible that the boy puppet who managed to break through the Toy Maker's demonic possession was the puppet that the Fallen Knight Scroll speculated as housing the soul of Edward.
