# Heart Cuirass

The **Heart Cuirass** is a type of body armor in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. While equipped, any damage taken by Shanoa will lower her Heart count instead of health. Once her Heart count reaches zero, however, she will take damage as normal.

An interesting side effect of this, is that Shanoa gains immunity to *all* of the game's status ailments if her Heart count remains above zero. With a high degree of DEF, she could have little trouble passing through rooms filled with enemies infamous for inflicting any of said status effects. This comes at a cost, however; failure to avoid attacks can rob Shanoa of the ability to make use of Glyph Unions.
