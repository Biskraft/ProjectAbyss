# Miso Soup

- Miso Soup** is a food item that recovers health in the *Castlevania* series of games. Its first appearance was as a food item for Maria in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and later in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*, and is the first kind of soup to appear in a *Castlevania* game.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* it recovers 20 HP, which is the lower amount of the two soups that appear in the game, being 10 less HP than those recovered by Ramen. It also recovers the same as the Grape Juice or the fast food item, Hamburger.
