# Legion Laser

right|290px
  - Legion Laser** is a laser beam emitted from one of the tentacles from Legion's core. Soma Cruz is able to use these lasers with Legion's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. In *Aria of Sorrow*, three tentacles appear behind Soma and fire three simultaneous beams. In *Harmony of Despair*, a single laser beam, which appears much like a laser emitted from one of Legion's tentacles, is sent from Soma, although no tentacles are shown.

## Trivia
- The three tentacles Soma grows when using this ability in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* are likely based on those from the Tentacle enemies from the *Gradius* series (as well as it is the specific version of Legion that appears in that game), whereas the laser beams themselves appear to be based on the Cyclone Laser, a specific type of laser shot in the same series.
