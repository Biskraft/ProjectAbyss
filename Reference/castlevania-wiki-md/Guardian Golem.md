# Guardian Golem

right|250px
  - Guardian Golem** is a guardian-type soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by defeating a Big Golem. When equipped, the torso of a Big Golem will appear on Soma's back, attacking enemies behind him.
