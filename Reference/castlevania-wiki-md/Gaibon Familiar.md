# Gaibon Familiar

right|250px

  - Gaibon Familiar** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over Gaibon's soul. It allows Soma to summon a Gaibon ally as a familiar.

Gaibon will screech and his body will flash just before sending himself to a nearby enemy. While dashing, he will also deal damage to all enemies in the way. He will stop moving and dealing damage right after hitting its target.

Gaibon can also be controlled with the stylus, holding it in the touch-screen for more than 2 seconds will make him attack said area. He will not respond to the style commands if there are still enemies in the area to attack.

Gaibon's soul also has an interaction with Slogra's Soul, Magic Spear Throw,  wich allow him use a second spear every time it is throw by Soma's, he will not however, use this ability while activiliy attacking on his own.
