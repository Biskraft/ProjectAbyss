Block 5A (Route 7) for the game *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* is the **Tower of Terror** portion of the **Curse Castle**.

Trevor's sub-weapons in order are the Dagger, Holy Water, Cross Boomerang, another Dagger, and an Axe.
