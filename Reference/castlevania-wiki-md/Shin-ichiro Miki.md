# Shin-ichiro Miki

is a Japanese voice actor and a member of 81 Produce. Some of his roles include Zamasu from *Dragon Ball Super* (who coincidentally had a similar genocidal goal against humanity that Dracula did).

He voiced Richter Belmont in the internet radio *Akumajō Dracula X: Tsuioku no Yasōkyoku* and Alucard in the *Castlevania* animated series.
