# Puppet Theatre

thumb|270pxThe **Puppet Theatre **is the Toy Maker's show room in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. Located in the Overlook Tower wing of the Castle.

The puppet show tells the sad tale of the Toy Maker. Upon completing the show, the Toy Maker is revealed, Dracula returns his heart and he awakens, only to be corrupted.

While fighting the Toy Maker Dracula will face his most deadliest puppets yet, the Puppet Master along with the knight Sir Walter deGrey and the dragon Sauraganthorix.
