# Sunstone

- Sunstone** is a gemstone that improves status after dawn. It appears in a necklace in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It improves statistics between 6am and 6pm game time. In *Symphony of the Night*, it improves many statistics by +5 (Strength, Constitution, Intelligence, Luck) between 6am and 6pm. It is the counterpart to the Moonstone. The Sun Ring has a similar role in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.

In the Saturn version of the game, the time is based on the current system clock setting, while in the PSX version (and all other ports) it is based on the total playtime, imagining the game to begin at midnight; "dawn" is therefore at 6 hours of play time, while "sunset" is after 18 hours. This continues to cycle at 12 hour intervals.
