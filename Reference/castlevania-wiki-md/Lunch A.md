# Lunch A

- Lunch A** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It recovers 25 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is one the lowest amount for any full lunch, being one less than Lunch B, but one more than the highest breakfast, Morning Set.
