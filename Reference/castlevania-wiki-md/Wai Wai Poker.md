# Wai Wai Poker

is a poker-themed medal game developed and published by Konami in 1997 in Japan only.

## Features
This solitaire card game features several Konami mascot characters, including Kid Dracula from *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*, Goemon and Ebisumaru from the *Ganbare Goemon* series, TwinBee from the *TwinBee* series, Takosuke from the *Parodius* series, and Penta from *Antarctic Adventure*.
