# Hierophant Ring

The Hierophant Ring is the ring of the Tarot persona, [The Hierophant](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hierophant) in Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia. Equipping it allows Shanoa to gain 50% more experience from kills than normal, in addition to a Defense boost of 2. The boost looks to be multiplicative, rather than additive; if Shanoa equips a second Hierophant ring, all the monsters she kills give her 225% of their listed experience, rather than simply 200%.
