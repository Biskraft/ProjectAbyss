# Phonograph

right|50px

The **Phonograph**, also known as **Record Player**, is a special item which appears in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, *Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia*. It allows access to listen to the game's soundtrack or change the background music which plays during the game, among other uses.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*
The **Phonograph** in *Harmony of Dissonance* is a collectible which Juste can find to decorate a special room in the Castle Treasury where all collectibles are stored during the game. It doesn't seem to have any use other than to complete the collection and be able to watch a little variation at the ending where Lydie hangs onto Juste's shoulder. It can be found at the Skeleton Cave (Castle A).

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The **Record Player** is a reward for completing Wind's quest: "The Lonely Stage". Jonathan and Charlotte must venture back into the Dark Academy and find an "empty" room where only a grand piano stands. Waiting quietly for a few seconds will result in an apparition of a musician completing his last performance and departing from this world.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
In *Order of Ecclesia*, the **Phonograph** is used to complete George's second quest: "The Killing Scream". Shanoa must use it to record the scream of a Banshee at the Monastery and bring it back to him.
