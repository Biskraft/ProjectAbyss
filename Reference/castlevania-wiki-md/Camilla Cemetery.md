  - Camilla Cemetery** (Japanese: カミーラ墓地 *Kamīra Bochi*) is the easternmost accessible area when starting out from the town of Jova. It is located just to the east of the town of Aljiba. If you place Garlic at the base of the cemetery, a Gypsy will appear to give you a Silver Knife, which is more powerful than a regular dagger, but consumes hearts. The west section is filled with Zombie Hands, but a high ledge prevents you from reaching the other part of the cemetery. This can be reached coming from a different path from the Joma Marsh (provided one has enough Laurels), which is to the right and to the north of the cemetery. The other section is also populated with Slime.

A jump glitch allows you to get across the impossible jump by jumping from the far edge
and whipping midair. But the slimes are too strong for the Chain Whip. Further right will take you to the other side of Joma Marsh (which will consume at least 2 Laurels) where you will encounter a skeleton which takes 4 hits from the Chain Whip.
