# Platinum Crown

The **Platinum Crown** is a crown made of platinum in *Harmony of Dissonance* that offers the highest amount of Defense (+30).

This is two greater than the Gold Headband. The total attribute worth is also equal to that which the Red Silk Hat, which trades 5 defense for 5 Intelligence. It can only be obtained at the Merchant's shop at the Entrance of either castle.
