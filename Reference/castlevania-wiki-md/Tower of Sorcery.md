# Tower of Sorcery

The **Tower of Sorcery** is a location in *Castlevania* and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. It is a tower entirely composed of crystals.

## Overview
The stage is comprised of countless glittering crystals hovering over an endless black abyss which act as platforms and item holders (replacing torches). The player must jump onto them, going higher and higher until they get to the top.

The level's layout is mostly arranged in the manner of an obstacle course, with disappearing platforms, timed hindrances, and the requirement of making several precision jumps.

Some of these crystals cannot be leaped onto unless another, separate crystal is destroyed. Monsters also appear from the crystalline floors, sometimes appearing when the player doesn't notice.

Carrie can find her second hidden item in this level.
