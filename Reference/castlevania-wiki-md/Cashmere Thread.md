# Cashmere Thread

The **Cashmere Thread** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They can be found inside of green chests in Misty Forest Road and the Skeleton Cave. They can also be dropped by White Fomors.

Five of them must be brought to Monica in order to complete her third quest, "Is That Cashmere?".

She will reward Shanoa with a Party Dress (DEF +24, INT +3, MND +9); and also both, the Party Dress and the Wedding Dress (DEF +26, STR +3, INT +2, MND +15), will become available at Jacob's shop for purchase.
