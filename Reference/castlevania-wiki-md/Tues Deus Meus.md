# Tues Deus Meus

- Tues Deus Meus** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*. It is the background music that plays during a general boss fight. The track has since been reused in *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula*, *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula II* and *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula: Lords of Shadow* for most likely the same purpose.

## Trivia
  - Tues Deus meus (in manibus tuis)* is a Latin phrase that means "You're my God (in your hands) [I put my fate]". It originates from Catholic religious texts. Oddly enough, the translation fits the intensive atmosphere and perils that transpire during the boss fights, especially when those that are crucial to the game's storyline are encountered.
