# Turkey

- Turkey** is a common type of Meat found in *Castlevania*.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, Turkey gives the second highest amount of HP for any Meat (+80 HP), which is greater than Pot Roast (+50), but lower than Sirloin (+100).
