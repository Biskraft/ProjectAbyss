# White Fang

right|250px

  - White Fang** is an attack that Soma Cruz is able to use with a Warg soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*. A silhouette of a warg's head appears and bites an enemy in front of him. This attack is similar to the Dextro Custos and Sinestro Custos glyphs, which are the left and right heads of Cerberus that Shanoa is able to use in *Order of Ecclesia* and *Judgment*.
