# Skeleton Chain

Skeleton Chains are any skeleton that wields a chained weapon. There are four basic kinds, Whip Skeletons that swing a whip like a Belmont does, Skeleton Flails, which swing a rod connected to a Morning Star with a chain, Mace Skeletons, which swing around a heavy Morningstar around their heads with a chain, and Skeleton Whip Swords which use an extend-able sword.
