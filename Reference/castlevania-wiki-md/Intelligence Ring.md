# Intelligence Ring

The **Intelligence Ring** is a ring in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* that increases INT at the expense of other stats. It increases INT by 50 and decreases STR and LCK by 10. It has a scaled down effect as the Sage Armband. It is the Intelligence counterpart to the Strength Ring, Hard Ring (Defense), and Luck Ring.
