# Jason Isaacs

- Jason Isaacs** is a British film and television actor. He is known for a variety of roles including those in *Armageddon*, *The Patriot*, the *Harry Potter* saga, *Brotherhood* and *Star Trek: Discovery*.

He voiced Satan in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* and *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. He will voice the Judge in the *Castlevania* animated series.
