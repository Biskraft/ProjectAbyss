}}**Akira Sōji**, better known by the pseudonyms  and **AKI**, is a Japanese composer. He was a member of Konami Kukeiha Club.

He composed the very famous Divine Bloodlines from the 1993 NEC PC Engine game *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and participated on the soundtrack Akumajō Dracula X. Several tracks composed by him are arranged for newer *Castlevania* games.
