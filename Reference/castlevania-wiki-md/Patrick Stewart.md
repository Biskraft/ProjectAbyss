# Patrick Stewart

- Sir Patrick Hewes Stewart**, OBE (born July 13, 1940) is an English film, television and stage actor and voice actor, as well as a university Chancellor. He is best known for portraying Jean-Luc Picard in *Star Trek: The Next Generation*. He also plays Professor Charles Xavier in the original *X-Men* film franchise.

He plays Zobek in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* and *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.
