# Pearl Ring

The **Pearl Ring** is an accessory in the *Castlevania* series. It is a ring with holy powers adorned with a pearl.

When equipped, it raises many statistics by one (STR, ATK, CON, INT and LCK). The Pearl Ring in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* gives a small of amount of DEF (+2).
