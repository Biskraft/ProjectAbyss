# Carmilla (disambiguation)

- Carmilla** may refer to:
- Carmilla: An enemy and servant of Dracula from the main *Castlevania* timeline.
  - Carmilla: An updated version of the main timeline's character of the same name, appearing in *Castlevania Judgment*.
- Camilla Fernandez: The vampirized cousin of Carrie Fernandez, appearing in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Legacy of Darkness*.
- Camilla: One of the main antagonists in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.
- Carmilla: A major antagonist and boss appearing in the *Lords of Shadow* reboot series.
- Carmilla: A version of the character based on that from the original timeline and one of the main antagonists of the *Castlevania* animated series.
