# Exploding Quake

- Exploding Quake** is an advanced move in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* that involves Shadow Magic. This combo creates a series of fiery explosions surging from beneath an enemy. The subsequent eruption will release a larger shockwave, hitting all nearby enemies that come in contact with it. However, Gabriel will be rooted to one spot through the duration of this move.
