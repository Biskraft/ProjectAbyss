# Tiramisu (food)

thumb|Castlevania: Harmony of Despair|thumb|[[Mousse Cakes, Canelé and Tiramisu]]

  - Tiramisu** is an Italian cheese cake.

It is the only two-star dessert food item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It provides a decent MP and HP boost (+50), as well as a good INT and LCK boost (+4). This is better than the one-star Strawberry Donut (+35 HP & MP, and +2 INT & LCK), but worse than the three-star Berry Mousse Cake (+80 HP & MP, +4 INT, +8 LCK).
