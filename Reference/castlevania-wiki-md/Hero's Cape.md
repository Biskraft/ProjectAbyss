# Hero's Cape

The **Hero's Cape** (also called the **Velvet Cloak** in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*) is a low grade cloak of velvet found in Castlevania.

It is blue in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* and gives a Defense boost of +3, which is one more than a Silk Cloak, but two less than the Emerald Cloak.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, it is gray and gives a Defense boost of +2, which is one more than a normal Cape and one less than the Blue Cape (which also gives INT +3).

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, the Hero's Cape is the first Cape likely to be found that gives an HP and MP boost (+2 each). It gives the same amount of Fire and Ice defense (+5 each) as most capes do, and one more HP than a Cloth Cape does. It is more common than the Cloth Cape, yet has better overall stats, which essentially trades 1 HP and 1 MP for a CON boost of +1.
