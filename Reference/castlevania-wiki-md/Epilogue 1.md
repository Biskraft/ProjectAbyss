  - Epilogue 1** is a music theme composed by Soshiro Hokkai for *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is played after Juste Belmont defeats Dark Maxim in Castle B without wearing both his and his comrade's bracelets. The track also plays after Dark Maxim is defeated in Castle A regardless if both bracelets are worn in that half of the castle.
