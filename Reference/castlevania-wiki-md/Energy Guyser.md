# Energy Guyser

right|162px

  - Energy Guyser** is a bullet soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained from a Werewolf and creates a mountain of magical fire in front of Soma which travels a brief distance forward. It cannot be used in the air.
