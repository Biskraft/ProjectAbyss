# Tower of Dolls

- Tower of Dolls** is a music theme originally composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania Chronicles*. It is also featured in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* as the background theme for the Mechanical Tower.

In *Castlevania Judgment*, it is the Golem's theme.
