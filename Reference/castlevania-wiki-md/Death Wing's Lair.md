# Death Wing's Lair

- Death Wing's Lair** is an upside down version Olrox's Quarters. It is not clear who "Death Wing" is, but it is not the boss of this stage, which is the mummy Akmodan II. The changes here are the same as the Reverse Entrance, and even shares the same music (Finale Toccata).

Death Wing's Lair can first be entered from the right from the Anti-Chapel and connects to the Reverse Colosseum up and to the right and the Black Marble Gallery above.
