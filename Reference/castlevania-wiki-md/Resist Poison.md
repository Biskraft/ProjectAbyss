# Resist Poison

- Resist Poison** is an ability that increases once resistance to Poison, which makes it less likely for attacks with a Poison attribute to actually poison the user.

This ability can be gained by Soma Cruz by stealing the souls of enemies. It is obtained with a Poison Worm soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, a Giant Slug soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, and a Zombie soul in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. Note that the Zombie soul in Aria of Sorrow gives you the Zombie Body ability, which makes you stronger when poisoning.
