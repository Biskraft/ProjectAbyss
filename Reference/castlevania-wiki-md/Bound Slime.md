# Bound Slime

right|298px

  - Bound Slime** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It summons a bouncy slime.

## Characteristics
This ability can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Slime's soul. It allows Soma to throw a slime in front of him which will then bounce on each surface it touches (much like the Ricochet Rock does).

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, it pierces through enemies and lasts for 4 seconds after being thrown, bouncing off the terrain an endless amount of times while it's still onscreen. Only two slimes can be active at a time.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it shares both its piercing and endlessly bouncing capabilities, but now it lasts for 10 seconds and up to seven slimes can be active onscreen at the same time.
