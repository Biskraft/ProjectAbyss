# Megingjord

right|70px

The **Megingjord** (called the **Covenant Stone** in the North American localization of *Symphony of the Night* and **Megingelos** in *Curse of Darkness*) is the belt that the Norse god Thor wore, which doubled his bodily strength. In 2D games, it increases both Strength and Attack. In 3D games, it causes you to deal greater damage to enemies the lower your health is.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it raises both Strength and Attack by +15. In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it raises strength by 10 and attack by 5.

In both *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, it causes you to inflict greater damage on enemies the less health that you have.
