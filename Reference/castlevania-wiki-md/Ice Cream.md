# Ice Cream

right|73px

  - Ice Cream** is a food item that recovers health in the *Castlevania* series. Its first appearance was as a food item for Maria in *Rondo of Blood* and later in *The Dracula X Chronicles*. There is also another variant called **Soft Serve Ice Cream** in the same game.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it gives the player 15 HP. It gives one more HP than a Cheesecake and one less than a Parfait. It also gives the same amount of HP than a Barley Tea.

Ice Cream is dropped by ice-based enemies such as the Frozen Shade in *Symphony of the Night* and *Dawn of Sorrow*, and the Frost Demon in *Curse of Darkness*. It can also be dropped by enemies that enjoy eating it, such as the Death Ripper in the latter game.

Ice Cream can also be found in the Burnt Paradise in *Portrait of Ruin*.
