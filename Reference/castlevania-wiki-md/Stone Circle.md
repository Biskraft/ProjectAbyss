# Stone Circle

right|204px

  - Stone Circle** is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. When cast, it grants the protection of spirits of stone.

A regular cast summons four boulders that will circle around any of the two protagonists, while a fully charged one will summon eight boulders that will circle around Charlotte.

This spell is especially useful in the battle against Abaddon in the Nest of Evil as it will consume most locusts, making the rest of the fight almost trivial.

This spell is similar to Projectile Circles that have appeared in other games, especially the Ice Circle.
