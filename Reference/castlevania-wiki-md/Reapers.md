# Reapers

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">[[Death, the most well-known reaper in the series.</p>]]

  - Reapers** are cloaked skeletal enemies found in the *Castlevania* series. They can usually be seen wielding scythes which they use to harvest souls.

The most famous reaper is Death, Dracula's confidant. A common reaper called a Night Stalker appears in *Castlevania: The Adventure*, *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge* and *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*. It throws scythes in a looping boomerang-like fashion, similar to an Axe Armor.

Galamoth also has a reaper emissary called the Time Reaper in *Castlevania Judgment*.

It's possible these beings are the same Witchi Wench from the European instruction booklet for the Game Boy version of *Kid Dracula*.
