# Summon Larva

right|250px
  - Summon Larva** is a bullet soul found in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be obtained from a Larva. When cast, a Larva will be summoned from Soma's hand and will fly straight ahead, damaging anything in its path.
