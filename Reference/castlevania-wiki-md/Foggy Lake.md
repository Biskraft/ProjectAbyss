The **Foggy Lake** is a location in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. It is a lake in Transylvania that surrounds the island to Dracula's Castle in 1844 to 1852.

## Overview
Little is known of this mysterious lake. The area is constantly shrouded in fog from day-to-night, and the water is toxic to the point where no ordinary life forms can survive in it. It is possible that Dracula is the leading cause for this pollution, and that the water was normal before his revival. But no one can say for certain. It is also possible that the pollution came from the Forest of Silence since the river there connects to one another.

There is a docking area in Foggy Lake that leads to the Forest of Silence, where Dracula's Castle is. The dock is made of stone, but has been completely destroyed. However, it's still possible for tourists to aboard/depart there by boat or ship (Provided they aren't attacked by monsters).

## Secrets and trivia
- If the player takes too long to kill the very first Lizard Man when on deck then a penalty is awarded: Two more Lizard Men will spawn, and the player will have to kill them before being able to proceed. This is NOT affected by in-game time; and will still spawn even if you had the game on pause.

- When on the second part of the ship's mast – There is an invisible flooring that takes you to the front mast which otherwise couldn't be reached well by climbing. The reward is Roast Beef and Axe. An invisible wall is also in place to prevent players from falling when they cross.
