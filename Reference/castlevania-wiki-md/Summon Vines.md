# Summon Vines

right|300px
  - Summon Vines** is a summoning spell that can be performed in the Saturn version of *Symphony of the Night* by using the Woodland Shield's Shield Combo Spell with a Shield Rod or Mablung Sword. It costs 30 MP and causes vines to immobilize and damage random enemies.
