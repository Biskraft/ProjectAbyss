# Famicom 4koma Manga Kingdom

is a series of *yonkoma* manga parody anthologies written and illustrated by various artists and published by Futabasha.

A strip based on *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*, titled *Another Legend - From the Legend of Demon Castle*, appeared on Volume 2, released on December 10, 1991.

While Kid Dracula also appears on the cover of that same issue, no *Boku Dracula-kun* content appears on it.
