# Horse-headed enemies

There are several kinds of man-like horse-headed enemies in the *Castlevania* series. The first was Amduscias, who is a demon with a horse or unicorn head, and who first appeared in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* (called **Hellfire Beast** in the English localization). The **Were-Horse**, a half man-half horse beast which appeared in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. A Unicorn enemy similar to Amduscias also appeared in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
