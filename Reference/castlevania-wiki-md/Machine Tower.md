The **Machine Tower** is a location in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It is one of the Clock and Machine Towers found in the *Castlevania* series, and it is similar to a clock tower in that it's dominated by gears working in the background; however, it does not feature a giant clock.

## Overview
The boss of this area, the Iron Golem, awaits for Nathan in one of the higher rooms. A dismissive Hugh arrives after the battle and warns Nathan against trying to steal his glory, claiming it's him the one who's in charge to defeat Dracula, then runs away.
