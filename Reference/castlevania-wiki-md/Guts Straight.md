# Guts Straight

right|100px
  - Guts Straight** is a bullet soul which appears in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Dawn of Sorrow*.

In *Aria of Sorrow*, the Guts Straight can be obtained from a Werejaguar and lets Soma use every ounce of energy to throw a strong straight punch. In *Dawn of Sorrow* it is instead named **Max Straight**, it can be obtained from a Slaughterer and it allows Soma to smack enemies with quick straight punches. This version cannot be used in the air.
