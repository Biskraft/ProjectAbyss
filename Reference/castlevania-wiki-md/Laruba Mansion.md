 is the fifth and last accessible mansion in the game.

## Overview
Laruba Mansion is just to the east of the Camilla Cemetery and is guarded by Camilla's spirit, but an impossible jump makes it impossible to reach from the cemetery coming from the west. Instead, to reach it from the west side of the cemetery, you must travel across the Dead River to the western most part of the game, Deborah's Cliff, use the Red Crystal to take a whirl-wind to transport you to the east side of the map at Bodley Mansion, continue heading west, using the Red Crystal again at the base of some water, continue heading west, cross the treacherous Joma Marsh and to reach it. Joma Marsh is on the other side of it as well, which leads back to Camilla Cemetery.

Laruba Mansion is notable as it is the only one that contains traps that move (boulders fall down on you in a dead end room, ground spikes or arrows shoot upwards at you while you are on a moving platform). It also is characterized by a large number of fake walls (some of which you have to jump in order to get through) and breakable blocks. It also the only mansion that has Spiders in it. There is a Gypsy hidden within the mansion that will beg you to take his Laurels, which is very useful getting across the Joma Marsh again. The entrance to the area that holds this mansion's relic, Dracula's Ring, is guarded by a giant mask referred to as **Vampira** in the manual, which generally thought to be the mask of Carmilla. While it is possible to skip fighting this boss in order to obtain the relic, it must be defeated in order to obtain the Magic Cross, which is also needed to enter Dracula's Castle

When exiting the mansion, heading left takes you towards Camilla Cemetery (requires at least one Laurel), while heading right takes you towards the Debious Woods.
