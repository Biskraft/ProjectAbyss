# Game Over (The Adventure)

- Game Over** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania: The Adventure*. This short jingle plays after the player has lost all their lives, thus ending the game. It also plays in the game's sequel, *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge*, and in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, serving the same purpose of announcing the game's end after losing all lives.
