# Max Mind Points

- Max Mind Points**, commonly abbreviated as **Max MP**, is a statistic found in nearly all *Castlevania* games. It represents the numeric total of how many Mind Points (MP) the player character can use in order to cast magic spells or perform other special abilities.

In the metroidvania style games, Max MP is increased each time the player character earns a Level Up or collects MP Max Ups throughout their journeys.
