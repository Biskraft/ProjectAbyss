The **Hakuba Shrine** (白馬神社 *Hakuba Jinja*) is a location in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*.

## Overview
It is here where most people celebrate the coming of eclipses. Until a ritual in Europe was conducted to seal away Dracula and his castle, strange things have happened. Especially, in the year 2035, Soma Cruz and Mina Hakuba were going to view the first solar eclipse of the 21st century until, in a flash, the two have ended up in Castle Dracula.

Hammer, a soldier in the US military, was sent on a mission to investigate the shrine, although he had been thinking about bailing on his mission and opening a shop during the eclipse. He also ended up being transported to Dracula's Castle instead. According to Mina, Genya Arikado and Yoko Belnades have been visiting the shrine over the years, which is why she knows them.
