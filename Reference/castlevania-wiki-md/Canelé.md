# Canelé

thumb|Castlevania: Harmony of Despair|thumb|[[Mousse Cakes, Canelé and Tiramisu]]
  - Canelé** is a French pastry first devised long ago in a convent. It is a very common item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* that recovers a small amount of HP (+15) and grants a small amount of CON (+2). Its benefits are identical to the Hamburger except that it can be sold for more gold. It gives one more CON than Popcorn (+1), but much less HP (+40). It is slightly surpassed by the Hot Dog (HP +18, CON +3). The Canelé is one of the few desserts in *Harmony of Despair* that does not increase both HP and MP.
