# Jewel (disambiguation)

- Jewel** may refer to one of the following:
    - Jewel** - General term. A type of item that can have many different uses depending on the game. In some games, these items can be sold to merchants in order to obtain profit.
      - Jewel of Open** - A relic that unlocks certain doors in some games, giving access to new areas.
      - Red Jewel** - Ammunition for sub-weapons akin to Hearts, appearing in *Castlevania: Bloodlines*, *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.
      - *Blue Jewel** - A higher value variant of the Red Jewel appearing only in *Castlevania: Bloodlines*.
      - White Jewel** - Environmental items akin to Save Rooms in other games, appearing in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.
