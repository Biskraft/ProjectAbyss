# Gambler Glasses

right|50px

The **Gambler Glasses** allow the wearer to see enemy drop percentages. They also give a LCK boost of +2.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Awarded for completing Wind's "The Gambler" quest. To achieve this, the player must find five cards dropped by different enemies:
    - Spade** (Yorick)
    - Club** (Coppelia)
    - Heart** (Nyx)
    - Diamond** (Treant)
    - Joker** (Killer Clown)
