# Greed Amulet

right

The **Greed Amulet** is a pendant in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* that increases luck in finding Gold. It was replaced by the Gold Ring in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and later games.

Despite its in-game description, it doesn't boost gold apparition rate, but "upgrades" all gold drops by one level (e.g.: candles which usually drop $100 now drop $250; a Skeleton Glass would drop $400 instead of $250). It can be found in the western part of the Castle Treasury in Castle A.

The player will never be able to gain more than $400, as it is the highest gold item in the game. Thus, candles or enemies which drop $400 won't drop more with the Greed Amulet equipped.

Juste can tirelessly farm gold near the amulet's location, in the vertical room just before the crystal cavern area: there are no enemies around and the gold-dropping candle is near the exit, so he can gain $400 each time by entering the room over and over. There are other "gold farming spots" (the best one being right before the boss room in Skeleton Cave), but this is the first one he will find after obtaining the amulet and the Merchant is conveniently located just three rooms away.
