# Carousel's Engine

The **Carousel's Engine** is the eleventh area Alucard explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. This is the engine to the carousel in the Toy Maker's Funfair, and it is here that Alucard must solve another puzzle in order to save Simon.
