# Shadow Daggers

right|250px

  - Shadow Daggers** are a secondary weapon of Dracula in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. They replace the Silver Daggers from the first game. Although they lack power, the shadow daggers have unlimited ammo that slowly replenish over time.
