# Blocking Mail

right|50px
The **Blocking Mail** (also called **Impervious Mail**) is armor that on occasion completely 'blocks' an attack, causing it to cause no damage.

Blocking Mail in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* grants +35 Defense, which is the same as the Silver Plate.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, Blocking Mail gives +25 Defense, which is the same as Chain Mail.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, the Impervious Mail gives Jonathan +8 Defense, which is one less than a Copper Plate.
