# Ancient Armor

The **Ancient Armor** is armor rumored to have been worn by the gods. In newer games, it causes its wearer's health to be reduced by a certain percentage of their max HP regardless of the strength of the attack. This makes taking damage similar to old-style *Castlevania* games.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it gives +45 DEF, which is 5 greater than Brigandine, but 3 less than Heavy Armor.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, it makes it so that any attack received reduces Jonathan's health by 10%. It's dropped by Old Axe Armor.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, it causes any attack received to reduce the health of the equipped character by 20%. It's dropped by Death in Hard Mode.
