# Justice Ring

right

The **Justice Ring** is a ring in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* and represents the Tarot card "Justice". Wearing it provides Shanoa a STR boost, scaling in effectiveness as more enemies are killed. For every 3,000 monsters slain, STR is increased by +1, up to a maximum of +34.

Given enough kills, this will become better than the Strength Ring, without its downsides. It also has a token DEF boost of +2.

## Availability
This ring can be found in the Monastery. To find it, Shanoa must first absorb the Cubus glyph. This can be achieved by going to the room will the falling rocks and destroying enough of them at once, giving her enough time to absorb the glyph (a simple way of doing this, is by activating a double Ignis Glyph Union). Once this has been accomplished, the number of falling rocks won't be infinite anymore and Shanoa may now keep destroying them through the wall until all are gone. Once the last of them is taken care of, a blue treasure chest will emerge containing the Justice Ring.
