# Courtyard

The **Courtyard** is the fourteenth area Simon explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. It is here that the Combat Cross is recovered and the Necromancer is fought.

## Related stages
- Ballroom - The previous stage for Simon.
- Library - The next stage on Simon's journey. He will return to the Courtyard from the Library. Once he obtains the Combat Cross, he can backtrack throughout the castle to areas he has been to. To advance to the next stage, go back to the Library and enter the Toy Maker's Funfair.
