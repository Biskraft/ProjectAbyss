# Kiss of the Demon

The **Kiss of the Demon** (or **Drain Kiss**) is an attack made by the Succubus. In some games, she is able to "blow kiss" damaging hearts. She may also grab an opponent and drain them of their vitality with a prolonged kiss.

In *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*, this is a spell that can be performed by the Succubus which turns all of the opponent's succeeding blocks into inactive blocks for the next 20 seconds.
