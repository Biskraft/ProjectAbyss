# Traveler's Hat

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">[[Eric Lecarde wearing a Traveler's Hat</p>]]

The **Traveler's Hat** (also called the **Wizard Hat** in the North American version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*) is a wide-brimmed hat with a feather made for travelers. It is the most valuable hat in *Symphony of the Night* and *Harmony of Despair* that male characters can wear (though female characters can also wear it).
