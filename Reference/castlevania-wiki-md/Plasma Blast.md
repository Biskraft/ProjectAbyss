# Plasma Blast

right|324px
  - Plasma Blast** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Lightning Doll. It is one of the most powerful bullet souls for cost in the game, shooting out long beams of lightning directly in front of Soma, which creates a wide area of damage and inflicts more hits on enemies (up to 3 hits) the closer they are to him. It is fairly costly at 46 MP per use, but very few souls can match it for cost-versus-damage.

A similar spell appears in *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*, simply called **Lightning**.
