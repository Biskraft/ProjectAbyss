# Emerald

The **Emerald** is a green jewel in *Castlevania*. It often appears in a ring (the **Emerald Ring**) and is associated with the Thunder element and Intelligence.

## Characteristics
The Emerald in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* is an item that by itself does nothing. It's needed to complete Laura's "A Pleasant Accessory" quest. If you bring her an Emerald, a Ruby and a Sapphire, she'll make available the Emerald Ring (greater Thunder offense), Emerald Pins (greater defense against Thunder), and the rings and pins of the other respective jewels brought to her at Jacob's shop.

## Other Emerald items
Allows Alucard to absorb Thunder-based attacks.

The Emerald Cloak in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, is the cloak that gives the most amount of DEF which only increases DEF.

The Emerald Corset is the only corset in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* that also gives an INT boost in addition to a DEF boost.

The Deep-Emerald Bracelet can be worn by characters in *Castlevania Judgment* and has no in-game effect.
