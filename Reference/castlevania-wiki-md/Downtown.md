# Downtown

right|300px

  - Downtown** is one of the major areas of Castlevania City in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.

## Overview
This section of the city is where legitimate businesses and housing for the citizens is located. Zobek's Emporium can be found here. It also houses the Basilica, the home of the city's central religious authority.

The Downtown area is built on the remains of Carmilla's Lair.

This section is also the one which houses the smallest number of secrets in the game.
