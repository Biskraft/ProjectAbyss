# Attribute Potion

- Attribute Potions** are one time use potions that alter the effect of an attribute or other damage type. In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, these potions cause one to be healed by attacks of a certain type. In *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*, these potions increase one's affinity to a certain element, although they do not help unlocking spells of that given element.
