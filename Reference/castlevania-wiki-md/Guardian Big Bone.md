# Guardian Big Bone

right|296px
  - Guardian Big Bone** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Creaking Skull's soul. When equipped, a Creaking Skull's arm wielding a gigantic femur will come out from Soma's body, effectively covering most areas around him, especially behind. When commanded, this arm will unleash a powerful swing with the femur, covering more than half of the screen.
