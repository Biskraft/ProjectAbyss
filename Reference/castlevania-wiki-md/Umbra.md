# Umbra

right|250px**Umbra** is a Darkness glyph.

  - Vol Umbra** allows Shanoa to shoot orbs of Darkness energy, although the range is limited. It can be found in the Mystery Manor, in the second room left of the Save Point and the boss door. The area where it is found is completely devoid of light, with almost no visibility whatsoever. The only visible objects are moving, spiked platforms and the glyph itself. Once the glyph has been absorbed, the room will illuminate.

Vol Umbra is especially useful in the fight against Albus.
