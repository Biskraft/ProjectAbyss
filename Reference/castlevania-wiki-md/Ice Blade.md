# Ice Blade

right|250px

  - Ice Blade** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Frozen Shade's soul and grants Soma the ability to summon a series of ice stalagmites that travel forward to impale ground enemies.
