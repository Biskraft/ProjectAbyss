# Bullet Soul

- Bullet Souls**, also known as **<span style="color:red;">Red Souls</span>**, are one of the four main types of tactical souls. They allow Soma Cruz to perform an instantaneous attack or action at a one time MP cost. Most of them act like other sub-weapons do in other games, although there are some notable exceptions.
