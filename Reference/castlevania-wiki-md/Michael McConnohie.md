# Michael McConnohie

- Michael D. McConnohie** is an American voice actor who is known for his recognizable deep booming voice as charismatic characters. He is well known for his works as the Narrator in *Bobobo-bo Bo-bobo*, Uther the Lightbringer and Kel'Thuzad in *Warcraft* series.

Michael voiced Rinaldo Gandolfi in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and Barlowe in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.
