  - Gagarin** is an enemy in *Kid Dracula*. He is an astronaut who flies straight up the screen with the aid of a jet pack on his back.

## Origins
Yuri Alekseyevich Gagarin was a Russian pilot and cosmonaut. He was the first human to journey into outer space, when his Vostok spacecraft completed an orbit of the Earth on April 12th, 1961.

## Description
A cosmonaut equipped with a rocket pack who appears in the Space Elevator stage. He flies up to the center of the screen, hovers in the air for a few seconds and then flies away to the top of the screen.
