# Tom Wyner

- Thomas Halperin Wyner** is an English-born voice actor. He voiced Death in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* and *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, and also voiced True Dracula in the latter game. He was later replaced in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* by Patrick Seitz.

Tom is famous for his work as M. Bison in both *Street Fighter II: The Animated Movie* and *Street Fighter II V*, the Puppet Master in *Ghost in the Shell*, and Master Vile in *Mighty Morphin Power Rangers*.
