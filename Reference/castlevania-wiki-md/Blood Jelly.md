# Blood Jelly

The **Blood Jelly** is an enemy in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.

## Description
In the Castle Center, the player eventually comes across the statue of an angel; when approached, a cutscene takes place where the divine figure starts shedding tears of blood. These tears form a puddle in front of it from where a new enemy is introduced: the **Blood Jelly**.

The Blood Jelly is an entity that uses blood as medium in order to take shape. These creatures can collapse into puddles of blood on the ground to avoid attacks. They can then rise again, forming a humanoid shape, and spew poisonous blood at the player.

Blood Jellies in *Legacy of Darkness* are twice as resilient than those encountered in *Castlevania* (N64).
