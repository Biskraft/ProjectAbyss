# Greenhouse

right|250px

The **Greenhouse **is an inner garden and part of the Overlook Tower wing of Dracula's Castle in* Lords of Shadow 2*. It is full of red-leaved trees.

This is where Agreus has made his home, waiting for Dracula to seek revenge for the death of his brother, Pan.

Dracula would travel here to find a piece of the Mirror of Fate and does battle with Agreus. Before the fight, Dracula must evade the Old God through the garden.

## Trivia
- This sequence is regarded by many as the worst part of the game due to its awkward controls and its stealth mechanics being perceived as too restrictive, as well as Agreus' tenacity.
- The red leaves mirror Agreas, who is a god of Death. Thus, the greenhouse is trapped in an eternal autumn.
