# Force Cannon

- Force Cannon** is an ability in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*
This is the skill that is used when combining the Dagger Sub-Weapon with the Green Orb.

### *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*
right
Force Cannon is an Innocent Devil ability which can be learned by the Gargoyle evolution of the Bird-Type. It's a potent offensive ability with the only drawback of having a slow fire rate. One cast of this ability allows the I.D. to form a Magic Circle in front and shoots three consecutive laser beams. The lasers have a long reach and are Dark-elemental, making this an useful ability against Nuculais.
