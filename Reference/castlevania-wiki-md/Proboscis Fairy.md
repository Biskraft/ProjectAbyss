# Proboscis Fairy

right|200px

The **Proboscis Fairy** is a very special Level 4 Fairy-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It resembles a slightly overweight middle-aged man with torn fairy wings kept together with scotch tape, and wears a hat with what appears to be a pair of antennae.

## Description
The Proboscis Fairy is an important Innocent Devil worth spending the effort it takes to evolve it. His Press It And See ability gives access to a whole new area in Aiolon Ruins, where many valuable treasures can be found. However, his other abilities are not as useful, as his Just A Little ability requires gambling constantly until giving Hector a major heal, Refresh only cures Hector from status ailments, which doesn't occur that often, and Skull Bomb only damages surrounding enemies minimally and can also damage Hector if he doesn't block or get away from the blast. However, Skull Bomb can be very useful during the battle against Death, as it will reduce 25% of his HP bar with a single blast.

## Availability and evolution
thumb|250px|**Proboscis Fairy**'s [[Innocent Devil Chart|I.D. Chart]]

The Proboscis Fairy can be obtained by evolving a Honey Bee with 90 **<span style="color:white;">Special</span>** Evo Crystals. Since he is a Level 4 Devil, he cannot be evolved any further.

## Trivia
- A proboscis is an elongated appendage from the head of an animal, such as a big nose or a snout. This explains why the Proboscis Fairy wears a large fake nose on his face.
- The big fake nose is also a reference to the N Demon familiar from *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, a special version of Demon Familiar appearing in the Japanese and *The Dracula X Chronicles* versions of this game, and who wears "funny" nose glasses, essentially having the same function of pressing switches beyond the player's reach.
- The term "press it and see" is a nod to a phrase the Devil Familiar says in *Symphony of the Night*, where he also has the ability to press a button out of the player's reach ("Hmm... a switch. Why don't I press it and see?").
- The big Skull Bomb the Proboscis Fairy sets, appears to be a nod to another big bomb the player can encounter in Stage 4 of *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*, and which also destroys a big wall to give access to a whole new area of the level.
- This is the only Fairy-Type evolution that can learn four abilities (which is also the highest among the other Innocent Devil evolutions).
  - Also, this is the only Fairy-Type evolution that gains DEF and AGI statistics per level.
- Proboscis Fairy's voice doesn't have alternative cast in English, making him speaks Japanese regardless of the voice language used. His voice actor isn't revealed in the staff roll.
