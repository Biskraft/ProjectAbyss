# Rondo of Blood Stage 6

Stage 6 of *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* is called **A Nightmare Reborn** (if playing as Richter) or **Undying Melody** (if playing as Maria). It consists of a battle against Shaft and four bosses from the original *Castlevania* which he summons via a pentagram.
