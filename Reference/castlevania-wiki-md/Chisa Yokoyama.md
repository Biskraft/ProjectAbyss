  - Chisa Yokoyama** is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is most known for her role of Lucrezia Noin in *New Mobile Report Gundam Wing*.

Yokoyama voiced Maria Renard in the Japanese version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.
