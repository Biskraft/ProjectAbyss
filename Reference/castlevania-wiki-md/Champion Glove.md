# Champion Glove

The **Champion Glove** is a Knuckle weapon in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be obtained by combining a Knuckle Duster with a Sports Jersey.

It is one of the weakest weapons in the game (ATK +2), only surpassed in this regard by the Piko Piko Hammer (ATK +1). Due to the complexity and the rarity of some of the materials needed throughout its creation, it can be considered to be a "failed" or "joke" weapon, with no real purpose other than to have a complete weapon set.
