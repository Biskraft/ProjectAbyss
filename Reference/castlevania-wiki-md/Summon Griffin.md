# Summon Griffin

right|350px
Summons a Griffin which brings along with it a crescents of wind throughout the screen, causing Wind damage to all enemies on the screen. This can be performed in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* by utilizing the Uranus + Griffin DSS combination.
