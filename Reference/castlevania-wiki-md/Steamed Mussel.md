# Steamed Mussel

thumb|150px|<p style="text-align: center;">*Harmony of Despair* gamerpic.</p>
  - Steamed Mussel** is a kind of food that recovers health. In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, it is the only food item that increases both CON (+5) and MND (+5), as well as recover HP (+50). It recovers as much HP as Quiche, which offers no additional statistical benefit.
