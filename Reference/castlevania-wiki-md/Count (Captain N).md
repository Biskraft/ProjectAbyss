# Count (Captain N)

- The Count** is a recurrent antagonist in *Captain N: The Game Master*, appearing in several episodes. He is loosely based on Count Dracula, the main antagonist from the *Castlevania* series, although he is never referred to by this name.

## Powers and abilities
Being a vampire, the Count is presumably immortal; he's known to have fought against Trevor Belmont several years ago (probably centuries), way before Simon's era. He is knowledgeable in the arts of magic and can transform into a bat at will, as well as animate and control undead creatures. He physically cannot withstand sunlight, however, so he's always seen either indoors or during nighttime.

## Trivia
- Apparently, the name "Dracula" couldn't be used in the series due to legal reasons, so he had to be referred to simply as "The Count".
