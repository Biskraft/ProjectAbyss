  - Lone Challenger** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is identified as the background theme plays in the starting room of each course in the game's Boss Rush Mode.

The track is notable for inclusion of several of the same instrumentals that are heard in Scarlet Battle Soul, a boss theme featured in Dawn of Sorrow.
