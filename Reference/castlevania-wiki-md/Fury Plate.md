# Fury Plate

The **Fury Plate** (called **Rage Armor** or **Breastplate of Anger** in Japan) is an armor in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* that increases your Defense power for a while after you take damage. It normally grants a +13 Defense boost and an unusual +1 Attack boost.
