# Onyx

- Onyx** is a jewel found in *Castlevania*, oftentimes found in a ring (sometimes called the **Onyx Ring**), and is associated with the Dark attribute.

Its first appearance was in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* as a salable ring. It could be sold to the Master Librarian for 3,000 Gold, which is more valuable than a Turquoise (1,500 G) and less valuable than Garnet (5,000 G). Onyx is rarely dropped by the Jewel Sword.

The Onyx in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* is an item that by itself does nothing. It is needed for Laura's *"A Heartwarming Accessory"* quest. If the player brings her an Onyx and a Diamond, she will make available the **Onyx Ring** (greater Dark offensive power), Onyx Pins (greater defense against Dark), Diamond Ring, and Diamond Pins at Jacob's shop.
