# Firearm

- Firearms** are any primary weapon that can fire an unlimited amount of projectiles without incurring a cost. These projectiles do not return, unlike Throwing Swords. Sometimes, these projectiles are not actually fired from a weapon like bullets, but rather, represent simply an unlimited supply of some other kind of throwing weapon.
