 can be entered by heading west from the lower path of the Sadam Woods. At the end, Simon can summon a gypsy with Garlic, who will give the player a Silk Bag. This bag increases the total amount of Laurels Simon is able to carry. There is nothing beyond this graveyard, so the player has to turn back.
