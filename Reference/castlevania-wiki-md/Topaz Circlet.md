# Topaz Circlet

The **Topaz Circlet** is a circlet made of topaz in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It gives Alucard HP whenever he is dealt Poison-based hits. This also enables Alucard to eat poisonous food and receive HP for them, such as the Toadstool and Smelly Rice (Sega Saturn only).

The Topaz Circlet also gives an INT boost of 10, which is two more than the Cat-Eye Circlet, two less than the Silver Crown, and the same as the Ruby Circlet, the Opal Circlet and Beryl Circlet.
