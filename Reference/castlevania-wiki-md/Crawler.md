The **Crawler** is an enemy in *Castlevania Chronicles*. It is a giant caterpillar-like creature that attacks with an extendible tongue.

## Description
  - Crawlers** are giant caterpillar-like creatures only found in the Courtyard level in *Castlevania Chronicles*. They normally crawl hugging the ground and are very tough. When Simon is within range, however, they will bend upward to be as tall as a man and will stick out their long tongues in an attempt to strike him. While they are in the upright position, their underbelly becomes exposed and they can be damaged more easily.
