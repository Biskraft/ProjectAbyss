# Climbing Skeleton

The **Climbing Skeleton** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series.

## Description
Skeletons that throw bones while climbing up and down ropes or walls that appear in *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge* and *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*.

## Appearances
### *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge*


### *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*
The ropes the Climbing Skeleton climbs are the only ropes that appear in *The Adventure ReBirth* and it's not possible for the player to climb them as they could in the original *Castlevania: The Adventure*.

## Trivia
- Climbing Skeletons appear in *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*, a remake of *Castlevania: The Adventure* originally released on the Game Boy; however, this enemy didn't appear in that game and instead it made its first appearance in *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge* for that same system.
