# Onikiri

right|116px

  - Onikiri** is a weapon in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* that is said to have killed a demon. There are two similar weapons, Kunitsuna and Yasutsuna, with Onikiri being the weakest one out of three, having ATK +25.

Like the Kunitsuna and Yasutsuna (as well as the Muramasa), Onikiri can hit twice on the ending part of the attack if the B button is held down.
