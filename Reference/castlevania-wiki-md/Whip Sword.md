# Whip Sword

right|50px
The **Whip Sword** is an extendable sword with a whip-like blade, probably a reference to the Vampire Killer or to whips in general, which have been one of the most notorious features in the *Castlevania* universe.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
It is found in the Study, in a room behind a door which opens only when you're facing away from it, use back dash to get through (this room is located opposite to the next boss room). It is one of the most powerful weapons Soma can find at the time of its acquisition, and it can prove to be very useful against the next boss, the Great Armor. Even when you will be able to find and purchase more powerful weapons later, it can also prove to be extremely useful against other bosses, like the Big Golem, who can be found at the Dance Hall, as its long reach allows Soma to inflict damage at a somewhat safe distance. The Whip Sword has the longest range for forward pointing weapons. Other than being extendable and being called the Whip Sword, it's really not all that different from any normal sword.
