# Melon

A **Melon** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It gives considerably higher amount of HP than any other fruit in the games it appears in, being listed as more healthy than a Strawberry (which was originally the highest HP fruit in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*). It appears in *Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Portrait of Ruin*. In *Aria of Sorrow*, it recovers 600 HP, making it 2nd best food item, being before Beef Curry (800 HP), and behind Melon being Ramen (500 HP),

A Melon can be found in the Study in Aria of Sorrow. It is dropped by human-like enemies who like to eat this fruit, such as a Witch in Aria of Sorrow, Erinys in Dawn of Sorrow, and the Invisible Man in *Portrait of Ruin*. It is also dropped by Alura Une in *Aria of Sorrow*, whose plant properties may produce melons.
