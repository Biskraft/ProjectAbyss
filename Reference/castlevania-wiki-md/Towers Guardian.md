The **Towers Guardian** is an enemy in *Castlevania Legends*.

## Description
This creature acts exactly like the Climbing Skeleton from *Belmont's Revenge*; that is, it's able to attack while hanging on ropes, although instead of throwing bones, it attacks by spewing a fireball in a straight line when it is in the direction of the player.
