# Dark Desires

- Dark Desires** (also known as **Dancing in Phantasmic Hell** or **Second Struggle**) is the standard boss theme for *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*, as well as one of the three boss themes for *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.

It was rearranged into the song Tues Deus Meus in *Rondo of Blood*'s remake *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*.
