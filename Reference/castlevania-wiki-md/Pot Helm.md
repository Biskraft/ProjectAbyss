# Pot Helm

The **Pot Helm** is a helmet in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It resembles an upside-down pot.

It grants 24 DEF, which is one more than the Cabacete, one less than a Silver Tiara, and two less than a Steel Helmet.
