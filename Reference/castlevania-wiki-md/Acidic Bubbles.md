# Acidic Bubbles

right|280px
  - Acidic Bubbles** is one of Charlotte Aulin's spells. When cast, it creates poisonous bubbles.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Acidic Bubbles is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Portrait of Ruin*. When cast, poisonous bubbles are created.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
While not available to the player in this game, Brachyura, one of the game's bosses, can perform this attack when faced in the Lighthouse.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
Charlotte Aulin is able to learn this spell by blocking an attack by a Glasya Labolas.

Soma Cruz is able to use the same attack by using a Glasya Labolas's soul.
