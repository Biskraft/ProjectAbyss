# VIP Card

The **VIP Card** is a special discount card given only to best customers in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. Jacob presents it to Shanoa after she has spent at least 100,000G at his shop. Once obtained, all item prices will have a 20% discount.

## Trivia
- V.I.P. is an enchanted soul which grants a similar effect in *Aria of Sorrow*.
- This system is comparable to how Shop Points worked in *Portrait of Ruin*, although while in *Portrait of Ruin* the player could check the amount of Shop Points they had accumulated so far, there's no way to check how much accumulated gold the player has spent in *Order of Ecclesia*.
