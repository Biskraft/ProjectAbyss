# Zobek's Emporium

right|300px

  - Zobek's Emporium** is the tower base of the Lord of the Dead, Zobek. Built many years ago by Zobek impersonating as a wealthy businessman. Located in the Downtown area, it can be accessed by a elevator from the parking lot, though it is scarcely explored.

In modern times, Zobek poses as his own descendants. With the help of his Lieutenant, Zobek has been able to influence the city for years and track down the Acolytes while Dracula slept. Zobek would use portals to send Dracula on missions from here, and it is here where Raisa Volkova is imprisoned after her capture. After Raisa is injected with her own antidote and takes her true form, she destroys the top floors of the emporium.
