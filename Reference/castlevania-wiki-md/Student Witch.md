# Student Witch

The **Student Witch** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. She is an immature witch-in-training who is still learning how to cast spells properly and can sometimes land her broomstick without crashing.

## Description
Witches who have not completely mastered the art of flying or landing their broomsticks. They often have shaky takeoffs and crash to the ground. When on the ground, they will take their broomsticks and whack their opponents with it. They are also accompanied by cat familiars or may turn into one on defeat.
