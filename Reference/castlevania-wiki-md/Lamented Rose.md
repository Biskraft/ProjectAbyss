  - Lamented Rose** is a music track that is featured in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.

It is identified as Rosa's second theme music, when she tries to end her own life by exposing herself to sunlight. The somber melody possibly represents her suffering from being stuck as a vampire.
