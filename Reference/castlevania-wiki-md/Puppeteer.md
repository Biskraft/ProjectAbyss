# Puppeteer

The **Puppeteer** used his spell to bond human soul into former castle retainers to create the Skeleton Warriors of the Vampire Castle.

## Trivia
- In *Mirror of Fate*, there is the Toy Maker who through his alchemical research was able to bond souls into puppets.
- In *Lords of Shadow 2*, there is the Puppet Master, a giant robot figure created by the Toy Maker for controlling his puppets in Walter Bernhard's theatre.
