# BradyGames

- BradyGames** is a US strategy guide publishing company and a subsidiary of DK Publishing. It has published many *Castlevania* official strategy guides.

## History
BradyGames was a publishing company in the United States operating as a DK imprint, which specializes in video game strategy guides, covering multiple video game platforms. It published their first strategy guide in November 1993 as a division of MacMillan Computer Publishing. In 1998, Simon & Schuster (which acquired Macmillan in 1994) divested BradyGames as part of its educational division to Pearson plc. BradyGames has grown to publish roughly 90-100 guides per year. On June 1, 2015, BradyGames merged with Prima Games, and future strategy guides made by the publishing company would be published under the Prima Games label.
