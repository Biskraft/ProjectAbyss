The **King Moth** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

## Description
Giant moths that fly slowly through the air. They are generally found in numbers and are usually found inhabiting underground storage places.

They sprinkle poisonous dust on their path, although fortunately they also often drop Antidotes to counteract its effect.
