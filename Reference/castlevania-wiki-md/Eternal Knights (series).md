    - Eternal Knights*** is a series of strategic role playing medal arcade games with slot machine elements, based in turn on the *Monster Gate* video game franchise.

## *Eternal Knights 2*
    - Eternal Knights 2*** introduced a new class of warriors called Vampire Hunters, which are based on the character designs of Richter Belmont, Maria Renard, and Shanoa.

## *Eternal Knights 3*
The third installment of *Eternal Knights*, also includes vampire hunters based on Richter's design.
