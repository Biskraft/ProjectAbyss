# Shoulder Ride

right
  - Shoulder Ride** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is learned by the Iytei evolution of the Battle-Type.

With this ability, Hector can ride the furry shoulders of the Iytei, giving the player control over the Innocent Devil. Pressing the attack button will cause Iytei to manually swipe nearby enemies. However, the player must be cautious and must not allow the I.D. to absorb large amounts of damage as Hector can be immediately knocked back.

This ability also grants access to a secret area in the Forest of Jigramunt by riding Iytei onto the pillars of bust statues arranged in a bowling like minigame fashion that block a door.
