# Fluffy White Rabbit Tail

The **Fluffy White Rabbit Tail** and **Fluffy Black Rabbit Tail** are body accessories in *Castlevania Judgment*. They can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character.

## Item Data


## Trivia
- The description of the **Fluffy White Rabbit Tail** saying to pair this garment with the matching [bunny] ears and "imagine you're serving cocktails at an exclusive club" is a reference to the Playboy Bunnies from the *Playboy* franchise.
