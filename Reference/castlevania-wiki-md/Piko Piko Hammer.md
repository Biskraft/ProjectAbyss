# Piko Piko Hammer

60px|right

The **Piko Piko Hammer** is a weapon in the *Castlevania* series. It is a strange hammer that makes an odd noise when it hits.

In *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, the only effect that it provides is to change the sound of the whip.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, it's a one-handed axe. It is the weakest among the axes, only increasing ATK by 1.

## Trivia
- The Piko Piko Hammer is a reference to a popular Japanese children's toy of the same name.
