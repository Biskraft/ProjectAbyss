# Reverse Outer Wall

The **Reverse Outer Wall** (異界側外壁 *Underworld Side Outer Wall*) is a copy of the normal Outer Wall, except for the color tones and of course that it is upside down.

Not too much to tell about this place, you can find items in the same places as in the normal outer wall, the enemies are a lot tougher though. It is noticeably filled with Stone Skulls which cannot be destroyed.

It can first be reached from the Reverse Clock Tower. Traveling up the tower leads to the Forbidden Library to the right. Continuing to travel up leads to the Black Marble Gallery to the right.

Its theme is Finale Toccata.
