# Silk Hat

A **Silk Hat** is a hat that appears in the *Castlevania* series.

There is a variation called **Red Silk Hat** in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, giving +25 DEF, which is the same as Silver Tiara, but it makes it up by also giving +5 INT.

In *Castlevania Judgment*, the **Silk Top Hat** and the **Baby Blue Silk Hat** are head accessories that can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character.
