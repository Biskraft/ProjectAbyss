# Simon's Cloak

- Simon's Cloak** is a mantle owned by Simon Belmont passed down generations of Belmonts in* Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. The cloak is particularly noteworthy for having one of the highest boosts to one's health, if not *the *highest boost, in addition to possessing a fair boost to their combat prowess. It can be first obtained in the downloadable stage The One Who Is Many and can be worn by any character who uses the Vampire Killer.
