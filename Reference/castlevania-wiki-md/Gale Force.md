# Gale Force

right|250px

  - Gale Force** (**Emerald Swallow** in the Japanese version) is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.

It later reappeared as a sub-weapon in *Castlevania Judgment*.

The spell was used by Death while in the body of Curtis Lang in *Akumajō Dracula: Kabuchi no Tsuisoukyoku*
