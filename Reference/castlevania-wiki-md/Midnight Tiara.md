# Midnight Tiara

The **Midnight Tiara** is the stronger of the two tiaras in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* that gives a MND boost (+5) in addition to a standard DEF boost (+7). It is more powerful than the Clover Tiara, which gives a DEF boost of +1 and a MND boost of +2.

It is a four star item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
