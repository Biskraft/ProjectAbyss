# Silver Plate

right|50px

The **Silver Plate** (called also **Silver Armor**) is armor made of silver (sometimes called the **Silver Armor**). It is the weakest of the "precious materials" armors, often being a step up from the Steel Armor, but a step down from the Gold Armor in effectiveness.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it grants +6 Defense, which is one more than a Steel Cuirass, but two less than the Gold Plate.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it gives a +75 Defense boost, which is higher than Battle Armor (+62), but less than Ceramic Mail.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, the Silver Plate gives a Defense boost of +35, which is 4 more than the Steel Plate and three less than the Gold Plate. It also has the same amount of defense as the Blocking Mail, which sometimes parries attacks.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, it gives Jonathan a Defense boost of +18, which is more than an Iron Plate (+15), but less than a Gold Plate (+25)

In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, the Silver Plate gives +28 Defense, which is greater than Iron Plate (+18), but less than the Gold Plate (+40). It can be obtained Eugen will make them available in the shop if you give 3 Silver Ores for his **What the Blacksmith Does Best** quest, as well as Silver Leggings.
