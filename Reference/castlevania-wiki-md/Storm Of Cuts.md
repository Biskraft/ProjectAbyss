# Storm Of Cuts

- Storm of Cuts** is an area special skill exclusive to the Crissaegrim. Alucard throws the Crissaegrim in a circle around him at blistering speed, giving the appearance that the sword is swinging itself. This attack rapidly deals damage to all surrounding enemies. If Glaciem magic is activated, it will also freeze all enemies in the player's immediate vicinity.
