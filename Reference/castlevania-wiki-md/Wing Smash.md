# Wing Smash

right|300px

  - Wing Smash** is an ability Alucard can use in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Judgment*. When performed, Alucard will dash forward, inflicting damage to everyone who stands in his path. This ability was first introduced in *Symphony of the Night*, where it could only be performed while Alucard was in bat form. It was later reintroduced in *Judgment* as an offensive combo move.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
Wing Smash causes Alucard's bat form to dash across the screen at high velocity while dealing out damage. To perform it, a specific button sequence must be input (hold [JUMP] + ↑↖←↙↓↘→, release [JUMP]).

The Wing Smash ability varies depending on the version of the game. In the PlayStation version and ports, it has a fixed duration and Alucard will automatically leave bat form after a short dash. The dash can be extended by re-inputting the command for the spell during the previous one, allowing a Wing Smash to continue until Alucard either hits something or runs out of MP. In the Saturn version, the Wing Smash will not automatically end and can continue for as long as Alucard doesn't hit something or runs out of MP.

### *Castlevania Judgment*
In *Judgment*, Alucard's Wing Smash is limited to being just a special attack move with his sword. When the command is input, Alucard dashes forward at great speed while swinging his sword, ending the move facing in the opposite direction. A large bat accompanies the move for aesthetics.
