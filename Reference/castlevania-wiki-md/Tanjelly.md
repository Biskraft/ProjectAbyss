# Tanjelly

The **Tanjelly** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is a poisonous jelly-like monster that is resistant to physical attacks. 

## Description
Tanjellys are blobs of an unidentified viscous matter which crawl over floors, walls and ceilings. They tend to jump or lunge toward their prey when approached and inflict Poison status upon contact. They are highly resilient against physical attacks, but are notoriously weak against Strike, Fire and Electric damage types.

## Notes
- In Hard Mode, Tanjellys gain the ability to inflict both Poison and Curse status upon contact.

- Its name may be a pun on "tangle" and "jelly".
