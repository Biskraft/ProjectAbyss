# Stone Breath

right|250px

  - Stone Breath** is the ability to "breath" a continuous petrifying gray mist. It's a signature attack of the Catoblepas. Soma Cruz is able to gain this ability from a Catoblepas' guardian soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. When Soma uses Stone Breath, it is emitted from his hand rather than his mouth. He can continue to unleash gray mist as long as desired or until all MP runs out.

Stone Breath is the stone counterpart to Flame Breath and Poison Breath.

## Trivia
- Like Flame Breath, this ability is affected by Soma's speed before activating the soul. Therefore, using this soul right after walking forward, doing a comet kick, or dashing, will enhance the range of the first stone breath sprite.
