  - Chromatische Phantasie** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge*. It plays during Christopher's and Soleil's confrontation at the end of the second stage of Dracula's Castle.

The music is a direct reprise of the "Chromatic Fantasia and Fugue" by composer Johann Sebastian Bach.
