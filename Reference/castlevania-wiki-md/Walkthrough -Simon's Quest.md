## Different endings
The game includes three different endings, determined by how long it took the player to complete the game and how many continues were used. All three are variations of the same ending, but with different twists featured (more information here). The can be obtained by:
- Bad Ending: Complete the game in 15 days or more.
- Normal Ending: Complete the game in 8 to 14 days.
- Best Ending: Complete the game in under 7 days.
