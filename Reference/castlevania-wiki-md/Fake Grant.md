# Fake Grant

- Fake Grant** is a boss in the *Castlevania* series. He is a zombie impersonating Grant Danasty.

## Description
Fake Grant appears in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* as one of a trio of bosses in the Reverse Colosseum, and also in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* as part of the same trio in the Nest of Evil. He will be teamed up with Fake Trevor and Fake Sypha, but unlike Trevor, he will not be resurrected by Sypha if she is still alive when killed. He wields knives as weapons and crawls around on the ceiling of the arena.

He only has two attacks. The first being that he throws down a knife if directly over the player. The second being that he will wield a knife and jump off the ceiling, striking the player. If struck by any attack, he will fall off the ceiling, remain stunned on the ground for several seconds and then jump right back up there. When defeated, he can occasionally drop a bowl of Ramen Noodles in *Portrait of Ruin*.
