# Barrel Helm

right|73px
The **Barrel Helm** is a tube-shaped helmet with a T-shaped slit for the face.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* (called **Barrel Helmet**), it grants Defense of +18, which is one more than a Silk Hat and two more than a Bascinet, but one less than a Morion.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the Barrel Helm is the final evolution of the Corinthian Helmet and a series of helmets which doubles the Defense of the previous one after each evolution. It is evolved from a Thracian Helmet by adding Carbon Steel and a Knight's Vein, doubling its Defense from +8 to +16. This overtakes the Frog Mouth Helm (DEF +12) and the Sallet (DEF +14). However, the Barrel Helm cannot be further evolved and will be overtaken in terms of Defense when the Frog Mouth Helm evolves to a Lobster Tail Pod (DEF +18) by adding White Steel and later as the Sallet continues to evolve.
