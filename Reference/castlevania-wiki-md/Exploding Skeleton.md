# Exploding Skeleton

The **Exploding Skeleton**, also known as **Bomb Skeleton**, is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series.

## Description
Some varieties of blue skeletons have a fuse on top of their heads which will cause them to self-destruct either when the timer runs out, when they get near the player, or when taking damage. The ensuing explosion will destroy the skeleton as well as damage the player.
