# Bioquimek Corporation

thumb|264px

The **Bioquimek Corporation** is a powerful pharmaceutical company owned and operated by one of Satan's acolytes in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. The factory is located in the Science District of Castlevania City.

## First Bioquimek Corporation mission
The third mission of the game involves infiltrating the corporation. It is the first normal mission and the first one where the Book of Dracul is accessible.

## Second Bioquimek Corporation mission
After Dracula has defeated the Gorgon Sisters in the City of the Damned, Zobek gave him the task of finding an antidote for reverting Raisa Volkova back to her human form.
