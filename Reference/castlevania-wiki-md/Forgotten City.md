# Forgotten City

The **Forgotten City** is a location in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. This stage reuses most sprites from Sandy Grave.

## Overview
This stage continues the Egyptian theme from Sandy Grave, with many Flying Skulls and Mummies, along with the addition of Buster Armors and Armored Fleamen. The boss of the portrait is the Mummy Man. An interesting feature of this stage is that the entrance is hidden in the quicksand pit under a Poison Worm.
