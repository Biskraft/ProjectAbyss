# Takashi Yoshida

- Takashi Yoshida** is a video game composer from Konami.

He mainly composed the combat musics of *Aria of Sorrow*, but also composed a remix of Chapel of Dissonance exclusively for the album Castlevania: Circle of the Moon and Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance Original Soundtrack.
