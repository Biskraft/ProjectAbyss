# Cipher's Charm

The **Cipher's Charm** (**Sypha's Crystal** in the Japanese version) is a pendant from Juste Belmont's ancestor, Sypha Belnades, which appears in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It speeds up MP recovery rate.

It replaces the Mystic Pendant from *Symphony of the Night*, and is replaced by the Rune Ring in *Aria of Sorrow*.
