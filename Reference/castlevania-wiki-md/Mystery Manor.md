{{Q|The subject of local legends, this mansion was the scene of a number of bizarre murders. Supposedly burned down, it has more recently been host to a number of curious incidents...|In-game description}}
  - Mystery Manor** is a location in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is Albus's final hiding place and it is there where both Shanoa and him will have their final encounter. The events that lead to the game's main plot twist take place after the battle, where Albus confesses the real motives that made him act the way he did.

This stage borrows many sprites from Giant's Dwelling.
