# Ring of Vlad

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">**The Ring** from the Japanese *[[Castlevania II: Simon's Quest|Simon's Quest* instruction booklet.</p>]]

The **Ring of Vlad** is a relic in the *Castlevania* series. It is one of Dracula's worldly possessions and has been in the Count's family for hundreds of years.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*
Found in Laruba Mansion. The Ring is one of the five relics needed in order to gain access to the ruins of the Demon Castle and fight Dracula at the end of the game. Equipping it serves no function.

### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The Ring of Vlad is one of the relics needed to enter Shaft's chamber. It gives an Intelligence boost of 10.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*
The Ring of Vlad is one of the six relics needed in order to enter the ceremonial room. It increases LCK (which had been a function of the Eye of Vlad in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*).
