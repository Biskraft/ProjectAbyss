# Camera

right|50px

The **Camera** is a usable item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It's given to Shanoa by Marcel, who wants her to take pictures of certain creatures. Taking pictures with the camera brings up a big "2x2" grid and takes the picture of whatever creature is inside of it. Badly taken photos result in a useless item called Poor Photo, while a good photo results in the picture of any creature that is taken by the camera. Photos 1, 2 and 3 can be given to Marcel in exchange for gold, but he'll not accept Poor Photos; and to make things worse, you cannot drop or sell them.

## Notes
- Taking a picture of the Jersey Devil requires Shanoa to jump right in front of the flying monster and use the item while in mid-air. Any pictures of the monster done on ground will yield to a Poor Photo.
