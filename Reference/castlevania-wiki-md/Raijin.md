# Raijin

- Raijin** is an enemy in *Kid Dracula*. He is a Japanese god that attacks by throwing lightning. He first appears in the roller coaster area of the storm level. He is depicted as a young energetic boy, as opposed to the old Fujin. Raijin throws lightning bolts at Kid Dracula, while Fujin hurls wind at him, attempting to knock him off his platform.
