# Astral Ring

right|50px
The **Astral Ring** is a ring in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and *Harmony of Despair*. It causes the wearer to consume HP instead of MP when performing actions that would normally cost MP. This negates the problems posed by the Curse status in the games where the Astral Ring appears in, but be forewarned: having only the exact amount of HP needed to use an action that would cost the same amount of MP has been known to kill the wearer in the process.
