# Magician Ring

The **Magician Ring** is a ring in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* and represents the Tarot card "The Magician". Wearing it provides Shanoa a boost in INT of +5, at the expense of a reduction in STR of -2.

## Availability
This ring is hidden to the naked eye. It can be found in the depths of Kalidus Channel (Serpent Scale required). Shanoa must make her way to the sunken ship located at the bottom of Kalidus and arrive to what looks like an empty cavern on the left side of the ship, where a Specter lurks alone. If the player is observant, they'll notice a pair of suspiciously-looking torches near the middle of this cave. Shanoa must crouch (or slide) between these torches and a blue treasure chest will  emerge containing the Magician Ring.
