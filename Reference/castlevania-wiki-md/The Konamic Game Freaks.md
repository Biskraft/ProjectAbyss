# The Konamic Game Freaks

is a video game soundtrack CD composed and arranged by the Konami Kukeiha Club and Expo. It was recorded by G.M.O. Records and released on March 25, 1987 in Japan, with a reprint released on November 25, 2005.

It contains music arrangements from several Konami games, including one called "Akumajo Dracula Arrange Version".
