# Frozen Trio

:*"**Seal**" redirects here. Not to be confused with Magic Seal.*

The **Frozen Trio** are a triad of enemies in *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*. Their group is conformed by an **Eskimo**, a **Snowman** and a **Seal**.

## Description
Three enemies await Kid Dracula in the early parts of the cold environment of Stage 4:
    - Eskimo**: An innocent-looking, all bundled-up eskimo who just stays there shivering, although he will occasionally throw a couple of popsicles at the player.
    - Snowman**: Jumps from place to place and wiggles its hips a little.
    - Seal**: A playful mammal balancing a bomb on its nose, which it then sends to the player.
