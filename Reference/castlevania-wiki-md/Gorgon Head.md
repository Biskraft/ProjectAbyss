# Gorgon Head

- Gorgon Heads** are enemies in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. Created from the head of Medusa. Turns its victims into stone.

## Description
A gray variant of the Medusa Head which appears in the Mechanical Tower and the Forsaken Cloister. Turns the player into stone on contact. When killed, they will always drop the Fidelis Medusa glyph.

A Gorgon Head will only/always appear after five Medusa Heads (any variant) have been killed.

## Trivia
- Gorgon Heads, Medusa Heads and Medusa, all are Greek female creatures called Gorgons, despite the fact that in the series, the enemy called Gorgon is inspired by an [ox-like mythological creature](http://penelope.uchicago.edu/oddnotes/topsellgorgon.html) of Libyan origin.
