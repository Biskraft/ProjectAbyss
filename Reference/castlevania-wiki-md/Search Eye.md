# Search Eye

- Search Eye** is an Enchanted Soul in the *Castlevania* series. It allows the player to identify the location of breakable walls. It can be used by Soma Cruz by gaining dominance of a Peeping Eye's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
