# Special Ops

- Special Ops** is an armor that can be equipped by Hector in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is one of the weakest defensive items, providing only +2 to DEF. As such, it can be considered to be just a simple Easter egg combination and creating it should be avoided unless looking on acquiring a complete item set.

## Trivia
- This item resembles Soma Cruz's coat.
- The texts written on the coat translate to:
- #年金前借希望 *nenkin maegari kibō* (I hope for the loan of the pension).
- #好キトカ嫌イトカ 最初ニ言イ出したノハ 誰ナノカ死ラ *suki to ka kirai to ka saisho ni iidashita no ha dare nano kashi ra* ([I wonder who will be the first who says either "like" or "hate"](http://www.animelyrics.com/game/tokimemo/tommmt.htm)). These lyrics belong to *Tokimeki Memorial*'s opening theme song.
