# Staurolite

Green ring made of **Staurolite** with a cross carved on it in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. This mineral often has crystals that form in the shape of a cross. It can strengthen the power of the Cross sub-weapon in Castlevania.
