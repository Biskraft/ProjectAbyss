# Sapphire Pins

The **Sapphire Pins** are hair pins made of Emerald in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They reduce the amount of damage taken from Ice based attacks. They can be purchased once you perform Laura's "A Pleasant Accessory" quest by giving her an Sapphire, a Ruby, and an Emerald.
