# Uranus (card)

The **Uranus Card** is a DSS Action Card in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It causes Nathan to summon the creature portrayed on a paired Attribute Card. This creature usually deals the type of damage associated with its attribute to enemies in a wide area.
