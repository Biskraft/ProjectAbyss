# Slinger

- Slinger**, also known as **Skeleton Rib**, is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is a skeleton that attacks by throwing its own ribs.

## Description
Slingers are specialized bone-throwing skeletons. They wear a Viking Helmet and throw their own ribs in a low arc. They tend to throw their rib bones at a faster, more consistent pace than normal bone-throwing skeletons.
