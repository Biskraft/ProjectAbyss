# Adventure Bestiary

A table of enemies that appears in the game *Castlevania: The Adventure*. Enemy names are as appear in the English manual when available and in the Game Boy Nintendo Player's Guide when not. Japanese or alternative names from the player's guide are in parentheses. Order of enemies is as it appears in the Japanese manual.
