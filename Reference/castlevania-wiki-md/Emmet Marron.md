# Emmet Marron

Dr. **Emmet Marron** is a mentioned character in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. He was a scientist who was studying the influence of time. Another character, Dr. Friedrich von Frankenstein, was inspired by his work.

## Trivia
- Emmet Marron is a reference to Emmett "Doc" Brown from the *Back to the Future* film series.
  - In addition, his name *Marron* means "brown" in French.
