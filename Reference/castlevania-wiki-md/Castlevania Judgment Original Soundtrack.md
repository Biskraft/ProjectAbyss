# Castlevania Judgment Original Soundtrack

- Castlevania Judgment Original Soundtrack** is the soundtrack for *Castlevania Judgment*. The music was composed by Yasushi Asada, Michiru Yamane, Kinuyo Yamashita and Kenichi Matsubara. Every song has been arranged by Yasushi Asada. It was published by Konami Digital Entertainment. It was first released on January 15, 2009.

## Additional information
- Tracks 1-3, 17-20, 25, 26: Composed for *Castlevania Judgment*.
- Tracks 4-16, 21-24: Arranged tracks from the *Castlevania* series.

## Trivia
- Dracula's theme, "Dance of Illusions" sounds different on the game. It has a "chorus" style effect on the main notes.
- There are many tracks that do not feature in the Official Soundtrack, but were found in the game's data: "Arduous Journey" that is an arrange of the same theme from *Castlevania: Bloodlines*, a more rock oriented version of "Beginning", an alternate "Game Over" theme that resembles the one from *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*, and the respective themes from the story intros for heroes and villains.
