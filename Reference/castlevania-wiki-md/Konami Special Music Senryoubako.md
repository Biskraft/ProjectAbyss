# Konami Special Music "Senryoubako"

is a 3 CDs soundtrack that contains music from several Konami games. It was composed by Konami Kukeiha Club and published by Konami, and was released on December 21, 1989 in Japan.

- Akumajō Dracula*'s ending "Voyager" from Konami Ending Collection appears on Disc 2.
