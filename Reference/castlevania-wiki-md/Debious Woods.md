The  are a forest wasteland area in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. It connects the Uta Lower Road to the Joma Marsh. A river not marked on the map runs through it, which you must cross by jumping on floating logs (though this is easier than in some other rivers).

This is the only area where Flame Throwers can be encountered.
