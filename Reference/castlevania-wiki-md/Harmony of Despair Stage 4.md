  - Esquisse of Violence** is the fourth chapter in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It is an amalgamation of the City of Haze, Nation of Fools, Master's Keep and the Great Stairway from *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.

### Hidden item
right|250px

Goemon appears as the hidden item in this chapter. At the room right of the boss's room, there's a platform that juts out. Go there, and Goemon would pop out from the opposite side of the room.

### Paintings
In this stage there are various paintings players can jump into to quickly travel from place to place on the map. Once a player jumps into the panting using the RT Button, the painting will jump from the wall and fly to a special patterned wall in a different place in the stage

This is a reference to how in Portrait of Ruin, players would have to jump into paintings and traverse special maps within said painting.
