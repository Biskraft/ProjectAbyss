# Perfect Selection Dracula

- Perfect Selection Dracula** is an album containing rap arrangements of music from early *Castlevania* games. The arrangements were done by Nazo² Project and the chorus and lyrics were done by Ornell Jones and Joey McCoy. The CD was released on September 5, 1991 and is very rare.

## Additional information
- Nazo² Project made also arrangements for *Perfect Selection Dracula ~New Classic~*.
- Tracks 8, 10: from *Castlevania*.
- Tracks 3, 9: from *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.
- Tracks 1, 2, 4~7: from *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*.
- Sound Producer: Nazo² Project
- Guitar: Nazo Nazo Suzuki
- Arrange: Jun Irie
- Manipulator: Hideki Matsutake
- Chorus & Rap: Ornell Jones & Joey McCoy
