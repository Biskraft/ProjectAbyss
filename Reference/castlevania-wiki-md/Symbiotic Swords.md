# Symbiotic Swords

thumb|250px|<p style="text-align: center;">A charged Symbiotic Sword.</p>

The five **Symbiotic Swords** are Joachim Armster's primary weapons. These swords orbit and hover around him in two different patterns: either orbiting him, or clustered behind his back. When attacking, one of the swords will leave its orbit and strike its target. In Joachim Mode, a Symbiotic Sword can be "charged" by holding down the attack button, which increases its size and damage output. With sufficient magic, he can release a powerful beam from his swords or send them all out slashing everything depending on his stance.
