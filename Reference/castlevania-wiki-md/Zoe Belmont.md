# Zoe Belmont

- Zoe Belmont** is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*. She is the elder of Desmond's two sisters. She and Dolores enter the mansion ahead of Desmond in order to find the inner sanctum (and consequently the sacrificial altar).

## Trivia
- The names "Zoe" and "Dolores" may be inspired by the anime television series *Z.O.E.: Dolores,i*, set in the *Zone of the Enders* universe, another Konami franchise.
