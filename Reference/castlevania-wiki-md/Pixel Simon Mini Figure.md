# Pixel Simon Mini Figure

In promotion for the NECA Castlevania Action Figures, NECA released a limited edition "Pixel" Simon Mini Figure at the San Diego Comic Con of 2007. A *Simon's Quest* version was later offered as a pre-order bonus for *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*.
