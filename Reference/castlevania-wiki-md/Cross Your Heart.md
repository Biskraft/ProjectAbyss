# Cross Your Heart

, also known as **Crucifix Held Close** or **Cross on the Breast**, is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Haunted Castle*. It is the background theme of the first level of the game.

Arrangements of this track have appeared in some sequels and independent productions, as well as a few products not belonging to the *Castlevania* franchise, such as the mobile game *Pixel Puzzle Collection* and the fighting game *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate*.
