  - The Ruined Mansion** is a location in *Super Castlevania IV*. This stage is made up of four areas, the entrance, the courtyard, the manor, and the stables.

The inclusion of a mansion type stage invokes thoughts of *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. However, this stage's layout is considerably different from those in that game.

## Sub-stages
1-1: This area consists of two parts; the manor's entrance, which includes a moat and drawbridge, and a courtyard with a fence that rises from the ground at the beginning.

1-2: The ruins of the manor house reminiscent of the mansions from *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.

1-3: The ruins of the mansion's stables.
