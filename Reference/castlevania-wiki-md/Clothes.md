# Clothes

- Clothes** are a type of body armor found in *Castlevania*. They can generally be worn by anyone regardless of gender. They are usually composed of fabric of some kind.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*


### *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*
In *Circle of the Moon*, clothes tend to offer a Strength boost, along with a Defense boost.


### *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*


### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Clothes are the only type of Body Protection that can be worn by either Jonathan or Charlotte.
