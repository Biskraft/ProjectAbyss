# Red Phoenix Key

The  is a key that appears in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is found in the Garden Forgotten by Time and allows Leon to unlock the Red Phoenix Door in the Anti-Soul Mysteries Lab.

## Location
The key is found in the Garden Forgotten by Time, behind the door with the three triangle-shaped locks. One of the three statues that control the lock is well hidden in a secret facultative area of the level.

## Usage
The key opens the Red Phoenix Door in the Anti-Soul Mysteries Lab, leading to the Wolf's Foot relic. In turn, the Wolf's Foot allows to get the Megingjord on the same level (across the crumbling bridge) and the Saisei Incense in the Garden Forgotten by Time (in the fallen bridge room, in the secret area).
