# King's Stone

The **King's Stone** is a gemstone buckle which increases Strength and Attack by 10 in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is a reference to the Japanese *tokusatsu* television series *"Kamen Rider Black"*, whose hero wears a belt called the "King Stone" which gives him the power to transform into the titular Rider and fight the evil secret society "Golgom". It is not as effective as the Covenant Stone, which increases both stats by 15.
