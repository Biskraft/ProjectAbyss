# Poetic Death

- Poetic Death**, also known as , is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is the boss battle theme played during the Hippogryph, Olrox, Granfaloon, Beelzebub, Death, Shaft and Galamoth fights.

It later appeared in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* as the boss fight theme for the second downloadable stage, The One Who Is Many.
