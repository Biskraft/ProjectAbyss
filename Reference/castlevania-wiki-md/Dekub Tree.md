# Dekub Tree

The **Great Dekub** was once the largest and most gorgeous tree in the Enchanted Forest. It was so tall, that its canopy could be seen from many miles away. Some people even believed that it was alive, and that its magical power protected the the forest from the attack of dark creatures. It was destroyed during the Necromantic Wars. The Dark Lords seeing the tree as a symbol of life, cursed it with a plague that still remains alive, beneath its dead stump. The trunk remained forever blighted by the dark energies that destroyed it.

## Behind the scenes
The Great Dekub is a nod to the Great Deku Tree of the *Legend of Zelda* series.
