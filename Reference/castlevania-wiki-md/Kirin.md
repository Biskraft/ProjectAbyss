# Kirin

- Kirin** is a mystical Chinese lion who guards the entrance to the Robot Factory in *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun* and *Kid Dracula* (GB). He is flame footed, can "run" in the air, and is only vulnerable to one of Kid Dracula's weapons: the Ice Ball.
