# Shudder (music)

Shudder}}
  - Shudder** is a song originally composed for *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. It plays during most boss fights in the game, as well as the Villa chase. It was later rearranged for *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, where it was also used as the main theme for the Cerberus, Necromancer, Iron Golem and Adramelech boss fights.
