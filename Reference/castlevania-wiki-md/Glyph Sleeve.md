# Glyph Sleeve

right|300px

The **Glyph Sleeve** is a relic which appears in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is found inside a golden chest located at the very last room of Minera Prison Island and it allows Shanoa to switch between three equipped sets of glyphs (A, B or C), resulting in a much more dynamic style of gameplay as the player won't have to keep entering the menu to equip different glyphs individually.

Additionally, it also allows having certain sets already prepared for different situations, like having one Slash- and one Strike-based set ready for different enemies, each weak to one of these (or other) types of damage.

It is very similar to Quick Change from *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.
