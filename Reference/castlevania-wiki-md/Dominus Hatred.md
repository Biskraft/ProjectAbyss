## Overview
One of Dracula's (relatively recent) signature attacks, also known as "**Fatal Ray**". When cast, a large pillar of energy (usually green colored) is summoned, which then rains down across the room in smaller portions, damaging anything on their path.

This attack was first introduced in the original mode of *Castlevania Chronicles* as a special move of the second form of Dracula, and in some games the player can gain the ability to cast it themselves. It later reappeared in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, *Curse of Darkness*, *Portrait of Ruin*, *Order of Ecclesia*, *Judgment* and *The Adventure ReBirth*.

Using it in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* reduces health; each use consumes 1/6 of Shanoa's max HP. Combining it with the two other pieces of Dominus: Dominus Anger and Dominus Agony, to defeat Dracula is the only method to obtain the good ending of the game.
