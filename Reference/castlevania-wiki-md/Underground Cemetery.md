The **Underground Cemetery** is an optional area in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. Arguably, this area is the place with the least amount of rooms to explore in the game. No warp room can be found here and is exclusively accessed via the Underground Reservoir. Other than that, no explicit information is given.


## Overview
Accessing the Underground Cemetery, based from its position below the castle, is actually quite optional. The main reason for visiting this area is to get its main treasure: the Gold Ring, and also to gain access to Galamoth's soul (Time Stop Immunity) which can be obtained from a Soul-Keeper inside a room only accessible after defeating Legion. However, if the player is not in urge to get these items, it's actually recommended to explore this area later, after obtaining the Hippogryph's and Black Panther's souls from the Top Floor, both of which will help greatly in defeating Legion and exploring the whole area in a very short time.

Once Galamoth's soul has been acquired, Soma will be immune to the time stopping spell of the Chronomage, thus gaining access to the complex of infants' rooms located at the Inner Quarters.
