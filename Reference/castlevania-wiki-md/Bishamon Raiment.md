# Bishamon Raiment

The Bishamon Raiment is a Japanese coat that increases MP recovery speed.It is an item that only appears in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and is available in the downloadable stage *The Legend of Fuma*, along with the Ebisu Raiment.

It will increase your MP recovery speed by 0.6 per second. It is equal to the maxed out Treant soul. It's an awesome piece of equipment for magic users such as Shanoa, Charlotte, and Yoko.

## Trivia
- Bishamon (the japanese name of Vaiśravaṇa) is one of the Seven Lucky Gods in Japan. He is lent the faculty to bring success. He is the god of agriculture and wealth.
