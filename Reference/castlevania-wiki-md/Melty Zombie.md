# Melty Zombie

The **Melty Zombie** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

## Description
The reanimated decomposing carcass of a large unknown creature. Only the torso remains from the entire body, having its rib cage exposed, dripping bloated flesh and dragging its spinal cord behind. It comes out from the many large sewer ducts found in the background of the Aqueduct of Dragons, and judging by the many weapons that are impaled on its back, in life it probably was a dangerous monster that was slain by several warriors.

It attacks by crawling toward the player and occasionally spewing out new Melty Zombies. An easy way to build up EXP is to stand on one of the raised platforms in the room, wait for the Zombies to spew out their maximum number and let them gather together, and then drop and kill them all quickly.
