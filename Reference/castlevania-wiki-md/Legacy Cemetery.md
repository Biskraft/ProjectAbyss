# Legacy Cemetery

thumb|300px|<p style="text-align: center;">Official artwork from the comic *The Belmont legacy*, published by IDW Publishing, drawn by E.J. Su.</p>

The cemetery where the Belmont Clan members are laid to rest. Contains a mausoleum dedicated to this family. Artwork of Trevor Belmont and Simon Belmont are found here, in addition to full-sized statues of Leon Belmont and his successors. It is here that Christopher Belmont goes to to find the strength needed to face off against Dracula.

As soon as he's revived, Dracula comes here and vandalizes the place, defiling the corpses of Christopher's parents. After this attack, the townsfolk become convinced of Dracula's return and put up crosses, garlic and start nailing windows shut.
