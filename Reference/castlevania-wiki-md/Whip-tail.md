# Whip-tail

right|250px
  - Whip-tail** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It allows the player to attack with a tail.

## Characteristics
When used, Soma forms a thin, brown tail that lashes forward like a whip. It causes massive damage and has good range too; however, it can't be leveled up.

It can be obtained from Malacoda.

It can be used during Weapon Synthesis to craft the Muramasa by fusing it with a Yasutsuna.
