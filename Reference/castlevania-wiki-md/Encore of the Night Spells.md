# Encore of the Night Spells

## Spells (Arcade Mode)
### **Alucard**
- Hellfire - 12 MP - Instantly does 25 + (1% of opponent's max HP) damage to opponent.
- Soul Steal - 20 MP - Instantly does (25 - opponent's shadow affinity / 2) damage to opponent and heals caster for same amount.
### **Richter**
- Cross Toss - 8 MP - The player instantly does 10 damage to their opponent.
- Cross Crash - 50 MP - All blocks on the user's board are destroyed. The opponent takes 1 damage for each block destroyed.
### **Maria**
- Blessing - 10 MP - The next time the player takes damage, that damage is lowered by 25%. Does not stack with other damage reduction spells.
- Memories - 30 MP - The next time the opponent makes a block attack, it is fully countered. No blocks are dropped on the user's board.
### **Shaft**
- Force Field - 20 MP - Reduces damage done to the user from spells and abilities by 50% for 10 seconds.
- Orb of Sorcery - 25 MP - A block color is chosen at random. All blocks of that color are destroyed on both the user's board and the opponent's board. For each block destroyed on the user's board, they gain 2 HP. For each block destroyed on the opponent's board, they do 1 damage to the opponent.
### **Succubus**
- Succubus - Kiss of the Demon - 20 MP - For the next 20 seconds, the opponent's block spawns are all inactive blocks.
- Succubus - Delusional Visions - 40 MP - For the next 30 seconds, the opponent cannot break any blocks by matching. At the end of the 30 seconds, all blocks that are matched and would otherwise break are instead turned into inactive blocks.
### **Death**
- Sickle - 18 MP - Does 4 damage times the height of blocks on the opponent's board.
- Dark Energy - 30 MP - All black and red blocks on the user's board are destroyed. Does 3 damage per block destroyed to the opponent.

### **Dracula**
- Dark Metamorphasis - 25 MP - For the next 20 seconds, every block broken on the user's board does 2 damage to the oppoenent and heals 1 HP for the user.
- Teleport - 40 MP - All inactive blocks on the user's board become active blocks.
### **Librarian**
  - Unlocked after getting the Veteren Explorer Honor**
- Surplus Inventory - 20 MP - Spawns 2 random item blocks in the block chain.
- Tome of Knowledge - 5 MP - For the next 20 seconds. the amount of MP gained by destroying blocks is doubled.

## Spells (Story mode)

### Fire
- Hellfire Level 1 - Available at 5 Fire Affinity - 5 MP - Instantly does 10 damage to opponent.
- Hell on Earth Level 1 - Available at 8 Fire Affinity - 20 MP - Instantly destroys all red block's on the caster's board; the opponent takes 2 damage per block destroyed.
- Hellfire Level 2 - Available at 12 Fire Affinity - 12 MP - Instantly does 25 + (1% of opponent's max HP) damage to opponent.
- Hell on Earth Level 2 - Available at 16 Fire Affinity - 40 MP - Instantly destroys all red block's on the caster's board; the opponent takes 5 damage per block destroyed.

### Lightning
- Aquen Level 1 - Available at 5 Lightning Affinity - 10 MP - Opponent takes additional (12 + # of green blocks on opponent's board) damage on next hourglass flip.
- Static Level 1 - Available at 8 Lightning Affinity - 10 MP - For next 20 seconds any attack opponent makes results in them taking 2 damage per block dropped on your board.
- Aquen Level 2 - Available at 12 Lightning Affinity - 20 MP - Opponent takes additional (36 + # of green blocks on opponent's board * 0.2% of opponent's max HP)<br />damage on next hourglass flip.
- Static Level 2 - Available at 16 Lightning Affinity - 25 MP - For next 30 seconds any attack opponent makes results in them taking (5 + 0.5% of opponent's max HP) damage per block dropped on your board.

### Shadow
- Soul Steal Level 1 - Available at 5 Shadow Affinity - 10 MP - Instantly does (10 - opponent's shadow affinity / 2) damage to opponent and heals caster for same amount.
- Dark Metamorphasis Level 1 - Available at 8 Shadow Affinity - 15 MP - For next 10 seconds spawned block pairs have a 20% chance of containing a blood block.
- Soul Steal Level 2 - Available at 12 Shadow Affinity - 20 MP - Instantly does (25 - opponent's shadow affinity / 2) damage to opponent and heals caster for same amount.
- Dark Metamorphasis Level 2 - Available at 16 Shadow Affinity - 30 MP - For next 15 seconds spawned block pairs have a 30% chance of containing a blood block.

### Holy
- Maria's Blessing Level 1 - Available at 5 Holy Affinity - 10 MP - Next damage received is reduced by 25%.
- Lisa's Sacrifice Level 1 - Available at 8 Holy Affinity - 15 MP - Restores 10% of max HP.
- Maria's Blessing Level 2 - Available at 12 Holy Affinity - 25 MP - Next damage received is reduced by 50%.
- Lisa's Sacrifice Level 2 - Available at 16 Holy Affinity - 25 MP - Restores 25% of max HP.

### Water
- Time Freeze Level 1 - Available at 5 Water Affinity - 15 MP - Delays next hourglass flip by 5 seconds.
- Frostbite Level 1 - Available at 8 Water Affinity - 14 MP - For next 10 seconds opponent spawns only inactive blocks. Less effective on repeated casts and on bosses.
- Time Freeze Level 2 - Available at 12 Water Affinity - 35 MP - Delays next hourglass flip by 10 seconds.
- Frostbite Level 2 - Available at 16 Water Affinity - 30 MP - For next 20 seconds opponent spawns only inactive blocks. Less effective on repeated casts and on bosses.
