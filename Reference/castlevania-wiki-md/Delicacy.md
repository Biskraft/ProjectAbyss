# Delicacy

There are three "Great Delicacies" in Castlevania games.  These are Caviar, Truffles, and Foie Gras.  They first appeared in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, then *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, and then in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. They are the rarest kind of Food items to be found in the games and offer a significant amount of HP restoration (5 stars in the late game).

In *Harmony of Despair*, they give 200 HP, which is double the amount of the next highest food items.  They all increase LCK significantly and some also increase INT.
