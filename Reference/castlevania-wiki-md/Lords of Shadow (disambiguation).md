# Lords of Shadow (disambiguation)

- Lords of Shadow** may refer to:

- The game *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*, and its sequels, *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate* and *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.
- Lord of Shadow — The three antagonist characters from the *Lords of Shadow* saga: Cornell, Carmilla and Zobek. Dracula is also considered to be a Lord of Shadow.
