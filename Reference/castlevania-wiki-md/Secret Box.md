# Secret Box

- Secrets Boxes** appear in *Revelations*, and act like Pain Boxes. Each one increases the bars without having to collect gems. When used, Alucard will upgrade his health, Glaciem or Igneas magic.

The secret box's design and function differs from the pain box's. One example is that it does not require blood as well as no gems are offered to its user, another example is that it doesn't feature a monstrous representation but a more complex, ornamented one. However, both boxes float in mid air and disappear after being activated by whoever wishes to use its power.
