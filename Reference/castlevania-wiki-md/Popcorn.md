# Popcorn

Popcorn is a food item that recovers HP in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

It gives much more HP (+40) than any other one star rarity food items.  This is over twice as much as Fried Chicken or a Hot Dog (+3).  It also gives a token CON Boost (+1), which is one less than a Canelé or a Hamburger (which give HP +15) do.  It gives less HP than the two star Quiche (+50).
