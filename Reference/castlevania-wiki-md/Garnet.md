# Garnet

- Garnet** is a red Jewel that is sometimes associated with Fire. It appears in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, where it appears as a "valuable ring". It could be sold to the Master Librarian for 5000 Gold, which is more valuable than a Turquoise (3000 G) and less valuable than an Opal (8000 G). Garnet is rarely dropped by the Jewel Sword.
