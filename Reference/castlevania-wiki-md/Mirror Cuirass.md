# Mirror Cuirass

The **Mirror Cuirass** (sometimes called **Mirror Armor**) is an armor that is highly polished for mirror-like reflectiveness. It usually provides immunity against Stone (petrification).

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, the Mirror Cuirass gives a Defense boost of +3, which is the same as a Bronze Cuirass. It also provides protection against Stone, making it extremely useful when tackling either version of the Clock Tower.

In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, the Mirror Armor, it gives more Defense than any other armor that does not also give some other benefit. This is DEF +300, which is greater than the Diamond Armor) (+210). This is less defense than the Needle Armor (+400), which also gives a Strength boost of +10. The Mirror Armor does not provide protection against petrification in this game.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, the Mirror Cuirass gives Jonathan protection against Stone and offers a Defense boost of 16. This is one more than an Iron Plate and 2 less than a Silver Plate.

In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, the Mirror Cuirass gives protection against Stone and Defense +25. This is more defense than an Iron Plate (+18), but less than a Silver Plate (+25).
