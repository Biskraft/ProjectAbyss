# Kitchens and Storage Areas

right|300px

  - Kitchens** and **Storage Areas** are types of environments in the *Castlevania* series. They generally conform the main basement of the castle and are usually located immediately below a more luxurious area (such as a marble corridor), often being just damp and murky places devoid of any decoration that only function as a storage place for crates of wine, meat and other perishables.

## Appearances
  - Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* — Underground Warehouse
  - Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* — Dance Hall
  - Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* — Library
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* — Refectory
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate* — Kitchen
