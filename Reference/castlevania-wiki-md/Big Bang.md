# Big Bang

right

  - Big Bang** is a Bird-Type Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by a Phoenix by defeating a certain amount of enemies which are at Level 25 or above, while this I.D. is summoned. Costs 49 MP to cast.

## Description
Phoenix sacrifices itself and produces a big explosion that causes major damage to all enemies on the screen. However, this attack won't work against most bosses.

In order to make Phoenix learn Big Bang, the player must defeat a certain number of enemies which are at Level 25 or above. These start appearing in the Forest of Jigramunt (Great Armors only), but in Cordova Town almost all enemies are above the required level (except for Assassin Zombies).

## Notes
- This ability is not very useful, as it costs the Innocent Devil its life. Even if all enemies on the screen are instantly killed, it just provides a brief breather as the next set of enemies will spawn shortly, not to mention the long buildup this attack needs before it is unleashed.
  - However, this attack is extremely useful during Isaac's fight in Cordova Town, as Hector can target Abel and let Phoenix kill it instantly as the battle starts, greatly reducing the difficulty of this encounter.
- If Phoenix "died" after using Big Bang, it can be revived once Hector rests at the next Save Room.
