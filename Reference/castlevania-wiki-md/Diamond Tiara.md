# Diamond Tiara

50px|right

The Diamond Tiara is a tiara adorned with Diamonds in Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin.  It is the accessory component of Charlotte's Diamond Outfit, which maximizes Charlotte Aulin's Defense (the body armor is a Diamond Corset, the accessory is the Diamond Brooch, and the leg component are the Diamond Shoes).  Like the other items, it gives more Defense (+10) than any other Charlotte-exclusive item for its equipment slot, including any other tiaras.
