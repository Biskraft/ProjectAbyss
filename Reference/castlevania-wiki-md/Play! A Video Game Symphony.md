  - Play! A Video Game Symphony** is a series of symphonic video games music concerts created by Nobuo Uematsu, *Final Fantasy* first composer, and Arnie Roth. On January 9, 2009, they released their CD **Play! A Video Game Symphony Live!**, containing a *Castlevania* medley.

## Additional information
- The *Castlevania* medley contains the following tracks: Vampire Killer and Wicked Child (from Perfect Selection Dracula ~New Classic~) ~ A Tocatta into Blood Soaked Darkness (*Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*).
- The recording session was done with the Czech Philharmonic Chamber Orchestra and Kuehn's Choir.
