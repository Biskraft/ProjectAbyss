The **Skeleton Tree** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is a skeleton that grows like a tree.

## Description
Skeleton Trees are encountered in the Burnt Paradise. They look and behave almost exactly as Hanged Bones, except they do not breathe fire. They are harmless to the touch and can in fact be used as helpful improvised platforms in the rooms in which they appear.
