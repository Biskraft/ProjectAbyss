# Giant Brothers

The **Giant Brothers** are a group of armored giants. These giants all wield different giant weapons and appear in several games such as *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. Members of that group are the Red Smasher (Blade, the eldest), Hammer Shaker (Hammer), and Gurkha Master (Gurka).
