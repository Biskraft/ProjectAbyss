# Leather Armor

right|73px

The **Leather Armor** (also known as **Hide Cuirass**, **Leather Cuirass**, **Soft Leather Armor**, or **Leather Plate**) is one of the weakest breastplates found in *Castlevania* and is made of Leather.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* (called Hide Cuirass in North America and **Leather Breastplate** in Japan), it is the weakest armor, giving +2 Defense, which is one more than a Cloth Tunic, but one less than Bronze Armor.

It is also the weakest armor in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, where it gives a Defense boost of +30. This is less than the Bronze Armor (+50).

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it is the weakest of all body protection gear, giving only +4 Defense, which is two less than Rubber Armor.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, the Leather Plate gives +10 Defense, which is 3 more than a Cloth Tunic, but 8 less than a Copper Plate.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, the Leather Armor is again the weakest of the armors, giving +8 Defense, which is four more than the Cloth Tunic, but four less than the Breastplate.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the Soft Leather Armor is the first type of armor that can be created (combine two Wild Memory) and is a the most basic armor, granting +5 Defense. It is normally upgraded to Hard Leather Armor by adding a Jet Black, increasing its Defense by 3. It can also be upgraded to Hide Armor by adding Sun Tears, which decreases its Defense to +3, but adds a Constitution boost of +5. It is also one of the ingredients needed to make a Spike Shield (by combining it with a Leather Helmet and adding Bronze and Steel).

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, the Leather Cuirass gives +4 Defense, which is one more than a Tailcoat and 5 less than a Copper Plate.

In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, the Leather Cuirass gives +5 Defense, which is 4 more than Casual Clothes, and 3 less than a Copper Plate.
