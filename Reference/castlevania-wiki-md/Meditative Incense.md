# Meditative Incense

The **Meditative Incense** is a relic in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* which recovers hearts gradually. It can be found in the Dark Palace of Waterfalls, in the coaster ride from B1F to B2F. You will need to get all the money from the coaster ride to get this relic.
