  - A Fleeting Respite** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is Yoko Belnades's theme in the game. It is played whenever Soma Cruz enters her study where she offers to either release his souls or upgrade his weapons with those that he collected.
