# Mirror Pendant

The **Mirror Pendant** is a pendant that offers resistance to Poison in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It was replaced by the Anti-Poison Ring in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. The pendant was removed in *Castlevania: Double Pack* version.
