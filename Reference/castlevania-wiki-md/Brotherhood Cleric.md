# Brotherhood Cleric

right|250px

The **Brotherhood Cleric** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. They are priest warriors of the Brotherhood of Light. They have magical abilities and can summon more Brotherhood Soldiers.

## Spells
### When Warriors are also present
    - Insigne ex magus** (): Invokes the Silver Blessing, increasing the offensive capability of all Warriors. Can be ended by attacking the Cleric.
    - Arcessere armas** (): Invokes the Golden Blessing, shielding one Warrior in an invulnerable golden aura. Can be ended by attacking the Cleric.

### When only Clerics are present
    - Telum magica** (): Tosses a vial of Holy Water.
    - Forma incendium** (): Flings a pillar of fire.
    - Globus ex ignifer** (): Slams staff into ground, generating shock wave of divinely-charged fog.
    - Pass through the portal, brothers: you must stop him.../Come to me, brothers!**: Summons three Brotherhood Warriors.
