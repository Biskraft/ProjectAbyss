# Fire Mail

The **Fire Mail** is an armor that gives protection against Fire-based attacks. It is similar in function to the Wyrm Robe found in other games.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it is the first elemental armor encountered, and gives less DEF than any of the other ones (+4). This is half as much defense as the Lightning Mail, Ice Mail or Dark Armor.

It is the only elemental armor found in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It grants +36 DEF, which is the same as a Guardian Armor, and 4 less than a Brigandine. Protection against Lightning can be obtained from the Lightning Robe in this game.

The **Armor of Fire** in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* grants a DEF boost of +34, which is one more than the Armor of Water and one less than the Silver Plate.
