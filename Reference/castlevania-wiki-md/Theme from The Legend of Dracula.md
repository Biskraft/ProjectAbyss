# Theme from "The Legend of Dracula"

- Theme from "The Legend of Dracula"** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania: The Adventure*. It is played during the game's end credits immediately after Dracula is vanquished at the hands of Christopher Belmont.

The theme was later arranged twice for the Perfect Selection Dracula ~New Classic~ and Perfect Selection Dracula Battle II albums, respectively.
