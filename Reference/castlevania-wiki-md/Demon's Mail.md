# Demon's Mail

The **Demon's Mail** is a suit of mail imbued with dark powers. It is similar to the Mojo Mail (also called Dark Armor) and the Dark Armor.

## Characteristics
In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, the **Demon's Mail** offers Soma Cruz some protection against Dark based attacks. It is also the most valuable armor in the game, granting +49 DEF, and +9 ATK and STR. This is better than the Eversing, which grants 45 DEF. While the Demon's Mail is the best of the best for a physical fighter, one should be aware that it offers less total attribute points than the Dracula Tunic, which only offers +41 DEF, but also offers +6 ATK, STR, CON, INT and LCK, but is worth less money (19,000G vs 33,000G). It can be obtained from the Demon Lord.

The Demon's Mail loses its protection against Dark attacks and other statistical bonuses in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It simply gives more DEF than any other armor (+56), and can be sold for the "ominous" price of $33,000. This is again higher than the Eversing (+46) and less effective overall than the Dracula Tunic, which retains its other attribute bonuses (+44 DEF, +6 STR, CON, INT and LCK). It can be obtained from the Arc Demon.

The Demon's Mail regains its defense against Dark attacks in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. However, wearing it also makes one more susceptible to Holy, Fire, Ice and Lightning based attacks. It keeps its STR and CON boost, looses its INT and LCK boost, and gains an HP boost. It's worth more than a Mojo Mail and increases DEF more than it, which doesn't boost HP or STR. The Mojo Mail, however, offers better protection against Dark attacks, doesn't increase vulnerability to certain types of attacks, and offers an INT boost.
