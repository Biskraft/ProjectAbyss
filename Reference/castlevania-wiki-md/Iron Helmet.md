# Iron Helmet

The **Iron Helmet** is a helmet made of Iron, appearing in some *Castlevania* games.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it grants DEF +21, which is one more than a Kettle Hat and one less than a Full Helmet.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, the Iron Helmet is the headgear that gives the most DEF (+5) that only gives a DEF boost, except the Porkpie Hat, which it is tied with. It is also the least effective Helmet in the game, as the other ones also give a STR boost, such as the Viking Helmet (DEF +4, STR +3).

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, the Iron Helmet provides good DEF (+5). However, it acts somewhat like a lightning rod and makes its wearer more susceptible to Thunder attacks. It can also notably be worn by Shanoa, as most helmets are male-only.
