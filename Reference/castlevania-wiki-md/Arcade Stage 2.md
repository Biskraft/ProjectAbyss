# Arcade Stage 2

The second stage of *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* consists of the interior of the castle and is divided between the Castle, Palace and Chapel sections. In addition to the enemies that were inhabiting the previous stage (Skeleton Soldiers, Skeleton Archers and Bats), this stage is home to some Axe Armors (which do not actually throw axes). A White Dragon tears up the castle hallway and attacks as the mid-boss. The Werewolf makes his first appearance in the Palace section, but he flees before you can finish him off. Dullahan fights you at the end in the chapel. After defeating Dullahan, the ground cracks and the hero falls a long way into the Underground levels.
