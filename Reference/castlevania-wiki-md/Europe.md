# Europe

right|300px

  - Europe** is a continent located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mostly in the Eastern Hemisphere. It is bordered by the Arctic Ocean to the north, the Atlantic Ocean to the west, Asia to the east, and the Mediterranean Sea to the south. It comprises the westernmost part of Eurasia.

This land has been the main setting on which most of the *Castlevania* games take place (some exceptions include *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*). Dracula's Castle can usually be found there, in the Romanian region of Transylvania.

The continent played a major role in *Castlevania: Bloodlines*, where the vampiress Elizabeth Bartley, niece of the vampire lord, Count Dracula, set on a long journey all across Europe in order to escape her two persecutors, John Morris and Eric Lecarde, with the ultimate intention of resurrecting her unholy uncle at her own abode in England.

## Relevant locations
- England - The Castle Proserpina
- France - Versailles Palace
- Germany - The Munitions Factory
- Greece - Atlantis Shrine
- Italy - The Leaning Tower of Pisa
- Romania - Ruins of Castle Dracula
- Transylvania
- Wallachia
