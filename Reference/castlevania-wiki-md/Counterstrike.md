# Counterstrike

right|300px

  - Counterstrike** is an attack in *Lords of Shadow* games that is performed immediately following a Synchronized Block while the enemy is off balance. A **Direct Counterstrike** only attacks the enemy that was blocked, while an **Area Counterstrike** also can affect other enemies around you. A series of counterstrikes of the same kind can be performed.
