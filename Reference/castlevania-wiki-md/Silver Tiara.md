# Silver Tiara

The **Silver Tiara** (also called the **Silver Crown**) is a Tiara made of Silver in *Castlevania*.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it raises INT (+12) more than any other crown or head equipment. This is two higher than the Ruby Circlet, Opal Circlet, Topaz Circlet, or Beryl Circlet. Unlike these circlets, it does not grant health when damage of a certain type is taken.

It also appears in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, where it grants +25 DEF, which is one greater than a Pot Helm, but one less than a Steel Helmet.
