# Steel Plate

- Steel Plate** (sometimes called **Steel Cuirass** or **Steel Armor**) is breastplate made of steel. It is often stronger than the Iron Armor and the strongest "normal" metal armor, but less powerful than the weakest "precious metal" armor, Silver Armor.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, the Steel Cuirass grants a Defense boost of +5, which is one more than an Iron Cuirass and one less than a Silver Plate.

In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, the Steel Armor grants +120 Defense, which is 20 more than Chain Mail and 30 less than Platinum Armor.

The Steel Plate in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* gives DEF +31, which is more than an Iron Plate (+25) and less than the Silver Plate +35.

There is not an item called the Steel Armor in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. However, the description for the Iron Plate indicates that it is made of Steel.
