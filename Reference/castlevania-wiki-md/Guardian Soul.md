# Guardian Soul

- Guardian Souls**, also known as **<span style="color:blue;">Blue Souls</span>**, are one of the four main types of tactical souls. Soma Cruz is allowed to use an ability which can be activated and deactivated at will and incur in a continuous MP cost while in use. These souls are either "passive" (i.e. pressing the button once) or "aggressive" (holding the R button down to maintain the effect).
