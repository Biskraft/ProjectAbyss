# Room of Clocks

The **Room of Clocks** is a location in *Castlevania* and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.

## Overview
As its name suggests, this stage is just a room full of clocks. It is accessed by finishing the Tower of Execution as Reinhardt, or the Tower of Sorcery as Carrie (or Cornell in *Legacy of Darkness*). It leads to the character's specific boss.

There are no significant differences between the version in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*, apart from what items are found in the candles.
