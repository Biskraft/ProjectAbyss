  - Sypha** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. It is the main music theme for Camilla Fernandez.

## Trivia
- The character design for Camilla Fernandez was originally meant to be used as a wreck of Sypha Belnades. This idea was abandoned during development of the game, but the music track used during her introductory cinematic is still referred to as "Sypha" on the game's official soundtrack release.
