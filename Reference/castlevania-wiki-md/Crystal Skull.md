# Crystal Skull

The **Crystal Skull** is a Relic in *Lament of Innocence* and a Innocent Devil ability in *Curse of Darkness*. In both games, it allows the player to become invisible.

It can be found as a drop from the Phantom in *Lament of Innocence* and can be learned by the Tiara Fairy-Type in *Curse of Darkness*, costing 20 Hearts.
