# Glow Soul

right

  - Glow Soul** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by the Rasetz Battle-Type evolution.

When cast, blue energy souls will be launched one by one which home in on surrounding enemies.

Costs 20 Hearts to cast.

## How to obtain
Defeat a certain number of enemies while Rasetz is summoned.
