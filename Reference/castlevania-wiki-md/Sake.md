# Sake

is an alcoholic beverage found in *Castlevania* which recovers one of the players meters. Like the other alcoholic beverages in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it was censored in the North American version and was referred to as **Green Tea**. It recovers 10 HP, which is the lowest HP recovering alcoholic beverage in the game, being 5 less than the Beer (Barley Tea). It recovers the same amount as a Chinese Bun and a Strawberry in that game.
