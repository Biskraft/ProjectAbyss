# Spin Devil

The **Spin Devil** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is an evil blade possessed by a demented demon.

## Description
A monster consisting of a devilish skull that sits atop a rapidly spinning blade. The blade produces a small tornado, which creates the effect of a tornado with a head. They are initially encountered floating in place in the air until they see the player, at which point they will fly at them. A couple standard attacks should finish it off.
