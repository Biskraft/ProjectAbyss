# Liam O'Brien

- Liam Christopher O'Brien** is an American voice actor. He firstly appeared in *Castlevania* as Isaac from *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. He later voiced Jonathan Morris in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* as well as Brachyura in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.

Liam is well known to his fans as Kenzo Tenma in *Monster*, Gaara in *Naruto* and Kain Highwind in the NDS version of *Final Fantasy IV*.
