# Ring (weapon)

right|73px

Carrie Fernandez's short range weapon consists of a pair of bladed **rings** that she uses to cut nearby enemies with, one in each hand. These rings are somewhat like a Chakram, except that they are not thrown. Carrie keeps more than one pair of these rings on her person.

These can also be used by Hector in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
