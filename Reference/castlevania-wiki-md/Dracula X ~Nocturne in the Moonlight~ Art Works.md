# Dracula X ~Nocturne in the Moonlight~ Art Works

right|200px
  - Dracula X ~Nocturne in the Moonlight~ Art Works** is artworks and a small prelude comic to *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* that was included in a CD-jacket sized artbook that was included with *Symphony of the Night* in Japan. The story and artwork was by Ayami Kojima, who had done the art for the game as well.
