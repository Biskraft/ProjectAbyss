# Meat Strip

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">*Harmony of Despair* gamerpic.</p>

  - Meat Strip** (known in Japan as simply **Meat**) is a food item in the *Castlevania* series. It is meat that gives a small amount of health. It is one of the few food items that actually describes the given amount of health that is recovered by eating the given thing, being 29 HP.


## Item Data


## Trivia
- The fact that the R. Strip Meat restores 86 HP instead of 29 could be a reference to the release date of the first *Castlevania* game in Japan (1986). It fit well since the item can only be found in Chapter 10, which is an entire replica of the whole original game map.
