# Dimensional Rift

The **Dimensional Rift** (異界側・外壁 *Underworld Side Outer Wall*) is a stage in *Castlevania Judgment*. The stage is on the top of one of the castle's towers with the Time Rift visible in the background. In Story Mode, this is the stage where the Time Reaper is fought.

As with the Crystal Cavern stage, characters cannot be "RING OUT" on this stage. An example is the Time Reaper's scythe swing attack, it sends the player flying way back to the battlefield, landing on the edge of the screen where no fence can protect from a RING OUT.
