# TwinBee PARADISE in Donburi Shima

is an interactive video game developed and published by Konami for Windows 95 on February 26, 1998 in Japan only. More than a game, it is an interactive multimedia CD-ROM including accessories such as wallpapers, music, videos and screensavers inspired by the *TwinBee* series.

Kid Dracula and Maria Renard make a brief cameo in "Wai Wai Arcade", and Pastel wears a Simon Belmont-like outfit.
