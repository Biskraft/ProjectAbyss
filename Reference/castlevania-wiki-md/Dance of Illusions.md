# Dance of Illusions

- Dance of Illusions** (also occasionally known as **Illusionary Dance** or **Third Struggle**) is a music theme composed by Tomoko Sano (Sanoppi) which is most commonly associated with Dracula battles. It is considered to be the main battle theme for Dracula, or at the very least similar characters. It was first played in the final battle against Dracula in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*. The theme is composed of various organ music (and in some cases, either symphonic or actual vocalizations) and percussion beats in a rhythm indicating a march. The version from *Castlevania: Judgment* features electric guitar beats and is slightly extended to also include bars from Black Night.
