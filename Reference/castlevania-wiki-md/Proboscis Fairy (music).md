  - Proboscis Fairy** is a music theme composed by Yuka Watanabe for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is played when the titular demon use his ability Just A Little.

With Waltz of the Lazy Chair Room, this is one of the only music in the game not to have been composed by Michiru Yamane.
