# Lucky Charm

right|50px
The **Lucky Charm** is a Japanese charm that increases Luck. It raises Luck by +10 in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* (half as much as the Lucky Ring in that game), +3 in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and by +1 in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.

The Lucky Charm was replaced with the Lucky Clover in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.
