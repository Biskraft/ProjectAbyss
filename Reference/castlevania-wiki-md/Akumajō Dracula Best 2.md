  - Akumajō Dracula Best 2** is a two-disc compilation containing the original soundtracks from *Super Castlevania IV*, *Castlevania: The Adventure* and *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge*. The music was composed by Konami Kukeiha Club and the soundtrack released on December 5, 1991.

A reprint of this soundtrack was released on September 23, 1998, along with Akumajō Dracula Famicom Best.
