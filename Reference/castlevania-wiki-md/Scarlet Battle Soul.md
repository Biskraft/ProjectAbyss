  - Scarlet Battle Soul** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the background theme for the boss battles against Dmitrii Blinov and Dario Bossi, two Dark Lord Candidates working with Celia Fortner.
