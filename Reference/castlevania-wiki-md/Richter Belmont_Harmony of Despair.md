## Overview
Perhaps the most powerful Vampire Hunter to have ever lived, Richter came the closest to finishing Dracula once and for all before the time of his distant and comparatively powerful successor, Julius Belmont. Richter Belmont is a downloadable playable character in *Harmony of Despair*. He plays much as he did in *Portrait of Ruin*. He can use the Vampire Killer whip, several sub-weapons, and a variety of martial arts.

## Attack
Richter is able to use the Vampire Killer. Unlike Julius Belmont, Richter cannot whip in all directions. While Jonathan even has access to down-right and down-left whipping directions, Richter is restricted to left and right whipping. However, Richter can whip much faster than Julius and Jonathan, thus giving him an advantage that only Simon Belmont is able to match.


## Sub-Weapons
Richter is able to utilize any "Belmont subweapon" that has been used by any member of the Belmont family in a previous game. All of his weapons reach level 9 just like everyone else. The grind is worth it, as it makes him stronger with his basic whip attack. A glitch was confirmed that putting Down-B subskills can be spammed (useful for training them on Survival Mode). Hold Down while pressing LB and B at the same time at fast speeds to spam.


 


## Martial Arts
Richter has a variety of Martial Arts that he can earn.
