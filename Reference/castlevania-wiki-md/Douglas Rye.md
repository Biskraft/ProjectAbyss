# Douglas Rye

- Douglas Forsythe Rye**, also known as **Doug Rye**, is a Californian voice actor and film actor. He provided the voice of Dracula as a replacement of Michael G. in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* and *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. He was later replaced in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* by Patrick Seitz. Rye has since portrayed Death in *Castlevania Judgment*, ironically replacing Seitz from earlier games, but has been replaced in the role by Richard Epcar for *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. He would later work in the fan-game *Castlevania: The Lecarde Chronicles 2*, in which he provided the voice of the final boss.

Douglas's most famous character roles to his fans are Mr. Gentleman in *Read or Die*, Silver Knight in *.hack* series and Cao Cao in *Dynasty Warriors*. He has also been a crew member for such famous musical acts as The Rolling Stones, Pink Floyd, The Commodores, The Eagles, The Oak Ridge Boys and Mel Tormé.
