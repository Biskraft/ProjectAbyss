# The Colossus

is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is the background theme for the Final Approach, the top floor of Dracula's Castle and its last section. An arranged version later appeared in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* as the main background theme for Stage 6, Come, Sweet Hour of Death.
