# Castle Siege

right|250px

The Tutorial stage for Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2 and the first mission in the game. It takes place in the Throne Room, during the Brotherhood of Light's siege on Castlevania.

Enemies Encountered:
- Brotherhood Soldier
- Siege Titan
- Paladin
