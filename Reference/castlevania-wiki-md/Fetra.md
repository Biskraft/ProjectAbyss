  - Fetra**, also known as **Doina**, is a town in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. It is located in between the Wicked Ditch and the North Bridge to the central river island. The residents are deeply fearful of Dracula and urge Simon to leave their town. He can get healed at the church, however. Laurels can also be purchased here. There are a particularly large number of fast zombies on this mostly one level town.
