# Cream Pie

right|200px

  - Cream Pie** is one of Jonathan Morris's sub-weapon skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.

## Description
While it may be considered as a joke sub-weapon, Cream Pie can be very helpful in the fight against the Whip's Memory.

About two cream pies can be tossed at a time. They don't travel very far and will start falling to the ground the moment they are thrown, although this pattern is precisely what makes them so useful, as the player can throw cream pies continuously in front and downward. Jumping while throwing these can be very effective, and by performing a double jump while tossing them will allow the player to throw two more in midair.

Mastering Cream Pie and wearing INT-boosting gear (Royal Sword, Silk Hat, Royal Sandals, Blue Cape, Paludamentum, etc.) will greatly increase the damage dealt by this skill. It only needs 100 SP to master, which can be easily achieved by attacking the first two bosses at least once with this sub-weapon and then collect the remaining 15 SP by using it against lesser enemies.

In the battle against the Whip's Memory, they can stun him for a considerable period of time if they hit in quick succession. Additionally, they deal Dark damage, to which the memory is weak against.
