# Silver Bangle

The **Silver Bangle** is a bangle made of silver in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It gives more defense (+ 20) than any other type of bracelet, giving more than a Crested Band (+15), and well more than a normal Bangle (+3).


Category: Harmony of Dissonance Items
Category: Bracelets
