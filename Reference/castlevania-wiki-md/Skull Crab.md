# Skull Crab

The **Skull Crab** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*.

## Description
A rather disturbing type of crawling skull that takes the appearance of a crab. It consists of a skull with legs made of bones, and crawls along the high structures of the aqueducts in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*. It attacks by vomiting foamy bubbles or by lashing out its long and pointy tongue.
