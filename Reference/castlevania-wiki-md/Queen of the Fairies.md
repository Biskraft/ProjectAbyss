# Queen of the Fairies

The **Queen of the Fairies** is the ruler of the Fairies living in the Land of the Lycans and surrounding areas. She pledged that she and her people would help the warrior who had come to destroy the Lycan Lord Cornell. She speaks in an unknown language, but those she speaks to can understand her just the same. She's capable of speaking to individuals in their dreams. She's beautiful, bewitching, mesmerizing, and her beauty is like nothing else in the world.

She spoke to one of the knights of the Brotherhood of Light, telling him of the great warrior that would come and save them.

## Trivia
- In the note that talks about the queen, the knight closes his scroll by saying: "I can happily declare that the 'Cake' is not a lie!" This is a nod to the game *Portal*. The game is known for the line "the cake is a lie".
