 is the first underground location found in the game *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. The lower portion is just beyond the Town of Veros, while the upper portion is just beyond the Denis Woods. Beyond this path lies the Aljiba Woods. In addition to Skeletons and Bats, the caves are the first place where Ghostly Eyeballs are encountered.

On the east section is a hidden area below the main path that can be entered using Holy Water. Using Holy Water again on the wall to the far left reveals the Sacred Flame sub-weapon.
