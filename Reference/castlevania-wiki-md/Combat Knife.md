# Combat Knife

right|112px

The **Combat Knife** is a common stabbing knife designed for close-quarters combat in the *Castlevania* series.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, the Combat Knife has a high damage class for a weapon that can be found quite early in the game (by killing a Zombie Officer). Like the similar Basilard, which is dropped by Zombies, it is a harder drop to acquire and will most likely take many kills before it is obtained.
