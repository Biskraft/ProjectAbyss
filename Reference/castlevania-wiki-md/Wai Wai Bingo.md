## Characters
This bingo game features Konami mascot characters such as Kid Dracula from *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*, Goemon and Ebisumaru from the *Ganbare Goemon* series, Vic Viper and Moai from the *Gradius* series, Light and Pastel from the *TwinBee* series, Takosuke from the *Parodius* series, and Penta from *Antarctic Adventure*.
