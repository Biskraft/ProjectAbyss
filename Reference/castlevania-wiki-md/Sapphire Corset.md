# Sapphire Corset

The **Sapphire Corset** is a corset made of sapphire. It is the only corset that in addition to a DEF boost also gives a CON boost. It's one of three corsets that give a DEF boost of +16 and a boost of +3 to some other attribute (the Emerald Corset boosts INT and the Ruby Corset boosts STR). This is more defense than an Amethyst Corset (+11), but less than a Lilith Corset (+17).

It's the only gemstone corset to appear in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
