# Akumajō Dracula Tribute Vol. 2

- Akumajō Dracula Tribute Vol. 2** is a remix album from Konami to celebrate 25 years of the *Castlevania* series It was released on January 13, 2011. It contains music from the series, arranged by Azusa Chiba, AKANE, Soyo Oka, Masato Kouda, Hiroki Kikuta, Maki Kirioka, Manami Kiyota, Kumi Tanioka, Hideki Sakamoto, Eriko Imura, Yoshino Aoki, Masashi Hamauzu and kukui.
