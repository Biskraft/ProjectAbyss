The **Buster Armor** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is an armored warrior who specializes in long-range attacks.

## Description
The Buster Armor has a large body of armor and a large weapon similar to a rocket launcher. These heavily armored enemies shoot an enormous missile that deals extremely high damage, although the player can hit it in midair to launch it back to the Buster Armor and kill it instantly in a great flare. They are vulnerable to Charlotte's lightning spells, and are also weak to Jonathan's whip attacks. Buster Armors are also weak to any spell and/or sub-weapon that deals Strike damage, such Charlotte's Rock Riot or Jonathan's Wrecking Ball.
