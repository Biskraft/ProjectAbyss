The **Dora Woods** are a wooden area in Castlevania II: Simon's Quest on a large river island. It can be reached from the Town of Fetra by crossing the North Bridge. Crossing the East Bridge leads to the Denis Marsh (which accomplishes nothing). To the south lies the last town of the game, the town of Ghulash (or Yomi). It is inhabited by the usual Skeletons and Two-Headed Creatures which shoot fireballs extremely fast.
