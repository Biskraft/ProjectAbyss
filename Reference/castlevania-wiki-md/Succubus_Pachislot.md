# Succubus/Pachislot

The **Succubus** is fought in all three *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula* games (*Pachislot Akumajō Dracula I*, *II* and *III*).

In the second game, she summons duplicates of herself to hold Trevor down while she steals a kiss and drains his energy. She then unleashes an electrical attack. She is not shown being destroyed at the end in either game, and instead just pouts in submission.
