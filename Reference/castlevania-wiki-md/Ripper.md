# Ripper

The **Ripper** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. He is a fiend who takes great pleasure in slaughtering people with knives.

## Description
Murderous hunchbacks usually armed with throwing knives and who possess strong jumping abilities. The first Ripper to appear in a *Castlevania* game may have been the boss Nasty Grant from *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*. This creature wanders around the screen, crawls on the walls and ceilings, and throws knives.

Rippers were properly introduced in *Kid Dracula* for the Game Boy handheld system. In this game, they wait on tree branches and occasionally jump up, throwing a dagger diagonally downward.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, the Ripper is basically a Flea Man who also throws daggers. Much like said enemies in other games, they are carried and deployed in the field by other flying enemies (Altairs) in one room of the Floating Garden.

A trap treasure chest found in one of the final rooms of the Forbidden Area contains a myriad of Rippers that will burst out and attack when destroyed. Due to them initially being all grouped and only requiring one or two swings from most weapons to kill them all, they make for a good EXP source that will allow the player to quickly grow up a few levels.

The **Death Ripper** in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* is an alternate version of the Flea Man and does not throw knives.
