# Cross Helmet

right|73px

The **Cross Helmet** is a helmet with a crest and visor in *Castlevania*.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the Cross Helmet has more Defense than any other head equipment. It is created when an Angel Halo is added to an Armet, raising its defense from +20 to +26. This overtakes the Crest Helm, which has Defense of +24. The only other type of helmet that may be worth equipping when you have a Cross Helmet is the Masakado's Helm, which has DEF +15, STR +10, CON +10, but LCK -10.
