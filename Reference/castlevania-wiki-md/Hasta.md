# Hasta

right|250px
  - Hasta** is a lance glyph available in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is the weakest of the three available lance glyphs. **Vol Hasta** is the second strongest lance glyph, **Melio Hasta** is the strongest of the three available lance glyphs.
