# Sequined Dress

right|50px

The **Sequined Dress** is a dress covered with glittering sequins.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The Sequined Dress can be found in the Great Stairway, at the top of the tower located between the Nation of Fools and Forest of Doom's towers.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
To obtain the Sequined Dress in *Order of Ecclesia*, Shanoa must complete Monica's second quest, "Silkworm Tragedy". To achieve this, she must bring five Silk Threads to her. Monica will reward her with a Silk Dress and the Sequined Dress will then become available for purchase at Jacob's shop.
