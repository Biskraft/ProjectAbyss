# Diamond Brooch

The **Diamond Brooch** is a brooch adorned with Diamonds in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is the accessory component of Charlotte's Diamond Outfit, which maximizes Charlotte Aulin's Defense (the body armor is a Diamond Corset, the head item is a Diamond Tiara, and the leg component are the Diamond Shoes). Like the other items, it gives more Defense than any other Charlotte-exclusive item for its equipment slot. It is the only brooch that has greater than +3 Defense (+5).
