# Needle Magic Circle

*(This article is a stub)*
right
  - Needle Magic Circle** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the second ability learned by the Brow evolution of the Devil-Type. Like the M. Circle Scissors, this provides an offensive capability to the Magic Circle. Unlike the *Scissors* which extends razors to the side, the *Needles* protrude several blades upwards in varying directions. The player is required to rapidly press the buttons for increased damage output.
