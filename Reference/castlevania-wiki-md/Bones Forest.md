The **Bones Forest** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the first stage of Chapter 9.

## Story
The Bones Forest is Gabriel's first step into entering the Land of the Dead to confront the final Lord of Shadow, and retrieve the final piece of the God Mask. After entering the forest through the mirror in Carmilla's castle, Gabriel traverses the forest with the aid of the Seraph Shoulders. Battling through undead, trolls, and Chupacabras, Gabriel eventually makes his way to his next destination, Baba Yaga's hut.

## Overview
The Bones Forest is a barren land with little to no plant life in sight, and any plant life seen is already dead. As is the forest's namesake, it is consists of nothing but dead and barren trees. Within the forest are also caves that are just as barren as the outside, and also are cliffs of which Gabriel must climb to progress. As well are ruins that seen in the beginning of the are before the forest is actually entered; the doorway through which Gabriel came through is among these ruins.
