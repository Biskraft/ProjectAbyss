# Plate Coat

The **Plate Coat** is a body armor in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is a long metal armor coat that protects its user above the knees.

It is the fourth weakest armor in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It grants +12 DEF, which is four more than Field Armor, but 2 less than a Cloth Tunic and 6 less than Bronze Armor.

It can easily be acquired by farming the Lizard Man in the early game, where it functions as a useful upgrade to the Leather Armor and Rubber Armor the player may have.
