# Dodo (Lords of Shadow)

right|250px

The **Dodo** is a bird that hatches from the Dodo Egg relic in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. The relic can be purchased for 300 Experience Points or dropped by enemies. When used, the bird will fly to the nearest available secret.
