# Winter Memories

right|250px
  - Winter Memories** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It creates a white orb of dark power that grows as it rolls.

## Characteristics
Winter Memories allows Soma to summons a snowball that gains power as it rolls down surfaces. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over the Yeti's soul.
