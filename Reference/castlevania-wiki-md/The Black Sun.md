  - The Black Sun** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the background theme for the game's title screen and the main menu.

## Trivia
- A "black sun" is a moniker for a solar eclipse, hence the name of the track. In addition, an animation of a solar eclipse occurs shortly before the menu screen appears.
