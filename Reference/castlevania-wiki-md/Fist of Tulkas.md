# Fist of Tulkas

The **Fist of Tulkas** (called the **Orichalcum** in Japan) is a glove with a monsters head on it that is blessed by Tulkas the Valar. Wearing it allows a powerful punch, a flurry of fist attack, or homing Fireballs. It is the most powerful fist weapon in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* or *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It is also a reference to *The Lord of the Rings*.
