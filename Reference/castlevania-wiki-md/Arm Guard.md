# Arm Guard

- Arm Guards** protect the entire arms. In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, one gives +10 Defense. They give +18 Defense in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, which is greater than a Guardian Glove (+14), but less than Arm Plates (+24).
