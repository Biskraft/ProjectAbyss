# Hai no Majutsushi

is a Konami mascot mahjong game developed and published by Konami for MSX2 and released in 1989 in Japan.

The player can choose to play against many iconic Konami mascot characters, including Simon Belmont.

## Characters
- Simon Belmont from the *Castlevania* series.
- Goemon from the *Ganbare Goemon* series.
- Dr. Venom from the *Gradius* series.
- Moai from the *Gradius* series.
- Penta from the *Antarctic Adventure* series.
- Aphrodite from the *Knightmare* series.
- Popolon from the *Knightmare* series.
- Snatcher from *Snatcher*.
