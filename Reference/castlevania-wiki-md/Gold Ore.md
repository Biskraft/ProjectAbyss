# Gold Ore

- Gold Ore** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. This ore is as valuable as it is heavy.

## Availability
Dropped by Tin Men, Double Hammers and Weapon Masters.

A Gold Ore can be found inside of a red chest located in the room above Albus's boss room in the Mystery Manor. Another one can be found inside of a red chest in a hidden room in the Final Approach (Dracula's Castle). Break the ceiling of the big room just outside the last Warp Point to find it.

They can also appear inside of wooden chests in the Training Hall and the Large Cavern, as well as green chests throughout all areas of Dracula's Castle.

## Usage
Gold Ore is required in order to complete Eugen's quest: "Work of the Finest Quality". If the player brings him 3 Gold Ores, he will reward them with 7,200G and the items Gold Plate, Gold Leggings, Platinum Plate and Platinum Leggings will be added to Jacob's shop.
