The **Grave Digger** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. He enjoys his cursed vocation because he meets new people.

## Description
Appearing in the Misty Forest Road area, he is a tall, well-built man in a gray trench coat and hat. He carries a shovel, which he flings at the player like a javelin upon first sight and then he advances at them.

He deals damage by touch, and also has two attacks:
- A side, straight-leg kick that knocks the player back.
- A choke that deals damage until his hold is broken.

He is weak to the Torpor glyph, which can temporarily freeze him.
