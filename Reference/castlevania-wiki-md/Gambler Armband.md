# Gambler Armband

The **Gambler Armband** is a bracelet in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It greatly increases LCK (+100) at the expense of other statistics (-25 STR, DEF and INT). It is more powerful than the Luck Ring (+50 LCK and -10 DEF and INT). It is the LCK counterpart to the Defense Armband, Strength Armband and Sage Armband.
