# Astral Veil

right|126px
  - Astral Veil** is a Bullet Soul acquired from the Killer Mantle. When used, Soma swipes a mantle a short distance straightforward. The hit will deal minor damage with no attribute, but the first strike on an enemy has the additional effect of switching its HP with its MP. Striking again will only deal damage; the player cannot re-swap the totals if the result was unfavorable. The points are swapped before the hit deals damage, meaning they take a hit on their new HP total. This can result in the Astral Veil killing a tough enemy instantly.

Since an enemy's MP is not displayed, the effect is unpredictable and can be good or bad. Magic-using and demonic enemies tend to have high MP, while creatures that owe their magic to an outside source and those with mainly physical attacks tend to have low MP. A hit with the Astral Veil will cause a Succubus to swap to her 3,000 MP, but will instantly kill a Red Minotaur. Some enemies such as the Man Eater and Big Golem have identical HP and MP totals; with these it will seem like the veil does a normal hit with its first strike.

The Astral Veil is most useful against the Iron Golem, which has resistances to all forms of attack and normally takes 1-2 HP of damage from any weapon, with 9,999 HP in total. The Iron Golem has only 1 MP, and hitting it with the Astral Veil will kill it with a single attack. Mina Hakuba will give a hint to this effect if Soma speaks to her a few times after defeating Graham with the correct items to unlock the "good" ending.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
#### Enemies weak to Astral Veil
- Wooden Golem, Golem, Flesh Golem, Iron Golem (all have 1 MP)
- Minotaur (200 HP to 40 MP)
- Catoblepas (200 HP to 10 MP)
- Gorgon (320 HP to 1 MP)
- Slime (200 HP to 1 MP)
- Alura Une (800 HP to 1 MP)
- Red Minotaur (929 HP to 30 MP)

#### Enemies strong against Astral Veil
- Tiny Devil (17 HP to 999 MP)
- Student Witch (80 HP to 999 MP)
- Lilith (220 HP to 999 MP)
- Witch (180 HP to 4,000 MP)
- Devil (300 HP to 1,000 MP)
- Imp (52 HP to 999 MP)
- Arc Demon (500 HP to 2,000 MP)
- Succubus (550 HP to 3,000 MP)
- Stolas (1,500 HP to 9,999 MP)
- Demon Lord (1,800 HP to 9,999 MP)
- Flame Demon (500 HP to 5,000 MP)

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
  - Astral World** is the Imp's soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It switches enemy HP for their MP for a brief time.
