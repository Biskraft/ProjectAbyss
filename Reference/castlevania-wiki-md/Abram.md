# Abram

is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. He is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. He is a former common thief who can prepare medicines.

## Description
Abram was a former common thief who found salvation in the church. He became an expert at making medicine, so by doing his quests, he will add items like Potions and Tonics to the shop. Most of his concoctions require the use of Sage.

When he speaks, he will put more emphasis on some rather random word choices, shown in all capital letters.

## Location
Minera Prison Island, inside the rightmost room after the large corridor with swinging pendulums and iron maidens. Once rescued, he will stay near Jacob's shop in Wygol Village.

## Quests
#"'''Running Out of Sage"
#***Objective**: Bring Abram some Sage.
#***Reward**: Adds Potion and Tonic to Jacob's shop.
#"'''Medicinal Ingredients Needed"
#***Objective**: Bring Abram some Chamomile and Rue.
#***Reward**: Adds Anti-Venom and Uncurse Potion to the shop.
#"'''Mandrake is the Best Medicine"
#***Objective**: Bring Abram some Sage and a Mandrake Root.
#***Reward**: Adds High Potion, Heart Repair and High Tonic to the shop.
#"'''Unusual Medicine Components"
#***Objective**: Bring Abram some Sage and Merman Meat.
#***Reward**: Adds Super Potion to the shop.
