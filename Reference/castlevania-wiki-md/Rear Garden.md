  - Rear Garden** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is played briefly after Sara Trantoul overheard her fiancé, Leon Belmont, and alchemist, Rinaldo Gandolfi, discover that she has been bitten by Walter Bernhard during her captivity as part of one of his usual "cat-and-mouse" games.

Although she is grief-stricken over her slow transformation into a vampire, she soon feels relieved when she realizes that if her soul was infused with the Whip of Alchemy, she will still be able to help Leon save other unfortunate souls, and thus the whip was upgraded into the Vampire Killer.
