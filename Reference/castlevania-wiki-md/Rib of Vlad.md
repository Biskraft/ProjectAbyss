# Rib of Vlad

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">**The Rib Bone** from the Japanese *[[Castlevania II: Simon's Quest|Simon's Quest* instruction booklet.</p>]]

The **Rib of Vlad** is a relic in the *Castlevania* series. It is an unholy rib and one of Dracula's bodily remains.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*
The Rib Bone can be found in Berkeley Mansion and selecting it gives Simon a shield that can block projectiles when he is not attacking or climbing stairs. It is one of the five relics needed in order to gain access to the ruins of the Demon Castle and fight Dracula at the end of the game.

### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The Rib of Vlad is one of the items necessary to enter Shaft's chambers. It gives an additional CON +10.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*
The Rib of Vlad is one of the six items needed to enter the ceremonial room. It has the ability to negate poison.
