# Selena Belmont

- Selena Belmont** is a mentioned character in the *Lords of Shadow* series. She is the wife of Simon Belmont.

Even though she has neither appeared nor been mentioned in any game, the official artbook *The Art of Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* mentions her.

## Notes
- No official art of her exists, even if her only mention takes place in the  official artbook.
- She is likely a reference to *Haunted Castle*'s character, Selena.
