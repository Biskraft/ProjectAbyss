# Jewel of Open

right|73px

The **Jewel of Open**, also called  in the Japanese version, essentially acts like a key that can open blue magically sealed doors in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*. It can be purchased from the Master Librarian. Richter and Maria do not need to have the Jewel of Open in order to open these sealed doors.

The item appears again in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* as the **Unlock Jewel** (though not as a relic) that is used to unlock the sealed door to the Prison of Eternal Torture.
