## Secrets and hidden areas
There are three 1 Ups in this stage:
- If you don't whip the very first candle in the stage, one of the candles prior to the second rope will contain a 1 Up.


- A hidden room is found at the top of the third and final right-sided rope. It's possible to continue climbing beyond the top of the third and final right-sided rope into a hidden room which contains a 1 Up and three other items.


- Beyond the final rope, there is a candle containing a 1 Up that can only be hit with a fireball whip from the top of a ledge.
