# Tremor Punch

The **Tremor Punch** is an advanced attack skill found in the *Lords of Shadow* games. It is generally performed by charging the Direct Attack button while blocking. This releases a powerful punch which is capable of knocking down enemies or destroying them in one blow. The Area Attack counterpart to this attack is the Earthquake Punch.

In *Lords of Shadow*, this move is possible as soon as the Dark Gauntlet is obtained. The amount of charging necessary can vary. Attacking without charging results in a relatively weak blow. Holding down the attack button charges the gauntlet until it becomes red for maximum power. The punch occurs as soon as the button is released. This can also activate some switches found on the walls in the castle. The Aerial counterpart to this attack in this game is the **Landslide Punch**, which dashes diagonally forward-downward while punching. Gabriel will hover in the air while charging this attack.

In *Mirror of Fate*, no relic is necessary to gain this attack and the user instead harness an unspecified energy from within themselves. The power of this attack does not depend on how long it has been charged. The punch occurs as soon as it has been charged for long enough. This attack can also be performed in the air in this game, which punches the enemy directly in front of the player.
