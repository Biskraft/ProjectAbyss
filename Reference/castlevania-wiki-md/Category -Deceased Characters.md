# Category:Deceased Characters

A category for characters who have story-related or canonically-recorded deaths.

Since the *Castlevania* series as a whole takes place in such a wide timespan conformed by several centuries, it's evident that most of its characters have died throughout the course of the years due to natural causes - these are not included in this category.

Survivor characters from alternate continuities are considered to still be alive in their respective timelines.
