# PlayStation 4

thumb|242px

The **PlayStation 4** is a video game console developed by Sony and first released on November 15, 2013.

## *Castlevania* games available on the system
  - Haunted Castle* (*Arcade Archives*, *Arcade Classics Anniversary Collection*)
  - Castlevania Requiem: Symphony of the Night/Rondo of Blood*
  - Castlevania Anniversary Collection*
