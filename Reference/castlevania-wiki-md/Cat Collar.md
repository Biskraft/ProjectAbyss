# Cat Collar

A cat collar made from chrysoberyl. This item is needed to complete Anna's *"Tom & Jewelry"* quest. To obtain it, Shanoa must first find a chrysoberyl inside a rare item box from either, Tymeo Mountains or Somnus Reef. Once acquired, she must first visit Laura and let her create the accessory by fixing the chrysoberyl into an old dog's collar she happens to have. After that, Shanoa may then take it to Anna to complete the quest and receive the Ribbon as a reward.
