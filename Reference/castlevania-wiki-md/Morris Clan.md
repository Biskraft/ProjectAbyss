# Morris Clan

The  is an offshoot of the Belmont Clan which mainly resides in America.

## History
After Alucard defeated his father, Count Dracula, in 1797, the Belmont Clan mysteriously vanished and the Vampire Killer was entrusted to the Morris family. In 1897, Quincy Morris successfully killed Dracula, although he was fatally wounded in the battle. In 1917, Elizabeth Bartley, the Count's niece, resurrected him but both were stopped by John Morris and Eric Lecarde.

John used the Vampire Killer, but because he was not a true Belmont, the whip sapped his energy and brought his life to an untimely end. The whip was later used by his son, Jonathan, who managed to make use of the whip's true power without being drained of his life force.

By the time of the Demon Castle War in 1999, the whip had presumably returned to the Belmonts. What happened to the Morris Clan afterward is unknown.
