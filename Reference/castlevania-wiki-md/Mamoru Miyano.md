# Mamoru Miyano

(born June 8, 1983) is a Japanese voice actor and singer from Saitama Prefecture. He is best known for his roles on *Steins;Gate, Durarara!!*, *Death Note*, *Soul Eater*, *Wolf's Rain*, *Ouran High School Host Club*, *Ajin: Demi-Human*, *Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood*, *Inazuma Eleven*, *Pokémon: Best Wishes*, *Tokyo Ghoul*, *Free!, Mobile Suit Gundam 00*, *Hunter x Hunter*, *Chihayafuru*, *Bungo Stray Dogs*, *Uta no Prince-sama* and *Zombieland Saga*.

He voiced Alucard in the internet radio *Akumajō Dracula X: Tsuioku no Yasōkyoku* and the Japanese version of *Castlevania Judgment*.
