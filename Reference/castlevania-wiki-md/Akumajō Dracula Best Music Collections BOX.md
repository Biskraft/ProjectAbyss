# Akumajō Dracula Best Music Collections BOX

- Akumajō Dracula Best Music Collections BOX** is a compilation of 18 CDs and one DVD, including every *Castlevania* soundtrack. It was released on March 24, 2010. It's one of the biggest soundtrack boxes ever created.

This box includes the first-ever releases of *Vampire Killer* (MSX2), *Castlevania Legends* (GB), *Kid Dracula (Game Boy)* (GB), additional tracks for the Saturn port of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, as well as the two arcade games: *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* and *Akumajō Dracula: The Medal*.

## Disc listing
    - Disc 01:** *Castlevania* (Disk System), *Vampire Killer* (MSX2), *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* (Disk System), *Haunted Castle* (Arcade), *Castlevania: The Adventure* (Game Boy)<br />
    - Disc 02:** *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* (NES), *Boku Dracula-kun* (NES), *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge*​ (Game Boy)<br />
    - Disc 03:** Super Castlevania IV (SNES)<br />
    - Disc 04:** *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* (PC-Engine), *Castlevania: Bloodlines/Castlevania: The New Generation* (Genesis)<br />
    - Disc 05:** *Castlevania Legends* (Game Boy), *Kid Dracula (Game Boy)* (Game Boy)<br />
    - Disc 06:** *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* (PlayStation, Sega Saturn)<br />
    - Disc 07:** *Castlevania* (N64)<br />
    - Disc 08:** *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* (Game Boy Advance), *Castlevania Chronicles* (PlayStation)<br />
    - Disc 09:** *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* (Game Boy Advance), *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* (Game Boy Advance)<br />
    - Disc 10:** *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* (PlayStation 2)<br />
    - Disc 11:** *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* (Nintendo DS)<br />
    - Disc 12:** *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* (PlayStation 2)<br />
    - Disc 13:** *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* (Nintendo DS)<br />
    - Disc 14:** *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* (PlayStation Portable)<br />
    - Disc 15:** *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* (Nintendo DS)<br />
    - Disc 16:** *Castlevania Judgment* (Nintendo Wii)<br />
    - Disc 17:** *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* (arcade), Akumajo Dracula The Medal (arcade)<br />
    - Disc 18:** Michiru Yamane's nine arranged tracks.<br />
    - DVD:** Features an interview with Michiru Yamane and videos of recording sessions.<br />
