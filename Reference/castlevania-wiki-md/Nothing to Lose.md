# Nothing to Lose

- Nothing to Lose** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania*. It appears during Block 6, after the Clock Tower, during the fight with Dracula.

Arranged versions later appeared in *Castlevania: Bloodlines* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
