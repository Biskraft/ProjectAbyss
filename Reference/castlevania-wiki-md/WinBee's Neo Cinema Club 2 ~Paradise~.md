# WinBee's Neo Cinema Club 2 ~Paradise~

is a music CD with arrangements by Kenichi Mitsuda of compositions made by the Konami Kukeiha Club. It was published by Konami and distributed by King Records on August 5, 1994 in Japan.

The soundtrack contains arrangements of music from many Konami games, including Prelude from *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* and "The Apocalypse" from *Getsu Fūma Den*.
