# Fishes

- Fishes** usually appear in the water, but can sometimes fly through the air. Killer Fish patrol the waters, but despite their ferocious appearance, are usually quite tame. Flying Fish of various types will jump out of the water. **Dead Trout** can be encountered over waterfalls. The Sky Fish flies at incredibly high speeds horizontally from one end of the screen to the other. Projectile-spewing Fish Head skeletons resemble underwater Bone Pillars. Anthropomorphic Fish Men are one of the staples of nearly all *Castlevania* games.
