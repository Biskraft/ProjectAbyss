# Golem (card)

The **Golem Card** is a DSS Attribute Card in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It adds an Earth attribute to a paired Action Card.

## Trivia
- The Golem had also previously appeared in card form in the game *Castlevania: Bloodlines*, where a card depicting the monster is summoned by Death, among other similar cards, and when struck, the player is teleported to fight it in a miniboss battle.
