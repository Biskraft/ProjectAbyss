# Silver Ring

The **Silver Ring** is an accessory found in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It has no other function except than to have access to the Castle Center when it is equipped in conjunction with the Gold Ring while Alucard is in the clock room located in the Marble Gallery.

Its inscription says: *"...In...Tower"*, and the inscription on the Gold Ring says: *"Wear...Clock..."*. These inscriptions are a hint to wear both of them in the clock room, where Alucard first met Maria, and complete the whole phrase:

<p style="text-align: center;">*"Wear in clock tower"*</p>
