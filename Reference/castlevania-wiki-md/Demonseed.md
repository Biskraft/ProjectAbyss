# Demonseed

- Demonseed** is a signature attack of the Man-eating Plants. It is a poisonous pollen.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, Charlotte Aulin can gain this spell by capturing a Man-eating Plant's attack, while Soma Cruz can gain the ability by stealing its soul.
