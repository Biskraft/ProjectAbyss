# Bracelet

- Bracelets**, Armbands, Wrist Bands, and Bangles are rings or bands that go around the arm.


## *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*
In *Circle of the Moon*, these are one of several types of items that can be equipped on one of Nathan's arms. The item called the Miracle Armband is not actually a bracelet, but instead is a Glove, and is called the Miracle Wrist in Japanese.


## *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*
In *Harmony of Dissonance*, these are considered Goods, rather than Hand Protection, so it is possible to wear more than one of them and also wear a Hand Protection such as a Glove at the same time.
Category: Arm Protection
