# Fairy (disambiguation)

The term **Faerie** or **Fairy** may refer to:
    - Faerie Familiar** — A familiar sprite that follows the player around and aids them mainly by automatically employing restorative potions or by providing hints.
      - Faerie Card**/**Fairy Card** — Relics that allows the player to summon either the "Faerie Familiar" or the "Fairy Familiar", respectively.
    - Faerie Scroll** — A relic that allows the player to see the name of an enemy on the screen when they attack it.
    - Fairy-Type** — A type of Innocent Devil in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
    - Fairies** — A sub-weapon in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* that allows the player to send out several fairies to distract enemies.
