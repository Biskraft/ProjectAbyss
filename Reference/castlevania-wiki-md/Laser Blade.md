# Laser Blade

right|73px
The **Laser Blade** is a one-handed sword in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. Shrouded in mystery and with varying reports of its weight and length, many say it does not even exist.

## Characteristics
The Laser Blade is the ultimate one-handed sword type weapon in the game. It is created by combining numerous other swords together, along with the Purple Glitter material stolen from the boss Dullahan. It gives ATK +55, being the game's most powerful one-handed sword and has the highest reach.

When sheathed, the sword has no blade; the blade's energy beam only appears when it is swung. The blade is semi-flexible and glows with a purple aura, making it the closest weapon to resemble a whip the player can wield.

It is the only weapon Hector is seen wielding in a cutscene. It is also the same sword Isaac uses in the second fight against him.
