# Ham and Eggs

- Ham and Eggs** are a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It recovers 22 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is one the lowest amount for any breakfast item, being one less than an Omelette, but one more than the highest fast food item Pizza.
