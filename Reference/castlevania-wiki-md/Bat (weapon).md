# Bat (weapon)

right|70px

A **Bat** is a club for playing baseball. In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the bat is spiked (with nails hammered through it) and appears as a Special weapon. It can be created by combining a Club with a Steel. As with all special melee weapons in the game, it will knock enemies down with a single hit. However, the animation for each swing is extremely slow and it only inflicts light damage (ATK +20), so it should be used moderately or to craft more useful items.
