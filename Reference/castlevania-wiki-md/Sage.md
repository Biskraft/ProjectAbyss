# Sage

right|50px

  - Sage** is a quest item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a medicinal herb required to complete three of the four quests Abram entrusts to Shanoa.

## Related quests
#"Running Out of Sage":
#***Objective**: Bring Abram some Sage.
#***Reward**: Adds Potion and Tonic to Jacob's shop.
#"Mandrake is the Best Medicine":
#***Objective**: Bring Abram some Sage and a Mandrake Root.
#***Reward**: Adds High Potion, Heart Repair and High Tonic to the shop.
#"Unusual Medicine Components":
#***Objective**: Bring Abram some Sage and Merman Meat.
#***Reward**: Adds Super Potion to the shop.
