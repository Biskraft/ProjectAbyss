# Mousse Cakes

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">Mousse Cakes, [[Canelé and Tiramisu]]
  - Mousse Cakes** are food items in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

The **Berry Mousse Cake** (called *Strawberry Mousse Cake* in Japan) recovers 80 MP and HP and increase INT by 4 and LCK by 8.

The **Mango Mousse Cake** recovers 80 MP and HP and increase INT by 4 and MND by 8.

The **Mint Mousse Cake** recovers 80 MP and HP and increase INT by 4, MND by 4 and LCK by 4. It is the most effective mousse cakes because it includes all the stats boost of previous cakes, even if the Luck increases by only 4, unlike Berry Mousse Cake.
