# Dorothy Elias-Fahn

- Dorothy Ann Elias-Fahn** is an American voice actress who is the wife of Tom Fahn (who is also a voice actor). She is best known for her roles as Meryl Stryfe in *Trigun*, Kaoru Kamiya in *Rurouni Kenshin*, Naru Narusegawa in *Love Hina*, and Chizuru Minamoto in *Kanokon*.

Dorothy provided the voice of Sypha Belnades in *Castlevania Judgment*.
