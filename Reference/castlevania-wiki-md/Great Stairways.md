# Great Stairways

- Great Stairway** areas are generally connector areas from one important location to another, and more often than not, do not offer a boss fight. The area generally consists of long vertical halls, with their numbers varying from game to game.

## Appearances
  - Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* - Abyss Stairway
  - Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* - Great Stairway
Category: Environments
