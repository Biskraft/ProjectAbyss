# Leaffle

right|200px
  - Leaffle** is the second evolution on the Fairy-Type evolutionary development chart and can be evolved from an Infant Fairy for 40 Knuckle/Sword Evo Crystals. These evolve into the Honey Bee and Killer Bee. They retain all of the Infant Fairy's abilities, as well as two new attacks: Time Heal for 40 EXP, which heals for 10 seconds; and Poison Powder for 100 EXP, which actually damages the enemy, poisoning them.
