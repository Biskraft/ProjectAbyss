# Sypha Belnades (disambiguation)

- Sypha Belnades** may refer to:

## Characters
    - 1989** — Sypha Belnades: A playable character in *Dracula's Curse* and *Judgment*.
    - 2013** — Sypha Belmont: One of the characters in the game *Mirror of Fate*, whose maiden name was Belnades.
    - 2017** — Sypha Belnades: A version of the character based on that from the original timeline and one of the main protagonists of the *Castlevania* animated series.

## Music
- Sypha — A music track in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness* used as the main theme of Camilla Fernandez, who was initially intended to be a wrecked version of Sypha Belnades.
