# Crow Witch Malphas

- Crow Witch Malphas** is a boss in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.

## Description
left
Reimagined as female in the *Lords of Shadow* saga, Malphas is said to haven been a once beautiful witch who threw herself off the highest tower of her castle after her lover was led to his death by a rival witch. She survived by forging a symbiotic relationship with the crows that came to feed on her broken body and over time became a crow demon herself. She is surrounded by a flock of crows and battles Gabriel by spewing heavy eggs that spawn half-bird half-human minions known as Witch Children.

Malphas sent her crows to spy on Baba Yaga and created the Scarecrows to keep the witch in check. It's suggested that Baba Yaga was the rival witch who took her lover.
