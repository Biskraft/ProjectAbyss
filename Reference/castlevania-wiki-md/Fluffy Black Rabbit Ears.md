# Fluffy Black Rabbit Ears

The **Fluffy Black Rabbit Ears** and **Fluffy White Rabbit Ears** are head accessories in *Castlevania Judgment*. They can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character.

## Item Data


## Trivia
- The description of the **Fluffy White Rabbit Ears** saying "show doc and everyone else what's up" is a reference to the classic cartoon character Bugs Bunny, whose most iconic catchphrase is: "What's up, Doc?"
