# Fireball

right|324px

A **Fireball** is a ball made of, or imbued with fire. It has been the most frequently used form of projectile since the first titles in the *Castlevania* series. In most games they are emitted from an enemy to attack the player. Fireballs appear as an enemy in their own right in *Castlevania Legends*. Fireballs can also be used as a spell a hero can use.

## Characteristics
The basic fireball attack is a small ball of fire that is launched forward at moderate speed. There are several variations of the Fireball spell, though. These include Dracula's triple Hellfire spell; the slow and large Dark Inferno; and the large and fast Flame Shot. Hellfire and Dark Inferno can only be used while standing, while the basic Fireball and Fire Shot can also be used in the air.

Soma Cruz is able to obtain this attack from a Fish Head in *Aria of Sorrow*. Charlotte Aulin is able to obtain it from a Bone Pillar in *Harmony of Despair*.
