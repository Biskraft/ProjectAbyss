  - Dracula's Fate** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the background theme that plays when Soma Cruz discovers his true identity as Dracula's reincarnation shortly after his fight against Graham.

The track also plays during the game's bad ending, when Soma loses to Chaos and succumbs to the castle's influence on his soul.

## Trivia
- The song's name might refer to Soma himself. As an inheritor of Dracula's vast power, he is preordained to be his successor. On that note, the track itself represents Soma's failure to annul what Chaos perceived was meant to be.
