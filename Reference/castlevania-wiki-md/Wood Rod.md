# Wood Rod

right|200px
  - Wood Rod** is a Mage-Type Innocent Devil in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the initial form of the Mage-Type. It is found in a room in Cordova Town.

It is a balanced familiar who natively knows the Time Stop skill and can learn Lightning Strike.
