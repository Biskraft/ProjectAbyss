# MIDI Power Pro6

- MIDI Power Pro6: Akumajō Dracula X: Gekka no Yasōkyoku** is a collection of MIDI arrangements of video game soundtracks. Number 6 of this series is *Dracula X: Nocturne in the Moonlight* and was released on November 27, 1998. The arrangers are Eisaku Nambu, Haruhiko Kuroiwa and Hiroshi Ebihara.
