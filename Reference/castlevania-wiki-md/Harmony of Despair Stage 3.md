  - The End of Chaos** is the third chapter from *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It appears to be mainly comprised of parts of the Skeleton Cave from *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, though the concept seems to be slightly based off of the Abyss from *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, in which the player must progress around the center of the level and reach the middle to fight Menace. Much like in the original game, Piercing Battle Fury is featured as the theme that is played when the creature is fought.

### Hidden item
The Moai appears as a hidden item in this chapter. After waking the boss up and having him destroy the middle platforms, go back to the top and go down in the middle of the map. you Must perform a Jump Kick through the center of the vortex, to have the Moai appear above you. This meaning Charlotte and Yoko can not get the item. You could also jump kick the boss to hit the center of the vortex if he's in the center of the room.
