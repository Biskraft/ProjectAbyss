# Medusa Pendant

The **Medusa Pendant** is a pendant that increases resistance to petrification in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is similar to the Medusa Ring that appears in *Curse of Darkness* and it's dropped by the Gold Medusa. Oddly enough, the pendant is mysteriously absent from the version of *Harmony of Dissonance* included in the *Castlevania: Double Pack*.
