# Battle of the Holy

- Battle of the Holy** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania: The Adventure*. It is the background theme for the first stage.

An arranged version later appears in *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth* as the bonus track in the Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth & Contra ReBirth Original Soundtrack.
