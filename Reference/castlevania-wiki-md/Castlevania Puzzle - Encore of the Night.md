    - Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*** is the first *Castlevania* puzzle game, featuring Alucard and other cast members of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* made for the iPhone and iPod Touch, as well as Windows Phone. It retells the story of the original *Symphony of the Night*. It features an RPG system and touched up sprites. It was removed from the iOS App Store some time before October 2016.
