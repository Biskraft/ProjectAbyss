  - Joma Marsh** (ジョマの沼 *Joma no numa*, Swamp of Joma) is a swamp area in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. The path enters the marsh from the Debious Woods, leads to the Laruba Mansion, and again heads towards and through to the Joma Marsh, and takes you to the other side of Camilla Cemetery that was encountered earlier. The marshes are poisonous and can take a while to get through and some parts are uncrossable without consuming at least one Laurel. Fortunately, there is a gypsy in the nearby Laruba Mansion who will fill up your supply of Laurels.
