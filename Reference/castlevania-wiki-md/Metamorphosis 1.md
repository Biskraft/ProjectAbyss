# Metamorphosis 1

- Metamorphosis 1** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is identified as the background theme that plays during the opening cutscene that depicts a CGI panning animation of Dracula's Castle leading to the tower containing his throne room.
