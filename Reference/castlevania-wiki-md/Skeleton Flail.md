# Skeleton Flail

The **Skeleton Flail**, or **Flail Guard**, is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is a skeleton armed with a spiked iron flail.

## Description
The Skeleton Flail (called **Skeleton Morningstar** in Japan) is a skeleton wielding a large flail with a morningstar at the end. It is similar to the Whip Skeleton, which has a morningstar attached at the end of a long whip. The Flail Guard spends much of its time sitting on the ground, but when approached, will stand up, move around slowly and then cast its flail toward the enemy, with the morningstar spinning around at the tip of the rod for a brief moment.

It is a type of Skeleton Chain.
