# Games Room

The **Games Room** is the ninth area Trevor explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. The massive game board on this stage is that of the Vampire Wargame from *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*, from the original Castle Hall.
