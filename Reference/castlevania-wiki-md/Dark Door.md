  - Dark Door** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is identified as the default background theme in Boss Rush Mode that plays in the starting area where the player can select their starting sub-weapon and in the intermission areas where they can refill their hearts before moving on to the next boss.
