# Morning Set

A **Morning Set** are a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It recovers 24 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is the highest amount for any breakfast, being one more than an Omelette. It is one less than the least health replenishing lunch, Lunch A.
