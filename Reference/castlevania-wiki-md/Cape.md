# Cape

The **Cape** is the most basic cape found in *Castlevania* and gives the least amount of Defense or Constitution of any capes. It is often made of cloth like the Cloth Cape.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, it gives a Defense boost of +1, which is less than a Crimson Cloak (DEF +3, INT +1). It gives the same in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it gives a Defense boost of +2, which is one less than a Traveler Cape.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, it gives a Defense boost of +1.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, like most capes, the basic Cape provides some defense against ICE and FIRE attacks (+5 each) and a token DEF bonus (+1). It is the only cape that does not also provide an HP and MP boost.
