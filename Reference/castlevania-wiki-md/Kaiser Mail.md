# Kaiser Mail

The **Kaiser Mail** is a body armor in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is an armor said to have been worn by emperors. It raises several other statistics other than Defense.

It raises DEF by 75, and STR, INT and LCK all by 5. This is the same total amount of attribute bonus that the Ceramic Mail has, which raises DEF by 90 (more than any other armor).
