# Red Bean Bun

A **Red Bean Bun** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it gives you 12 HP, which is the 2 less than a normal Chinese Bun and 5 less than a Pork Bun. It is dropped by a Black Crow, in a similar manner in which the Red Bean Bun is dropped by the Blue Raven.
