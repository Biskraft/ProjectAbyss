# Main Tower Outside

right|300px

  - Main Tower Outside** is the seventeenth area Simon explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. It is the outside of the Vampire's Tower. Here, Simon is continually harassed by a single Gargoyle.
