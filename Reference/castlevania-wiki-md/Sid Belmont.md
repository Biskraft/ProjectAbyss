# Sid Belmont

is a character in the alternate timeline gamebook *The Legend of Satanic Castle: The Vampire Hunters*. He is a descendant of Trevor Belmont.

## Trivia
- His surname is "Belmont" and not "Belmondo" in spite of being a Japanese version.
