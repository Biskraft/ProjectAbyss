# Area Attack

right|300px

The **Area Attack** is a basic attack in the *Lords of Shadow* games. It attacks enemies on all sides of the player once using the player's Primary Weapon by pressing a designated button.

In the first *Lords of Shadow*, the whip is swung in a circle attacking enemies at the same height as Gabriel all around him.

In *Mirror of Fate*, it is swung overhead, capable of attacking enemies in front of, above, and behind the player. The Area Attack can be followed up with other attacks to perform chains, such as the Area Attack Combo.
