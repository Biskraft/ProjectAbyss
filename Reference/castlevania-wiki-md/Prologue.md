# Prologue

Six different music themes are called **Prologue** in the *Castlevania* series:
- A theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club, for the original NES game *Castlevania* and *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula*.
- A theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Super Castlevania IV*.
- A theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.
- A theme composed by Masahiko Kimura, Motoaki Furukawa, and Mariko Egawa for *Castlevania* (N64).
- A theme composed by Soshiro Hokkai for *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.
- A theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*.
- A theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.

## Trivia
- In Castlevania: Lament of Innocence Original Soundtrack, there is no voice speaking during the song "Prologue", while in the first preorder CDs there was a narrator's voice. This also happens with the song "Bloodstained Demise".
