# Bone Throw

right|268px
  - Bone Throw** is an ability to throw a giant bone, and is a signature attack for skeletons, which tend to rip out their own ribs and then toss them. They are normally thrown at a high angle upward, in an arcing pattern, and then come back down. Soma Cruz is able to perform this move by stealing a Skeleton's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*. His throw in *Dawn of Sorrow* is at a much lower angle, to help differentiate the move from his Spinning Edge axe toss.

Other characters are able to throw bones if they morph into skeletons, such as Nathan Graves in *Circle of the Moon* with the Pluto Card and Black Dog Card combo; or when a player dies in *Harmony of Despair*, but the game is still going. There is 12.5% chance for throwing a gigantic bone that deals a lot of damage. The small bone is as strong as 45% of Nathan's STR, while the big bone is as strong as 3,000 STR. Summoned skeletons can also use this attack.
