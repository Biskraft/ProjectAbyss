# Nintendo Power - Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse guide

Issue #18 of Nintendo Power featured a guide to the first part of *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* before reaching the castle. It contained a significant amount of original artwork for the characters and creatures found in that game.
