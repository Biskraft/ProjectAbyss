# Ricochet Rock

right
The **Ricochet Rock** (called **Diamond** in *Simon's Quest* and **Rebound Stone** in *Symphony of the Night*) is a sub-weapon that is thrown downward at a 45° angle and bounces off any surface it strikes, doing this repeatedly every time it strikes a surface for a while or until it leaves the screen.

Its first appearance was in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* and it reappeared later in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and as one of Jonathan's skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
