# Memorial Ticket

right|65px

The **Memorial Ticket** is an item which appears in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. Its usage is similar to that of the Magical Ticket, but instead of being transported to the game's shop, the player will be returned to the last registered Save Room they accessed.

In *Lament of Innocence*, the ticket will only work if the player actually saved the game at a Save Room. Just entering one or attempting to save and then canceling won't register it as the next warping location.
