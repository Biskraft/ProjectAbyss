# Ripple Laser

right|278px
The **Ripple Laser** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained from a Man-Eater. This attack is an homage to a variety of ripple laser attacks which appear in the *Gradius* series, also from Konami. For example, Soma's version of this attack in *Aria of Sorrow* is reminiscent to the ring shaped lasers used by Moais in several *Gradius* games, going to the extent of portraying them blue and red colored. Gaibon's attack in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* resembles the Ripple Laser shot type which a player can choose to equip for the Vic Viper in most *Gradius* games.
