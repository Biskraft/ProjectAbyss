# Mask of the Time Reaper

The **Mask of the Time Reaper** is a face accessory in *Castlevania Judgment*. It can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character. Its effigy represents the Time Reaper.

## Characteristics
A different mask can be obtained for each playable character the player beats the Time Reaper with on Brutal Mode. A different symbol appears on each mask, which usually represents the weapon or characteristic fighting style of that character.
