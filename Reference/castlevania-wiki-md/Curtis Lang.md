# Curtis Lang

is the apprentice of Julius Belmont, as seen in the novel *Akumajō Dracula: Kabuchi no Tsuisoukyoku*. He wields the Holy Whip, which is a whip with less power than the Vampire Killer that Julius uses. He works as a hunter of the church and is assisted by his possible romantic interest Michelle Danasty, who is a formidable hunter in her own right and a descendant of Grant Danasty.

## Notes
  - Akumajō Dracula: Kabuchi no Tsuisoukyoku* does not have the cut-in illustration. His official art does not exist.
