# Keizo Nakamura

, also known as **Jigokuguruma Nakamura**, is a music composer, sound designer and video game programmer.

He has not been affiliated with Konami since his departure.

## Background
Keizo Nakamura composed several music pieces for the 1993 NEC PC Engine game *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*, including: Cross a Fear, Cemetery, Slash, Ghost Ship Painting, Op.13, The Nest, and March of the Righteous.

He also composed some tracks for the Sharp X68000 game *Akumajō Dracula*, released earlier that same year.

## Selected works
  - Moon: Remix RPG Adventure* (1997) (sound programmer)
  - Snatcher* (1994) (sound designer)
  - Tokimeki Memorial* (1994) (music)
  - Castlevania Chronicles* (1993) (sound designer)
  - Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* (1993) (sound)
