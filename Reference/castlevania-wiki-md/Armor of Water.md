# Armor of Water

The **Armor of Water** is an armor that offers protection against Water based attacks. It is similar in function to the Ice Mail found in other games, which have an Ice element type instead of a Water element.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, it grants +33 DEF. This is one less than the Armor of Fire.
