# Perfect Guard

right|300px

  - Perfect Guard** is a skill that completely blocks most enemy attacks if a character rises their guard at the exact moment the attack would connect. With each successful Perfect Guard, a small amount of MP and Hearts will be replenished.

In *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, Leon must learn this technique before it can be used, while Hector must first obtain this skill in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. Additionally, Trevor Belmont starts with this skill available in his own mode in *Curse of Darkness*. 

In Hector's mode in *Curse of Darkness*, many enemies require the player to successfully perform a Perfect Guard against one of their specific attacks in order to open their stealing windows.
