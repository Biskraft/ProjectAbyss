# Magic Book (Dracula's Curse)

- Magic Books** are three different sub-weapons Sypha Belnades is able to obtain in *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*, each of which allows her to cast a powerful elemental-based spell:
- Flames (Fire) - A quick, long and damaging burst of flame is cast in front of Sypha.
- Frozen Crystals (Ice) - Five frozen crystals will spread out in front, above and behind Sypha. They have a relatively small range, but any enemies or projectiles that touch them will be instantly frozen.
- Goodness Gracious Great Bolts of Lightning (Lightning) - Three large powerful lightning orbs are created, which then home in on an enemy.
