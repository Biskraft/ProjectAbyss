# Evil's Symphonic Poem

- Evil's Symphonic Poem** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is considered to be Death's theme. An arrangement later appeared in *Castlevania Judgment* serving the same purpose.
