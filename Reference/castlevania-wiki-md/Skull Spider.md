# Skull Spider

The **Skull Spider** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a monstrous spider with a skull-shaped body.

## Description
A monstrous spider with a skull-shaped body that can climb and crawl on any surface it comes across with, either ground, wall or ceiling. Making contact with it will poison the player. Being a skeletal monster, it is susceptible to Strike-type attacks, so equipping a Macir glyph when fighting them would be a good idea.

It drops the Fidelis Aranea glyph, which summons a Skull Spider to fight alongside Shanoa, poisoning any enemies it comes in contact with unless they are resistant to this status effect.

In *Castlevania Judgment*, the **Skull Spider** is an arms accessory that can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character.
