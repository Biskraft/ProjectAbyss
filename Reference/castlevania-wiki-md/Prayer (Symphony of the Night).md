  - Prayer** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. A rare combination of medieval chant, mixed with polyphonic and homophonic voicing (independent melodies and harmony together). It plays during the main menu and Name Entry screens.

The vocals are sung by Michiru Yamane's younger sister.
