# Crest

A **Crest** is the portion of a heraldic display that is depicted on the top of a helmet.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*
The **Crest** in *Legacy of Darkness* is separated in two pieces, both found in the Villa :
    - Crest Half A** is found at the top of the fountain and requires the Rose Brooch in order to reach.
    - Crest Half B** can be found in a torch at the top of a stone structure in the middle of the Hedge Maze.
Inserting both halves near the entrance to a building in the Hedge Maze allows access to an underground chamber where the boss of the stage, Gilles de Rais, is encountered.

### *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*
The **Crest** is an medal-like item that can be found in Trevor Mode. It increases all of Trevor Belmont's stats ( +50 HP, +5 Hearts, +5 ATK, +5 DEF). It appears after defeating a boss, excepted for Isaac's second fight, Death and Dracula. These only appear on the main playthrough and are not available in Boss Rush Mode.

The player must make sure that the item should be obtained. If a Crest item is not picked up before exiting a boss room, it will vanish forever, even if the player re-enters the room.

Two of these items can be picked up without having to fight a boss: the first can be found in the Aiolon Ruins, in the room where Trevor and Isaac clash for the first time, and the second in front of stairs leading to the Throne Room, just after the fight against Death.
