  - Sorrow's Distortion** is a music theme composed by Yasuhiro Ichihashi for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia.* It is the background music that plays during the battle against Albus. It begins with a pipe organ piece before it quickly changes to an electric guitar solo for the duration of the theme.
