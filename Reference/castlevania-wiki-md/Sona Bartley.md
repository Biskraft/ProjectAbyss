# Sona Bartley

- Sona Bartley** is a character in the graphic novel *Castlevania: The Belmont Legacy*, published by IDW. She is the niece of Lord Ivan Bartley. She has loose morals and lusts after Dracula and his power.

## Plot
Following Dracula's resurrection, Sona mocks her uncle for his failure to restore the vampire's full strength, offering her own blood as a *fine vintage*, as well as her own body to satisfy his urges.

Dracula soon tires of her, however, which seems to have an adverse effect on her mind. By the time Illyana is captured, Sona is seen wandering the catacombs, naked and covered with scars, although still fanatically loyal to Dracula.

She is beheaded by Pascha during the final battle.
