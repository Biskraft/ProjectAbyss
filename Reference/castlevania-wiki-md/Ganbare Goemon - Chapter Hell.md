# Ganbare Goemon: Chapter Hell

is a two-issue manga in the *Ganbare Goemon* series, published by Kodansha in 1993 in Japan.

## Plot
Goemon and Ebisumaru meet Pierre Belmont (the alleged grandfather of Simon Belmont) in the underworld, and joining forces with him, they manage to defeat Count Dracula.
