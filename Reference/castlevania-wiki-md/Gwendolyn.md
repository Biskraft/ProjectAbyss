# Gwendolyn

- Gwendolyn** was Eric Lecarde's girlfriend. She was turned into a vampire by the Countess Elizabeth Bartley soon after she was revived. Her fate is unclear, but it does appear that Eric was unable to change her back to a human and he may have been forced to destroy her. Her turning is the catalyst that motivates Eric to hunt down the vampire Countess.
