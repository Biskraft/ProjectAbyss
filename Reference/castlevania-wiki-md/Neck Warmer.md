# Neck Warmer

The **Neck Warmer** is the most effective scarf in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It gives a CON boost of +10 and a DEF boost of +3, which is greater than the +5 CON and +2 DEF that a Red Scarf gives.
