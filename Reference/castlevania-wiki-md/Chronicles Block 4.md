# Chronicles Block 4

- The Red-Brick Cathedral** is the fourth stage in *Castlevania Chronicles*.

## Overview
This stage is based on the second stage, acting as both an expansion and a rearrangement of the original game. The first and third block remain roughly intact while the second block is omitted entirely. Notable new additions include a huge statue which presumably depicts Medusa in her human form and a Stained Glass Knight who promptly attacks the player.
