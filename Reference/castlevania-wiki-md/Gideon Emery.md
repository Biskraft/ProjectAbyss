# Gideon Emery

- Gideon Emery** is an English actor and voice actor. He voiced Trevor Belmont as a replacement of Terrence Stone in *Castlevania Judgment*.

Gideon's famous character roles are Balthier in *Final Fantasy XII* and Steve Fox in *Tekken* series.
