# Go! Getsu Fuma

- Go! Getsu Fuma** is a music theme originally from the 1987 action/role playing game *Getsu Fūma Den*. It was arranged by Yasushi Asada for *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It later appeared in *Super Smash Bros Ultimate*.
