# Pudding

- Pudding** is a food item that recovers health in the *Castlevania* series. Its first appearance was as a food item for Maria in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and later in *Dracula X Chronicles*. In *Symphony of the Night*, it gives the player 17 HP, which is the highest amount given by any dessert. It gives you one more HP than a Parfait and one less than a Cheese, which gives you the least amount of HP for any entree. It can only be obtained with a Meal Ticket in that game.

Pudding also appears in *Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow*, *Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia*. *Portrait of Ruin* also contains a Pudding Bucket, which gives a substantial higher amount of HP back.

Pudding is dropped by enemies that like to eat it, such as Chronomage and Succubus in *Aria of Sorrow*, Dead Mate in *Dawn of Sorrow*, and Amalaric Sniper in *Portrait of Ruin*. Pudding can also be found in the Underground Reservoir in *Aria of Sorrow*.

In *Harmony of Despair*, it is a dessert which increase HP and MP by the same small amount (+15). It gives a good boost to both, INT and LCK (+5). This is better than the Red Licorice, which is otherwise the same except that it reduces LCK by 5 rather than increasing it. It gives more LCK and MP than any other dessert, but gives less health than some others (the common Berry Cruller increases HP and MP by +25 but LCK and MP by +1, Strawberry Donut gives +35 HP and MP and +2 LCK and MP).
