# Frying Pan

A **Frying Pan** is intended for cookery and hot from the kitchen. It causes first degree burns to all who are hit with it. It is the second weakest axe in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.

It is a "trap" combination devised to waste the rare Phlogiston material and creating it should be avoided unless looking on acquiring a complete item set.
