The **Bee Hive** (or **Beehive** when referred to as an item) is a bizarrely shaped hive that continually spawns Killer Bees until destroyed. It resembles an upside-down skull and bones opening its thoracic cage, from where the bees come out.

## Description
The Bee Hive takes the shape of a grotesquely shaped skull, complete with an open mouth and eye sockets. It appears in the Forest of Doom, hanging to the ceilings of some rooms. It spawns Killer Bees endlessly but does not attack the player itself. One may kill the Killer Bees even before they start flying in the air, but they will keep spawning until the hive is destroyed. Players should use either Jonathan's whip or Charlotte's Raging Fire spell if close enough, but if they are far away, Jonathan should use the Crossbow skill to quickly destroy the hive. If dealing with a crowded spawned swarm, Charlotte should still use Raging Fire and Jonathan should use Grenade if they are low enough. 

Every time the hive is hit, it will spawn one or two Killer Bees. Most of the times, these will be killed with the same attack used to hit the hive itself, but if they manage to survive they'll be very hard to dodge.

## Quest
Obtaining a Beehive is the requirement of one of Wind's quests: "Build Your Strength 1". Not surprisingly, this item can be obtained as a drop from the Bee Hive itself. Completing this quest will earn the player with a maximum HP increase.
