# Blessed Wings

right|200px
  - Blessed Wings** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It allows to raise INT and LCK.

## Characteristics
When cast, the player is given a bonus to INT and LCK; however, one must hold down the **R** button and charge it to gain the effect. The longer the button is held, the larger the bonus received. The maximum bonus amounts this soul yields are INT +1-75 and LCK +0-18 based off charge time, and can be obtained by holding the button for up to 9 seconds (consumes 15 MP/TIME while charging). It must be noted, though, that even if the player collects 9 Flying Humanoid's souls, there won't be any further extra bonuses granted. The soul ceases to function —even when fully charged— if the MP meter is depleted.

When cast, a pair of angelic wings will grow from Soma's back; this is only an aesthetic, though, as they won't allow flight or affect gameplay in any way.

## Availability
This ability can be obtained by gaining dominance over the Flying Humanoid's soul. It can be obtained in the Cursed Clock Tower, in the room between the Save Room and Zephyr's boss room. The only way to kill it is by using the Mandragora's soul, Last Scream. It has a 100% drop rate.
