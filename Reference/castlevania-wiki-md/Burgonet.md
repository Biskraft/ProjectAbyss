# Burgonet

The **Burgonet** is the second type of helmet that can be evolved from a Sallet in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be created by adding Damascus Steel and Red Steel to the Burgonet, increasing its Defense from +14 to +22. This will overtake the Defense of the other type of helmet that can be evolved from a Sallet, the Armet (+20). It can be evolved once more using Ceramics to form the second toughest helmet in the game, the Crest Helm (+22).
