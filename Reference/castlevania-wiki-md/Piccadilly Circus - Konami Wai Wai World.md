# Piccadilly Circus: Konami Wai Wai World

is a redemption roulette-themed game released in 1994. It was developed and published by Konami and is part of the *Piccadilly Circus* subseries in the *Wai Wai* series.

This game features many Konami mascot characters, such as Simon Belmont from the *Castlevania* series, Goemon and Ebisumaru from the *Ganbare Goemon* series, Konami Man and Konami Lady from *Konami Wai Wai World*, and Moai from *Moai-kun* and the *Gradius* series.
