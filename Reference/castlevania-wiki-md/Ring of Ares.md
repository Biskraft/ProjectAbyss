# Ring of Ares

The **Ring of Ares**, known as **Laurin's Ring** in Japan, is a ring that allegedly confers the strength of twenty-four men to its wearer.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The Ring of Ares can be found by destroying a wall in the Abandoned Pit to the Catacomb. Wearing it confers the strength of twenty-four men. In practice, that means that the player will gain STR +24 (presumably meaning men have a strength stat of 1). Unfortunately, it takes what it gives: DEF -24.

There are no other accessories in other games that have this same effect, but the Berserker Mail in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* increases STR by 30 and then decreases both CON and MND by the same amount.
