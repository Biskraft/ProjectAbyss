# Wind Ring

The **Wind Ring** is a ring that has different effects in different games.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, it offers resistance against Wind-type attacks.

In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, it increases the power of Glyphs that have the Slash attribute. It also doubles the range of the Pneuma Glyph.
