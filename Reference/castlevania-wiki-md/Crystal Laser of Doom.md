The **Crystal Laser of Doom** is a boss in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.

## Description
The Crystal Laser of Doom encompasses a whole room at the end of the Tower of Science. It is armed with lasers and a variety of other machines guns. The crystal is actually the weak point, and getting past its defenses is the only way to take down this guardian.
