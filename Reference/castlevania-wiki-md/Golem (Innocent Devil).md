# Golem (Innocent Devil)

right|200px


  - Golem** is a Battle-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a Level 2 Innocent Devil and can learn Hip Press and Hip Press Lv.2. It can be obtained from a Magmard Devil by obtaining 40 **<span style="color:red;">Sword</span>**/**<span style="color:white;">Special</span>** Evo Crystals.

## Description
Being able to learn the Hip Press ability and its upgrade, the Golem form is another necessity when it comes to map exploration. The Hip Press is helpful for breaking down the cracked floors found in dungeons to reveal secret areas. This evolution of the Battle-Type also has a very high Heart growth stat, but trades off with a low ATK and AGL growth.
