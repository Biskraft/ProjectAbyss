# Haruko Kitahama

- Haruko Kitahama** is a veteran Japanese voice actress born on July 1, 1938 in Tokyo. She is currently affiliated with Aoni Production.

Kitahama is known for her work in *The Little Mermaid* (1975) and *Mazinkaiser* (2001), among a vast repertoire of other works throughout her voiceover career.

She provided the voice of the Succubus and Medusa in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*.
