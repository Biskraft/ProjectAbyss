  - Vlad Graveyard** (or Vrad Graveyard) is the last stage containing any enemies before reaching Dracula's Castle. It is filled with Pirate Skeletons, Mummys and Eagles. It is located just beyond the town of Ghulash and leads to the West Bridge, which can only be crossed if you have all the items you need.
