# Sallet

right|73px
The **Sallet** is a helmet that appears in *Castlevania*.

It can be purchased from Merchant in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It grants a Defense boost of +7, which is one greater than a Leather Helmet, but one less than a Bicocette.

It is created by adding Indigo Steel to a Gallic Helmet, which raises its defense from +10 to +14. This is 2 higher than another type of helmet that can be evolved from the Gallic Helmet, the Frog Mouth Helm. It will be overtaken when the Thracian Helmet evolves to a Barrel Helm (+16) and again when Frog Mouth Helm when it evolves into a Lobster Tail Pod (+18). However, both of these helmets cannot be further evolved and the Sallet can be evolved to overtake them. Adding Red Steel creates an Armet (+20). Adding a Damascus Steel and Red Steel to the Sallet creates a Burgonet (+22). All of the materials needed to form a second Sallet in order to create both helmets are common drop items (Indigo Steel, Knight's Vein, Bronze, Wild Memory). Each of these can be evolved further, although the Armet is the one that will evolve to become the toughest helmet in the game, the Cross Helmet.
