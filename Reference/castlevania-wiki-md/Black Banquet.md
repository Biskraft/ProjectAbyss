  - Black Banquet** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is played during the final battle against Dracula.

It later appeared in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, mixed in another theme called "Castlevania Reincarnation".
