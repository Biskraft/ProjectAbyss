# Cream Soda

- Cream Soda** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It consists of mint chip ice cream with soda topped with whip cream and a cherry. It is called **Cream Soda** in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Dawn of Sorrow* and is referred to as a **Mint Sundae** in *Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia*, but it would perhaps be best described as an "Ice Cream Soda", traditionally made with melon flavored soda and vanilla ice cream in Japan.

Cream Soda is dropped by female enemies who enjoy it, such as the Student Witch in *Aria of Sorrow*, Witch in Dawn of Sorrow, and Fake Sypha in *Portrait of Ruin*. It can also be found in Dracula's Castle in *Order of Ecclesia*.
