# Beverage

- Beverages** are meter replenishing food items that can be drunk. In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, they replenish HP like any other food item. In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, they are distinguished from food in that they replenish MP instead of HP. In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, they increase MP recovery rates.

## *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
Beverages in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* recover a small amount of food that is a multiple of five. These items give the same amount of health as food of other types do. These are all alcoholic beverages in the original Japanese versions, but their names have been censored in the North American versions. Sake was changed to "Green Tea", Beer to "Barley Tea", and Wine to "Grape Juice".
