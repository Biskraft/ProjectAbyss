# Eagle Shot

right|324px
  - Eagle Shot** is a bullet soul which summons an Eagle which is then sent flying straight ahead, damaging anything in its path. It can be used by Soma Cruz with an Altair's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, and an 8-bit version can also be used by him in *Harmony of Despair* with an R. Eagle's soul.
