# Cestus

right|85px

  - Cestus**, or **Knuckles**, is a hard knuckle-guard worn by ancient boxers. In the *Castlevania* series, they can be either a particular weapon or a complete class to which all fist or punch based weapons belong.


## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
In *Symphony of the Night*, **Cestus** refers to an entire class for fist based weapons. Alucard will also always attack with his fist if he has no other weapons equipped, and any fist-based attack in any context has the attribute of increased attack rate/speed the closer he is to an attack-able target.


### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
In *Dawn of Sorrow*, **Cestus** refers to an entire class for fist based weapons.


### *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*
In *Curse of Darkness*, **Knuckles** refers to an entire class for fist based weapons.


### *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
In *Harmony of Despair*, **Cestus** refers to an entire class for fist based weapons.
