# INT Boost

right|300px

  - INT Boost** is a Stat Boost spell or power that increases Intelligence.


## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
In *Aria of Sorrow*, it increases exclusively the damage power of Guardian Souls. Soma Cruz is able to obtain this stat by gaining dominance over the Enchanted Souls of four different creatures, thus dividing the INT granted in four levels of power, with a difference of four units between each other:
    - Craft** - Red Crow (INT +4)
    - Wisdom of Abstinence** - Lilith (INT +8)
    - Devil's Wisdom** - Bael (INT +12)
    - God's Wisdom** - Stolas (INT +16)

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* / *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
In *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*, Soma is able to gain this ability from a Lilith's and Imp's souls respectively, and the more such souls he has, the more powerful the effect.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
INT Boost is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Portrait of Ruin*. When cast, it boosts INT temporarily. A regular cast boosts INT +15, while a fully charged cast boosts INT +30.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
  - Sapiens Fio** is a back glyph that increases Shanoa's intelligence. This bonus scales as her INT stat increases, and thus, grows in power as she levels up.

It can be obtained on a high ledge in Oblivion Ridge, just before entering Gravedorcus's boss room. To reach it, all that is needed is the Ordinary Rock (obtained in the Skeleton Cave). The player should stand on the platform that is in the middle of the room, facing to the left; as soon as an Altair appears from the left side, double jump over it and perform a jump kick, ending with another double jump to reach the ledge.
