# Kitchen

The **Kitchen** is the tenth area Simon explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*, and the fifth area Alucard explores in his journey. This area is highly likely where the hunchbacks reside to set traps/ambushes in the area for intruders, capture them and use them for carrion like Brotherhood of Light Soldiers (most likely successful), Simon Belmont (Failed), Alucard (Failed).
