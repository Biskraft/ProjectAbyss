# Potato Pancake

- Potato Pancake** (*Monja* in Japanese version) is a food item in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is fourth (fifth if counting Spoiled Milk with right soul) best healing item, being before Ramen (500 HP), and after Tasty Meat (290 HP).
