# Fool's Tablet

The **Fool's Tablet** is a writing pad that can be equipped in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It causes the wearer to have the Poison status when equipped. A small compensation for this, is that it does offer protection against Curse and Stone status, protection against Dark attacks, and offers a STR and INT boost of +6. It can be found in the downloadable chapter *Lord of Flies*.
