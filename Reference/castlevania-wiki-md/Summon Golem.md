# Summon Golem

right|300px

  - Summon Golem** is one of the ten summoning spells that Nathan Graves is able to perform using the Dual Set-Up System card mechanic in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. Nathan must equip the Uranus and Golem cards and activate DSS to summon the Golem and cause Earth damage to all enemies on screen.
