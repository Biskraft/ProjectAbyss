# Sketch Book

The **Sketch Book** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. A villager from Wygol, Daniela, bestows this item to Shanoa, asking her to make drawings from various places throughout her journey. These are all locations with a sentimental value for Daniela, as they were places where she lived great adventures with her grandfather when she was younger, like monster hunting, learning the secret arts, or her own grandfather's resting place.

## Trivia
- The Sketch Book's in-game description says it's *"a grandmother's gift to her favorite grandchild"*. Since Daniela talks very fondly of her grandfather, the description may hint she was also very close to her grandmother; or, the act of Daniela giving the book to Shanoa, hint that she considers her as her own granddaughter.
