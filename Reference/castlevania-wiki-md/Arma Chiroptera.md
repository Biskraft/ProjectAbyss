# Arma Chiroptera

right|300px
  - Arma Chiroptera** is a back glyph that turns Shanoa into a werebat, albeit with a different color scheme on her body.


## Overview
While in this form, you cannot use the menu without getting out of the transformation. Pressing the primary attack button will allow you to throw bats, up to five at a time. These bats do not disappear after hitting an enemy, making it a very useful tool in long corridors. Pressing the secondary attack button makes Shanoa do a kick, dealing damage and giving her +1 HP for every hit to an enemy or object (like a candle). Unlike its fellow transforming glyphs (Arma Felix and Arma Machina), Werebats, Draculinas and Liliths will still attack you.
