# Magnes

right|250px

  - Magnes** is a back glyph that creates a magnetic field, allowing Shanoa to pull and fling herself from metal spheres. It is also Shanoa's Personal Skill in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

In order to use this glyph, the player must find a magnetic disc found on obvious areas of the dungeon (usually in mid-air), and hold the R trigger. There is a radius wherein Shanoa automatically attaches herself to the center of the magnetic disc, as a blue circumference appears around her. The player can then use the directional keys to aim and propel Shanoa in a straight line like a slingshot. Should she fall off, or should the player miss, Shanoa can still re-attach to the disc by holding the R trigger again. The length of the pull affects the distance and velocity of Shanoa, and it is usually better to utilize the glyph's maximum pull length.
