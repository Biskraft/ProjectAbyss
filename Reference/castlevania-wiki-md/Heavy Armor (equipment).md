# Heavy Armor (equipment)

The **Heavy Armor** is a suit of armor that is extremely heavy.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it gives +48 DEF, which is 3 more than Ancient Armor and 4 less than Plate Mail.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, it gives 55 DEF, which is more than any other item in the game, plus Strength +5 and Mind +40. The armor is very heavy, however, and wearing it will slow down Jonathan. He will need good timing, or the aid of Speed Up if he wishes to dodge attacks.
