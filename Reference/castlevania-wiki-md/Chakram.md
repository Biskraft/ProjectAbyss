# Chakram

right|50px
The **Chakram** is a throwing weapon with a sharp, circular blade.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it is dropped by the Discus Lord. If the button is pressed twice, a second Chakram is released along with the first. By holding the weapon, the throw will be longer. If two are equipped (in both right and left hands) it is possible to throw both (given the player's ability to fire two chakrams from each hand).

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, it is a Soma-exclusive weapon, despite the fact that Alucard was able to use one in Symphony of the Night.
