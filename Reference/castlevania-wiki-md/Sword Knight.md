# Sword Knight

- Sword Knights** are an armored type of knights who wield a sword. In some games, the "regular" knight will use a sword instead of a spear. The first armor shown to use a sword was the boss Sir Grakul in *Super Castlevania IV*, who could also swing an axe. Many of the elemental armors in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* wield swords. Many Final Guards also wield swords.
