# Shiitake

- Shiitake** (called **Delicious Mushroom** in Japanese games to distinguish it from poisonous types of mushrooms) is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It recovers 30 HP, which is the same amount as a Dim Sum Set or Ramen. It is the only edible mushroom that appears in *Symphony of the Night*, as a Toadstool will poison Alucard.
