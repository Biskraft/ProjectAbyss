# Orchid Corsage

The **Orchid Corsage** is one of Charlotte Aulin's Corsages in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It gives +2 Defense, which is between the other two Corsages, being one more than a White Corsage and one less than a Rose Corsage.
