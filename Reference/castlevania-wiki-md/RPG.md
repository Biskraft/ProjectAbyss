# RPG

right|250px

The **RPG** (acronym for Rocket-propelled grenade) is a Russian *panzerfaust* which fires anti-tank grenades.

The RPG launcher first appeared in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be viewed as a replacement for the Positron Rifle from *Aria of Sorrow*, as both are heavy firearms that can only be obtained as Boss Rush Mode rewards. However, unlike the Positron Rifle (which is more of a futuristic weapon), the RPG is based on a real life weapon.
