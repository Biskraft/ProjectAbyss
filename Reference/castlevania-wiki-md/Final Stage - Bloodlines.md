 is the prologue to *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and it represents the final stage of *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*. The player is Richter Belmont in this stage and he faces off against Dracula.

## Overview
Even though the structure of the level remains almost identical to that in *Rondo of Blood*, many details have been added to take advantage of the technical capabilities of the PlayStation:
- The presence of a guardrail along the final staircase.
- The clock tower can be seen in the background scenery.
- The invisible staircases leading to the bonus room have been replaced by an escalator that will have to be activated by pressing a hidden switch at the entrance to the pinnacle. In addition, the bonus room no longer contains gold bags, but objects that can only be used when playing as Alucard.
- The room before the throne room is decorated with a large table, several windows and a chandelier.
