# Kyle Hebert

- Kyle Henry Hebert** is a American voice actor and podcaster. He voiced various characters such as Ferryman, Master Librarian, N Demon Familiar and Narrator as a replacement of Michael Guinn in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* and its portable game.

Kyle is famous for the voice of Adult Gohan in the Funimation dub of *Dragon Ball Z*, Kamina in *Gurren Lagann* and Ryu from *Street Fighter IV*.
