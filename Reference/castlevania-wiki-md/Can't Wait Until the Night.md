# Can't Wait Until the Night

is a music theme composed by the Konami Kukeiha Club for *Haunted Castle*. It was later arranged by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* alongside Heart of Fire, another classic song that is fused with the track. It is recognized as Julius Belmont's music theme in the game, as well as in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

## Trivia
- The beginning of the music is very similar [to the level 4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kf5FIMCbu18) of *Journey to Silius*, but on a faster rhythm. However, the latter having left two years after *Haunted Castle*, it is unknown whether it is a mere coincidence or if the composer of *Journey to Silius* was inspired by this passage to compose his own.
