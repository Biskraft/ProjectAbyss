# Eyeball monsters

Eyeball monsters of many varieties have appeared in many *Castlevania* games. Depending on the game they appear in, they may come in many forms and shapes, as well as having different abilities and attack methods.

## Description
The **Ghostly Eyeball** from *Simon's Quest* floats slowly through the air toward the player, similar to the Ghosts from other *Castlevania* games.

Big Eyeballs (plain eyeballs which bounce and/or roll) appear in *Haunted Castle* (where they bounce), *Castlevania: The Adventure*, *Belmont's Revenge* and *The Adventure ReBirth*.

A floating lidded **Fire Eye** moves along a horizontal line and drips acid tears over the player. It appears in *Dracula's Curse* (where it is also called a "**Big Eye**") and *Super Castlevania IV*.

The most common eyeball monster found in modern games is the Peeping Eye, and its variant, the Bugbear. These are eyeballs with lids and long retina chains which float about and home toward the player.

Particularly, large eye monsters include the Peeping Big and Dogether.

- Symphony of the Night* featured a giant eyeball that looked from outside a window in the Marble Gallery, although it's never fought.
