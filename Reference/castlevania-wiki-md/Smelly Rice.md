# Smelly Rice

is a food item that decreases health in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* (Saturn-only). If the player eats it, they will suffer 1 damage and receive Poison status. Like other poisonous foods, eating it while wearing the Topaz Circlet will avoid poison status and give 1 HP.
