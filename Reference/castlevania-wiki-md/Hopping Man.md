# Hopping Man

right|300px

  - Hopping Man** is the ability to summon a Flea Man. Soma Cruz is able to do so with the Flea Man soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*. The summoned flea man will advance forward in front of Soma, performing little and big hops sporadically until he reaches the edge of the screen. Due to his erratic behavior, the flea man may be able to inflict multiple hits upon an enemy.
