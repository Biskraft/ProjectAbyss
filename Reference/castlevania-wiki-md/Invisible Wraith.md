The **Invisible Wraith** is an enemy in *Super Castlevania IV*.

## Description
The Invisible Wraith is found in the Transylvanian Wilds. It is an invisible spirit covered in leaves or composed of their essence. It attacks by jumping out of the background and quickly running back and forth. The Invisible Wraith's greatest strength is how well it blends in with the leafy background, thus rendering it nearly invisible.
