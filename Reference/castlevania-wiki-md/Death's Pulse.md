# Death's Pulse

right|300px

  - Death's Pulse** is a material in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can only be obtained by stealing from Death during the encounter with him in both story and Boss Rush modes. It is one of the items required in order to forge Death's Scythe.

In *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula*, Death's flame attack is also called Death's Pulse.
