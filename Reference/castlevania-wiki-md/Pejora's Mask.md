# Pejora's Mask

is a demon whose hobbies include spinning, rotating, and turning. It is originally from *Getsu Fūma Den* and appears in the crossover stage The Legend of Fuma in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

## Trivia
- Its localized name is a possible parody of Majora's Mask from *The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask*, which was also a mask of demonic origin, and several of its attacks in its first phase were similar to Pejora's Mask's stated hobbies.
