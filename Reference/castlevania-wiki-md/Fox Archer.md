The **Fox Archer** and **Fox Hunter** are enemies in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

## Description
Fox-like werebeasts similar to the Skeleton Archers found in other *Castlevania* games, although they don't move from their positions.

The **Fox Archer** is the basic variant. It wears a red coat atop its fluffy orange fur. The **Fox Hunter** is an upgraded variant of the Fox Archer and can be encountered in the later areas of the game, as well as in the Battle Arena. Its fur and weapon are identical to those of the Archer, but its coat is green.

Hyenas are similar enemies that use rifles.

## Trivia
- These enemies (the Fox Hunter in particular) appear to be based on the Disney 1973 animated film version of *Robin Hood*, where the title character is portrayed as an anthropomorphic fox archer.
