# Confodere

right|150px**Confodere** is the name for any of Shanoa's rapier glyphs, and means "to pierce" in Latin. In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, there are three different Confodere glyphs of different levels of power and lengths, including the regular Confodere (which she starts with), the powerful **Vol Confodere**, and the superior **Melio Confodere**.

## Characteristics
While seen as weak in comparison to even the Secare glyphs, the strength of the Confodere glyphs is a *very fast* rate of attack. With the correct timing, Shanoa can let loose multiple slashing stabs per second, amounting to a lot of damage in that time-frame. This can be used against large, non-moving opponents such as Arthroverta. However, at this fast pace, Shanoa will also lose a good deal of MP in said time-frame, especially if she is using Melio Confodere.

When any two Confodere glyphs are involved in a Glyph Union, Shanoa uses a red rapier which is accompanied by rose petals, which fly out and can cause damage. Confodere glyphs count as normal weapons for other Glyph Unions.

Shanoa uses two of these as her initial weapons in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, but it doesn't cost MP to use repeatedly unlike in her origin game. It is her only "normal" weapon glyph from Order of Ecclesia that she can use in Harmony of Despair. Two of her more unique glyphs can take its place though, including Pneuma and Lapiste.

The union version of this glyph is most often depicted as Shanoa's weapon of choice in her art depictions.
