  - The Lost Village** is a location and the opening stage of *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.

## Overview
A snow-covered village located in front of the entrance to With Light's headquarters. The different buildings are accessible by rope and wooden bridges. Unlike other locations seen in this game, this one has a modern-day theme for it, featuring streetlamps and even vehicles.

The player can navigate the streets and also advance through the roofs. The place is connected to the Wizardry Lab via a secret underground passageway accessible by draining the castle moat through this side. It also connects to the Demon Guest House's main entrance via the drawbridge, and upper Demon Guest House via the secret puzzle area.
