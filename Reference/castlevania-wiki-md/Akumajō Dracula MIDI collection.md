  - Akumajo Dracula MIDI collection** is a soundtrack which contains MIDI arrangements of *Castlevania* music. It was released on October 22, 1997. The different arrangers are Dota Ando, Kimitaka Matsumae, Manami Matsumae, Aki Hata and USP.

## Additional information
The spelling mistakes on tracks 02 and 09 in the track list are intentional and were in the original track list of this CD. Their real names are "Stalker" (*Castlevania*) and "Battle for the Holy" (*Castlevania: The Adventure*).

### Arrangements
- Tracks 01~11: Original Arrange.
- Tracks 12~17: GameSound Simulation.
- Tracks 18~19: Piano Arrange.
- Track 20: Kukeiha Club Arrange MIDI Ver.

### Original games
- Tracks 1~7, 12 and 13: from *Castlevania.*
- Track 8: from *Super Castlevania IV.*
- Track 9: from *Castlevania: The Adventure*.
- Tracks 10 and 14: from *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.
- Tracks 11, 16, 17 and 18: from *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.
- Track 15: from *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*.
- Track 19: from *Kid Dracula* (GB).
- Track 20: from *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*.

### Arrangers
- Dota Ando: tracks 01~04, 06.
- Kimitaka Matsumae: track 05.
- Manami Matsumae: track 07.
- Aki Hata: tracks 08~11.
- USP: tracks 12~20.
