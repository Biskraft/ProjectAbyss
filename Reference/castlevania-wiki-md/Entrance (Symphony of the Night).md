The  is the first stage in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and is entered as the hero crosses the draw bridge in the opening sequence. It leads into the Alchemy Laboratory. Its counterpart in the Reverse Castle is the Reverse Entrance. The theme music of this area is Dracula's Castle.

## *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* (LCD)
The Entrance is the first stage of the *LCD* version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. Zombies and Medusa Heads are fought here. Defeating the boss Medusa will give Alucard a Sword to replace his Dagger.

## *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*
The Castle Entrance in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* includes some enemies from the original Castle Entrance and from its reverse version, the Reverse Entrance.
