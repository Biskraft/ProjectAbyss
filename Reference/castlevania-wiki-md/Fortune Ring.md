# Fortune Ring

right

The **Fortune Ring** is a ring in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* and represents the Tarot card "Wheel of Fortune". Wearing it provides Shanoa a LCK boost, scaling in effectiveness as the in-game's play time increases. For every hour of play time, LCK is increased by +1.

## Availability
This ring can be found in Mystery Manor. To find it, Shanoa must first obtain the Arma Machina glyph (dropped by the Automaton ZX27, found in the Mechanical Tower and in Final Approach). Once she has made acquisition of this glyph, she must equip and activate it (transforming herself into an invincible Automaton) in the darkened room with moving spiky platforms found in Mystery Manor, and make contact with each one to destroy all of them. Once this has been achieved, a blue treasure chest will emerge containing the Fortune Ring.
