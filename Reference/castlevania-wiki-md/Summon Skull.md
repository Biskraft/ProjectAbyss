# Summon Skull

right|300px
  - Summon Skull** is a spell that can be performed by using the Dark Shield's Shield Combo Spell with a Shield Rod or Mablung Sword. It costs 30 MP and causes a giant rotting skull to appear and levitate meteors from the floor to the ceiling, damaging every enemy on the screen once per second for about 5 seconds.
