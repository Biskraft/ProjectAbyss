# Reincarnated Soul

- Reincarnated Soul** is a music theme originally composed by Yasushi Asada for *Castlevania: Bloodlines*. It is the background theme for the first stage, Ruins of the Castle Dracula. An arrangement of this song later reappeared as the background theme for Stage 1 in *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*.
