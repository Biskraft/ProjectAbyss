# Protect Ring

The **Protect Ring** is an item found in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* that increases Defense (by 6). It is the only accessory in this game that only increases Defense, and does so more than any other accessory. It takes over the role that protection necklaces such as the Necklace of J had in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.
