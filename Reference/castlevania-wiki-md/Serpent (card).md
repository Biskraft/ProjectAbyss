# Serpent (card)

The **Serpent Card** is a DSS Attribute Card in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It represents the element of Ice, and can be combined with other cards, producing many effects. When the Uranus card is obtained, the Serpent can be summoned. A large number of snowflakes, varying in size, spiral outward from behind Nathan when it is summoned. Damage varies depending on enemy location and timing. Costs 120 MP.
