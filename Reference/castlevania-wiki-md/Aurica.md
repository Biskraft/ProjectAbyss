# Aurica

In Paul Anderson's original movie script, **Aurica** is the lone survivor of a band of gypsies who get attacked by wolves. She is rescued by Simon Belmont and his group, who were seeking refuge in Dracula's Castle from a Turkish army. She's very beautiful and finds out that she looks exactly like Dracula's former lover.
