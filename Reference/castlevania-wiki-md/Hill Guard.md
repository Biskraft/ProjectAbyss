# Hill Guard

The **Hill Guard** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. He is an armored warrior who specializes in fighting on uneven ground.

## Description
Hill Guards are knights that patrol stairways and other inclined planes. They move up or down this slant slowly, but then they can perform a fast thrust with their swords either up or down the ramp (in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, they can perform a dash thrust going down the stairs but not up). They cannot step onto ground level, though.
