# Monica

- Monica** is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. She is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. She is a seamstress still in search of her full potential.

## Description
Monica will make dresses for Shanoa if she brings her certain types of threads. She appears to be rather confused, as she tends to command her and immediately scolds herself for doing that.

## Location
Kalidus Channel
