  - Denis Marsh** is a long poison marsh that comes after the East Bridge that leads to nowhere and offers nothing. It appears to lead to Berkeley Mansion from the East Bridge on the map, however, it is not possible to get to there from here and if you make it all the way to the end, you are forced to turn back. Fortunately, on the way to the dead end, you can take an upper path above the marsh. The way back can consume a few Laurels if you don't know about the invisible stairs that leads back up to the upper path.
