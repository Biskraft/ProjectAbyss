# Escargot

thumb|150px|<p style="text-align: center;">*Harmony of Despair* gamerpic.</p>
  - Escargot** is the snails cooked in butter sauce. In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, they are a two star food item that grants the highest amount of MP for any food item (+10), along with a decent amount of HP (80).
