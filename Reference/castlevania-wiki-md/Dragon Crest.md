# Dragon Crest

The **Dragon Crest** is a special item that appears in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It allows Leon to unlock the door to the throne room, and can be found in Pagoda of the Misty Moon. In the second game, Hector uses it to open a door in the Abandoned Castle.
