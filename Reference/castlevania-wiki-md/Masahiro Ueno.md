# Masahiro Ueno

- Masahiro "Mitch" Ueno**, also known as "**Jun Furano**", is a video game designer, programmer, producer and product manager. He was the lead director of games such as *Super Castlevania IV* and the Famicom version of *Metal Gear*.

## Education
Kindai University<br />
Bachelor of Engineering, Reactor Engineering<br />
(1983 - 1987)
