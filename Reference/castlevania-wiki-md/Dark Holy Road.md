  - Dark Holy Road** is a song composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is identified as the theme that plays when Barlowe crawls toward the seal that contains Dracula's remains and revives him at the cost of his life.

The song's title might be a reference to Barlowe's zeal considering his position as the order's leader and his maniacal intentions.
