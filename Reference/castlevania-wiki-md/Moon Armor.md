# Moon Armor

right|73px

  - Moon Armor** (also called **Moonlight Plate**) is a magic plate with a beautiful moonlight relief which is affected by the moon.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it grants +40 Defense during the day time, but +80 at night. At night, it's the second most powerful armor after the Ceramic Mail. During day, it has the same defense as a Brigandine. Its counterpart is the Sun Armor, which has the same stats for the opposite time of day.

In *Lament of Innocence*, the Moonlight Plate is the third armor which can be purchased from Rinaldo and is available for 4,000 gold once 60% of the map has been uncovered. It reduces damage by 20% from all enemies, which is 10% more than the Meteor Plate but 10% less than the Solar Plate.
