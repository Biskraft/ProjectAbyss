The  is the second forested area normally found in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. It is located just beyond the South Bridge when approaching from the town of Jova. The path eventually forks. The lower fork leads to the town of Veros.

Berkeley Mansion, the first mansion encountered in the game, is encountered early in the upper fork. Beyond the mansion are the Denis Woods.
