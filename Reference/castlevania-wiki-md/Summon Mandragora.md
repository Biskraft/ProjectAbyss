# Summon Mandragora

right|300px

Summons a Mandragora whose scream damages enemies.

In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, this can be performed by using the Uranus + Mandragora DSS combination. Several circular "screams" appear throughout the screen, dealing Plant damage.

  - Last Scream** is a bullet soul obtained from a Mandragora. Soma will throw a mandragora in front of him and it will emit a high-pitched scream before dying in a big explosion (hence the name of this ability). The scream will kill or severely damage any enemies who are within range, and it has a considerable area of effect.
