# Signed Pencil Board

- Signed Pencil Board** (Japanese: サインいりしたじき *Sain iri Shitajiki*) is Secret item in the Sega Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It has no in-game function other than to be collected.

## Present Campaign
One time there was a contest associated with this item. The rules were simple:
- Find the Signed Pencil Board in the game.
- Take a photograph of it in your inventory.
- Send Konami the photograph and receive a pencil board signed by Ayami Kojima in return (first 15 arrivals).
