# Santa Lilio Sangre ~Akai Yuri~ Ayami Kojima Art Works

- Santa Lilio Sangre ~Akai Yuri~ Ayami Kojima Art Works** is a collection of artworks by Ayami Kojima released in November, 2010. It includes many previously unreleased concept art for past *Castlevania* titles she has worked on.
