# Theaters

right|300px

  - Theaters** have occasionally appeared in some *Castlevania* games. They usually comprise only one big room of a larger stage, but it's worth mentioning them for the visual impact they produce when one is entered, drastically changing the atmosphere in comparison to the rest of the level. Theaters usually appear merely as backgrounds, where several rows of seats look toward a distant stage with big red curtains.

One of the stages in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* is the theater building itself, which is conformed by a series of halls, hallways and parlors interconnected with each other, with the main stage located at the center of the stage and where the boss battle takes place.

## Appearances
  - Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* — Observation Tower
  - Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* — Ghostly Theatre
  - Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* — Forest of Doom
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate* — Theatre
