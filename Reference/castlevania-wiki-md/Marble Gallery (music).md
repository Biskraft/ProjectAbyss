# Marble Gallery (music)

- Marble Gallery** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is background theme for the titular area.

- Consistent slower arrangements of this song can be heard in Tower of Mist, the next area's music theme.
