# Heart Brooch

right|73px

The **Heart Brooch** is a brooch in the shape of a heart that reduces the amount of Hearts that using a weapon or ability consumes.

It first appeared in the 1997 game *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, where it reduces Heart consumption by half. It returned in the 2003 game *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It can only be used by Charlotte Aulin in the 2006 game *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.

## Trivia
- The Heart Brooch from *Lament of Innocence* also appears in *Curse of Darkness*, where it is known as the Innocent Supporter. Both items have the exact same usage (reduce the amount of Hearts consumed) and in-game item model designs; however, in the Japanese version of *Curse of Darkness*, the Innocent Supporter was originally called Brisingamen.
