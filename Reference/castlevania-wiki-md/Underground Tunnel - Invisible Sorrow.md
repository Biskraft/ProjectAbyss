# Underground Tunnel - Invisible Sorrow

- Underground Tunnel - Invisible Sorrow** is a music track that is featured in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. It is the background theme of the Tunnel.

An arrangement of this music appear as bonus track on the Castlevania: The Original Game Soundtrack album.
