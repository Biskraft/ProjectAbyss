# Ruby Corset

The Ruby Corset is a corset made of Ruby.  It is the only corset that in addition to a Defense boost also gives a Strength Boost.  It is one of three corsets that give a Defense boost of +16 and a boost of +3 to some other attribute (Sapphire Corset boosts Constitution and Emerald Corset boosts Intelligence).  This is more defense than an Amethyst Corset (+11), but less than a Lilith Corset (+17).
