# Thunder

right|300px

  - Thunder** is one of the basic elemental attributes found in the *Castlevania* series.

## General information
This attribute covers all effects based on electricity, making it an attribute that is especially effective on water-based or metallic enemies. Thunder is, however, not very effective on plant creatures, electric-based creatures, and magic users like witches. Most of the time, thunder-based weapons and attacks can damage multiple times (e.g.: Fulgur and Vol Fulgur glyphs inflict multiple weak hits, or the Gungner, an electric spear which hits 2-3 times).
