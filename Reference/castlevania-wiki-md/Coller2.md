# Coller2

(preliminary name) is a canceled character that was planned to appear in *Castlevania* (N64).

She was going to be somehow related to Coller and was originally intended to be his rival. She was supposed to be a creature for which a newer technology than Coller's was used.

During production, the character was eventually abandoned completely and never made it into the game, with only a single concept artwork of her remaining.
