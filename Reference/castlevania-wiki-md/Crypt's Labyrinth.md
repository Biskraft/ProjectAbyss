# Crypt's Labyrinth

The **Crypt's Labyrinth** is the sixth area Trevor explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. This subterranean environment can be accessed through a protected barrier with light magic after receiving the light magic medallion.
