# Kunai

right|250px

The **Kunai** is a type of knife used by ninja. Three are thrown at one time, one straight forward, another a little diagonally downward and another a little diagonally upward.

It is one of Jonathan Morris's sub-weapon skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
