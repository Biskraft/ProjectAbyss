# Tuxedo Coat

right|50px

The **Tuxedo Coat** is the finest of formalwear in classic black.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The Tuxedo Coat can be found inside a secret room below the first big room after defeating Dullahan (two rooms left from the boss room). To enter it, the player must destroy a breakable floor just in front from where the first Slinger in that area is.
