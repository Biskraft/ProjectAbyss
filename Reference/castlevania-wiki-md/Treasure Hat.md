# Treasure Hat

right|50px

The **Treasure Hat** is a hat in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a reward from Daniela for completing her second quest: "Important Resting Place". Shanoa must make a drawing of the waterfall located at Tristis Pass by using the Sketch Book Daniela gave her.

## Characteristics
Wearing the hat will raise the probability of green chests (rare) to appear in a level. The effect is registered the moment the player enters the level, meaning that by wearing the hat and entering the level will set all new green chests throughout it. The most efficient way to benefit from this effect is to wear the Treasure Hat each time the player is going to enter a level and switch it for any other preferred head-gear as soon as it is entered.

## Notes
- The Treasure Hat increases the chances of green chests to appear in a level, not the chances of enemies dropping their rare items when defeated.
- The quest for obtaining this item can be completed by drawing the waterfall located at Tristis Pass, regardless if it's still frozen.
