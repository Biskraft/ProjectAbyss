# Transmutation Rune

thumb|220x220px
The **Transmutation Rune **is a relic in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2* that is exclusive to the Relic Rune Pack dlc. It allows you to take a relic you already possess and transform it into another relic. This only works on relics that you've already discovered.
