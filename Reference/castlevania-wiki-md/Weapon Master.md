# Weapon Master

The **Weapon Master** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. He uses any manner of weapon as easily as a part of his own body.

## Description
The Weapon Master is an armored knight who is sort of a combination of the Gurkha Master, the Red Smasher, and the Hammer Shaker. He is only found in the Large Cavern.

He wields two twin-bladed gurkha knives, one in each hand, has several daggers fastened to his belt, and carries a warhammer on his back.

He uses two boomerang-like attacks or a lunging dash. When he has sustained roughly 1,500 damage, he will jump and throw the gurkhas as javelins, ridding himself of them. Then he will alternate his attacks, either by jumping and spinning with the hammer, hammering forward if the player is near, or by flinging waves of daggers if the player is far from him. Note that his hammer can hurt player even if they are behind him.
