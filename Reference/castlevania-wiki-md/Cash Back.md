# Cash Back

- Cash Back** is an Enchanted Soul in the *Castlevania* series. It allows the player to gain money when taking damage.

## Characteristics
  - Cash Back** is an ability that allows Soma Cruz to obtain Gold every time he takes damage. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Mimic's soul.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, it gives $5 per every 1 HP lost, transferred instantly into the 'Gold' counter.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it turns the percentage (%) of the total value of HP lost into money outside of Soma after he takes damage:
- Losing 1-9% max HP generates $1 per hit.
- Losing 10-24% max HP generates $100 per hit.
- Losing 25-35% max HP generates a $500 gold bag per hit.
- Losing 50% or more generates a $1,000 gold bag per hit.

  - Note**: Generated coins are not affected nor have any interaction with the Gold Ring.

This ability also appears in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
