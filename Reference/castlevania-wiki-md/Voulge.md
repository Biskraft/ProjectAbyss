# Voulge

right|200px'''

  - Voulge** is a very strong axe-type weapon appearing in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It can be purchased at Vincent's shop after defeating Death at the Tower of Death, and it's also a very rare drop from the Old Axe Armor. While the Voulge's Critical Art is no different compared to other axes, it does also possess the Dark attack attribute. As a result, while resisted by a good portion of the inhabitants of Castlevania, Jonathan might see potential use in the weapon against the Whip's Memory. Additionally, this weapon raises Jonathan's ATK +100.
