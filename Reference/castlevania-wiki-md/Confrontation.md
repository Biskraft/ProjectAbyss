  - Confrontation** is a music theme composed by Takashi Yoshida for *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the theme that plays during the boss battles against the Manticore, Great Armor, Big Golem, Headhunter, Death and Legion.

The theme briefly plays when the Giant Bat appears before Soma Cruz in the The Arena before it gets squeezed to death by Balore.
