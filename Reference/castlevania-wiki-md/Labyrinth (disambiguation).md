# Labyrinth (disambiguation)

- Labyrinth** may refer to:

## Games
  - Akumajō Dracula: Gallery of Labyrinth* — The Japanese title for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
  - Gallery of Labyrinth* (minigame) — A Flash minigame based on *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
  - Castlevania: Labyrinth of Fire* — A slot machine spinoff of the *Castlevania* series.
  - Castlevania: Labyrinth of Love* — A slot machine spinoff of the *Castlevania* series.

## Literature
- Konami Akumajō Dracula: Gallery of Labyrinth Official Guide — A Japanese official guide for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.

## Locations
- Underground Labyrinth — A location in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.
- Labyrinth Entrance — The third level of the second chapter in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.
- Crypt's Labyrinth — The sixth area Trevor explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*.

## Music
- Gallery of Labyrinth — A song composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
- Malak's Labyrinth — A song composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.
- Labyrinth - Pan's music theme in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.

## Other media
