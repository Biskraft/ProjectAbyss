# Tombstone

The **Tombstone** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. They are gravestones that rattle around violently, possessed by evil spirits of dead.

## Description
Gravestones possessed by the grudges of the dead which attack all living things. These grave markings spring to life when someone gets near them and launch themselves in a series of hops to attack them. It is sometimes hard to tell which tombstones will come to life and which ones are merely in the background.

In the Japanese version of *Castlevania: Dracula X*, tombstones had a crucifix icon on the stone, although when localized, it was replaced with a demonic face with red eyes instead.
