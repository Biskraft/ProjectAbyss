# Cabriolet

The **Cabriolet** is a headgear in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a women's hat with a collapsible hood.

Fixed drop from a red treasure chest in Minera Prison Island. Its most noteworthy characteristic is that it gives an INT +2 in the early game, which, if the player knows about beforehand, may decide not to spend their gold on the Babushka at the shop, which only gives INT +1.
