# Heaven's Ring

right|186px
The **Heaven's Ring** is a magical ring in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* that powers up the Axe subweapon so that it is larger and travels slower, potentially allowing it to deal more hit as it flies by. Hearts consumption is increased to 6 when wearing this ring.
