# Bathrobe

right|50px

The **Bathrobe** is a soft, luxurious terrycloth robe. It can only be worn by female characters in the games it appears in.

## Game specific information
The Bathrobe can be found hidden inside a breakable block in the cavern section of the Great Stairway, in a big room near the entrance to Sandy Grave where some Lerajies keep guard (the block is located next to one of the collapsible floors at the lower right part of the room).

This robe provides Charlotte a DEF +6, LCK +5 boost, and is the best Luck boosting chest gear the player can get until they have the opportunity to get their hands on a Princess Coat (DEF +28, LCK +10) as an item drop much later in the game.
