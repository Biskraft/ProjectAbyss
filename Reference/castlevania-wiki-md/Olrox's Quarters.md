# Olrox's Quarters

- Olrox's Quarters** are a section of the Castle devoted to, apparently, one of Dracula's closest allies, Olrox. Dark, elegant hallways give way to dungeon-like corridors on the top floor, as well as a rather large moonlit room housing what seems to be an abandoned village. Olrox, one of the main bosses, resides here.

This area's theme is Dance of Pales.

## Geography
Olrox's Quarters is first reached through the Room of Clocks in the Marble Gallery. A large set of stairs is navigated, which is populated with Skelerangs. This leads to a large open chamber dominated by the Spectral Sword. To the left of this room, the Colosseum can be entered. A long hallway is to the right of this room, where the giant brothers Hammer and Blade guard. The rest of Olrox's Quarters requires the Form of Bat, which is at the end of this long hallway. The area can be exited through a teleportation room before that is obtained. Once the Form of Bat is available, a long vertical area can be traversed leading to the dungeons. To the left and below is a broken bridge and the villa. At then end of the broken bridge is an area leading to Olrox's chamber. The Royal Chapel can be entered by taking the lower path to the left.
