# Patrick Seitz

- David Patrick Seitz** is a American voice actor and ADR director. He provided the voice of Dracula as a replacement of Douglas Rye in four *Castlevania* games: *The Dracula X Chronicles*, *Order of Ecclesia*, *Judgment* and *Harmony of Despair*. Additionally, he replaced Tom Wyner as Death in these first two titles.

Patrick is well known for the voices of Bob from the *Tekken* series, Bass Armstrong from *Dead or Alive 5*, Franky from *One Piece*, Samson from *Skullgirls* and Dio Brando from *Jojo's Bizarre Adventure*. He has also done the voice of the popular *Mortal Kombat* character, Scorpion.
