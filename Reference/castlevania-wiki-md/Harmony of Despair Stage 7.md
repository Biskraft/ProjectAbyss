  - Beauty, Desire, Situation Dire** is the first DLC released for *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It was released to the Xbox 360 Xbox Live Marketplace on September 29th, 2010 for 400 Microsoft Points and comes free with the purchase of the base game on PlayStation 3. It is the 7th stage in the game and appears to be based off of the Sandy Grave from *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.

## Hidden Item
The hidden item of Chapter 7 is Vick13. It is obtained by going to the very top of the pyramid, and once there the player must jump and the item will appear on the ground under them.
