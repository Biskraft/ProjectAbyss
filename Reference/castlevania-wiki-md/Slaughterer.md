# Slaughterer

The **Slaughterer** and **Hell Boar** are enemies in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. They are a type of anthropomorphic porcine.

## Description
### Slaughterer
The **Slaughterer** (**Fat Slaughter** in the Japanese version) is an hog-like savage beast. His main attack consists in releasing a fireball in an arch, but he also can perform a big punch when approached.

His soul, Max Straight, allows Soma to perform quick straight punches on enemies.


### Hell Boar
The **Hell Boar**, as indicated by his name, is a boar-like savage beast. It attacks almost the same way as the Slaughterer, but by performing an uppercut instead, which deals far more damage. He also spews a fireball in a straight line.

His soul, Max Upper, allow Soma to deliver an uppercut to enemies up close, inflicting high damage. When executed on Zombies or Ghouls, their torso and head will be torn apart from their bodies and fly far up in the air.
