# Piros Helm

The **Piros Helm** is headgear in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is described as a helmet from a faraway land and looks somewhat like a metal Mexican sombrero. It is referred to as a **Pylos** in Japanese, which is a type of Greek helmet. It grants DEF +11, which is one more than a Face-Guard and one less than a Bagonette.
