# Master Ring

right|50px

The **Master Ring** (called the **Ring of Feanor** in the North American version of *Symphony of the Night*) is a ring that increases statistics in the *Castlevania* series.

It first appeared in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, as one of several rings that increased several statistics, with an emphasis on strength. It increases ATT by 1, STR, CON and INT by 7, DEF by 1, and LCK by 2, resulting in a total attack boost of +8 and a total defense boost of +4. It is more effective than the Ring of Pales (which increases each stat by only 1), but less effective than the Ring of Varda (which increases most stats by multiples of ten).

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, it increases the amount of mastery gained of Jonathan's skills whenever he uses them.

In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, it increases the rate of Shanoa's Attribute Points acquisition when she uses a glyph with a certain attribute type. This bonus does *not* apply to the 1 point gained from absorbing glyphs, however.
