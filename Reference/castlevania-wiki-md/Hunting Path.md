# Hunting Path

right|150px
The Hunting Path is the second level of the first chapter of Castlevania: Lords of Shadow. 
## Zobek's Journal
:*I follow him into the old forest watching him from afar, he is strong indeed.  The one God comes to drive out the many but here, in this place, God's influence is thin and threadbare.  The old gods still hold sway here.  Few venture this far into the wood.  He is disturbed by dreams, dreams that gnaw at his very soud.  He will rest for the night, but rest will not come easy.  The battle has left him weakened.  He knows the dream will return, and though he fears nothing on this Earth, his nightmares sap the strength within him and leave a cold grip on his heart.  Tomorrow he will use the old hunting path, long has it been in disuse, but for now...he dreams.*
