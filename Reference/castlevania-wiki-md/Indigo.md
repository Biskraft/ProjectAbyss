# Indigo

right|250px

  - Indigo** is a Bird-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a Level 4 Innocent Devil, and can learn Icicle Shot and Blizzard Breath. It can be obtained from a Wingosaurus Innocent Devil, using green or yellow crystals.
