# Magnus

- Magnus** is the main antagonist in the audio drama *Castlevania: Nocturne of Recollection*. He is an incubus who takes pleasure in the torture of innocent humans.

## Background
One year after the events of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, Magnus appears and plans to resurrect Dracula's Castle and make Alucard its new lord.

He tries to convince Maria Renard that Alucard is evil, and torments the dhampir in the meantime. He is also responsible for the "Dangerous Potion" that Cyril has.
