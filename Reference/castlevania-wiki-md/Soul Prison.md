The  is a location in *Akumajō Dracula X: Nocturne in the Moonlight* (Saturn). It is the Reverse Castle's version of the Cursed Prison.

## Overview
The Soul Prison is a dungeon-like area mainly inhabited by Zombie and Ghost-type monsters. It is connected to the Black Marble Gallery to the right and the Reverse Caverns to the left. It is a very ominous location with colder colors compared to its normal version.

It is the Reverse Castle's counterpart of the Cursed Prison.
