# Lucifer's Sword

right|50px

The **Lucifer's Sword** is a relic which appears in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It can be found at the second floor of the Ghostly Theatre, in a room hidden above the big chamber with falling spiked platforms. To access this room, the player must find a pole on the wall located above the little safe spot left of the knight's statue, and whip it to pull himself up. When activated, this relic will damage enemies and tackle them backward, sometimes knocking them down. Its function is somewhat similar to that of the Svarog Statue, but the added effect of stunning enemies makes it more useful.

## Trivia
- The design of the Lucifer's Sword is similar to that of the 7 Bladed Sword from *Curse of Darkness*, the main difference being that this has four protrusions along the blade and the other has six.
