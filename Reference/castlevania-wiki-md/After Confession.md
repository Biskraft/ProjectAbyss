# After Confession

- After Confession** is a music theme composed by Masahiko Kimura for *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the background theme for The Dark Chapel.

It is also played in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* as the default theme for Chapter 2: *Lord of Unseen Strings*.
