# Chauve-souris

right|73px

The  (French for "bat", meaning *bald mouse*) is the main weapon Isaac uses during his first battle with Hector and during the battle with Trevor. It is essential in getting the final evolution of the Devil-Type Innocent Devil, The End.

The deciphered message that only a Tiramisu Fairy-Type Innocent Devil can read is in the Infinite Corridor. The Chauve-souris is mistranslated as "Shyovasli" in the US version; it correctly says "Chauve-souris" in the European version.

The Chauve-souris is also needed to create Death's Scythe, the most powerful weapon in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
