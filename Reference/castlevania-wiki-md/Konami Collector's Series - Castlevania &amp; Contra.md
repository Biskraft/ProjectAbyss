# Konami Collector's Series: Castlevania &amp; Contra

- Konami Collector's Series: Castlevania & Contra*** is a compilation of five classic Konami games which gained popularity on the Nintendo Entertainment System, this time for PC. All games are ports of their NES counterparts, even when ports of the arcade versions of *Contra* and *Super Contra* had already been released for MS-DOS.

## Game list
The games included are:
  - Castlevania*
  - Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*
  - Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*
  - Contra*
  - Super C*
