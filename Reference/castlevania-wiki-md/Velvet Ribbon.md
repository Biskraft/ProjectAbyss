# Velvet Ribbon

The **Velvet Ribbon** is a Ribbon that Charlotte Aulin can equip in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It gives a Defense +2, which is equivelent to Jonathan's Head Guard and the Glasses that either character can wear.
