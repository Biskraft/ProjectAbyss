# Flame Mine

right|106px

  - Flame Mine** is a signature attack of Ukoback, who lights fires in mid-air throughout the screen which hinder the way and cause Fire damage if touched. These flames can be put out by attacking them or will dissipate over time. Soma Cruz is able to use this ability by stealing Ukoback's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*.
