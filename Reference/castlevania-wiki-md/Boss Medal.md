# Boss Medal

right|50px

  - Boss Medals** are special items in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They are medals awarded to the player as a proof of valor for defeating a boss without taking any damage.

## Description
Defeating a boss without getting hit once earns the player certain medals as proof of their valor. Unfortunately, they're nothing more than a decoration, although they do give a small description of the boss they refer to.

The player cannot get these in Albus Mode, since there is no menu in most alternate modes in the *Castlevania* series, including *Order of Ecclesia*.
