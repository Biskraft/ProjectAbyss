# Princess Tiara

right|50px

The **Princess Tiara** is a tiara covered with brilliant gems. It's the only tiara in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* that provides a Luck boost (+7) in addition to the standard Defense boost (+5). It grants higher stats than the similar Uraeus crown, which grants LCK +5 and DEF +2.
