# White Crystal

right
The **White Crystal** is the first of three Crystals found in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.

## Characteristics
According to the first person you can talk to, buying a white crystal should be the first thing you do in the town of Jova. It can be purchased for 50 Hearts.

According to the instruction manual, it "offers a weakened source of magical power" and it allows you to see hidden platforms. You will need this item in order to be able to tell how to get into the first mansion of the game, Berkeley Mansion. It will allow you to see an invisible platform that moves up and down at the entrance to the mansion, making it a lot easier to enter.

It can be traded for a Blue Crystal from the Crystal Trader in the town of Aljiba, which has the same ability as the White Crystal, but also allows you to drain water areas to reach areas below.

## Notes
- Even without having possession of the White Crystal, the player will still be able to stand on invisible platforms (e.g.: they can make a leap of faith at the initial body of water immediately after entering the first mansion to successfully hop onto the first invisible platform). However, due to its requirement for trading it with other, more powerful crystals in order to access other areas of the game later on, the White Crystal is mandatory to purchase.
