# Earth Ring

right|73px
The **Earth Ring** is a type of ring in the *Castlevania* series. Depending on the game it appears in, it may have different uses.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, the ring will power up Juste's Bible sub-weapon so that it revolves fast around him instead of only spiral around the player as it did in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, although it also increases Heart consumption to 5.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, it gives Hector resistance to Earth based attacks.
