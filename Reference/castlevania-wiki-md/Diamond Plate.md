# Diamond Plate

The **Diamond Plate** (sometimes called **Diamond Armor**) is an armor made of diamond, the most endurable natural material known. It is often the strongest armor that only increases DEF, being stronger than a Platinum Plate.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, the Diamond Plate it the strongest DEF-boosting armor. It gives a DEF boost of +15, which is higher than Platinum Mail (+10). It has the same as Mojo Mail, Axe Lord Armor (turns its wearer into an Axe Armor), and only less than Alucard Mail (+20, resists Fire, Thunder and Ice).

In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, the Diamond Armor is also the strongest DEF-boosting armor. It gives a DEF boost of +210, which is higher than Platinum Armor (150). It is not as strong as the attack boosting Needle Armor.
