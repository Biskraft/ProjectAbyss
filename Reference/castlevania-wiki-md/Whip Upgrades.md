# Whip Upgrades

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, Juste can find several **upgrades** for his whip. These vary from the ones that induce it with elemental powers, to the ones that just raise its base attack power. There are also some upgrades which grant it with special powers, like the ability to destroy stone walls, swing the whip in a circular manner or shoot a bullet with each swing of the whip to attack distant enemies.
