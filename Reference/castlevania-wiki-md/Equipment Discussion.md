# Equipment Discussion

- Equipment Discussion** is a music track in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the background theme that plays in Hammer's shop in the The Lost Village.

It reappeared in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, this time called **Enterprising Mercantilism** as bonus track, but with a few instrumental differences.

## Trivia
- The theme's title in *Dawn of Sorrow* is a reference to Hammer's role as a merchant in the game, given that his shop sells weapons, armor, clothes and accessories.
