  - Martin Robinson** is a writer and journalist. He is the author of The Art of Castlevania: Lords of Shadow and other monographic gaming books such as *Halo: The Art of Building Worlds* and *The Art of Dead Space*. He works as a Features Editor at Eurogamer.
