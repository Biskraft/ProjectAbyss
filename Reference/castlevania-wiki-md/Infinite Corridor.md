The **Infinite Corridor** (*Corridor of the Inferno* in the Japanese version) is a location in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.

## Overview
The Infinite Corridor lies in an an alternate realm in another dimension which can be accessed from the boss room of the Abandoned Castle in the later part of the game. The plane appears as multiple platforms and walkways floating in a void.

It is here where Dullahan is encountered.
