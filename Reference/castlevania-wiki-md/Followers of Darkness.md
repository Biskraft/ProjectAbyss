# Followers of Darkness

thumb|300px|<p style="text-align: center;">A group of Followers of Darkness conducting a black mass to resurrect Count Dracula in 1691.</p>

The  is a sect conformed by a group of fanatic people sworn to resurrect the Dark Lord, Count Dracula, by any means (Black Mass being the most common method). Characters such as Drolta Tzuentes, Barlowe or the dark priest Shaft are considered to be leaders of this organization.
