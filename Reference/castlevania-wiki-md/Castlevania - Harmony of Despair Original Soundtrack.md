# Castlevania: Harmony of Despair Original Soundtrack

- Castlevania: Harmony of Despair Original Soundtrack** is the soundtrack for the Xbox Arcade game, *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It was released on January 13, 2011. The music was composed by Michiru Yamane, Masahiko Kimura (*Dawn of Sorrow*), Yuzo Koshiro (*Portrait of Ruin*) and Yasuhiro Ichihashi (*Order of Ecclesia*) and arranged by Yasuhiro Ichihashi, Norikazu Miura, Sota Fujimori, Tomoaki Hirono, Atsushi Sato, Nobuyuki Akena, Yasuhisa Ito and Toshihisa Furusawa. It contains original tracks and many arrangements from various *Castlevania* games.

### Arrangements
- Tracks 1-4, 8, 17-19 and 40 are original tracks.
- Track 5 is from *Aria of Sorrow*.
- Tracks 5-7, 9 and 30 are from *Dawn of Sorrow*.
- Tracks 10, 11, 20, 21, 32 and 33 are from *Portrait of Ruin*.
- Tracks 12-15 and 34 are from *Order of Ecclesia*.
- Tracks 22-25 and 31 are from *Symphony of the Night*.
- Tracks 26 and 27 are from the original *Castlevania*.
- Tracks 28 and 29 are from Japan-exclusive Konami game Getsu Fūma Den.
- Track 35 is from *Haunted Castle* and *Castlevania*, using the arrangement from *Aria of Sorrow*.
- Track 36 is from *Dracula's Curse*.
- Track 16, 37 and 38 are from *Rondo of Blood*.
- Track 39 is from *Super Castlevania IV*.
