The  is an exclusive location in *Akumajō Dracula X: Nocturne in the Moonlight* for the Sega Saturn.

## Overview
The Cursed Prison is a dungeon-like area mainly inhabited by the still-moving skeletal remains and ghosts of its former inmates. It is connected to the Marble Gallery  to the left and the Underground Caverns to the right. However, it can only be fully explored when entering from the underground caverns due to a wall that must be opened using a switch located from that side.

Its Reverse Castle counterpart is the Soul Prison.
