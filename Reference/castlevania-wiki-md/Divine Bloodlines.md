# Divine Bloodlines

, also commonly known simply as "**Blood Relations**", is a music theme composed by Akiropito originally introduced in the 1993 NEC PC Engine game *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* as the background theme for Stage 1, as well as the background theme for Stage 1 in the 1995 Super NES remake/reimagining *Castlevania: Dracula X*.

There are five major variations:
    - Overture**, which comes with a faster tempo, is the music for the intro animation before Stage 0 in the game. It is marked as Richter Belmont's theme in the soundtrack.
- Ending theme of *Castlevania: Dracula X*.
- , which was introduced in the 1997 Sony PlayStation game *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* as the music for the battle against Richter Belmont. In *The Dracula X Chronicles* version, the song also plays during the battle against Maria Renard. This version is arranged by Michiru Yamane and is also known as "Strange Bloodlines" or "Blood Relations".
- , which was introduced in the 1999 Nintendo 64 game *Castlevania* as the intro music of the game.
- , which was introduced in the 2006 Nintendo DS game *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* as the music for the battle against the Whip's Memory.
