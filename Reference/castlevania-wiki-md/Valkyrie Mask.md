# Valkyrie Mask

The Valkyrie Mask is the mid-tier mask in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It gives Defense +6 and Strength +2, which is 2 more DEF and 1 more STR than the Crimson Mask, but it less the Minerva Mask by the same amounts, which also gives a Mind Boost of 3.
