# UMA News

- UMA News** is a gazette actively circulating in the 2030s which covers sensationalist events. Hammer, the owner of the local shop, always has the latest issues available for purchase. A diehard collector may still be able to find some old issues lying around if they take the time to look for them.

These publications often offer hints about the existence and locations of the three cryptids that appear in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*:
- The first article covers the story behind recent apparitions of the Yeti near a village.
- The second article covers sightings of a strange insect-like creature called the Mothman and who is attracted to intense sources of light.
- The third article covers eye-witness of what appears to be a creature flying high in the distance, nicknamed by the editors as the Flying Humanoid.
