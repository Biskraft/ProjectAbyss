The **Vulture** is an enemy in *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun* and *Kid Dracula* (GB). It is a large scavenging bird.

## Description
In *Boku Dracula-kun*, normal vultures appear inhabiting the skies and deserts. They fly moving up and down slowly across the screen, somewhat like a large bat.
