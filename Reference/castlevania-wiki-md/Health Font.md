# Health Font

right|200px
  - Health Fonts** are used to recover full health in the *Lords of Shadow* series.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*
Health Fonts appear as small fountains with green water. When Gabriel plunges his hand in, a green fire covers him and his health is restored.

### *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*
Health Fonts appear in *Mirror of Fate* just as they do in *Lords of Shadow*. All characters, except Gabriel, can use them.

### *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*
Health Fonts appear as statues of what appears to be a holy man with a demon perched around his shoulders. A hole in the statue's chest contains a heart. Dracula grabs the heart and crushes it to drink its blood, recovering with this his full health.

## Trivia
- The concept of utilizing the statue of a holy figure to replenish full health in the *Lords of Shadow* series is seemingly derived by the holy statues found in Save Rooms in metroidvania titles that pertain to the original *Castlevania* timeline, which also regain full health to the player as well as replenishing MP and allowing to save the game.
