# Wine

right|85px

 is an alcoholic beverage in *Castlevania* which recovers one of the player's meters. It first appeared in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, but like the other alcoholic beverages, was censored in the North American version and was called **Grape Juice**. The ghost priest at the Royal Chapel sometimes leaves wine behind if you sit on the chair in the confession booth long enough. It recovers 20 HP, which is the highest HP-recovering beverage in the game, being 5 more than Beer (Barley Tea); this is the same amount of HP recovered as with the Miso Soup or Hamburger.

It also appears in *Lament of Innocence* and *Curse of Darkness*. In *Curse of Darkness*, there is a cellar in the basement of the Abandoned Castle containing barrels that can be destroyed, some of which drop Wine.
