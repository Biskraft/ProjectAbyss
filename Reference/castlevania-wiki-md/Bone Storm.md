# Bone Storm

right

  - Bone Storm** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by the Corpsey Battle-Type evolution. To obtain it, Hector must defeat a certain number of enemies while this Innocent Devil is summoned. When activated, Corpsey will lock-on to an enemy and shoot out a stream of sharp-pointed ribs out of his thoracic cage, dealing constant damage (and massive damage overall, especially on a single target). Costs 12 Hearts to cast.
