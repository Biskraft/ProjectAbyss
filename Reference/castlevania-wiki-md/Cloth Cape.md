# Cloth Cape

The **Cloth Cape** is the most basic cape found in the *Castlevania* series. In games that don't have a simple Cape, it gives the least amount of DEF or CON of any capes.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it gives a CON boost of +1 and no DEF boost. This is 2 CON and 1 DEF less than a Reverse Cloak.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, the Cloth Cape is one of the weakest capes. It's one of the few capes that gives a Constitution Boost (+1), though (the Holy Mantle and Black Cloak provide +2 CON and other unique attribute boosts). It gives the standard +5 Fire and Ice defense boost, an exceptionally low amount of HP and MP boost (+1, less than the more common Hero's Cape), and the same amount of DEF as a Hero's Cape (+2).
