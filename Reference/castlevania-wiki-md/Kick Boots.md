# Kick Boots

right|200px
The **Kick Boots** are a Magic Item in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. They allow Nathan to jump against walls and perform an extra jump in the opposite direction. He can use this to his advantage in order to reach areas that would otherwise be out of his jumping distance. To activate it, the player must jump toward a wall and press the Special Move button to jump off of it.
