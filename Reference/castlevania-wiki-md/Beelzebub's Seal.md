# Beelzebub's Seal

- Beelzebub's Seal** is a material in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the spirit body of Beelzebub, sealed in amber and overflowing with phantom energy.

It is part of the required materials to forge the 7 Bladed Sword.
