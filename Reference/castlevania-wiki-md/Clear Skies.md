# Clear Skies

right|200px

  - Clear Skies** is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is a defensive spell that neutralizes ranged attacks.


## Item Data


## Notes
- When Clear Skies is used by the Doppelganger boss, it can block most ranged sub-weapons used by Jonathan, with the exception of the Holy Water, Bible, Yagyu Shuriken, Discus, Kunimitsu, Grenade and Steel Ball.
- The only spells it can block used by Charlotte are Summon Gunman, Acidic Bubbles and Ice Needle.
- 1,000 Blades is the only Dual Crush that does not work on this spell.
