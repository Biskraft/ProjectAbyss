# Norio Wakamoto

is a Japanese voice actor who currently affiliated with the Sigma Seven Talent Agency. He also acts as a narrator in many animes and video games.

Norio Wakamoto is well known by his fans: Cell in *Dragon Ball Z*, Oskar von Reuenthal in *Legend of the Galactic Heroes*, Vicious in *Cowboy Bebop* and Chiyo-chichi in *Azumanga Daioh*.

Wakamoto is the voice actor who mostly acted Dracula in the Japanese versions of *Castlevania* games: *Symphony of the Night*, *Portrait of Ruin*, *Dracula X Chronicles*, *Order of Ecclesia* and *Harmony of Despair*.
