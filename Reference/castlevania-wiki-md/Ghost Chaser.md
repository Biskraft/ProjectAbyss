# Ghost Chaser

right|200px

  - Ghost Chaser** is a signature spell of Dullahan that unleashes a vengeful spirit. Dracula is also able to use his move in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, it can be obtained from both enemies by Charlotte Aulin by binding their attack, and by Soma Cruz by stealing Dullahan's soul.
