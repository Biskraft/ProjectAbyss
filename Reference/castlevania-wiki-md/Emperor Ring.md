# Emperor Ring

The **Emperor Ring** is an accessory in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a ring that represents the Tarot card "The Emperor". It increases the effectiveness of health recovering items by 125% for every accessory slot used (e.g.: a High Potion will heal 450 HP instead of 200 HP with one equipped).

It performs a similar function as the Ankh of Life from *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.
