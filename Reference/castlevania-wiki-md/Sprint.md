# Sprint

- Sprint** is the ability to run really fast in short bursts. It is performed by double tapping the movement analog stick or directional pad in the *Lords of Shadow* games.

In *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*, the Cyclone Boots bestow this ability. Trevor is able to sprint in a similar fashion by using the Speed Boots in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. Dracula starts with this ability in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.
