# Castlevania: Curse of Darkness Original Soundtrack

- Castlevania: Curse of Darkness Original Soundtrack** is the 2 CD complete soundtrack for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. The music was composed by Michiru Yamane, with the help of Yuka Watanabe and Teshigawara. It was released on November 30, 2005. Russell Watson performs the song "True to Your Dreams".

This soundtrack was released on iTunes on December 11, 2007, though it wasn't the complete score.

## Additional information
- Michiru Yamane composed tracks 1~22, 24 (with Teshigarawa), 25~28 (Disc 1), 1~27 (Disc 2).
- Yuka Watanabe composed tracks 23 and 29 (Disc 1).
- "Dracula's Castle" (track 16 (Disc 2)) has the same title than *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night Original Soundtrack*'s track 6, but they're not arrangements. They're two different tracks with the same name.
