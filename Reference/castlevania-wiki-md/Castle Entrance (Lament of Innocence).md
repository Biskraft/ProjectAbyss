The  is a location in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is the first proper area of the game and it mainly houses a warp room which gives access to all other areas of the castle.

## Overview
This area is the main hub of the game, providing access to all other stages. It comprises Rinaldo's cottage at the outskirts of the castle, a tutorial area, a warp room providing access to the five main stages of the game, as well as the main door to the Pagoda of the Misty Moon, where Walter dwells. Lastly, it also provides access to a very large staircase that goes deep below the earth, where the entrance to the Prison of Eternal Torture can be found.

Just behind the castle's main gates, a mysterious room with five orb statues is presented to the visitor, with a little staircase that leads to the entrance to the Pagoda of the Misty Moon. The door is locked with a magical seal, however, and the orb statues appear as if devoid of life. To remove the seal, a warrior of great might and courage must fight his way through the other five areas of the castle and gain possession of five magical orbs. When an orb has been obtained, its respective statue will react to it and work its magic in releasing its part of the seal. When all five have been found, the seal will be completely removed.
