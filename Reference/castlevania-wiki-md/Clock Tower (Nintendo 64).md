The **Clock Tower** is a location in *Castlevania* and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. It is the famous clock tower preceding the final battle.

## Overview
The Clock Tower is the final normal stage in the game. All characters, excepted Henry Oldrey, must pass through here in order to reach the Castle Keep.

Most areas within this stage consist in tall vertical rooms filled with diverse clockwork-themed machinery, like rotating giant gears, mechanic wheels, and metal beams. In addition, several Pillars of Bones and flamethrowers have been placed in certain strategic spots to hinder the path of any intruders. In many cases, even a single misstep could mean the player falling from a long distance and dying instantly as a result.

This is one of the most demanding areas in the game, and to add to the difficulty the player is constantly required to risk their lives in finding diverse keys with which to access the next sections of the tower (three in *Castlevania* and five in *Legacy of Darkness*).
