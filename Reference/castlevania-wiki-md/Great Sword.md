# Great Sword

right|50px
A **Great Sword** is a massive sword that is made to smash through its targets. It is usually obtained from a Guardian type armor. It is also a class of swords in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* that refers to two-handed European swords that are swung overhead.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The Great Sword is the strongest Great Sword in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It has over 50% more Attack than the Obsidian Sword, but lacks the thrusting ability of the other Great Swords.

About half of the 2-Handed weapons in *Symphony of the Night* can be considered Great Swords. These generally are swung in a slow fashion, and swung in a little bit more of an arc than other swords. They all have the ability to thrust, usually by pressing back, then forward and attack.


### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
Large 2-handed European swords are referred to as Great Swords in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.
