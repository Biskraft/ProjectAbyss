  - Stones Hold a Grudge** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It plays during certain cutscenes that contain tense moments, such as the moment when Albus interrupts the ritual involving Dominus at the beginning of the game.
