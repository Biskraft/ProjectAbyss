# Ghost Ship

The **Ghost Ship** is a stage in *Castlevania Judgment*. It is apparently based off of the Death's Ghost Ship stage from *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* or *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*, and the Castle Gate stage can be seen as a background.

Its main stage hazard is a Giant Squid that sends its tentacles overboard, damaging whoever it contacts. It is the first stage played in Castle Mode.
