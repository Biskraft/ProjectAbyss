# Athletes

Several different types of **Athletes** have appeared in the *Castlevania* series as joke enemies. The first one is the **Swimmer**, which swims from one end of the "pool" to the other repeatedly in the water stage of *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*, trying to run into Kid Dracula. Later in the game, a **Figure Skater** takes the ice and attempts to twirl and kick him.

Skeleton Athletes, and even faster, Skeleton Medalists, would later appear in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.
