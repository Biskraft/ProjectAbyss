# Akumajō Dracula Tribute Vol. 1

- Akumajō Dracula Tribute Vol. 1** is a remix album from Konami to celebrate 25 years of *Castlevania*, released on January 13, 2011. It contains music from the series, arranged by Tsukasa Masuko, TECHNOuchi, Takuya Hanaoka, Motoi Sakuraba, Aki Hata, Denji Sano, Manabu Namiki, Yousuke Yasui, Masafumi Takada, Yoshitaka Hirota, Mitsuhiro Kaneda, MANYO and Tenpei Sato.
