# Theo James

- Theodore Peter James Kinnaird Taptiklis** (born December 16, 1984) is a British actor and model, known for portraying the role of Tobias "Four" Eaton in *The Divergent Series* film trilogy based on the New York Times bestselling novels. He played Detective Walter William Clark, Jr. in the crime-drama series *Golden Boy* (2013), and David in the films *Underworld: Awakening* (2012) and *Underworld: Blood Wars* (2016).

He voices Hector in the *Castlevania* animated series.
