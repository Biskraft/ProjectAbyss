# Scott McCulloch

- Scott McCulloch** (born **Ramsay Scott McCulloch** in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada — died September 24, 2000 in Japan) was the original voice actor for Richter Belmont in the PlayStation version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. He also appeared as the narrator in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. He was replaced as Richter by Liam O'Brien and David Vincent in later titles.

McCulloch was also known for voicing Chris Redfield in the original 1996 PlayStation version of *Resident Evil.*

## Trivia
- Scott McCulloch delivered the now iconic phrase "Die monster. You don't belong in this world!", along many other of Richter Belmont's catchphrases that appeared in the original English PlayStation version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.
