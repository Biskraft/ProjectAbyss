# Platinum Stud

The **Platinum Stud** is a stud earring made of platinum that appears in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It gives +5 DEF and +1 INT, which is the most DEF of any earring in the game, giving 1 more than a Bloody Stud, but it has one less Intelligence than a Bloody Stud as well. It gives two DEF more than a Gold Stud.
