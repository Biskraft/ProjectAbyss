# Pulsus

right|194px

  - Pulsus** is the Glyph Union of otherwise incompatible glyphs. It doesn't have much range for a Glyph Union and it does about half the damage of the double weapon Unions, but it only costs one third of the price in Hearts and the attack itself is very fast to perform and to recover from. It also doesn't have an attribute, which is useful for enemies that resist these type of attacks.
Category: Glyph
