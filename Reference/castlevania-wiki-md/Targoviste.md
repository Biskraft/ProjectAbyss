# Targoviste

thumb|300px|<p style="text-align: center;">Targoviste in 1475</p>

  - Targoviste** was an important Romanian city in Wallachia. Lisa Tepes was imprisoned there before her execution in 1475. The city was destroyed by Dracula in 1476, as a revenge for his wife's death.
