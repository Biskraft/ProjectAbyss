# Chamomile

- Chamomile** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. One can be obtained as a fixed drop from an item box in Kalidus Channel, and another can be found at plain sight on a high ledge at Oblivion Ridge. They can also be found as random drops from common wooden item boxes in Minera Prison Island, Tymeo Mountains and Somnus Reef.

A Chamomile must be given to Abram, along with a Rue, in order to complete the *"Medicinal Ingredients Needed"* quest, which adds Anti-Venoms and Uncurse Potions to Jacob's shop.
