# Venus

right|300px

  - Venus** is the glyph union of Nitesco and any other weapon glyph in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It's one of the most devastating attacks Shanoa can obtain in the game, as it deals heavy damage, can be performed in quick succession, covers a large area of the screen (including above her, which allows to attack flying or normal enemies from a floor below), can destroy projectiles, deals Slash damage, and due to the natural properties of Nitesco, also deals Flame and Light elemental damage at the same time. Since a considerable amount of enemies in the game are weak to Light (Holy), its offensive value increases. These characteristics make Venus one of the most useful abilities while attempting a run through the Large Cavern.
