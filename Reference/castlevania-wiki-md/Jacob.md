# Jacob

is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. He is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. He runs the town's local shop.

## Location
Kalidus Channel

## Description
Jacob runs the local shop in Wygol Village, and as such, will try to give Shanoa fair deals on items.

## Shop


## Notes
- After defeating the Man Eater at the Skeleton Cave, the Combo Boots, Baggy Boots and Battle Boots will be added to the shop.
- After defeating Goliath at the Giant's Dwelling, the Crochet, Ghillie Boots and Cavalier Boots will also be added to the shop.
- After spending 100,000G at the shop, Jacob will present Shanoa with a VIP Card, which will lower all the shop's item prices by 20%.
- SoyBeanFlour, one of the four cats found in the game, will stay to live with Jacob at his shop once he has been rescued in Minera Prison Island.
