# Goggles

- Goggles** are protective eye-wear in *Castlevania*.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, they raise Intelligence by 4 in addition to a token Defense boost of +1. This is better than Sunglasses, which actually reduce Intelligence by 1 (along with the same DEF boost of +1), but inferior to the Holy Glasses, which raise intelligence by one more.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, goggles are the type of glasses that raise Defense (+5) (as opposed to the Gambler Glasses that raise Luck and regular Glasses that raise Intelligence).
