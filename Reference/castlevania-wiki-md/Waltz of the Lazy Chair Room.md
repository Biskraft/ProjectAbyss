  - Waltz of the Lazy Chair Room** is a music theme composed by Yuka Watanabe for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the background theme of the Chair Room in Cordova Town.

With Proboscis Fairy, this is one of the only themes in the game not to have been composed by Michiru Yamane.
