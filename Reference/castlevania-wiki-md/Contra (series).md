# Contra (series)

right|300px

    - Contra*** and *Castlevania* are two of Konami's biggest side-scrolling platform franchises. They often reference each other and are sometimes bundled together side by side.

## Game specific information
### *Contra: Hard Corps*
    - Contra: Hard Corps*** (1994) for the Sega Genesis features a sub-boss called Simondo Belmont, who looks and behaves similar to Simon Belmont from the *Castlevania* series. The theme "SIMON 1994RD" played in the background is a remix of Vampire Killer.


### *C: The Contra Adventure*
right|200px
The Rolling Eyeball boss in this game is likely a reference to the Big Eye.

### *Contra Advance: The Alien Wars EX*
right|200px
*Main article: BradyGames Konami Game Boy Advance Combo Official Strategy Guide‎*.
BradyGames packaged its ***Contra Advance: The Alien Wars EX*** strategy guide with its *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* guide.

### *Konami Collector's Series: Castlevania & Contra*
right|200px
    - Konami Collector's Series: Castlevania & Contra*** is a compilation of five classic NES Konami games ported to the PC. Three are from the *Castlevania* series and two from *Contra*.

The games included are:
  - Castlevania*
  - Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*
  - Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*
  - Contra*
  - Super C*

### *Contra ReBirth*
The second game in Konami's "*ReBirth*" series made for WiiWare. It was later followed up with *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*.

## Arthroverta and Kimkoh
Arthroverta from *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* is an homage to Kimkoh, a boss found in many games throughout the *Contra* series.
