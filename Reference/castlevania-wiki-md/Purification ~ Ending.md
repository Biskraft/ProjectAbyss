  - Purification ~ Ending** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the background theme that plays during the cutscene where Soma Cruz is temporally incapacitated after the destruction of Dracula's Castle and shortly before he wakes up at the Hakuba Shrine with Mina by his side.
