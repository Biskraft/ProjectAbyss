# Icy Crest

- Icy Crest ** is a special skill in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It uses a frontal attack that projects an icy wind capable of freezing any enemies in its path. This skill freezes enemies faster than Void Projections, while simultaneously damaging them.
