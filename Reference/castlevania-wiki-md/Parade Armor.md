# Parade Armor

73px|right

  - Parade Armor** is gaudily decorated armor.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it grants +26 Defense, which is 1 more than a Puff Coat and 2 less than Chain Mail.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, it can be created by adding 2 Orichalcum and 3 Ceramics to a Field Armor, which raises its defense from +35 to +45 and surpasses the defense of Cuirassair Armor (+40). Adding an Angel Halo and 2 Holy Man's Veins increases its Defense to 50 to create a Maximillian Armor, which is the toughest armor in the game.
