# Doll Decoy

right|128px
  - Doll Decoy** is a spell which Soma Cruz can use in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, and it can be obtained from a Killer Doll. Soma will toss a doll forward which resembles himself, and most enemies will draw their attention to it, allowing him to move freely through a room. In rare cases, the doll will resemble Mina Hakuba.
