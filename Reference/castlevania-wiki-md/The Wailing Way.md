  - The Wailing Way** is a location in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It consists on a large graveyard complex which contains tombstones and mausoleums. Spectral, skeletal and ghostly enemies call this locale "home". It connects directly to the next area, the Shrine of the Apostates, which is just a continuation of the Wailing Way.
