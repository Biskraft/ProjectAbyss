# Raccoon Charm

right|73px

The **Raccoon Charm**, also called **Raccoon Figure**, is a collectible item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* and an accessory in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* that increases LCK by 10. It is more effective than the Coin of Happiness, which raises it by 5.
