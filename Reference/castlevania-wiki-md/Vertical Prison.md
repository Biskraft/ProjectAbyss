# Vertical Prison

The **Vertical Prison** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. It is the ninth area Simon explores. During Simon's exploration, there are water levels that must be adjusted to reach higher areas, although this becomes unnecessary upon obtaining the Combat Cross. Previously, it was explored by Trevor as the twelfth area in his journey.
