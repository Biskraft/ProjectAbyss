# Agreus Familiar

right|300px

The owl familiar of Agreus, appearing in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It steals the piece of the Mirror of Fate, leaving his master to hunt down Dracula to claim vengeance for his brother, Pan.

If Dracula fires a Chaos Bomb at it before it flies to another perch, it will be cooked into a pot roast and the player will be rewarded with a hidden Achievement/Trophy.
