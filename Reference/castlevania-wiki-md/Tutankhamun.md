# Tutankhamun

- Tutankhamun** ("Kāmen Kameo" in Japanese) appeared as a boss in *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*. Its hands were detached from the head and used as platforms and orbited the central mask, which spit out poisonous bubbles.

Another enemy featuring a Tutankhamun-like mask is Elgiza in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
