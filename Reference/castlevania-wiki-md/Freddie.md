  - Freddie** is an enemy in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. He is a humanoid creature who advances toward the enemy while swiping with his claws.

## Description
Only three Freddies are encountered in the entire game, all in the room along the Sadam Woods that gives access via an underground path to the Storigoi Graveyard.

They pace back and forth at a moderate speed while swiping with their claws. When the player approaches, they'll increase their speed. They're quite resilient, especially at night, so it's advised to come armed with a powerful whip (likely the Chain Whip at that point in the game).

If entering the room from the right, the first two will be found moving on lower levels, so they can be dealt with without much effort by throwing downward Sacred Flames or vials of Holy Water.

## Trivia
- Freddie's Japanese name is *Kagitsume*, which translates into "Claw". This similarity with the horror films character Freddy Krueger is likely what gave this enemy his English name.
  - Timothy Bradley points out this similarity in the novelization, in which he states that the fact Freddie's name is spelled with an "-ie" instead of a "-y" may mean that he's a "nice monster".
