# Michelle Danasty

was a descendant of Grant Danasty and learned the arts of the vampire hunter. She is armed with a bowgun and uses silver arrows. She is the partner and possible romantic interest for Curtis Lang, Julius Belmont's apprentice, who works as a hunter for the church.

## Notes
  - Akumajō Dracula: Kabuchi no Tsuisoukyoku* does not have the cut-in illustration. Her official art does not exist.
