# Liza

- Liza** is an original character who appeared in the *Akumajō Dracula: Lament of Innocence* comic, which is a prequel to and a retelling of the PlayStation 2 game. Her fiancé was killed by Walter.

During the course of the story, Liza is killed by Joachim Armster.
