# Lollipop

right|73px
A **Lollipop** is a food item that recovers health in the *Castlevania* series of games. Its first appearance was as a food item for Maria in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and later in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*, and is the first kind of candy to appear in a *Castlevania* game. It later made an appearance in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
