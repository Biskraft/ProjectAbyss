# Fingers of Frankenstein

The **Fingers of Frankenstein** are items that can be collected in the *Reverie* DLC for *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. Presumably, they are fingers taken from Friedrich Von Frankenstein by Carmilla. They are hidden items that serve no other function other than to be found. Finding all of them them unlocks a Steam achievement / Xbox trophy entitled *Master of the reliquary*.
