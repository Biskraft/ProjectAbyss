# Ceramic Mail

- Ceramic Mail** is an armor crafted from a material that modern alchemists can't comprehend.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it grants a +90 Defense boost, which is the highest amount for any armor in the game. This is 15 more than Silver Armor and 10 more than the Moon Armor or Sun Armor are at their maximum. It has the same total attribute boost that the Kaiser Mail has, which only has +75 Defense, but also has +5 Strength, Intelligence, and Luck.
