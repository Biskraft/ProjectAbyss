# Leila

- Leila** is a character in the alternate timeline gamebook *The Legend of Satanic Castle: The Vampire Hunters*. She is a descendant of Alucard.

## Trivia
  - The Legend of Satanic Castle* came out in 1990, soon after the release of *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*. At that point, Alucard was still perceived merely as a vampire by design; the whole concept of him being a dhampir was implemented years later with the inclusion of Lisa into the storyline. As such, Leila was simply designed as being a vampire too, and her ability to manipulate holy items could simply have been a liberty taken by the designers.
