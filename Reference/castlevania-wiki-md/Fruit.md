# Fruit

- Fruits** are a type of food that recovers HP or MP in the *Castlevania*. In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, they are the least HP replenishing food.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, they recover MP and raises MND. In the same game, the strawberry, also increases LCK, as well as any Sweet that is Strawberry-flavored.
