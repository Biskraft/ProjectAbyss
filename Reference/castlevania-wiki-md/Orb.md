# Orb

The , introduced in the 2003 Sony PlayStation 2 game *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, is a variant of the Magic Crystal, which can be obtained after the player defeat the stage bosses, except for the White Orb, which is placed in a hidden room in the Anti-Soul Mysteries Lab. There are seven Orbs in the game, only five of which are necessary to complete the game. Once an orb is obtained, it is never lost or used up. Orb effects consume hearts.

The player can choose one of the Orbs to combine with a Sub-Weapon, creating a more powerful attack or even an Item Crash. There are various attacks depending on the chosen orb. In general, Orbs in *Lament of Innocence* are similar to the Spell Books in *Harmony of Dissonance*.

Joachim Armster can also pick up these orbs after defeating the bosses in his mode, although they don't provide any gameplay benefits other than replenishing HP.
