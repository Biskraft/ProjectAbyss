# Throne Entrance

The **Throne Entrance** is the fourteenth area Alucard explores in Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate. It is here that Alucard must place the two Blood Orbs found along his journey in order to open the entrance to the Throne Room. The area outside of the throne room is decorated with a giant red door with a large golden dragon seal attached to the middle and on each side of the door are paintings of large dragons on war-torn battlefields.

## Trivia
