# Sand Free

- Sand Free** is an Ability Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It prevents loss of mobility even in sand.

## Characteristics
Obtained by gaining dominance over a Mud Demon's soul. It allows Soma to move at normal speed while traveling on sand. Its uses are limited, though, as only The Abyss has rooms with sand in.
