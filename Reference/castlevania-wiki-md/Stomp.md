# Stomp

A **Stomp** in *Lords of Shadow* games is similar to dodge, but performed while airborne. This is a good defense against enemies that attack you while you are in mid-air. Landing back on the ground can also knock back smaller enemies surrounding you. The move is similar to a Jump Kick in previous *Castlevania* games.
