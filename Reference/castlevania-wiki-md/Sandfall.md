# Sandfall

- Sandfall** (known as **Fifth** in *King's Valley II*) is a music theme composed by Masahiro Ikariko for the MSX game *King's Valley II: The Seal of El Giza*.

The theme was arranged by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and is the background theme of the Forgotten City stage.
