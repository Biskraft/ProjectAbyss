# Clock Tower (Harmony of Dissonance)

The **Clock Tower** in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* is the place where you fight Max Slimer. In Castle A it resembles a generic clock tower, but in Castle B the sky turns an ominous red and the tower's machinery appears to be broken down. The Clock Tower is the first place with a teleport room that can switch between castle layers. It's also the place where the map is upgraded to distinguish between castle layers. In Castle B the Peeping Big is fought.
