# Card/Encore of the Night

Cards can be collected in Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night. Combining them into poker hands allows for the completion of certain Honors, which in turn may award items. Cards come in two suits, "Crosses" (representing the forces of good) and "Skulls" (representing the forces of chaos). Cards can be found throughout the castle, or as drop items, or as rewards for accomplishing other Honors. There are two types of Honors that can be completed by collecting cards, those that award you something for completing a Poker hand, and those that award you for collecting a certain number of cards. Completing one of these honors rewards you with an item. These Honors are also Achievements in the Windows Phone 7 version of the game and are worth a varying amount of Gamer Points.
