# Claws

right|150px
  - Claws** are short range weapons which can be used to swipe at opponents, in some cases they pertain to a weapon class. There are many variations of claws throughout the series, either by being natural claws grown out from fingers, or wrist worn weapons which provide large metal claws to a character. They usually deal Slash damage.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*
Freddies were notorious in *Simon's Quest* for rushing at Simon while swiping their claws. Originally named "Claw" in Japan, in the Western release his name was changed to "Freddie", due to his similarity with the horror movie character, , which was notorious for wearing a metal glove with large claws to murder his victims.

### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
Malachi attacked by performing a powerful punch-like swipe with his claws in *Symphony of the Night*, a feature which remained in later games in which he also appeared.

### *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*
Claws are the short-range weapon that Cornell uses in *Legacy of Darkness*. His normal attack actually projects a blade-like energy swipe which travels through the air, allowing him to perform medium range attacks.

### *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*
Forging the Claws is a "trap" combination devised to waste the extremely rare Miracle Egg material and creating it should be avoided unless looking on acquiring a complete item set.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
Shanoa utilizes claw swipes as her main attack when she takes the form of a black panther using the Arma Felix glyph, which recovers +1 HP after each hit.

### *Castlevania Judgment*
Similarly to his incarnation in *Legacy of Darkness*, many of Cornell's attacks in *Judgment* utilize his claws as well.

### *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*
Dracula utilizes the Chaos Claws in order to gain punching and clawing attacks that are augmented with the power of fire. They also enable him to break through enemy armor through heat and smashing damage.
