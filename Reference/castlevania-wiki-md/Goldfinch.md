# Goldfinch

right|200px


  - Goldfinch** is a Bird-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a Level 2 Innocent Devil, and can learn Caltrops. It can be obtained from a Crow Devil, using 40 green, yellow or white crystals.

What's unique about this evolution is that it is capable of dropping $1 coins either when attacking or being attacked.
