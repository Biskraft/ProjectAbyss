# Plate Mail

right|73px

The **Plate Mail** is armor made entirely of metal and thus extremely heavy.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it gives Defense +52, which is 4 more than Heavy Armor and 3 less than Double Mail.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, Plate Mail is created from scratch by combining two White Steel for a defense of +32, which is 2 more than Banded Mail and 3 less than Field Armor. It cannot be evolved further.
