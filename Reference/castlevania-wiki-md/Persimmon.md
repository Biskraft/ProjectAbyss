# Persimmon

The **Persimmon** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. Being a "bitter fruit", it is the only fruit to appear in a *Castlevania* game that gives less HP than Grapes. The only game it has appeared in is *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is dropped by a Dryad tree.
