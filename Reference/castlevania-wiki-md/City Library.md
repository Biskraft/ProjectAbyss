# City Library

The **City Library** is an area within the Arts District of Castlevania City in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It is located right next to the Cathedral.

It is here that Dracula both encounters Victor Belmont for a second time and is forced to fight and kill Abaddon in the library's courtyard.

## History
According to several City Memorials, the library holds much historical evidence of myths and legends collected by the Society of Esotericism. These myths include the Child Wolf of Dracula, the festival of the Chupacabras, and the White Vampire. It also hides paintings and tapestries (much of which is from Dracula's Castle) that the Church deems unholy and spiritually dangerous to its congregation.
