# Daniela

is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. She is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. She is an elderly woman who claims that in her youth she was a vampire hunter.

## Description
Daniela is the only villager besides Nikolai and Jacob to treat Shanoa as if she would treat anybody else. She gives her missions to help her recollect her past. She mentions being a vampire hunter in her youth. After completing her quests, she offers to take Shanoa on a picnic when she has completed her task of defeating Dracula (she appears to be somewhat aware of Shanoa's quest, seeing as she was a vampire hunter herself in her youth). She also says that she will prepare a lovely cake for her when she returns.

## Trivia
