# Big Balloon

The **Big Balloon** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

## Description
Very similar to other balloons, when the Big Balloon is hit, it bursts and releases three regular Balloons, which in turn behave normally when struck by releasing poisonous spores.

When first encountered in the Sky Walkway (Castle A), fighting the Big Balloon is a good way to earn EXP, as the player can unleash a well-timed whip strike that hits the Big Balloon and all three spawns at once, and then quickly exit and reenter the room, allowing them to gain several levels before fighting the Devil boss.
