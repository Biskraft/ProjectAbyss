The **Storm Skeleton** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is a skeleton of a person who died in a storm. He comes apart and attacks in pieces.

## Description
This green colored skeleton armed with a mace can separate its body in order to teleport and become invulnerable, either at will or when the player performs a Perfect Guard.

An lightning aura can be seen around it each time he recomposes, but despite his name he never delivers any lightning attack.
