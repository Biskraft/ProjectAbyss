# Yuka Watanabe

- Yuka Watanabe** (also known as 渡邉愉香 *Watanabe Imaka*) is a Japanese music composer who worked on various *Castlevania* titles.

## Selected works
### Programming/Engineering
  - JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Eyes of Heaven* (2016)
  - Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4* (2016)
  - Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm Revolution* (2014)
  - The Sword of Etheria* (2005)

### Audio
  - Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 3* (2013)
  - Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 3 - Full Burst* (2013)
  - Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* (2010)
  - Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* (2005)
  - The Sword of Etheria* (2005)
  - Blue's Journey* (1990)

### Special thanks
  - Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* (2006)
  - Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* (2005)
