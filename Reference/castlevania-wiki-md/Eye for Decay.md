# Eye for Decay

The **Eye of Decay** (or **Eye for Decay**) is a special eye-piece that allows one to see breakable blocks in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is similar in function to Dracula's Eyeball in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.
