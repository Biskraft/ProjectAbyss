# Pitiful Scion

Young Nobleman of Madness}}

  - Pitiful Scion**, also known as **The Tragic Prince** or **Young Nobleman of Sadness**, is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It is the background music played in the Clock Tower. Some fans consider it as Alucard's theme, along with Dracula's Castle.
