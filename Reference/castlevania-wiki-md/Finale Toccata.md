# Finale Toccata

- Finale Toccata** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It can be heard as the background theme for most stages in the Reverse Castle (Reverse Clock Tower, Reverse Entrance, Reverse Outer Wall, Death Wing's Lair, Black Marble Gallery and Necromancy Laboratory).
