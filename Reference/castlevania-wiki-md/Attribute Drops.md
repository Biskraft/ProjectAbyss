# Attribute Drops

- Attribute Drops** are consumable items in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* which permanently increase Shanoa's Attribute Points of a certain element by 1,000.

## Availability
Attribute Drops appear in many places throughout the course of the game. The first ones can be found by breaking certain walls or by crouching at specific spots in a level, revealing blue treasure chests. Others can be obtained as rewards for completing quests, mainly from Serge and Anna. But most of them can be found throughout the diverse areas that conform Dracula's Castle.
