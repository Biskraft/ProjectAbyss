# Summon Unicorn

right|300px
A summoning spell found in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. A unicorn is summoned that heals about 1/6 of Nathan's health. It is cast by combining the Uranus + Unicorn DSS cards. This spell does not deal damage to enemies.
