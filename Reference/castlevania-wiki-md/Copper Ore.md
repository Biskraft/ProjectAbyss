# Copper Ore

- Copper Ore** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is just a chunk of copper ore.

## Availability
Dropped by Axe Knights and Spear Guards.

## Usage
Copper Ores serve no other purpose than to be sold at the shop for a modest amount of gold.
