  - Title Screen** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is the background theme that plays when a short animation leading up to the game's title screen starts. When the animation concludes with a slow vertical panning that directs the scenery to Dracula's Castle, the title screen itself falls silent.
