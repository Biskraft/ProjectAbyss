# SD Famicom Girl Force

right|250px
, or **Famicom Card**, are playing cards inspired by classic Konami Famicom games. They are part of the *Famicom Runners High* series.

These cards include Sypha Belnades from *Castlevania*, Yae from *Ganbare Goemon*, and Whip from *TwinBee*, among other Konami female characters.

## Characters
- Card no. 10: Aphrodite from *Majō Densetsu II: Daima Shikyō Gariusu*
- Card no. 14: Whip from *Moero TwinBee*
- Card no. 15: Lang from *Yie Ar Kung-Fu*
- Card no. 17: Sypha Belnades from *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*
- Card no. 172: Risa Tachibana from *Risa no Yōsei Densetsu*
- Card no. 181: Nana from *Ganbare Goemon Gaiden 2*
- Card no. 182: Hadzuki from *Ganbare Goemon Gaiden 2*
- Card no. 183: Yae from *Ganbare Goemon Gaiden*
- Card no. 184: Rin from *Ganbare Goemon Gaiden*
- Card no. 185: Omitsu from *Ganbare Goemon! Karakuri Dōchū*
- Card no. 244: Chris from *Lagrange Point*
- Card no. 245: Rita from *Lagrange Point*
- Card no. 263: Konami Lady from *Konami Wai Wai World*
- Card no. 275: Miss Mishtarina from *Parodius Da!* (Famicom version)
