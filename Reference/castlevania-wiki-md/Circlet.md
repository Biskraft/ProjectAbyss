# Circlet

A **Circlet** is a simple ring-like crown found in the *Castlevania* series.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it increases INT by 5. More powerful circlets can be found in that game that also have an effect on an attack type. These items cause Alucard to "Absorb" attacks of a certain type so that he gains HP when he is attacked by them.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* (called a **Plain Circlet** in the North American version), it is a low grade defensive head item, granting +3 DEF. This is one greater than a Sturdy Turban, but one less than a Leather Cap.
