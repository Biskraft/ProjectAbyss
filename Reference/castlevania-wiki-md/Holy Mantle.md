# Holy Mantle

The **Holy Mantle** is a cape in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is a long cape worn by high-ranking clergymen.

It can be purchased from Vincent Dorin for $10,000G after defeating the first boss, Dullahan. It gives the same statistical boost as the Blue Cape (+3 DEF, +3 INT). It is outclassed by the Paludamentum, which gives a +5 boost of each.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, the Holy Mantle is the cape with the highest proportional emphasis on MP, as well as the only cape that gives both a CON and MND boost.
