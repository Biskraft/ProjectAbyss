# Weapon

The term **Weapon** (also called **Primary Weapon**, **Base Weapon**, or **Standard Weapon**) usually refers to the main weapon held in a playable character's hand, attack in front of the character, is assigned a single button press, and does not consume resources such as Hearts or Mind Points.

There are several different types of weapons that can be equipped:

- Whip
- Dagger
- Short Sword
- Sword
- Great Sword
- Katana
- Rapier (or "Foil")
- Snake Sword
- Throwing Sword
- Cudgel (or "Club")
- Cestus (or "Fist")
- Axe
- Tome
- Glyph
- Lance (or "Spear" or "Polearm")
- Staff
- Firearm
- Special Weapon (or "Miscellaneous")
It should be noted that in many cases where there exist the mechanic of equip-able weapons that be removed, the user will always attack with their fist by default.
