# Jade Corset

The **Jade Corset** is a corset made of Jade. It is the second weakest of the corsets in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and gives +6 DEF, which is greater than a Leather Corset (+3), and less than an Amethyst Corset (+11).
