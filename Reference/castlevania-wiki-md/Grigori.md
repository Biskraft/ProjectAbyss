# Grigori

thumb|300px|Death of two guards.

In IDW's *Castlevania: The Belmont Legacy*, **Grigori** is one of two men guarding Dracula's tomb during Christopher and Illyana Belmont's midnight wedding ceremony. They're easily dispatched by Sona Bartley's crossbow.
