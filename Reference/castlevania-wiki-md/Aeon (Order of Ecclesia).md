# Aeon (Order of Ecclesia)

is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. He is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. He is the town's local chef.

## Description
Aeon can cook up just about anything... or so he says. He claims that he's the best chef in the world, but he is always finding flaws in his cooking. By doing his missions he will realize his full potential, and they will add food items to the shop.

## Location
Aeon is found in the central tower of Minera Prison Island. However, Shanoa will not be able to rescue him on her first visit to this place as the Ordinary Rock is required in order to reach him, so she will have to wait until she obtains this item after completing the Skeleton Cave.
