# Shape of Happiness

is a *tankobon* (collection) of comic stories based on company video game properties. It was published by Famitsu Comix and released from June 20, 1986 to March 4, 1994 in Japan. A re-edition was later distributed by ASCII Comix. The manga were drawn by Tamakichi Sakura.

- Castlevania*, *Gradius* and *Moero TwinBee* are some of the video games included in this manga collection.
