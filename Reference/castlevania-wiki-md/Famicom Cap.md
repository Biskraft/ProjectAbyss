# Famicom Cap

is a Japanese manga by Takeshi Kobayashi, serialized in Comic BomBom and published by Tentomushi Comics from February 1986 to August 1987, and later released in three *tankōbon* volumes.

- Castlevania* is one of the video games featured in the manga.

## Characters
The *Castlevania* characters appear in Volume 3.
- Mummy
- Frankenstein
- Medusa
- Dracula
