# Miracle Egg

The **Miracle Egg** is a material in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. No one knows what will be born from this egg. Looks as though it could suck you right in.

## Availability
- Guaranteed drop from Golden Bones in Baljhet Mountains, Garibaldi Temple and the Tower of Evermore.
- Rare drop from Evil Cores in Garibaldi Temple.
- ×1 found in Dracula's Castle.

Even if the Miracle Egg is one of the rarest materials in the game, there's a somewhat easy way to obtain them early on. In Baljhet Mountains, go to the Save Room located before reaching the Wyvern's boss room. Keep entering and exiting the Save Room until a Golden Bones appears instead of the three Skeletons which generally spawn in that area; once defeated, it will leave behind a Miracle Egg.

Golden Bones always spawns alone and he will <u>never</u> spawn in front of the Save Room, only on either end of the room, so as soon as an enemy is seen spawning after exiting the Save Room, reenter it and try again. 

It is unknown if equipping LCK-boosting items/Innocent Devils raises the chance of encountering a Golden Bones.
