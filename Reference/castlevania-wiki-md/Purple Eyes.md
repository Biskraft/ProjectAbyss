# Purple Eyes

- Purple Eyes** is a music theme composed by Tomoaki Hirono for *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It is heard in the Options menu of the game. It can later be heard again in *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula: Lords of Shadow* during a segment.
