# Satan's Ring

The  is a stat-boosting accessory which greatly increases DEF in addition to other stats at a constant smaller value.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, it increases DEF by 7, and ATK, STR, CON and INT by 2, being similar in value to the Ancient Belt.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it increases DEF by 6, and STR, CON and INT by 3, and is notably sold for 33,333 gold.

## Trivia
- On the English localizations, the ring is sometimes mistakenly said to have been worn by Satan. When this is not the case, the ring still keeps the name "Satan's Ring", implying it once belonged to him. On the original Japanese version, both the ring's name and description are actually referring to Dracula, who is the Dark Lord. The confusion stems from the fact that the Japanese word used to refer to the  appears on the ring's name and description, and is the same word understood by the Occident to mean "Satan", which probably escaped the localization team's attention. Another evidence for this is the fact that the ring in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* bears a bat motif, a symbol most commonly associated with Dracula.
  - It could thus be assumed that both the Satan's Ring and the Ring of Vlad are one and the same. Supporting this theory is the fact that both rings have never appeared together in the same game.
