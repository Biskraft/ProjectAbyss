# Beetles

:*This article is for the insect. For the arthropod, see Arthroverta.*

Beetles are insects that appear in the *Kid Dracula* series. They are not "true" beetles or insects though, as they only have two pairs of legs. The Scarab beetle from the Famicom version sit in a sand pit. When you wander above it, it jumps up and tries to bite you. **Percy Pincher** from the Game Boy game inhabits the forests and comes down at you from the tree tops.
