# Svarog Statue

- Svarog Statue** is a relic appears in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. When you use this relic, a flames sprout in your footsteps. This is found on the tutorial phase in the Castle Entrance, and is required to defeat the unique Golden Knight enemy.

## Item Data


## Trivia
- This is the only mandatory relic in the game, being incorporated in the tutorial, and the player can finish the main story even without collecting all of the other relics.
