# Death/Musical Themes

Throughout the *Castlevania* series, Death had several musical themes accompanying his various appearances, during cutscenes or battles. In *Lament of Innocence*, his official theme was called Evil's Symphonic Poem (subtitled "Death's Theme"), then arranged in *Castlevania Judgment*.
- Poison Mind (*Castlevania*, *Vampire Killer*)
- Room of Close Associates (*Super Castlevania IV*)
- Dancing in Phantasmic Hell (*Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*, *Castlevania: Dracula X*, *Castlevania* (N64))
- Poetic Death (*Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*)
- Dark Covenant (*Harmony of Dissonance*)
- Evil's Symphonic Poem (*Lament of Innocence*, *Judgment*)
- Portal to Dark Bravery (*Dawn of Sorrow*)
- The Dark Holy Man (*Curse of Darkness*)
- Vampire Killer (*The Arcade*)
- Symphony of Battle (*Harmony of Despair*)
