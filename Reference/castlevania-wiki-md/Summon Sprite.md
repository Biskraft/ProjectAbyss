# Summon Sprite

right|300px
  - Summon Sprite** is a spell that can be performed by using the Herald Shield's shield combo spell with either a Shield Rod or Mablung Sword. It costs 10 MP to cast and gives Resist Fire and Resist Thunder.

Can also be summoned in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* through the combination of the Saturn + Griffin DSS card. A small green sprite hovers near Nathan, attacking enemies with an energy ball attack that has slight homing properties.
