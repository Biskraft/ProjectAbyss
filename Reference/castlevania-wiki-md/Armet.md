# Armet

right|73px
The **Armet** is a helmet fashioned to protect the back of its user's head in *Castlevania* series.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance,* it grants Defense +15, which is one more than a Guardian Helm, but one less than a Bascinet.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, it is the first type of helmet that can be evolved from a Sallet and can later evolve into the toughest helmet, the Cross Helmet. It can be created by adding Red Steel to the Sallet, which brings its defense up from +14 to +20. This overtakes the defense of the Barrel Helm (+16) and the Lobster Tail Pod (+18). Its toughness can be overtaken when another Sallet evolves into a Burgonet (+22) by adding Damascus Steel, which can be evolved further into a Crest Helm (+24) with Ceramics. The Armet can overtake this by evolving into the Cross Helmet (+26).
