# Master Librarian (Lords of Shadow)

The **Master Librarian** is a mentioned character in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. He is an inhabitant of Dracula's castle and presumably the caretaker of the castle's libraries. He is an expert on a number of monsters and demons, and his notes can be found scribbled on many monster texts.

He does not appear physically in the game, although quotes and notes from him appear in five of the game's bestiary entries, usually providing a hint on how to defeat them, save for the Chupacabras' entry which instead reveals the Master Librarian's contempt for him due to the Chupacabras stealing rare books from him.

The other entries that his notes appear in are Abaddon, Dark Monk, Bloody Skeleton and Gorgon.

## Trivia
- He is a reference to the Master Librarian from *Symphony of the Night*.
- It is possible that the Chupacabras stealing rare books from the Master Librarian may have been one of the reasons why Dracula had the imp imprisoned.
