# Skeleton Rider

The **Skeleton Rider** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a skeleton knight atop a dragon steed. Killing the rider will not stop the mount's berserk charge.

## Description
### Skeleton Rider
A skeleton lancer atop a dragon steed. It attacks by throwing its lance at the player or by having its steed breathe fire. Once the rider is killed, the steed will charge in a circle and open its stealing window, allowing the opportunity to steal a Dragon Scale from it.


### Skeleton Trooper
A Skeleton Rider clad in heavy armor both for itself and its mount. Its main method of attack consists in charging at the player. The charge will continue even long after the rider has been disposed of.
