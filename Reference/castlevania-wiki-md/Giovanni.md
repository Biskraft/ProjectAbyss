  - Giovanni** is a supporting character of the 2007 cellphone game *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*. He is a descendant of Rinaldo Gandolfi who aided the Belmont clan in tracking The Order.

The Belmont Sisters came upon the Alchemic Gauntlet in Order's possession and Giovanni tells them that it will be able to give its owner the ability to use alchemical artifacts.
