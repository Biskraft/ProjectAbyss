# Jorge Benedito

- Jorge Benedito Chicharro**, sometimes credited simply as **Jorge Benedito**, is an Spanish lead concept artist and character designer at MercurySteam since January 2008. He created concept art for all three *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* games, serving as the main character designer and action storyboard artist for *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. A portrait of Jorge is unlockable in the first *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* game.

Benedito also conceptualized artwork for *Metroid: Samus Returns*. He previously worked at Zinkia Entertainment as an illustrator for a year. His latest project is the character design for the game *Raiders of the Broken Planet*.
