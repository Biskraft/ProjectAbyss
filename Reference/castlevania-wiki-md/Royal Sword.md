# Royal Sword

right|200pxThe **Royal Sword** is a Holy based great sword which can be wielded by Jonathan Morris in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It provides an ATK boost of +84, as well as +6 boosts in STR, INT and CON.

## Availability
Given to the player by Wind after completing his *"A Rank Hunter"* quest. To accomplish this feat, the player must have defeated at least 1,500 enemies throughout the course of the game. An easy way to do this, is to enter the painting that leads to Sandy Grave and defeat the constantly spawning Mummies in both rooms of the desert area.

## Notes
- This is one of the most powerful weapons the player can obtain at the moment it is given, due to its boosts to STR, INT and CON, added to its raw physical attack power of +84; not to mention it is also Holy based, which most enemies in the game are weak against.
  - However, due to this element, it also makes it quite impractical for fighting Holy based enemies, and the player should refrain from using it against these types of enemies (Aliorumnae, Amalaric Snipers, Whip's Memory, etc.).
