# Maria Renard/Symphony of the Night/Saturn

250px|thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">Maria in *Symphony of the Night*.</p>

  - Maria Renard** is a boss and a playable character in the Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.

## General information
Maria Renard is a playable character in the Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which was only released in Japan, conversely to her appearance in the PlayStation version of that game, where she only appeared as a supporting, non-playable character. She is a teenage version of the original 12 years old Maria who was introduced into the series in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*.

Maria is selectable from the beginning of the game. This version of her is different than the one that would later be available in the *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* version of *Symphony of the Night*, which played more like her original *Rondo of Blood* incarnation.

Maria also needs to be fought as a boss during Alucard's quest in order to receive the Holy Glasses. However, while playing as Maria, she cannot fight against her own boss version since she cannot have access to the central areas of the castle.

## Moveset
Maria does not have a weapon equipped. Many of her attacks involve kicking an enemy.


## Spells
Maria's primary spell is a chargeable energy ball which can be unleashed at the push of a button. While Maria does not use her traditional holy beasts as sub-weapons in this game, she is able to summon them through magic spells. Each beast has a different spell. These spells consume MP.


## Sub-Weapons
*Main article: Four Sacred Beasts*
Maria is able to use her very own set of animal sub-weapons in a similar way to Richter. Most of her sub-weapons only consume one Heart, but she can fire several of them at once for certain sub-weapons. While Maria cannot perform item crashes with her sub-weapons, as an alternative she uses spells with her animals (as stated above).

She starts the game with the ability to hold 999 Hearts.


## Items
Maria is able to collect Small Hearts and Large Hearts. Most items that Alucard could collect during his quest are replaced with Small Hearts that can be repeatedly collected. Life Vessels restore all life and increase life capacity, although it is not readily apparent from the health bar on the side of the screen. Heart Vessels increase Hearts by 30 but do not increase Heart capacity. Gold can be collected but has no effect, as the Master Librarian ignores Maria.


## Maria as a boss
During Alucard's quest, Maria is encountered as a boss. If he wins the battle, she will give him the Holy Glasses.
