# Rainbow Robe

The **Rainbow Robe**, also known as **Rainbow Mantle**, is either a robe or cloak in the *Castlevania* series. It is a colorful robe.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The **Rainbow Mantle** is a cloak that is constantly changing colors, found only in the Sega Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. The amount of CON it gives is +7, which bridges the gap between the Crystal Cloak (+6) and the Royal Cloak (+8).

### *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*
The **Rainbow Robe** is rarely dropped by Forest Armors and Poison Armors and commonly dropped by Ice Demons. It has the highest LCK boost of any body equipment in the game.

## Trivia
- The Rainbow Robe may be a reference to Saruman in *The Lord of the Rings* book trilogy; as Saruman, after throwing his lot with Sauron, proceeded to wear a multi-colored set of robes and started calling himself "Saruman of Many Colors". This is further supported by the Japanese version of *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, where it's called the "Robe of Many Colors".
