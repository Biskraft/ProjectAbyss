# PlayStation 3

right|300px
The **PlayStation 3** is a Sony video game console released on November 11th, 2006 in Japan, November 17th, 2006 in United States and March 23rd, 2007 in Europe. It is a HD console and the successor of PlayStation 2.

## Games available
  - Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*
  - Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
  - Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*
