# Lady of the Crypt's Chamber

The **Lady of the Crypt's Chamber** is the seventh area Trevor explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. It is here that the Lady of the Crypt is fought. Mainly it is just a large cavern for the Lady of the Crypt to reside and the entrance to this area is at a hole from the Crypt's Labyrinth, This area won't be accessible once it is visit.
