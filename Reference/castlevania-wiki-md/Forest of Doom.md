# Forest of Doom

The **Forest of Doom** is a location in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and is the fourth portrait in the game.

## Overview
This portrait is a murky swamp, complete with an abandoned sorcery academy, now filled with monsters. This level features enemies such as Treants, Maneating Plants and Frogs. The level's boss is Dagon, a fish-like creature with a tail that has a separate head that looks like a frog.

The portrait Dark Academy is an alternate version of this stage.
