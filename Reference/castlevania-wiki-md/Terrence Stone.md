# Terrence Stone

- Terrence Stone** (b. March 2, 1955, Wicklow, Ireland) is an Irish-American actor, singer, voice director and producer. He is best known for his work as the Innkeeper/Harth Stonebrew in *Hearthstone*, Myuri Kurotsuchi in the anime *Bleach*, and Zhao Yun in the computer game *Dynasty Warriors*. He is also a widely known voice director who has recently worked on the animated series *Spy Kids* on Netflix and *Where's Waldo?* for Dreamworks.

He voiced Trevor Belmont in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
