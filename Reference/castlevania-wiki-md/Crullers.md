# Crullers

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">[[Donuts and Crullers.</p>]]

  - Crullers** are common desserts in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. They are choux pastries with a nice, light texture and often embellished with different flavors, offering additional benefits.

The **Plain Cruller** recovers 20 MP and HP and increase INT by 1.

The **Chocolate Cruller** recovers 25 MP and HP and increase CON and INT by 1. It can be considered the Constitution upgrade to the Plain Cruller.

The **Berry Cruller** recovers 25 MP and HP and increase LCK and INT by 1. It can be considered the Luck upgrade to the Plain Cruller.

The **Green Tea Cruller** recovers 25 MP and HP and increase MND and INT by 1. It can be considered the Mind upgrade to the Plain Cruller.
