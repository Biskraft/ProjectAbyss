# The 6 Servants of the Devil

- The 6 Servants of the Devil** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Bloodlines*. It plays during battles against major level bosses.

It is the 34th track in the fourth disc of Akumajō Dracula Best Music Collections BOX.
