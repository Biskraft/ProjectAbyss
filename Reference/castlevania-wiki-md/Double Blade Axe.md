# Double Blade Axe

right|250px
The **Double Blade Axe** is a two handed axe in the Sega Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It gives ATT+42 and can be dropped by a Gargoyle [lv. 35]. This is the only primary Axe weapon that can be found in any version of *Symphony of the Night*.
