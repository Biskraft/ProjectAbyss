# Eyewear

- Eyewear** is a type of Head Gear that is worn over the eyes to improve vision or for protection. They can boost DEF as they offer protection to the eyes, increase MP or INT as they can improve focus, allow you to see things in the game that you normally can't see, or offer protection again "Holy" ("Light") if they are tinted.
