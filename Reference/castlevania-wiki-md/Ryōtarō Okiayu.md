# Ryōtarō Okiayu

is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is currently affiliated with Aoni Production.

Okiayu is the original voice actor for Alucard in four Japanese versions of *Castlevania* games: *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* (as Genya Arikado), *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, and also reprising the role in *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate*. He also provided the Japanese voice of Trevor Belmont in the Netflix *Castlevania* animated series.

Okiayu's other famous character roles include Treize Khushrenada in *Mobile Suit Gundam Wing*, Hisashi Mitsui in the *Slam Dunk* series, Yuu Matsuura in *Marmalade Boy*, Meisuke Nueno in *Hell Teacher Nūbē*, Vamp in *Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty* and Zero in the *Mega Man X* series.
