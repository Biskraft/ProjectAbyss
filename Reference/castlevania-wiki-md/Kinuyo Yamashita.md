# Kinuyo Yamashita

, also known as "**yamako**", is the first composer of the *Castlevania* series.

In the original *Castlevania*, her debut work, she shared credits with another composer, Satoe Terashima, under the pseudonym of "James Banana", although they didn't actually collaborate; each composer contributed with her own themes and all in conjunction conformed the entire soundtrack of the game.

She was part of the Konami Kukeiha Club and composed the music for various other games, such as *Mega Man X3* (1995) for Capcom and *Buffy the Vampire Slayer: Wrath of the Darkhul King* (2003) for THQ.

## Discography in the *Castlevania* series
  - Castlevania* (Konami, 1986) (Debut, with Satoe Terashima)
  - Vampire Killer* (Konami, 1986)
  - Wicked Child
  - Walking on the Edge
  - Heart of Fire
  - Poison Mind
  - Nothing to Lose
  - Black Night
  - Sound effects (*Castlevania*)
