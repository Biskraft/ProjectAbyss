# Cubus

right|100px

  - Cubus** is a glyph in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It creates blocks of stones.

## Characteristics
Found in the Monastery near the beginning of the game, although it's recommended to refrain from attempting to get it until Ignis has been obtained, as the Cubus glyph is covered behind a wall of blocks that are infinitely replaced as soon as they're destroyed. The Ignis Glyph Union can clear out enough of these blocks at once, allowing Shanoa to absorb the glyph.

Despite the effort required in obtaining it, this glyph isn't very useful, except for creating walls which inflict minimal damage. However, only Cubus and Fidelis Medusa the only glyphs capable of inflicting petrifying status to enemies.

Once Cubus has been absorbed, the blocks of the wall will stop spawning infinitely (although a great quantity of them will keep on falling to replace any destroyed). If the player takes a moment to destroy all of the remaining blocks, a treasure chest containing the Justice Ring will appear.
