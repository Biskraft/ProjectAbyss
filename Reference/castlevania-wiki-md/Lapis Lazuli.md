# Lapis Lazuli

The **Lapis Lazuli** is a jewel in *Castlevania* that is associated with Luck and is often found in a ring.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it is a ring that increases Luck by 20. It starts out in the inventory when playing Luck Mode. It is also dropped by Orobourous.

This ring was replaced with the Luck Ring in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

The Lapis Lazuli in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* is an item that by itself does nothing. It is needed for Laura's "A Lucky Stone" quest. If the player brings Laura a Lapis Lazuli, she will use it to create an earring shaped like a clover and make the Lucky Clover available at Jacob's shop.
