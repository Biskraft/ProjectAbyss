# The Final Battle

- The Final Battle** is a music theme originally composed for the late 80s arcade game, *Haunted Castle*. An enhanced remix later appeared in *Castlevania: The Adventure Rebirth* as both the main boss theme as well as the song used for the second phase for the final battle against Dracula.
