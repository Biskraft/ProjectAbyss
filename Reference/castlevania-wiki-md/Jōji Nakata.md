# Jōji Nakata

- Hitoshi Nakata**, better known by his screen name **Jōji Nakata**, is a Japanese actor, voice actor and narrator. He was a member of Ken Production but later moved to Office Osawa.

Jōji is well known for voicing Alucard in *Hellsing*, Kirei Kotomine in *Fate/stay night*, and Corporal Giroro in *Sgt. Frog*.

He voiced Dracula in the Japanese version of *Castlevania Judgment*.
