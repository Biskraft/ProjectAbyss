# The Legend of the Mystical Ninja

is a Super Famicom game by Konami, and the first installment in the *Ganbare Goemon* series for that system.

Kid Dracula appears as a secret item in the first stage.

## Trivia
- The cover artwork for both the North American and European versions of the game was done by Tom duBois, who had also created many cover artworks for several other Konami games, especially from their so-called "golden era".
- The first level of *Gradius* can be played as one of the minigames hosted by Konami Lady.
