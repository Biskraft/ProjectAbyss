# Kit Bag

right|73px
The **Tool Bag** (later known as **Kit Bag** in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*) is an item that increases the maximum number of items that can be equipped. In *Lament of Innocence* it can be found in a room of the Garden Forgotten by Time that requires the Blue Dragon Key, in which the said key is found in the Dark Palace of Waterfalls.
