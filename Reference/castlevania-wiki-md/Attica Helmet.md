# Attica Helmet

right|50px

The **Attica Helmet** is a Greek Helmet in Castlevania.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, it is one of Jonathan's highest ranking helmets in the facet of offense, giving a Defense boost of 7 and a Strength boost of 5. This can be considered an upgrade to the Viking Helmet (+4 DEF, +3 STR). It gives less Defense than the Shogun Helmet, which gives DEF +10 and STR +3, but at the expense of INT -5.
