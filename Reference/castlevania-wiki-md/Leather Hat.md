# Leather Hat

A **Leather Hat** is basic Head Gear.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, this gives the lowest amount of any Head Gear that only increases DEF. It gives identical protection as the Ballroom Mask.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, the Leather Hat drops in the first stage and is probably the best non-boss chest helm found in first chapter on normal.
