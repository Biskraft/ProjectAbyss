# Trevor Belmont (disambiguation)

The name **Trevor Belmont** can refer to:
- **1989** — Trevor Belmont: Protagonist of *Dracula's Curse*, supporting character of *Curse of Darkness*, and appearing in *Judgment*.
    - 1993** — Trevor Belmont: A character mentioned in *Captain N: The Game Master*.
    - 1997** — Sonia's child: A character briefly mentioned in *Castlevania Legends*, newborn son of Sonia Belmont and Alucard, speculated to be Trevor Belmont. (retconned)
    - 2013** — Trevor Belmont: One of the playable characters in *Mirror of Fate*.
    - 2017** — Trevor Belmont: A version of the character based on that from the original timeline and one of the main protagonists of the *Castlevania* animated series.
