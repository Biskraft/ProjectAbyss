# Carpet Bombs

right

  - Carpet Bombs** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by the Skull Wing evolution of the Bird-Type. When cast, the I.D. will soar tracing circles over any nearby enemies and starts dropping "bone-bombs" all over the battlefield, potentially dealing massive damage to crowds.
