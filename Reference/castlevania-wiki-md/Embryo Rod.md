# Embryo Rod

right|200px
  - Embryo Rod** is a Mage-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It's a level 4 Innocent Devil and can learn Explosion B and Demonic Disaster. It can be obtained from a Nautilus Rod using red, yellow or white crystals; or from an Ogre Rod using red or green crystals.
