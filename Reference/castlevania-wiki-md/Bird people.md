# Bird people

right|250px

  - Bird people** are several types of demons and creatures in the *Castlevania* series whose bodies are conformed with both bird and human parts.

## Appearances
The first and most recognizable of these creatures is without a doubt the Harpy, a monster of Greek origins, part bird/part human, who first appeared in *Haunted Castle* and which since has become a frequent enemy in following titles. *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* features a Bird Man which would fly in the air, then come down and walk on the land for a while. Later versions of this monster would also shoot fireballs. *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* introduced the crow demon Malphas boss (also called **Karasuman** and **Feather Demon**), which later became a regular enemy. The Siren, mythical human/bird beauties whose singing could drive men to crash their ships into dangerous rocks, became a palette swap for the Harpy in some games.
