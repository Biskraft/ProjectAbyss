# Portal to Dark Bravery

- Portal to Dark Bravery** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the game's third boss theme, which plays while fighting Aguni, Death and Abaddon.

It reappeared in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, this time called **Great Gate of Darkness**, as the boss theme of the Nest of Evil, featuring the return of many bosses from *Dawn of Sorrow*.
