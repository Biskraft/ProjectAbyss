# Clubbing Knight

The **Clubbing Knight** is an enemy in *Super Castlevania IV*.

## Description
An armored knight (or skeleton) that hides in the background and comes out swinging the club he wields as the player passes by. His location is given away by him constantly letting out his free hand with which he supports himself as he leans out to attack.

It is not necessary for the knight to completely come out from his hiding place in order to be damaged; he can be defeated simply by continuously striking his hand.
