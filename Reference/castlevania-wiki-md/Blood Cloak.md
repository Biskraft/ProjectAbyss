# Blood Cloak

right|73px

The **Blood Cloak** is a cape that converts damage taken into either Hearts or MP. It is similar in appearance to the Crimson Cloak, which only modifies statistics.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it converts each point of damage taken into Hearts. It gives +4 CON and +2 DEF, which is one more CON than the Reverse Cloak and one less than the Elven Cloak.

In *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* (called the **Bloody Cape**), it also converts damage to Hearts as it did in *Symphony of the Night*. It gives no stat boosts in this game.

Wearing the Blood Cloak in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* while taking damage causes the MP meter to fill up. The cape is otherwise unremarkable (+3 to HP, MP and DEF) and is the only cape that does not offer protection against some kind of attack.
