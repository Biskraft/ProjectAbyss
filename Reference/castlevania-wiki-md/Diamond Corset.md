# Diamond Corset

50px|right

The Diamond Corset is a corset made of Diamond.  It is the body component of Charlotte's Diamond Outfit, which maximizes Charlotte Aulin's Defense (the foot item are the Diamond Shoes, the head item is a Diamond Tiara, and the Diamond Brooch is an accessory).  Like the other items, it gives more Defense than any other Charlotte-exclusive item for its equipment slot.  It also gives the highest amount of Defense for any corset (+32), which is greater than a Platinum Corset (+25).
