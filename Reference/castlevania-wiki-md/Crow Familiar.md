# Crow Familiar

right|368px
The **Crow Familiar** is a crow that can be summoned by combining a Saturn card with a Golem card in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* and can electrocute enemies. In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, an 8-bit raven can be summoned using a R. Raven soul.
