# Christopher Belmont (disambiguation)

The name **Christopher Belmont** can refer to:

- **1989** - **1991**  Christopher Belmont, the protagonist of the games *Castlevania: The Adventure* and *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge.*

- **2005**  Christopher Belmont, the protagonist of the comic *Castlevania: The Belmont Legacy*.

- **2009**  Christopher Belmont, the protagonist of the remake game, *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*.
