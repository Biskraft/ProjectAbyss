# Doubled Sub-Weapon

The **Doubled Sub-Weapon** is a power allowing to summon a double of the sub-weapon used to hit an enemy twice or to increase their power.

## Trivia
- This ability could be considered to be a variant of the Double Shot power-up from previous games, although instead of allowing the player to shoot two sub-weapons in a row, it does so simultaneously.
