# Herb

right|150px

The **Herb** is a unique "sub-weapon" found in *Castlevania Chronicles* which can restore HP for a price of 10 Hearts.

It can be obtained as a random drop from enemies, from certain candles, or "bought" from the Flute Player in Stage 2.
