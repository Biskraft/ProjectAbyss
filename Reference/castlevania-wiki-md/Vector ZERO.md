# Vector ZERO

right|130px

  - Vector ZERO** is the ability to hover in mid-air at the middle of a jump or while falling. Soma Cruz can gain this ability with a Medusa Head's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*.
