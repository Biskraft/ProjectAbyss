# Vassago

- Vassago** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is an evil spirit said to be summoned by those who seek knowledge.

## Origins
Vassago is a prince of Hell, commanding 26 legions of demons. In spite of his appearance in *Castlevania* as an enemy, Vassago is considered a "good" demon who reveals facets of the past long since forgotten by men, as well as future events yet to come.
