# Toy Maker's Funfair

right|200px

The **Toy Maker's Funfair** is the sixteenth area Simon explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. It consists of a carousel wheel designed to kill intruders. It is here that Simon encounters Alucard a second time.

## Trivia
- The creature heads on the carousel wheel are all references to previous Castlevania series enemies - Medusa or Medusa Head, Behemoth, Manticore and Man Eater.
- Underneath the carousel wheel is the area Carousel Engine where Alucard saves Simon by moving glass spheres to reflect a laser and direct it to a gear which makes the wheel spin.
