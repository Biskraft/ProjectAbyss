# Rue

- Rue** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a medicinal herb native to southern Europe.

One must be given to Abram, along with a Chamomile, in order to complete the "Unusual Medicine Components" quest, which adds Anti-Venoms and Uncurse Potions to Jacob's shop.

One can be found at plain sight on a high ledge at Misty Forest Road. They can also be found as random drops from common wooden item boxes in Tymeo Mountains, Tristis Pass and Argila Swamp.
