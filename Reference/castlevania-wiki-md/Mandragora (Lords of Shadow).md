The **Mandragora** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. They are bipedal plants appearing in various levels, such as the Maze Gardens and Woes Moor.

## Description
Common ingredients in the creation of magic potions, mandrake roots are an important resource for alchemists and mages the world over. However, the harvesting of mandrake roots is a dangerous activity; when the root is dug up, the plant will scream loudly, killing anyone near it. When they are fully grown, mandrakes become Mandragora - sentient beings who survive by draining the life force of nearby creatures.

## Strategy
Mandragoras are easy to kill, just whip them a couple of times or grip and successfully finish a timed event. They attack by attempting to absorb Gabriel's health. Attack them quickly and that should not be an issue.

## Maze Gardens Trial
Defeat 15 Mandragoras in less than 15 seconds.
