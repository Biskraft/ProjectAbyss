# Invitation of a Crazed Moon

- Invitation of a Crazed Moon** is a song composed by Yuzo Koshiro for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is identified as the background theme for the Entrance in the game. It later reappears in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* as a selectable theme.
