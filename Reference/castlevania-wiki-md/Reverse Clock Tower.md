The  is a location in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is an upside down version of the Clock Tower.

## Overview
An upside down version of the Clock Tower, now mainly decorated in purple tones. It can first be entered from the Reverse Keep and eventually leads to the Reverse Outer Wall.

The Darkwing Bat is fought right at the beginning of the area (in the same room where Karasuman was fought in the normal castle). It drops the Ring of Vlad, one of Dracula's five relics which must be collected in order to enter Shaft's chamber at the center of the castle and obtain the true ending of the game.

The area is inhabited by loads of Cloaked Knights, which may drop the valuable Heaven Sword.
