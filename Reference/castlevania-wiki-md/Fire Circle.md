# Fire Circle

right|158px

  - Fire Circle** is a spell that involves small flames revolving slowly around its caster, providing some protection against nearby foes or projectiles. It is referred to as a "cloak of tumbling fire". It was first able to be performed by Nathan Graves in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* by combining the Jupiter + Salamander DSS cards. It can also be used by Soma Cruz by using the Buer guardian soul in *Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*.

Circle Fire is the slowest of all the Projectile Circles in the Castlevania series, including the Ice Circle and Stone Circle, but also the most damaging due to its base damage and also its Fire attribute, to which many enemies throughout the series are weak to.
