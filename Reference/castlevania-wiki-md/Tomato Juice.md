# Tomato Juice

right|73px

The  is a beverage found in *Castlevania*. It is made of tomatoes and heals the player's health.

In *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, the Tomato Juice can be dropped by hunting down Flea Men.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the player can steal it from Dracula.


## Item Data


## Trivia
- It being a stealable item from Dracula in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* is probably a reference to the character Blade from Marvel Comics, as the character, a vampire, often drinks tomato juice as a substitute for blood.
- The can design for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* is also a parody on the American brand Campbell's, who while are known more for their lines of canned soups, also produce tomato juice and currently own V8 as a subsidiary.
