# Arma Felix

right|250px

  - Arma Felix** is a back glyph that turns Shanoa into a werecat, a Black Panther, albeit with a different color scheme. While in this form, Ladycats and Black Panthers will not attack her, nor be damaged by her. Instead, they will attack other enemies.

Pressing the primary attack button will make Shanoa do a lunging claw attack, with each hit adding +1 HP. This attack can be performed multiple times at once, allowing Shanoa to cover an area faster. Pressing the secondary attack button will make Shanoa attack with her tail. This attack hits multiple times at once, usually three or four times, depending on the size of the enemy.

Using this glyph also allows Shanoa to talk to the cats she has rescued and now reside in Wygol Village. As with Arma Chiroptera, the player cannot access the menu while transformed.
