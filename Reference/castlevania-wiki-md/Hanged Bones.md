# Hanged Bones

- Hanged Bones** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is a skeleton that sways like a swing.

## Description
Hanged Bones resemble a large basket made of ribs with a skull at the center attached to a "stem" of long bones. They slowly sway back and forth, intermittently breathing fire. Aside from their attack, the Hanged Bones are mostly harmless and can even be used as impromptu platforms.
