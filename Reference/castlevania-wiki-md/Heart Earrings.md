# Heart Earrings

- Heart Earrings** are earrings in the shape of a heart. They are old jewelry that was found by Anna. She will give them to Shanoa if she can bring a mouse to her cat, Tom (which can be found roaming the chambers of the sunken ship in Kalidus Channel) during her "Mice Make for Good Eats" quest. With it equipped, only big Hearts will be dropped from breaking candles, which are worth five Hearts each, as opposed to normal Hearts being worth only one. Also, it makes small Hearts drop frequently from defeated enemies.
