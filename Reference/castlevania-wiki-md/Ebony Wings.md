# Ebony Wings

- Ebony Wings** is a music theme composed by Yasuhiro Ichihashi for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is the background theme for two sections of Dracula's Castle: the Arms Depot and the Barracks.

It later appeared in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* as the default background theme for the fifth chapter, Song of the Unslakable Blade, as well as in *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate* under the name **Jet Black Wings**.
