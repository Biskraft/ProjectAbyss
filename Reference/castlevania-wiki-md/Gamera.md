## Trivia
- Gamera is based on a kaiju created by the Daiei Motion Picture Company in 1965. The original monster has the general configuration of a turtle, albeit a tremendously large one, that is capable of walking on two legs and flying. He possesses a pronounced sagittal crest on top of his head and his mouth is filled with teeth, plus a pair of large tusks protruding upward from the lower jaws.
