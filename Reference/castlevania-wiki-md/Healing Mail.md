# Healing Mail

The **Healing Mail** (sometimes called Walk Armor) is a body armor that slowly regenerates the player's HP over time.

## Characteristics
In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, the 'Healing Mail restores about 1 HP of damage for every three seconds of continuous walking. It also grants a +4 DEF boost, which is the same as the Iron Cuirass.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, the Healing Mail grants +15 DEF and restores HP while not moving. There is also a similar armor, the **Walk Armor** (called **Heal Walker** in the Japanese version), which restores health only while in motion, but has twice as much DEF (30), which is the same as the Scale Armor.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, the Healing Mail restores Jonathan's HP while walking. It grants +12 DEF, which is 3 more than the Copper Plate, but 3 less than the Iron Plate. It's located in the City of Haze, inside a hidden room accessed by breaking a part of the ceiling midway through the underground railway with the pump trolley. Since this entrance is high above, it can only be accessed later in the game, once the player has acquired means to reach it (such as Owl Morph or Griffon Wing).
