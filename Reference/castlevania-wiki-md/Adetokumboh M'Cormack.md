# Adetokumboh M'Cormack

- Frederick Adetokumboh M'Cormack** (born December 16, 1984) is a Sierra Leone Creole actor, known for his roles in the television series *Lost* and *Heroes*.

He voices Isaac in the *Castlevania* animated series.
