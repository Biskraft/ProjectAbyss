# Corpseweed

The **Corpseweed** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. They are grown Unes with razor-sharp leaves that feed off the nutrients of corpses and bear the face of the human they fed on.

## Description
Corpseweeds are plants that have grown upon a grave and absorbed the contents, becoming half living plant, half zombie. They first appear as a normal Une, but after some time they grow a tall stalk with a venom-spitting flower at the top.
