# Ground Meat

The **Ground Meat** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.

## Availability and usage
Obtaining Ground Meat is a requirement for Wind's second quest, "Supersonic Punch". In order to obtain it, the player must first make sure that the quest is active; otherwise, the item will not spawn. Go back to the City of Haze and find the butchery (room on the top-left of the first building). Inside, there will be three hanging animal carcasses (there are two chopped legs to their left, but these won't yield the item). Unequip Jonathan's current weapon (it can't be done with Charlotte, as she has to hold her Encyclopedia at least) and punch them to mince the meat and obtain an already packaged Ground Meat.

Once the first Ground Meat has been acquired, a message will appear confirming that the quest is ready for turning in and the rest of the carcasses will once again drop Small Hearts; however, if all three carcasses are minced before picking any item, up to three Ground Meats can be collected.
