# Decipher

- Decipher** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned as an innate skill of the Tiramisu Fairy-Type. It allows the player to read old scripts in some parts of the game, giving them subtle hints.

## Trivia
- All of the inscriptions mention a character named Sage Eneomaos.
- In the US version of *Curse of Darkness*, the Chauve-souris was mistranslated as "Shyovasli" in the inscription found in the Infinite Corridor. This was corrected in the European version of the game.
