# Sachertorte

right|50px

A **Sachertorte** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It is a type of chocolate cake that appears in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It can be obtained by dropping a $1 gold piece into the register at the City of Haze bakery, which is easier to get than a NY Cheesecake ($5).
