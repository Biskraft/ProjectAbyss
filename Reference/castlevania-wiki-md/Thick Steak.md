# Thick Steak

- Thick Steak** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It becomes available for purchase at Jacob's shop once Aeon's third and final quest, "Can't Cook Without Ingredients", has been completed. It replenishes HP +1,000 upon usage, the highest amount of health restored by any other item in the game.
