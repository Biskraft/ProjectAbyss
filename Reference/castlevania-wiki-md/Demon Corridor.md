# Demon Corridor

300px|thumb|right|Alucard fighting Shaft in the Demon Corridor.

The **Demon Corridor** is a final stretch in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* that includes a series of boss battles that lead to Dracula, replacing the Reverse Castle. With one exception, the first five bosses are the same bosses that held Dracula's Relics in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which are also the bosses from the original *Castlevania* game. Beelzebub must be fought instead of Akmodan II, who does not appear in this game. This leads up to the encounter with Shaft and ultimately Dracula.
