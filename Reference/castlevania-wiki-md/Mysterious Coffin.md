  - Mysterious Coffin**, also known as **Secret Coffin**, is a brief track that plays in *Castlevania* (N64). It plays in the villa's crypt, when the player attends a meal of a vampire.

The beginning of the song contains a brief dramatic pipe organ arrangement of Bloody Tears.
