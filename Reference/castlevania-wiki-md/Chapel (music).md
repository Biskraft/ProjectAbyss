# Chapel (music)

- Chapel** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the background theme of the Chapel area in Dracula's vacant castle.

A remixed version called **Requiem for the Dark Souls** was included in the Castlevania: Lament of Innocence Original Soundtrack. It is later recycled into *Castlevania: Grimoire of Souls* as a cutscene background music, where it is given the name **Dracula Appears**.
