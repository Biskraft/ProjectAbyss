# Lady Liberty

- Lady Liberty** is the Statue of Liberty as a boss at the end of the New York City stage in *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*. She is unable to fight, however, so she challenges Kid Dracula to a quiz instead. Kid Dracula must defeat the other contestants, the Parent Ghost and Mr. Chicken. If he loses to them, he loses a life.
