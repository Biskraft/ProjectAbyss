# Shuriken

right|73px
The **Shuriken** is a throwing star weapon. Several varieties exist with different strengths, including a **Cross Shuriken**, **Fire Shuriken** (also known as a **Flame Star**), and the **Yagyu Shuriken** (also known as a **Buffalo Star**).

Shurikens are thrown straight ahead in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is thrown downward and forward in a circular loop in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* by Maxim Kischine. In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, shurikens are thrown downward slightly as if they were thrown at an enemy's feet.

The  is sub-weapon used by Getsu Fuma in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
