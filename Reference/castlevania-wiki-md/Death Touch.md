# Death Touch

- Death Touch** is an Enchanted Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It allows to absorb enemies' HP when an ordinary attack is delivered.

## Characteristics
The **Death Touch** ability allows Soma Cruz to gain +5 HP for each hit delivered to an enemy with a normal attack. It can be obtained by gaining dominance of the Succubus soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It is heavily implied that this was one of Dracula's primary abilities.

Some weapons also count with this ability, such as the Belnades Staff in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.
