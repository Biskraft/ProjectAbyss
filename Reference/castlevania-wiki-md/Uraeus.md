# Uraeus

right|50px

The **Uraeus** is an Egyptian royal crown decorated with a golden cobra. In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, it grants Defense +3 plus a decent Luck +5. This is inferior to the similar Princess Tiara, which grants DEF +3 and LCK +7.
