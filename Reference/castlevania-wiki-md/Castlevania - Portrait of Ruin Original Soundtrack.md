# Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin Original Soundtrack

- Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin Original Soundtrack** contains two CDs with the same track list, but Disc 1 covers the game's DS arrangements soundtrack, while Disc 2 is a composer-original album. The music was composed by Michiru Yamane and Yuzo Koshiro. Arrangements on Disc 1 were done by Norikazu Miura. The soundtrack was released on March 23, 2007.

## Additional information
- Composer and arranger: Michiru Yamane.
- Composer and arranger: Yuzo Koshiro - Tracks 4, 12, 15, 16 and 32.
- Composer: T and Michiru Yamane - Tracks 10, 14.
- Composer: Konami Kukeiha Club and arranger: Michiru Yamane - Tracks 21, 24~26, 31 - Disc 2 - 37).
- Composer: Konami Kukeiha Club and arranger: Norikazu Miura (Disc 1 - 37).
- "Great Gate of Darkness" is the new title of *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*'s "Portal to Dark Bravery". The two tracks are exactly the same.
- The track "Bad Situation" was named "Troubled Times" the first time the track list was revealed.
- "Sandfall" and "In Search of the Secret Spell" are from Konami's *King's Valley II: The Seal of El Giza*.
