# Bangle

A **Bangle** is a wrist protector in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is the second least effective Bracelet, giving +3 Defense, which is one more than a Wrist Band, but much less than a Silver Bangle (+20). It also gives the same amount of defense as the Kaiser Knuckle (which also gives a +3 strength boost).
