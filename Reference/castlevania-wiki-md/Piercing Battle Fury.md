# Piercing Battle Fury

- Piercing Battle Fury** (known in Japan as **Sharp White Fighting Spirit**) is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the boss battle theme for the game's final boss, Menace.

It is later used in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* in The End of Chaos, the game's third stage, where Menace also fought.
