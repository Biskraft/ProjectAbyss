# Chrysoberyl

- Chrysoberyl** (also known as "Cat's Eye") is a jewel found in *Castlevania*. In *Order of Ecclesia*, it has no innate ability, but it can be used on Anna's *"Tom & Jewelry"* quest. She will request that you go see Laura and present her with a chrysoberyl in order to make a cat collar made with it, which will be able to prevent Tom from dying from the toxic flowers he ate. It can be found from rare item boxes in Tymeo Mountains and Somnus Reef.

A Cat-Eye Circlet can be found in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* which gives you health whenever you are hit by a feline-type enemy.
