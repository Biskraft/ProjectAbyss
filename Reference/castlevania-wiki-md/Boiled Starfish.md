# Boiled Starfish

- Boiled Starfish** is a food item that recovers health in the *Castlevania* series. Its first appearance was as a food item for Maria in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and later in *Dracula X Chronicles*. It also appears in *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Portrait of Ruin*.
