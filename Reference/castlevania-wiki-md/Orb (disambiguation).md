# Orb (disambiguation)

- Orb** may refer to:
    - Orb** — An item in *Castlevania: Bloodlines* that grants temporary invincibility on pickup. Has the same function as the Invisibility Potion.
    - Orb** — Special items in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* obtained after defeating bosses. They can be combined with sub-weapons to make them more powerful and to allow the player to perform item crashes.
    - Orb of Gehenna** — An attack by Death that sends large dark energy balls at an opponent. Appears in *Castlevania Judgment* and *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*.
    - Orb of Sorcery** — An attack by Shaft in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* that involves mystical orbs that can change their element.
