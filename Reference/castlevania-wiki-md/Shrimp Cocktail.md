# Shrimp Cocktail

thumb|150px|<p style="text-align: center;">*Harmony of Despair* gamerpic.</p>
  - Shrimp Cocktail** is a food item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

It recovers more HP (+100) than any other food item that only recovers HP. This is more than Gazpacho (+60). Several other items that give stat boosts give more HP than Shrimp Cocktail.
