# Lightning Doll

The **Lightning Doll** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is a doll possessed by the spirit of an electrocuted prisoner.

## Description
The Lightning Doll is a high-level version of the Killer Doll, and as such shares a similar behavior. They start in an inert, seated pose and only become aggressive when approached, whereupon they will float erratically in the player's general direction. They will routinely stop and electrify their bodies, causing increased damage if touched during this period. They are exclusively found in the Clock Tower.

The Lightning Doll is a magical construct and therefore is vulnerable to the Killer Mantle's soul, Astral Veil, which will kill it instantly.

Gaining dominance over its soul grants Soma the Plasma Blast ability, which allows him to discharge lightning from his fingertips.
