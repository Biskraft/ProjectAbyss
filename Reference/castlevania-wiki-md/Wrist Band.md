# Wrist Band

The **Wrist Band** is a low grade bracelet in Castlevania. It provides STR +5 in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, which is not much strength as the Gauntlet (+15).  In *Harmony of Dissonance*, it provides DEF +2, which is the lowest amount for any bracelet, being one less than a Bangle.
