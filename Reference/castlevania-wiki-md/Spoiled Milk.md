# Spoiled Milk

50px|right

  - Spoiled Milk** is a food item that decreases health in *Castlevania*. It appears in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow*, *Portrait of Ruin*, *Order of Ecclesia* and *Harmony of Despair*.

In *Aria of Sorrow*, it will damage Soma if he attempt to drink it without the Flesh Golem's soul equipped (Demon Stomach). In that case, the Spoiled Milk will recover 800 of Soma's HP, making it a wise alternative to shelling out cash for potions.

In *Harmony of Despair*, it is the most damaging bad food item. It reduces health by a full HP -300, which is three times as much as a Rusty Food Tin.

### Dropped by
  - Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* - Hell Boar
  - Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* - Jack O'Bones
  - Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* - Ghoul
