# Holy Symbol

The **Holy Symbol** is a relic that allows Alucard to walk through water without sustaining damage. Oddly enough, it resembles a snorkel.

  - Cleansing** has a similar effect in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, except that touching water while possessing Cleansing removes all poison from it and restores its color.
