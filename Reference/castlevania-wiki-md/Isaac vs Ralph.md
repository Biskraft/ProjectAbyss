  - Isaac vs Ralph** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is played during the cutscene when Zead warns Hector about Isaac's presence on the Abandoned Castle's hidden chamber.

Oddly, the name of this track doesn't fit the scene its played in, which precedes the fight between Trevor (Ralph) and Isaac.
