# Castle Map

right|73px
:*This item may sometimes be referred to as "Scroll". For the Lords of Shadow parchments, see Fallen Knight Scroll.*
In most *Castlevania* games, a **Map** item basically allows the player to reveal areas on the dungeon which are not yet accessed, signified by a gray color on the dungeon's map. However, a map does not necessarily reveal all of the hidden rooms and areas.

### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
In *Symphony of the Night*, the map item purchasable from the Master Librarian only contains the basic rooms for just the normal version of the castle. Interestingly, the original price of $103 for the PSX version was increased to $105 in the Dracula X Chronicles PSP version. This change references the increase of the Japanese consumption tax (1997=3%, 2007=5%).

### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
In *Aria of Sorrow*, there are three Castle Maps. Two can be bought in Hammer, instantly after he sets his shop, third needs to be found. Getting them uncovers some parts of in-game map, but areas discovered by map are in gray color (to distinguish them from areas where player was already).
