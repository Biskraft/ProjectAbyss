# Cheese

right|70px

 is a food item that recovers health in the *Castlevania* series. It recovers 18 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is one more than the highest replenishing dessert (Pudding) and one less than the lowest replenishing fast food item (a Frankfurter).

Its first appearance was as a food item for Maria Renard in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and later *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*.
