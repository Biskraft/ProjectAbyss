# Wai Wai (series)

The ***Wai Wai*** series (also translated as "**Krazy**" in English) are a number of mascot games published by Konami featuring characters from this company. The series ranges a variety of types of games, including platformers (*Konami Wai Wai World*), solitaire (*Wai Wai Poker*), horse racing simulation (*Wai Wai Jockey*), bingo (*Wai Wai Bingo*), racing (*Konami Krazy Racers* and *Krazy Kart Racing*), and box puzzles (*Konami Wai Wai Sokoban*). *Castlevania* characters frequently make appearances in these games.

Games in the series which include *Castlevania* characters:
  - Konami Wai Wai World* - Simon Belmont and a variety of enemies.
  - Wai Wai World 2: SOS!! Parsley Castle* - Simon Belmont and a variety of enemies.
  - Wai Wai Poker* - Kid Dracula
  - Wai Wai Jockey* - Kid Dracula
  - Wai Wai Bingo* - Kid Dracula
  - Konami Krazy Racers* - Dracula
  - Konami Wai Wai Sokoban* - Simon Belmont
  - Krazy Kart Racing* - Dracula
  - Piccadilly Circus: Konami Wai Wai World* - Simon Belmont
Category: Cameo Games
