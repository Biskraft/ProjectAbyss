# Coller

is a character who was intended to appear in the game *Castlevania* for the Nintendo 64, but was eventually canceled. He was a huge man with a shotgun for a primary weapon and a chainsaw for his secondary weapon. His character design served as one of the inspirations for the Gardener.

## Trivia
- It's possible that Coller was partially inspired by Ash Williams, from *The Evil Dead* series. Ash's main arsenal across most media is a shotgun and a chainsaw replacing one of his hands (as was intended for Coller). Not enough is known to confirm this, however.
