# Silver Stud

The **Silver Stud** is a stud earring made of silver that is found in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the third least effective earring, giving +2 DEF and +1 INT. This is 1 more DEF than a Turquoise Stud, but one less than a Gold Stud.
