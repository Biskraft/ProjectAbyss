# Castlevania: Lords of Shadow Exclusive Director's Cut Soundtrack

- Castlevania: Lords of Shadow Exclusive Director's Cut Soundtrack** is the expanded soundtrack composed by Óscar Araujo for *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*, featuring new tracks never released before. It was released on October 29th, 2013.
