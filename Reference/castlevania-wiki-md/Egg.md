# Egg

right

The **Egg** is one of Maria Renard's sub-weapons. It appears in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and its *Dracula X Chronicles* remake.

When an egg is equipped, Maria raises her hand holding an egg and says: "Egg". The egg's shell then breaks and a group of birds come out in a circular pattern, spinning toward the direction Maria is facing. A small red bird gets to be in the middle of some doves. It reduces a small amount of the enemy's health.
