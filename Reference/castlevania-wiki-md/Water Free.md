# Water Free

- Water Free** is an Ability Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It increases mobility in water.

## Characteristics
This ability can be obtained by gaining dominance over Procel's soul. It allows Soma to move underwater with the same speed as outside water.

Its uses are somewhat limited, as by the time Soma fights against the first Procel encountered, he will already have passed one of the two heavily flooded water areas.
