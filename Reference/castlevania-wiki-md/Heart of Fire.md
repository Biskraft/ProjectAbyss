# Heart of Fire

- Heart of Fire** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania*. It is the background theme of Stage 5. Arrangements of it have appeared in other games as well, such as *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, where it is Julius Belmont's theme.
