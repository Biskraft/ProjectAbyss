# Mitsumete Knight R: Daibouken-hen

is a role playing game with dating simulation elements developed and published by Konami for the PlayStation video game console on November 26, 1999.

## Features
Print Stations can be used throughout the course of the game where stickers of several mascots from popular Konami franchises make a cameo, including:
- Alucard from the *Castlevania* series.
- Kewne from the *Azure Dreams* series.
- Goemon and Ebisumaru from the *Ganbare Goemon* series.
- Vic Viper and Moai from the *Gradius* series.
- Power Pro-kun from the *MLB Power Pros* series.
- Takosuke and Koitsu from the *Parodius* series.
- Tir McDohl from the *Suikoden* series.
- Shiori and Saki from the *Tokimeki Memorial* series.
- TwinBee and Madoka from the *TwinBee* series.
