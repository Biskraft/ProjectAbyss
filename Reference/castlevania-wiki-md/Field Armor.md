# Field Armor

73px|right

  - Field Armor** is armor woven from long blades of grass in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, while it is extremely heavy armor in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it is the third weakest armor. It grants +8 Defense, which is two more than Rubber Armor and 4 less than a Plate Coat.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, it can be created from scratch using two Red Steels. It has a defense of +35, which is three more than Plate Mail. It can be upgraded to a Cuirassair Armor for 5 additional Defense points by adding Adamantine.
