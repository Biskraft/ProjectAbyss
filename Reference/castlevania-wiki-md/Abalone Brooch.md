# Abalone Brooch

The **Abalone Brooch** is the most average of Charlotte's brooches. It offers +3 DEF, midway between the lowest (Laurel Brooch, +1) and highest (Diamond Brooch, +5) and it offers no other benefit. Two other brooches increase DEF by the same amount, but have an additional affect, the Moon Brooch (LCK +5) and the Venus Brooch (INT, MND and LCK also +3).
