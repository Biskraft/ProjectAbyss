# Thracian Helmet

right|73px

The **Thracian Helmet** in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* is the second in a series of helmets that can be evolved from a Leather Helm which doubles the helmet's Defense boost. It can be created by adding Sun Tears to a Corinthian Helmet, which can first be obtained by stealing one from an Ifreet. This increases the Corinthian Helmet's defense from +4 to +8. This helmet will be outclassed by 2 Defense once you are able to obtain a Knight's Vein (first found in the Garibaldi Temple) to create a Gallic Helmet from another Leather Helm. Its Defense can be doubled yet again, however, by combining it with Carbon Steel and a Knight's Vein to form the Barrel Helm.
