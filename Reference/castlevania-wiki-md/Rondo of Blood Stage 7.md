# Rondo of Blood Stage 7

Stage 7 of *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* is called **Hear Now the Requiem of Blood** or **Believe in the Dawn**. This stage is the Clock Tower of the castle. Annette resides in this stage and must be rescued to get the best ending.

In the remake of *Rondo of Blood*, if the player does not rescue her, she will be bitten by Dracula, turned into a Lesser Vampire and must be defeated.
