# Fedora

The **Fedora** is a fashionable hat that just feels right with a whip.

## Item Data


## Trivia
- The item's description is a reference to fictional adventure films character Indiana Jones, as he is usually depicted wearing a fedora hat and wielding a whip.
- Producer Koji Igarashi is well known for donning a Fedora and a whip at conventions.
