# Laurel

right
The **Laurel** is a magical plant found in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. They can be bought in various towns, two plants for 50 Hearts, or be given a full supply by a traveling Gypsy for free in Laruba Mansion. When used, Simon will become invincible for several seconds, enabling him to safely walk through/past monsters and safely cross through the extensive poisonous marshes found in some areas, thus making Laurels a necessity (the huge marsh west of Jova, for example, cannot be passed unless Simon has Laurels in his inventory).

Simon can only hold four Laurels at any given time, but if he finds the Silk Bag, he can carry up to eight.
