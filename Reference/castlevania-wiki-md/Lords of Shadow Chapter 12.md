# Lords of Shadow Chapter 12

The twelfth and final chapter of Castlevania: Lords of Shadow (not including DLC). The only stage of this chapter is The Final Fight. Here, Gabriel  is able to achieve the last part of the God Mask, and many terrible revelations are uncovered, both to and about Gabriel Belmont himself, as well as the true mastermind behind the plot of separating Heaven and Earth. With his newfound knowledge and the truth behind the death of his wife Marie, Gabriel must face down and defeat the ultimate evil if he is to stand any chance of saving the trapped souls and reviving his love.
