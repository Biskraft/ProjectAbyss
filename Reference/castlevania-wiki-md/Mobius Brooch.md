# Mobius Brooch

The **Mobius Brooch** allows you to use Sub-Weapons or Innocent Devil powers without consuming Hearts in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can only be purchased after beating the game on Crazy Mode. It is similar in function to the Chaos Ring of *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and the Magus Ring in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
