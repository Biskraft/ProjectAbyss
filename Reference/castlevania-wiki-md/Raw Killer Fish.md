# Raw Killer Fish

- Raw Killer Fish** is a food item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is dropped by the Killer Fish. If Shanoa gives Aeon this item during his second quest, "I've Never Eaten That", he will make Killer Fish BBQ and Ramen Noodles available at Jacob's shop.
