# Chicken

- Chicken** is the spirit of the last chicken that Kid Dracula ate in *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun* and *Kid Dracula* (GB). It flutters around trying to hit Kid Dracula and attacks with its young chicks. It is also an opponent in the quiz game hosted by the Lady Liberty in *Boku Dracula-kun*, where it wears an "Uncle Sam" top hat.

A similar bird (falcon) wearing an top hat appears in *Gokujō Parodius!*, which can be fought by Kid Dracula.

It is possibly a precursor to the Hippogryph which Alucard would face in *Symphony of the Night*, which also attacks by charging and with its recently hatched baby birds.
