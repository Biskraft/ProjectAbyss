# STR Boost

right|100px**STR Boost** is a Stat Boost spell or power that increases Strength.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
In *Aria of Sorrow*, increasing STR increases the damage of both hand-held weapons and Bullet Souls. Soma Cruz can obtain dominance over a number of types of Soul that give him boosts to his strength when equipped. One type is a series of Enchanted Souls that give flat boosts to STR as a passive buff:

    - Knight's Power** - Skeleton Knight (STR +4)
    - Fighter's Power** - Minotaur (STR +8)
    - Great General's Power** - Golem (STR +12)
    - Battle God's Power** - Triton (STR +16)

There is also the Guardian Soul of the Great Armor, which is actually called **Strength Booster** and works differently to the others. Rather than providing a passive boost, this only increases STR while it is activated, and drains a substantial 60 MP/sec during this time. The boost is also not a fixed amount, and is described in a slightly misleading way: the Soul increases Soma's STR *to* 120%, not *by* 120% as the wording implies. In other words, if Soma's current STR is 50 and he activates Strength Booster his STR will increase to 60, not 110.

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* / *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
In *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*, Soma is able to gain this ability from a Golem's and Minotaur's souls respectively, and the more such souls he has, the more powerful the effect.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
  - STR Boost** is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Portrait of Ruin*. When cast, it boosts STR temporarily. A regular cast boosts STR +15, while a fully charged cast boosts STR +30.

Jonathan Morris has a counterpart for it, named **Offensive Form**.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
  - Vis Fio** is a back glyph that increases Shanoa's strength. This bonus scales as her STR stat increases, and thus, grows in power as she levels up.
