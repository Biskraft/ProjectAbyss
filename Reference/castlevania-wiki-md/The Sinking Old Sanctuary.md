# The Sinking Old Sanctuary

- The Sinking Old Sanctuary** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Bloodlines*. It is the background theme for Atlantis Shrine.

It later reappeared in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*, where it served as the background theme for the Art Tower level.

It also made an appearance in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* as the background theme for several areas, such as the Abyss Stairway, Audience Room, Outer Wall, Triumph Hallway and the Ceremonial Room.
