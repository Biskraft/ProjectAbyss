# Angel Halo

right|73px

  - Angel Halo** is a combining material in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a rare drop from Amduscias.

## General information
Hector can find an Angel Halo inside a chest located in the boss's room where he fought the Skeleton Diver at Mortvia Aqueduct. This area becomes explorable only after Dullahan has been defeated at the end of Infinite Corridor, as all flooded areas in the game will then become drained of water.

The Angel Halo is definitely the most difficult material to get in a normal game (even more so than a Jade, Beelzebub's Seal or Midas' Gold), as the enemy which drops this material, Amduscias, has an extremely low drop rate (even if wearing the Prince's Clothes/Clown Suit, Rare Ring, and summoned the player's best LCK-boosting Fairy-Type Innocent Devil). What makes this item so difficult to obtain is the way Amduscias reacts to Hector's attacks, as he's prone to perform his whirlwind attack (which makes him almost invulnerable) very frequently, even if he's in the middle of another attack. This attack happens at random and whenever the game decides Hector's attacks should not connect at that moment; however, some attacks, like a Battle-Type's Aura Blast, can pass through this whirlwind, making it a bit easier to obtain.

This valuable material can be spent in crafting the best DEF-boosting gear in the game, as well as the highest level two-handed sword and one-handed piercing sword in the game:
- Feather Sword (ATK +70)
- Estoc (ATK +38)
- Cross Helmet (DEF +26)
- Maximillian Armor (DEF +50)
