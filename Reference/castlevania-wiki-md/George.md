# George

- George** is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. He is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. He is a musician.

## Description
George comes off as a snobby music composer, and as such, will ask Shanoa to do tasks for him that no one else would want to do. The records he creates (and the ones found in treasure chests) are actually music from the classic *Castlevania* games.

## Location
Skeleton Cave
