# Black Hole

right|362px
A **Black Hole** can be used as a weapon in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. The Apollo + Black Dog DSS combination allows a Black Hole to be thrown across the screen. This will latch onto any enemy it touches, causing continual damage over several seconds. The Jupiter + Black Dog combination causes a Black Hole Barrier to protect Nathan Graves by remaining stationed where he is, but also drains his health.
