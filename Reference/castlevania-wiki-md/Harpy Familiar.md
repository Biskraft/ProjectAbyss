# Harpy Familiar

right|150px
The **Harpy Familiar** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It summons a Harpy as a familiar. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Harpy's soul.

## Characteristics
Once active, the Harpy Familiar will be summoned behind Soma's back, she will not attack on her own, only when Soma's uses his weapon or bullet soul. Her attack consist a barrage of feathers covering the area below Soma than can hit up to four times.

Like the Mini-Devil Familiar, she can be controlled by holding her in the touchscreen and moving her at any point on the screen, so her feather barrage can reach better spots.
