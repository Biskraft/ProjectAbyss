# Brigandine

right|73px

The **Brigandine** is an armor made from a combination of wood and metal or golden plates, but they could also be dragon scales.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, the Brigandine gives +40 DEF, which is 4 more than the Guardian Armor, but 5 less than the Ancient Armor.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the Brigandine can be created by adding a Knight's Vein to an Eastern Armor. This loses the Easter Armors +5 Attack boost, but and only gains a single DEF boost (DEF = 16). It cannot be evolved any further.
