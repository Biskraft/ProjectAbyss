# Dracula's Curse Block 3

- Block 3** (also known as Route 3) for the game *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* is the **Forest of Darkness**. The subweapons that Trevor can collect in order are the Holy Water, Cross (the first time this appears in a candle in the game), Axe, and Dagger.

The first stage 03-00 is only entered if you choose to skip the Clock Tower and enter the block from the end of Block 1. It consists of an area of spiked traps leading up some stairs to the forest area. Taking the path to the Clock Tower will lead you to Stage 3-01 of this block. At the end of 03-02, you are given the choice to continue along this route (go to 03-03), or exit early and go to Block 4 (Route 5, Marsh). If you choose to continue along this path, you will fight the Cyclops, rescue Sypha, and choose her as an ally. The path then continues to Block 4A (Route 4, Haunted Ship).
