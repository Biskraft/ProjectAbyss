# Bestiary Card

- Bestiary Cards** are items in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. They can be found throughout the castle and unlock a Bestiary entry for a particular enemy in the game's main menu. These entries contain information about an unlocked enemy and a 3D model for that enemy.

For a list of all bestiary entries and where they can be obtained, see Mirror of Fate Bestiary.
