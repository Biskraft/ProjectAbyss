# Science District

right|350px

The **Science District **is one of the major areas of Castlevania City in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2. *This part of the modern city is devoted to housing laboratories of scientific and technological research.

Fast travel here can be done with the Map Room or Wolf Altar.
