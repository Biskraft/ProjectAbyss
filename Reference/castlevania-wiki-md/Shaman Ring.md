# Shaman Ring

The **Shaman Ring** (called **Sherman Ring** in *Aria of Sorrow*) is an accessory in the *Castlevania* series which causes the protagonist to gain increased experience throughout their quests, be it from gaining it via just walking, or by increasing it through killing monsters.

It can be found in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* (DEF +1), *Dawn of Sorrow* (DEF +1), and in *Portrait of Ruin*.

The ring in *Dawn of Sorrow* can be found in a secret room located at the Cursed Clock Tower. To access this room, the last three digits of the current amount of gold the player is currently holding must end in "573". This value is an in-joke Konami uses in many of its games.
