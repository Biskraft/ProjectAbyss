# Sun Armor

Sun Armor (also called **Solar Plate**) is armor that is affected by sunlight.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it grants +40 Defense during the night time, but +80 during the day. During the day, it is the second most powerful armor after the Ceramic Mail (+90). At night, it has the same defense as a Brigandine. Its counterpart is the Moon Armor, which has the same states for the opposite time of day.

In *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, the Solar Plate is the fourth and last armor that can be purchased from Rinaldo Gandolfi and is available for 15,000 Gold once 80% of the map is covered. It reduces damage by 30% from all enemies, which is 10% more than the Moonlight Plate.
