# Head Throw

right|200px
Soma Cruz is able to throw a giant Fish Head in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* by utilizing its captured soul. It's not thrown very high or very far compared to most other projectiles.
