# Game Over (disambiguation)

The term Game Over may refer to:

## Music
- Game Over – An iconic short jingle appearing since the very first game which plays whenever the player loses of a life.
- Game Over (The Adventure) – An alternate short theme serving the same purpose introduced in *Castlevania: The Adventure* and appearing in a few other games.
- Game Over (Portrait of Ruin) – An alternate short theme serving the same purpose appearing exclusively in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.

## Other
- Death (event) – The end of a game after losing either all lives or the only life given; often colloquially referred to as "game over".
