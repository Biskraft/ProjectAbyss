The **Orc** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. They are pig-like monsters operating in packs and attack from long distances with burning arrows or a rapid firing blowgun.

## Origins
thumb|left|<p style="text-align: center;">Orcs in Peter Jackson's *[[wikipedia:The Lord of the Rings (film series)|The Lord of the Rings*.</p>]]

Orcs are a staple of many fantasy stories, especially *The Lord of the Rings*. They are often always portrayed as a tough, warmongering race of humanoids, though occasionally also shown to be a proud and honorable people as well. The origin of their racial name is derived from Old English "orc" which means "demon", itself presumably derived from the Latin "orcus" which was used to refer to "Hell."

The orcs's pig-like appearance in-game is possibly a reference to the superficially similar Gaelic word "orc", derived from the Proto-indo-european word *porkos* - "young pig".

## Description
### Orc
Orcs are not a particularly dangerous adversary, they fire one arrow at a time and are rather slow, the only real threat they would pose is when they fight in conjunction with other, faster enemies.


### Sniper Orc
An orc armed with a crossbow that can fire three successive shots. They are encountered often, and can be a nuisance. Sometimes one will drop a rare material item called Carbon Steel.


### Rapid Sniper
An upgraded version of its predecessors. Its bow can fire successive rapid shots, as the name implies. However, its arrows can be dodged by sidestepping.
