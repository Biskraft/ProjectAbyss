# Glasses

- Glasses** (otherwise labeled as **Sunglasses** in the English version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*) make one look more intelligent in the *Castlevania* and actually raise Intelligence by +2 in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. Normal glasses are the ones that raise INT, while Gambler Glasses raise LCK and Goggles raise DEF.
