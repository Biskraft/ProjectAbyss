# Zweihander

right|73px
The **Zweihander** is the third two-handed Great Sword in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* that possesses a slow swing and a special thrust move. It is an upgraded version of the Flamberge, but slightly weaker than the Obsidian Sword. It is also more powerful than the special Great Swords, Sword of Dawn (can summon allies) and the Estoc (always thrusts).
