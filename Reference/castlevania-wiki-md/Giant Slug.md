The **Giant Slug** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is a gigantic mollusk with an equally huge shell.

## Description
The Giant Slug is a snail-like enemy which belongs to the species of the mollusks. It is an advanced version of the Mollusca but, unlike the latter, it has a hard, spiked shell on its back which can block all enemy attacks. It also has the ability to poison its enemies on contact. To defeat it, the player must concentrate on attacking its unprotected areas.

## Soul Data
The Giant Slug's soul, Resist-Poison, grants total immunity to the Poison status.
