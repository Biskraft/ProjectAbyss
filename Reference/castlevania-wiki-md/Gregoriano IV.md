# Gregoriano IV

was the king of Transylvania around the late 1300s or early 1400s, according to *The Devil Castle Dracula: The Battle of Old Castle*. However, he was killed by Dracula, who then took his throne. He then became a servant ghost to Dracula.

His soul is finally released during the filming of a motion picture about Simon Belmont's battle against the Count.
