The **Heart Eater** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is a horrific bug that loves to feed on Hearts.

## Description
Heart Eaters float around Soma's head in a somewhat erratic manner and then suddenly dive-bomb on him, feeding from his supply of Hearts. Instead of dropping items or money when killed, they drop several Small Hearts on their trail while dying.
