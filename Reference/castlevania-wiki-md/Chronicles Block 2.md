  - The Underground Aqueduct** is the second stage in *Castlevania Chronicles*.

## Overview
The second stage of *Chronicles* leads the player through some underground chasms and walkways. All until a long vertical section awaits the player, where the water starts to rise rapidly and Mermen constantly spawn. Right after that, the stage's boss is fought.
