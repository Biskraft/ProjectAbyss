# Sign of Bloodlines

- Sign of Bloodlines**, also known as **Introduction** or **Sign of Blood Pulse**, is a music theme originally featured in *Castlevania* (N64). Containing a rearrangement of Divine Bloodlines, it plays during the introductory cutscene that precedes the game's title screen.

It later appeared in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, where it's played during the game's prologue sequence, which describes Dracula's impending return at Camilla's Castle.
