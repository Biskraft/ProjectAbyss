# Piercing Beam

right|304px

  - Piercing Beam** is the signature attack of the Nova Skeleton. It is a medium-width beam of energy emitted from its body, possessing considerable power, as well as long but limited range.

This ability can be obtained from the Nova Skeleton by several playable characters in several games. It can be obtained by Soma Cruz by capturing a Beam Skeleton's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* (called **Skeleton Beam**) and *Harmony of Despair*. Shanoa is able to absorb its related glyph (called **Nitesco**) in *Order of Ecclesia* and *Harmony of Despair*. In *Portrait of Ruin*, Charlotte Aulin has a beam attack (unrelated to the Nova Skeleton) called Piercing Beam that has unlimited range, but she can use the classic Nova Skeleton's beam (also called Piercing Beam) in *Harmony of Despair* by capturing its attack.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Piercing Beam is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Portrait of Ruin*. When cast, it fires a concentrated beam of powerful Holy light.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
The Nitesco glyph is both a Flame and Light spell which emits a purplish beam that reaches about half the screen's length. When used with another Nitesco glyph in a Glyph Union, the beam becomes twice as wide and long, and deals twice as much damage. When used with any normal weapon glyph, it turns into the Glyph Union Venus, where the Nitesco beam is swung overhead like a giant sword. Nitesco is not compatible with any other glyph.
