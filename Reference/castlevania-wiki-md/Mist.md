  - Mist** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is a mist that swallows up the living.

## Description
This disturbing mist with human face can spew out a poisonous cloud and, when it turns red, it charges at the player. Due to its nature, it is the only enemy in the game the player can pass through.
