# Electric Bomb

right|300px

The **Electric Bomb** is a Secondary Weapon for Trevor Belmont in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*.

These weapons are "batteries" for the Toy Maker to power his mechanical monsters, and are used by Trevor as a dangerous electricity bomb. When activated, they release multiple bolts of energy that leave enemies trapped into an electric sphere while being electrocuted. Some enemies are unaffected by them.
