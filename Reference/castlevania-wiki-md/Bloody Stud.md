# Bloody Stud

A Bloody Stud is a red stud earring made of Ruby that appears in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.  It gives more Intelligence than any other earring(+2), but gives only +4 Defense, which is one less than a Platinum Stud, which makes them tied for the most effective earring.  It also gives 1 more Defense than a Gold Stud.
