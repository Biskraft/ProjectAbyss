# Antivenom

right|73px

  - Antivenom** (sometimes called **Serum**, **Antidote**, or **Cure Ampoule**) is a one-time use item that heals Poison status. It can be found in most games in one form or another which have a Poison status, and can usually be bought at the shop. It is often dropped by enemies which can poison the player.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, Antidote is also the name of the spell which can be learned by the Herbest Innocent Devil. Basically, it does the same effect of removing poison status at the cost of 5 Hearts.
