# Hellhound (ability)

right|250px

  - Hellhound** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It allows Soma to summon a dog.

## Characteristics
This ability can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Hellhound's soul. When used, a dog will materialize out of thin air and charge forward, dealing Slash damage to anything on its path until it leaves the screen.
