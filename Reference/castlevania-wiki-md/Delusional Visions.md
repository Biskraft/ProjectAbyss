# Delusional Visions

A Succubus has the ability to give its victims Delusinal Visions which usually involves the Succubus appearing as someone important to their victim, either in a dream or overlapped with reality. A Succubus appeared as Alucard's mother Lisa in his dream in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Encore of the Night*, as Leon Belmont's betrothed Sara in *Lament of Innocence*, as Soma's childhood friend Mina in *Aria of Sorrow*, and as his friend Yoko in *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*.

In *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*, this can be used by the Succubus as a spell to prevent matching blocks from breaking them for a time.
