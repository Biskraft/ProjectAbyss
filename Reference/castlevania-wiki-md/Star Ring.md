# Star Ring

The **Star Ring** is the ring of the Tarot persona, "The Star", in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It increases the rate of MP recovery. This is the counterpart to the World Ring, which decreases its consumption. It replaces the Sage Ring from *Portrait of Ruin*.
