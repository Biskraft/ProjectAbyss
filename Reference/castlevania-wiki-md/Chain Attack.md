# Chain Attack

right|300px

  - Chain Attack** is one of Hector's skills in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. As he executes certain combos, the word *"Chain"* may appear. If the Attack button (20px/20px) or Final Attack button (20px/20px) is pressed at that moment, then the Innocent Devil will warp in front of him and add a cooperative attack to deal extra damage to enemies. Some chain attacks even have special effects.
