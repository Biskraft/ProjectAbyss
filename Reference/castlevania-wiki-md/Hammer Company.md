  - Hammer Company** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is played when Soma Cruz first interacts with Hammer when he discovers him in the Study. Afterward, when the latter has set up shop in the Castle Corridor, the song plays whenever he's spoken to at any point in the game, including points in time where items are bought and/or sold.
