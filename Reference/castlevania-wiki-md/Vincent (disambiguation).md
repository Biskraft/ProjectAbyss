# Vincent (disambiguation)

There are five different people known as **Vincent**:

## Characters
    - 1999**  Charlie Vincent, a character from *Castlevania* (N64).
    - 2006**  Vincent Dorin, the priest and merchant from *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
    - 2010**  Abbot Vincent Dorin, the mad monk from *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.

## Staffs
    - Voice actor**  David Vincent, English voice actor for Eric Lecarde and Richter Belmont.
    - Composer**  Vincent Diamante, composer for *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*.
