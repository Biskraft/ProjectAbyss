The **Wicked Ditch** (Japanese: 邪悪の溝 *Jyaaku no mizo*) is the area surrounding Bodley Mansion, which consists of a desolate land and fierce creatures. Heading left from the mansion takes you to a seemingly unpassable river, which can be crossed by kneeling and using the Red Crystal, which reveals the Uta Lower Road. Heading right from the mansion takes you to the town of Fetra (also called Doina).
