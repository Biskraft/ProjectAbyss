# Thunderbolt

right|250px

The **Thunderbolt**, also known as **Lightning Strike**, is a Thunder-based spell appearing in two *Castlevania* titles to date. As its name suggests, it is a spell that allows the caster to call forth a lightning bolt from the sky to strike nearby enemies.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*
The **Lightning Strike** is an ability of the Wood Rod Mage-Type Innocent Devil. It can be learned by killing a certain number of enemies while summoned and costs 8 Hearts to cast.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The **Thunderbolt** is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills. It allows her to call forth a lightning strike from the sky.
