# California Roll

thumb|150px|<p style="text-align: center;">*Harmony of Despair* gamerpic.</p>
  - California Roll** is a sushi roll garnished with tobiko. In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, it is a common food item that only recovers HP that gives the least amount of HP (+20). This is less than the two star Quiche.
