# Relic (Lords of Shadow)

- Relics** are items in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2* that assist Dracula and Alucard. They have a wide variety of uses, ranging from increased weapon mastery to discovering secrets on the map.

## Overview
Items from the most exotic corners of the world were stolen from Dracula's Castle after its owner disappeared. Over time, with the arrival of the modern era, the potions, seals, talismans, and other relics came to decorate the windows of antique shops or ended up in the city's rubbish. Converted into mundane objects, camouflaged under the innocent gaze of the humans who never knew their true power.

## Trivia
- Many of Dracula's relics channel the power of classic *Castlevania* enemies. This could be a reference to Soma Cruz's Power of Dominance, where he uses the souls of the castle's monsters as special abilities.
- The relics could be a reference to Dracula's Relics from the classic games, which take the form of his body parts.
