This stage represents the castle entrance in *Vampire Killer*. It wraps around and is the level prior to the traditional Mermen-infested sewer area. Unlike its *Castlevania* counterpart, this area contains a second floor.

## Segments
### Block 1
- Items in Candles: Ring, Small Heart, Big Heart, Gold Cross, Black Bible
- Items in the Open: Key, Treasure Chest
- Items in Breakable Blocks: White Key, Old Crone
- Items in Treasure Chests: Boots, Wings, Big Heart, Red Shield
- Items Sold: Dagger (normally 50 hearts, 30 with White Book, 90 with Black Book)

### Block 2
- Items in Treasure Chests: Scroll, Boots, Hour Glass, White Bible
- Items in Candles: Small Heart, Big Heart, Gold Cross, Blue Crystal, Ring, Red Crystal, Black Bible
- Items in the Open: Key, Treasure Chest
- Items in Breakable Blocks: White Key, Old Crone, Axe, Big Heart, Key
- Items Sold: Potion (40 Hearts), Yellow Shield (20 Hearts), Holy Water (30 Hearts)

### Block 3
- Items in Treasure Chests: Yellow Shield, Ring, Hour Glass, Gold Cross, Red Shield
- Items in Candles: Small Heart, Big Heart, 1000 Gold, Blue Crystal, Ring, Black Bible
- Items in the Open: Key, Treasure Chest
- Items in Breakable Blocks: White Key, Merchant
- Items Sold: Blue Cross (one hidden Old Crone only gives hearts, the other only removes hearts)
