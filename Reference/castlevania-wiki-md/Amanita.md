# Amanita

- Amanita** (sometimes referred to simply as a **Toadstool**) is a poisonous food item that appears in *Castlevania*. It is a poisonous red toadstool that normally reduces health if accidentally consumed. The dangerous fungi appears in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, *Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia*.

In *Symphony of the Night*, it does not reduce health, but causes Poison status. It can give Alucard +1 HP without poisoning him if he eats it while wearing a Topaz Circlet.
