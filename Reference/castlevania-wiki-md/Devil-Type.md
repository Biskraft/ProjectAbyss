# Devil-Type

The **Devil-Type** is an Innocent Devil type in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is considered to be a balance between the Battle-Type and the Bird-Type, as it can fly and perform Chain-Attacks with Hector. It can use the Magic Circle ability to allow Hector to slide under low openings, accessing various secret areas in the game. As it advances, it can cause spikes, blades and powerful explosions to issue from the Magic Circle.

Unlike other Innocent Devils, a Devil-Type evolves in a linear pattern. It requires a "special spear" (Chauve-souris) in order to get its final form, named The End.

Isaac's equivalent to "The End" is Abel, which has appeared in official artworks, cutscenes, battles and the comic.
