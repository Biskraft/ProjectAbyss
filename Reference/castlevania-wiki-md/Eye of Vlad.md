# Eye of Vlad

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">**The Eyeball** from Japanese *Simon's Quest* instruction booklet.</p>

  - Eye of Vlad** is a part of Dracula's Relics.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*
Dracula's Eyeball can be found in Brahm's Mansion and allows Simon to see the locations of hints hidden in breakable walls. It is needed in order to fight Dracula at the end of the game.

It is similar in function to the Secret Candle found in the game *Vampire Killer*.

### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The Eye of Vlad is one of the items needed to enter Shaft's chambers. It increases LCK by 10.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*
The Eye of Vlad is one of the six items needed to enter the ceremonial chamber. It has the ability to negate curses, which was the function of the Heart of Vlad in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. Found in a weird shaped room in the Chapel of Dissonance in Castle A. A skillful player can acquire this relic early on by divekicking on one of the Flying Bones that are present in the room. Otherwise, the Griffin's Wing is required to get it.
