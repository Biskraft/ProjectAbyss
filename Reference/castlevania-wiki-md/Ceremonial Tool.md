# Ceremonial Tool

The **Ceremonial Tool** is a material in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is an amber artifact used in ancient rituals. An ancient weapon is said to be made from it.

## Availability
- Stolen from Blaze Master:
- :Perfect Guard a Blaze Master's backflip attack to make it open its stealing window. Staying close to a wall will prevent this enemy from bouncing too far away.
- Stolen from Vassago:
- :Throw the enemy in the air with an attack, jump and steal.
