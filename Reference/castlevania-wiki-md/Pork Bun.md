# Pork Bun

A **Pork Bun** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it gives you 17 HP, which is the highest amount of HP for any type of Chinese Bun (5 more than a Red Bean Bun). It is dropped by a Blue Raven, in a similar manner in which the Red Bean Bun is dropped by the Black Crow.
