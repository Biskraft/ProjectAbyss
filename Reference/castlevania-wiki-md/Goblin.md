The **Goblin** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. These diminutive, mischievous and crabby creatures usually avoid contact with humans.

## Description
Diminutive, mischievous and crabby creatures that usually avoid contact with humans. They make their homes around tree roots and mossy damp areas and hunt in packs. One of their main weapons are bombs, which go off after a set amount of time after lit and thrown. These bombs can be taken from them and used to destroy obstacles.

It seems that two sub-species of goblins exist: green-skinned goblins who populate forests and other temperate areas, and pale-skinned goblins who dwell in cold and snowy climates.
