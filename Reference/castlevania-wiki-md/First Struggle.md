# First Struggle

- First Struggle** is a song originally composed for *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. It plays during the boss fights against Actrise, Rosa and Malus (the latter in his adult form), as well as against J.A. Oldrey, Ortega and the Harpy.
