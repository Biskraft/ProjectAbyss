# Necklace of J

The **Necklace of J** (simply called **Necklace** in the Japanese version) is a necklace in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is the least effective of Alucard's starting items, only giving DEF +5, which is less effective than the Nauglamir, which increases it by 15.

## Trivia
- In the English localization of *Symphony of the Night*, the item was renamed "Necklace of J" by the translator Jeremy Blaustein, as a reference to his son, Jin.
