The **Aljiba Woods** are the easternmost wooded area in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. They are located beyond Dabi's Path. Halfway through, a fork is encountered. Taking the lower path exits the woods and enters the underground area of the Lower Road that leads to the Yuba Lake and Rover Mansion. Taking the upper path continues along the woods and ends in the town of Aljiba.
