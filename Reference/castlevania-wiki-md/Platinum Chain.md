# Platinum Chain

The **Platinum Chain** is the strongest of Charlotte's necklaces in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* that increases DEF (+3) and CON (+2). It is more effective than the Crystal Necklet (+2 DEF, +2 CON).
