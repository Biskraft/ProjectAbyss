# Magic Vacuum

right|102px

The **Magic Vacuum** is a vacuum cleaner that the demonic maid Persephone sometimes uses to clean the castle's floors. This device is constructed with what appears to be the neck and skull of a dragon-like creature protruding from the mouth of a big humanoid skull. It can be used by Soma Cruz when he steals Persephone's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*. When activated, it appears and "sucks" the life out of any enemy it encounters, transferring it to Soma.
