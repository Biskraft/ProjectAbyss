# Danasty (Lords of Shadow)

right|300px
  - Danasty** was the name of one of the world's great families. One such Danasty was a great pirate king and member of the Brotherhood of Light. He swore vengeance on the Dragon, Dracula, for the death of his family. Unfortunately he was killed in the Clock Tower.


## Trivia
- Obviously based on the character of Grant Danasty from *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*. One of the helpers of Trevor Belmont. In Mirror of Fate, Sypha and Alucard show up in the game. In Castlevania III, Grant Danasty was fought in the Clock Tower, and joined Trevor on his battles.
- A sprite of Grant is used as an achievement in the game (beating Boss Rush Mode in Hardcore difficulty).
