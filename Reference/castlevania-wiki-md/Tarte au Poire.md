# Tarte au Poire

- Tarte au Poire** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It is a pear Tart that is considered a "cake" in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It can be obtained by dropping a $500 gold piece into the register at the City of Haze bakery, which is harder to get than a Wheat Roll ($100), but easier to get than a Gateau Fraise ($1,000).

Wearing one or two Gold Rings while attempting to drop the coin into the register will increase the odds of spawning a $500 money bag.
