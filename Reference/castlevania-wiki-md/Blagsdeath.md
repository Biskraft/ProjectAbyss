# Blagsdeath

right|200px

  - Blagsdeath** is a Bird-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a Level 4 Innocent Devil and can learn Beat Progress. It can be obtained from a Khaos Devil by feeding it 90 red, green or yellow Evo Crystals.
