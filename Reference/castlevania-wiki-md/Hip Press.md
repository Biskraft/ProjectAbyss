# Hip Press

right

  - Hip Press** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by the Golem Battle-Type evolution. When activated, the Golem will jump, using its huge body to press enemies into the ground when landing. The impact's shockwave damages surrounding foes and is able to destroy certain cracked floors. Costs 12 Hearts to cast.
