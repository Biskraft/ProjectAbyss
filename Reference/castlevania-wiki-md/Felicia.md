# Felicia

- Felicia** is a supporting character in *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula: Lords of Shadow*.

## Background
Felicia accompanies Gabriel Belmont on his quest to defeat the Lords of Shadow. She is an archer for the Brotherhood of Light who is adept at ranged combat and is capable of performing somersaults to dodge attacks. Felicia is also unfazed by the perils that await her and her widowed ally, indicating that she is a brave, resilient and adventurous individual.

## Trivia
- Her role as Gabriel's ally is an allusion to Angela's position as Trevor Belmont's own in both *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula* and *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula II*. Both characters are notably acrobatic and have been damsels in distress on at least one occasion.
- Besides Carmilla, Felicia is the only known female member of the Brotherhood of Light who has made an onscreen appearance in any media tied to *Lords of Shadow*.
- Felicia is a feminine name that means "happy" in Latin.
