# Bat Swarm

- Bat Swarm** is a sub-weapon in *Castlevania Judgment* which fires a swarm of bats toward the enemy.

Multiple characters can use bats in *Judgment*, like Dracula, Carmilla, or Death. When used, bats rush toward the enemy and deal damage for a few seconds. If charged, the bats explode when in contact to the enemy, dealing more damage and making the enemy fall to the ground.
