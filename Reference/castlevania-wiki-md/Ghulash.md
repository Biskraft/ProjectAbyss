The **Town of Ghulash**, also known as , is the last town encountered before entering Dracula's Castle. It is the only town on the large central river island and is entered from the Dora Woods from the north. It is mostly depopulated except for a lone old woman who urges Simon to live with her. At night, the town is exceptional for a large flock of Ravens that have to be dealt with. It is only the second town to feature this type of enemy and the only town devoid of Zombies. Beyond it is the last area that has any enemies before reaching the Castle, Vlad Graveyard.
