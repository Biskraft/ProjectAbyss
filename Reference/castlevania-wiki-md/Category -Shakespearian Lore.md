# Category:Shakespearian Lore

- William Shakespeare** (April 26, 1564 – April 23, 1616) was an English poet and playwright widely regarded as the greatest writer of the English language, as well as one of the greatest in Western literature and the world's preeminent dramatist.

Several elements from Shakespeare's plays were introduced in the English translations only of *Castlevania* games. These include the Firebrand (which is described as the Fire Sword of Oberon and the Fire Sword of Agni in Japan) and Yorick (as in "Alas, poor Yorick", known as "Soccer Boy" in Japan).
