The **Werebat** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They are female, half-human bats.

## Description
Werebats are encountered in the Misty Forest Road. They attack by sending forth little bats and slide backward if the player attempts to attack them from too close. If they spot the player, they will sometimes exclaim: "Let's play, cute little kitty!"

They are the only enemies in the game that drop the Arma Chiroptera glyph. This allows Shanoa to transform into a werebat herself. It has a 2 star drop rating, so she won't drop it too often.
