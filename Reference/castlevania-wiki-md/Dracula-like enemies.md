# Dracula-like enemies

Dracula fights with signature attacks, including the original Hellfire, teleportation, a second "true" form found in his first appearance, along with several newer attacks, such as summons and flame/energy pillars. Several other enemies related to Dracula share some of these abilities and have similar fighting styles, such as Walter Bernhard (who Dracula may have inherited many of his abilities from), Alucard (Dracula's son and whose power is said to be "the same as Dracula's"), Gilles de Rais (who pretended to be his master Dracula), the Dracula Wraith (a being born of Dracula's remains), Graham Jones (who believed he had inherited Dracula's powers), and Dario Bossi (who inherited Dracula's flame abilities).

A vampire resembling Dracula appears as a normal enemy in *Wai Wai World 2: SOS!! Parsley Castle*. It attacks with folding fans and moves similar to a Medusa Head.
