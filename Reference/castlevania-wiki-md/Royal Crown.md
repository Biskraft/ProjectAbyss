# Royal Crown

right|50px

The **Royal Crown** is a crown in the *Castlevania* series. It is a crown encrusted with precious jewels from all over the world. It boosts many stats by a small but respectable value.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The **Royal Crown** boosts DEF, INT, MIND and LCK, all by +2.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
Obtained from Laura, who makes it as part of her "The Job of a Lifetime" quest once the player gives her an Alexandrite. It boosts Shanoa's CON, INT, MND and LCK, all by +5.

### *Castlevania Judgment*
The **Royal Crown** is a head accessory that can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character.

## Trivia
- The description of the Royal Crown in *Castlevania Judgment* ends with the phrase: "Hail to the king, baby!" This is a reference to Duke Nukem, the main protagonist of the eponymous franchise who can frequently be heard saying this as one of his catchphrases.
- Another type of royal crown is the Egyptian Uraeus.
