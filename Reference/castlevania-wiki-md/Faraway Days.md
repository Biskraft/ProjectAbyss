  - Faraway Days** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It plays during some cutscenes, such as the events following Brauner's defeat in the Lost Gallery.

It is notably a slow and sorrowful reprise of part of a stage theme heard in Dracula's Castle.
