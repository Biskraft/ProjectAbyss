# Pitch Black Intrusion

- Pitch Black Intrusion** (known in Japan as **Pitch Black Attack**) is a music theme composed by Masahiko Kimura for *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the background theme for The Lost Village and an enhanced arrangement is prominently featured in Cross of the Blue Moon.

It was later used as Soma's second theme in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
