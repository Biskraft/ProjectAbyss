# The Wolf Revealed

- The Wolf Revealed** is a music theme composed by Yasushi Asada for *Castlevania Judgment*. It is Cornell's theme.

This is a medley of [Demon Salesman](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mX_TWRnujjc), Toothed Wheel and Melodies of Castlevania.
