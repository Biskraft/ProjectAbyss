# E-Amusement Pass

The **e-Amusement Pass** is a Konami card that keeps track of a user's activities on supported Konami arcade games and functions as a sort of virtual memory card. It can be used to save one's progress or high scores in a game or unlock bonus content. Often, having made some progress in one game will unlock a feature in a different game. *Castlevania* related features of the e-Amusement Pass include:
- In *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade*, beating the game with Vampire Hunter or Lady Gunner with a pass allows the player to be able to play as the Little Witch.
- In *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade*, beating the game with the Little Witch allows the player to play a special mode with her that involves the appearance of yellow chickens. Shooting all the chickens leads to a bonus ending.
- Meeting certain conditions in *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* unlocks music in *Akumajō Dracula: The Medal*.
- Meeting certain conditions in *Akumajō Dracula: The Medal* unlocks a special item in *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade*.
- Having a save for *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* unlocks a *Castlevania* song in *GuitarFreaks & DrumMania V6*.
- Having a save for *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* unlocks the Death avatar in *THE ★ BishiBashi*.
