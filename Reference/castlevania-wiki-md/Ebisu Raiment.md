# Ebisu Raiment

The Ebisu Raiment is a Japanese coat that increases your chances of finding rare items. It is an item that only appears in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and is available in the downloadable stage *The Legend of Fuma*, along with the Bishamon Raiment.

## Trivia
- Ebisu is one of the Seven Lucky Gods in Japan. He is the god of fishermen and luck.
