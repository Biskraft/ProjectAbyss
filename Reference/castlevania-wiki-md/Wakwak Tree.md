# Wakwak Tree

The **Wakwak Tree** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is a horrifying tree that can produce pods of men.

## Origins
thumb|left|<p style="text-align: center;">Alexander the Great conversing with the waq-waq tree, 14e century, Iran (*Freer Gallery of Art*).</p>

The [waqwaq tree](http://cryptidz.wikia.com/wiki/Waqwaq) is a giant tree in Indo-Persian lore, whose branches or fruits become heads of men, women or monstrous animals (depending on version) screaming "Waq-Waq", which means *screams* in Persian.

It is a familiar element in all Persian tales and legends, especially in the *Shahnameh* where Alexander the Great would have conversed with a similar tree, the [Indian Tree of the Sun and the Moon](http://cryptidz.wikia.com/wiki/The_Indian_Tree_of_the_Sun_and_the_Moon), where the latter would have announced his death, far from his country.

It is somewhat similar to the Japanese Jinmenju, another human-like tree.

## Description
Unlike the original myth, the Wakwak Tree produces Fleamen instead of human heads in both games where it appears in. However, the enemy itself is not a big threat because it never moves. In addition, it is possible to quickly get rid of the Fleamen it produces before they hatch by cutting its branches quickly enough.
