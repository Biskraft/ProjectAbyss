# Axe Bomber

right|250px**Axe Bomber** is a Dual Crush in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. Both Jonathan and Charlotte will go offscreen, each to one side, and return to cross paths while a powerful stream of energy generates from their hands, unleashing a powerful attack when its contained energy finally disrupts.
