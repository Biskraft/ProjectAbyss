# Wait Cube

right|300px

  - Wait Cube** is a relic item in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is basically a mechanic item for the game's Partner System. It allows the player to command the partner to stand still by inputting ↓ + **A**.

The effect of this relic is used early in the game when reaching a high ledge at the Entrance. One character must stand on top of the pillar while the other must push it near the ledge. The player then needs to switch control to the character standing on the pillar and jump in order to proceed.
