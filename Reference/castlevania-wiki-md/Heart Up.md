# Heart Up

A Heart Up (called simply a **Heart** in localizations) is an inventory item in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* that can be used to replenish a small amount of Hearts.  It does not recover as many hearts as a Heart High.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it's a Enchanted Souls that can be gained by stealing the soul of Heart Eater. It increases the regenerative value of hearts.
