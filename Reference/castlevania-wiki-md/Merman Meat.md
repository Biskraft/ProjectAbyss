# Merman Meat

- Merman Meat** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It can be obtained as a rare drop from Lorelai, although at 1% drop rate chance it's one of the most difficult items to get in the game. Once obtained, it can then be given to Abram to complete his fourth and final quest, "Unusual Medicine Components", which adds Super Potions to Jacob's shop.
