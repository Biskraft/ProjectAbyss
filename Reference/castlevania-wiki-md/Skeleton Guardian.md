# Skeleton Guardian

The **Skeleton Guardian** is an enemy in the Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is a skeleton equipped with a shield and a spear, assigned to guard important places.

## Description
Like the Final Guard, the skeleton guardian protects himself with his shield if he is attacked and is vulnerable only when he attacks.

However, unlike the final guard, he is able to charge the player and, when protecting himself, he will try to keep the player at bay by pointing his spear forward.

These behaviors make it a stubborn enemy and difficult to defeat, so do expect to get hurt here when you encounter them.
