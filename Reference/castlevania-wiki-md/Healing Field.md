# Healing Field

right


  - Healing Field** is a Fairy-Type Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by a Honey Bee and costs 10 Hearts per use. When cast, the I.D. will spawn a small circle in front of Hector. Stepping into it will rapidly restore a considerable amount of HP. A full heal from this field will restore around 150 HP, so the player should waste no time in entering it and remain still until the field vanishes.

The player is advised to use Healing Field as a passive health-restoration resource and not while in combat. However, a skilled player should be able to use it effectively even if under attack, although it's always better to use it between fights.

Due to its very low cost and the large amount of health it provides, Healing Field can be considered to be one of the most useful health-restoring sources in the game.

## Notes
- The speed of HP regeneration and the audio chime when stepping into the Healing Field, is very similar to entering a Save Room.
- Healing fields also appear every time a boss is defeated, they restore Hector's and Trevor's HP and Hearts to maximum.
