# Tempest

right|224px

  - Tempest** is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. She will summon a huge tornado which will inflict Wind-based elemental damage to all enemies who come in contact with it. However, its real usefulness is that it has the ability to suck many types of projectiles into it.

This spell comes especially handy when fighting the Creature, as the tornado can suck up all machine-gun bullets and missiles the Creature could shoot, rendering them useless. Furthermore, this spell may be considered invaluable when fighting the two Creatures who appear in one of the rooms of the Nest of Evil.
