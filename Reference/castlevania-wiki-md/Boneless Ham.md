# Boneless Ham

- Boneless Ham** is a food item that recovers HP.  In Castlevania: Harmony of Despair, it is a three star food item that recovers 129 HP and adds +5 CON.  This is like a souped up version of the two star Bagel Sandwich (+80 HP, +5 CON).  It gives the most CON of the three items that have the same amount of HP, which are Roast Beef (+3 STR +3 CON) and Tasty Meat (+5 STR).

Ham has appeared in Castlevania: Symphony of the Night before as part of the Ham and Eggs dish.
