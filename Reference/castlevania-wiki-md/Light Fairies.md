# Light Fairies

- Light Fairies** are fairies that are released while performing Light Magic. Instead of simply distracting their targets, they will home in on them and self-destruct, causing physical damage.

## Item Data


## Damage
Many enemies which can tolerate regular fairies can be damaged by an exploding fairy. All enemies which are moderately tolerant to fairies are susceptible to damage from an exploding fairy. Living humanoid creatures tend to be weak to both kinds of fairies. Undead creatures tend to be very susceptible to the light energy released by a Light Fairy, which are otherwise immune to fairies. Beasts tend to be strong against an exploding Light Fairy, but distracted by a normal fairy.
