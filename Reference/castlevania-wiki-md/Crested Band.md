# Crested Band

The **Crested Band** is a bracelet found in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It gives +15 DEF, which is the second highest for a bracelet in that game, being less than a Silver Bangle (+20). It is more effective than a Bangle (+3 DEF) or the Kaiser Knuckle (+3 DEF +3 STR).


Category: Bracelets
