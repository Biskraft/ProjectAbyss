# Rotten Meat

The  is a food item that decreases health in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Dawn of Sorrow*. Normally, it deducts HP instead of healing Soma, but if he has the Flesh Golem's (*Aria of Sorrow*) or the Ghoul's souls (*Dawn of Sorrow*) equipped, it then becomes one of the most powerful healing items in the game.

It is the least harmful bad food in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, reducing HP by only 29, which is less than the Rusty Food Tin (HP -100).
