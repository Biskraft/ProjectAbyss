# Gold Knife

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">**Gold Knife** artwork from the Japanese ''[[Castlevania II: Simon's Quest|Simon's Quest instruction booklet.</p>]]

The **Gold Knife** is a sub-weapon in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. This is the strongest knife, and it has a mysterious power.

## Availability
Obtained upon defeating Death in Brahm's Mansion.

## Description
The Gold Knife is a more powerful version of the Silver Knife. It has the same attack power as the Flame Whip, consumes 2 Hearts per use, and bursts into a flame on contact, which remains in place for a brief moment, dealing constant damage until it consumes.
