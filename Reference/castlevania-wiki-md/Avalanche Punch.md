# Avalanche Punch

- Avalanche Punch** is an advanced skill for the Dark Gauntlet in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.

While airborne, Gabriel launches a wave of power downward, sending smaller enemies up in the air where he can continue the combo with further attacks. Press and hold the [Block] and the [Area Attack] buttons to charge the gauntlet, and then release to set off the attack.
