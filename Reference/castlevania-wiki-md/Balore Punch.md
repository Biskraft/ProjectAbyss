# Balore Punch

right|190px

  - Balore Punch** is a bullet soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It allows Soma to build up a big amount of energy for a moment and then letting it go, throwing a huge devastating punch. This ability is obtained from Balore once he has been defeated in The Arena.

This is the second strongest non elemental bullet soul in the game under the Red Minotaur soul.
