# Concentration

- Concentration** is an Enchanted Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over Stolas' soul. Much like Defender, it raises Soma's INT by 4, but lowers his STR by 2 per soul obtained, up to a STR -18 / INT +36 to his stats when 9 souls are collected.
