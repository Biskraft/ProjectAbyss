# Kingdom Dragonion

is an Android and iOS real-time strategy game developed by Konami. It features several of the company's mascot characters, including: Simon Belmont, Alucard and Dracula from the *Castlevania* series, Goemon from the *Ganbare Goemon* series, TwinBee from the *TwinBee* series, and Bomberman from the *Bomberman* series (Hudson Soft).

The game was released on January 28th, 2015, and ended service on August 31st, 2015.

## Overview
The game is set on an isometric-view layout where players can control a variety of monsters in order to defend their kingdom, attack other countries, collect resources, or recruit other monsters; all with the ultimate goal of becoming the strongest kingdom of the realm.

The key to the development of the kingdom lies not only in the storage of resources or the construction of facilities; players also need to find and collect monsters in order to defend and attack, and be able to seize enough resources to ensure the prosperity of their kingdom.
