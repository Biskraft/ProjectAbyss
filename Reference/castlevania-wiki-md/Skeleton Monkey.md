# Skeleton Monkey

The **Skeleton Monkey** (called **Headhunter Bat** in Japan) is an enemy in *Castlevania: Bloodlines*.

## Description
The Skeleton Monkey, as its name implies, is a monkey-like skeletal monster initially found hanging upside-down from trees in wait for prey. When approached or attacked, it will swing down and swipe with its sickle-like appendages. Its extensible tail allows it to reach considerable distances, slowly returning to its initial position in a spiraling manner after each attack.

It boasts little health, however, and a few hits are usually enough to kill it.

## Trivia
- The Headhunter Bat was designed by a reader named K. Kinjo in a contest held by MegaDriveFAN for their then upcoming game *Vampire Killer*. It won the silver medal for enemy designs and was included in the game, along with the Silhouette Demon and Flying Beetle Roller.
