# Summon Spirit

right|209px
  - Summon Spirit** summons a spirit which homes-in on enemies. A very maneuverable spirit can be summoned by Alucard in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*; he can also summon four spirits at once with the Tetra Spirit spell. Soma Cruz can summon two spirits with the Ghost's soul, although these tend to pick a direction and then just head there.
