# Krazy Kart Racing

- Krazy Kart Racing*** is a Konami mascot kart racer video game developed by Polarbit AB and published by Konami for the iOS and Android mobile systems. It is a sequel to *Konami Krazy Racers* and forms part of Konami's *Wai Wai* (*"Krazy"*) series. It features a total of 12 characters from Konami franchises, including new ones not present in the previous game.

## Characters
    - Frogger**: From the *Frogger* series.
    - Dracula**: The most significant antagonist of the *Castlevania* series.
    - Sparkster**: From *Rocket Knight Adventures*.
    - Pyramid Head**: From the *Silent Hill* series.
    - Goemon**: The player character from the *Ganbare Goemon* series.
    - Evil Rose**: A player character from the *Rumble Roses* series.
    - Pentarō**: The heroic penguin from *Dream Penguin Story* and the *Parodius* series.
    - Robbie Rabbit**: From the *Silent Hill* series.
    - Power Pro-kun**: A character from the *MLB Power Pros* series.
    - Tako**: An octopus player character from the *Parodius* series.
    - Ming Ming**: A player character from *New International Track & Field*.
    - Dewy**: A player character from the *Dewy's Adventure*.
