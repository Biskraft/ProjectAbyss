# Son of Christopher

The **son of Christopher** is a character in the graphic novel *Castlevania: The Belmont Legacy*, published by IDW. He is the yet unborn son of Christopher and Illyana Belmont.

## Background
Christopher's son appears at the beginning of the novel as a young boy who is captured by Dracula and turned into a vampire by him. However, this scene occurs in a dream Christopher has the night of his wedding.

At the end of the novel, and while still in his mother's womb, Dracula's just defeated spirit enters his body, where he awaits his coming of age to take control.

## Trivia
- In the original timeline, Christopher's son is called Soleil Belmont and is fought as a boss in *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge*. In the novel, however, the child is never given a name, and since *The Belmont Legacy* is not considered canon in the series, it would be speculative referring to him as Soleil.
  - Furthermore, Christopher, Dracula, and the rest of the characters in the novel that have counterparts in the established timeline, are considered to take part in an alternate continuity.
  - The novel, however, states that Dracula's spirit enters the child's body at the moment of his defeat, as a matter of tie-in with the events that take place in *Belmont's Revenge* when Christopher's son, Soleil, reaches adulthood and turns against his father.
