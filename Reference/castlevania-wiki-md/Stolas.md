# Stolas

- Stolas** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. He is a wise, old creature said to have considerable intelligence.

## Origins
thumb|left|<p style="text-align: center;">Illustration of Prince Stolas by Louis Le Breton, from the *[[wikipedia:Dictionnaire Infernal|Dictionnaire Infernal*.</p>]]

Stolas (also known as Stolos or Solas) is a Great Prince of Hell. He commands twenty-six legions of demons and teaches the art of Astronomy, the knowledge of poisonous plants and the virtues of herbs and precious stones.

He is usually depicted as either a crowned owl with long legs, a raven or a man.

## Appearances
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
In *Aria of Sorrow*, Stolas has red feet and a yellow beak; the rest of his feathered body is white. He wears a golden crown atop his head. He continually walks forward as he summons creatures and will kick the player if near enough.

There are two types of these monsters, one which summons Dead Crusaders and Skeleton Knights, and another which summons Red Minotaurs.

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
In *Dawn of Sorrow*, Stolas's feathers are brown and he wears a crown of white and brown plumes. He primarily summons Erinyes and occasionally a Devil or a Flame Demon. If close enough to the player, it may emit a Ripple Laser attack.
