# Cross a Fear

- Cross a Fear**, also known as **Cross Fear**, is a music theme originally composed by Keizo Nakamura for *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*, *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* and *Castlevania: Grimoire of Souls*. A hard rock rearrangement was produced and released for the Japanese heavy metal album Perfect Selection Dracula Battle in 1995.
