# Tonfa

right|146px

  - Tonfa** are dual clubs with handles on them. They are inaccurately referred to as Nunchaku (Nunchakus) in the English version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. Alucard wields one on each arm and they make up a 2 Handed Weapon. He strikes across with one of them automatically followed by the second one striking low if he is not in the air.
