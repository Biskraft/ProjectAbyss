# Poison Mind

- Poison Mind** is a music theme originally composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania*. It was used as the only boss theme of this game.

Arrangements of this theme have appeared in more recent titles, usually when fighting minibosses or as background music for the game's castle keep.

## Trivia
- This track is similar to "Op. 2, No. 9 SONATE A VIOLINO E BASSO" by Antonio Vivaldi (second movement) ([YouTube](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wIzgn5uZSlQ)).
- The track "Evil's Symphonic Poem" from *Castlevania Judgment* features a small segment of "Poison Mind".
