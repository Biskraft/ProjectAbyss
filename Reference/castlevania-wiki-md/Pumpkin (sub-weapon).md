# Pumpkin (sub-weapon)

The **Pumpkin** is a sub-weapon used by the character of the same name. Pumpkin cannot lose it, for it is the only sub-weapon he can use. The pumpkin will bounce toward enemies and explode, but it can, like Leon's sub-weapons, be upgraded using the orbs.

    - Red Orb** (Large Pumpkin): A larger, more powerful version of the normal pumpkin. Covers more ground. Consumes 3 Hearts.
    - Purple Orb** (Pumpkin Missile): Seven pumpkins will form a semi-circle, and will one-by-one dart at enemies clockwise. Consumes 8 Hearts.
    - Blue Orb** (Shock Pumpkin): A large pumpkin will fall from the ceiling and crush enemies. Consumes 8 Hearts.
    - Green Orb** (Rampage Pumpkin): Pumpkin will transform into a flat disc pumpkin and slice through enemies. Consumes 5 Hearts.
    - Yellow Orb** (Grand Pumpkin): Several large pumpkins emerge and will circle a flying Pumpkin (much like the Grand Cross). Consumes 12 Hearts.
    - White Orb** (Spread Pumpkin): Pumpkin will shoot several pumpkins out of an arm-cannon. Consumes 6 Hearts.
    - Black Orb** (Pumpkin Serpent): Pumpkin will summon a snake-pumpkin creature to home in onto enemies. Consumes 12 Hearts.
