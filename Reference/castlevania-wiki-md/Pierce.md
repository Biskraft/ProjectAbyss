# Pierce

- Pierce** is a damage type found in the *Castlevania* series. It involves taking a bladed item and thrusting it forward in a kind of jab instead of swinging with it, in an attempt to *pierce* through the defenses of an enemy.

Spears are the most common weapon associated with this type of damage; however, these are sometimes associated with Slash instead whenever the **Pierce** damage type is not made available in a game.

- Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* are the only games that distinguish between Piercing and Slashing attacks. Any weapon that thrusts its sharp pointed tip into an enemy, deals piercing damage. This includes knives, rapiers and spears. Guns can deal piercing damage too, since shooting is considered piercing.
