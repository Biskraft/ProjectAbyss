# Owl Familiar

right|132px
The **Owl Familiar** is an Owl that follows the player around and helps them.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* (Saturn)
The first owl familiar appeared in the Sega Saturn version of *Symphony of the Night*. It can be used by Maria after casting the Summon Suzaku spell, which behaves much like Alucard's bat familiar.

### *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*
An owl familiar can also be used by Nathan Graves in *Circle of the Moon* by combining the Saturn and Mandragora cards. This owl shoots pellets.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
Shanoa can also summon an owl familiar with the **Fidelis Noctua** glyph. As the level of this glyph is raised, more owls will be summoned.

Obtaining this glyph is the requirement for completing Serge's "Show Me the Owl!" quest. Owls can be found prominently perched on tree branches in Tristis Pass. When the glyph has been obtained, Shanoa must return to Wygol Village and activate it to call an owl familiar. Talking to Serge at this moment will complete the quest.

- Familiars in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* reach **Level 2** at 32,767 XP and max out at 65,535 XP, which is **Level 3** (the message "LEVEL UP" will appear onscreen when a new level has been reached). Unfortunately, there's no in-game way of checking how much experience a familiar has gained. They gain 3 XP for hitting an enemy and they gain 2,000 XP when Shanoa absorbs another copy of their own glyph. So, according to this math, it takes 17 copies (rounded up from 16.38 and not taking into account enemies hit/killed by the familiar itself) of the glyph to grow one level. If the familiar is onscreen at the moment it gains a level, it will not immediately display its new abilities; it must first be sent away and summoned again.

## Trivia
- The Owl Knight enemy in *Symphony of the Night* and *Order of Ecclesia*, has an owl pet which plays a similar function as a protagonist's owl familiar.
  - Fidelis Noctua* roughly translates to "faithful little owl"; *fidelis* being "faithful" in Latin and *noctua* referencing *athene noctua* or "the little owl".
- Agreus in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2* also has an owl familiar.
