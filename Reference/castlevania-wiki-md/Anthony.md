# Anthony

is a non-playable character in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. He is one of the children who were captured and brought to Dracula's Castle. It is Henry's mission to find this child and five other children.

Anthony is hidden somewhere in the Forest of Silence, down some platforms near the switches to raise the hidden footholds. After descending, shoot the wall and follow the path.

Finding him unlocks Hard Mode.
