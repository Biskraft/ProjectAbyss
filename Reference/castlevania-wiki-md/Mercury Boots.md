# Mercury Boots

- Mercury Boots** are leg gear that allow their wearer to move faster (+20%). They were first introduced into the series in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, and they also made an apparition in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.

They are the slowest speed-increasing boots in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. They are slower than the Winged Boots or the Sonic Boots.
