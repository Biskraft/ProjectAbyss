## *Super Castlevania IV*
When you enter the room filled with a bunch of gold, many gold coins will gather up and will compost to make an image of the Giant Bat that serves as the boss of world 9 act 2, he attacks very similar to his NES counterpart, he will fly around and will swoop down at Simon to damage him, if Simon damages the Giant Bat it will drop 4 large golden pieces to damage Simon. Once it loses around 23 HP, it will split up into three smaller bats. They will all rush at Simon while dropping little pieces of gold to damage him as well, they can also drop four pieces of gold at him when Simon damages them, just like their larger counterpart.

## *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*
The Giant Bat is the first boss summon by Shaft to fight Richter or Maria, he will fly around while shooting a fireball, he can also turn into a drill and will dive down towed the player.


## *Castlevania: Dracula X*
When you enter the room a group of bats will fly and gather around to form the Giant Bat, he will swoop at you, and when damage he will split apart into smaller bats to damage Richter.


## *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The Giant Bat acts as the boss of the reversed castle, this incarnation is considered one of the easiest giant Bat bosses in the series as he only has two attacks and does very little damage compare to other incarnations of the Giant Bat, he will fly around and will swoop at you and can blow a gust of wind to push Alucard.
