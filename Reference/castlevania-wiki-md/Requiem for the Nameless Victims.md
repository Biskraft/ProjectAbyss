# Requiem for the Nameless Victims

- Requiem for the Nameless Victims** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Bloodlines*. It is the background theme that plays during the ending credits.

It is the 39th track in the fourth disc of Akumajō Dracula Best Music Collections BOX, but it was also arranged by Yamane as track 9 (renamed as "Requiem for the Nameless Souls") on the 18th disc as a celebration of her career. This track can be acquired separately from the box set as part of the [Akumajo Dracula × Michiru Yamane Autobiography Music](https://vgmdb.net/album/54198) disc.

This track has also been arranged by Naoto Shibata for the Perfect Selection Dracula Battle album released in 1995, and by [Soyo Oka](https://vgmdb.net/artist/500) for the Akumajō Dracula Tribute Vol. 2 album released in 2011.
