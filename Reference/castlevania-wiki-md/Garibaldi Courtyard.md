# Garibaldi Courtyard

- Garibaldi Courtyard** (otherwise known as **Garden in Garibaldi**) is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the background theme of the Garibaldi Temple's exteriors.
