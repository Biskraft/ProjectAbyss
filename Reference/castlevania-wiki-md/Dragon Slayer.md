# Dragon Slayer

right|73px
The **Dragon Slayer**, also known as **Dragon Killer**, is a sword forged to slay dragons.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, it can be created by combining the Nodachi, Claymore and Ceramics.

## Trivia
- This sword is possibly a reference to *Berserk*, where the character Guts wields a huge sword called "Dragon Slayer". Its appearance in *Curse of Darkness* also strongly resembles Guts' sword.
- The *Portrait of Ruin* version of the Dragon Slayer uses the same sprites as the *Dawn of Sorrow* version of the Balmung.
