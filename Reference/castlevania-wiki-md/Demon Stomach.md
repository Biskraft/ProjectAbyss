# Demon Stomach

The **Demon Stomach** soul allows to eat food that is normally unpalatable. For instance, when eating Bad Foods, Soma Cruz regains health instead of losing it when the soul is activated. Soma can use this by stealing the souls of Flesh Golem in *Aria of Sorrow* and Ghoul in *Dawn of Sorrow*.
