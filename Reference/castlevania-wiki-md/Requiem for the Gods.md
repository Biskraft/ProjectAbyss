# Requiem for the Gods

- Requiem for the Gods** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is the background theme for the Royal Chapel.

An arranged version, referred to as **Requiem of the Gods**, was later included in Akumajō Dracula Tribute Vol. 2.
