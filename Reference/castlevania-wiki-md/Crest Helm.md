# Crest Helm

The **Crest Helm** is the second toughest helmet in the game *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be created from a Burgonet by adding Ceramics, increasing its Defense from +20 to +22. It will be overtaken when an Angel Halo is added to an Armet to form a Cross Helmet (+24).
