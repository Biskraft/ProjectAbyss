  - Yoko Belnades** is a playable character in the game *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. She is purchased as DLC in the Xbox 360 version, but comes free with the purchase of the full game for PlayStation 3. Yoko plays with a style similar to that of her appearance in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.

## Official background
- A descendant of the famous Belnades clan of sorcerers, Yoko excels at the use of fire, ice, and lightning magic.*

## Attack
Yoko retains the Belnades Staff that she had in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. However, it does not give a life bonus when striking an enemy in this game as it did in that game.


## Spells
Yoko retains her three spells from Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow, Holy Lightning, Holy Flame, and Blue Splash, each of which is unique to her in this game, but which are ultimately derived from Sypha Belnades's spells in Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse. She starts with all three spells available in her inventory.


## Unique Items
Yoko has one item that she can find that is unique to her. This is the Philosopher's Stone. It is a healing item that can be equipped and used like food or a potion, but is not removed from the inventory when used. Instead, it costs 50 MP to use.
