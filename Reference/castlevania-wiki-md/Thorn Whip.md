# Thorn Whip

The **Thorn Whip** is a whip formed from the stem of a rose.

In *Castlevania: Simon's Quest*, it is an upgrade to the Leather Whip. It can be purchased in the town of Jova (the same town where the game begins) for the price of 100 Hearts. Despite being a "thorn" whip, its appearance is similar to the Leather Whip, both in-game and in the manual, although it is longer and deals out twice as much damage.

In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, it can be formed by combining the Mercury card with the Mandragora card.

In *Castlevania Portrait of Ruin*, the **Rose Stem Whip** can be dropped by the Maneating Plant in the Forest of Doom or can be found in the Tower of Death later.
