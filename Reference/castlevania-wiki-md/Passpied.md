  - Passpied** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge*. It is the background theme for the second stage inside of Dracula's Castle.

The music is a direct reprise of "Passepied", the fourth movement of the Suite Bergamasque by composer Claude Debussy.
