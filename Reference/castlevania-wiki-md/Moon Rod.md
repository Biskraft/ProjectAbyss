# Moon Rod

right|162px

The **Moon Rod** is scepter adorned with lunar markings. It is Alucard's most powerful Cudgel in both *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It has a special that summons five large crescent-blades behind him, which then travel forwards across the screen.

## Trivia
- The **Moon Rod** comes from the first season of the anime *Sailor Moon*, where it is used by the eponymous superhero Sailor Moon to execute her special move "Moon Healing Escalation". It is lost after the final battle with the evil Queen Beryl.
