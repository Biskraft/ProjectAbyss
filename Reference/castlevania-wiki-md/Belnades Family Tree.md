# Belnades Family Tree

The **Belnades Family Tree** contains all Belnades relatives in the traditional saga.

This family tree only contains characters from the same bloodline. Other characters should not be added to this tree.

<small>***Legend***: </small>
- <small>Solid lines denote blood parent-child relationship </small>
- <small>Dashed horizontal lines denote engagement relationships </small>
- <small>Dashed vertical lines denote the rest of the family tree, that contains unknown members of the family</small>
- <small>A cross denotes a deceased character, who died before, during, or at the end of the events of the game(s) they appear in</small>
