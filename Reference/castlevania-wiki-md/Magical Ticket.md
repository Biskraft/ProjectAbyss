# Magical Ticket

right|70px

A **Magical Ticket** (called **Library Card** in the North American version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*) is an item which can be used to instantly transport a player to wherever the shop in the game is.

## Game Specific Information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
Library Cards can be bought at the Master Librarian's shop or found at certain parts of the map. Using them teleports Alucard at the door in the Long Library leading to his shop, even from the Reverse Castle.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Magical Tickets can be bought at Vincent's shop for $1,000. However, they won't be available for purchase from the beginning of the game, but only after the player has accumulated 20,000 Shop Points (pressing **R** at the main shop menu will display the current amount of Shop Points the player has) which is equivalent to spending at least $200,000 in his shop. Using the Magical Ticket from the "USE ITEM" menu will instantly transport the player, wherever he or she is, to Vincent's shop. There are some exceptions, however, like certain parts of the Nest of Evil where the ticket won't have any effect.

It's worth mentioning that the ticket cannot be used if the game was not saved at least once during a playthrough, which means that if a player makes progress through the game, has accumulated the necessary Shop Points for Magical Tickets to appear at the shop, has bought at least one, but didn't take the time to save the game at a Save Point at least once during the game, the ticket won't be able to be activated, a buzzing sound will play and nothing will happen.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
Magical Tickets can be bought at Jacob's shop or can be found in wooden treasure chests (mostly in Minera Prison Island), but instead of teleporting Shanoa directly to the shop, using this item will teleport her to the center of Wygol Village.
