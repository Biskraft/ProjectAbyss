# José Luis Vaello

- José Luis Vaello Bertol** is a Spanish illustrator who recently worked with MercurySteam on the newer incarnation of the *Castlevania* franchise, starting with *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.

## Works on video games
  - Severance: Blade of Darkness* (Codemasters, 2001)
  - ONE* (Nokia, 2005)
  - Clive Barker's Jericho* (Codemasters, 2007)
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* (Konami, 2010)
  - RiME* (Tequila Works, 2016)
