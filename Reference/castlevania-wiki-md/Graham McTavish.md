# Graham McTavish

- Graham McTavish** (born January 4, 1961) is a Scottish television, film and voice actor. He provides the voice of Dracula in the *Castlevania* animated series.

McTavish is perhaps best known for his roles as Dwalin in the *Hobbit* film series, Dougal Mackenzie in the television series *Outlander*, and the Saint of Killers in the television series *Preacher*.

His voiceover roles include Dante Alighieri in *Dante's Inferno*, Thundercracker in *Transformers: War for Cybertron*, Zoran Lazarević in the *Uncharted* series, and Loki in *The Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes*.
