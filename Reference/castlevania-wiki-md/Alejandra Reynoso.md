# Alejandra Reynoso

- Alejandra Reynoso Agueda** is an American actress and voice actress. She is best known for voicing Flora the Fairy of Nature on Nickelodeon's *Winx Club*.

She voices Sypha Belnades in the *Castlevania* animated series.

## Other works
  - Winx Club: Bloomix* (2013)
  - Winx Club: Power of Believix* (2012)
  - Castlevania* (2017)
