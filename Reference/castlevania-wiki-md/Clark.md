# Clark

is a non-playable character in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. He is one of the children who were captured and brought to Dracula's Castle. It is Henry's mission to find this child and five other children.

Clark is hidden somewhere in the Villa. In the hedge maze, near the area where you needed the Rose Brooch to open, there's an alcove with the child inside.

Finding him unlocks Reinhardt Schneider's alternate costume.
