  - Rinaldo's Cabin** (otherwise known as **Old Man's Cottage**) is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is played when the player is near Rinaldo's cabin.

The track later reappears in *Castlevania: Grimoire of Souls* under the name "**Research Headquarters**".
