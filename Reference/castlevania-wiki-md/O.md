# O

|  |


  - O** (often misinterpreted as "0" or "Zero") and the **Mimic** are enemies in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

## Description
### O
A thief that, when defeated, will flee by jumping away, leaving either a random sub-weapon or a Large Heart. This enemy is the only way to unequip sub-weapons, which can allow Juste to use his healing spell, God Bless, by using the Summoning Tome.

In Maxim Mode, it will rarely drop anything at all, since sub-weapons are not used in that mode. Instead, it may drop a single Heart.

### Mimic
As implied by its name, the **Mimic** (or **Manemane**) will mimic the player's behavior. It appears only in a couple of rooms in the Skeleton Cave (Castle A). There is even a minor puzzle that requires to lead a Mimic to an unreachable switch (both the Mimic and the switch are located in the closed off top section of the room), by having it mirror Juste's movements. The Mimic does not steal any items.

## Trivia
- O (Ou) is probably a parody of O-jirō, a character from the *Little Ghost Q-Taro* series.
- The Mimic's Japanese name is Manemane; *mane* (真似) translates from Japanese as "mimicry".
