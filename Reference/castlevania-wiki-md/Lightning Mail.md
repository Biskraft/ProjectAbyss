# Lightning Mail

The **Lightning Mail** is an armor that offers protection against Thunder-based attacks.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it gives the normal amount of DEF for an elemental armor, +8, which is twice as much as the Fire Mail, the same as the Ice Armor and Dark Armor, and 3 less than the Holy Mail.
