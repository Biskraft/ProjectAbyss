  - Carmilla's Fight** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It is the background theme that plays when Carmilla is fought in her hideout. Near the end of the storyline in the Revelations DLC, the track is featured again when Zobek's Lieutenant is fought by Alucard in the cathedral where his weakened father was laid to rest.
