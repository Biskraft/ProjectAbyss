# Move in a Crouch

A few characters are able to move slowly while crouching. In 2D games, it is performed by pressing down and forward or backward at the same time. Only a few characters have this ability (Simon Belmont in *Super Castlevania IV* and Sonia Belmont in *Castlevania Legends*). Most modern characters slide instead. In the 3D games *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*, it is done when moving while holding down the crouch button.
