# Joseph's Cloak

- Joseph's Cloak** (called a **Made to Order** mantle in Japan) is the second most effective cloak in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. Its colors can be set to anything through a menu option. It gives a Constitution boost of +9 and a Defense boost of +4, which is one more CON and DEF than a Royal Cloak and one less CON than a Twilight Cloak. It's North American localized name is likely a reference to Joseph's Coat Of Many Colors in the Bible.
