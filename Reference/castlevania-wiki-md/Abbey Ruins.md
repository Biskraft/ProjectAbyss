# Abbey Ruins

right|300px

The **Abbey Ruins** is a stage outside of Dracula's Castle in *Castlevania Judgment*. In Story Mode, it is where the playable character first enters the Time Rift and encounters Aeon. Zombies may spawn, even when fighting against one of the main combatants.

The only destructibles on this stage are two barrels; otherwise, this is a straightforward stage with no strategy or danger to it.

## Trivia
- The Abbey Ruins is the place where the Black Mass is held, resulting in the resurrection of Dracula. This stage is a reference to the intro of *Castlevania Chronicles*.
