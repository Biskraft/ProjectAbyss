# Dracula X: Remixies

- Dracula X: Remixies** is a remix soundtrack of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Nightʼ*s music made by different DJs, in a techno/dance genre. It was released on October 3, 1997. The remixers are known as DJ FEILONG, DA 604 CREW, IRON DISCO, SCREMIN DISCO, carina (lyrics/vocals) and Yoshiharu Ohta.

- "Dracula X - GATE TO HEAVEN - DJ FEILONG Xbeat MIX" - Remixed by DJ FEILONG / Manipulated by Yoshiharu Ohta.

- "BIG BURN X-STORM DUB" - Remixed by DA 604 CREW.
- "TRANS FORM" - Remixed by IRON DISCO (S.tomioka/Disco King records).
- "CEMETARY OF RAINBOW" - Special Remixed by SCREMIN DISCO (Kenn.N/Disco King records).
- "VAMPIRE KILLER" - Vocal by carina / Remixed by Yoshiharu Ohta.
