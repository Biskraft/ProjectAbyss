# Ruby

right|73px

The **Ruby** is a jewel in *Castlevania*. It often appears in a ring (the **Ruby Ring**) and is associated with the Fire element, Strength, destruction, and royalty.

## Characteristics
The Ruby is the second most valuable gem in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is worth 600 Gold, which is more than a Sapphire (500 G), but less than a Diamond (1,000 G).

Using the Ruby with the Jewel Crush causes damage to all enemies on the screen.

The Ruby in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* is an item that by itself does nothing. It is needed for Laura (Order of Ecclesia)'s *"A Pleasant Accessory"* quest. If you bring Laura a Ruby, an Emerald, and a Sapphire, she will make available the Ruby Ring (greater Fire offensive power), Ruby Pins (greater defense against Fire), and the rings and pins of the other Jewels brought to her at Jacob's shop.

## Other Ruby items
The Ruby Circlet in *Symphony of the Night* gives the player HP whenever you they hit with a Fire based attack. The Bloody Stud gives more intelligence than any other stud in *Dawn of Sorrow*. The Ruby Corset is the only corset in *Portrait of Ruin* that also gives an Strength boost in addition to a Defense boost.

The Royal-Ruby Bracelet can be worn by characters in *Judgment* and has no in-game effect.
