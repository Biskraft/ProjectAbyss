# Grab

The [Grab] button can be used to **grab** enemies in *Lords of Shadow* games. Players without a chained whip can **grip** nearby enemies with their hands. Dracula and Alucard can feed off of some enemies after grabbing them in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2* and *Revelations*. A weapon with a Combat Chain, such as a Combat Cross or Dark Pain, is able to perform a **Chained Grab**, which can grab enemies from a distance. Grabbing capabilities may increase as the weapon is improved.

In *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*, this mechanic is named *Grab and Interact* wherein the player can press the grab button to have Dracula interact with objects, or finish off enemies that glow a yellowish hue. Finishing enemies with a grab replenishes a portion of Dracula's health.
