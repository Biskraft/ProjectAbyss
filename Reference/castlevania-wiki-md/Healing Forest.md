# Healing Forest

- Healing Forest** (also called **Cleansing Forest** or **Forest Bath**) increases the rate of MP restoration. It can be gained by Soma Cruz with a Wooden Golem soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and from a Treant soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
