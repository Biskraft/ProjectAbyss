# Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night/Arcade Mode

Arcade Mode in Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night allows you to select one of eight playable characters to play as and another as an opponent for a quick match. Each character has two unique spells available to them. It lacks many of the role playing elements found in Story Mode, such as equipable items and built up levels. It contains a hidden character, the Librarian, who can be unlocked by earning the Veteran Explorer Honor.
