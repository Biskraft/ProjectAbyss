# Sharp X68000

right|250px

The **Sharp X68000**, often referred to as the **X68k**, was a home computer released only in Japan by the Sharp Corporation. The first model was released in 1987, with a 10MHz Motorola 68000 CPU, 1MB of RAM and no hard drive; the last model was released in 1993 with a 25MHz Motorola 68030 CPU, 4MB of RAM and optional 80MB SCSI hard drive. RAM in these systems was expandable to 12MB though most games and applications didn't require more than two.

## *Castlevania* games available on the system
  - Akumajō Dracula*

## Trivia
- This system wasn't released outside of Japan, so a remake for *Akumajō Dracula* was made available on the PlayStation in 2001.
