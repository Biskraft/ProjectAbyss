# Needle Mine

right
The **Needle Mine** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It tosses mines into the air that explode at fixed intervals.

## Availability
Dropped by Needles. This enemy can be found in the flooded areas of the Clock Tower and the Underground Reservoir.

## Characteristics
Soma will place a Needles "mine" in the air and it will explode after a brief moment or when an enemy touches it. Five mines can be set on the screen at the same time.
