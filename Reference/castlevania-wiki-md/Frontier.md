# Frontier

- Frontier** is a sports magazine actively circulating in the 1800s which also covers sensationalist news. Marcel, a villager from Wygol, works there as a journalist but has not been heard of after he went into the mountains following a hunch for a scoop. Apparently, the magazine has a good income as it can afford to pay its employees big sums of money.
