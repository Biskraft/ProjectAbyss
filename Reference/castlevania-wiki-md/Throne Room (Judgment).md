The **Throne Room** is a stage in *Castlevania Judgment*.

## Overview
The Throne Room is most likely located in the Castle Keep. The large chamber is lined with suits of armor on the balconies and Dracula's throne on the far end.

When playing in Castle Mode and players get to battle a main character on this stage, sometimes the opponent won't follow the player way to the back where the picture of Dracula hangs. This allows for safe use of long range sub-weapons such as the charged Axe or charged Gale Wing. The only exception is Dracula, who will use his Fire Pillar attack in order to reach your character.
