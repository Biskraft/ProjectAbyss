# Mushroom

A **Mushroom** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It is one of the non-poisonous mushrooms that appears in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, *Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia* (as opposed to an Amanita, which is poisonous and reduces HP if consumed).
