# Skeleton Breeder

The **Skeleton Breeder** is an enemy in the Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is the skeleton of a tracking soldier who walks with a Skeleton Beast.

## Description
A skeleton who breeds Skeleton Beasts and will attack any intruders that try to harm his undead animals. He brandishes a long whip made of bones.
