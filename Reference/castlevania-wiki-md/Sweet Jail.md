# Sweet Jail

right|250px

  - Sweet Jail** is an ability in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over Alura Une's soul. It grants Soma the ability to summon her as a guardian.

## Characteristics
Alura grows out of a flower, cuddles into Soma's arms and calls him informally: "Soma-sama" ("Lord Soma"), while her three flower vines lock-on to the nearest enemies to stab them, causing Slashing or Piercing damage. Alura's vines also can be controlled with the stylus, making her attack in a considerable faster rate. She costs 10 MP per second to summon. Soma cannot move while Alura is summoned and she cannot be summoned while in midair. Alura Une also stabs her enemies out of annoyance and sometimes can be heard saying: "jamaa" ("annoying").

Alura Une levels up one level per soul until Level 9 is reached. She can potentially deal great damage even at Level 1. She is a Plant-type soul and the Skeleton Farmer's soul boosts her piercing power even more. She can be boosted with a level 9 Lilith's soul to cause even more damage than with the Skeleton Farmer's soul. She can also be combined with the Une's soul, which allows her vines to spew one Une per second from their flowers without costing MP (up to three can be onscreen at once).

Alura Une is useful for damaging some still enemies such as Death, Balore, or some parts of Aguni's attacks. If the player works very hard, Alura can cause up to 260+ damage per hit of her vines, and about 600 damage per second. Alura Une is recommended during Boss Rush Mode due to her great damage potential. An interesting technique consists in equipping the Dead Crusader's soul with Alura Une, which if Soma's damage is 1 or less, the damage is nullified and allows him to summon Alura without much interruptions, unless by enemies giving damage more than 1.
