# Fire Sword

right|200px
Any sword with Fire-based attacks. There are two recurring fire swords found in *Castlevania*, Agni's Flame (also called *Firebrand*) and the more powerful Laevatain (also called *Marsil*). Nathan Graves also has use of a fire sword by combining the Mars + Salamander DSS cards. Joachim Armster is able to add the fire element to his symbiont swords in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* after defeating the Flame Elemental. Alucard is able to add the fire element to his own sword in *Castlevania Judgment* by touching torches with it.
