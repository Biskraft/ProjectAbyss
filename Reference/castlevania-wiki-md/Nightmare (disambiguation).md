# Nightmare (disambiguation)

The name **Nightmare** could refer to:

    - Nightmare** - A common demon enemy in the shape of a horse.
    - Nightmare (music)** - Two different music themes under the same name that originally appeared in *Dracula's Curse* and *Lament of Innocence*.
    - Nightmare (spell)** - A gigantic ball of negative energy that is usually thrown by Malachi.
    - Nightmare (Symphony of the Night)** - A small area in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* where Alucard confronts the Succubus.
