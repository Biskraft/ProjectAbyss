# Demon Guest House

The **Demon Guest House** is a location of *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.

## Overview
One of the biggest areas in the castle. It contains many vertical passageways and brightly wallpapered rooms. There are seven guest rooms that have either items, enemies, or both. Also, it rightfully earns the name "Guest House" because, if players are to enter the castle from the Lost Village, they will enter this stage first. This area contains two distinct themes: the western half of the guest house is themed to toys and clowns, while the eastern half is themed to ballroom dancing. The area's bosses, Puppet Master and Paranoia, reflect this.
