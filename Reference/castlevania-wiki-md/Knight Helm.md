# Knight Helm

The **Knight Helm** is a knightly helmet found in *Castlevania*. In the facet of protection, the Knight Helm is the best head gear item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, even against the Queen of Hearts that is received as a reward for completing Hard Mode, or when connecting with *Castlevania Judgment*. It is the only helmet in this game that only boosts statistics and does not give a special effect.
