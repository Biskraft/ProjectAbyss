# Tokuma Shoten

is a Japanese publishing company, headquartered in Shinagawa, Tokyo. It was established in 1954 by Yasuyoshi Tokuma in Minato, Tokyo. The company's product portfolio includes music publishing, video game publishing, movies, anime, magazines, manga and books.

The company has published a few *Castlevania*-related guides, as well as magazines that have covered the franchise's titles.

On 17 March 2017, it was acquired by Culture Convenience Club.
