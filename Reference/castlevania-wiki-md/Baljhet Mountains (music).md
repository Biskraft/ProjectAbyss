# Baljhet Mountains (music)

- Baljhet Mountains** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the background theme of the titular location. The track was later featured in Pachislot Akumajō Dracula as background music that plays during Regular Bonus segments.
