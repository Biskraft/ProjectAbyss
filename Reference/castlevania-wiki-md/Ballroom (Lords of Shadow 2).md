# Ballroom (Lords of Shadow 2)

right|300px

The **Ballroom** is a section of Bernhard's Wing in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It is here that Dracula learns the identity of the mysterious boy guiding him: the visage of his son, Trevor Belmont.

There are a total of four Pain Boxes in this area.
