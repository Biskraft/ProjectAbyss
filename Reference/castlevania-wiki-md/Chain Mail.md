# Chain Mail

right|73px
  - Chain Mail** is a type of body protection found in *Castlevania*.

In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, Chain Mail gives +100 Defense, which is 20 more than Gold Armor and 20 less than Steel Armor.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, Chain Mail gives +28 Defense, which is 2 more than Parade Armor and 2 less than Scale Armor.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, Chain Mail grants +25 Defense, which is one more than an Army Jacket and 3 less than Aquarius.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, Chain Mail is made from 2 Indigo Steels and has a Defense of +20. This surpasses the defense of the Brigandine (+18), but is surpassed when the Brigandine evolves into a Half Plate by adding Carbon Steel.
