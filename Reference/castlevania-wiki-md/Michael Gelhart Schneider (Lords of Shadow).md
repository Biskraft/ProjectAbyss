# Michael Gelhart Schneider (Lords of Shadow)

- Michael Gelhart Schneider** is a character in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. He was a most noble knight and highly skilled archer. He was captured by the Bernhards and imprisoned in the castle. He died there and vowed vengeance on its occupants. His spirit will only come to the aid of a true warrior. That warrior ultimately proves to be Simon Belmont, whose mother, Sypha, convinces the spirit to join her son's cause, thus granting him the power of the *Spirit of Schneider*.

## Spirit of Schneider
The Spirit of Schneider is one of Simon's two Spiritual Powers available to him in the game (the other being the Spirit of Belnades). It offers an offensive cover of arrows. As a type of Magic Power, its usage reduces Simon's magic meter.
