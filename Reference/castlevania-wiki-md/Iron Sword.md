# Iron Sword

The **Iron Sword** is the only unique weapon to *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*.

If worn with the other two iron items, the Iron Shield and the Iron Cuirass, the **Ferroucious** set bonus can be obtained, which gives ATK and DEF +3. It can be found where the Gladius (which looks just like it) was found in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is in the room right after the first Doppelganger fight.
