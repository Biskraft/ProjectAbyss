# Key

- Keys** unlock locked treasure chests and doors in various *Castlevania* games. They were first introduced in *Vampire Killer*, where a yellow key opened chests, while a white key opened locked doors.

They returned in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and in *Castlevania: Dracula X*, where they take the place of a sub-weapon and can even be used to attack enemies. Its Item Crash makes Richter levitate in the air for a brief moment while holding the key in one hand. He is impervious to attack during this animation, and if the key makes contact with an enemy, this will take damage. The key itself does more damage than any other sub-weapon, being four and a half times more powerful than a single whip strike. They unlock doors where maidens have been locked up in.

The **Last Key** Magic Item in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* must be used on the door leading to Dracula. After defeating Hugh, Nathan can find this key in the next room.

The **Tower Key** in  *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* allows to open the gate located a couple of floors below from where the key is found, giving access to the Cursed Clock Tower. It can be found in the Condemned Tower, in a room situated two rooms left from Gergoth's boss room; however, since the floor gets destroyed during the battle, the player is forced to climb the tower a second time and jump across the gaps left in the main tower's shaft in order to reach it.

Many keys can be found in *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*, which are used to take alternate paths through the stages.
