# Lament of Innocence (music)

- Lament of Innocence** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is Leon Belmont's main theme and it is played several times throughout the course of the game.

It features a piano playing the main theme while violins, trumpets and drums play in the background. Within it is a reprise of Dracula's battle theme from *Super Castlevania IV*.
