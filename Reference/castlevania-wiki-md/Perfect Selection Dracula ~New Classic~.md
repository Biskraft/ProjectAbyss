# Perfect Selection Dracula ~New Classic~

- Perfect Selection Dracula ~New Classic~** is a classical arrangement album from different *Castlevania* games. It was released on April 22, 1992, with arrangements by Jun Irie, along with the Konami Symphonic Ensemble and Nazo² Project.

The first track, Vampire Killer ~ Wicked Child, is used in many symphonic game music concerts nowadays.
