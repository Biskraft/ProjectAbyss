  - Cross of the Blue Moon** is a music theme composed by Masahiko Kimura for *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the background theme that plays during the game's opening animation sequence. An enhanced arrangement of Pitch Black Intrusion plays throughout the track.
