# Resist Fire

- Resist Fire** is an Attribute Potion in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* that causes Fire-based attacks to actually heal Alucard.

The item is called **Fire Essence** in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* and raises one's fire affinity, although it doesn't help unlock Fire spells.
