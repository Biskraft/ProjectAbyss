# ATK Up

- ATK Up** is an them that permanently increases ATK. It is found in the Joachim Mode of *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and the Trevor Mode of *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* to compensate for the lack of an equipment inventory in the former and the absence of the level-up system in the latter.
