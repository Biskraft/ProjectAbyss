# Hikaru Midorikawa

is a Japanese voice actor and singer who is currently affiliated with Aoni Production.

Midorikawa is famous for his fans as Tamahome in *Fushigi Yugi*, Kaede Rukawa in the *Slam Dunk* series, and Heero Yuy in *New Mobile Report Gundam Wing*.

He voiced Soma Cruz, the protagonist of *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Dawn of Sorrow*, and voiced him one last time in *Harmony of Despair*.
