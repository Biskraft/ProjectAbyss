# Gun

right|104px
  - Gun** is a projectile-firing weapon class in the *Castlevania* series, which ammunition may consist of normal bullets or magical properties.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*
Henry's main weapon is his gun. It is relatively weak but can fire six rounds in rapid succession, and also has infinite ammo. After those six rounds have been shot, he will automatically reload it. The gun can be leveled up two times upon picking up a power-up.

### *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*
By using the Mars and Black Dog DSS combination, Nathan can attack with a gun at the cost of 32 MP per use.

### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* / *Dawn of Sorrow* / *Harmony of Despair*
Soma Cruz can use three different kinds of guns in *Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*. These are the Handgun, Silver Gun and the Positron Rifle, which is strangely the only piece of futuristic weapon in the series.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
Albus wields Agartha, a gun which fires both, normal and magical bullets. Although he already has a weapon, he can still equip glyphs (specifically Ignis, Torpor, and most importantly, Acerbatus) and use Agartha to fire them.
