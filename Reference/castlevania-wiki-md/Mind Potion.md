# Mind Potion

- Mind Potions** are medicine that recovers MP. In general, the Mind Up recovers a small amount of MP, the High Mind Up a large amount of MP, and the Mind Ex recovers all MP. Sometimes Manna Prism is used as the general name for a Mind Potion, and other times it is used for a Mind Potion that recovers all MP.
