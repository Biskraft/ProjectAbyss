# Ogre Rod

right|200px

  - Ogre Rod** is a Mage-Type Innocent Devil in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a Level 3 Innocent Devil and can learn Agnea and Synchron Saucer B. It can be obtained from a Scissor Rod by consuming enough red or blue Evo Crystals, or from a Talon Rod by consuming red, blue or white Evo Crystals.
