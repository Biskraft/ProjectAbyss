# Mud Ball

right|290px
  - Mud Ball** is an attack used by mud men in the Castlevania series. A ball made of mud will be hurled toward the enemy, inflicting damage upon contact. In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, Soma can gain this ability by absorbing the soul of a Mudman.
