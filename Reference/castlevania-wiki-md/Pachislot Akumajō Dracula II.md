# Pachislot Akumajō Dracula II

is a pachislot casino game and is a sequel of *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula*. It was released to Japanese pachislot halls on July 20th, 2010. It features much the same cast as the original game, which is based on the cast of *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* and *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It was followed by *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula III* in 2012.

## Gameplay
Many of *Pachislot II*'s gameplay mechanics are inherited from the first Pachislot game. The player progresses through a series of quick time events in which the outcomes are always determined by the score achieved on the slot machine. Failing to line up similar items on the slot machine results in Trevor being defeated by the current boss.
The goal of the game is to complete Zead's challenges (fighting a Succubus, a Wyvern, and Dullahan) before battling Death himself, followed by Dracula. In addition to the standard boss battles, the role of Isaac from the first Pachislot game is effectively replaced by Dark Angela.
