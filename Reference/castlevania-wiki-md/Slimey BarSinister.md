# Slimey BarSinister

- Slimey BarSinister** is an enemy in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.

## Origins
thumb|left|<p style="text-align: center;">[[wikipedia:Yochlol|Yochlol from *Dungeons & Dragons*.</p>]]

Yochlols are fictional demonic creatures in the *Dungeons & Dragons* fantasy role-playing games, having been first introduced in the first edition *Advanced Dungeons & Dragons: Queen of the Demonweb Pits* in 1980.

They appear as tentacled blobs of melted wax and continually emit a foul stench. Yochlols also have the power to change their shapes, usually to a comely drow female or to a spider (usually a black widow). They can also change themselves into a gaseous form, allowing their unknowing victims to inhale them, and then suffocating them.

It is possible that they originate as the souls of high priestesses of Lolth who died in the favor of their goddess.

Their main purpose is to establish a mental connection to the high priestesses of Lolth, scouring their thoughts and memories to find proof of their loyalty and dedication.

The English name of this enemy may be a reference to Simon Bar Sinister, the primary villain of the *Underdog* cartoon.

## Description
Slimey BarSinisters are big eyes enwreathed in slime who inhabit poisonous marshlands. When approached, they quickly stand up and shoot a fast-traveling fireball.

They are encountered late in the game, in Joma Marsh, and may pose a threat when trying to get out of a marsh. It is advised to approach them while holding the shield.
