  - You're Not Alone** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the background theme that plays before Soma Cruz enters his final battle with Chaos to escape his destiny as Dracula's inheritor.

## Trivia
- The theme is, in a way, a battle cry because Mina Hakuba, Yoko Belnades, Hammer, Julius Belmont and Genya Arikado all root for Soma to stay true to himself despite his true dark nature.
- "Momentary Moonlight", the end credits theme of *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, contains an arranged version of this track.
