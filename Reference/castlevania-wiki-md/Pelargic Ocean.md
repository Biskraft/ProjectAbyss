# Pelargic Ocean

The **Pelargic Ocean** is an ocean near the Land of the Lycans and the former city of Agharta. Most of the secrets that reside in the crumbling civilization being lost, many of them sunk beneath the sea.


Gabriel explored the palaces that were slowly being eroded by the ocean. The waves could be heard far below, and the rivers of the city poured into sea.
