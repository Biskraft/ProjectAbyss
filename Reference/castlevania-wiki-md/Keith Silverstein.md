# Keith Silverstein

- Keith Silverstein** is an American voice actor. He provided the voice of Albus in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* and Simon Belmont in both *Castlevania Judgment* and *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate*.

Keith is known for the voice of Gantetsu and Kimimaro Kaguya in *Naruto*, as well as Detective Kun-Kun in *Rozen Maiden* and HUNK from *Resident Evil: The Umbrella Chronicles*.
