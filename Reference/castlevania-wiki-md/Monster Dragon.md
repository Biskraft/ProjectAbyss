# Monster Dragon

The **Monster Dragon** is a more powerful version of the Monster Fish. It looks like a Gargoyle.

## Appearances
### *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* (watch)
left|thumb

They take two hits to destroy and are worth 10 points. An airborne variety of this monster exists, called the Flying Monster.

### *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* (handheld)
left|thumb

They inhabit caste areas. They can fire two fireballs in rapid succession. They take 10 whiplashes to kill and are still only worth 20 points. A good target for a silver knife, which can destroy them in one hit.
