# Blessed Ankh

The **Blessed Ankh** (called **Ankh of Life** in the North American localization of *Symphony of the Night*) is a cross necklace that increases the amount of HP recovered by healing items powers. It increased this power by a half in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* (on a Potion or a High Potion) by half. It returned in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. This item was replaced with the Emperor's Ring in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.
