# Gold Stud

The Gold Stud is a stud earring made of Gold that appears in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.  It gives +3 Defense and +1 Intelligence, which is one more defense than a Silver Stud, but 2 less than a Platinum Stud and 1 less Defense and Intelligence than a Bloody Stud.
