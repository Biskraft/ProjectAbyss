# Laura (Order of Ecclesia)

is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. She is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. She is the town's local jeweler.

## Description
Laura has an upbeat and warm personality, but also possesses a sort of flirtatious demeanor about her. She thinks Shanoa looks gloomy because she doesn't smile; she will try to make her happy by crafting her jewelry.

## Location
Tymeo Mountains
