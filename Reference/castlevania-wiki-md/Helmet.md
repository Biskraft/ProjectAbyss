## *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
Most Helmets can only be worn by male characters. An exception is the Steel Helm, the weakest Helmet. Shanoa is also able to wear the Iron Helmet. The Head Guard can reduce the damage from Blunt attacks. The Iron Helmet acts a bit as a lightning rod and actually makes its wearer more susceptible to Thunder attacks. The Dragon Helm reduces an enemies Defense, but makes its wearer more susceptible to Holy attacks.
