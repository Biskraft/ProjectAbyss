# Akihiro Yamada

is a Japanese illustrator and manga artist. Mostly known by *Castlevania* fans due to his work as the character designer and illustrator for *Castlevania: Dracula X* for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System.

## Portfolio
Among his known works, some of the most known outside Japan are his illustrations of Fuyumi Ono's long-running *The Twelve Kingdoms* light novel series, and the manga *Record of Lodoss War: The Lady of Pharis*, who he made in conjunction with writer Ryo Mizuno.

He has also worked as a designer for several video games, being very prolific during the 1990s. A short list of them includes:
  - Black Rainbow* (PC-98) [1990] - Cover art, character designer
  - Black Rainbow II* (PC-98) [1992] - Cover art, character designer
  - Might and Magic III: Isles of Terra* (PC Engine) [1993] - Cover art (Japanese ver.)
  - Ancient Magic* (Super Famicom) [1993] - Cover art, character designer
  - Gaiapolis* (arcade) [1993] - Promotional art
  - Wizardry I・II* (PC Engine) [1993] - Cover art
  - Wizardry III・IV* (PC Engine) [1994] - Cover art
  - Kingdom Grand Prix* (arcade, Sega Saturn) [1994/1995] - Cover art (Sega Saturn), character designer
  - Mystic Ark* (Super Famicom) [1995] - Cover art, character designer
  - Castlevania: Dracula X* (Super Famicom) [1995] - Cover art, character designer
  - Terra Phantastica* (Sega Saturn) [1996] - Cover art, character designer
  - Milandra* (Super Famicom) [1997] - Cover art
  - Front Mission 3* (PlayStation) [1999] - Character designer
  - Mystic Ark* (PlayStation) [1999] - Cover art, character designer
  - Meremanoid* (PlayStation) [1999] - Cover art, character designer
  - Saiyuki: Journey West* (PlayStation) [1999] - Cover art, character designer
  - Fire Emblem Heroes* (Android, iOS) [2017] - Character designer (Jagen, Ogma, Zephiel, Gunter)
