# Opal

right|73px
The **Opal** is a rainbow colored jewel found in *Castlevania* that is associated with Ice (like the Sapphire).

Its first appearance was in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* as a "Valuable Ring". It could be sold to the Master Librarian for 5,000 Gold, which is the second highest value for a jewel ring, being more valuable than a Garnet (5,000 G) and less valuable than a Diamond (20,000 G). Opals are extremely rarely dropped by the Jewel Sword.

The Opal is much less valuable in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, where it is the least valuable jewel sold, giving only 150 Gold, which is less than a Turquoise (200 G).

Using an Opal with the Jewel Crush, blows an enemy away but deals out small damage.
