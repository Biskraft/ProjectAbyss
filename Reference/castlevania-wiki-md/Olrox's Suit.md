# Olrox's Suit

- Olrox's Suit** is a suit favored by Count Olrox.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, it can be obtained by defeating Brauner with Alucard or Soma Cruz.

## Item Data


## Trivia
- It being an enemy drop for Soma and/or Alucard to collect upon defeating Brauner in *Harmony of Despair* references Brauner's similarity to Olrox.
