# Heart Pendant

The **Heart Pendant** is a pendant that boosts both Luck and Defense and may affect the appearance of Hearts.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* it provides +1 Luck and +1 Defense. In *Dawn of Sorrow*, it provides +2 Luck and +1 Defense. It also acts like a Heart Brooch, and makes the appearance of hearts and big hearts more likely to happen. The Heart Pendant is a step up from a normal Pendant, which only provides +1 Luck, without a Defense boost in both games.
