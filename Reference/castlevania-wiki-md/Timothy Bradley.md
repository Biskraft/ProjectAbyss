# Timothy Bradley

- Timothy "Tim" Bradley** is an American eight grader who helps Simon Belmont lift his curse in the *Worlds of Power 4 - Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* novel. He is afraid of scary movies and novels, but likes playing *Castlevania* because he feels he's in control of the situation.

His mother is called Judy and he has a crush on a girl named Janet.
