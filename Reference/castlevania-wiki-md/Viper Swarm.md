The **Viper Swarm** is an enemy in *Super Castlevania IV*.

## Description
A revolting entanglement of snakes that hangs on the ceiling in wait for prey. When approached, it will drop down and slowly slither on the ground still in tack as a swarm. It will also pursue if the player attempts to avoid it. They are encountered in the Ruined Mansion, shortly after entering the stables.

A similar enemy, the Poison Worm, appears in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.
