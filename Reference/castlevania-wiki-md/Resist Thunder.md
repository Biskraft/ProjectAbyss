# Resist Thunder

- Resist Thunder** is an Attribute Potion in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* that causes Thunder-based attacks to actually heal Alucard.

The item is called **Thunder Essence** in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* and raises one's lightning affinity, although it doesn't help unlock Lightning spells.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, this ability is obtained with a Bugbear's soul.
