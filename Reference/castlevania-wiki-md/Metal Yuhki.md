  - Metal Yuhki**, real name , is a composer notable for his membership in the Konami Kukeiha Club and his work on the *Tokimeki Memorial* series.

He arranged Vampire Killer, Bloody Tears, Beginning and Cross Your Heart for the 1993 NEC PC Engine game *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and participated on the soundtrack Akumajō Dracula X.

Some of his other works include the composition of the musics of *Gradius II* (PC-Engine), *TwinBee Taisen Puzzle-Dama* and *Thunder Cross II*.
