# Soul Weapon

:*This article includes the Soul Weapon known as "Wind". For other uses, see Wind (disambiguation).*

The  is a special power possessed by Sonia Belmont that can be used as a replacement for Sub-Weapons in *Castlevania Legends*. Each Soul Weapon is obtained by defeating an area boss, and obtain their souls afterward.

The Soul Weapon is the captured power of a spirit of the mother nature. Each weapon can be acquired by using a boss as the sacrifice to a spirit.
