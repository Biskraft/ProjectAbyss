# Tart

A **Tart** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it gives you 12 HP, which is the third smallest amount for any dessert. It gives you one more HP than a Shortcake and two less than a Cheesecake. It is dropped by a Diplocephalus.

A tart is the only dessert item found in the cell phone game *Castlevania: Order of Shadows* and it restores 12.5% of your health.

The tart in *Symphony of the Night* is a Strawberry Tart. A pear tart Tarte au Poire is found in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and an upside-down apple tart Tart Tatin is found in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.
