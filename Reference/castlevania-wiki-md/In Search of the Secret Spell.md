# In Search of the Secret Spell

- In Search of the Secret Spell** (known as **The Next Pyramid** in *King's Valley II*) is a music theme composed by Masahiro Ikariko for the MSX game *King's Valley II: The Seal of El Giza*.

It was arranged by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and is the background theme for the Forgotten City.
