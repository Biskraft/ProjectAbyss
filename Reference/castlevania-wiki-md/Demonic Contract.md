# Demonic Contract

right|250px
  - Demonic Contract** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It allows Soma to enter a pact with the Devil to sharply raise STR.

## Characteristics
This ability can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Devil's soul. When activated, it grants Soma 140% bonus STR and 180% bonus ATK based off his current values, while slowly losing health (10 HP per second), in addition to completely reducing CON (and by extension DEF) to 0. 

The spell starts taking effect 1.5 seconds after its activation. Once Soma's current HP reaches single digits, he will stop losing health, although due to the soul activation it will still keep on draining MP.
