# Mary Elizabeth McGlynn

- Mary Elizabeth McGlynn** is a Californian voice actress. She is well known for her roles as Major Motoko Kusanagi in *Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex* and Nina Williams in the *Tekken* series.

She provided the voice of Medusa in the English version of *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*.
