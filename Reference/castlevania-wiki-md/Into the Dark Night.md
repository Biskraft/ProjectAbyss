# Into the Dark Night

- Into the Dark Night** (known in Japan as **Dark Night Crash**) is a music theme composed by Masahiko Kimura for *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the second boss theme. It plays when battling Puppet Master, Gergoth, Zephyr and Paranoia.

It later appeared in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and *Grimoire of Souls*.
