# Chariot Ring

The **Chariot Ring** is the ring of the Tarot card "The Chariot", in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It increases innate physical statistics (STR and CON by 3) at the lesser expense of the corresponding magical statistics (INT and MND by 2). It is the counterpart to the Hermit Ring.
