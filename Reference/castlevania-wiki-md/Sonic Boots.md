# Sonic Boots

The **Sonic Boots** are leg gear in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* that allow the player to move faster (+60%). They are an upgrade from the Winged Boots, allowing one to go slightly faster and increasing some stats. They can be obtained in the downloadable stage Lord of Flies (Chapter 9). They are similar to Fuma's Greaves, which provide +40% speed and raise different stats (whip-wielding characters only).
