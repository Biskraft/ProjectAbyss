# Silver Ore

- Silver Ore** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is an ore containing the finest silver.

## Availability
Dropped by Owl Knights, Tin Men, Hammer Shakers and Automatons ZX27.

They can also appear inside of wooden chests in Somnus Reef, Tristis Pass, Argila Swamp, and throughout all areas of Dracula's Castle, as well as inside of green chests in Tymeo Mountains, Misty Forest Road and the Skeleton Cave.

## Usage
Silver Ore is required in order to complete Eugen's quest: "What the Blacksmith Does Best". If the player brings him 3 Silver Ores, he will reward them with 3,600G and the items Silver Plate and Silver Leggings will be added to Jacob's shop.
