# Konami Magazine - Nocturne in the Moonlight comic

A comic appearing on a single page spread in Konami Magazine Volume 4 (September 1997) as an advertisement for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It shows Dracula and Alucard's reaction upon the death of Lisa and how the fate of mankind depended on what they would do next. The story and artwork was by Ayami Kojima, supervised by IGA, who did the artwork for the game as well.

## Trivia
- The pose Dracula was in when mourning for Lisa's death via crucifixion in the comic was later reused in promotional artwork for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, also done by Ayami Kojima, where Mathias Cronqvist is seen mourning for Elisabetha in a similar manner, foreshadowing that character's connection to Dracula.
