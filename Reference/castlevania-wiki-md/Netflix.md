# Netflix

- Netflix** is an American media services provider. The company's primary business is its subscription-based streaming media service, which offers online streaming of a library of films and television programs.

Netflix released the *Castlevania* animated series, produced by Adi Shankar, based on the *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* video game. As of October 2018, three seasons were announced.
