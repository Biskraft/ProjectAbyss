  - Ezra E. Weisz** is an American voice actor. He is well known for his character roles as Fred Luo in *Outlaw Star*, Mao in *Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion*, Schneider in *Rave Master*, and Kaname Kuran in the *Vampire Knight* series.

Ezra provided the voice of Aeon in *Castlevania Judgment*.
