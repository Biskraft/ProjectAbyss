# Knami 4koma Manga Wai Wai World

is a panel-*seien* manga released on November 12, 2017 in Japan. It is part of the *Famicom Runners High* series.

## Characters
- Simon Belmont from *Castlevania*
- Charlie from *Circus Charlie*
- Goemon, Sasuke and Goemon Impact from *Ganbare Goemon*
- Getsu Fūma from *Getsu Fūma Den*
- Golem and Moai from *Gradius*
- Konami Man and Konami Lady from *Konami Wai Wai World*
- Solid Snake from *Metal Gear*
- Chichibinta Rika from *Parodius*
- Mama Pig from *Pooyan*
- Gillian Seed, Mika Slayton, Harry Benson and Benson Cunningham from *Snatcher*
- TwinBee, Pastel and Princess Melora from *TwinBee*
