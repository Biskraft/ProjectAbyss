# Submerged Areas

- Submerged Areas** appear in some *Castlevania* games in which the game is more exploration-based, such as *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*, *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They are almost always connected to underground waterways and oftentimes a special item is required to access these hard to reach areas, mostly due to the fact characters tend to float.

In Alucard's case (being part vampire), he actually takes damage from even touching water and thus requires several items before he can reach these areas. Oddly enough, air doesn't ever seem to be an issue with these stages.

Typical enemies are what one would expect underwater, mostly fish with the occasional water demon. Because they are naturally mostly unexplored areas, they tend to have excellent items, some of which even have sunken ships as sub-areas.
