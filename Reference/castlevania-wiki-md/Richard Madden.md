# Richard Madden

- Richard Madden** is a Scottish stage, film and television actor best known for portraying Robb Stark in the HBO series, *Game of Thrones*.

He voiced Trevor Belmont/Alucard and Zobek's Lieutenant in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate* and *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.
