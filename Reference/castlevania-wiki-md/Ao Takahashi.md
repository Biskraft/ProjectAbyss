# Ao Takahashi

(born August 8, 1979), also known as **Hiroko Takahashi**, is a Japanese voice actress affiliated with Kenyu Office and formerly affiliated with Aoni Production and Aksent. 

She voiced Mina Hakuba and Yoko Belnades in the Japanese version of both *Sorrow* opus and Yoko alone in the Japanese version of *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
