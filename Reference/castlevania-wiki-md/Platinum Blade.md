# Platinum Blade

The **Platinum Blade** is a very fast sword due to its light weight. This sword can be found in candle along the upper bridge section of the Bat's Belfry. It is swung at a about his waist.

It is somewhat faster than the Leather Whip, but it is a little weaker and is used as a magic-consuming sub-weapon. It is one of two weapons that are considered "Fast". It is weaker than the other one, Muramasa, but consumes less energy and is longer.
