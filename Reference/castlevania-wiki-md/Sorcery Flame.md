# Sorcery Flame

right

  - Sorcery Flame** is a Mage-Type Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by a Scissor Rod by defeating a certain amount of enemies while this I.D. is summoned. Costs 20 MP to cast.

## Description

Scissor Rod unleashes a constant stream of flames from his staff, while enemies stagger helpless receiving medium to heavy damage by the constant fire.

## Notes
- Learning this spell is essential in order to burn the vines around one of the doors in Aiolon Ruins and gain access to a couple of rooms where the following treasures can be found:
  - Leather Chair
  - Frenzy of Wind (MP Max Up for Trevor)
  - HP Max Up (Abyss Whip for Trevor)
  - Damascus Steel
  - Hilarious Cushion
  - Ancient wall inscription (decipherable only with a Tiramisu's Decipher ability).
