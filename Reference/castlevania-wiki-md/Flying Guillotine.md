# Flying Guillotine

right|200px
  - Flying Guillotine** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It summons a guillotiner from hell.

## Characteristics
This ability can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Guillotiner's soul. It allows Soma to summon a Guillotiner which will chomp the enemy directly at its position, dealing Slash damage to it.
