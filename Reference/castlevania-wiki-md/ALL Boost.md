# ALL Boost

right|300px

  - ALL Boost** is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. When cast, it boosts all status attributes temporarily. A regular cast boosts STR, CON, INT, MND and LCK +7, while a fully charged cast boosts STR, CON, INT, MND and LCK +15.
