  - Laser Crystals** are environmental enemies in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. They are large crystal shards which emit laser beams at intruders.

## Description
Laser Crystals appear in the Tower of Sorcery, a stage only accessible to Cornell and Carrie. With only 10 HP, it theoretically shouldn't be a problem dealing with them, but as the stage they appear in is mostly comprised by small platforms floating over a black abyss where even the slightest misstep could mean an instant death, they should not be taken lightly.

A single hit from Cornell's primary attack or a charged shot from Carrie should be enough to destroy them from a distance. In case they are further away, throwing an Axe or a Cross is recommended.
