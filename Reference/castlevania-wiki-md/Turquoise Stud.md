# Turquoise Stud

The **Turquoise Stud** is a stud earring made of Turquoise and is the second least effective earring in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It gives +2 Defense and +1 Intelligence, which is one more DEF than a Hoop Earring, but one less than a Silver Stud.
