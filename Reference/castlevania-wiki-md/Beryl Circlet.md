# Beryl Circlet

The **Beryl Circlet** is a circlet made of Beryl that appears in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. In addition to protecting from Thunder attacks, it will actually heal the player when they receive a Thunder based hit. It also gives an INT boost of 10, which is two more than the Cat-Eye Circlet, two less than the Silver Crown, and the same as the Ruby Circlet, the Topaz Circlet and Opal Circlet.

It is a very useful piece of head gear because it resists Galamoth's electrical attacks.
