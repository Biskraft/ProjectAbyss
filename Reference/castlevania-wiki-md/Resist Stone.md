# Resist Stone

- Resist Stone** is an Attribute Potion in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* that increases one's resistance to petrification, which makes it less likely for attacks with a Stone attribute to actually petrify the caster.

This ability can be gained by Soma Cruz by stealing the souls of enemies. It costs nothing for the effect to remain in effect, though it cannot be equipped with another Yellow Soul. It is obtained with a Gargoyle soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and a Tombstone soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.
