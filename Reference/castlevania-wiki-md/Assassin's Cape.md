# Assassin's Cape

The **Assassin's Cape** is one of the stronger capes found in *Castlevania*.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* (called the **Ninja Cloak** in North America and **Thief's Mantle** in Japan), it is the second most effective cape in the game, giving DEF +15 and LCK +5, which is better than the +12 DEF offered by the Crimson Cloak, but outclassed by the Twilight Cloak which gives 3 more DEF and LCK.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, the Assassin Cape, in addition to the standard Defense boost, also gives an STR boost (+5 DEF, +3 STR). This is in contrast to the Blue Cape, Holy Mantle, and Paludamentum, which all give an INT boost, and the Elven Cape which gives a MND boost.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, the Assassin Cape is the only cape in the base game that offers a STR boost (+1), as well as defense against Blunt attacks (+5). It gives a rather average and even +4 to HP, MP and DEF.
