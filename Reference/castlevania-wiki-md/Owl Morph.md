# Owl Morph

right|200px

  - Owl Morph** is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. As the name suggests, the spell transforms either herself or Jonathan into an Owl, allowing them to freely fly around and explore. It serves much the same function as other characters' Bat Form, although no extra abilities can be acquired for this form (such as Wing Smash or Fire of Bat) other than just flying to access hard to reach areas.
