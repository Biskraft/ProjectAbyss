# Deadly Waterfalls

*(This article is a stub)*
The **Deadly Waterfalls** is the fourth area Simon explores in Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate, as well as the fourth area overall in the game. It is named "Deadly Waterfalls" because the water can wash off a human's flesh, bones and soul like acid. The water is like that since it is mentioned that thousands of humans were thrown in to the moat and infected the underground streams and many people died trying to get past a location so dangerous.
