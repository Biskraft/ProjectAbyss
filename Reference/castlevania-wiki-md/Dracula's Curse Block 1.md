Block 1 (also known as Route 1) for the game *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* is the town of **Warakiya**. Nintendo Power dubs the entire block (1-01 to 1-04) as "Route 1". After defeating the Skull Knight boss at the last block, the path is split and you are offered the choice to go to Clock Tower of Untimely Death (Route 2) or Forest of Darkness (Route 3).

Trevor's sub-weapons as they appear are the Dagger, Holy Water, Stopwatch, Axe and another Dagger.
