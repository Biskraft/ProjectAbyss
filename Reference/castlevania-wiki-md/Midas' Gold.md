# Midas' Gold

- Midas' Gold** is an extremely pure chunk of raw, unworked gold dropped by Jins in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It's one of the most valuable raw materials you can sell in the game, but doesn't have many things it can be used for. It is used to forge the Alucart Mail, as well as one or two other items.
