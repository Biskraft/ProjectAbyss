# Ganbare Goemon Gaiden 2: Tenka no Zaihō

is a *Ganbare Goemon* game released for the Family Computer on January 3rd, 1992 in Japan. It was also re-released for the Wii U Virtual Console on May 20th, 2015 in Japan.

Simon Belmont is a recruitable character in this role-playing game. He is met by the party when they arrive to Europe. In combat, he can use a Holy Water ability.

## Overview
This is a sequel to the first *Ganbare Goemon Gaiden* role-playing game; which is a side story in the entire *Goemon* series. Goemon, a cunning thief and an avid treasure hunter, has nothing to do and sits in his house being bored, but his trusty friend Ebisumaru has good news: there is a big treasure to find somewhere in a big city across the sea. Of course the treasure will not be just given to Goemon; he will have to perform a dangerous mission before he can acquire it. The two friends board a ship and thus the journey begins.
