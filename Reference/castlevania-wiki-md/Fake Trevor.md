# Fake Trevor

- Fake Trevor** is a boss in the *Castlevania* series. He is a zombie impersonating Trevor Belmont.

## Description
An evil duplicate of Trevor Belmont who fights alongside Fake Grant and Fake Sypha, and created to confuse Alucard, who fought alongside the real versions of these heroes during the events of *Dracula's Curse*.

The fake heroes first appear trying to kill Alucard in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and are all defeated. The group reappears in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* in the Nest of Evil, where the player can fight bosses from previous titles.

### Combat notes
- Fake Trevor uses twisted versions of the real Trevor's attacks, such as a cross made of bloody bones that deals Dark damage.
- Fake Trevor's most dangerous attack consists in him casting a variant of 1,000 Blades. This is amply telegraphed by him first standing in place and glowing indifferent colors while he gathers enough power. It can be avoided either by flying (Bat Form, Owl Morph), turning into mist, or by staying airborne (continuous uppercuts), among other methods, although care must be taken from attacks from his other allies (Fake Sypha and Fake Grant).
- If Fake Trevor is killed in battle, Fake Sypha will constantly resurrect him, although as a rotten zombie. However, he is weaker once this occurs and can be destroyed again within a matter of seconds. If touched, this zombie may curse the player.
