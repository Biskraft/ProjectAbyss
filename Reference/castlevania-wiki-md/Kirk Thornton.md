# Kirk Thornton

- Kirk Thornton** is an American voice actor and ADR director. He voiced Grant Danasty in *Castlevania Judgment*.

Kirk's famous character roles include Hotohori in *Fushigi Yūgi*, Jin in *Samurai Champloo*, Hajime Saito in *Rurouni Kenshin*, Don Patch in *Bobobo-bo Bo-bobo*, Xiahou Dun in several installments of the *Dynasty Warriors*, Harry Mason in *Silent Hill: Shattered Memories*, and Shadow the Hedgehog in the *Sonic the Hedgehog* series.
