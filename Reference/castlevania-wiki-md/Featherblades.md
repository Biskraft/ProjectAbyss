# Featherblades

- Featherblades** is an attack in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, gained from a Harpy that shoots razor-sharp feathers.

Soma Cruz earns the ability by absorbing and using a Harpy's soul, while Charlotte Aulin can learn this spell by blocking a Harpy's attack with her Book of Binding.
