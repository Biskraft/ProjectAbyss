# Kiwi

The **Kiwi** is a fruit that replenishes HP or MP.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, it is the second lowest HP (+40) and MND (+3) increasing fruit. This is above a Strawberry (+30 MP +3 MND +1 LCK), but below the two star Grapes (+60 MP +5 MND).
