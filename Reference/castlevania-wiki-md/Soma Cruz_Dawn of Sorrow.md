# Soma Cruz/Dawn of Sorrow

thumb|right|160px|<p style="text-align: center;">Soma Cruz in *Dawn of Sorrow*.</p>

  - Soma Cruz** makes his appearance as a protagonist again in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. He can now collect up to nine souls of the same kind; having multiple souls will increase the power of their abilities in various increments, depending on the soul itself. His physical weapons can be evolved into more powerful weapons by going to Yoko's shop and having her combine them with one of his collected souls. The Doppelganger soul (Quick Change) can be found later in the game, which allows him to set up two completely different sets of equipment from his inventory and quickly swap between them.
