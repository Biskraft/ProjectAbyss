# Strength Armband

The **Strength Armband** is a bracelet in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* that greatly increases STR +100 at the expense of other stats (-25 DEF, INT, and LCK). It is more extreme than the Strength Ring (+50 STR, -10 DEF and INT). It is the Strength counterpart to the Gambler Armband, Defense Armband, and Sage Armband.
