# List of Curse of Darkness Materials

This list contains materials found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, and used to forge various weapons and armors with the *Combine* menu.

They usually drop from a family of monsters (i.e. Bronze can drop from Skeletons Lv.1 or Lv. 9 or Lv.38). Hector can only carry a maximum of 99 per material. Monster information can be found on this page.

## Notes
Some materials can be found in chests:
- Aluminum (×1) can be found in the Abandoned Castle, B1
- Orichalcum (×1) can be found in Aiolon Ruins
- Carbon Steel (×2) can be found in the Forest of Jigramunt and in the Infinite Corridor, 3F
- Damascus Steel (×2) can be found in Cordova Town, 1F, and in Aiolon Ruins
- Adamantine (×1) can be found in the Infinite Corridor, 3F
- Spirit of Fuji (×3) can be found in Mortvia Aqueduct, F1, Aiolon Ruins, and Infinite Corridor, 3F
- Aquamarine (×2) can be found in Mortvia Aqueduct, 1F, and in the Forest of Jigramunt
- Dragon Scale (×2) can be found in Baljhet Mountains and in the Forest of Jigramunt
- Frenzy of the Wind (×1) can be found in Aiolon Ruins
- Ceremonial Tool (×2) can be found in Garibaldi Temple, 1F, and in the Infinite Corridor, 3F
- Sun Tears (×1) can be found in Garibaldi Temple, 1F
- Lightning Stone (×1) can be found in the Infinite Corridor, 2F
- Angel Halo (×1) can be found in Mortvia Aqueduct, 1F
- Dark Crystal (×2) can be found in Baljhet Mountains and in the Infinite Corridor, 3F
- Holy Man's Vein (×1) can be found in the Infinite Corridor, 3F
- Devil's Vein (×1) can be found in the Forest of Jigramunt
- Vampire Blood (×1) can be found in the Tower of Evermore, 2F
- Phlogiston (×1) can be found in the Infinite Corridor, 3F
- Ether (x4) can be found in the Forest of Jigramunt, in Garibaldi Temple, 1F, in Cordova Town, 1F, and in the Infinite Corridor, 3F
- Dark Matter (×1) can be found in Mortvia Aqueduct, 1F
- Miracle Egg (×1) can be found in Dracula's Castle, B1
