# Eric Lecarde/Portrait of Ruin

{{Q|I'm truly glad I could see you both at the moment of my death. Live for all you're worth. And you, Jonathan. Charlotte... I truly am grateful.|Eric's last words to his twin daughters and Jonathan and Charlotte}}
:*This page is for the Portrait of Ruin character. For other uses, see Wind (disambiguation)*.
  - Eric Lecarde**, protagonist of *Castlevania: Bloodlines*, appears as a supporting character in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, where he goes under the pseudonym of "**Wind**". His primary role is to assign Jonathan and Charlotte various quests and give them new items and abilities upon completing them. In the end, Eric sees that his daughters, Stella and Loretta, are alive and well, and fades away.

In the English version of the game, Eric/Wind is voiced by David Vincent.
