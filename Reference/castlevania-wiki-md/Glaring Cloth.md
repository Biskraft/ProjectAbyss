# Glaring Cloth

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">Alucard wearing the **Glaring Cloth**.</p>
The **Glaring Cloth** is a silvery white cloak in the Sega Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It gives +6 CON, +2 DEF and provides complete protection against the Dark attack attribute.

It substitutes the Crystal Cloak (called simply "Invisible" in Japan) from the original game. The reason for the change was because of the Sega Saturn's performance.
