# Skull Millione

- Skull Millione** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. He is a Hell spawn with poisoned claws.

## Origins
thumb|left|<p style="text-align: center;">The Malebranche threaten Virgil and Dante, portrayed by Gustave Doré.</p>

Scarmiglione is a demon who appears in *Inferno* of Dante's *Divine Comedy*. In the poem, he is part of a demonic troop called the Malebranche, who guard the fifth bolgia of Malebolge. The Malebolge is found in the eighth circle of Hell, Fraud, where corrupt politicians are immersed in burning pitch.

## Description
Depicted as a Hell spawn with poison claws, Skull Millione himself is not poisonous to touch, but his claw attacks are. Initially, he is found hovering in midair; when approached, he quickly positions above Soma and then lunges downward at great speed, attempting to poison him with his toxic claws.

## Trivia
- The name Skull Millione is a mistranslation from Scarmiglione, a demon who appears in Dante's *Divine Comedy*.
- The name *Scarmiglione* arguably translates to "Trouble Maker".
