# Water of Life

- Water of Life** (called the **Wonder Drug of Life** in Japanese) is a multiplayer item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* that allows players to revive their fallen team-mates. They are always found in blue chests while playing multiplayer and are not featured in single player. Each player may only carry one Water of Life. Any Waters of Life still carried when the chapter ends will fade away. As a result, it is impossible to begin a chapter with one.
