# Bloodstone

- Bloodstone** is a pendant of Bloodstone that doubles Alucard's blood healing ability in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, such as the Dark Metamorphosis spell or the abilities of the Gurthang or Muramasa swords.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, this item's alleged improvement of Alucard's blood healing does not work.
