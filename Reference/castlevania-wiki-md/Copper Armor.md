The **Copper Armor** is an enemy exclusive to the the Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is an antique piece that is very valuable.

## Description
An undead warrior clad in a copper plate that keeps him protected from enemies. The damage done to his armor is cut in half, thus making him difficult to kill.
