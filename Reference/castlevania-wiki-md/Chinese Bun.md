# Chinese Bun

A **Chinese Bun** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it gives you 10 HP, which is the lowest amount of HP for any type of Chinese Bun (2 less than a Red Bean Bun). This is the same amount as the highest fruit (Strawberry) and the lowest beverage (Green Tea). It can only be obtained using a Meal Ticket.
