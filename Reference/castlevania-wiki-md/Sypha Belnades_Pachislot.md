# Sypha Belnades/Pachislot

right|250px

  - Sypha Belnades** appears in *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula III*. She replaces Angela from the previous two *Pachislot* games in the role of Trevor's ally with feminine charms. She plays the pipe organ in one scene. She appears in another scene involving magical wardrobe transformations. She joins Trevor and Alucard during Dracula battles.
