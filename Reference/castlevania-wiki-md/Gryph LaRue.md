# Gryph LaRue

thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">A possible concept art of Gryph LaRue before his cancellation.</p>

  - Gryph LaRue** is a character that was initially intended to be the protagonist of the American-made cell phone game *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*. Not much is known about him, only that he belonged to a family of mages and witches.

Koji Igarashi, veteran director and producer of the *Castlevania* series, recommended the development team to use a member of the Belmont Clan instead, and so Desmond Belmont was created and replaced Gryph as the game's protagonist.
