# Corn Soup

- Corn Soup** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It appears in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia*. It is available in *Order of Ecclesia* at Jacob's shop once Shanoa gives Aeon some Salt during one of his quests.
