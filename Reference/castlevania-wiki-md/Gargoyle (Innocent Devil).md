# Gargoyle (Innocent Devil)

right|200px

  - Gargoyle** is a Bird-Type Innocent Devil in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a Level 4 Innocent Devil, and can learn Conflict Fall and Force Cannon. It can be obtained from a Khaos Devil by feeding it blue or white Evo Crystals.
