# Tony Oliver

- Rafael Antonio "Tony" Olivier** is an American voice actor. He voiced Vincent Dorin in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. Later, he provided the voice of Shaft as a replacement of Jeff Manning in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* and its portable game.

Tony is famous for anime fans as the voice of Hiromu Shinbo in *Chobits*, Arsène Lupin III from *Lupin III*, Sha Gojyo in *Saiyuki* series and Ulquiorra Cifer in *Bleach*.
