# Morning Star

right|73px

The **Morning Star** is usually the highest form possible for the Vampire Killer whip, although there are some exceptions. The Morning Star power-up is an item —generally in the form of a whip or a crest— that can be picked up to upgrade a weapon to its next level, even when not upgrading it to a Morning Star. Playable characters who do not normally use whips can also use Morning Stars as clubs or hammers, which are sometimes called by their German name, **Morgenstern**. Juste Belmont and Trevor Belmont can also attach an iron ball or a platinum cube to the tip of the whip to increase the attack power.
