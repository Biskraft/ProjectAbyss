# Boomerang Armor

The **Boomerang Armor** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

## Description
The Boomerang Armor is giant armored knight found near the end of the Aqueduct of Dragons in Castle A. He carries a shuriken-like weapon that he hurls forward and then returns to him like a boomerang.

His armor has a different design than those seen on other giant armors encountered in the game, featuring small details more akin to a sea creature, like fins and scales, making it a more appropriate apparel to the water-based area he's encountered in.

While at first it would seem there's nothing worth the effort of fighting him, as the area beyond him appears to be a dead end, this is actually not the case, as the big wall behind him is illusory and can be traversed by jumping and crossing through by the upper part, which leads to a secret chamber holding the Nail of Vlad (STR +10).
