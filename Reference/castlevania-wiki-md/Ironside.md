# Ironside

right|200px

  - Ironside** is a Battle-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a Level 4 Innocent Devil, and can learn Chain Punch and Machine Gun Shot. It can be obtained from a Juggernaut Devil, using **<span style="color:blue;">Blue</span>** or **<span style="color:white;">White</span>** Evo Crystals.

## Description
The Ironside features a bulky robotic appearance and its size clearly determines the growth rate of its statistics upon leveling-up. Its main role is being a tank of sorts, as it has a high boost of Hearts, ATK and DEF per level. Its weaknesses stat-wise are a low Agility growth, which means its attacks are slow or sometimes sluggish. It also has the lowest MAG growth rate, which makes its abilities deal minimal damage. The Ironside's abilities, Chain Punch and Machine Gun Shot, makes it almost like a turret, hence it is immobile while casting any of these two; the former having a two-hit blunt damage, while the later having the property to hit several enemies multiple times. The player can evolve the Battle-Type into an Ironside should they wish to have an Innocent Devil capable of tanking high amounts of damage.
