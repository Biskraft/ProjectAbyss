# Cuirassair Armor

right|73px

The **Cuirassair Armor** (also called **Cuirass Armor**) is body armor in the *Castlevania* series. It is armor made from fabric treated with a special liquid.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, the Cuirass Armor grants DEF +22, which is two more than the Rock Leather and 1 less than the Padded Armor.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the Cuirassair Armor is created by adding Adamantine to a Field Armor, increasing its DEF from 35 to 40. It cannot be further evolved and is 5 less than the Parade Armor, which can also be created with a Field Armor.
