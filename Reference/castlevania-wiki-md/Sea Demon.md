# Sea Demon

The **Sea Demon** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is an evil demon of the sea, skilled in ice magic. It is a replacement for the Frost Demon in some games.

## Description
This demon, in *Order of Ecclesia*, only appears in underwater areas of Kalidus Channel and Somnus Reef. It will only materialize once the player gets close to its spawning point. It mainly hovers around while attempting to inflict contact damage with its legs.

Soon enough, however, it will quickly position itself to a side and at player's level and cast Grando, at which point this glyph can be absorbed.
