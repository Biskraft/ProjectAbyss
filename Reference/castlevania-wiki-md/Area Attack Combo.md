# Area Attack Combo

An attack combo in Castlevania: Lords of Shadow games that involves quick successive Area Attacks. In Lords of Shadow, Gabriel can chain up to eight Area Attacks from the beginning of the game, each which swings his whip around in a circle. In Mirror of Fate, the first attack is a 90 degree attack from above the players head towards the ground in front, which is followed by a 90 degree attack back from the ground to above his head, followed by a 180 degree attack from the ground behind the player arcing above towards the ground in front of the player, followed by another 180 degree attack in the reverse direction. Once the Area Attack Combo Final has been obtained, this can be followed with a 360 degree attack that ends up smashes down hard on the ground in front of the player. This combo can also be performed in the air in both games.
