# Spawns of Leviathan

- Spawns of Leviathan** are enemies in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* Reverie DLC. They are the spawn of one of the Old Gods. More an environmental hazard than a true enemy, Spawns of Leviathan cannot be fought: their purpose is to punish the player if they allow Gabriel to fall into the water during platforming sections by immediately devouring him.
