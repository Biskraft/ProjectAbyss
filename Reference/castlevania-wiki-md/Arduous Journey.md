# Arduous Journey

- Arduous Journey** is a music theme originally composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Bloodlines*. It is the background theme that plays during the map screen. It was later arranged by Yasushi Asada for *Castlevania Judgment*.

It is the 20th track in the fourth disc of *Akumajō Dracula Best Music Collections BOX*.
