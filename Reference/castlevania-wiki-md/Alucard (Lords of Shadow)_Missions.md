# Alucard (Lords of Shadow)/Missions

This list contains all the little lines pronounced by Alucard in the Revelations loading screen. After defeating Zobek's Bodyguard, ALucard will stop speaking during the loading screen.

## Introduction/Tutorial
- The castle henchmen are taking the Chaos and Void relics. I must find them and return them to where my father left them.
- The castle henchmen are taking the Chaos and Void relics. I must find them and return them to where my father left them.
- That group of jailers has entrenched itself with the Void Sword in a strange building. I must find a way to get in there.

## Guest House
- I must locate the group of jailers and recover the Void Sword.
- I must find each one of the three jailers to retrieve the keys they have taken.
- The jailer boss is hiding in this room. I must remain alert.
- This jailer is different from the others: he seems to have absorbed some of the power of the Sword. I must defeat him and get it back.

## Forbidden Wing
- The Chaos Power is in this place. I must find it.
- I must find a way to return to the room where the Chaos Power was and retrieve it.

## Ending
- I must return to the city and protect my father from his enemies.
- It's Zobek's lieutenant! He won't get out of here alive.
