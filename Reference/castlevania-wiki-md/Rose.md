# Rose

The **Rose** is a projectile in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It is can be thrown when using an Apollo + Mandragora DSS combination. Several other Mandragora combinations utilize aspects of a Rose in that game, including the Thorn Whip (Mercury card, appears in other games as well), Rose Sword (Mars card), and Petal Plume (Diana card).
