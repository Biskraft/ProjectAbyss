The **Pike Master** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

## Description
The Pike Master is the most powerful of all giant armored knights encountered in *Harmony of Dissonance*. He has both, the highest Level and EXP of any non-boss enemy in the game.

Appropriate to a knight of his rank, this elite warrior comes clad in a victory armor set and wields a gigantic spear, going to the extreme as to having the bloodied corpse of one of his hapless victims still impaled on its tip.

Encountered late in the game, in the Marble Corridor of Castle B, the Pike Master's attacks are different from those seen on other giant armors. He initially paces back and forth, and then quickly stabs with his spear at ground level, dealing an astounding 100 damage units (Skeletons, Zombies, and Bats in comparison deal about 5).

He's also notoriously resilient and many hits are required to put him down, which could potentially put the player in a risky situation while he's attempting to fend off the knight. Throwing sub-weapons that deal multiple hits or casting Spell Fusions that let Juste keep his distance are advised.

He drops the rare Floating Boots, which allow Juste to float down slowly in the air after performing a jump.
