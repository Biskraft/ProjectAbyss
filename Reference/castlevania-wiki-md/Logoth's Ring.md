# Logoth's Ring

right|306px
  - Logoth's Ring** (**Logos Ring** in Japan) is a magical ring in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* that powers up the Dagger subweapon so that three daggers are thrown instead of one, but increases Heart consumption to 2.
