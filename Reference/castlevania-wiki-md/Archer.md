The **Archer** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. He's a fiendishly accurate archer.

## Description
The Archer is one of several types of archers found in *Symphony of the Night*, along with the Bone Archer and the Sniper of Goth. This one sits in a pulley-operated platform and waits for the right opportunity to strike.
