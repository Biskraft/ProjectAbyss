# Relic Rune Pack

right|264px

The **Relic Rune Pack** is a DLC for *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.

## Description
- Gives you the exclusive Transmutation Rune which allows you to absorb any of the game's relics and transform them into one of your choice. However, you can only do this once you have picked up your first relic. Also, you can only transform a relic into one you have already discovered.*

## Trivia
- This DLC pack gives the access to the Transmutation Rune which allows Dracula to transform one relic into another relic.
- Due to its destructive power, the Talisman of the Dragon is the only relic that cannot be transmuted.
