# Ice Sword

right|194px

Any sword with Ice-based attacks. There are two recurring ice swords found in Castlevania, Rahab's Frost (also called Icebrand), and the more powerful Ice Brand (not to be confused with Icebrand/Rahab's Frost). Nathan Graves also has use of an ice sword. Joachim is able to add the ice element to his symbiont swords after defeating the Ice Elemental.
