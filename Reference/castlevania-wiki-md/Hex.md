# Hex

right|280px

  - Hex** is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is a powerful Curse spell dropped by Vapula. It does considerable magic damage and can curse the enemy, preventing them from using abilities. It is also cost effective, expending only 25 MP per use.
