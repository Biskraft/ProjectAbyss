The **Earth Armor** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

## Description
The Earth Armor is some of the weakest armor types in the game. Like his other armor counterparts, it moves slowly and attacks periodically. When he swings his sword, it releases an earthquake which travels along the ground (simply jump to avoid it).
