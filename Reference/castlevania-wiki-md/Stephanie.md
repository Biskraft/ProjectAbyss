# Stephanie

- Stephanie** is a hair accessory that sports a fancy engraved dedication that is dropped by a Draculina in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It increases magical abilities greatly, providing INT and MND both +8 (each well more than any other head equipment), as well as a small DEF boost of +1.

## Trivia
- Stephanie or Stefanie is a female name that comes from the Greek name Στέφανος (Stefanos) meaning "crown".
