# Rune Ring

The **Rune Ring**, or **Sage Ring**, is a ring that increases the rate of MP restoration and part of the sage equipment.

It yields a DEF boost of +2 in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, and +1 in *Dawn of Sorrow*. Wearing it in *Portrait of Ruin* and *Harmony of Despair* increases MP recovery rate by 0.2 per second.

It replaces the Cipher's Charm from *Harmony of Dissonance*, and is replaced by the Star Ring in *Order of Ecclesia*.
