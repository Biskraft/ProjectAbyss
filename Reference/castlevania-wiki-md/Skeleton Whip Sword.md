The **Skeleton Whip Sword** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Bloodlines*.

## Description
Skeleton Whip Swords are skeletons that at first glance appear to be armed with a regular sword. However, when they swing their blade, it extends and is shown to be a whip sword instead.

They are a type of Skeleton Chain and Skeleton Soldier.
