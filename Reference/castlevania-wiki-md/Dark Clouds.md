  - Dark Clouds** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the background theme that plays during several instances where Soma Cruz encounters Celia Fortner inside With Light's headquarters.

It is notably played during the cutscene where the sorceress kills a doppelganger of Mina Hakuba to goad Soma into succumbing to his identity as the reincarnated Dracula.
