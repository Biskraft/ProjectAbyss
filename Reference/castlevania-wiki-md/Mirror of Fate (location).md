# Mirror of Fate (location)

The **Mirror of Fate** is the location within which the titular object is held. This was the original throne room of Carmilla in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the first area explored by Alucard in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*, and the location of his grave.

Previously, Trevor explored this area, being the sixteenth and final area in his journey. It was here that he encountered Dracula and met his death at his hands.
