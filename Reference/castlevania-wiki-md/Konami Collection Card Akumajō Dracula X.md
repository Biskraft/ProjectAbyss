# Konami Collection Card Akumajō Dracula X

thumbA set of 56 Japanese trading cards sold in packs for the Japanese version of Castlevania: Symphony of the Night.  They contained mostly official artwork.  Cards 3, 6, 15, and 53 were extra rare.
