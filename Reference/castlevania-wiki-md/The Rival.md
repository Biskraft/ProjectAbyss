# The Rival

"**The Rival**" is a canceled character from an unknown *Castlevania* game. From the original description, it's implied that contrary to the Richter's and Maria's sprites, he was not a character from the canceled Sega 32X *Castlevania* game known in fan circles as "*Castlevania: The Bloodletting*". Possibly, he was a canceled character from *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*.
