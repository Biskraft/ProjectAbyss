# Direct Attack Combo

An attack combo in the *Lords of Shadow* games that involves five successive Direct Attacks. Each attack strikes the enemy in front of the player. The final strike is capable of knocking down an enemy. In *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*, all five attacks in the combo are available at the beginning of the game and Gabriel is able to change the direction he's facing between attacks in the combo. In *Mirror of Fate*, the final attack that knocks down an opponent in only available when the Direct Attack Combo Final is obtained. This combo can also be performed in the air in both games, which may slow the descent of the player when an enemy is struck.
