# Fried Chicken

- Fried Chicken** is a food item that recovers HP. In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* is a common item that recovers 18 HP and offers a CON boost of +5. This is better than a Hot Dog, which gives the same amount of HP, but only +3 CON.
