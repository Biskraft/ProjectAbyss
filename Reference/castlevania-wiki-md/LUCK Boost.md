# LUCK Boost

right|50px**LUCK Boost** is a spell or power that increases Luck.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
In *Aria of Sorrow*, Soma Cruz is able to obtain this ability by gaining dominance over the souls of two different creatures, thus dividing the LCK provided in two levels of power, with a difference of four units between each other:
    - Fortune of Light** - Ghost Dancer (LCK +4)
    - Miracle of Light** - Gremlin (LCK +8)

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* / *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
In *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*, Soma is able to gain this ability from a Ghost Dancer's soul and Dead Warrior's soul respectively, and the more such souls he has, the more powerful the effect.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
LUCK Boost is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Portrait of Ruin*. When cast, it boosts LCK temporarily. A regular cast boosts LCK +30, while a fully charged cast boosts LCK +60.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
  - Felicem Fio** is a back glyph that increases Shanoa's luck. This bonus scales as her LCK stat increases, and thus, grows in power as she levels up.

## Trivia
- Even though it's called a "potion" in Western releases of *Symphony of the Night*, the item resembles a pill. The original Japanese name for it is **Lucky Drug**, which may explain why it looks that way.
  - Felicem Fio* is Latin for "(I) become lucky/blessed/happy".
