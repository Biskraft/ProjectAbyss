# Jamieson Price

- Jamieson Kent Price** is an American voice actor who voiced Walter Bernhard in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. He also provided the voice of Brauner in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, as well as Golem in *Castlevania Judgment*.

Jamieson is well known for voicing Lu Bu in the *Dynasty Warriors* series.
