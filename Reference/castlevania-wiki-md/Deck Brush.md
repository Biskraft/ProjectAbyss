# Deck Brush

The **Deck Brush** is the weakest spear in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. Although originally used for cleaning, sailors have also been know to ably defend themselves with it.

It is a "trap" combination devised to waste the rare Aquamarine material and creating it should be avoided unless looking on acquiring a complete item set.
