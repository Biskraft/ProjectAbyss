# Rubber Armor

The **Rubber Armor**, or **Rubber Suit**, is an armor made from a rubber-like, soft material.

It is the second weakest armor found in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It grants Defense +6, which is two points more than the Leather Armor and two less than the Field Armor.
