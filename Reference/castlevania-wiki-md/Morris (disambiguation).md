# Morris (disambiguation)

The name **Morris** can refer to:

- Morris Clan, an offshoot of the Belmont Clan:
  - Quincy Morris, based on a character from the *Dracula* novel
  - John Morris, from *Castlevania: Bloodlines*
  - Jonathan Morris, from *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
- Morris Baldwin, a character from *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*
