# Medusa Ring

right|75px

The **Medusa Ring** is a ring in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* that offers resistance to Petrification. It's similar in function to the Medusa Pendant from *Harmony of Dissonance* and the Perseus Ring from *Lament of Innocence*. It can be found by breaking the wall behind the Highbrow Chair at Dracula's Castle.
