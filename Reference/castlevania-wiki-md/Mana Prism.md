# Mana Prism

right|73px

  - Mana Prism** (also called **Mind Ex** or **Super Tonic**) is a Mind Potion that usually fully recovers all MP. This is more than a High Mind Up or a Mind Up. An exception to this effect can be found in *Harmony of Dissonance*, *Lament of Innocence* and *Encore of the Night*, where it only recovers some MP.

In *Order of Shadows*, the use of the item has been switched to that of the Super Potion: instead of restoring MP, it restores HP.
