# Bernhard's Wing

right|300px

  - Bernhard's Wing** is a major area of Castlevania in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.

Dracula first travels back into this part of the castle after following young Trevor, making the modern factory around him transform back through time. By its name sake this wing is most likely the former living area of Walter Bernhard, the castle's original owner. Dracula reacquires his Void Sword here in his first visit, and witnesses the castle's living entity for the first time.

Dracula can access this wing from the Map Rooms and Wolf Alters.
