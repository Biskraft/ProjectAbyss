# Nebula

right|50px

  - Nebula** is a weapon in the *Castlevania* series. It' a holy chain with a will of its own that binds and destroys evil.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
The Nebula is a short chain that has sword-like properties. Its magical strike is a quick stab forward.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The Nebula is extremely long and fast, and will home toward the nearest enemy. Its Critical Attack is a tornado-like effect around Jonathan.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
Soma can wield this weapon. It is found on Chapter 3, *The End of Chaos*, as a treasure drop while playing as Soma. This must be done on Hard difficulty. It is also found on Chapter 11, *The Legend of Fuma* (Hard), as well.
