# Emerald Pins

The **Emerald Pins** are hair pins made of Emerald in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They reduce the amount of damage taken from Thunder-based attacks. They can be purchased from Jacob's shop after completing Laura's "A Pleasant Accessory" quest by giving her an Emerald, a Ruby and a Sapphire.
