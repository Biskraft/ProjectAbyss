# Hanged Man Ring

The **Hanged Man Ring** is a ring in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* and represents the Tarot card "The Hanged Man". In addition to a token DEF boost of 2, it allows its bearer to recover from jumps quickly.

This accessory also allows an easier acquisition of the Pneuma glyph from the blowing wind in an area of Tymeo Mountains.
