# Alexandrite

- Alexandrite** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. This valuable jewel changes color in different light.

Alexandrite by itself does nothing; it is needed in order to complete Laura's "The Job of a Lifetime" quest. If the player succeeds in bringing her one, she will create the Royal Crown, a jewel-encrusted tiara that increases all attributes by 5. The headpiece is then added to the player's inventory free of charge.

Alexandrite can be found inside of green chests in the Training Hall and the Large Cavern.
