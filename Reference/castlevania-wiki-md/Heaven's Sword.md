# Heaven's Sword

right|50px
The **Heaven's Sword** is a sword that can be thrown and then returns.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, equipping two Heaven's Swords allows a special attack by pressing both attack buttons at same time. This attack consist of multiple Heaven's Swords appearing behind Alucard, which then all fly forward ending with a single slash returning. Hits all targets in horizontal range.
