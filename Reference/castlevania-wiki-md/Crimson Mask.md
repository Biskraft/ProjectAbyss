# Crimson Mask

The **Crimson Mask** is the lowest grade mask in found in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It gives Defense + 4 and Strength + 1. This is lower than the Valkyrie Mask, which gives 2 more DEF and 1 more STR.
