# Blue Crystal

right
  - Blues Crystals** are blue-colored crystals that have appeared in *Vampire Killer* and *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. Their use is different in either case.

## Game specific information
### *Vampire Killer*
The **Blue Crystal** (sometimes called **Blue Orb**) takes the place of an Invisibility Potion in this game, in that it renders the player invisible for a short period of time, allowing to pass through enemies without harming them. It is not as powerful as the Saphire Ring, which destroys any enemy the player comes in contact with.

### *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*
The **Blue Crystal** has the ability to drain a waterlogged area in order for the player to pass through. It also has the same ability as the White Crystal, which allows to see invisible platforms. In order to obtain a Blue Crystal, the player must first have bought a White Crystal from a merchant in the town of Jova and then trade it for a Blue Crystal from a Crystal Trader in the town of Aljiba.

The only waterlogged area this item can help to get through is Yuba Lake, which is at the end of the Lower Road to the east of the Aljiba Woods. Pressing down for a few seconds will cause the water to drain and the path to Rover Mansion will be accessible.

This item can be traded up for a Red Crystal from a Crystal Trader from the town of Aldra, which has all the abilities of the White Crystal and the Blue Crystal, plus the ability to summon a transportation tornado spirit at a certain location. The Red Crystal's water drain location can also help in getting to another area later in the game.
