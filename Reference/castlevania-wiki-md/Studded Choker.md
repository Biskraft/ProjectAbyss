# Studded Choker

The **Studded Choker** is a necklace in the *Castlevania* series. It is a choker with metal studs.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The **Studded Choker** is available early in the game. It increases DEF by +1.

### *Castlevania Judgment*
The **Black Studded Choker** and **Crimson Studded Choker** are body accessories that can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character.
