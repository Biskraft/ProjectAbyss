# Vampire's Tower

The **Vampire's Tower** is the thirteenth area Alucard explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. The tower was constructed after the death of Trevor, towering over Carmilla's old tower.

## Trivia
- There are three large statues at the lower portion of the tower: one of a vampire attacking a woman, and two war elephants.
- The stature presiding over the blood orb is the design of weakened Dracula from the beginning of *Lords of Shadow 2*.
