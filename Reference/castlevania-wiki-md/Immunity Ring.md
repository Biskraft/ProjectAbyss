# Immunity Ring

right|73px
The **Immunity Ring** (called **Anti-Poison Ring** in *Lament of Innocence* and **Purity Ring** in *Curse of Darkness*) is a ring that offers protection against Poison. It offers a 75% chance of prevention of poison in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, at least some or total protection in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, and complete immunity in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It replaced the Mirror Pendant found in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.
