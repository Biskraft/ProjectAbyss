# Wolf Form (Lords of Shadow)

- Wolf Form** is an ability Alucard possesses in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*.

Alucard can transform into a bipedal white werewolf. This form is slightly stronger than normal, allowing Alucard to open special doors and inflict more damage with his attacks, at the price of using up most of his magic.

## Trivia
- Wolf Form is Alucard's equivalent of Shadow Magic and the Igneas Blade in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*.
- In *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*, the Wolf Form of a werewolf is not used. Alucard's other form is of a normal white wolf he uses to guide Dracula back and forth between the castle and Castlevania City.
  - The Spectral Wolf in the *Revelations* DLC is basically a hybrid of Wolf Form and Mist Form.
- The Wolf Form's face is based on *Berserk*'s Berserker Armor. Alucard unleashing his inner beast power is also a reference to it.
