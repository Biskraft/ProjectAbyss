# Mind

- Mind** is a statistic found in the *Castlevania* series. It represents the amount of magical defense the character has.

Mind was first introduced in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, and has since then been a staple in metroidvania-style *Castlevania* games. Prior to this, Defense was allowed to work against magical attacks as well.

In *Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia*, for every 2 points a character's Mind increases, they gain +1 damage reduction against magical attacks.

Attacks subject to a character's Mind stat are the following:
- Fire
- Ice
- Thunder
- Holy
- Dark
Category: Statistics
