# Akumajō Dracula XX: Holy Bible

right|250px

Japanese guidebook for *Castlevania: Dracula X*. Its artwork matches that found in the Japanese instruction booklet. It contains some artwork not found in the booklet, which could possibly be simply official art that never made it to the final product. Published by Kodansha.
