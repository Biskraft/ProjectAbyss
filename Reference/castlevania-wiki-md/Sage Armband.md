# Sage Armband

The **Sage Armband** (called the **Philosopher's Bracelet** in the Japanese version) is a bracelet in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* that greatly increases INT +100 at the expense of other stats (-25 STR, DEF and LCK). It is more extreme than the Intelligence Ring (+50 INT, -10 STR and LCK). It is the Intelligence counterpart to the Gambler Armband, Strength Armband, and Defense Armband.
