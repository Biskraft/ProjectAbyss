# Minerva Mask

The Minerva Mask is the best mask found in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.  It gives a Defense boost of +8 and a Strength and Mind boost of +3.  This is 2 more DEF and 1 more STR than the Valkyrie Mask, which doesn't give any MND boost.  It can be found in rare chests from the Large Cavern.

It is a Shanoa-exclusive item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and the most rare Mask in the game.
