# Ring of Thunder

right|70px

The **Ring of Thunder** (called the **Storm Ring** in *Curse of Darkness* and **Ring of Depth Charge** in the Japanese versions) is a ring that offers resistance to Thunder based attacks. It appears in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
