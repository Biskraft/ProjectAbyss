# 13th Street

- 13th Street** is a location in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. This stage reuses most sprites from the City of Haze.

## Overview
13th Street is another of Brauner's paintings that needs to be destroyed in order to enter his Lost Gallery. At the beginning, both Jonathan and Charlotte must use the power granted by the Strength Glove to stop a locomotive and continue into the city.

It is the alternate version of City of Haze and its boss is the Werewolf.

## Notes
- Completing 13th Street makes Wind's "The Nest of Evil" quest available.
- In Richter, Sisters and Old Axe Armor Modes, an additional Bone Ark and an additional Vapula will appear on the train tracks where the train was in Normal Mode.
