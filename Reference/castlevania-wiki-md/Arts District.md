# Arts District

right|300px

The **Arts' District** is one of the major areas of the modern city in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. This area contains places like the old Cathedral where Dracula first awakes in.

The District also houses the library where Abaddon is fought, as well as Victor Belmont's church.

## Trivia
- Early in the game, after Dracula leaves the Cathedral and starts following young Trevor on the street in front of the alley way where Dracula encounters the Satan's Soldier. A street prostitute with pink hair can be seen walking up and down the street. She only appears during this time and isn't encountered anywhere else in the game, nor does her design model appear in any of the game's concept art.
