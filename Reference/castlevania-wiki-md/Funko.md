# Funko

- Funko Inc.** is an American company that manufactures licensed pop culture collectibles, best known for its licensed vinyl figurines and bobbleheads. Funko's *Pop! Vinyl* line are figures modeled in a style similar to the Japanese super deformed style, typically depicting licensed characters from pop culture franchises.

## *Castlevania* related products
A line of five *Pop! Vinyls* was announced by Funko on 2019's New York Toy Fair based on characters from the first season of Netflix's *Castlevania* animated series. These include Dracula, Trevor Belmont, Sypha Belnades, Alucard and Blue Fangs.
