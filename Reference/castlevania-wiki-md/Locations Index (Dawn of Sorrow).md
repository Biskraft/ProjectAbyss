An extra note is the Boss </span>Abaddon<span style="color:rgb(58,58,58);line-height:20px;text-align:-webkit-auto;"> who is found in the Abyss is a reference to the Christian Bible's Book of Revelation regarding Abaddon.</span> |


## Background
This castle was built by the With Light sect (possibly the people who live in the village) led by Celia Fortner, who seeks to create a new Dark Lord to replace and claim Dracula's power.
