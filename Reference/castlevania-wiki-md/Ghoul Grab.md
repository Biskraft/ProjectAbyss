# Ghoul Grab

The **Ghoul Grab** is an action that can be performed by Laura in Reverie. By pressing the [Grab] button, Laura can grab enemies (specifically Ghouls). She will then proceed to drain their blood when the player repeatedly presses the Y button (Xbox360 and PC versions), replenishing her health. Be careful, as the ghoul will eventually explode and damage Laura if you feed for too long.
