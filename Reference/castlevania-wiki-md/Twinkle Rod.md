# Twinkle Rod

right|200px
  - Twinkle Rod** is a very special Mage-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It's a level 4 Innocent Devil and can learn Twinkle Star and Dancing Stars. It can be obtained from a Goat Head Innocent Devil using white crystals.

## Trivia
- This is the only Mage-Type that features a unique appearance compared to the other evolutions. It is also the only female Mage-Type which is further revealed in the description of the Twinkle Shot ability - *Pulls a star out of orbit & down onto foes' heads. Apparently only female mages can perform the spell*.
