# Dean Silva

is the lead for the novel *Akumajō Dracula: Akuma no Chi Chi no Akumu*. He is a bishop who becomes a missionary under orders of the Archbishop. He is Elizabeth Arye's boyfriend and struggles between a "belief in God" and a "girlfriend who became a vampire".
