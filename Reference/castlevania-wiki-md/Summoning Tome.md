# Summoning Tome

The **Summoning Tome** is a Spell Book in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. When combined with each of the various subweapons found in the game, it allows Juste to summon a different monster to come to his aid. These are in general powerful clear screen attacks, although some may be used to heal him or even summon the creature as a familiar to follow and support him.
