# Alucart

"**Alucart**" is a playoff on the name Alucard. In several games, Alucard's equipment, which are among the best to be found, have the name "Alucard" in it. Obtaining an item with the name "Alucart" in it may initially elicit some excitement, but ultimately disappointment when it is realized that it is not the real thing. There are three pieces of Alucart gear: the Alucart Sword, Alucart Shield and Alucart Mail, which correspond with the Alucard Sword, Alucard Shield and Alucard Mail.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
In *Symphony of the Night*, Alucard loses most of his high grade "Alucard" gear to Death early in the game. The player may then search the castle in hopes of re-obtaining it. The player may then find all of the "Alucart" gear hidden in an area above the room of clocks in the Marble Gallery, where Maria is first encountered. This gear turns out to be mostly useless, though. While they don't have the worst statistics found in the game, they are likely well below what the player has already obtained by the time they find it. The player will also find they don't have the special properties that the true Alucard gear has (such as the Alucard Sword's warping attack, the Alucard Shield's heart recovery when blocking and its Shield Rod spell, or the Alucard Mail's resistance to Fire, Ice and Thunder). On the other hand, Alucard gets a gigantic LCK boost of +30 by equipping all of the Alucart gear. His name will then be replaced as "Alucart" in the pause menu screen.


### *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*
In *Curse of Darkness*, the Alucart Mail can be created via Weapon Combining by adding a Midas' Gold to a Half Plate, which significantly reduces its DEF. It is a "trap" combination devised to waste the extremely rare Midas' Gold and creating it should be avoided unless looking on acquiring a complete item set. Unfortunately, the Alucard Mail is not obtainable in this game.


### *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*
In *Encore of the Night*, the "Alucart" gear is not so much useless, as it is early useful gear to have. Obtaining the gear is welcome, as it is most likely a little more powerful than what was being worn before. Getting all three pieces completes the **Family Heirlooms** set, which provides a Max HP boost of 30.


### *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
In *Harmony of Despair*, Alucard starts his quest with the Alucart Sword equipped. He can soon obtain the Alucart Shield by purchasing it from the shop. The Alucart Mail does not make an appearance in this game and there is no "Alucart" status LCK bonus to be had.
