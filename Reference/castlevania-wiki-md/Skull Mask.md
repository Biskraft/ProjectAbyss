# Skull Mask

right|50px

The **Skull Mask** is a mask designed to resemble a human skull. Like other "skull" items, it's cursed and reduces luck. Jonathan can wear it in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, where it gives him a great deal of Defense (by an unlucky +13), but reduces Intelligence, Mind and Luck by -3. This is opposed to Jonathan's Stone Mask (which increases Luck by +1, along with DEF +3) and Ballroom Masque (which increases INT by +1, along with DEF +4).
