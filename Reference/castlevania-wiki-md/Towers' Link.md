# Towers' Link

The **Towers' Link** is the third area Alucard explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*, linking the two main towers of the castle. It is here that the Daemon Lord is fought for the first time, although the fight is not intended to be won. After Alucard is defeated, he is thrown off the tower and into the water of the Abandoned Mine. When first entering this area, it is not shown on the map except the area with the water. The water comes from the Vertical Prison overflowing and Simon sees Alucard falling down. It is called the Towers' Link because there are large really heavy looking chains that hold the main tower (connected to resurrection room) and the other tower (connected to the Belfry). 30 years ago, this area never existed until perhaps it was a long way for monsters or Brotherhood knights to travel underground (abandoned mine) all the way up to the mirror of fate.
