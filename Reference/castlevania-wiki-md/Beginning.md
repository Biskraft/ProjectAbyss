# Beginning

- Beginning** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club. Its first appearance was in *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* during the first stage. It is considered as *Dracula's Curse* and Trevor Belmont's main theme and many arrangements of it have appeared throughout the *Castlevania* series.

## Other usages
  - Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* - The theme when fighting the Water Dragon.
  - Monster Retsuden ORECA BATTLE* - The theme when fighting Dracula.

## Trivia
- The background music for the first stage in *Kid Dracula* (Famicom and Game Boy versions) uses a couple of arranged sections of "Beginning".
