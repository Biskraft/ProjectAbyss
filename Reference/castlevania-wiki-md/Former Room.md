# Former Room

- Former Room** is a music theme composed by Sanoppi for *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*. It is the background theme that plays in the level sections preceding the bosses, excepted for the last level.

The music reappears for the same function in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*, however it replaces Poison Mind during the fight against the demons summoned by Shaft.
