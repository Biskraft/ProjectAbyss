# Prologue ~Endless Sorrow~

- Prologue ~Endless Sorrow~** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is played in the introduction cutscene.

A longest version appears as bonus track in the *Curse of Darkness Original Soundtrack*.
