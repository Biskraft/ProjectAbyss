# Photo

- Photos** are images captured with a Camera. They appear in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* and are requested by Marcel to complete all three of his quests. The three photos requested are:
#**Photo 1** - Take a picture of Cave Troll.
#**Photo 2** - Snap a shot of the elusive Jersey Devil.
#**Photo 3** - Track down the Yeti and take a picture.

If Shanoa wasn't able to capture a good image of a creature by centering it correctly inside the camera's 2x2 grid, then she'll get a **Poor Photo**, which she won't be able to sell, use or drop, and must remain in her inventory for the rest of the game.
