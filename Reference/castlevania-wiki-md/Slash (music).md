# Slash (music)

- Slash** is a music theme composed by Jigokuguruma Nakamura and introduced in the 1993 NEC PC Engine game *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*. It is the background theme for Stage 4'. It is also the theme for Maria Renard in the 2008 Nintendo Wii game *Castlevania Judgment*.
