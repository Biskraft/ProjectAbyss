# Draupnir

The **Draupnir** is an arm ring that increases ATK by +5 (about 25% of Leon's attack) in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is the only accessory that increases attack in this game. It is located behind the "Black Turtle" door at bottom level of the Dark Palace of Waterfalls. It needs the Black Turtle Key, found in the Ghostly Theatre, to be reached.
