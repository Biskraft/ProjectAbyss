  - Sandy Grave** is a location in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. This stage closely follows an Egyptian theme.

## Overview
The stage starts outside of a large pyramid, with the character exploring in, out and below the pyramid itself. The main enemies in the stage are Mummies, Red Skeletons and Ghosts. The boss, Astarte, is located at the very peak of the pyramid. Astarte resembles a common stereotype of the Egyptian queen Cleopatra.
