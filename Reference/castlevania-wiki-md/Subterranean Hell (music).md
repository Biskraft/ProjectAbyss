  - Subterranean Hell** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the background theme for the titular area in With Light's headquarters.

## Trivia
- The song's title and the name of the area itself is an allusion to its labyrinth-like environment. While Hell is generally associated with the molten realm that punishes those who have sinned, it is also a word used to describe torment and hardship. The latter description fits the environmental hazards and bestiary that are encountered throughout the cavern.
- The track is heavily arranged in the final song to be featured in the game's soundtrack "Amber Scenery".
