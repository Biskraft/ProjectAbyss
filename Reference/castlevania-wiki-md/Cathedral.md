# Cathedral

thumb|264px

The **Cathedral** is the old church Dracula awakens in the modern city. Located in the Arts District in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.

The inner throne is first seen in the *Lords of Shadow* epilogue, when Zobek first reunites with the Prince of Darkness. The cathedral is where Dracula faces Victor Belmont, and where Satan is summoned.

After the release of the virus, Victor led uninfected citizens to take refuge in the catacombs below.

Dracula's tomb and the window room throne can be explored in the *Revelations* DLC. Alucard battles Zobek's Lieutenant in the lower part of the church.

## Trivia
