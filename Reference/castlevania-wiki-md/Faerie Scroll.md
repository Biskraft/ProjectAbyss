# Faerie Scroll

right|50px

The **Faerie Scroll** is a relic that when activated allows the player to see the name of an enemy on the screen when they attack it.

## Overview
Its first appearance took place in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which was also the first game that showed the name of the enemy when it was attacked. It is called a **Fairy Journal** in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* and the **Book of Spirits** does the same thing in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. This ability is always turned on without the need to collect the relic in these games for alternate characters. It is also an ability that is always on in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, and *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*.
