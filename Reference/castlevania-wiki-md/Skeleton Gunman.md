# Skeleton Gunman

The **Skeleton Gunman**, also known as **Bone Musket**, is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. They are skeletons carrying muskets. They often travel in groups of three so that one is always firing, one is at the ready, and the other is reloading. They were replaced with Hyenas in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.
