# Big Battle

- Big Battle** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*. It plays during the second and third phases of the final battle against Dracula (with the latter being at a faster pace).

An arranged version of this song appeared later in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* for the boss fights against the Dragon Zombies, Death, Camilla and Hugh Baldwin.
