# Dove

right|198px
  - Doves** are Maria Renard's main attack weapon in most games she has appeared in. Two doves are released and attack forward. In *The Dracula X Chronicles* version of *Symphony of the Night*, her main attack birds behave like doves do, but appear as Owls.

Doves were able to be utilized by Maria's ancestors, who allowed Desmond Belmont to use them as a sub-weapon.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*
Desmond can find the **Doves** sub-weapon at the end of the upper broken bridge in the Castle Grounds. He uses them differently than Maria does; he releases two doves which flutter forward and downward and fly off the end of the screen, or downward and forward.
