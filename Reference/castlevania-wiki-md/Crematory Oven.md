The **Crematory Oven** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the fourth and final stage of Chapter 10.

## Zobek's journal
:*Fueled by the very flames of hell itself, the crematory oven is where the Gravedigger does his ghastly work. A powerful demon whose mighty shovel can cleave a man in two with one stroke. The Gravedigger will rip the very soul from Gabriel, given half the chance, yet I sense that our hero is more than a match for this creature. This could be interesting indeed. I sense Gabriel’s rage welling up within him and I wonder who is the more terrible, our hero or the demon?*
