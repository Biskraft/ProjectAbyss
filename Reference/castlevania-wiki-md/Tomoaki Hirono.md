# Tomoaki Hirono

- Tomoaki Hirono** (also known as **Gekidan Record** or **T-Hironois**) is a Japanese game composer. He is mostly known by his work with Bemani.

He worked on a number of music track arrangements for Castlevania: Harmony of Despair Original Soundtrack.
