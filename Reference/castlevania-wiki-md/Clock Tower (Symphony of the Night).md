The  appears in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is accessible from the Outer Wall section and leads to the Castle Keep. Its inverted castle counterpart is the Reverse Clock Tower. This area's theme music is The Tragic Prince.

## *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The two SoN Clock Towers share a common secret: four gears are located in the main tower, two in each chamber. Hitting each of these until they make a small click rather than a loud clank will raise the gate in the lower-left of the tower (upper-left in the inverted version). In the regular Clock Tower this allows access to a room containing a Steel Helm, Gold Plate and a Star Flail.

There is a destroyable wall in the upper-right of the left-hand room that leads through a chamber guarded by Flea Armors to a larger one containing an HP Max up and a Heart Max Up.

The room with the swinging pendulums on the left side of the area has a destroyable wall in the lower-left, a slab with obvious seams at the top and bottom. Behind this is the Healing Mail.
