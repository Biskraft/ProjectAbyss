  - Prologue ~ Mina's Theme** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is Mina Hakuba's theme for the game and is played whenever Soma Cruz speaks with her in the hub located in the Castle Corridor.

The track also plays during the game's introduction.
