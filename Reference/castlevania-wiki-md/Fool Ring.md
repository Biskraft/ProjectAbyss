# Fool Ring

The **Fool Ring** is an accessory in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a ring that represents the Tarot card "The Fool". It increases LCK by 7, but decreases STR, CON, INT and MND, each by 3.

It can be obtained in a normal game as soon as the Ordinary Rock is obtained at the end of the Skeleton Cave (and with it, the ability to double jump). The ring can then be acquired in the Monastery inside a treasure chest located on a high platform which was previously impossible to reach. The chest is in the room to the left from the tall vertical room where the Magnes glyph was previously acquired (the entrance is at the top left).
