# Epilogue (Aria of Sorrow)

- Epilogue** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the theme that plays during the game's best ending where Soma Cruz and Mina Hakuba are returned to the latter's family shrine after the destruction of Dracula's Castle.

## Trivia
- The track appears to be a continuation of Purification ~ Ending, since much of its melody remains the same. An arrangement version of Bloody Tears composed by Michiru Yamane is also played in the beginning of the track.
