# Peter Stormare

- Rolf Peter Ingvar Storm** (born August 27th, 1953), known professionally as **Peter Stormare**, is a Swedish actor, voice actor, theatre director, playwright and musician active in the United States. He is best known for playing the roles of Gaear Grimsrud in the 1996 film *Fargo*, Uli Kunkel / Karl Hungus in *The Big Lebowski*, John Abruzzi in the Fox TV series *Prison Break*, and Ingmar in the comedy web television series *Swedish Dicks*. Stormare also voiced Count Dracula (who appeared at a party using the alias "Alucard") in the animated film *The Batman vs. Dracula*.

He voices Godbrand in the *Castlevania* animated series.
