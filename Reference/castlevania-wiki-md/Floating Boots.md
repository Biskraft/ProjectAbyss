# Floating Boots

- Floating Boots** are footwear from *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. If Juste equips these boots, he can float in the air after performing a jump. They are dropped by the Pike Master. If Juste stays suspended in the air without moving, while having the boots equipped, he will descend slowly until he touches the ground. Since directional buttons change to control the boots, Juste cannot use subweapons while floating. Also Juste cannot Dash or Backdash while wearing the boots.
