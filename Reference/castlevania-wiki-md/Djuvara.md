# Djuvara

- Mrs. Djuvara** is a minor character in the *Castlevania* animated series. She's an old lady resident of the town of Lupu and a patient of Lisa. She seems not to completely agree with the cruel methods The Church has recently adopted.

She comes to the remains of Lisa's house to pay her respects after the Church took her away to be burned at the stake. Because of her kindness, Dracula spares her and allows her family to leave Wallachia.
