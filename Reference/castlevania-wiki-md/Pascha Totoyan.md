# Pascha Totoyan

- Pascha Totoyan** is a character in the graphic novel *Castlevania: The Belmont Legacy*, published by IDW. She is the granddaughter of Gaspar Totoyan and a skilled warrior.

## Plot
Pascha brings the Vampire Killer to give it to Christopher Belmont and participates in the siege on Dracula's Castle. She's later forced to kill her vampirized brother and grandfather.

With her only family now gone, she joins the Belmont household.
