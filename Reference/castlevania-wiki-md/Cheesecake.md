# Cheesecake

The **Cheesecake** is a food item that recovers health in the *Castlevania* series.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
Gives the player 14 HP, that's two more HP than a Tart and one less than an Ice Cream. It is dropped by the Hunting Girl.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Called **NY Cheesecake**. It can be obtained by dropping a $10 gold coin into the register at the City of Haze's bakery, which is harder to get than a Sachertorte ($1), but easier than a Mille-feuille ($50).

### *Castlevania Judgment*
The **Creamy Cheesecake** and **Mixed-Berry Pie** are head accessories that can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character.
