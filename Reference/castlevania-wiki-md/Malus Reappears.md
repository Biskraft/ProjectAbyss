# Malus Reappears

- Malus Reappears** is a music track that is featured in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.

It is identified as the background theme that plays when Malus is encountered a second time during the player's journey in Dracula's Castle.

The music [Demon Salesman](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mX_TWRnujjc) is an integral part of this track, and the music's second part can also be heard during the bad ending with Carrie.
