# Bardiche

The **Bardiche** is a dual-handed war axe with a long blade, which make it excellent for attacking enemies at a distance with large swinging arcs. In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, Bardiche can be created by combining the Bullova and Morgenstern.

## History
While the bardiche is classified as a polearm, it is indeed the shortest. The use of two hands was required to unleash the full power of its large axe head. The bardiche was believed to be developed from the broadaxes of Scandinavia, and later used as rests for early firearms when firing. Although Generally respected by infantrymen for its quickness and better wielding in tight spaces, the Bardiche lacked the basics of a real polearm.
