# Artemisian Ring

- Artemisian Ring** (also called the **Moon Ring**) is a ring that improves status during night time (6pm to 6am based on the system clock). It is the night-time counterpart to the Apollonian Ring (also called Sun Ring), which give the exact same amount of stats, but during the daytime. It has a similar effect as the Moonstone does from *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* is shown to have a greater effect.

The ring first appeared in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, where it is called the Moon Ring, after the tarot card [The Moon](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Moon_(Tarot_card)).

Its second appearance was as a green/purple/gold chest drop (Normal mode only) in the The One Who Is Many downloadable stage of *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, where it was renamed the Artemisian Ring. It and its counterpart, the Apollonian Ring, are the only accessories from Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia to appear in this game. During the day, it increases defense by 1. During the night, it is has a much increased effect, increasing all of the stats by <span style="font-style: normal; ">4 (STR, CON, INT, MND, LCK). (STR raises ATK and CON raises DEF)</span>

The Artemisian Ring is the second item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* related to the Greek goddess , the twin of Apollo and a moon goddess. The other item is the Artemis Shoes.
