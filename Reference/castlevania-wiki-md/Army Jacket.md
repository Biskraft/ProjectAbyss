# Army Jacket

An **Army Jacket** (also known as **Battle Jacket** or **Military Wear**) is woven with a special fabric that resists cuts. This was first introduced in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*.

The **Battle Jacket** from *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* is found in the Nation of Fools.

The body gear **Military Wear** appears in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* with the same function of resisting Slash attacks.
