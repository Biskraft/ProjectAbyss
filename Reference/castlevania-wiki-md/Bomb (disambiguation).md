# Bomb (disambiguation)

- Bomb** may refer to one of the following:
    - Bomb** - An explosive sub-weapon.
    - Bomb (Symphony of the Night)** - An one-time use weapon in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
      - Dynamite** - A projectile in the same game.
    - Fire Bomb** - The censored name of the Holy Water in the Western version of *Castlevania* and *Super Castlevania IV*.
