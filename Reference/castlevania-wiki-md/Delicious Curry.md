# Delicious Curry

right|212px

  - Delicious Curry** is a signature attack of the Waiter Skeleton which is found in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. The skeleton will toss a bowl of curry at Soma, which will damage him upon contact. Soma is able to gain this technique for himself by absorbing the Waiter Skeleton's soul in both games. He will toss a bowl of curry onto the ground, which will stay there for a while and damage any enemy that comes in contact with it.

In *Dawn of Sorrow*, Soma is also able to lure out the Yeti from the background in the very first room of the Lost Village by tossing a plate of curry to the ground.
