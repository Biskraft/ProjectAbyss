# Infant Fairy

right|200px

  - Infant Fairy** is the first evolution of the Fairy-Type Innocent Devil and is the first overall Innocent Devil found in the game. It evolves into a Herbest or a Leaffle, depending on what type of weapon is used, and has two abilities: Unlock, which opens treasure chests which may contain valuable items, and Heal, which gives Hector 20 HP and may become crucial when in low health.
