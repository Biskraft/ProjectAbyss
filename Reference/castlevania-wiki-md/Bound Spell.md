# Bound Spell

- Bound Spells** are spells which have been captured into the Book of Binding by Charlotte Aulin in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. They can be learned from enemies by blocking an enemies magical attack by using an energy shield (which is also referred to as "Bound Spells" in the game). Capturing a spell that is already acquired increases the power of that spell and also the attack power of the Book of Binding, which will change forms over time.

This shield can also block physical projectiles and is used as Charlotte's Personal Skill. This shield costs 10 MP when first used, and then slowly drains MP over time as it is used.

Once captured, Bound Spells can be used as Hunter Skills and assigned to the four Hunter Skill button sequences.
