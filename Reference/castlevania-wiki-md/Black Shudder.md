  - Black Shutter** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the background theme that plays during two separate instances where Soma Cruz encounters two of the Dark Lord Candidates in With Light's headquarters.

The theme would play shortly before the encounters escalate into a boss fight with both respective men.
