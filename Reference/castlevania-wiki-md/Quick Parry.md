# Quick Parry

- Quick Parry**, or **Quick Bullet** is is an ability in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over the Dead Warrior's soul, and it allows Soma to interrupt his weapon attack mid-animation with his currently equipped bullet soul (UP + B). It only works while standing.

It is generally more useful with slow weapons, does not work with throwing weapons in *Dawn of Sorrow*. And even if certain souls, like Blood Sucker, successfully interrupt an attack, they will not follow up with their own.


## Trivia
- In *Aria of Sorrow*, it's game description "*Deflect normal attacks by pressing UP + B.*" is a mistranslation. It should read, "*Interrupt normal attacks by pressing UP + B.*".
