 is a Japanese comic artist. She worked on the *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* manga Vol. 1 and 2 before it was canceled.

Ayami Kojima also produced a pre-order bonus *Curse of Darkness* short manga depicting many storyline details such as Rosalee's death or the sequence of battles differently from Sasakura's story, although she commented that Sasakura's version —as the most complete and detailed— should be considered the main canonical reference for the events previous to the plot of the game.
