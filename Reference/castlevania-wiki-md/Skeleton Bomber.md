## Description
The Skeleton Bomber is the first enemy encountered in the game. Much like any basic Skeleton, it paces back and forth, and occasionally throws a bomb in an arc. These projectiles only explode on contact and inflict Fire damage in a small radius.

Even though they are one of the weakest enemies in the game, some care should be taken when approaching them (especially if they are standing on a higher ledge), as they often throw their bombs even before they are onscreen.

They have a very high chance of dropping the Salamander card (20%), which is likely the first DSS card the player will obtain in the game, and which allows combinations imbued with the Fire element.
