# Minerva Mail

The **Minerva Mail** is an armor blessed by a war goddess that prevents the effects of Curse.

In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, it is a reward for completing the Large Cavern, as are the other Minerva items.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, the armor, along with the other Minerva items, can only be obtained after defeating Dracula on Hard mode.
