# Yoko Belnades/Dawn of Sorrow

right|250px

  - Yoko Belnades** appears again in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. She encounters Soma along with Julius over a bridge in The Lost Village. After Julius performs a jump across the chasm to the other side, Yoko is forced to turn back with Soma. Soma escorts her to a safe place in the Lost Village were she teaches Soma how to use Magic Seals.

She opens a shop of sorts in the Lost Village afterward where Soma can synthesize his weapons into more powerful forms by using the souls of the monsters he gains dominance over. Furthermore, she can release those same souls from Soma's dominance, allowing Soma to change their effects, decrease their power, and allow him to gather new ones.

## Julius Mode
In Julius Mode, Yoko appears as a playable character who can join with Julius and Alucard in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. She attacks using his staff, each blow to enemies restores some energy. The amount of HP restored increases with the character level.

She can also perform the Holy Flame, Blue Splash and Holy Lightning, the same spells used by his ancestor Sypha Belnades.
