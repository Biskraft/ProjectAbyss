The  is an area from *Akumajō Dracula X: Nocturne in the Moonlight* (Saturn).

## Overview
The entrance to this area is in other versions of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, but was incomplete and contained only a save point. In this exclusive version of the game, it can be accessed through the castle's entrance in an underground passageway.

The corresponding area in the Reverse Castle is the Hell Garden.
