# Scarf

A plain **Scarf** found in *Castlevania*, which is less effective than a Red Scarf.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, it gives a DEF boost of +2, which is considerably less effective than the Red Scarf, which also gives an Attack, STR, and CON boost of +1.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it gives a DEF boost of +1 and a CON boost of +2. This is also outclassed by the Red Scarf, which gives +2 and +5 of these attributes respectively.
