# Strength Booster

right
  - Strength Booster** is a Guardian Soul gained from a Great Armor in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. When it is activated, Soma is granted a STR increase with a 60 MP consumption rate. The description is poorly worded as it implies this soul is far better than it actually is; it does not increase Soma's strength *by* 120%, but *to* 120%, making him only 20% stronger than usual. This is a very poor boost for such a costly soul.
