  - The Legend of Fuma** is the fifth DLC and the eleventh overall stage of *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, released on February 2, 2011 for 240 MSP. It is based on the 1987 Konami game *Getsu Fūma Den*.

Its boss is Ryukotsuki (literally "Dragon Bone Demon"). Its default stage music is Go! Getsu Fuma from the original game and the boss theme is Ryukotsuki. Its hidden item is the Facade Card. The original game's hero, Getsu Fuma, is also available as a separate DLC playable character.
