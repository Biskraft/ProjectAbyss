# Akumajo Special: Boku Dracula-kun SOUNDTRACKS

- Akumajo Special: Boku Dracula-kun SOUNDTRACKS** contains every background theme and bonus track from *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*, which were composed by Konami Kukeiha Club (by Satoko Miyawaki and Shinji Tasaka). This CD was released by EGG MUSIC, a division of D4 Enterprise, on August 14, 2014 exclusively in Japan.
