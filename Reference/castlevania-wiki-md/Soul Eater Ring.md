# Soul Eater Ring

right|50px

The **Soul Eater Ring** is a ring that increases the rate that souls appear in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It also gives a LCK boost of +5. It can be purchased from Hammer's shop for 300,000G in both games.

## Availability
To obtain it, the player may opt to grind 9 Muramasas from Lubicants and sell them to obtain 243,000G, then equip Tsuchinoko's soul, V.I.P., which will drop the Soul Eater Ring's price at Hammer's shop to 240,000G, at which point it can be afforded.

Naturally, there are many other methods to obtain enough gold to purchase the ring, but in general the player will still need V.I.P in order to afford it in a reasonable amount of time. Regardless, the player must expect to do a lot of item farming.

If the player only needs a few thousands more in order to afford the ring, the Giant Skeleton corridor in The Arena can be used to reliably get $1,000 each time it is explored. However, this will be potentially even more time consuming to complete over 200 times than farming Lubicant.
