# Licorices

Licorices are common desserts in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

The **Licorice** recovers 10 HP and MP and increase INT by 3, but reduces LCK by 3.

The **Red Licorice** recovers 15 HP and MP and increase INT by 5, but reduces LCK by 5. It can be considered an upgrade to the Licorice.
