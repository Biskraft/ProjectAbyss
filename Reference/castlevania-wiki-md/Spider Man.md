# Spider Man

- Spider Man** is an enemy in *Konami Wai Wai World* and *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*.

## Appearances
### *Konami Wai Wai World*
In *Konami Wai Wai World*, the **Spider Man** (or  in the Japanese version) appears as a naked man hanging down from a web string that comes out of his rear end, with his arms and legs positioned in such a way that they resemble the front and rear legs of a spider on its web. His behavior is a parody of the Spider enemies found in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.


### *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*
In *Boku Dracula-kun*, **Spider Man** is outright a parody of the iconic Marvel Comics eponymous superhero character. As such, his costume is very similar, although instead of a red mask, he wears a blue one. He is able to scale tall buildings, and once he gets to the top, he jumps around a bit.

## Trivia
- The design of this enemy in *Konami Wai Wai World* appears to be based on a specific design of the Arachne enemy from the *Shin Megami Tensei* series, which in turn is based on the mythological Arachne of Greek origins.
