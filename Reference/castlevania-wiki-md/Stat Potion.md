# Stat Potion

- Stat Potions** are actually one-use pills that temporary raise Alucard's statistics in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*.

## *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, the given statistic is raised for about a minute and a half.


## *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*
Most Attribute Potions in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* raise their given statistic for until the end of a battle. Attack Potion only remains until the end of the attack (when the hourglass if flipped) though.


Category: Stat Potions
