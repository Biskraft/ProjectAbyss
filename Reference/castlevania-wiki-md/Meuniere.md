# Meuniere

- Meuniere** (or **Salmon Meuniere**) is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It is a seafood dish flavored with curry sauce found in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
