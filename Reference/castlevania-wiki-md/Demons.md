# Demons

- Demons** are common enemies found in the *Castlevania* series. They are usually high-ranking, powerful enemies in the games they are encountered in. As with Golems and large Armors, there are usually multiple variations of them in a game.

The "tiny devil" family of demons consists of Mini Devils and Imps. The "elemental demons" family consists of many demons which manipulate the elements (fire, ice, thunder, earth, etc.). Finally, the "devil" family consists of powerful demons: the Devil, the Flame Demon, the Arc Demon and the Demon Lord, in order of rank. Other demons do not belong in any class. The male, horned, winged demon is called the Classic Demon.
