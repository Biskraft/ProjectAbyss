# Gold Circlet

The **Gold Circlet** is a circlet made of gold (or silver in the Japanese version, where it is called the **Silver Circlet**) that appears in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. In addition to protecting from Holy based attacks, it will actually heal the player when they receive an Holy-based hit. It also gives an INT boost of 6, which is one more than a normal Circlet, but one less than a Coral Circlet.
