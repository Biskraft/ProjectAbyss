# Arcade Stage 3

- Stage 3** is the third stage in *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade*. It takes place in areas beneath Dracula's Castle and consists of the **Underground**, **Underground Waterways** and the **Lowest Floor**.

## Overview
The first Underground section is an old dungeon inhabited by the Specters and Skeletons of those who had been imprisoned there. Some skeletons can hop around and others can climb the ceiling. Beneath this area is the Underground Waterway, where the Minotaur is encountered. The area after that is infested with Mermen, followed by the Water Dragon end boss (which is assisted by Bats, Mermen and falling stalactites). After defeating the Water Dragon, the water drains, revealing a stairway that leads to the outer wall of the castle.
