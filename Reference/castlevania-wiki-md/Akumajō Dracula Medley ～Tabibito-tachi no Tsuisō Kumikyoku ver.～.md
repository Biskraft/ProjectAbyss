# Akumajō Dracula Medley ～Tabibito-tachi no Tsuisō Kumikyoku ver.～

{{Nihongo|**Akumajō Dracula Medley ～Tabibito-tachi no Tsuisō Kumikyoku ver.～**|悪魔城ドラキュラメドレー ～旅人たちの追想組曲 ver.～|Akumajō Dorakyura Medorē ～Tabibito-tachi no Tsuisō Kumikyoku ver.～|lit. "Demon Castle Dracula Medley ～The Travelers' Memories Suite ver.～"}} is an orchestra arrange album from different *Castlevania* games. It was released on January 23, 2018.

## Additional information
- Track 1 is from *Super Castlevania IV*
- Track 2 is from *Castlevania*
- Track 3 is from *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*
- Track 4 is from *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*
- Tracks 5 & 6 are from *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
- Track 7 is from *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*
- Tracks 8, 9 & 10 are from *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*

## Trivia
- The subtitle roughly translates as "The Travelers' Memories".
- The medley formed part of an event by the name "The Travelers' Memories" held at the Tokyo Metropolitan Theatre Concert Hall on August 26, 2017, in which there were also played medleys from *Chrono Trigger*, *Tales of Destiny*, *Final Fantasy X*, *Tales of Destiny 2*, *Final Fantasy VII*, *Tales of the Abyss*, *Undertale*, *Chrono Cross*, *MOTHER2* (*Earthbound*), *The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past* and the first two *Suikoden* games.
