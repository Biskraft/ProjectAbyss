# Orange

The  (called a **Mandarin** in the Japanese version, but referred to as a "Japanese tangerine" in the North American description) is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.

It recovers 6 HP, being one more HP than the Grapes and one less than the Banana.

It can only be obtained by using a Meal Ticket.
