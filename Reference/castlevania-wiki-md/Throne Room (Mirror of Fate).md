# Throne Room (Mirror of Fate)

right|350px

The **Throne Room** is the eighteenth and final area Simon explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*, although it is referred to as **????** in the loading screen. During Simon's exploration, the area is disguised by the Succubus to appear as a realm based off of her victim's desires, and it is here where she is fought, but after her defeat, the realm dissolves to reveal that Simon was actually in the throne room.

The Throne Room is also the fifteenth and final area that Alucard explores in the game. During his journey, it is here that Dracula is fought for the first time, with the aid of Simon.
