# Throwing Sword

- Throwing Swords** are swords or other bladed weapons that are normally thrown and then return without incurring any cost. The Heaven's Sword is the most common throwing sword.

## Symphony of the Night
Throwing Swords were known as "Throw 1" items in the North American version of Castlevania: Symphony of the Night.  


## Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin
The Heaven's Sword makes its second appearance in this game.

## Harmony of Despair
The Heaven's Sword makes its third appearance in this game and is the only throwing sword. Soma's Chakram is considered a Firearm in this game.
