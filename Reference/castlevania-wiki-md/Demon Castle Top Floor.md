# Demon Castle Top Floor

- Demon Castle Top Floor** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the Top Floor's background theme.

An arranged version of the track called **Holy Cross Obsessed by the Moon** was later included in Castlevania: Lament of Innocence Original Soundtrack.
