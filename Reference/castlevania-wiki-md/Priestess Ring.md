# Priestess Ring

The Priestess Ring the ring of the Tarot personna, [The Priestess](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_High_Priestess), in Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia that increases defensive statistics (Constitution and Mind by 5) at the lesser expense of their correlating offensive statistics (Strength and Intelligence by 2).  It is the counterpart to the Empress Ring.
