# Pumpkin Mace

right|73px

The **Pumpkin Mace** is a one-handed Axe-type weapon in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is required to get access to the Pumpkin-Type Innocent Devil. The weapon itself is very weak, its only purpose is to awaken the Pumpkin I.D.

It can be crafted by combining the Piko Piko Hammer and the Pumpkin Head, both of which require extremely rare items to make on their own, like at least two Miracle Eggs (obtained from Golden Bones and Evil Cores) and Jade (obtained only from Assassin Zombies). Once the player has the Pumpkin Mace, they need to hit the Innocent Devil with the weapon in order to awaken it.
