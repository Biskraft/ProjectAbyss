# Beef

- Beef** (referred to as **Sirloin** in the North American version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*) is one of the highest recovering Meats found in *Castlevania*.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it recovers a full 100 HP, which is 20 more than a Turkey. This is tied with Sushi for the food item that recovers the most amount of health.
