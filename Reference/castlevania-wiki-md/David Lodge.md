# David Lodge

- David Lodge** is an American voice actor who is well known for his voice role as Villamax in *Power Rangers Lost Galaxy* and Loki in *Power Rangers Lightspeed Rescue*. He is also known as Kenpachi Zaraki in *Bleach*, Jiraiya in *Naruto*, and Archimonde in *Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos*.

Lodge voiced Julius Belmont, Death and the Puppet Master in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
