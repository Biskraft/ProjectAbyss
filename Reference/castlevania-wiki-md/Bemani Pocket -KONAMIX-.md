# Bemani Pocket -KONAMIX-

- Bemani Pocket -KONAMIX-*** is a portable music game released for Bemani Pocket (LCD) on January 18, 2001. It was developed by Bemani and published by Konami.

It contains themes from *Gradius*, *TwinBee*, *Parodius*, *Castlevania*, and many other Konami franchises.
