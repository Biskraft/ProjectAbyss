  - Hunter and Prey** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It is the background theme that plays at any point in the game where Raisa Volkova is fought. The track is an enhanced arrangement of Lady of the Crypt, another boss theme in *Mirror of Fate*.
