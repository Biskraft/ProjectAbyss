# Chupacabras' Shop

right|300px

The **Chupacabras' Shop** is a store operated by the Chupacabras in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. Located in the City of the Damned wing of the Castle, Chupacabras will open it after being freed by Dracula. The Kleidos mirror is also found here to access challenges.

## Wares
The Chupacabras will sell anything of value to Dracula in exchange for points.
- Dungeon Key - 200
- Tears of a Saint - 300
- Dodo Egg - 300
- Seal of Alastor - 400
- Dragon Scale - 400
- Ensnared Demon - 800
- Chaos Gem - 5,000
- Void Gem - 5,000
- Life Gem - 5,000
