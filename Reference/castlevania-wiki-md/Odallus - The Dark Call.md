# Odallus: The Dark Call

- Odallus: The Dark Call*** is a classic exploration/action game, inspired by *Ghosts 'n Goblins*, *Demon's Crest* and *Castlevania*.

It was released in July 15, 2015 through PC platforms GOG.com and Steam.

## Plot
:*"The old gods have forgotten this land. No one could save our people when darkness started spreading and collecting human souls for its army".*

## Overview
The player takes control of Haggis, a warrior tired of fighting who must wield his sword once more to rescue his son from the Darkness.
