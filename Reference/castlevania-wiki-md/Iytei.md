# Iytei

right|200px

  - Iytei** is a Battle-Type Innocent Devil in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a Level 3 devil and can learn Shoulder Ride and Ultra Scream. It can be obtained by evolving a Golem by collecting 90 **<span style="color:white;">Special</span>** Evo Crystals.

## Description
This Innocent Devil is the key to pass through the group of statues blocking a door in one of the rooms at the Forest of Jigramunt. Hector must stand at the top of the ramp and order Iytei to cast Shoulder Ride. He will then climb onto Iytei's shoulder and both will descend the ramp with increased velocity, smashing through the statues with great force, simulating a game of bowling. Behind this door, a series of corridors and rooms will be now explorable, yielding the following treasures:
- Dragon Scale
- Rare Ring
- Woodman's Chair
- Potion (Trevor only)
- Whip of Ice (Trevor only)
