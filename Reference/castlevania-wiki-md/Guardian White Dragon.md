# Guardian White Dragon

- Guardian White Dragon** is a White Dragon that is affixed to one's back that will lash out against enemies ahead. It can be gained by Soma Cruz in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* by stealing a White Dragon's soul. It behaves much like the Stinger did in *Dawn of Sorrow*.
