# Mille-feuille

A **Mille-feuille** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It is a type of "cake" that appears in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It can be obtained by dropping a $50 gold piece into the register at the City of Haze bakery, which is harder to get than a NY Cheesecake ($10), but easier to get than a Wheat Roll ($100). It can also be found in the 13th Street painting.
