# Satoe Terashima

- Satoe Terashima** is a music composer. She composed the famous music theme *Vampire Killer* for the 1986 game *Castlevania*. In the game, she shared credits with another composer, Kinuyo Yamashita, under the pseudonym of "James Banana", although they didn't actually collaborate; each composer contributed with her own themes and all in conjunction conformed the entire soundtrack of the game.

## Discography in the *Castlevania* series
  - Castlevania* (Konami, 1986) (with Kinuyo Yamashita)
  - Vampire Killer* (Konami, 1986)
  - Vampire Killer
  - Stalker
  - Out of Time
  - Voyager
  - Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* (Konami, 1987) (with Kenichi Matsubara and Kouji Murata)
