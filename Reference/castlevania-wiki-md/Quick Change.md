# Quick Change

- Quick Change** is an Ability Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It allows Soma to switch between two sets of equipment (A and B) by pressing the **X** button, allowing the player to spend less time going through the menu.

It is similar to the Glyph Sleeve from *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.
