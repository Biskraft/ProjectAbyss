# Magic Shield

right
  - Magic Shield** is a guardian soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained from a Witch and will allow Soma to block some attacks from enemies and reflect them back as projectiles imbued with the holy element attribute.

## Trivia
- The Witch (from which this soul is obtained from) will frequently cast this spell on her while making swoops through the room, trying to hit Soma.
- Reflecting the Tri-Cutter attack of a Tiny Devil will provide Soma with a high number of projectiles without actually vanishing the attack.
