# Boomerang (disambiguation)

- Boomerang** may refer to one of the following:
    - Boomerang** - A throwing weapon that returns to the thrower in an arc.
    - Boomerang** - The censored name of the Cross in the Western version of *Castlevania* and *Super Castlevania IV*.
    - Boomerang** - A weapon in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*
