# The Lost Portrait

- The Lost Portrait**, also known as **Lost Painting**, is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is the background theme that plays in the Reverse Caverns and the Anti-Chapel, as well as the Forbidden Library in the Reverse Castle.

A remake of this theme is also heard in the Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth & Contra ReBirth Original Soundtrack and the 8th chapter of *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

A remix by Tetsuya Shibata appears in the game *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate*.
