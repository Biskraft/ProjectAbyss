  - Epilogue ~A Time of Hope and Resolution~** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is played during two cutscenes :
- When Julia, contemplating the castle's ruins with Hector, hopes that humanity will recover from the curse and forget it.
- When Julia offers to Hector to live with her.
