# Death Doll

70px|right

The **Death Doll** (known as a **Sacrificial Doll** in *Lament of Innocence*) is an equipable doll that is killed instead of the player whenever they die, basically serving as an extra life. It also recovers some amount of HP. It can be purchased in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* for a relatively large amount of gold. The player cannot purchase one in either game if they already have one in their possession.
