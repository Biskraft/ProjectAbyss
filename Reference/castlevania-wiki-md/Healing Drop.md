# Healing Drop

right

  - Healing Drop** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by the Tiara evolution of the Fairy-Type. It costs 6 Hearts to be cast. During its duration, it causes green orbs to drop every time Hector hits an enemy. Each green orb would then heal him for 2 HP when collected.

Should the player resort to using this spell, it is advisable to equip fast-hitting weapons such as Spears or Knuckles on multiple enemies to maximize its effect. Having a slow-hitting weapon such as the Two-Handed weapons, or facing only a few targets at the same time would put this spell to waste.
