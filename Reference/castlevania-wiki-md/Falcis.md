# Falcis

right|174px
  - Falcis** is the name for any of Shanoa's sickle glyphs, and means "sickle" in Latin. In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, there are three different Falcis glyphs of different levels of power and lengths, including the regular Falcis, the powerful **Vol Falcis**, and the superior **Melio Falcis**.

When any two Falcis glyphs are involved in a Glyph Union, Shanoa swings a giant scythe that causes damage to a significant amount of enemies on screen.
