# Sally Knyvette

- Sally Knyvette** is a British actress, director and drama teacher. She is best known for her roles as Jenna Stannis in the first two seasons of the British science fiction series *Blake's 7* and as Kate Sugden in the soap opera *Emmerdale*.

She voiced Carmilla in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* and *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.
