# Skulls

- Skulls** of humans or animals that float around and deal damage to the player. There are many different kinds of skulls in *Castlevania*. Many of these skulls are also ghosts. Some bosses are accompanied by skulls, such as Olrox and Adramelech.

Death's second form in *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* is that of a giant skull.
