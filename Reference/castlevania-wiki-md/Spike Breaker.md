# Spike Breaker

- Spike Breaker** is a special armor in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. True to its name, it is made to destroy spikes that block certain areas of Dracula's Castle. It is a good armor stats-wise, as it offers DEF +12, but it is far outclassed by other armors found later in the game, partially because the Spike Breaker does not offer any bonus or has any special properties aside from breaking spikes. Still, it should be used for a while, so as to break the spikes of the castle permanently. It is mandatory to obtain in order to beat the game, as it must be used to break the spikes that guard the room in the Royal Chapel where the Silver Ring can be obtained.
