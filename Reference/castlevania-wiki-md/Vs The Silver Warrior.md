  - Vs The Silver Warrior** is an original music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula: Lords of Shadow*.

Based on the track's title, it is presumably the background music that plays during a segment where Pan is fought when he takes on the form of the Silver Warrior.
