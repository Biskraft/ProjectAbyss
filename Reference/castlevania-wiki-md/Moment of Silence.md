  - Moment of Silence** is a music track that is featured in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.

It plays in the Castle Keep during the bad ending's first part, when Malus appears to Reinhardt and Carrie after Dracula's defeat.
