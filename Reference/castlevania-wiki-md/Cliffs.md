# Cliffs

right|300px

  - Cliffs** are a type of environment in the *Castlevania* series. These hillsides are differentiated from Mountain areas by their lack of vegetation and less emphasis on vertical progression. They usually feature barren landscapes with big and eroded rock formations.

## Appearances
  - Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* - Deborah Cliff
  - Castlevania: Dracula X* - Clock Tower
  - Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* - Oblivion Ridge
