    - Pachislot Akumajō Dracula: Lords of Shadow*** is a pachislot game based on *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* developed by Konami Parlor Entertainment. It was announced on April 6, 2017. It serves as a spin-off of the *Lords of Shadow* chronology; as such, it features many characters from the first game, with new events and characters that were created exclusively for this game.

## Characters
- Gabriel Belmont
- Zobek
- Cornell
- Pan
- Succubus
- Carmilla
- Mechanical Monstrosity
- Gravedigger
- Laura
- Marie Belmont
- Felicia, an original character wearing the Brotherhood of Light's uniform and wielding bow and arrows.
