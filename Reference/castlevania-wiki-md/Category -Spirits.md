# Category:Spirits

- Spirits** are supernatural beings that are often, but not exclusively, non-physical entities, such as a ghost, a fairy or an angel. The concepts of spirit and soul often overlap, and both are believed to survive bodily death in some religions. A "spirit" can also have the sense of ghost (that is, a manifestation of the spirit of a deceased person).
