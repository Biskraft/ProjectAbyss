# Remedy

- Remedy** is a medicine  in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* that can be used once to cure all status ailments. It has the effect of an Anti-Venom (cures Poison) and Uncurse Potion (cures Curse) at the same time, and can also cure Petrification.
