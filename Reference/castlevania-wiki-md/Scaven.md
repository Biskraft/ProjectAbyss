# Scaven

The **Scaven** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. It is an undead animal-like creature under the Night Watchman's command. 

## Description
The Scaven is a type of undead creature created by the Night Watchman and commanded by him (until they turn against him). Scavenger's by trade, they scour the castle in search of those who have died within the walls and eat portions of their flesh, the rest of the body is mutated into more Scavens, and with no shortage of corpses within the castle walls, these beasts have the potential to expand without limit.

There is a mutated version of this creature, called **Flying Scaven**. This is basically a winged version of the common Scaven that also appear in Mirror of Fate, said to be the result of a mutation of the Scaven.
