# David Vincent

- David Vincent**, also known as **David Earnest**, is a Californian voice actor. He voiced Eric Lecarde / Wind in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. Since then, David replaced Scott McCulloch by playing the role of Richter Belmont in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* and its portable game. He provided the voice of Richter twice again in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate*.

David is well known for his voiceovers as Senketsu in *Kill la Kill*, Jin Kisaragi in the *BlazBlue* series and Archer / Gilgamesh in *Fate/Zero*; as well as Li Xingke in *Code Geass*, Marshall Law in the *Tekken* series, Grimmjow Jeagerjaquez, and Kouga Kuchiki in *Bleach*.

He was nominated Best Voice Actor by the American Anime Awards for his work in *Gun X Sword*.
