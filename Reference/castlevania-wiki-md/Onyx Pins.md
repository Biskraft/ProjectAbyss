# Onyx Pins

The **Onyx Pins** are hair pins made of Onyx in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They reduce the amount of damage taken from Darkness-based attacks. They can be purchased from Jacob's shop after completing Laura's "A Heartwarming Accessory" quest by bringing her an Onyx and a Diamond.
