# Galleries and Marble Corridors

- Galleries** and **Marble Corridors** are a type of environment in the *Castlevania* series. These areas are characterized for including very long corridors, all exquisitely crafted in marble both on their walls and floors. Their walls are usually adorned with large picture windows and paintings with finely sculpted marble columns between them.

## Appearances
  - Castlevania: Bloodlines* — Versailles Palace
  - Castlevania: Dracula X* — Courtyard
  - Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* — Marble Gallery, Black Marble Gallery
  - Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* — Audience Room, Eternal Corridor
  - Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* — Marble Corridor
  - Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* — Dance Hall
