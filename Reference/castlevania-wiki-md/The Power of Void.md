  - The Power of the Void** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It is notable for being featured as an atmospheric theme that plays during the cutscene that takes place after Dracula stabs a Dishonored Vampire in its mouth with his Void Sword. There, young Trevor provides his father with the White Wolf Medallion.

The track is also played when the Chupacabras first greets the weakened Prince of Darkness in his shop beneath his castle.
