# Riddle

- Riddle** is a music theme originally composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*. It is the background theme for Block 9.

A 2008 version arranged by Yasuhiro Ichihashi appeared later in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is the background theme for the Training Hall and the Large Cavern.

Most recently, it was used as the background theme for Dracula's final form in *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*.
