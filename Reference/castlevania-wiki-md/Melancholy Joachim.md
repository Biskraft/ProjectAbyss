  - Melancholy Joachim** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is played when Leon fights Joachim.

## Notes
- Some tracks in Castlevania: Lament of Innocence Original Soundtrack are arrangements of **Melancholy Joachim**, such as Young Nobleman of the Water Prison and Joachim of the Clear Blue Lake.

## Trivia
- The secret character, Pumpkin, will hum a piece from this theme if he stands still long enough.
