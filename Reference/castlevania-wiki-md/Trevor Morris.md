# Trevor Morris

- Trevor Morris** (born May 25, 1970) is a Canadian orchestral composer and music producer. He is probably best known for creating the soundtracks for the television shows *The Tudors*, *The Pillars of the Earth*, *The Borgias*, and *Vikings*, the film *Olympus Has Fallen* and its sequel *London Has Fallen*, and the video game *Dragon Age: Inquisition*.

He composed the music for all episodes of the *Castlevania* animated series, as well as the opening theme.
