# Assassin Blade

right|50px
The **Assassin Blade** is a sword in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is a poisoned blade preferred by assassins.

## Availability
Reward for completing the "Poison vs. Poison" quest. The player must bring a Long Sword, a Moldy Bread, and an Amanita to Wind. Care must be taken not to sell the Long Sword at any time, as it can only be obtained in one of the earlier levels (City of Haze) and no hint is ever given that it will be required later for a quest.
