# Shield Rod

right|172px
The **Shield Rod** is a club in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is extra effective when used in conjunction with a shield.

## Description
The Shield Rod is a low-end, slow club weapon that deals Hit damage and has a small DEF boost, but its major utility comes from the "Shield Rod Spells" it can be used to cast.

To cast a Shield Rod Spell, Alucard must have a shield equipped in his other hand. Both attack buttons (16px + 16px) must then be pressed at the same time; the resulting effect costs a set amount of MP and what happens depends on the shield used, with a different effect for each (see below for a list of effects). It should be noted that persistent effects do not require the Shield Rod to still be equipped.

right|200px

The Shield Rod also features a hidden ability which acts at the same time as a little Easter Egg. By performing the following sequence: ←, →, 16px (or, alternatively, pressing both 16px + 16px at the same time twice), a star-shaped shield like those from the *Gradius* series will be summoned in front of Alucard. The shield will be sent forward a short distance and remain in place for a short time. While it doesn't deal any damage to enemies, it can in turn block projectiles and any other attacks a normal shield can. Up to three stars can be summoned at a time, which will disappear roughly after ten seconds of being spawned. These stars can be summoned whether a shield is equipped or not.

The Mablung Sword ("Lemuria Sword" in the Japanese version) has an undocumented ability to cast Shield Rod Spells, and also higher damage output and the same defense bonus, plus it is slightly faster to wield, although it cannot create options.
