  - Meeting of Destiny** is a song composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is one of the songs that plays during a majority of cutscenes in the game such as when Eric Lecarde first appeared before Jonathan Morris and Charlotte Aulin and near the end of the game, when they defeat Brauner in the Lost Gallery.
