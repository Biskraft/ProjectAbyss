  - Trezier de spirit** is an original and iconic music track composed by Takeshi Kuramochi for *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula*. It is identified as the background theme that plays during the Big Bonus segments and was later used in the promotional trailer for CR Pachinko Akumajō Dracula.
