  - The Throne Room** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the fourth and final stage of Chapter 8, as well as the final stage of the "Land of the Vampires". Carmilla acts as the boss of this level.

This area is the location of the Mirror of Fate, which would later become Dracula's throne room, as well as Alucard's grave.
