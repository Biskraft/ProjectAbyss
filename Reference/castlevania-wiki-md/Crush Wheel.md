# Crush Wheel

right|250px

  - Crush Wheel** (or **Summon Decarabia**) is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Dawn of Sorrow*. It allows Soma to throw either wooden wheels or Decarabias at enemies. It goes at a considerable speed and length and moves like an ordinary rolling wheel (i.e., getting slower when rolling up and faster when rolling down). It will vanish if it stops rolling.

It can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Gladiator in *Aria of Sorrow*, and from a Decarabia in *Dawn of Sorrow*.
