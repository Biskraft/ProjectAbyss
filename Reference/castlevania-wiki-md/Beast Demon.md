The **Beast Demon** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

## Description
The Beast Demon is a large and hulking demon that will simply stand in place and spew fireballs at about an 80° angle in front of it. Although it may appear intimidating at first, it can be very easily defeated by standing under its chin and attacking it, as its fireballs will be incapable of touching the player.
