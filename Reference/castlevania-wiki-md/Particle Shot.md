# Particle Shot

right|250px

The **Particle Shot**, also known as **Optical Shot**, is one of Albus's signature attacks in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a spiraling shot providing Light and Darkness damage.

## Description
When Albus uses Particle Shot, he draws out his pistol and summons two balls of energy: one made of Light and the other made of Darkness. He then shoots both simultaneously, which are sent spiraling and spreading as they travel forward.
Category: Order of Ecclesia
