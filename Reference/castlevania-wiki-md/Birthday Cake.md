# Birthday Cake

right|50px

A **Birthday Cake** is a food item that recovers health in the *Castlevania* series of games.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*/*Dracula X Chronicles*
Its first appearance was as a food item for Maria in *Rondo of Blood* and later in *Dracula X Chronicles*.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
It can only be bought at Vincent's shop on the day that you have registered your birthday to be on your Nintendo DS.

## Trivia
- The birthday cake in *Portrait of Ruin* restore 573 HP. It is actually an in-joke Konami uses in many of its games :"5" in Japanese is *go*, changed to the voiceless form *ko*; "7" in Japanese is *nana* shortened to *na*; "3" in Japanese is *mittsu*, shortened to *mi*; "573" = *ko*-*na*-*mi*.
