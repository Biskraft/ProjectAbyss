# Reverse Cloak

The **Reverse Cloak** (called the **Reversible Mantle** in Japan) is the second least effective Cloak in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It gives a Constitution boost of +3 and a Defense boost of +2, which is greater than the CON +1 that a Cloth Cape gives, but has one less CON than a Blood Cloak. There is also a menu option that allows you to wear the cloak one way or inside out.
