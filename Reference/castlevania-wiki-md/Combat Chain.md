# Combat Chain

A **Combat Chain** is a retractable chain found in a Combat Cross.

In *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*, only Primary Weapon with a retractable combat chain (such as the Combat Cross, Improved Combat Cross, Dark Pain, or Vampire Killer) are able to Rappel or grab enemies from a distance, which are equipped with retractable spurs similar to a Hook Tip that enables them to do this. The only primary weapon that lacks this chain is Simon Belmont's Beast Hunter at the beginning of his quest.
