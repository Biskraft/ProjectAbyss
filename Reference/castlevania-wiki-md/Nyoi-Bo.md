# Nyoi-Bo

The **Nyoi-Bo** is a Spear weapon that can be used by Hector in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* A mythical pole that can extend or shrink at will. Extremely rare, few have actually seen it.

## Trivia
- The nyoi-bo would be directly inspired by the [Power Pole](http://dragonball.wikia.com/wiki/Power_Pole) of the [Dragon Ball](http://dragonball.wikia.com/wiki/Main_Page) series. Nyoi-Bo is also the Japanese equivalent of the Chinese name Ruyi Bang, the weapon used by the Monkey King Sun Wukong (whom Dragon Ball's Goku is based from).
