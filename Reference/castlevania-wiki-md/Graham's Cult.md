# Graham's Cult

right|thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">Graham Jones, founder and leader of the cult.</p>

A new religious sect with hordes of devout followers, founded by Graham Jones, who is rumored to have magical powers. Graham gained converts to this cult by preaching that the apocalyptic prophecies that were so popular at the end of the last century will come to pass in the year 2035 A.D.

After Graham's death that year, the members of the cult continued to cling to his teachings and began following the vampire Olrox.
