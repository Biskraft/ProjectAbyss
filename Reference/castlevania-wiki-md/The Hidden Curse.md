# The Hidden Curse

- The Hidden Curse** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is the background theme for the Forest of Doom. An arrangement of it appears in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and it was included in the first downloadable content music pack.
