# Sense Up

- Sense Up** is an ability in both *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It enables Soma to absorb more EXP from fallen enemies.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
Can be obtained by gaining dominance over Erinys's soul and enables Soma to absorb 20% more EXP from fallen enemies.

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
Can be obtained by gaining dominance over the Mothman's soul and enables Soma to absorb 25% more EXP from fallen enemies.
