# Gaze Up at the Darkness

- Gaze Up at the Darkness** is a music theme originally composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is the background theme that plays in the Master's Keep. It later reappeared as the background theme for Esquisse of Violence (Chapter 4) in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
