# Eneomaos Machine Tower

- Eneomaos Machine Tower** ("Clock Tower of Eneomaios" in the Japanese version) is a location in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the clock tower of this game.

## Overview
The level's layout isn't conformed by the typical side-scrolling metroidvania-like style seen in other versions of clock towers in the series. Many rooms do not resemble corridors and also feature several gears and mechanisms which the player can interact with.

The Devil-Type Innocent Devil is found here. Saint Germain is fought at the end as the area boss.
