# Intelligence

- Intelligence**, commonly abbreviated as **INT**, is a statistic found in the *Castlevania* series. In most of the games it appears in, Intelligence indicates the strength of sub-weaponry, magic attacks, and similar means. A general rule of thumb, although not always the case, is that if an attack uses up Hearts or MP and/or does not make use of the hero's primary weaponry, it receives a damage bonus from Intelligence.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
- Attacks made by Alucard that do not make use of his equipped weapons deal 1 more point of damage for every two points in his Intelligence.
### *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*
- Unlike the rest of the games in which this stat makes an appearance in, Intelligence primarily determines how much MP Nathan regenerates per second of in-game time. The rate of this regeneration is equal to 1/100th of his total Intelligence. If the result ends in a fraction, it is rounded down.
  - An exception to the rounding down rule is if Nathan's Intelligence is below 100, as in this case, rounding down would make him unable to regenerate MP at all otherwise.

## Intelligence boosting items
### Items which only boost Intelligence
#### Head
- Circlet - *SotN* (+5 INT)
- Silver Crown - *SotN* (+12 INT)

#### Hand
- Magic Gauntlet - *CotM* (+10 INT)

#### Accessories
- Magic Necklace - *HoD* (+5 INT)
- Mystic Brooch - *HoD* (+15 INT)
- Aroma Earring - *LoI* (+5 INT)

### Items which boost Intelligence at the expense of other stats
- Intelligence Ring - *CotM* (+50 INT, -10 STR, -10 DEF, -10 LCK)
- Sage Armband - *CotM* (+100 INT, -25 STR, -25 DEF, -25 LCK)
