  - Dracula's Castle** lies abandoned and in ruins. Simon Belmont enters the castle from the West Bridge, which will only allow passage if he has all five of Dracula's relics with him along with the Cross. He descends the empty underground area in order to burn his body parts. Dracula revives and uses the final relic, the fang, to attack Simon, although he's soon put down. The ending depends on how much time it took the player to beat the game.
