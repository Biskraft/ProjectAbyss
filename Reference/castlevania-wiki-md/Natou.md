# Natou

is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It is a smelly fermented soybean dish that restores 32 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is 2 more than Ramen, but less than food items granting 50 HP, such as Peanuts or Pot Roast.

In *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*, this item is named as **Fermented Soy Beans**. It recovers half of the player's health: the most amount of health for any food item in the game.
