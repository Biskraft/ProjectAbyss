The **Warthog** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.

## Description
Giant boar-like creatures ridden by Goblins. Warthogs can also be tamed by Gabriel, although the task is rather difficult. They can be used to smash open thick wooden doors by sprinting toward them and letting their tusks do the rest. They will also kill most creatures they run through with their tusks.

Warthogs are depicted as wearing dark-colored armor and having black furred manes.
