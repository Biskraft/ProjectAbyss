## Secrets and hidden areas
Christopher will be tested with a little puzzle where he must choose the correct paths to advance through the level.
- On the first screen, the candle on the upper-left ledge will have a 1 Up if you can walk across the falling platforms fast enough. When you arrive here, follow this path: right, right, left.


- Hit a Big Eye when it's rolling on the central platform and the floor will crumble, revealing an invisible rope that leads to a secret room.
