# Heavy Ring

right|200px

The **Heavy Ring** is a magic item in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It can be obtained in a room of the Underground Gallery, only accessible after defeating the twin Dragon Zombies. The ring bestows the ability to push certain wooden boxes, thus allowing access to new areas or solve various box puzzles in the game.
