# Star Bracelet

The **Star Bracelet** is a bracelet in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. Its power gives +25 to two statistics, which depend on which arm it is equipped on. DEF and LCK are increased when on the right arm, while STR and INT are increased when on the left arm. Having one equipped on each arm gives +25 to all of these stats, which is a greater net gain than any other two equipped arm items, except for two Double Grips, which gives three times as much for each statistic.
