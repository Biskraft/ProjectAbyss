# Energy Flow

right|280px

  - Energy Flow** is a signature attack for a Witch. She manipulates magical energy and launches it at enemies. It can be obtained from a Witch in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* by Charlotte Aulin by capturing her attack or by Soma Cruz by stealing her soul.
