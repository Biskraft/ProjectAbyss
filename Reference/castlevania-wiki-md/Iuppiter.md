# Iuppiter

right|250px

  - Iuppiter** is a Glyph Union in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.

## Description
Iuppiter can be performed by combining any Weapon glyph with a Fulgur glyph. The result is a giant lightning spear that forms in front of Shanoa and travels a short distance forward. The spear deals small, multi-hit, Thunder-based damage on its way, potentially being able to deal a great amount of damage to a single foe.
