# Guillotiner

The **Guillotiner** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is a demon who has executed countless victims in its bloody guillotine.

## Description
A rather bizarre-looking flying demon whose body is composed of two parts. Each part has its own set of wings and moves independently of the other. The entity as a whole somewhat resembles a demonic head or skull with a big, fanged mouth.

Once they see the player, they divide in half revealing a sharp blade attached to the upper half. The halves fly around together and occasionally stop to close together like a guillotine, causing damage if the player is in the way. Their flight path is erratic, making it hard to hit them, and once hit, they seem to fly wildly away before they return.

They are not difficult enemies if fought individually, although there are times when they are fought along with harder enemies, in which case it is better to take them out first before they become a nuisance.
