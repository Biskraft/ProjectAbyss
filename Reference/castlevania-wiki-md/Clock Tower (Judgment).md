# Clock Tower (Judgment)

right|300px

The **Clock Tower** is a stage in *Castlevania Judgment*. It consists of four platforms of grinding gears. In story mode, it is the stage where the player meets Aeon for a second time, who challenges them for the honor of fighting the Time Reaper.

The stage's hazard must be activated by shattering a yellow pillar on the smaller gears, this will prompt the platform to fall down after a short time, enough for the player to escape.
