# Sky Walkway

The **Sky Walkway** is a location in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is found near and through the Chapel of Dissonance.

## Overview
After using the portal in the Luminous Cavern, you'll be in Castle A at the Sky Walkway. There are obscure bloody paintings in the background of the hall. Juste will encounter Maxim down the hall, where they'll exchange dialogue. A High Potion can be seen very early in the next room. In the same room you can obtain a Life max up at the bottom floor and an Anti-Venom at the top floor.

The Devil boss can be encountered in the third room and a Save Room is located below their entrance. It's best to save first before you go whipping the flying demon. Once you do defeat the raging demon, you can obtain the Night Goggles in the next room. Equipping them makes traversing the dark underground of the Sky Walkway easier. In the same room before the boss, climbing up the platform will take you to the Chapel of Dissonance. The Castle B version of the Sky Walkway will have Legion (saint) and the Shadow boss.
