# Roast Beef

thumb|150px|<p style="text-align: center;">*Harmony of Despair* gamerpic.</p>
The **Roast Beef** is a food item in *Castlevania* (N64), *Legacy of Darkness* and *Harmony of Despair*. It is cooked with Incredible precision; only a hint of pink remains.
