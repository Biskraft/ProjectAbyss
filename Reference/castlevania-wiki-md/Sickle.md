# Sickle

*Main article: Deathscythe*

The **Sickle** is a hand-held agricultural tool designed with variously curved blades and typically used for harvesting, or reaping, grain crops. It generally conforms the bulk of Death's signature attack: Deathscythe (although in some occasions they are replaced by tiny scythes instead).

The Deathscythe attack consists in summoning an infinite number of little sickles out of thin air and on random locations on the screen which either float in the air or launch themselves to attack the player.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, Soma Cruz can absorb Death's soul, which allows him the ability to unleash sickles on his enemies.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, Hector can forge Death's Scythe, which can launch sickles during a special attack.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, Charlotte Aulin is able to learn this attack as a spell, where she unleashes a large quantity of sickles in a general direction. In that same game, she can learn a variant of the spell called 'R. Deathscythe', where they are replaced by tiny scythes instead.

## Trivia
- The original item used during the Deathscythe attack were tiny scythes, in reference to Death's iconic weapon. It was in later games where these were replaced by sickles.
  - The fact that both tiny scythes and sickles have indistinctively been used to portray the same function may be due to them both being reaping tools, which would allude to their main user, Death, who is commonly referred to as the "reaper of souls".
