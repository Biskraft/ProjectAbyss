  - Alison Lester** is a Californian voice actress. She was the original English language voice actor for Lisa (while being impersonated by the Succubus, who in turn was played by Barbara Whitlow) in the PlayStation version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. She was replaced in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* by Wendee Lee.
