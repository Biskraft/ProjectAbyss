# A Lullaby Sent to the Devils

- A Lullaby Sent to the Devils** is a music theme originally composed for the late 80s arcade game, *Haunted Castle*. An enhanced remix later appeared in *Castlevania: The Adventure Rebirth* as the background theme for Stage 2.
