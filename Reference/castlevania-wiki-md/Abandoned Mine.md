# Abandoned Mine

The **Abandoned Mine** is the fourth area Alucard explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*.


It was previously explored by Trevor as the thirteenth area in his journey. This is the area where the Daemon Lord was freed from his prison. Witches reside in a area of the mine protected by lava stream and an ancient door where the key is a odd cube with the same design as the door. In the northwestern part of the mine, a large bell from the Belfry came crashing down the tower destroying parts of the castle's mechanisms.
The Reaver makes a small appearance in the foreground after spotting Alucard.
