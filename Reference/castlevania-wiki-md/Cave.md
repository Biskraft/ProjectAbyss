The **Cave** is an upside down version of the Abandoned Pit to the Catacomb in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. A slight blue color has been added to everything in the Pit.

The Cave can be reached from the Reverse Caverns below and traveling further upwards leads to the Floating Catacombs.
