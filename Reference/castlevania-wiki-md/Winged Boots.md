# Winged Boots

- Winged Boots** are boots that increase a character's running speed. They were first introduced into the series in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. They also made an apparition in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* as a reward from Irina for defeating the Jiang Shi and completing the quest: *"An Unwelcome Guest"*.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* they increase speed by +50%. They are an upgrade to the Mercury Boots, but can be replaced with the more effective Sonic Boots (+60%), which are available in the downloadable stage Lord of Flies (Chapter 9).
