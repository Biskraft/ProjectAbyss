# Coral Circlet

The **Coral Circlet** is a circlet made of Coral in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It offers immunity to Curses. It also grants an Intelligence boost of 7, which is one more than the Gold Circlet, but one less than a Cat-Eye Circlet.
