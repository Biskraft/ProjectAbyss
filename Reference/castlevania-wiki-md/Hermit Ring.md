# Hermit Ring

The **Hermit Ring** is a ring in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It represents the Tarot card, "The Hermit".

## Characteristics
When worn, the ring increases magical statistics (INT and MND by +3) at the lesser expense of the corresponding physical statistics (STR and DEF by -2).

It may be considered the counterpart of the Chariot Ring.
