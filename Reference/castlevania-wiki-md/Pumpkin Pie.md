# Pumpkin Pie

- Pumpkin Pie** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It is the only kind of pie that appears in *Castlevania* and appears in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Portrait of Ruin*. In can be found in the Lost Village in *Dawn of Sorrow* and Sandy Grave in *Portrait of Ruin*.
