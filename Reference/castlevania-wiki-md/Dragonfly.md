The **Dragonfly** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is an insect that spits burning liquid.

## Description
A bizarrely morphed version of the real life insect of the same name. The Dragonfly is a large flying insect that attacks by spewing out large balls of a burning liquid which arcs to the ground.

Even though it is classified as an insect, it displays some features that resemble those of an European dragon, such as bat-like wings and a long tail. Even its spewing acid attack can be seen as reminiscent of a dragon spewing fire, as both elements have burning qualities to them. However, despite its namesake, the dragonfly is a weak creature that can usually be killed by a single attack.
