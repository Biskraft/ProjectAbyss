# Paralysis

- Paralysis** is a special status modifier similar to Petrify or Frozen, found only in two games: *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Curse of Darkness*.

When paralyzed, the player won't be able to move for a determined amount of time, giving the enemies a free chance to damage them.
