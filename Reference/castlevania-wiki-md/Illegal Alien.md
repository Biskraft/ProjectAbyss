The **Illegal Alien** is an enemy in the Game Boy version of *Kid Dracula*.

## Description
The Illegal Alien is a lizard-like alien monster that attacks by shooting fireballs. It appear in Stage 8, Robot Factory.

## Enemy Data


## Trivia
- This enemy is inspired by the Xenomorph, the main antagonist of the *Alien* franchise of science fiction horror films.
- Its English name is also a pun on both extraterrestrial and the concept of immigrating illegally into another country.
