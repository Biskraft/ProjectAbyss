# Crispin Freeman

- Crispin McDougal Freeman** is a Californian voice actor and ADR director. He voiced Mathias Cronqvist in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and Hector from *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. He also appeared as an ADR Director in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.

Crispin is famous for his character roles to his fans as Alucard in *Hellsing*, Itachi Uchiha in *Naruto*, and Guan Yu in *Dynasty Warriors*.
