# Fang of Vlad

The **Fang of Vlad** (also known as **Tooth of Vlad** or **Dracula's Fang**) is one of Dracula's relics.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*
According to the guidebook to *Simon's Quest*, **Dracula's Fang** is a relic Simon Belmont never knew about. It was kept in the room where Dracula was resurrected in and it became Dracula's Ghost's only weapon against him in that game.

### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The **Tooth of Vlad** (called the **Nail** in the Japanese version) is one of the items necessary to enter the Reverse Castle Center. It adds Strength +10.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*
The **Fang of Vlad** is one of the six relics needed to enter the ceremonial room. It provides a Defense +10 stat boost (a function that had been previously provided by the Rib of Vlad in *Symphony of the Night*). It can be found in the Clock Tower's boulder race room (Castle A), in the small room to the left. There's no wall to break to gain entry to that room, instead Juste must slide through a hidden crawlspace.
