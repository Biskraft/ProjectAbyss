The **Creeper** (**Ji Zee** in Japanese) is a featherless bird-like creature in *Castlevania: The Adventure*, and its remake, *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*.

## Description
It appeared more human-like in the original game, but was more bird-like in the remake. It moves along minding its own business for a while on the ground, but when it is struck with a weapon, it gets startled and leaps toward its assailant quickly.
