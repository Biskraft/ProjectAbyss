# Enric Álvarez

- Enric Alvarez** (sometimes credited as **Enric J. Álvarez Benito**) is a co-founder and owner of MercurySteam, the developer of *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.

## *Castlevania* production roles
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*, *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*, *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2* - Director/Writer
