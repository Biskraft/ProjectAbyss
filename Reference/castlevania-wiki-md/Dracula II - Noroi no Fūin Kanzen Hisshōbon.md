# Dracula II: Noroi no Fūin Kanzen Hisshōbon

right|250px

  - Famicom Hisshōbon - *Dracula II: Noroi no Fūin* Kanzen Hisshōbon** (ファミコン必勝本 ドラキュラⅡ呪いの封印 完全必勝本, lit. "Famicom Certain Victory Book *Dracula II: Seal of the Curse* Complete Certain Victory Book") is the 58th book of the "Friday Special" series published by Takarajimasha.

The book is a Japanese guide for the game known in America as *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.
