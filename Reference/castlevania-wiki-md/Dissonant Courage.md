  - Dissonant Courage** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is the boss theme that plays when fighting the Giant Skeleton, Rusalka, Goliath and the Jiang Shi. It also had an arranged version that placed more emphasis on electric guitar strings.
