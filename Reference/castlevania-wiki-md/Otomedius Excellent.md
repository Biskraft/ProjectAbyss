# Otomedius Excellent

is an Xbox 360 horizontal scrolling shooter game heavily inspired by the *Gradius* series, and the sequel to the 2008 game *Otomedius G (Gorgeous!)* by Konami for the same system. It was released on April 21, 2011 in Japan, and in North America on November 8 of the same year.

- Otomedius Excellent* features female characters representing different Konami franchises riding on vehicles or equipped with flying armor.

## *Castlevania* references
This game includes , a downloadable character representing *Castlevania*. She is voiced by Hitomi Harada.

Kokoro is paired with , a female character inspired by Getsu Fuma, who in turn has a cameo in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. She is voiced by Satomi Satō.

The main antagonist of the game, Dark Force, can be seen sitting on a throne similar to the one Dracula sits on in the *Castlevania* games.
