# Infinite Jump

- Infinite Jump** is the ability to perform an infinite amount of jumps in the air, which is superior to a double or a triple jump. The only *Castlevania* character to gain this ability was Juste Belmont in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* by equipping the Infinite Boots. He gains this ability late in the game, which allows him access to any area, regardless of height. Other characters gain this kind of map access by obtaining a high jump or the ability to fly.
Category: Actions
