# Ring of Fire

right|73px
The **Ring of Fire** (called the **Boiling Ring** in *Curse of Darkness* and **Ring of Scorching Heat** in the Japanese versions) is a ring that offers resistance against Fire-based attacks. It appears in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
