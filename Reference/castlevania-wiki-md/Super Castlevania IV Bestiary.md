# Super Castlevania IV Bestiary

Enemies that appear in *Super Castlevania IV*. Order and categorization is from the *All About Akumajō Dracula* Japanese guide book. Primary names are based on the English manual, or by a similar monster's English name in a different game or a descriptive name, or by the Japanese name. Names in parentheses are Japanese or alternate names according to the *All About Akumajō Dracula* guide book when different.
