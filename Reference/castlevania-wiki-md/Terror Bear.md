# Terror Bear

right|150px

The **Terror Bear** is an insidious stuffed toy filled with iron sand. In action, Soma will swing it overhead and everything caught in the toy's path will be dealt extremely fast, although receiving weak damage, in a very rapid succession. Its special attack increases its damage slightly and makes it last longer.
