# Immortal Fragment

- Immortal Fragment** is a material in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is a piece of eternity itself, which releases endless amounts of energy. It is an ingredient for crafting the Gatling Gun special weapon.

## Availability
Stolen from Saint Germain. When he uses Time Stop on Hector, use Time Stop as well and steal from him during the small window.
