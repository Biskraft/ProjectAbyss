## Plot
This game is a spinoff of *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* featuring Hector, Trevor Belmont, Dracula, and other enemies such as bats.

## Gameplay
The base game has action stacks for possible full screens, including wilds. Randomly wild reels may be added. Three to five bonus symbols trigger free games. During the free games, the reels stretch up and the wild feature may occur more frequently. When wilds land, they may crack a gem above the reels. When the gem is cracked, the progressive feature is enabled. The feature is a picking bonus where you work your way into the castle for possible progressive prizes.
