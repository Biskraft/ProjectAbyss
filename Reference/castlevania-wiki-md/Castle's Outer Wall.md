# Castle's Outer Wall

*(This article is a stub)*
The **Castle's Outer Wall** is the second area Simon explores in Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate, as well as the second area overall in the game. Simon gains his first glance of the Lost Soul, who appears to beckon him to follow.
