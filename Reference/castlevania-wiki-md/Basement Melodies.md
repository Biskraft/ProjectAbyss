# Basement Melodies

- Basement Melodies**, also known as **Underground Melodies** or **Basement Melody**, is a music theme originally composed for *Haunted Castle* by Kenichi Matsubara.

It later reappeared in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* arranged by Masahiko Kimura and was the background theme for the Mine of Judgment.
