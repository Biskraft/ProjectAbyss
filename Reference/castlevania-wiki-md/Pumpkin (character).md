# Pumpkin (character)

- Pumpkin** is a secret character in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It can be unlocked by completing Joachim Mode.

## Gameplay
Pumpkin has a special sub-weapon, named after none other than itself. Like many of the secret playable characters in Koji Igarashi's *Castlevania* games, it has very little health but unbelievable super-strength, which actually adds a touch of humor to the game.

## Trivia
- If Pumpkin stands still, it will hum Joachim's theme song, Melancholy Joachim.
- Pumpkin's dying cries are actually Joachim's cries, only sped up. This happens in both games for the PlayStation 2.
- Pumpkin reappears in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* as an Innocent Devil.
  - The Pumpkin Mace also refers to him, describing him as a "legendary hero".
  - Pumpkin also inspired the Pumpkin Head equipment item.
