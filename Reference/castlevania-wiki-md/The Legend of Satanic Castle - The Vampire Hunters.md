# The Legend of Satanic Castle: The Vampire Hunters

is a Japanese gamebook (choose-you-own-adventure style novel) published by Futabasha in 1990.

The book features Sid Belmont, Row Danasty‎, Zouk Belnades and Leila; they are the descendants of Trevor Belmont, Grant Danasty, Sypha Belnades and Alucard respectively.

## Notes
- Despite being an official product, *The Legend of Satanic Castle: The Vampire Hunters* is not canonical to the current Konami official *Castlevania* timeline.
