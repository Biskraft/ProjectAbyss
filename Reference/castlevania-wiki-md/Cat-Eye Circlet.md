# Cat-Eye Circlet

The **Cat-Eye Circlet** (abbreviated as **Cat-eye circl.** in the item menu) is a circlet made of Chrysoberyl that restores HP when hit by a cat enemy in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* (the cats that the Witches turn into). It also gives an INT boost of 8, which is one more than the Gold Circlet, but 2 less than the Opal Circlet, Ruby Circlet, Topaz Circlet or Beryl Circlet.

Unlike other abilities from later games that somewhat have a similar effect, such as the Arma Felix glyph, while wearing the circlet the player will still take full damage from other feline enemies than the Witches' cats, such the Lion and the Black Panther.
