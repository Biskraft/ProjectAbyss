# Corpseweed Familiar

right|150px
  - Corpseweed Familiar** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It summons a Corpseweed as a familiar. This familiar behaves like a Guardian from *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* in that is is always located on a fixed position relative to Soma, namely the top of his head.
