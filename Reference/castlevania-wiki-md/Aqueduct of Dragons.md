The **Aqueduct of Dragons** is a sewer area in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

## Overview
### Castle A
Mermen and Fish Men walk the waterways of this area, while Bats and Slimes wander the ceilings. A Boomerang Armor is encountered at a dead-end in one of the waterways, and as well as a dead-end in another one of the waterways contains a row of guillotines. The boss of this area is the Giant Merman, whose death will give Juste access to a different location: the Clock Tower.

### Castle B
The waterways are crawling with Melty Zombies along with slimes, mermen and fish men. Arabakis fly around the waterways as well, and White Dragons are sometimes encountered at dead-ends. In the upward access areas of the Aqueduct, Bone Liquids fill the area. Taking the leftmost exit of this area will take Juste to the Sky Walkway.
