  - Incarnation of Darkness** (also known as **Dracula's Theme**) is a music theme composed by Soshiro Hokkai for *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is played during the cutscene when the Dracula Wraith materializes from Maxim's body after it senses Dracula's remains. Just confronts him afterward. As its title indicates, the theme is considered as the wraith's theme rather than Dracula himself.
