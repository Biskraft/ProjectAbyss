# Walkthrough:Circle of the Moon

This is an **incomplete walkthrough for** *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It is not official, nor endorsed by Konami: it was written by fans for fans.

This page is a table of contents: click on the links to the sub-articles to find the detailed walkthrough.

*Main article: Walkthrough:Circle of the Moon/Adramelech*

## Defeat Dragon Zombie
*Main article: Walkthrough:Circle of the Moon/Dragon Zombie*
Explore the Underground Gallery and defeat the Dragon Zombies.

This quest is optional. Following this walkthrough will allow Nathan to obtain more power-ups and explore both the Underground Warehouse and the Battle Arena.

## Defeat Death
*Main article: Walkthrough:Circle of the Moon/Death*
Explore the Underground Warehouse and defeat Death.

This quest is optional and can only be accessed after defeating Death and obtaining Heavy Ring. Following this walkthrough will cleanse the water in the Underground Waterway to prevent it from damaging Nathan.

## Defeat Camilla
*Main article: Walkthrough:Circle of the Moon/Camilla*
Explore the Underground Waterway and defeat Camilla.

## Defeat Hugh
*Main article: Walkthrough:Circle of the Moon/Hugh*
Defeat Hugh and obtain the Last Key.

## Defeat Dracula
*Main article: Walkthrough:Circle of the Moon/Dracula*
Defeat Dracula and save Nathan's mentor, Morris Baldwin.

## Battle Arena
*Main article: Walkthrough:Circle of the Moon/Battle Arena*
Explore the Battle Arena.

This quest is optional and can be repeated indefinitely, as no boss exist in this quest. This quest can be accessed only if Nathan has obtained Heavy Ring and Roc Wing.
