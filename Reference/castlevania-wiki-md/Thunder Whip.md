# Thunder Whip

right|73px
The **Thunder Whip** is a whip with an added Thunder element. It can be obtained by defeating the Thunder Elemental while exploring the Garden Forgotten by Time and imbues the Whip of Alchemy with the element of Thunder in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*.
