# Charm Necklace

The **Charm Necklace** is a pendant in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* which offers resistance against curses, in addition to a MND +10 boost. It replaced the Holy Ring found in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It later reappeared in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, where it had different stats (HP +4, MP +4, Curse +15).
