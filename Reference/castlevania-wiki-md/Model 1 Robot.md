The  is an enemy in *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun* and *Kid Dracula* (GB).

## Description
The **Model 1 Robot** is a robot that walks back and forth along a platform.

In *Boku Dracula-kun*, they appear in Stage 7, Galamoth's Realm; and in *Kid Dracula*, they appear in Stage 5, Space Elevator.
