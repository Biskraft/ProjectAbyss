# Reflec Beat

- REFLEC BEAT** is a music/rhythm game released for arcades and iOS. Its soundtrack features medleys with music from *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*, *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun* and *Getsu Fūma Den*.

## Featured songs
- Castlevania II Simon's Quest Music Medley
- KID DRACULA Music Medley
- Getsufuu Maden Music Medley
