# Trevor Belmont/Pachislot

**Trevor Belmont** (known as **Ralph C. Belmondo** in Japan) is the main hero of the *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula* trilogy. He's a vampire hunter and a descendant of Leon. His family was exiled from Wallachia because of their powers. 

The first two games feature an older version of Trevor which has a scar across his cheek which occurred after the events of *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*.
The third game features a scar-less face, indicating it takes place during that game.  His character design is similar to his appearance in *Curse of Darkness*.

He is allied with Angela in the first two games, while he is with his companions Alucard and Sypha in the third game. In all games, his ultimate goal is the defeat of Dracula.

## Story
### *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula III*
The story is the same as told in *Dracula's Curse*. Trevor is requested by the Pope to defeat Dracula after previously attempts had ended in failure. *Pachislot III* recounts small snippets of his adventure.

- Battle Mission: Succubus
- Battle Mission: Doppelganger
- Battle Mission: Dracula
- Alucard Mission: Alucard
- Dracula Battle: Dracula (True Form)

### *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula I* & *II*
The story of *Pachislot I* and *II* are a story from an alternative timeline set around the time of *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
- Battle Mission: Medusa
- Isaac Mission: Isaac
- Battle Mission: Wyvern
- Battle Mission: Dullahan
- Battle Mission: Death
- Dracula Battle Bonus: Dracula
- Dracula Battle Bonus: D. A.
