# The Throne Room (Portrait of Ruin)

The **Throne Room** is the final location in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is connected to the Master's Keep and blocked off by Brauner's power, preventing the player from entering.

## Overview
After Brauner is defeated, the player is teleported, via painting, to the Throne Room and the area is no longer blocked off. This area is the second smallest in the game (after the Lost Gallery), with only four rooms. Dracula and Death are fought at once here, and after their defeat, True Dracula is fought.
