# La-La-La

right|250px

  - La-La-La** is a long tongue lashing attack which is a signature move of the Cave Troll (also known as a Chupacabra in Japanese game versions). Soma Cruz can gain this ability by gaining dominance over a Cave Troll's soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
