# Forget-Me-Not

The **Forget-Me-Not** is a necklace patterned after a beautiful flower.

It is the weakest of Charlotte's necklaces in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* that increases DEF (+1) and CON (+2). It is less effective than the Crystal Necklet (+2 DEF, +2 CON).
