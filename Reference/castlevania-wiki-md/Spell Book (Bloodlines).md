# Spell Book (Bloodlines)

The **Spell Book** (called **Mystery Book** in the Japanese and European versions) is a hidden item in *Castlevania: Bloodlines* which can be found inside of breakable walls. Collecting a Spell Book will cause one of two different effects which happen at random; either the player will get twenty Red Jewels or a third Coat of Arms power-up effect, powering up the characters' main weapon to its fourth degree.

## Trivia
- The *Castlevania: Bloodlines* instruction booklet claims that the Spell Book could be either a "help or a hindrance"; however, both effects seem to benefit the player regardless, so it's unknown what is the reason behind this statement.
