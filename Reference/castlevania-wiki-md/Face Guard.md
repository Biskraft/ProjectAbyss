# Face Guard

The **Face Guard** is a mask in the *Castlevania* series. Exactly who this is, no one knows, but their latent power can still be strongly felt.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*
An bloodstained hockey mask that can be created by combining a Stone Mask and a Gano.

### *Castlevania Judgment*
The **Splatter Mask** is a face accessory that can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character.

## Trivia
- This hockey mask seems to be inspired by the iconic face gear generally worn by Jason Voorhees, the main antagonist from the *Friday the 13th* slasher film series. The many bloodstains it has scattered on it support this theory.
- "Jason" and the Mad Snatcher, two enemies from *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun* and *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, respectively, also wear hockey masks, also being both a reference to Jason Voorhees.
- The Cursed Pumpkin a Pumpkin-Type Innocent Devil from *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, wears a humorously small hockey mask.
- The name of this item in *Castlevania Judgment*, "**Splatter Mask**", is most likely a reference to the horror video game series *Splatterhouse*, in which the protagonist wears a demonic face gear that also resembles a hockey mask and which imbues him with supernatural powers.
  - This mask, in turn, is also a nod to Jason Voorhees.
