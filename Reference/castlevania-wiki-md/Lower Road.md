The **Lower Road** is an alternate path that takes you from the Aljiba Woods to the Yuba Lake (which is a dead-end if you do not possess the Blue Crystal). You enter through some underground tunnels which are filled with Skeletons and Ghostly Eyeballs and then exit at the base of a lake.

The path contains a lower section with water that you have to jump to moving platforms to get over, and an upper section that goes above those lakes. The safer upper path can be used on the way to Yuba Lake, but it is not accessible on the way back.
