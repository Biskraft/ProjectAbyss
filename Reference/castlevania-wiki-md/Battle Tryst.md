# Battle Tryst

is a 1998 3D fighting arcade game developed and published by Konami.

## Features
Characters "Shemon" and "Rheter", based on Simon Belmont and Richter Belmont from the *Castlevania* series, were planned to appear in the game, as well as Madoka from the *TwinBee* series, and concept art for all three of them exist, but they were canceled.
