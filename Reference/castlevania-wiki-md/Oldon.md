The town of , also known as **Ondol** or **Andole**, is a location in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.

## Overview
Oldon is a town in the northwest part of Transylvania. It is most important for having a shop where the player can buy the Morning Star whip (200 Hearts), although it does not have a church in it. It can be reached from the upper path of Sadam Woods. Beyond it lies the northern section of the Jam Wasteland and Deborah Cliff.
