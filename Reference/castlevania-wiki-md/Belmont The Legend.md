# Belmont The Legend

- Belmont The Legend** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is Trevor Belmont's main theme and can be heard during his boss battles.

The track was later featured in Pachislot Akumajō Dracula, called **Legendary Belmondo**. It plays during the Skeleton elimination mission.
