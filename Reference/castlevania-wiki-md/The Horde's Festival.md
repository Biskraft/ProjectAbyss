# The Horde's Festival

- The Horde's Festival**, also known as **Festival of Servants**, is a music theme originally composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is the first main boss battle theme of the game.

An arrangement later appeared in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* for the downloadable stage Lord of Flies.
