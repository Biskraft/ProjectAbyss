# Troy Baker

- Troy Edward Baker** is a Californian voice actor who is most known as Joel in *The Last of Us* and the Joker in *Batman: Arkham Origins*.

He voiced and captured the motion of Alucard in the canceled PSX/Xbox 360 game, *Castlevania*.
