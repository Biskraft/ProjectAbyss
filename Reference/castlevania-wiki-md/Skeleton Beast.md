# Skeleton Beast

- Skeleton Beast** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. According to the game it appears in, it has either the form of a dog's skeleton or the form of a gigantic skeleton warrior (which is a variant of the Giant Skeleton).


## Appearances
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
Man's best friend can also apply to the dead as well. As for the Skeleton Beast, it will follow its undead master, the Skeleton Breeder, with his commands and will attack anyone who tries to hurt him.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
Unlike *Symphony of the Night*, the **Skeleton Beast** (known as **Barbarian Skeleton** in the Japanese version) in *Order of Ecclesia* is a Giant Skeleton enemy, except he is green in color, wears a helmet and wields a spiked club.
