# M. Circle Scissors

right

  - M. Circle Scissors** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by the Brow evolution of the Devil-Type. This skill is an enhancement of the Magic Circle ability, but with offensive capabilities. An attack can be used while in this form that extends several razor blades around the circle.
