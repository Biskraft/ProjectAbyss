  - Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles Original Soundtrack** is the soundtrack for *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*. It also contains *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*ʼs soundtrack on disc 2. Some arranged or new tracks can also be found in the track list. The music was mostly composed by Konami Kukeiha Club and arranged by different composers. It was released on May 20, 2008 with the official CD and the iTunes version.

## Additional information
### Original games ~ Tracks origin
- Tracks 26~29 on disc 1 are arranged versions.
- Tracks 23 and 24 on disc 2 are *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* new tracks.
- Tracks 25~27 on disc 2 are extra arranged tracks.

### Trivia
- "Tues Deus Meus (In Manibus Tuis)" is a Latin phrase that means "You're my God, In your hands [I put my Fate]". It is from Catholic religious texts.
- "Cavern of Dark Spawn" is an arrangement of "The Nest (also known as Den)", which wasn't used in *The Dracula X Chronicles*.
