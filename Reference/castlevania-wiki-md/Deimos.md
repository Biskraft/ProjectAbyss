# Deimos

- Deimos** is a character in the graphic novel *Castlevania: The Belmont Legacy*, published by IDW. He is Christopher Belmont's butler.

## Plot
Deimos participates in the siege on Dracula's castle. He's very loyal to Christopher and will never hesitate, even if he has to kill a friend who has been turned into a vampire.
