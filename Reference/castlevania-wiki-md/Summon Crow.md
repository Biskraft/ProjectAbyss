# Summon Crow

right|112px
  - Summon Crow** is an attack that works by flying a Crow at enemies. Variations of this attack allow to have more than one crow on screen at the same time, or summoning a complete flock to attack enemies.

### Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
Soma throws out a crow at an upward angle, which loops out and returns. This attack is very similar to Maria's Cardinal sub-weapon from *Rondo of Blood*.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Summon Crow is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Portrait of Ruin*. Several crows are summoned and take turns swooping at the enemies.
