 is a Japanese illustrator residing in Saitama Prefecture, Japan. Besides painting, he also enjoys hobbies such as motorcycles and cars racing. Since 2002, he became a free illustrator after leaving the company he was working for.

He is best known for his work on *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. Hirooka was brought on board after the more traditional anime styling of *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
