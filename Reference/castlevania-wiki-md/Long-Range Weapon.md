# Long-Range Weapon

- Long-Range Weapons**, also known as **Distant Attack Weapons**, are a character's principle attack in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. Like Short-Range Weapons, they are unique to each playable character and do not consume any resources to use. They are more powerful than Short-Range weapons, though slower, and can be leveled up twice upon finding a Power-Up.
