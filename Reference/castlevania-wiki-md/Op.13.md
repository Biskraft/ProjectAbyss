# Op.13

- Op.13**, or **Opus 13**, is a music theme composed by Jigokuguruma Nakamura for *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*. It is the background theme for Stage 5'. It later appeared as the background theme for Stage 5 in *Castlevania: Dracula X*.
