# Splash Needle

right|206px

  - Splash Needle** (also called **Water Pistol** or **Water Spray**) is a signature attack of a Merman, first seen in *Super Castlevania IV*. It shoots out a stream of compressed water at its enemy.

## Availability
Soma Cruz is able to use this attack with a Merman's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*. Charlotte Aulin can learn this spell by blocking a Merman's attack with her Book of Binding's energy shield in *Harmony of Despair*.
