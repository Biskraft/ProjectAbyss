# Hekiru Shiina

is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is known for voicing a variety of animes and films over the course of her career. She provided the supporting role of Miles "Tails" Prower in the Japanese dub of *Sonic the Hedgehog: The Movie*, and she portrayed the main character Asami in *Seirei Tsukai*.

Shiina provides the voice of the Faerie Familiar in *Castlevania: Nocturne in the Moonlight* (*Symphony of the Night*) for the Sega Saturn when she recites the lyrics of the song Nocturne to Alucard after he has remained seated in a chair for a certain period of time.
