# Waiter Skeleton

The **Waiter Skeleton** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is a skeletal waiter that inhabits the castle. It always carries around a bowl of delicious curry.

## Description
A unique skeleton dressed in a waiter's uniform which carries a tray of curry in one hand and a cloth on the other. It throws the tray at the player when they approach and then flees, usually hitting a wall and crumbling.

## Appearances
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
Waiter Skeletons appear in the Dance Hall. They throw bowls of Beef Curry at the player when in their vicinity. Sometimes they will even throw their skulls instead.

This variant will not run away after their first attack and instead will keep on throwing bowls at the player until killed.


### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
Waiter Skeletons appear in the Demon Guest House and the Silenced Ruins. They're more skittish this time around, as they'll flee after throwing their tray of curry. Ironically, they usually end up hitting a wall and crumbling, unintentionally defeating themselves in the process.

Gaining dominance over its soul, Delicious Curry, is the only way for Soma to defeat the Yeti. After throwing some curry in the area where the game begins, a Yeti will leap from the background to eat it and then leap back, giving a small opportunity to beat it and, with some luck, get its soul.
