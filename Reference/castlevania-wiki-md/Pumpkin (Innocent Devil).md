# Pumpkin (Innocent Devil)

right|200px


The **Pumpkin** is an Innocent Devil in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the initial form of the optional Pumpkin-Type family.

It can evolve in many forms, but remains weak, even at high levels. Its only advantage is the large stat boost it gives to Hector.

## Trivia
- The description of the Pumpkin Mace in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* refers to a "legendary hero". This is obviously a reference to the character Pumpkin from *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, who was the third playable character unlocked.
- Pumpkin wears the Prince's Clothes.
