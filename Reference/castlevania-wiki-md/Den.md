# Den

- Den** is a music theme originally composed by Jigokuguruma Nakamura for *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*. It later appeared as the background theme for Stage 7 in *Castlevania: Dracula X*.

This theme contains arrangements of Vampire Killer, Bloody Tears and Beginning in it.
