# Devil Shard

right|300px

Innocent Devils will occasionally drop egg-like items called **Devil Shards**. They can create new Level 1 Innocent Devils with the aid of Julia, through the process of Devil Forging at her shop. Those Innocent Devils will have higher stats compared to their "parents", being a new generation (10% of the parent Devil's stats are added to the new generation Devils). The higher the generation, the higher will be the base stats of the newborn Innocent Devil at Level 1.

The appearance and theme of a Devil Shard is based on the Level 1 Stage, depending on the type of the Innocent Devil that created the shard. Newly-forge Innnocent Devils will always start at the base evolution form.
