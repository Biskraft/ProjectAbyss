# Forgotten Caves

The **Forgotten Caves** is the third area Simon explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*, as well as the third area overall in the game. Simon meets up with the Lost Soul in this stage and learns from it that his father's battle cross is still in the castle. He also meets his dead mother Sypha's spirit, who proceeds to protect him throughout his quest, although he does not realize the identity of this Belnades spirit (it is also hinted that the spirit is the spirit of a women and healer whose shrine was built under the castle centuries before and visited by pilgrims).
