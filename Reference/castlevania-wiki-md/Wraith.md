The **Wraith** is an enemy in the Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is a black robed, red eyed, purple clad spirit of death that squeezes the souls out of helpless victims.

## Description
In addition to his HP amount, this enemy is quite dangerous because it is able to absorb the vital force of the player to regenerate with its giants hands.
