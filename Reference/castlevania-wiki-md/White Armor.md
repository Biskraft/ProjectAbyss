The **White Armor** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

## Description
It is an improved version of the Holy Armor and only appear in the Battle Arena. They walk forward four paces, raise their swords and fire a large laser-like beam straight forward which deals Light damage.

However, since they only fire the holy beam straight forward, their attacks can be easily avoided by ducking and attacking. They drop the Unicorn card.
