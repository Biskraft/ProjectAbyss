Block 5 for *Castlevania* are the dungeons and crypts of the castle.

## Overview
Hunchbacks and bone-throwing skeletons swarm Simon in the stage's opening, but upon ascending upward, blood skeletons and hunchbacks will appear as well. In the next room the axe knights make their entrance. Next up is an area with several torture devices in the background. Here blood skeletons and axe knights are around as well. In the next area, there are more blood skeletons combined with bone pillars. In the final hall, several axe knights have to be fought while Medusa Heads come swooping in.
