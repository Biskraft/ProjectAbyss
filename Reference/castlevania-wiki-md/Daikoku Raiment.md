# Daikoku Raiment

The **Daikoku Raiment** is a Japanese coat that increases your luck. It is an item exclusive to *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and can be found in the downloadable stage Lord of Flies, along with the Benten Raiment.

Equipping this item increases the amount of money dropped from candles by 1 tier (money drops when MP is full). The default tiers are $1, $10, $50. Wearing this raiment, as well as two Gold Rings, raises the player's tier by 1 for each. Granting $100, $500 or $1,000 from candles when equipping all three.
