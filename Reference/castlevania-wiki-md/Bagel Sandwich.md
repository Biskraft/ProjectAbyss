# Bagel Sandwich

The **Bagel Sandwich** is a food item that recovers HP. In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, it is a two star item that grants +80 HP and +5 CON. This is the second highest amount for any food item, but is tied with several other items. Of those that only give an HP and CON boost, its HP surpasses that of the one star Fried Chicken (+18), but is surpassed by the three star Boneless Ham (+129). It gives less CON than the two star Fried Crab Claw (+10), but gives more HP (+70).
