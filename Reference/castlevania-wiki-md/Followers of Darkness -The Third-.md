# Followers of Darkness -The Third-

- Followers of Darkness -The Third-** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the second common boss theme of the game. It plays when fighting the Skeleton Diver, the Minotaurus and Dullahan.

The track was later featured in Pachislot Akumajō Dracula. It plays during a segment in the game where Trevor Belmont battles against Medusa.
