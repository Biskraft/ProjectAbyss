# Magic Circle

right

  - Magic Circle** is an Devil-Type Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is readily available for use by the Gale evolution which turns both Hector and the I.D. into a powder-like substance before converging into the circle.

This skill is used for moving through small cracks found on the base of some walls. It is required for entering the Aiolon Ruins through the cracked gate in Cordova Town as well for exploring many secret locations in the game, particularly the cracked wall in Garibaldi Temple that provides a passage to the optional boss Nuculais.

It costs 10 Hearts upon activation, and additionally drains 1 Heart per second while in effect. This skill has no offensive capabilities, but the Brow evolution provides this with new abilities M. Circle Scissors and Needle Magic Circle.
