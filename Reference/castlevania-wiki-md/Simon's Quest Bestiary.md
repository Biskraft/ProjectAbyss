# Simon's Quest Bestiary

List of monsters that appear in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.

Names are from the English manual when available and in the order they appear in the manual. Art is from the Japanese manual. Hit Points listed is the number of "Leather Whip" hits it would take to destroy the enemy during the day if they are out during the day, or at night if not.

Please see Simon's Quest World Map for a map of the locations referenced in this Bestiary.
