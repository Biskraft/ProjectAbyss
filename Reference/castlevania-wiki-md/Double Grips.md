# Double Grips

The **Double Grips** are bracelets in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. Their power is released when both are equipped.

## Characteristics
The Double Grips are dropped by the Dark Armors in the Observation Tower. They won't have any effect unless one is equipped on each arm, meaning that at least two have to be collected in order to release their power; then they will give a cumulative of +75 STR, DEF, INT and LCK. This is three times the amount gained if two Star Bracelets were equipped (although it's not necessary to equip both of those in order to gain their benefit). This is by far the greatest amount of statistical boost the player can get from two arm equipment.
