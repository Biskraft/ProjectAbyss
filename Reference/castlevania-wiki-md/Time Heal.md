# Time Heal

right

  - Time Heal** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is learned by the Leaffle evolution of the Fairy-Type. Unlike the instant Heal and its other three upgrades, this skill gradually replenishes Hector's HP for about 10~15 seconds, with a total output of 33 HP. It only costs 6 Hearts to cast. If Hector is not damaged for the duration of the spell, Time Heal proves to be superior than the ordinary Heal ability.

## Notes
- Under the effect of Time Heal, Hector will be surrounded by blue sparks.
