# Phlegeton

right|280px
  - Phlegeton** is a massive area attack involving fire which somewhat resembles the surface of the Sun. It is Charlotte Aulin's signature Dual Crush attack in Castlevania: Harmony of Despair. Flames spread across the floor, walls, and ceiling, and loops of flames churn for several seconds.

Phlegeton is named after one of the rivers of Hades, as is its icy counterpart, Cocytus.
