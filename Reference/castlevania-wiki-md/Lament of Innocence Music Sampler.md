# Lament of Innocence Music Sampler

This Limited Edition Sampler of *Lament of Innocence* was released on October 18, 2003, with music composed by Michiru Yamane from *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. This CD was given at a press conference about Lament of Innocence.
