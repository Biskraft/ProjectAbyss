# Phase Shifter

right|250px

  - Phase Shifter** is an ability in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over Paranoia's soul and it enables Soma to visit a parallel world by entering mirrors. Obtaining this soul is essential in order to get the good ending of the game. It is required to enter the big ornate mirror in the Pinnacle during Dario's fight and instead wage battle against Aguni.

## Mirrors of Paranoia
Many other mirrors are scattered throughout the castle, which can also be entered and contain powerful items.
