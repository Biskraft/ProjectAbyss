# Castlevania: Lords of Shadow PlayStation Home Virtual Items

A set of virtual items that can be worn in the virtual world of PlayStation Home from the game *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. This is the first time Konami has offered items in PlayStation Home and were released at the same time that *Contra* virtual items were also made available. A Laura outfit was later made available.

- Lords of Shadow* items available are:
- Gabriel Belmont's Brotherhood of Light Armor
- Cyclone Boots
- Combat Cross
- Dark Gauntlet
