  - Evil Invitation** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is a boss theme that plays when battling the Flying Armor, Balore, Malphas, Rahab and Bat Company. It's also played at the beginning of the game when Celia Fortner summons a few basic monsters to attack Soma Cruz to get an idea of how well he handles combat.
