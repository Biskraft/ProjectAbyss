# Lucky Clover

The **Lucky Clover** is an accessory in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is an earring shaped like four-leaf clovers with lapis lazuli stones embedded on them.

## Availability
This accessory can be purchased at Jacob's shop once Shanoa completes Laura's first quest, "A Lucky Stone", by finding and bringing her a Lapis Lazuli.

## Characteristics
The Lucky Clover grants LCK +3 and replaces the Lucky Charm found in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.

## Trivia
- Its price at Jacob's shop is 777G, a number frequently associated with good luck or jackpot numbers for slot machines.
- This item is probably a nod to the properties the Lapis Lazuli had as a luck-boosting item in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.
