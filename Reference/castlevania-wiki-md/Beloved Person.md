  - Beloved Person** is a music theme composed by Soshiro Hokkai for *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is Lydie Erlanger's theme. It plays when she is encountered in the Castle Top Floor, shortly before she is abducted by Death.

An arrangement of the song titled **Epilogue 2** plays during the game's best ending.
