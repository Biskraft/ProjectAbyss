# Chest (Mirror of Fate)

- Chests** in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate* can be found throughout the castle. They improve the maximum capacity of the player's Health Bar or Magic Bar or the number of Secondary Weapons the player can hold. In order to retrieve the item inside the chest, a quick time event involving pressing a button rapidly is required. If the event is failed, it is possible to try again.
