# EX Potion

70px|right
An **EX Potion** (also called **Elixer**, **Elixir**, **Potion Ex**, **XS Potion** or **Super Potion**) is a potion that restores all HP. This is more than a High Potion or a normal Potion.

In *Order of Shadows*, the use of the item has been switched to that of the Super Tonic: instead of restoring HP, it restores MP.
