# Traveler Cape

The **Traveler Cape** is the second least valuable cape in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is grants +3 DEF, which is one more than a normal Cape, but one less than a Crimson Cloak.

The Traveler Cape in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* is the only cloak that offers resistance to Slash type attacks (+5). It gives the best stats of any of the two-star capes, with the standard +5 defense against Fire and Ice, HP +5, MP +3, and DEF +4.
