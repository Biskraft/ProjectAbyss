# Empress Ring

The **Empress Ring** is the ring of the Tarot persona, [The Empress](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:RWS_Tarot_03_Empress.jpg), in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It increases physical and magical offensive statistics (Strength and Intelligence by 2) at the greater expense of their correlating defensive statistics (Constitution and Mind by 3). It is the counterpart to the Priestess Ring.
