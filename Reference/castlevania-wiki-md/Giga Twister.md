# Giga Twister

right|300px
  - Giga Twister**, or **Mega Twister**, is an ability that summons a gigantic axe which twists around the screen, damaging just about everything. Soma Cruz is able to gain this ability from the soul of a Red Minotaur in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, and from a Great Axe Armor in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is generally one of Soma's most damaging attacks. If aimed properly, it can hit the same enemy twice, dealing a massive amount of damage.
