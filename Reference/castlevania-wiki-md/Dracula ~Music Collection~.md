This bonus CD was released on March 20, 1997 Japanese *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It contains music from various early *Castlevania* games composed by Konami Kukeiha Club. It is also known as "**Symphony of the Night: Limited Edition Soundtrack Sampler**".
