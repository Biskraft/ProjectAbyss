# Konami Kukeiha Club

right|300px

 is the sound team of Konami. The team composed several soundtracks for various Konami games, including the *Castlevania* series.

## Members
### Known members
- Akezo Ankeina
- Kazuo Hashi
- Kenichi Mitsuda
- Mami Asano
- Metal Yuhki (Mikio Saitou)
- Tyltyl Yamane (Michiru Yamane)
- Nazo² Suzuki

### Former members
- Miki Higashino
- Motoaki Furukawa
- Tappi Iwase
- James Banana (Kinuyo Yamashita)

## *Castlevania* discography
  - Akumajō Dracula Famicom Best*
  - Akumajō Dracula Best 2*
  - Perfect Selection Dracula ~New Classic~*
  - Akumajō Dracula X*
  - Castlevania: Circle of the Moon and Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance Original Soundtrack*
  - Akumajō Dracula Best Music Collections BOX*
