# Eugen

- Eugen** is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. He is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. He is the town's local blacksmith.

## Description
Being a carpenter, and for what can be seen, also a blacksmith, Eugen has a cold demeanor. However, he will be willing to help Shanoa out by crafting her armor. He will ask her to bring him diverse materials she finds during her travels, these being Iron, Silver and Gold Ores, in order to start crafting wares of a better quality each time, respectively.

Curiously, he will never ask her to bring him a Copper Ore.
