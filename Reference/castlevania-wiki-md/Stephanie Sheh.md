# Stephanie Sheh

- Stephanie Ru-Phan Sheh**, also known as **Jennifer Sekiguchi**, is a Chinese-American voice actress and ADR director. She provided the voice of Charlotte Aulin and Loretta Lecarde in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* (reprising the former for *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*), and Eric Lecarde in *Castlevania Judgment*. She also served as the English ADR voice director for *Judgment*.

Stephanie is well known for her fans as the voice of Orihime Inoue in *Bleach*, Eureka in *Eureka Seven* and Kyrie in *Devil May Cry 4*.
