# Reverse Colosseum

The **Reverse Colosseum** is an upside down version of the Colosseum in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. A chaotic, red glow pulses around the architecture in this version.

The Reverse Colosseum can first be entered from the left from the Anti-Chapel. It leads to Death Wing's Lair.

This area's theme is Door of Holy Spirits.
