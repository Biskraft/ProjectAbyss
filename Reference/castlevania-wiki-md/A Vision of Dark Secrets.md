# A Vision of Dark Secrets

- A Vision of Dark Secrets** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Bloodlines*, heard in the introductory cutscene to the game.

An arrangement of this theme is also featured in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* with the first appearance of Dracula and Camilla at the beginning of the game.
