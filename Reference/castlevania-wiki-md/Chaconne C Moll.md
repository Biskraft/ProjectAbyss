  - Chaconne C Moll** is a music theme composed by Y. Iwata, T. Ohsono and K. Okada, exclusive to the Sega Saturn version of *Akumajō Dracula X ~Gekka no Nocturne~* (*Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*). It is the background theme played in the Cursed Prison, which itself is also exclusive to this version of the game.
