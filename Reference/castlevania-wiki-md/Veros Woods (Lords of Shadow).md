  - Veros Woods** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the first stage of Chapter 5.

:*So it seems Pan has saved Gabriel once more; his arrival is most fortuitous! Gabriel now enters the outskirts of the vampire's territory. A cold winter has gripped this land for centuries, driving out the inhabitants to warmer climes. Those stubborn enough to stay have contended with the cold death that visits them each and every night, taking their loved ones away forever. The vampires thrive in the cold and terrorize the living who crave warmth and companionship. This night, a warrior will come. A knight in shining armor with death at his side. He comes for you.
