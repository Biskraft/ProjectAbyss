# Crab Monsters

A few games in the *Castlevania* series have included crab-like enemies; three different types have appeared so far. They were first introduced in the non-canonical games *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun* and *Kid Dracula* (Game Boy), where they appeared as cute little robots resembling crabs. They later appeared as bizarre crawling skull monstrosities in *Rondo of Blood*, to finally appear as a menacing giant crab boss in *Order of Ecclesia*.

All of these enemies, even if they belong to different series, have used bubbles as one of their forms of attack.

## Appearances
### *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun* / *Kid Dracula* (Game Boy)
- Boku Dracula-kun* and *Kid Dracula* were the first to introduce crab-like enemies, where they appeared as robot crabs that crawled in between indentations of metallic walls.

### *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*
In *Rondo of Blood*, the rather disturbing Skull Crab made its debut into the series. It was a crawling skull consisting of a skull with legs made of bones. It crawled along the ground and emitted foamy bubbles to attack, or eventually lashed out with its rather long and pointy tongue.


### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
Brachyura, a giant crab which could scale among the walls of the Lighthouse, appeared as *Order of Ecclesia*'s third boss.
