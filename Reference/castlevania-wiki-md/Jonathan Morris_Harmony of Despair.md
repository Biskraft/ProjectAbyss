  - Jonathan Morris** appears as a playable character in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. He is one of the five starting characters, alonside his partner, Charlotte.

## Background
A young man whose clan the Belmonts entrusted with their sacred whip, the Vampire Killer. While he comes across as less than serious, he never backs away from his responsibilities: he chose to fight Dracula even with his knowledge that the whip would steal part of his life to make up for his lack of experience. Jonathan can learn a variety of Martial Arts.

## Sub-Weapons
Jonathan is able to equip up to four Sub-Weapons as his Hunter Skill. These sub-weapons level up as they are used. As they level up, Jonathan's Vampire Killer also gains in attack power. A glitch was confirmed that putting Down-B subskills can be spammed (useful for training them on Survival Mode). Hold Down while pressing LB and B at the same time at fast speeds to spam.


## Martial Arts
Jonathan can perform any martial arts he has earned by following a specific button combination. All of his martial arts are unique to him, with the exception of Uppercut, which Julius and Richter can also use.
