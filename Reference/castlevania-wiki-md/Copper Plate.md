# Copper Plate

The **Copper Plate** (sometimes called **Bronze Cuirass** or **Bronze Armor**) is breastplate made of copper or bronze and is usually one of the first and weakest armors encountered in the game, being a step above Leather Armor and a step below Iron Armor.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, the Bronze Cuirass (called **Copper Breastplate** in Japan) gives a Defense of +3, which is one more than a Hide Cuirass and one less than the Iron Cuirass. It also is the same amount as the special armor Mirror Cuirass, which gives protection against Stone.

In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, the Bronze Armor increases Defense by +50, which is better than Leather Armor (+30), but not as good as Gold Armor (+80).

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it gives a Defense boost of +18, which is 4 more than a Cloth Tunic, but 2 less than Rock Leather.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, the Copper Plate gives +18 DEF, which is 9 more than Leather Plate and 7 less than Iron Plate.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, the Copper Plate gives Jonathan +9 Defense, which is 5 more than a Leather Plate and 6 less than an Iron Plate.

In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, the Copper Plate grants a +8 Defense boost, which is is 3 more than a Leather Cuirass and 10 less than an Iron Plate.
