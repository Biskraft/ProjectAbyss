# Famicom Fūnji

is a manga by Shigeto Ikehara, published by Shogakukan and CoroCoro Comic and serialized in Comic BonBon from 1985 to 1987 in Japan. *Castlevania* appears as one of the many games featured in the series.

## Plot
Three young software programmers called Ken, Kato and Takuya are hired by "NES", a video game company, but a mysterious rival company wants to take over the NES software, so these three young heroes will have to embark on a fierce battle against it in order to save their company from destruction.
