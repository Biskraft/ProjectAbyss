The **Abbey Catacombs** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the third stage of Chapter 5.

## Zobek's journal
:*We delve deep below the abbey ready to face the evil confronting us. The abbot, Vincent Dorin, was once a good and kindly man who helped the people of Wygol, but now he has become a devious coward who has holed himself up in the abbey and rigged it with traps of cunning. The people, unprotected, have paid a heavy price for his treachery. Night has fallen outside, and we will be open to vampire attack at any time. We must be on our guard. We must work together, if we are to succeed in acquiring the relic that the abbot guards. This will help us greatly in defeating the vampire horde later.*
