# Aguni Fire

right|250px

  - Aguni Fire** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It grants Soma the ability to create a pillar of fire that races across the ground.

## Availability
Obtained upon defeating Aguni at the top of The Pinnacle. To fight this boss, the player must first have obtained Paranoia's soul in order to enter the large mirror located inside of Dario Bossi's room in his rematch boss fight.

## Trivia
- This spell may be the base of Dracula's iconic yet unnamed flame pillars attack (first seen in *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*), as this was a power only the Dark Lord Candidates were able to control (the same way as Dmitrii mastered Dracula's Power of Dominance).
