  - Castlevania 20th Anniversary Deluxe Music Collection** is a soundtrack album that came included with pre-ordered *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* games. It contains music from many *Castlevania* games throughout the series. The composers are Konami Kukeiha Club, Michiru Yamane (main composer since *Symphony of the Night*), Soshiro Hokkai (*Harmony of Dissonance*), Masahiko Kimura (*Dawn of Sorrow*) and Yuzo Koshiro (*Portrait of Ruin*). It was released on November 28th, 2006.
