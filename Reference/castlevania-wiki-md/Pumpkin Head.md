# Pumpkin Head

The **Pumpkin Head** is a mask in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* shaped like the character Pumpkin's head. It can be evolved from an Iron Mask combined with a Miracle Egg. It only adds +5 Defense, though. It can be evolved into the Pumpkin Mace, which is needed in order to recruit the Pumpkin Innocent Devil.
