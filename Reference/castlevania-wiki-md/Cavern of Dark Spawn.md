# Cavern of Dark Spawn

- Cavern of Dark Spawn** is a music theme featured in the Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles Original Soundtrack. It is a heavy metal arrangement of "Den", a song originally featured in both *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and *Castlevania: Dracula X*, although an obvious arrangement of Vampire Killer can be heard throughout the track as well.
