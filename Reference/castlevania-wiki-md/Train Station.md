# Train Station

The **Train Station** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It is an area within the Science District.

## Overview
The railway travels from the Science District and ends in the Victory Plaza. After the train mission, the train tunnel can be used to cross between the two districts.

### The train
Dracula searches for the antidote on a runaway train that speeds through the city. The train transports the Scientist passenger and is patrolled by Golgoth Guards. Dracula invades the train and has a mini-boss battle with a powerful demon Soldier. The train becomes more unstable and eventually wrecks.
