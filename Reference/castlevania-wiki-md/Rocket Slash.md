# Rocket Slash

right|200px

  - Rocket Slash** is one of Jonathan Morris's sub-weapon skills in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. Upon activation, Jonathan will dash forward, becoming invincible for a brief moment and dealing damage upon landing to the other side to all enemies in his way (using this skill right in front of a pit will make Jonathan fall into it).
