# Corinthian Helmet

right|73px

The **Corinthian Helmet** (called **Greek Helm** in *Harmony of Dissonance)* is a type of Greek helmet found in Castlevania.

The Greek Helm in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* gives a Defense of +9, which is one more than a Bicocette, but one less than a Face-Guard.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the Corinthian Helmet is the first in a series of upgrades to the basic Leather Helm that can be made which doubles the helmet's Defense. It can be created by combining the Leather Helm with Aluminum, which can be found in the Abandoned Castle, doubling its Defense from +2 to +4. The helmet's DEF can be doubled again by combining it with Sun Tears, which can first be stolen from the Ifreets at the end of the Baljhet Mountains, which creates a Thracian Helm, whose defense can be doubled one last time with Carbon Steel and Knight's Vein to form a Barrel Helm (DEF +16).
