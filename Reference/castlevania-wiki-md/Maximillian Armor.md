# Maximillian Armor

- Maximillian Armor** is a very light, aerodynamic, and smooth armor that is actually very tough.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the Maximillian Armor is the toughest armor of the game. It can be created by adding an Angel Halo and two Holy Man's Veins to Parade Armor, raising its defense from 45 to 50. It has a slightly better overall statistic boost than Dracula's Clothes, which offers 30 Defense, 10 Strength and Constitution, but a -5 Luck penalty.
