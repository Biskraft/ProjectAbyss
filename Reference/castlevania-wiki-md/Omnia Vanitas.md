# Omnia Vanitas

right|250px
  - Omnia Vanitas** (Latin for **All is Vanity**) is an evading technique used by Julius Belmont in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. When the player uses it, Julius becomes invincible and travels a large distance forward, turning around while performing it. He can pass through enemies without being harmed. It also allows him to pass through the various fire walls that block chests and passages unharmed.

In *Aria of Sorrow*, it's possible to perform Omnia Vanitas in the air once continuing the previous one. In this case, Julius lifts his leg slightly higher than when he uses it on the ground.
