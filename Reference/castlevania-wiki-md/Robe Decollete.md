# Robe Decollete

right|50px

The **Robe Decollete** (from French *décolleté*, cleavage) is an elegant low-cut dress for the most formal of events. It has higher DEF than any other dress found in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, *Order of Ecclesia* or *Harmony of Despair*, and boosts several different statistics.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The Robe Decollete can be found inside a secret room at The Throne Room. To find it, the player must destroy the ceiling of the room before the chamber where the final battle with Dracula and Death will take place. Along with the dress, Charlotte's Speed Up spell can also be found.

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
In *Order of Ecclesia*, it can be found inside the rare treasure box at the end of the Training Hall. Since the content of the box is random each time Shanoa enters the level, it may take many tries until the dress is finally found.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
It was made available in *Harmony of Despair* in the DLC stage Beauty, Desire, Situation Dire (Chapter 7) and can be obtained from the boss Astarte on Hard Mode. It replaced Europa's Dress as the most valuable dress in the game and that which provides the highest Defense. In addition to its boost in HP, MP, CON and MND, it also offers protection against many elemental attack types. The dress was enhanced during a patch in December of 2010 to also offer a STR and INT boost, as well as increase the rate of MP recovery by 0.4 per second.
