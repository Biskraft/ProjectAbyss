  - Vs The Path of the Lycan** is an original music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula: Lords of Shadow*.

Based on the track's title, it is presumably the background music that plays during a segment where Cornell and/or his pack of Lycanthropes is encountered.
