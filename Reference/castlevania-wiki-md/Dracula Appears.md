  - Dracula Appears** is a brief music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is played during the scene when Leon discovers that Mathias has become a vampire and that it was him the mastermind behind the events of the game.

The track contains an arrangement of Melancholy Joachim and a reprise from the Prologue theme.
