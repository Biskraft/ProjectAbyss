# Ruler Sword

The **Ruler Sword**, also known as **Spectral Sword**, is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is a sword controlled by an evil will. Causes poltergeist phenomena.

## Description
The Ruler Sword is a sword possessed by poltergeist-causing spirits. It is able to levitate and control a variety of other weapons, which sometimes circle around it or move about in random paths. Sometimes these include shields, which the Ruler Sword can use to protect itself. Destroying the central Ruler Sword also destroys all the other weapons being controlled by it.

## Appearances
### *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*
The Ruler Sword makes its debut in *Rondo of Blood*, where it appears as a one-time enemy the player must destroy in order to continue through the Ghost Ship. Each of its swords and the Ruler Sword itself die in one hit, but the Ruler Sword usually resides at the top of the screen, using the other weapons to attack for it.

### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
The Spectral Sword in the Reverse Castle drops the Mablung Sword, which is a better replacement for the Shield Rod. If the player carries the Duplicator and a bomb item that can destroy the shields, they can farm the room quickly. By killing the spears and shields with a bomb, the player will be able to gain EXP very fast and max out a familiar in approximately 20-30 minutes.

### *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*
The Spectral Sword returns in Stage 3 of *The Adventure ReBirth*, this time as a boss. The sword resembles two Final Swords from *Aria of Sorrow*, which sometimes open up to spawn new weapons. The weapons, in this case, are daggers, axes, swords, and lances which circle around to protect it. It can stop to fly into the ground, along with any remaining weapons.
