# Akumajō Densetsu SOUND TRACKS

- Akumajō Densetsu SOUND TRACKS** (**Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse SOUND TRACKS**) is the original soundtrack for the game *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*. It is a new recording, released on October 7, 2014. It is the first time tracks from *Dracula's Curse* are separated in the track list.
