# Explosive Daggers

right|200px

  - Explosive Daggers** are the result of throwing a Silver Dagger while using Shadow Magic. Some enemies are weaker or stronger to Explosive Daggers than they are to regular Silver Daggers.


## Item Data


## Damage
Flammable enemies tend to be weaker to Explosive Daggers than they are to normal Silver Daggers. Most enemies that are tolerant or very tolerant to Silver Daggers are weak to Explosive Daggers. Enemies that are very tolerant to silver daggers are not any less tolerant to Explosive Daggers. Enemies that are already weak to Silver Daggers are usually not any more weak to Explosive Daggers.
