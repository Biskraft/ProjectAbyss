# Leviathan

The name **Leviathan** could refer to:
    - Lesser Demon** - A magical demon that was renamed as "Leviathan" in the Western localization of *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*.
    - Spawns of Leviathan** - An enemy included as part of the *Reverie* downloadable content for *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.
    - Leviathan** - One of the Titan bosses in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*.
