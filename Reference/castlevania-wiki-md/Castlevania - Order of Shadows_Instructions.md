# Castlevania: Order of Shadows/Instructions

## IN-GAME HELP MENU

WELCOME TO 
CASTLEVANIA

SPLASH SCREENS

- PRESS EXIT SOFT KEY TO EXIT GAME.
- PRESS OK OR 5 TO SKIP SPLASH SCREENS.

MENUS
- PRESS BACK SOFT KEY TO BACK OUT OF A MENU.
- PRESS EXIT SOFTKEY TO EXIT GAME.
- PRESS CONTINUE SOFTKEY TO CONTINUE TO NEXT SCREEN.
- PRESS LEFT OR 4 TO CHOOSE PREVIOUS OPTION.
- PRESS RIGHT OR 6 TO CHOOSE NEXT OPTION
- PRESS OK OR 5 TO SELECT AN OPTION
- PRESS UP, 2, DOWN, OR 8 TO SCROLL UP AND DOWN.

NAME ENTRY SCREEN
- PRESS BACK SOFT KEY TO GO BACK TO MAIN MENU IF YOU ARE ON THE FIRST LETTER
- PRESS LEFT, 4, OR CLEAR SOFTKEY TO ERASE CURRENT LETTER
- PRESS RIGHT OR 6 TO MOVE TO NEXT ENTRY SPACE
- PRESS UP, 2, DOWN, OR 8 TO CHOOSE A DIFFERENT LETTER FOR THE CURRENT ENTRY SPACE.
- PRESS OK OR 5 TO CONFIRM NAME.

IN-GAME BASIC MOVES
- PRESS BACK SOFTKEY TO PAUSE THE GAME
- PRESS SKIP SOFTKEY TO SKIP A CUTSCENE
- PRESS OK OR 5 TO USE WHIP
- PRESS LEFT OR RIGHT ON DIRECTIONAL PAD AND/OR 4 AND 6 ON THE KEYPAD TO MOVE DESMOND LEFT AND RIGHT
- PRESS DOWN OR 8 TO CROUCH
- PRESS UP OR 2 TO JUMP UP
- PRESS AND HOLD UP TO MAKE DESMUND JUMP FORWARD
- PRESSING THE OPPOSITE DIRECTION DURING A JUMP OR FALL WILL MAKE DESMOND FACE IN THAT DIRECTION
- SUB-WEAPONS, ALCHEMY AND ITEMS ARE USED BY PRESSING 9 OR DOWN, THEN FORWARD
- ALCHEMY ITEMS CAN ONLY BE USED ONCE PER ITEM PICKED UP
- DIFFERENT SUB-WEAPONS WILL CONSUME THE HEART METER AT DIFFERENT RATES

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
- ADDITIONAL MOVES WILL BE ACQUIRED THROUGH DESMOND'S QUEST, ON-SCREEN PROMPTS WILL INDICATE HOW THESE MOVES ARE PERFORMED
- IF YOU CANNOT REACH A CERTAIN AREA, COME BACK TO IT AFTER DESMOND HAS LEARNED MORE MOVES
- DESMOND WILL EARN LEVELS AND INCREASED STATISTICS IN BOTH POWER AND DEFENSE AS HE LEVELS UP, KILL MORE ENEMIES TO EARN EXPERIENCE
- SOME ENEMIES WILL REQUIRE MORE THAN ONE HIT TO KILL
- CANDLES AND CONTAINERS DROP HEARTS, ITEMS AND WEAPONS.  TRY COMING BACK TO THE SAME CONTAINER, IT MIGHT DROP SOMETHING YOU LIKE.
- DESMOND BEGINS HIS QUEST WITH THE LEATHER WHIP, LOOK CAREFULLY AND YOU MAY FIND THE FLAME WHIP AND VAMPIRE KILLER.
- BEAT THE GAME TO UNLOCK HARD MODE AND CLASSIC MUSIC.
- BEAT THE GAME ON HARD MODE TO UNLOCK UNLIMITED ITEMS.

ABOUT CASTLEVANIA
- CASTLEVANIA FIRST APPEARED IN JAPAN IN 1986.
- IN JAPAN, CASTLEVANIA IS KNOWN AS DEMON CASTLE DRACULA.
- SIMON BELMONT IS THE FIRST BELMONT TO APPEAR IN ANY CASTLEVANIA GAME.
- KOJI IGARASHI IS THE PRODUCER OF THE CASTLEVANIA SERIES.
