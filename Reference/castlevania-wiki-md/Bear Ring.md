# Bear Ring

The **Bear Ring** is a ring in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* that holds the "curse of the bear", and is dropped by the Skeleton Medalist. Its effect is to lose 100 STR, DEF, INT, and LCK, with no apparent benefits. However, if the player uses the Pluto + Black Dog DSS combination, Nathan will turn into a bear instead of a Skeleton.

The bear is Bear Tank from *Rakugakids*, who attacks by throwing different kinds of bombs.
