# Yasuhiro Ichihashi

is a Japanese composer who formerly worked for Konami.

Besides his works in the console releases of *pop'n music*, he also composed music for the *Castlevania* series, including the Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia Original Soundtrack (along with Michiru Yamane) and Castlevania: Harmony of Despair Original Soundtrack. He also arranged several tracks on Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles Original Soundtrack and the first two *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula* games.

Currently, he is the president of Studio Halfmoon.
