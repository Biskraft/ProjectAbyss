# Arcade Stage 4

The fourth stage consists of the exterior of the castle and includes the Rampart and Lookout Tower sections. In the Rampart section, a Witch armed with a magic staff harasses you while disappearing and reappearing while Skeletons and Skeleton Soldiers climb over the wall in order to attack you. Sword Lords are also present and the Werewolf returns to finish his battle, backed up with Flea Men and Axe Armors. When the Werewolf is defeated, the bell tolls and torches along the path to the Lookout Tower are lit up. At the tower itself, a Hanging Skeleton tries to startle you. The Harpy then attacks, with the Clock Tower in the backdrop. When enough damage is inflicted on the Harpy, it falls the entire height of the tower to its death.
