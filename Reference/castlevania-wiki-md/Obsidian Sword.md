# Obsidian Sword

right|218px
The **Obsidian Sword** is the most powerful 2-handed Great Sword in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* which can thrust. It is only slightly more powerful than the similar Zwei Hander, but only two-thirds as powerful as the Great Sword (which lacks the thrusting ability).
