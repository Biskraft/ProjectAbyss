# Dodge

- Dodges** can be performed by moving while blocking in *Lords of Shadow* games. They can be used to avoid unblockable attacks. Dracula and Alucard can knock down some enemies in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2* and *Revelations* when moving into them with this maneuver.

## Trivia
- Gabriel, Trevor, Simon and Victor Belmont dodge by utilizing a short roll.
- Dracula and Alucard dodge by dashing shoulder first with a quick burst of speed.
  - This is due to their vampiric speed.
- Laura dodges in a similar manner to Dracula, but utilizes her mist form.
  - This makes Laura the only character who is invulnerable while in mid-dodge.
