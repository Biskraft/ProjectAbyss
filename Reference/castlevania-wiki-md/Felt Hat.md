# Felt Hat

A **Felt Hat** is basic head protection. In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it is the weakest Head Gear that increases both DEF (+1) and INT (+1). It is less useful than a Velvet Hat, which increases 2 INT (along with the 1 DEF).
