# Crystal (disambiguation)

The term **Crystal** can refer to one of the following:
    - Magic Crystal** — A crystal sphere that marks the end of a stage in most *Castlevania* titles and is often picked up after defeating the area boss.
    - White**, **Blue** and **Red Crystals** — Crystal balls Simon Belmont can acquire in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. Each of these has their own unique properties.
    - Crystal** — An item appearing in all three Game Boy-related *Castlevania* titles that acts as a power-up for the whip.
    - Flashing Crystal** — Summons the stage boss upon pick up in *Castlevania: The Adventure*, *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge* and *Castlevania Legends*.
    - Purifying Crystal** — Consumable item that cures vampirism in both *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.
    - Laser Crystal** — An environmental enemy appearing in the Tower of Sorcery in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.
    - Crystal Laser of Doom** — A boss in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.
    - Crystal** — A sub-weapon introduced in the 2003 Sony PlayStation 2 game *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*.
