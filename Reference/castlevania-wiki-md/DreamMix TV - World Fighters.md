# DreamMix TV: World Fighters

is a multiplayer, crossover fighting video game developed by Bitstep and published by Konami, Hudson Soft and Takara for the GameCube and PlayStation 2 gaming consoles. It was released for both systems on December 18, 2003 exclusively in Japan and features licensed characters from these three well-known gaming companies.

The game features several Konami mascot fighters, including Simon Belmont from *Castlevania Chronicles*, TwinBee from the *TwinBee* series, Solid Snake from the *Metal Gear* series and Power Pro-kun from the *MLB Power Pros* series.
