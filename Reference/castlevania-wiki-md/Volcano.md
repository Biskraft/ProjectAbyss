# Volcano

right|250px
  - Volcano** is a Dual Crush attack in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. When activated, an erupting volcano appears which launches flaming rocks at various arcs, making it a powerful but somewhat inaccurate means of attack.

The volcano resembles those of the *Gradius* series.
