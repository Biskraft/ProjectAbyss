# Deadly Absorb

right
  - Deadly Absorb** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is learned by the Wingosaurus evolution of the Bird-Type. For 30 Hearts, this ability allows the Innocent Devil to absorb the vitality of the nearby enemies, healing Hector in the process.

This is the only Bird-Type ability with the capability of healing Hector.
