The **Brutal Moray** is an enemy in *Castlevania Chronicles*.

## Description
Brutal Morays are giant eels which are only found in the Underground Aqueducts. They lie in wait in narrow pools of water, clacking their jaws and waiting for prey to get within reach; they then spring out and curve toward it. Some of them will try to ambush their victims from both sides.
