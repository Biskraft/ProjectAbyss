# Encyclopedia of Castlevania

- Encyclopedia of Castlevania** is a Japanese two-page spread published in 2001 containing a timeline and storyline for *Castlevania*. It includes some background details relevant to Dracula's origins that would soon be explored in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, including his birth year (now known as Mathias Cronqvist) as 1000 AD and that Lisa resembles Dracula's former wife (now known as Elisabetha).
