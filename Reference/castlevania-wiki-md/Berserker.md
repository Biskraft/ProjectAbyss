# Berserker

right|200px**Berserker** is an ability or spell that increases STR while conferring some form of penalty to the player.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
Berserker in an ability in *Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over Lubicant's soul and allows Soma to gain more stats (mainly deal more damage) the lower his HP is.
Soma will receive a different bonus to each stat each time he loses a certain amount of HP:
- Every 10 HP lost, he will gain 1.5 ATK
- Every 200 HP lost, he will gain 1.5DEF
- Every 10 HP lost, he will gain 1.43 STR
- Every 100 HP lost, he will gain 1.47 CON
- Every 10 HP lost, he will gain 1.15 INT

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Berserker is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Portrait of Ruin*. This spell increases STR, but the player is limited to attacking only.
