# Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia Original Soundtrack

- Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia Original Soundtrack** is the two CDs complete soundtrack for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, while the Promo Soundtrack only has six tracks. Music was composed by Michiru Yamane and Yasuhiro Ichihashi. It was released on October 23, 2008, but only through Konami Japan's online shop.

### Trivia
- "An Empty Tome" is also Shanoa's theme in *Castlevania Judgment*.
- All of the songs have different names between the Japanese official OST and how they appear in-game in the Western releases. Many of them are alternate translations or spellings, and the boss battle themes reference the subtitles of previous *Castlevania* games, for example:
  - "Symphony of Battle" (originally "Fighting Soul Rhapsody"), a reference to *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.
  - "Dissonant Courage" (originally "Warped Black Cold"), a reference to *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.
  - "Sorrow's Distortion", a reference to *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*/*Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.
  - "Lament to the Master" (originally "Indignant Locus"), a reference to *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*.
  - "Chamber of Ruin" (originally "Insane Expectations"), a reference to *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*.
  - "Order of the Demon", a reference to *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*.
- An arrangement of Vampire Killer can be heard in "Ecclesia".
