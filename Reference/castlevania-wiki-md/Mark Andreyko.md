  - Mark Andreyko** is an American comic book writer and screenwriter, known for his work on books such as *Manhunter* and *Wonder Woman '77* for DC Comics, and *Torso* for Image Comics.

He was the screenwriter behind the comic miniseries *Castlevania: The Belmont Legacy*.
