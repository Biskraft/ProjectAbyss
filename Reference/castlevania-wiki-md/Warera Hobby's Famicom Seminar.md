# Warera Hobby's Famicom Seminar

is a Japanese manga by Aoki Kei, serialized in Hobby's Jump and published by Shueisha from June 1988 to January 1991, and later released in four *tankōbon* volumes. It was later re-released under the title  on January 29, 2016 in Japan.

The *Castlevania* series is featured in the manga.
