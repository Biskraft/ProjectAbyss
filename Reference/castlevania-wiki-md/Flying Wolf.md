The **Flying Wolf** (referred to as **Smaller Dragon** in Nintendo Power) is a miniboss in *Castlevania Legends*.

## Description
The Flying Wolf was created by combining the abilities of a bat and a wolf. It is fought midway through the Clock Tower (Stage 3).

When encountered, it will first pace back and forth a couple of times and then go airborne, spewing three fireballs downward. It will then fly to the other side of the screen and repeat the same attack until defeated.
