# Absolute Zero Ring

right|73px
The **Absolute Zero Ring** (called **Arctic Ring** in *Lament of Innocence* and **Ring of Extreme Cold** in the Japanese versions) offers resistance against Ice based attacks. It is found in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
