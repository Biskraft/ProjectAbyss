# Classic Demons

- Classic Demons** are one of several types of demons that fit the *cliché* of a male demon usually depicted with wings and horns. Classic demons can generally be ranked as follows: Tiny Devils, Lesser Demons, Devils, Arc Demons, Demon Lord. Some classic demons specialize in elemental powers, such as the Fire Demon, Thunder Demon, Frost Demon and Earth Demon. Some classic demons that represent specific demons from literature are armed with pitchforks, such as Lubicant, Cagnazzo, Barbariccia, Draghignazzo, Malacoda and Skull Millione.
