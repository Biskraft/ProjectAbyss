# 7 Bladed Sword

right|73px
The **7 Bladed Sword** is a weapon that is rarely used but has an awesome potential. It is one of the relatively rare elemental weapons in the game, and deals Dark damage. Each time it hits, a small orb recovers Hector's HP by 1 point.

## Origins
The  is a sword of continental manufacture believed to be identical with the artifact of that name. As a gift of the king of Baekje, it was bestowed upon a Yamato ruler who is mentioned in the Nihon Shoki in the fifty-second year of the reign of the semi-mythical Empress Jingū. The original sword has been conserved since antiquity in the Isonokami Shrine in Nara Prefecture, Japan and is not on public display. An inscription on the side of the blade is an important source for understanding the relationships between kingdoms of the Korean peninsula and Japan in that period.
