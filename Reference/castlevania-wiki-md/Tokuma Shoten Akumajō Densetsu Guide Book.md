# Tokuma Shoten Akumajō Densetsu Guide Book

right|250px

A Japanese guide book to *Akumajō Densetsu* (known in the US as *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*) by Tokuma Shoten. It features original artwork of the characters in various situations. It is possible that the artwork contained in it is actually official artwork that did not make it in the packaging for the game. The artwork for Trevor, Grant, and Sypha is identical in design to what would later be Fake Trevor, Fake Grant, and Fake Sypha from the game *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.
