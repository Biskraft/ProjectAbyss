# Guardian Helm

The **Guardian Helm** is the head component to the "Guardian" set in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is obtained along with the Guardian Armor (DEF +36), Guardian Gloves (DEF +14) and the Guardian Boots (DEF +8) when the Guardian Armor is destroyed by gears. They give DEF +14, which is one greater than a Barbuta, but less than an Armet.
