# Production Timeline

## 2002
### February
- 22nd - Xbox is released in Japan.

### March
- 14th - Xbox is released in Europe.

### June
- 6th - Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance is released in Japan.

### September
- 16th - Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance is released in North America.

### October
- 11th - Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance is released in Europe.

### November
- 16th - Castlevania and Castlevania II: Simon's Quest are released for the Personal computer / Microsoft Windows in North America.
