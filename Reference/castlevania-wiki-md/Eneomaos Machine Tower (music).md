  - Eneomaos Machine Tower** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the background theme of the titular location.

An arrangement of this music, called **Narcissistic Reflection**, appear as bonus track on the Castlevania: Curse of Darkness Original Soundtrack album.
