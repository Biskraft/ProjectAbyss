# 5th Plague

right|250px
  - 5th Plague**, known as  in Japan, is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is obtained by gaining dominance over Abaddon's soul. It allows to summon a cloud of locusts to swarm foes.

## Characteristics
When Abaddon's soul has been obtained, players can "summon a swarm of locusts" to attack enemies in the general direction Soma is facing. Players can also use the touchscreen to command the swarm anywhere on the screen. This spell is useful for damaging enemies while maintaining movement.
