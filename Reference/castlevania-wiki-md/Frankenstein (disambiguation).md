# Frankenstein (disambiguation)

There are three different characters known as **Frankenstein**:
- The Creature — Frankenstein's Monster, often referred to simply as "Frankenstein" in the early games, as well as in diverse media. Appears in many *Castlevania* games.
- Victor Frankenstein — *The Devil Castle Dracula: The Battle of Old Castle* character.
- Friedrich von Frankenstein — A mentioned character in the *Lords of Shadow* saga.
