The **Dead River** (Japanese: デッドリバー *Deddo Ribā*) is a water area where Simon is guided by the Ferryman on his boat. Two-Headed Creatures patrol the river banks and Fish Men attack from the water when you are on the ferry. It is first reached from the east from the Belasco Marsh and the ferryman normally takes Simon across the river to the town of Aldra. If Simon presents the ferryman Dracula's Heart, he will take him upstream a bit to the Brahm's Mansion instead, which is on the same side of the river as Belasco Marsh, but now heading left now heads east instead of west. Continuing beyond Brahm's Mansion takes you yet again across the Dead River which will lead you to the Jam Wasteland. Instead of using the ferryman to get you across this time, you have to jump on a series of logs floating on the river which are bobbing up and down. Timing is essential to get across the river. As the Jam Wasteland is a dead end, you'll have to cross the river again from the other side.
