  - Toy Maker's Heart** is a music theme in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. As the title indicates, this theme is featured in a cutscene where Dracula returns the sleeping Toy Maker his dislodged heart and afterward inquires about the piece of the Mirror of Fate he seeks out. The track is later used as ambient music that plays in random locations in the game.
