# Konami mascot games

There are several games featuring Konami mascots in which *Castlevania* characters appear:

- Wai Wai* series:
  - Konami Wai Wai World* — Simon Belmont III (playable), Dracula (enemy)
  - Wai Wai World 2: SOS!! Parsley Castle* — Simon Belmont (playable), Dracula (enemy)
  - Wai Wai Poker*
  - Wai Wai Jockey*
  - Wai Wai Bingo*
  - Piccadilly Circus: Konami Wai Wai World*
  - Konami Krazy Racers* — Dracula (playable)
  - Konami Wai Wai Sokoban* — Simon Belmont (playable)
  - Krazy Kart Racing* — Dracula (playable)

- Parodius* series:
  - Gokujō Parodius! ~ Kako no Eikō o Motomete ~* — Kid Dracula (playable)
  - Jikkyō Oshaberi Parodius ~ forever with me ~* — Kid Dracula (playable)
  - Sexy Parodius* — Medusa (boss)
  - Gokuraku Parodius* (pachislot) — Trezire de Spirit (music from *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula*)

Other:
  - Hai no Majutsushi* (mahjong) — Simon Belmont (playable)
  - Evolution Skateboarding* — Simon Belmont-like character (playable) Dracula (opponent)
  - DreamMix TV: World Fighters* — Simon Belmont (playable)
  - New International Track & Field* — Simon Belmont (playable) Dracula (opponent)
  - Super Bomberman R* — Belmont Bomber (playable) Dracula Bomber (playable), Alucard Bomber (playable)
  - Pixel Puzzle Collection* — Simon Belmont, Dracula, Kid Dracula
