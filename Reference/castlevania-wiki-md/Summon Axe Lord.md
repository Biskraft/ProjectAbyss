# Summon Axe Lord

right|300px
  - Summon Axe Lord** summons an Axe Lord that walks toward its opponents while swinging its axe up and down.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, this spell can be performed by using the AxeLord Shield's Shield Combo Spell with the Shield Rod or Mablung Sword. It costs 20 MP per cast.

This is the same armor that can be equipped with the special Old Axe Armor Mode code.
