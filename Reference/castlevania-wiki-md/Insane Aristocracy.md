Young Nobleman of Sadness}}
  - Insane Aristocracy** (otherwise known as **Young Nobleman of Madness**) is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is Isaac's boss battle theme.

This track contains *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*'s two songs: Festival of Servants and Death Ballad.
