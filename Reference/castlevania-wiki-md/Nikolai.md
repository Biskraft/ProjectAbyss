# Nikolai

is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. He is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. He is the founder of Wygol Village.

## Description
Commonly referred to as "Father" by the town villagers, Nikolai is the founder of Wygol Village in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. He cares for nothing more than the villagers' happiness and will often offer guidance to Shanoa throughout the events that take place in the game.

He won't give her any quests; however, he will reward her with the Master Ring in gratitude once she has completed all of the villagers' quests; that is, when she has fulfilled all of their hopes and dreams.

## Location
