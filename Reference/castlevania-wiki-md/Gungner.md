# Gungner

right|192px

  - Gungner** (originally Gungnir) is the Norse god Odin's divine thunderbolt that pierces everything it touches. In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, it hits the enemy up to three times, making up for its deceptively low damage value. In *Dawn of Sorrow*, its super attack launches a powerful long range lightning strike.
