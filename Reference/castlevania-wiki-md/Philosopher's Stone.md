# Philosopher's Stone

The **Philosopher's Stone** is an item mentioned in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It supposedly provides eternal youth to its owner and its creation is the ultimate goal of alchemy. The Ebony and Crimson Stones were created accidentally while attempting to forge this stone. It is not known if it was ever created successfully.

A stone by this name is available to Yoko Belnades and Maria Renard in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*, which allows them to heal themselves.
