# Blood Orb

right|300px

  - Blood Orbs** are magical items that appear in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*.

They were forged with Dracula's blood and are the keys to the Throne Room. Alucard must find the two orbs to reach Simon before fighting Dracula at the end of Act II.
