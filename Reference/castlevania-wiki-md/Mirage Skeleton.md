The **Mirage Skeleton** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is a skeleton that uses illusions of self to protect itself.

## Description
The Mirage Skeleton is scarlet in color. It attacks by creating copies of itself, although there are differences between the real skeleton and its clones. One thing is that the clones reassemble themselves when defeated, while the real one does not. Another is that the real one tries to run away instead of attacking, and can quickly slide to escape, unlike the clones. Also, the real skeleton is in the room from the beginning, while the others start out as a pile of bones on the ground. However, the most notable difference is that the real skeleton's reflection can be seen on the floor, while the clones do not project one.
