# Glyph Union

  - Glyph Union** is a method of combining the powers of two glyphs on Shanoa's left and right arms to create a third, more powerful glyph.

## Overview
The Glyph Union relic allows the player to perform **Glyph Union Magic**. These are done by combining the power of the equipped Main Glyph (glyph by default mapped to the **Y** button) and Sub Glyph (glyph by default mapped to the **X** button) by pressing ↑ on the D-Pad while attacking with one of those glyphs. These are powerful attacks and are the only ones in the game that consume Hearts.

The exact Glyph Union attack that is performed depends on the types of the glyphs equipped. Two glyphs of the same type usually perform a special Glyph Union for that type. Two glyphs of opposite types also may perform a special attack. Any kind of Weapon Glyph plus certain types often perform a single special giant elemental weapon attack.

Dominus is the only Glyph Union that also requires a particular Back Glyph to be equipped. The Pulsus Glyph Union is performed for any two glyphs that are otherwise not compatible.
