# Burtgang

right|140px

The **Burtgang** is a "mystical sword of protection". It is found in both *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the only sword in any *Castlevania* game that increases both DEF and CON. It is most similar to the Dark Blade in *Symphony of the Night*, which gives the highest defense boost of any sword in that game.


## Origins
The Burtgang is most likely Blutgang ("Blood Fetcher"), which was the sword of the giant Heime in Norse lore.
