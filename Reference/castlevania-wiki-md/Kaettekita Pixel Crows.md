# Kaettekita Pixel Crows

is a pixel comic based on the manga and anime *Crows* by Hiroshi Takahashi. It was released on April 29, 2019 by Nikkan Gecchan website exclusively in Japan.

The comic features a number of characters from Konami franchises, including Simon Belmont from *Castlevania*.

## Characters
- Simon Belmont from *Castlevania*
- Pentarō from *Antarctic Adventure*
- White Bomberman from *Bomberman*
- Challenger from *Challenger*
- Goemon from *Ganbare Goemon*
- Vic Viper, Golem and Moai from *Gradius*
- Moai Alexandria, Konami Man and Konami Lady from *Konami Wai Wai World*
- Caesar from *Star Soldier*
- TwinBee from *TwinBee*
- Lee from *Yie Ar Kung-Fu*
