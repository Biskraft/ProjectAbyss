# Raging Blaze

- Raging Blaze** is a fire spell that produces a column of flames to erupt from the ground in front of its caster. It is similar to the Mystic Flame weapon in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*, except that nothing is thrown toward the ground, the flame just rises up.

It's called **Energy Geyser** in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and can be performed by Soma Cruz when using the soul of a Werewolf. It is cast by Shanoa using the **Vol Ignis** glyph, and is one of two Ignis glyphs in that game. It can only be performed by Soma while on the ground, while it's possible to use in the air by Shanoa.
