# Shield

- Shields** are items that can block attacks.

Alucard has the largest variety of shields available to him in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. He is able to equip one in one arm and they can usually block projectiles, although some can be used as weapons. He is unable to equip a shield if he is wielding a 2 Handed Weapon. In *Harmony of Despair*, some shields can reflect attacks.

## Game specific information
### *Vampire Killer*
  - Shield (A)** (a.k.a. Red Shield) in *Vampire Killer* increases Simon's Defense. **Shield (B)** (a.k.a. Yellow Shield) blocks projectiles, much like Dracula's Rib does in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*.

### *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*
The **Spike Shield** is the only shield featured in this game. But it is used not for defensive purposes like an ordinary shield but instead offensively like a weapon. It has only one attack animation which consists of Hector swinging the weapon upwards from his hand.

Additionally, **Shield** is also an ability of the Crystal Rod which basically blocks any enemy attack. However, each cast of the spell is only for one-time protection and costs 20 Hearts to be cast.


### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
The Scutum and its upgraded versions come as Glyphs and are the only shields available in Shanoa's weaponry.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
Alucard is able to use all of his shields from *Symphony of the Night*. He is unable to combine them with a Shield Rod or Mablung Sword in this game, however.
