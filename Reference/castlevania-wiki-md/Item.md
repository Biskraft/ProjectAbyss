# Item

- Items** are objects that can be found in *Castlevania* games that give some kind of benefit to the player upon contact. In games with roleplaying elements, these are considered standard items, as other kinds of items can be equipped or otherwise accessed through in-game controls. Standard items usually increase some kind of game statistic, increases the power of a weapon, or provide a temporary benefit. These items can usually be found by destroying a candle or other container or enemy. Secondary playable characters, which usually cannot equip other items, can usually obtain standard items.
