# Followers of Darkness -The First-

- Followers of Darkness -The First-** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is played when Hector is forced to fight certain types of enemies encountered in the game and throughout the Tower of Eternity and Tower of Evermore stages. Parts of Heart of Fire can be heard during this track.

The track was later featured in Pachislot Akumajō Dracula. It plays during a segment in the game where Trevor Belmont battles against the Succubus.
