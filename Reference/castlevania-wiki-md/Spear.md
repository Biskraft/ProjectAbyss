# Spear

right|50px
  - Spears** are weapons and classes of weapons (sometimes called **Polearms**) in *Castlevania*.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
In *Dawn of Sorrow*, Spears are a weapon class referred to as Polearms. The weapon called "Spear" is the most basic entity in this class.


### *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*
While there's no weapon called "Spear" in *Curse of Darkness*, "Spears" is a weapon class that includes all pole shaped long range weapons.
