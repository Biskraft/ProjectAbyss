# Nintendo Switch

thumb|300px|<p style="text-align: center;">The Nintendo Switch console in docked mode with Joy-Cons on attached to the Joy-Con grip.</p>
thumb|300px|<p style="text-align: center;">The Nintendo Switch console in handheld mode.</p>

The **Nintendo Switch** is a hybrid portable home console made by Nintendo. It is Nintendo's current home console and the successor to the Wii U. It is designed to played both at home when docked and on the go while undocked. It features detachable controllers called Joy-Cons which can be split into two to provide multiplayer gameplay. In addition, the Joy-Cons also feature motion sensing capabilities, similar to the Wii Remote.
