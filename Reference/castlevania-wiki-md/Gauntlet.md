# Gauntlet

A **Gauntlet** is an armored glove that appears in *Castlevania* that increases Strength or Defense. Its magical counterpart is the Magic Gauntlet, which increases Intelligence or allows users to utilize Alchemy.

The Gauntlet first appeared in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, where it added 5 to Strength. It raised it by 15 in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it raised Defense by 4, which is greater than Leather Gloves (+2), but less than normal Gloves (+7).
