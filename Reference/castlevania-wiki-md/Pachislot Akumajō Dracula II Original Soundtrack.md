# Pachislot Akumajō Dracula II Original Soundtrack

- Pachislot Akumajō Dracula II Original Soundtrack** is the soundtrack for *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula II*. It was released on July 21st, 2010 and it also contains tracks from other previous games (*Judgment*, *Dracula X Chronicles*, etc.).
