# Marie (song)

- Marie** is a song in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* named after the deceased wife of Gabriel Belmont, a warrior sent by the Brotherhood of Light to seek out Pan. This track plays when the lovelorn hero communicates with Marie's trapped soul at Oblivion Lake after the Ice Titan is slain. It is during that meeting that Gabriel first becomes aware of the prophecy regarding the Lords of Shadow.
