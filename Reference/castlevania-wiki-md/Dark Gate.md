# Dark Gate

right|250px
  - Dark Gate** is a Dual Crush in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. When cast, two gigantic demonic hands will appear out of nowhere and rip the very fabric of reality, opening a rift to another dimension where the monster stares while Dark winds escape into our world, damaging all enemies which make contact with it.
