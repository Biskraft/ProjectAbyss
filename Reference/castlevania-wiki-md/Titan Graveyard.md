The **Titan Graveyard** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the first stage of Chapter 10.  

## Zobek's journal
:*Baba Yaga sent our hero to the Titans Graveyard and I have sent her soul to the underworld. She will trouble us no more. Now my friend enters the Land of the Dead and his final battle is coming. I must find my own path and try to meet with him at some point. For now he is on his own. The land requires sacrifice… a sacrifice I hope that he can make, because without it he will not get very far. I must have faith in him now. The seed was sown long ago and it flowers beautifully. He has come this far down the path… I must believe that he will go all the way.*
