# Peanuts

- Peanuts** are a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It recovers 50 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is the second highest non-meat food in the game, giving more than Natou (32 HP), but less than Sushi (100 HP), and gives as much health as a Pot Roast.

In order to be consumed and gain the HP, however, Alucard first tosses them up and then must catch them into his mouth. To catch the peanut, press ↑ when the peanut is about to land on Alucard's head.
