# Knuckle Duster

- Knuckle Dusters** are iron-studded brass knuckles. It is the weakest type of Fist weapon that Alucard can find in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. As a fist weapon, it is fast, but have low range.
