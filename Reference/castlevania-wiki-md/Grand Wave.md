# Grand Wave

*(This article is a stub)*

right
  - Grand Wave** is an ability a Corpsey Battle-Type Innocent Devil can learn in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. To obtain it, Hector must defeat a certain number of enemies while this Innocent Devil is summoned. When activated, Corpsey will leap into the air and land with great force while piercing the blade of his 7 Bladed Sword into the ground, releasing a wave of yellow energy that will travel in a frontal direction (8 MP). It can also be learned by the Rasetz.
