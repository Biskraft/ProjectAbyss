# Hard Leather Armor

right|73px
  - Hard Leather Armor** (also called **Rock Leather**) is armor made of multiple layers of leather and hardened.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, Rock Leather gives +20 Defense, which is 2 more than Bronze Armor, but two less than Cuirass Armor.

In *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, Hard Leather Armor is created by adding Jet Black to Soft Leather Armor, which increases its defense from +5 to +8. It cannot be upgraded any further and is outclassed when a Lorica (+10 DEF) can be created by combining Bronze with Ancient Memory.
