# Aroma Earring

The **Aroma Earring** is an accessory in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* that increases INT by 5. It is the only item that increases intelligence in this game. 

As Leon has a steady stat of 20 in INT, the boost is 25%, and as INT increases sub-weapon damage in *Lament of Innocence*, this item increases sub-weapon damage by 25% (tested on Shadow Wolf and Cyclops). It is found in the west wing of the Ghostly Theatre, three rooms after the Holy Water. It is unknown yet if effect stacks if the player drops several of these on Lesser Demons.
