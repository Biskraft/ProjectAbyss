# Chain Lightning

right|200px

  - Chain Lightning** is a spell or ability which causes lightning to head toward an enemy and then bounce around all enemies onscreen.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
Chain Lightning could first be performed by Soma by gaining dominance over Rycuda's soul in *Dawn of Sorrow*.

This ability is probably the most feasible way of spawning Mothman in the Pinnacle. Soma can hit with this ability the searchlight's mechanism located in a room between two Final Guards, thus attracting Mothman to it and allowing him to slay it in search of obtaining its valuable soul.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Chain Lightning is one of Charlotte Aulin's magic spell skills in *Portrait of Ruin*. When cast, it creates a chain reaction of lightning bolts.
