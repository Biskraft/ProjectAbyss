# Masahiko Kimura

is a Japanese video game music composer known for being a member of Konami Kukeiha Club.

He composed music for Castlevania - The Original Game Soundtrack, the soundtrack for the Nintendo 64 games *Castlevania* and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. He also designed music for *Castlevania: Dracula X*, as well as working with Michiru Yamane on *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*<nowiki>'</nowiki>s original soundtrack.
