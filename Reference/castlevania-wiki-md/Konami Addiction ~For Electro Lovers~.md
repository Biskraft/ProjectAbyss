# Konami Addiction ~For Electro Lovers~

- Konami Addiction ~For Electro Lovers~** is a remix CD composed by Konami Kukeiha Club with arrangements made by Tongarikids, Toshihiko Mori, Takeshi Nakatsuka, Sugiurumn, Junichi Soma, Kai Kawasaki, Hiroshi Nakamura, DJ CLAZZIQUAI, michitomo, Tomoyuki Tajiri and Toshiyuki Yasuda. It was published by Universal Music K.K. on May 21, 2008 in Japan.

It contains remixes of music from many Konami games, including *Castlevania*.
