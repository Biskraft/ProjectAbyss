# Mobile phone

- Mobile phones**, also known by a variety of names such as **cell phones** or **mobiles**, featured a few *Castlevania* titles and other related media, most of them being ports of the classic NES games, with one original game titled *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*.

The original *Castlevania* in particular has received numerous ports, with each version featuring their own original sprites.

## *Castlevania* games available on the system
  - Castlevania*
  - Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*
  - Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
  - Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
  - Castlevania: Order of Shadows*
  - Akumajō Dracula: Lament of Innocence* (comic)
  - Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*
  - Castlevania: Grimoire of Souls*
