# Theme of Simon Belmont

, also known as **Simon Belmont's Theme**, is a music theme introduced in the 1991 Super Nintendo game *Super Castlevania IV*. It is the background music for the second part of the Stage I, as well as the second part of the last battle with Count Dracula. The theme was composed by Konami Kukeiha Club and is one of the series' most popular tracks. It features synthesized pipe organs and strings in a fast-paced action tone. A variation of this theme plays as the stage clear jingle after collecting the Magic Crystal at the end of each level.

There is another major variation known as "**Simon's Theme**", which was introduced in the 1993 Sharp X68000 game *Akumajō Dracula* as the background music for Stage 8.
