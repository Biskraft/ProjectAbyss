# Secondary Weapon (Mirror of Fate)

- Secondary Weapons** in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate* are special weapons fueled by Ammo Hearts. They are activated by pressing the "**A**" button. These can also be charged by holding down "**A**" for a while for a more powerful attack. These are similar to sub-weapons found in other *Castlevania* games. Each character other than Gabriel has two different sub-weapons they can find and use throughout the game. They can be switched between at any time.
