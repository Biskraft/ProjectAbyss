# Elizabeth Arye

is the female lead for the novel *Akumajō Dracula: Akuma no Chi Chi no Akumu*. She is a priestess and Dean's girlfriend, and follows along when he travels. Her blood is ingested by a vampire, turning her into one of their kind. However, Simon Belmont believes she is still alive.
