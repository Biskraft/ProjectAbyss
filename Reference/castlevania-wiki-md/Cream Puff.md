# Cream Puff

right|50px

The **Cream Puff** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It appears in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow*, *Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia*.

It is dropped by a Giant Worm in *Aria of Sorrow* and a Student Witch in *Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be found in the Forest of Doom in *Portrait of Ruin* and Dracula's Castle in *Order of Ecclesia*.

In *Harmony of Despair*, a Cream Puff is an common item that increase HP and MP by a modest +15. It also gives a token LCK boost of +1 and is the only HP and MP recovering dessert in that game which only gives a LCK boost. It gives the same MP and HP boost as Red Licorice, but has a slight net stat boost of one better than Red Licorice, which trades -5 INT for its +5 LCK. It is inferior to Pudding, which has the same MP and HP, but raises both INT and LCK by 5.
