The **Water Leaper** is an enemy in the Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is a jolly creature of the river. Their texture is similar to chicken meat.

## Origins
thumb|left|<p style="text-align: center;"> Artist's view of water leaper.</p>
In Welsh folklore, the llamhigyn y dwr (the water leaper's welsh name) is a giant toad-like creature with a serpent's tail, bat's wings and two needle-sharp fangs, but lacking back legs. It preys upon both humans and animals (often sheep), dragging them to their deaths if they venture too close to the water.

## Description
A flying fish that can leap high bounds to eat its prey. They are usually encountered in great numbers, so it is best not to dwindle in one place fighting them for too long.
