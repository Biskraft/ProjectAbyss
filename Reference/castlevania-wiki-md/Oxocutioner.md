# Oxocutioner

The  is a miniboss in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It is a bovine corpse that commands a deadly ring of spinning swords.

## Description
A skeletal bovine boss who rules over the  in Chapter 11, *The Legend of Fuma*. The stage itself originally appeared in the classic Famicom game *Getsu Fūma Den*.

Oxocutioner is armed with several swords that he can control telepathically. He is one of three minibosses that must be destroyed before reaching the final boss, Ryukotsuki. He becomes more powerful after either of the other two minibosses, Decapiclops and Chimyriad, are destroyed.
