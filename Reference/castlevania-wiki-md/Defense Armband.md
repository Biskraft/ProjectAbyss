# Defense Armband

The **Defense Armband** (called the **Solid Bracelet** in Japan) is a bracelet in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* that greatly increases Defense +100 at the expense of other stats (-25 Strength, Intelligence, and Luck). It is more extreme than the Hard Ring (+50 DEF, -10 STR and LCK). It is the Defense counterpart to the Gambler Armband, Strength Armband, and Sage Armband.
