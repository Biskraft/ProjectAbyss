# Cotton Clothes

- Cotton Clothes** (or **Cotton Dress**) is a regular body gear found in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* and *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They are similar to Cloth Tunics and Hobo's Clothes that are dropped by Zombies in other games.
