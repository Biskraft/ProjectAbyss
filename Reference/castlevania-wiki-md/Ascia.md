# Ascia

right|250px
  - Ascia** is an axe glyph. It allows Shanoa to throw an axe into the air. It is very useful for taking out flying enemies like the Nominon, and it crosses through walls and floors, making it useful for reaching enemies in odd corners. It is similar to the Axe sub-weapon in the previous games. **Vol Ascia** is a medium axe glyph. **Melio Ascia** is the most powerful axe glyph. It launches axes in an arc downward. The effective range of the throw rises with each glyph, as does the area covered by the axes. **Union Ascia** is the Glyph Union of two Axe Glyphs.
