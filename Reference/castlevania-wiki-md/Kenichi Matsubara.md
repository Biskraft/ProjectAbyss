# Kenichi Matsubara

is a Japanese video game composer. He worked at Konami during the late eighties and early nineties as part of Konami Kukeiha Club, and composed themes for *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* and *Haunted Castle*.

He is credited as the original author of "Bloody Tears", one of the series' most well known tracks to date.
