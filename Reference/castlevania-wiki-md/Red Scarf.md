# Red Scarf

The **Red Scarf** is a mid-tier scarf in *Castlevania*, which is more effective than a plain Scarf, but less effective than a Neck Warmer.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, it gives a DEF boost of +2 (which is what a normal scarf gives) plus a STR, INT and CON boost of +1.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it gives +2 DEF and +5 CON, which is more than the +1 DEF and +2 CON that a normal scarf gives, but less than +3 DEF and +10 CON that a Neck Warmer gives.
