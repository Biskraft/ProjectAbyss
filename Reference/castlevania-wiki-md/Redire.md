# Redire

right|250px
  - Redire** is a glyph in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It fires out a blade that travels across the screen like a boomerang. Its core has a magnetic center and the back glyph Magnes can be used on it. With careful timing and directional inputs, it is possible to launch Shanoa great distances and travel very quickly.
