# Ghost Ships

right|300px

  - Ghost** and **Sunken Ships** are relatively common environments within the *Castlevania* universe. The first one appeared in *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*, called "The Haunted Ship of Fools". These locations are generally inhabited by the reanimated corpses of their former crew members, although other times, creatures who have been forever estranged from the surface tend to lurk through their corridors.

Sunken ships are usually found stranded deep below a flooded complex of caves, and most of the times they serve as an access point to another, deeper, area where valuable treasures can be found.
