# Castlevania: 1000 Piece Puzzle

- Castlevania: 1000 Piece Puzzle***, also known as ***Castlevania Premium Puzzle***, is a jigsaw puzzle game based on the video game of the same name. It was published by Mondo and released on December 17, 2018. It contains a 1,000 piece premium puzzle celebrating 30 years of the franchise.
