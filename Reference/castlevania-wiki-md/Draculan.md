# Draculan

is a boss in the *Ganbare Goemon* series. He is a parody of Dracula from the *Castlevania* series, and appears as a boss in *Ganbare Goemon Gaiden 2: Tenka no Zaihō* and as a secret boss in *Ganbare Goemon 2: Kiteretsu Shōgun Magginesu*.

## Appearances
### *Ganbare Goemon Gaiden 2: Tenka no Zaihō*
Draculan appears as a boss, costuming as Arsène Lupin, but defeated by Goemon.

### *Ganbare Goemon 2: Kiteretsu Shōgun Magginesu*
Draculan as secret boss, when Goemon, Sasuke and Ebisumaru find the fourth mask to the virtual Hell.
