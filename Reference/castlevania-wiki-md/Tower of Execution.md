# Tower of Execution

The **Tower of Execution** is a location in *Castlevania* and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. It is a tower filled with lava and sharp traps.

## Overview
True to its name, the stage is a vast killing field where its many traps incorporate the various methods in which people are executed, such as huge guillotine blades. Most of the monster types in this stage, from skeletons to ghosts, fit the scheme of things. The architecture also adds to the deathly ambiance, including blood stains, rusted metal, and fiery torches.

The player must traverse the tower from the ground up in a similar fashion to the way they went through the Duel Tower, leaping from platform to platform while avoiding the executing traps and monsters (some of whom can attack from a distance, making timed leaps that much trickier).

This stage also contains a hidden key which unlocks a door somewhere in the level, leading to the second secret item.

In *Legacy of Darkness* the tower changes significantly: the central pillar of the tower has sunk into the pit of lava at the base of the level and is only accessible via an invisible path that leads to its top, and instead of ascending the tower alternating between the pillar and platforms around the walls, the player character makes their way through a series of hallways that loop back to various levels of the main room. In this version there is no key, since secrets are instead unlocked in the Henry Oldrey playthrough.
