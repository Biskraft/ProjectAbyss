# Nova Skeleton

The **Nova Skeleton**, also known as **Beam Skeleton**, is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is a green skeleton with three red eyes that can shoot laser beams.

## Appearances
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
This enemy can be found in the Reverse Outer Wall. It's the only enemy with the Terminus Est as an item drop. It can shoot a Hit-elemental beam which is the strongest enemy attack in the game (even stronger than Dracula's attack).

### *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*
Appearing in Dracula's Castle, this enemy drops the highly useful Nitesco glyph, a magical attack with Flame and Light attributes. It uses this attack on the Normal difficulty setting, making it easy to avoid by crouching. However, the Nova Skeleton uses Union Nitesco on harder difficulty settings, which can only be avoided by standing behind or on a different level to the skeleton.
