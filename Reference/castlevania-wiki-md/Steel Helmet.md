# Steel Helmet

The **Steel Helmet** (also called **Steel Helm**) is a strong helmet made of Steel in *Castlevania*.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it grants +3 DEF and +1 INT, which is one more DEF than a Bandana and gives more DEF than any other head protection other than the Dragon Helm and Wizard Hat.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it is the second strongest type of helmet, granting +26 DEF, which is one more than a Silver Tiara and two more than a Pot Helm, but one less than a Viking Helm.
