# Summon Cockatrice

right|350px
This spell, used by combining the Uranus card with the Cockatrice card, causes a Cockatrice to fly across the screen from right to left, bringing with it a flurry of boulders to attack any enemies onscreen. Each individual boulder breaks when it hits an enemy. Costs 200 MP.

Tied with Summon Thunderbird for the title of most devastating spell in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.
