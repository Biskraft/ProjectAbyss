  - Dark Covenant** is a music theme composed by Soshiro Hokkai for *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is Death's theme in the game. It plays during cutscenes where he appears before Juste Belmont throughout his journey to rescue both Maxim Kischine and Lydie Erlanger.
