# Needle Familiar

right|266px
The **Needle Familiar** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It allows to summon Needles as a familiar.

## Availability
Dropped by Needles. This enemy can be found in the flooded areas of the Subterranean Hell.

## Characteristics
This soul allows Soma to summon up to three Needles allies which will follow and support him wherever he goes.
