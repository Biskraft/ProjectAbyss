  - The Black Knight** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the ninth and final stage of Chapter 2.

## Zobek's journal
:*A great victory! Gabriel has won the trust of the girl, together they wield a fearsome power. She is so young, so beautiful. Life is so fragile here on the edge of humanity, and yet in this unforgiving place she has managed to survive with the help of her Black Knight protector. Sleep will come and take them soon. Tired and exhausted, they rest, but this night, fate once again comes calling, and I fear my friend will never be the same again.*
