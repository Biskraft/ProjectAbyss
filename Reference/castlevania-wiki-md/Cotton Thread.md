# Cotton Thread

The **Cotton Thread** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They can be found inside common chests in the Monastery, Ruvas Forest, Minera Prison Island and Kalidus Channel.

Five of them must be brought to Monica in order to complete her first quest,  "Making a Dress!". Once done, she'll reward Shanoa with a Cotton Dress (DEF +7, INT +1, MND +3) and the Sequined Dress (DEF +12, INT +3, MND +7, LCK +2) will also become available at Jacob's shop for purchase.
