# Arcade Archives

- Arcade Archives*** is a series of emulated arcade games from the 1980s and 1990s released for the PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows and Nintendo Switch. It is published by Hamster Corporation.

## Overview
- Arcade Archives* was first released for the PlayStation 4 on May 2014. It also supports various system specific features. Players can share screenshots and videos, and compete with others' scores for online ranking. The Xbox One version of the service was started on February 2017 for the Xbox Games Store and only includes Neo Geo games. The Nintendo Switch version of the service was started on the Nintendo eShop at the system's launch on March 2017, initially focusing on Neo Geo games before the addition of other arcade games, including Nintendo's own games. The Windows 10 version of the service was started on December 2017 for the Microsoft Store, and like the Xbox One version, it also only includes Neo Geo games.
