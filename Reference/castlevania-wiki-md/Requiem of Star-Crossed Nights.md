# Requiem of Star-Crossed Nights

- Requiem of Star-Crossed Nights** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is the music theme that plays during the end credits.

Brief arrangements of An Empty Tome and Chapel Hidden in Smoke can be heard within the track.
