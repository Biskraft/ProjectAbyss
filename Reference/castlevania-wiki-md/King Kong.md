  - King Kong** is an enemy in *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*.

## Description
King Kong is a giant ape-like creature resembling an enormous gorilla who was introduced in entertainment media as the main antagonist of the 1933 film of the same name, and who since then has also appeared in a large number of sequels and spinoffs.

In *Boku Dracula-kun*, he appears on the top of high buildings in the New York stage and throws airplanes at Kid Dracula.

## Trivia
- Konami had the video game license for King Kong for some time and developed and published a couple of games under a series known as *King Kong Lives*. He later appeared as a playable character in the early mascot game *Konami Wai Wai World*, where it's possible to infiltrate Dracula's Castle as him and rescue Simon Belmont. Konami later lost the rights for King Kong, so newer versions of *Konami Wai Wai World* replaced him with another franchise character.
