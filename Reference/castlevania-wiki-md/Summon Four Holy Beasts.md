# Summon Four Holy Beasts

right|300px
:*This article is about the invincibility spell. For the beast sub-weapons, see Four Sacred Beasts.*
Maria is able to **Summon Four Holy Beasts** (also known as the Four Symbols) in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. Her Four Holy Beasts (Suzaku the fire bird, Genbu the turtle, Seiyruu the dragon, and Byakko the white tiger) are summoned, which grant herself or an ally temporary invincibility.

During Final Stage: Bloodlines, she casts the spell if Richter loses all his health during his battle against Dracula. She continues praying during the rest of the battle, which prolongs the effects of the spell. Richter is unable to take damage while this spell is active and is able to defeat Dracula.

In the Sega Saturn version of the game, the playable Maria is able to cast the spell on herself by entering the sequence ↑↗→↘↓↙←↖↑(hold) ↓ + Fire. This spell grants her invincibility for about forty seconds.
