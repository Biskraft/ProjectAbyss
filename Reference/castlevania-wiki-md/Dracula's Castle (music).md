# Dracula's Castle (music)

- Dracula's Castle** is a music theme originally composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. Appropriately, it is heard in the first section of Dracula's Castle, the Entrance. It came to be considered to be Alucard's theme, which was later confirmed by Konami in *Castlevania Judgment*.

In the game *Frogger: Hyper Arcade Edition*, once all 20 challenges are completed, a stage based off in *Castlevania* is unlocked as well as this theme, which plays during this stage.

Another theme, also named **Dracula's Castle**, was composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. Not surprisingly, it's the background music that plays while exploring Dracula's Castle.

## Trivia
- An [electronic-style remix](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Wiql4TfKVq0) of this song exists as raw CD audio on the US release of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* for the PlayStation. It is preceded with a warning from Alucard not to play cut (track) #1, as it contains computer data. Amusingly, Alucard adds that the listener "probably won't listen to him anyway" before the song starts.
