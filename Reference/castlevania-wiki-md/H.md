# H

- H** (as he signs his name) is a mentioned character in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. He was one of the knights of the Brotherhood of Light. In his scroll, it is mentioned he was thinking of Brother Bernard and Sister Laverne.

## Trivia
- 'H' presumably stands for "Hoagie", and along with the two other names mentioned in his scroll, they reference the three characters from the 1996 video game, *Day of the Tentacle*: Bernard Bernoulli, Hoagie and Laverne.
