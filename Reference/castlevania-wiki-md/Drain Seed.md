# Drain Seed

right|172px
  - Drain Seed** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Dryad. It allows Soma to throw an apple-like fruit forward; if the fruit lands on an enemy, it will deal ten consecutive hits, each stealing 8 HP that will be restored to Soma's own health.
