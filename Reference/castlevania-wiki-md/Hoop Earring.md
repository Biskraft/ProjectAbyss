# Hoop Earring

The **Hoop Earring** is an earring in the shape of a hoop in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is the first and weakest of the earrings found in this game, which each give a Defense boost with a small Intelligence boost kicker. It gives +1 DEF and +1 INT, which is one less DEF than the Turquoise Stud.
