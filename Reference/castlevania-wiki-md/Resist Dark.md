# Resist Dark

- Resist Dark** is an Attribute Potion in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* that causes Dark-based attacks to actually heal Alucard.

The item is called **Shadow Essence** in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* and raises one's shadow affinity, although it doesn't help unlock Shadow spells.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, this ability is obtained with an Arc Demon's soul.
