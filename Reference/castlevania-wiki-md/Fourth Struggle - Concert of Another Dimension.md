# Fourth Struggle - Concert of Another Dimension

- Fourth Struggle - Concert of Another Dimension** is a music track that is featured in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.

It plays during the boss fights against the Behemoth and Dracula's second form, when the player reach the Castle Keep in less than 16 days with Reinhardt and Carrie.
