# Member Plate

The **Member Plate** is an item in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* that reduces the price of goods at Rinaldo's Shop by 10%. The effect is cumulative, so equipping two or three will reduce the price by 20 or 30% respectively. It is dropped by Spartacus.
