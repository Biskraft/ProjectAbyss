# Cynthia Harrell

- Cynthia Elaine Harrell-Vaughn**, commonly known simply as , is an American soul and funk vocalist.

## Background
Harrell provided the lyrics for the ending theme on the original 1997 PlayStation release of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, "I am the Wind".

She's also known for singing the James Bond-like opening theme for *Metal Gear Solid 3: Snake Eater*, "Snake Eater", which won her the "Best Original Vocal Song - Pop" award from the Game Audio Network Guild at the Game Developers Conference in August 2005.
