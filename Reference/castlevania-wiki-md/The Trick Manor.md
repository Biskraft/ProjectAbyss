# The Trick Manor

- The Trick Manor** (also known as **Clockwork Mansion**) is a music theme originally composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Super Castlevania IV*.

The theme is divided in two main parts, with different arrangements each, both appearing in different sections of Stage IV: the first part plays throughout the first three sections of the stage (The Sorcery Tower, The Tower Entrance and The Spinning Room) and the second part plays throughout the last two (The Rotating Tomb and The Levitation Chambers).

An arranged version mixing both parts appeared later in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It is the background theme for the Underground Warehouse.
