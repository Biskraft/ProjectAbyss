# Kung Fugu

- Kung Fugu** is an enemy that tends to get all puffed up over nothing. It is originally from *Getsu Fūma Den* which appeared in the cross-over stage The Legend of Fuma in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

## Enemy Data


## Trivia
This enemy's name is a pun on *fugu*, the Japanese word for pufferfish.
