# Hammer (item)

The **Hammer** in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* is an item that can be used to get rid of the Stone status. It can only be used by a Faerie Familiar if it is in the inventory when Alucard becomes petrified. The familiar will take the hammer and whack him on the head, which will crack the stone, thus freeing him.
