  - Inversion** is a song featured in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* that plays briefly before the game's beginning cutscene conclusion. It is at that moment when Dracula summons a bat to create the chasm in the Sealed Room directly beneath Nathan Graves and Hugh Baldwin. As a result, they are both involuntarily sent down to the Catacombs under Camilla's Castle.
