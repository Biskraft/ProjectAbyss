The  is an area in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It can be accessed from the Marble Gallery's clock room if Alucard enters it equipped with both, the Gold Ring and the Silver Ring. It is a small area, at the center of which Maria Renard can be found. After a short dialogue she will give you the Holy Glasses, which are required to free Richter Belmont from Shaft's control. In the Sega Saturn and the *Dracula X Chronicles* versions, Maria must be defeated in battle before she gives them to you. The Reverse Castle Center is the Inverted Castle's counterpart of this area.
