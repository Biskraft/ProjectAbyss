# Tactical Soul

- Tactical Souls** are souls of defeated enemies that Soma Cruz can absorb and equip in order to gain that enemy's abilities.

There are four types of Tactical Souls:
- Bullet Souls (**<span style="color:red;">Red Souls</span>**)
- Guardian Souls (**<span style="color:blue;">Blue Souls</span>**)
- Enchanted Souls (**<span style="color:orange;">Yellow Souls</span>**)
- Ability Souls (**<span style="color:gray;">Silver Souls</span>**)
