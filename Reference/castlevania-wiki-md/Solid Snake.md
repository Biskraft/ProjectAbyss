# Solid Snake

thumb|200px|<p style="text-align: center;">Solid Snake's render from the 2008 [[Nintendo game *Super Smash Bros. Brawl*.</p>]]

  - Solid Snake** is the main protagonist of Konami's *Metal Gear* series. His bandana and Solid Eye are featured as hidden items in the 2010 game *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.

## Notes
- Snake, Bomberman and Simon Belmont (*Chronicles*) are playable characters in the 2003 game *DreamMix TV: World Fighters*.
  - All three characters later reappeared in *Super Bomberman R* and *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate*.
- Along with other regular *Metal Gear* voice actors, Akio Ōtsuka, who voices Solid Snake, has took the role as Pan in the Japanese version of *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. In addition, the render for Gabriel Belmont's Solid Eye and bandana bonus equipment has him holding a cigarette in a similar manner to Solid Snake, with the aforementioned equipment also being used for Snake in *Metal Gear Solid 4*.
- Although Snake himself does not appear in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, he is given an indirect allusion in that game's Albus Mode, where the titular character when pulling the lever to defeat Brachyura says "Showtime!" in a similar tone to that of Solid Snake.
