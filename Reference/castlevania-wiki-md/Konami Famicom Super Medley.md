# Konami Famicom Super Medley

- Konami Famicom Super Medley** is a music CD arranged by Motoaki Furukawa and Ryo Sakai, published by Scitron Digital Contents and released on October 19, 2005 in Japan.

This CD was contains arrangements of music from many Konami games, including a *Castlevania*, *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* and *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*.
