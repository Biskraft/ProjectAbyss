# Pendant

right|50px

A **Pendant** is an accessory that is hung around the neck by a necklace and which can grant special powers.

The first pendant in the series was called the **Mystic Pendant** in the localized North American version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It could increase the rate of MP recovery and was replaced by the Cipher's Charm in *Harmony of Dissonance*.

Items called "pendants" in later games were described as "ordinary" or "plain". These items gave a small amount of stat boosts. It increases DEF by +1 in *Harmony of Dissonance*. It increases LCK by +1 in *Aria of Sorrow* and *Dawn of Sorrow*, being inferior to the Lucky Charm in the former but exactly the same in the latter. It's also surpassed by the Heart Pendant, which provides both, a small LCK and DEF boost.
