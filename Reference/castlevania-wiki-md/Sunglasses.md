# Sunglasses

- Sunglasses** are the first type of glasses to appear in the *Castlevania* series.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
Called simply **Glasses** in Japan. They raise DEF by 1, but lower INT by 1. They are inferior to the Goggles, which give the same amount of DEF, but instead raise INT by 4.

### *Castlevania Judgment*
This game features two types of sunglasses: the **Bronze Sunglasses** and the **Sporty Sunglasses**, with two recolored variants called **Silver Rimmed Glasses** and **Serious Spectacles**, respectively. They are face accessories that can be equipped to customize the appearance of a character.
