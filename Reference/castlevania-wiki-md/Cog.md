# Cog

The  is a key item in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It can be obtained in the central room of the Nation of Fools. The moment the player picks it up, Legion will suddenly materialize out of thin air and his boss battle will begin.

## Usage
This items is used to complete the gate's opening mechanism located beyond Stella's boss room in the Tower of Death, allowing the player to proceed further into the stage.
