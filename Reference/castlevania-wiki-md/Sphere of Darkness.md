# Sphere of Darkness

right

  - Sphere of Darkness** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by the Khaos Bird-Type evolution. It consists of firing four fireballs that slowly home on nearby enemies.
