# Prima Games

- Prima Games** is the largest publishing company of video game strategy guides in the United States. It has published guides for some *Castlevania* games.

## History
Formerly, Prima was an imprint of Dorling Kindersley, a division of Penguin Random House, and produced print strategy guides, featuring in-depth walkthroughs for completing games, with other information such as character sheets and move charts.

Prima was acquired by Asteri Holdings in March 2019, which will transition the business to provide strategy guides in online form only, alongside other gaming news.
