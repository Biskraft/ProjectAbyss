# Konami Akumajō Dracula X Chronicle Official Guide

right|250px

  - Konami Akumajō Dracula X Chronicle Official Guide** is Konami's official Japanese guide book for *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*. This book also includes a simple guide for the original *Rondo of Blood* and *Symphony of the Night*.

## Related products
  - Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* - The game this is a guide for.
- Konami Akumajō Dracula: Gallery of Labyrinth Official Guide - The previous Konami Castlevania guide.
- Konami Akumajō Dracula: Ubawareta Kokuin Official Guide - The following Konami Castlevania guide.
