## Hand Items and Weapons
Alucard can place a different weapon or item in each hand and uses a different button to use each one. There are a variety of different kinds of weapons and items he can equip.

### One Handed Weapons
Most weapons at Alucard's disposal can be placed in a single hand. Short Swords include smaller swords, knives, and rapiers and do Cut damage. Swords are generally similar, but longer, more powerful, and fast. Throwing Swords are thrown and indefinite number of times as their normal attack without consuming Hearts or MP and return to Alucard. Clubs are flails with long reach, a little slower, and have some overhead attacking ability. Fist weapons enhance Alucard's punching ability, do blunt damage, can be done repeatedly very rapidly, but have low range. Several weapons are elementally enhanced or have special moves associated with them.


### Two Handed Weapons
Some large weapons require two hands to wield. These take up both hand slots, preventing the use of any other item or weapon. Most two-handed weapons are one of two basic types, the large, powerful, and slow Great Swords, and the fast Katanas. Like one handed weapons, these may have special attacks associated with them.


### One Time Use Weapons
Many weapons can only be used once for each such weapon that has been acquired. These include Projectiles which are thrown like Sub-Weapons, and Bombs, which damage everything on the screen. These weapons can be used an infinite number of times once the Duplicator relic is obtained.


### Shields
Shields can be equipped to raise defense and block projectiles. Some of them have special properties. Each shield has a special Shield Rod Spell that can be performed when a Shield Rod or Mablung Sword is equipped in the other hand. These will summon some kind of creature, which grants statistical enhancements or protection, or attacks enemies on the screen.


### Food and Medicine
A wide variety of Food, which recovers health, can be equipped in one of Alucard's hands. Pressing that hand's button will cause the food to "pop out" near Alucard, and will recover some health once he touches it again. Medicine, most of which restore one of his meters, temporarily boosts his statistics, or recovers from a status ailment, can also be equipped. Its effect is immediate after pressing the corresponding button. In the Saturn version of the game, Alucard has a designated "third hand" that he can use for using food and medicine.
