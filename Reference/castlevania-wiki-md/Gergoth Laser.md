# Gergoth Laser

right|270px

  - Gergoth Laser** is a signature attack of Gergoth. The beast unleashes a wide and powerful laser beam from its mouth that passes through walls. It can be gained by Soma Cruz by absorbing its soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. When Soma uses it, an unlimited beam that is about as tall as him is released in the direction he's facing.

In *Dawn of Sorrow*, it is a Guardian Soul and can be sustained for as long as he likes or until he runs out of MP.

In *Harmony of Despair*, it is a Bullet Soul and is fired in bursts of a specific amount of time.
