# Death/Symphony of the Night

150px|thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">Death's artwork from *Symphony of the Night*.</p>

  - Death** greets Alucard after he begins his assault on the castle in the Castle Entrance in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. After Alucard refused to back down, he steals his powerful equipped gear (Alucard Sword, Alucard Shield, Alucard Mail, Necklace of J, and Dragon Helm), and indicates he will see him again soon. He later makes his stand in the Reverse Caverns, but is defeated.

In the English version of the game, Death is voiced by Patrick Seitz (*The Dracula X Chronicles*, as a replacement of Dennis Falt).
