# Wai Wai World 1 &amp; 2 Sound Collection

- Wai Wai World 1 & 2 Sound Collection*** is the original soundtrack for the games *Konami Wai Wai World* and *Wai Wai World 2: SOS!! Parsley Castle*. Released on May 19, 2014 in Japan.
