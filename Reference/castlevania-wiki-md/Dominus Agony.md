# Dominus Agony

right|100px
 is a back glyph appearing in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It boosts STR, INT, CON and MND stats by 66 each, but consumes 66 HP per second. This glyph does less damage to Shanoa than Dominus Hatred or Dominus Anger.

If used wisely, this glyph provides a serious power boost. It's most useful when combined with the player's most powerful glyph unions, although Dominus will kill Shanoa if left unattended, so it's only useful for a limited amount of time and if the player is in possession of healing items. A good strategy is to activate it right before attacking and deactivate it immediately after, using it in short bursts. If Shanoa's health is low, using Arma Custos would be a better strategy.

When Shanoa has the Death Ring equipped, the glyph will not instantly kill her, making it a good technique for defeating strong enemies/bosses quicker.
