  - Adam D. Clark** is an American voice actor. He is well known to his fan as the voice of Arkham/Jester in *Devil May Cry 3: Dante's Awakening*, Steven Chapman in *Dead Rising* and Oswell E. Spencer in *Resident Evil 5*.

Adam voiced Saint Germain in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, and acted as the motion capture actor for Isaac in the same game.
