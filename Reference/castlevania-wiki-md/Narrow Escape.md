# Narrow Escape

- Narrow Escape** is an Enchanted Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained from the Zombie Officer and allows Soma to regain balance after taking damage. Balance can be regained by pressing **[JUMP]** whenever he gets knocked back or takes damage while in midair. Damage will still be taken, but he won't be sent flying back after taking the hit.

The soul's description implies it causes health to be restored if the player takes damage while jumping, although this is incorrect.
