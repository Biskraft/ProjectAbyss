# Uppercut

right

The **Uppercut** is an upward swinging attack with one's fists. For whip wielders, it is often performed with the whip wrapped around the fist while leaping high up in the air, which may also count as a high jump depending on its height.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
  - Guts Upper** is a bullet soul and it can be obtained from a Weretiger. Soma will perform an uppercut while leaping a short distance straight up. Using it while in air won't increase Soma's jump height.

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
Soma gains this ability, this time called **Max Upper**, from the soul of a Hell Boar. Unlike the uppercut from *Aria of Sorrow*, this time he will perform it only while standing on the ground.
