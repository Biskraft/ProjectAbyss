# Were-Panther Skeleton

The **Were-Panther Skeleton**, also known as **Wereskeleton**, is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is the reanimated skeleton of a Were-Panther.

## Description
  - Were-Panther Skeletons** are the reanimated remains of a Were-Panther. These enemies have appeared in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*, *Castlevania: Dracula X* and *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.

There are two types:
;Beast form
:This type moves in all fours like an animal and charges at the player when approached. It behaves and attacks almost identical to a Black Panther.
;Human form
:This type paces back and forth in an upright stance. When approached, it extends its neck and lashes it in a whip-like manner. After a while, it will open its rib cage and send out a large barrage of bones in an arcing pattern that are harmful to the touch.

A special variant called **Wolf Skeleton** starts appearing from Mortvia Aqueduct and onward in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. Described as the corpse of a Fenrir revived as a zombie, it can hide inside its own shadow and move freely underground until it leaps out to attack.
