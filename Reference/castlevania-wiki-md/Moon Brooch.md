# Moon Brooch

right|50px

The **Moon Brooch** is an elegant brooch that shines with the light of the moon.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
In *Portrait of Ruin*, it increases LCK (+5) and also increases DEF by the average amount (+3), which is the same as the Abalone Brooch.

It can be found inside a room at the Nation of Fools; if a player wants to find it while making their way to the top of the level (where the entrance to the boss room is) without having to explore the whole area, they must start by exploring the left side of the level.

It can also be purchased from Vincent Dorin at the Entrance for 30,000G.
