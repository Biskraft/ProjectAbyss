# Lucky Ring

The **Lucky Ring** is a ring that increases Luck. It replaced the Lapis Lazuli ring found in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and was phased out in favor of the Lucky Charm in later games.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*
Called Luck Ring in *Circle of the Moon*. Its central jewel is star shaped, and in addition to raising LCK (+50), it also reduces DEF and INT by 10. It has a scaled down effect as the Miracle Armband. It is the Luck counterpart to the Intelligence Ring, Hard Ring (DEF), and Strength Ring.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*
In *Harmony of Dissonance*, it has a red jewel on its crown with a gold framework around it and simply increases LCK by 20 (which is twice as much as the Lucky Charm). It can be found hidden inside a secret room of the Clock Tower (Castle B), roughly at the middle of where the metal rolling boulder puzzle is. Otherwise, it is a very rare item, as it is only dropped by the Rare Ghost, which only occasionally appears.
