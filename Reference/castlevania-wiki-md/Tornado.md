# Tornado

right|232px
  - Tornado** can be used as weapons in the *Castlevania* series.

In *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, a tornado is formed with an Apollo + Griffin DSS combination.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, **Devil Tornado** is a Guardian Soul which has the ability to have a tornado circling around the Soma so enemies get injured that gets near it. It also has the capability to suck and diminish enemy projectiles.
