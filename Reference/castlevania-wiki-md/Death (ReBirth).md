 is a boss in *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth*.

## Character's history
In 1576, after Count Dracula was resurrected, Death presumably returned alongside him. He later fought Christopher Belmont at the Clock Tower, although he was ultimately defeated by the vampire hunter, with Death only expressing disbelief at being defeated before emitting an agonized scream.

## Boss battle
Death is the boss of the fifth level, the Clock Tower. At the beginning of the battle, Death does not use a scythe and instead summons his mini-scythes. He can also duplicate himself, and the two Deaths will hurl the mini-scythes from the sides of the room (the duplicate dies in one hit). He can also use a fragile crystal scythe and slide it across the ground.

After his health is reduced by one half, he will use his own, massive scythe and swipe with it. The warning for this attack is when he pulls his scythe back and then a red line where his attack will occur will appear. He can also summon three scythe blades that will circle around him for a short time while he moves around. After this, he will summon homing skulls that can be destroyed. He'll repeat the pattern after the skull summon until defeated.
