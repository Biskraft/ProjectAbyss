The  is a location in *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. It is the first bridge encountered. It connects the Jova Woods to the west with the Veros Woods to the east. It is a segmented bridge infested with Skeletons and Mermen.

The bridge goes across the south-east fork of the great river of Transylvania.
