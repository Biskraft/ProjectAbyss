The **Giant Worm** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. Transformed into a giant insect by exposure to castle's magic.

## Description
In *Castlevania Legends*, it attacks from below the ground. In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, it doesn't do much aside from crawling around, but its soul will allow Soma to recover HP by standing around and not moving.

Variations of the giant worm include the Poison Worm and the Sand Worm, as well as some other worm-like creatures.
