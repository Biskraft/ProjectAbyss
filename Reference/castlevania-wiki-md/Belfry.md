The **Belfry** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. It is the second area Alucard explores. It was previously explored by Trevor as the fifteenth area during his journey.

## Overview
In Trevor's quest, two Hunchbacks can be seen cutting the restraints of a large bell in order to crush him, although he managed to escape. After the accident, the floor where the bell fell was replaced with wooden boards as a provisional repair. The Toy Maker makes an appearance looking down after the defeat of the Daemon Lord by Trevor. 30 years later, when Alucard comes to this area, the floor was already repaired.
