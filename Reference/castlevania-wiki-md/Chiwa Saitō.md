# Chiwa Saitō

(born March 12, 1981) is a Japanese voice actress represented by I'm Enterprise. She is best known for her anime roles, which include Hitagi Senjougahara in *Monogatari*, Homura Akemi in *Puella Magi Madoka Magica*, Yona in *Akatsuki no Yona*, Chloe von Eizenbern in *Fate/kaleid liner Prisma Illya*, and Aoi Asahina in the *Danganronpa* video games and anime.

She voiced Maria Renard in the Japanese version of *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
