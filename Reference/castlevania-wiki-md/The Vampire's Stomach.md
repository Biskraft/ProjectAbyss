# The Vampire's Stomach

- The Vampire's Stomach** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Bloodlines*. It plays during the final battle with Dracula.

It is the 36th track in the fourth disc of *Akumajō Dracula Best Music Collections BOX*, but it was also arranged by Yamane as track 5 on the 18th disc as a celebration of her career. This track can be acquired separately from the box set as part of the [Akumajo Dracula × Michiru Yamane Autobiography Music](https://vgmdb.net/album/54198) disc.
