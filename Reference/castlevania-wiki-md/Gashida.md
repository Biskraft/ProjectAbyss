  - Gashida** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a living statue that can petrify human flesh with its breath.

## Description
Gashidas are living statues of winged bestial creatures with the face of a bearded man. They attack by swiping with their claws or by pouncing over their prey from long distances. They also have the ability to breath a gas that causes petrification upon contact.

They appear to be extremely weathered and readily fall apart upon defeat.
