# Medal

A **Medal** is a type of accessory that appears in *Castlevania*. It first appears in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, where it increases both attack and defense by 1. It can be "awarded" as a drop item for defeating a tough Owl Knight at the beginning of the Entrance stage before Alucard levels up.

In *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*, the Medal is given as a reward for defeating the game's first boss, Gaibon.
