# Three 7s

right|250px

  - Three 7s** are clothes that bring good luck.

In *Dawn of Sorrow*, the Three 7s clothes can be found in a secret room located at the Garden of Madness. To access this room, the last three digits of the current amount of gold the player is currently holding must end in "777". Once this requirement has been satisfied, Soma must then touch the altar shaped like a slot machine to move it out of the way and finally claim the clothes.

## Trivia
- The number 777 inscribed on the item is a number frequently associated with good luck or jackpot numbers for slot machines.
