# Requiem

Castlevania Requiem|Requiem for the Nameless Victims}}

  - Requiem** is a song composed by Sanoppi for *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*. It is the background music played during the main menu at the beginning of the game.

It was later used for the title screen in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

## Trivia
- The song sounds remarkably similar to the well-known prayer of Christian liturgy "Kyrie Eleison" (translit. *Kýrie Eléēson*). This phrase is chanted repeatedly in the lyrics and translate from Ancient Greek to "Lord, have mercy".
