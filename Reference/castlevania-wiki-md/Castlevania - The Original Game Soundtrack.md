# Castlevania: The Original Game Soundtrack

- Castlevania: The Original Game Soundtrack**, known in Japan as **Akumajō Dracula: Apocalypse Original Game Soundtrack**, is the soundtrack for the 1999 Nintendo 64 game *Castlevania*.

It was composed by Masahiko Kimura, Motoaki Furukawa and Mariko Egawa, and released on March 26, 1999.

## Additional information
- The three bonus tracks were arranged by Masahiko Kimura and Motoaki Furukawa.
- "Mysterious Coffin" contains the famous *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest* track, "Bloody Tears".
- "Second Struggle" is an arrangement of *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*'s track "Dancing in Phantasmic Hell", which is also a boss theme.

## Sheet music
Sheet music from the bonus track "A Night of Peace and Quiet" included with the booklet from the soundtrack.
