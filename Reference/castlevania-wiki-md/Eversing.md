# Eversing

The **Eversing** is a body armor in the *Castlevania* series. It is an armor tempered underneath a waterfall for many millenniums. It is generally one of the toughest armors found in the game.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
The Eversing is the highest armor that only provides a DEF boost (+45).  This is greater than the Gold Plate (+38), but less than Demon's Mail (+49 and +9 STR and ATT, with protection against the Dark element). It can be found behind the waterfall leading to the Forbidden Area, but you must use one of the three Rush Form souls in order to pass it.

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
The Eversing is again the second highest DEF armor in the game (+46), which is greater than a Cuirass (+38) but less than Demon's Mail (+55). Like in the previous game, it can be found behind a waterfall, in the Subterranean Hell, but you must break a wall and use either the Bat Company or Hippogryph's soul to reach it. Another method to reach it is to perform a precise diagonal jump kick from a platform above the Eversing wall, bypassing the waterfall and landing inside of the room containing the armor.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
The Eversing can only be equipped by Soma and provides multiples bonuses: DEF boost (+27), MND boost (+10), Blunt boost (+15), Ice boost (+15), and 20 additional HP.
