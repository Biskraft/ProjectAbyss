# Death (disambiguation)

- Death** (also known as the **Grim Reaper**) in the *Castlevania* series is the personification of life's end. A being presumably born since the beginning of time who is tasked with ripping the souls of the living once their existence in this world has reached its end. He is Count Dracula's confidant and his loyal servant due to the allegiance he pledges to whomever possesses the fabled Crimson Stone.

Several incarnations of this character have appeared throughout the series.
    - Death**
  - Death/Symphony of the Night
  - Death/Harmony of Dissonance
  - Zead — Death's alternate human identity in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, used to deceit others, gain their trust and manipulate them to do his bidding.
  - Death/Portrait of Ruin
  - Death/Judgment — Intended to be the same character, it is debatable if this incarnation fits in the main canon.
  - Death/Pachislot — Intended to be the same character, it is debatable if this incarnation fits in the main canon.
  - Death/Pachinko — Intended to be the same character, it is debatable if this incarnation fits in the main canon.
- Grim Reaper (Legends) — A boss in *Castlevania Legends*. Unlike most of his appearances, he has no character development or involvement in the game's storyline. Probably initially intended to be the same character from the main timeline, he, as well as all events taking place in *Castlevania Legends*, has been removed from the main canon.
- Death (Circle of the Moon) — A boss in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. Unlike most of his appearances, he has no character development or involvement in the game's storyline. Probably initially intended to be the same character from the main timeline, he, as well as all events taking place in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, has been removed from the main canon.
- Death (Nintendo 64) — Initially intended to be the same character from the main timeline, he, as well as all events taking place in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*, has been removed from the main canon.
- Death (ReBirth)
- Zobek — The personification of Death in the *Lords of Shadow* saga. An alternate human identity used to deceit others, gain their trust and manipulate them to do his bidding.
