# Iron Ore

- Iron Ore** is an item in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. Iron ore is part of a balanced blacksmith.

## Availability
Dropped by Rock Knights and Automatons ZX26.

An Iron Ore can be found inside a red chest in the bottom-leftmost big room in Kalidus Channel. They can also appear inside of wooden chests in Kalidus Channel and Tymeo Mountains, as well as green chests in Minera Prison Island.

## Usage
Iron Ore is required in order to complete Eugen's quest: "Poor Preparation is Costly". If the player brings him 3 Iron Ores, he will reward them with 2,400G and the items Iron Plate and Iron Leggings will be added to Jacob's shop.
