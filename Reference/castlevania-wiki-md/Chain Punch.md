# Chain Punch

right

  - Chain Punch** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by the Ironside Battle-Type evolution.

When cast, the Battle-Type will lift both its arms and launch its fists toward an enemy.

Costs 10 Hearts to cast.

## How to obtain
Defeat a certain number of enemies while Ironside is summoned.
