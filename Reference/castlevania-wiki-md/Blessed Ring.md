# Blessed Ring

right|73px
The **Blessed Ring** (called the **Holy Ring** in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, **Cleric's Ring** in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and the **Curse Sealing Ring** in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*) is a ring that helps prevents Curses. It gives a 75% chance of preventing them in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, it completely overcomes curses (as opposed to the Charm Necklace, which only offers some resistance).
