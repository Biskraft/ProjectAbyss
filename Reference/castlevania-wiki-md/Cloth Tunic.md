# Cloth Tunic

- Cloth Tunic**s are usually one of the least-protective body equipment found in a given game. They are usually dropped by Zombies, which in other games drop similar low-level protective gear such as Cotton Clothes, Casual Clothes, or Hobo's Clothes.
