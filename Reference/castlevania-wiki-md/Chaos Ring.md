# Chaos Ring

The **Chaos Ring**, also known as the **Magus Ring**, is a ring which in most games it appears in greatly increases MP regeneration rate.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*
An item with the same name first appeared in *Harmony of Dissonance*. It increases the strength of the Holy Water sub-weapon when equipped.

### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
Found in the Chaotic Realm. Soma must have acquired every soul, then it will appear at the top level of this area.

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
Found in the Abyss. After Soma has acquired all souls, the ring will appear upon a ledge within the frigid section of this area.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The Magus Ring is a ring that increases MP restoration rate to the point where it is practically limitless. It is only obtained once all of Wind's quests are complete.

### *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*
Regenerates 0.4 MP per second, twice the effect of the Sage Ring. Drops off of Menace in Chapter 3 (Hard Mode), and Ryukotsuki in Chapter 11 (Hard Mode).
