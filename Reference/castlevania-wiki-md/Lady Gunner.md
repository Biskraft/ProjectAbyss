# Lady Gunner

One of the two selectable characters in *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* is a generic **Lady Gunner**. She is equipped with a gun more traditional of this style of game. She can be use four different kinds of special bullets as sub-weapons and also uses martial arts.
