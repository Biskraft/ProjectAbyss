The **Room of Illusion** is a location in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* dominated by Medusa Heads and a swirling colored backgrounds.

## Overview
This is the smallest area of the game, consisting only of just a few underground rooms and caves. Its main purpose is to house the first teleport room of the game, allowing passage to Castle B for the first time (even though Juste doesn't realize this at first). This location has an overall green colored scheme and statues of men can be seen decorating the background. In Castle B, three Victory Armors can be found in the same room as the four Lizard Men are in Castle A. Additionally, a Rare Ghost may also appear in this same room (Castle B).
