# The Art of Castlevania: Lords of Shadow

- The Art of Castlevania: Lords of Shadow** is a 192-page artbook published by [Titan Books](http://titanbooks.com/) and written by Martin Robinson. It was released on March 4th, 2014. The book was released both as a regular edition signed collector's edition that features a certificate, a box for keepsake and a different cover.

The book contains artworks, concept art and sketches from all three games in the *Lords of Shadow* subseries (*Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*, *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate* and *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*). It was created during the development of the final game, and because of that, it presented some artwork never seen before. It also features some in-depth explanations for visual concepts and unused designs, such as Satan's second form and removed enemies.

The book is divided in 10 parts, titled *Introduction*, *Gabriel Belmont*, *Dracula*, *The Belmont Family*, *Allies & Antagonists*, *Whips & Chains*, *Supernatural Bestiary*, *Locations & Environments*, *The Modern Age* and *Acknowledgements*.

The author, Martin Robinson, works as Features Editor in Eurogamer and has written other gaming monographic books such as *Halo: The Art of Building Worlds*.
