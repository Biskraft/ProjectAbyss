# Category:Arthurian Lore

- Arthurian Lore** comes from the legends surrounding King Arthur. He was a legendary British leader of the late 5th and early 6th centuries, who, according to medieval histories and romances, led the defence of Britain against Saxon invaders in the early 6th century. King Arthur was the owner of the legendary sword Excalibur.
