# Dim Sum Set

- Dim Sum Set** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It consists of three Dim Sum dishes and gives triple the amount of health as a single Dim Sum (called a Chinese Bun in the North American version). It recovers 30 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is one more than a Gyros Plate, but two less than Natou.
