# Backstab

- Backstab** is an ability in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It is obtained by gaining dominance over the Dead Pirate's soul and allows Soma to inflict double damage to enemies if he attacks them from behind. Such ability makes it easier to defeat stronger enemies, such as Great Armors or Iron Golems, which is useful if the player is looking to collect their souls.

While this ability is almost useless against enemies who can turn around fast, used in conjunction with the Puppet Master's soul, Substitute Puppet, to quickly position oneself behind them can prove to be devastating.

This soul is effective on most enemies, but against bosses it's only helpful against Dmitrii, Malphas, Dario, Rahab, Zephyr and Abaddon.
