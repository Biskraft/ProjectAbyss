# Diana

is a non-playable character in *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. She is one of the children who were captured and brought to Dracula's Castle. It is Henry's mission to find this child and five other children.

Diana is hidden somewhere in the caves of the Tunnel. After getting off the blue platform, go over the pits to the right; at the end should be a "moon door" with the child inside (to exit the cave, defeat Queen Algenie, located to the right after exiting the room with the child).

Finding her unlocks Carrie Fernandez as a playable character.
