# Monster Vial

right|300px

The **Monster Vial** (called **Capsule Monster** in Japanese versions) is a consumable item in the *Castlevania* series. It summons a monster ally.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
Monster Vials can be used once to summon a low-level monster to come to Alucard's aid. They are usually dropped from the kind of monster they summon, although they can be found elsewhere as well.
    - Monster Vial 1** summons a Merman.
    - Monster Vial 2** summons a Bat.
    - Monster Vial 3** summons a Skeleton.

### *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*
A Bullet Soul called **Capsule Monster #4** allows Soma to summon a lesser Homunculus by gaining dominance over a Homunculus.

## Trivia
- "*Capsule Monsters*" is the name of an early design concept for the *Pokémon* franchise. The original Japanese name for this item "Capsule Monster", is probably a reference to it.
