# Alucard (disambiguation)

- Alucard** may refer to:

    - 1997** — Alucard: Alucard's appearance from *Castlevania Legends*, which was later retconned as a parallel story.
    - 1993** — Alucard: A character from the *Captain N: The Game Master* animated series.
    - 2013** — Alucard: A character from the *Lords of Shadow* reboot, formerly known as Trevor Belmont.
    - 2017** — Alucard: A version of the character based on that from the original timeline and one of the main protagonists of the *Castlevania* animated series.
