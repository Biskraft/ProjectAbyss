# Elemental Tactician

- Elemental Tactician** is a music theme originally composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is played when Leon fights the Fire Elemental, Ice Elemental and Thunder Elemental.

In *Castlevania Judgment*, it is played during battles against lesser enemies.
