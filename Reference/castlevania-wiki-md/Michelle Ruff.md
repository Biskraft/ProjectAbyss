# Michelle Ruff

- Michelle Suzanne Ruff** is a Californian voice actress. She voiced Maria Renard as a replacement of Kimberly Forsythe in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles* and its port of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. She also provided the voice of Shanoa in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, *Castlevania Judgment* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. Her role as Maria was eventually handed over to Philece Sampler.

Michelle is also known for voicing characters such as Chi in *Chobits*, Rukia Kuchiki in *Bleach*, Aoi Sakuraba in *Ai Yori Aoshi*, Yoko Littner in *Gurren Lagann*, Euphemia li Britannia in *Code Geass*, and Yuki Nagato in *The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya*; as well as Fujiko Mine in *Lupin III*, Luna in the Viz Media dub of *Sailor Moon*, and Jill Valentine in *Resident Evil: Revelations*.
