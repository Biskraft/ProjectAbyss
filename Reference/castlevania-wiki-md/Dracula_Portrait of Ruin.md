# Dracula/Portrait of Ruin

250px|right|thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">Dracula artwork from *Portrait of Ruin*.</p>

  - Dracula** was destroyed by John Morris and Eric Lecarde during World War I. His eternal servant, Death, intends to revive him during the events of *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. After been revived, Dracula stands with him and faces Jonathan and Charlotte, but nonetheless he is ultimately defeated by them.

In the English version of the game, Dracula is voiced by Douglas Rye.
