The **Fire Pinnacle** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the second stage of Chapter 10.  

## Zobek's journal
:*Powerful Necromancers have used this once sacred ground to build an army of the dead. Conquering nearby lands, legions of Zombies have devoured the living and given rise to this desolation. No man has travelled here without meeting death. Gabriel now faces his toughest challenge. He now bangs his fists on the very doors of hell on Earth and the Lord who commands here is listening, waiting for him. Smiling…*
