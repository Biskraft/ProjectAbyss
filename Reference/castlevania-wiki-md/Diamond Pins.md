# Diamond Pins

- Diamond Pins** are hair pins in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. They reduce the amount of damage taken from Light based attacks. They can be purchased from Jacob's shop once the player completes Laura's "A Heartwarming Accessory" quest by giving her both a Diamond and an Onyx.
