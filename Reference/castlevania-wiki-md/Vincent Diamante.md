  - Vincent Diamante** is the composer of the music featured in the game *Castlevania: Order of Shadows*.

His most known works are the soundtracks for the PlayStation 3 versions of *flOw*, *Flower* and *Journey* from Thatgamecompany. He currently alternates between working as audio director for Thatgamecompany and teaching at the University of Southern California.
