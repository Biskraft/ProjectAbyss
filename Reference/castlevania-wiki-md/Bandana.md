# Bandana

The **Bandana** is a piece of cloth designed to protect the forehead in *Castlevania*.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it is a relatively high grade head protector, giving Defense +2 and Intelligence +1. It's usefulness is in between the two masks which give the same Defense boost, the Ballroom Mask (no Int boost) and the Stone Mask (INT +4).

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, Jonathan Morris can equip it for DEF +3 and DEF and CON +1. It is somewhat less useful than the other head gear that boosts these three physical attributes, the Combat Beret (+2 each).

- Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* features both the regular Bandana and Simon's Bandana, which was passed down through generations of Belmonts.
