  - Prologue** is the introductory stage in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*.

## Overview
Richter Belmont rides a carriage during a rainstorm, heading to a village that is being attacked by monsters and where his girlfriend Annette lives. Along the way, Death appears to him to test his strength. After surviving a few attacks, Death tells him that next time things will not be so easy and bids him farewell.
