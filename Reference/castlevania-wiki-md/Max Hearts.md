# Max Hearts

- Max Hearts** is a statistic found in nearly all *Castlevania* games. It is a total of how many Hearts the player character can carry. Hearts are often used as ammunition for sub-weapons or special abilities.

In most metroidvania style games, Max Hearts are increased each time the player character earns a level up or collects a Heart Max Up. The amount of Hearts capacity gain per event ranges between 5 to 10 extra Hearts.
