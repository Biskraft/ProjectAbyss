# Tart Tatin

- Tart Tatin** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It looks like an upside-down Tart, and it is given as a reward given by Irina for killing a Werewolf in the "*Do You Hear Howling?*" quest.
