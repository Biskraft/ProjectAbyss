## Trivia
- It is notable that Richter is seen wielding a Combat Cross from the *Lords of Shadow* timeline instead of the usual leather form of the Vampire Killer that his *Dracula X Chronicles* incarnation is usually seen with.
- Similar to other *Castlevania* casino games, this pachislot game features some elements of erotic fanservice, which are even highlighted in the trailer where it displays the phrase "Erotic Violence" in both English and Japanese nearing the end.
