# Invisibility

right|70px

  - Invisibility** is a Bullet Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. A cloaking shield makes the player invisible before enemies' eyes.

## Availability
Dropped by Nemesis. This enemy can be found in the Dance Hall as well as the Inner Quarters.

## Characteristics
Invisibility is the ability to render oneself invisible before enemies. Some enemies (such as Alastor and Nemesis) are able sense this ability and they will chase the player immediately once the effect ends, although they won't sense them with much precision if they are too far away.
