# Contract

The Contract is a scroll written in an ancient demon script, impossible to read.  It is found in several locations throughout the game and can be used to summon Renon, who will then allow you to purchase items from him using Gold.  However, the fine print in this contract stipulates that if one spends more than 30,000 gold, one's soul is forfeit to Satan.  Renon will attempt collect on this soul near the end of the game, requiring you to battle him.
