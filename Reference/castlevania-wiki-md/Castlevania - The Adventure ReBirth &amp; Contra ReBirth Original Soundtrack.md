# Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth &amp; Contra ReBirth Original Soundtrack

- Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth & Contra ReBirth Original Soundtrack** is the original soundtrack for *Castlevania: The Adventure ReBirth* and *Contra ReBirth*. A main part of this soundtrack contains arranged versions of former *Castlevania* games themes. Music was arranged by Manabu Namiki and originally composed by Michiru Yamane and Konami Kukeiha Club.
