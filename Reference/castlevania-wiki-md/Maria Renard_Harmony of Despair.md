  - Maria Renard** appears as a DLC playable character in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.




## Attack
Maria can attack with her classic Doves as her main weapon. Unlike in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*, she cannot run while attacking with these doves.


## Sub-Weapons
Maria can use three of her four traditional animal spirits as subweapons. In this game, they are referred to by their Chinese names, rather than their usual Japanese names. She also has access to her Hymn sub-weapon, which she lacked in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. She is still missing her Egg sub-weapon from *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* though.


## Personal Action
Maria's personal action is her protective turtle shell, Xuan Wu. When hiding under the shell, damage is reduced by half.


## Martial Arts
Maria's martial art, Guardian Knuckle, is the only ability of hers that she does not start the game with. Her spirit is sent out to flail away madly at whatever enemy is in front of her.


## Dual Crushes
Maria will always perform an Aura Blast with any of the male characters or with another version of herself. She will always perform a female character's signature Dual Crush with them, including Yoko Belnades' Thor's Hammer, Shanoa's Cocytus, and Charlotte's Phlegeton.
