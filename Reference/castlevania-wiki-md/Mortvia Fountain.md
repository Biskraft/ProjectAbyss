  - Mortvia Fountain** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the background theme of the Mortvia Aqueduct's interiors. A small instrumental piece that is identical to Lost Painting can be heard one minute and twenty seconds into the track.
