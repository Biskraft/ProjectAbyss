# Gatling Knight

The **Gatling Knight** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Bloodlines*.

## Description
The Gatling Knight is an armored knight who bears a gatling gun attached to his arm. Only one appears in the entire game, near the end of a long hallway in the Versailles Palace and just preceding the Silhouette Demon.

He attacks by firing an extensive barrage of bullets at the hero and then takes a moment to reload. Crouch when he's shooting and approach while he's reloading, then strike repeatedly until the knight is defeated.
