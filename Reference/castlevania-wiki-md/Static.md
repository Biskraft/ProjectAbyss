# Static

- Static** is a Lightning spell that Alucard can perform in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*. He lifts up his cape as if to perform a Hellfire spell and then radiates an expanding field static electricity. After performing the spell, for the next 20 (or 30 for level 2) seconds, any attack the opponent makes causes them to take damage for each block dropped on Alucard's board.
