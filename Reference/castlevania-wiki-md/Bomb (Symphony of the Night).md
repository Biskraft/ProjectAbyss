# Bomb (Symphony of the Night)

- Bombs** in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* are any one-time use items that when thrown can cause Hit damage to everything on the screen. Their effects are very similar to each other, with some variance in animations or the amount of damage they deal.

## General information
The **Karma Coin** is slightly different from the other normal bombs (**Neutron Bomb**, **Power of Sire**, **Pentagram**, **Bat Pentagram**). In its case, a coin is tossed onto the floor. If it turns up heads, every enemy on the screen will get damaged like a normal bomb and their health will be transferred to Alucard. If it turns up tails, the attack is simply a localized lightning strike that hits the coin.

All of these items can be very useful when used in conjunction with the Duplicator.

## Trivia
- The **Power of Sire**'s splash is a multi-colored version of Vlad III Ţepeş's portrait.
- If a bomb item does enough damage it removes a Spectral Sword's shields in the Anti-Chapel, the player can use a method to level up a familiar to its maximum in just about 20-30 minutes. Simply enter the room, quickly activate the bomb, then leave and repeat.
- The **Bat Pentagram** will change forms depending on which hand the player uses it with.
- The **Bat Pentagram**'s design comes from an astrological/zodiacal interpretation found on the *Oedipus Aegyptiacus*, tomes of studies on Egyptology done by 17th century German scholar Athanasius Kircher.
