# Order of the Demon

- Order of the Demon** is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is Dracula's theme in this game and the song heard during the battle against him. Besides its original version, *Order of Ecclesia*’s original soundtrack features an arrangement of this track as well. Another arrangement of this track is featured in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* during the first half of the fight against Dracula in Come, Sweet Hour of Death.
