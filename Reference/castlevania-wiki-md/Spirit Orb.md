# Spirit Orb

The  is a relic that displays the amount of damage done to enemies in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is called a **Soul Orb** in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. This allows the player to experiment with different kinds of attacks to find out what's most effective against a given enemy. The ability to view damage done to enemies is always on for the unlockable characters Richter, Maria and Maxim in these games.

Most games with role-playing elements do not require the use of an orb to display this information, including *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, *Castlevania: Order of Shadows* and *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.
