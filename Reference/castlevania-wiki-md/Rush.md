# Rush

right|250px

  - Rush** is a Dual Crush in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It is the first Dual Rush expected for the player to collect in the game and it can be found in one of the rooms from the market area in the City of Haze, the first portrait the player has access to.

It involves the two playable characters, Jonathan and Charlotte, quickly taking turns to attack with their current weapons and deal considerable to heavy damage to an enemy. It must be used with caution, tough, as it leaves the player on the open and at the mercy of any surviving foes when it ends.
