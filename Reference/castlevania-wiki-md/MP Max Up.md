# MP Max Up

The **MP Max Up** is an item created for the purpose of increasing the maximum Mind Points of the characters who acquire them. They are usually spread out amongst Dracula's Castle and other locales visited within the *Castlevania* series.

## Trivia
250px|right
- In the Trevor Mode of *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*, the item that increases Trevor's Heart meter is labeled as "MP Max Up" in the lower-right of the screen, despite the center text clearly displaying "Heart max up". But for gameplay purposes, the meter functions much like the Hearts system from other Castlevania games - Defeating enemies and destroying objects provide collectible Hearts, which can then be consumed to utilize Sub-weapons.
