# Jewel Crush

The **Jewel Crush** is a glove that destroys Jewels and releases their hidden magical potential. When equipped, the description of the Jewels changes as follows:

- Diamond - Totally recovers all status abnormalities and HP with power hidden in Diamonds.
- Ruby - The energy hidden within the Ruby damages all enemies in the area.
- Sapphire - Damages enemy, and with each damage you recover a little energy.
- Opal - The damage is small, but it blows away the enemy.
- Turquoise - Arbitrarily switches the sub-weapon. The previous item will not return.
- Zircon - Increases your money with the zircon's power. Luck determines if selling it is worth more.
