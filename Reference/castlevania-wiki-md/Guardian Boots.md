# Guardian Boots

The **Guardian Boots** are the leg component to the Guardian set in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is obtained along with the Guardian Armor (DEF +36), Guardian Gloves (DEF +14), and the Guardian Helm (DEF +14) when the Guardian Armor is destroyed by gears. They give Defense +8, which is two greater than Bronze Boots, but one less than Iron Boots.
