# Healing Spirit

The spirit of a woman who lived hundreds of years prior to the events in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*, who it's said had the power of healing. Pilgrims built an underground tunnel in order to worship her spirit, which acts as an access point to the castle.

It's possible (but not confirmed) that this woman was related to the Belnades Clan, as women of that clan have been known to be permitted to have their spirits remain on the earth in order to help those in need. In addition, the Spirit of Belnades is found near the shrine for this woman.
