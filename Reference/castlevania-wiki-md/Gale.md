# Gale

right|200px


  - Gale** is the Initial Devil-Type Innocent Devil form found in the Eneomaos Machine Tower. It knows the skill Magic Circle.


## General Information
With only the Magic Circle ability from the start. The Gale plays a role nothing more than a support, lacking offensive skills, and only having few combat prowess. The player must then take note of the Devil-Type Innocent Devil's single-line evolution tree, the next one would be the Brow which features more offense. Hector must evolve this form fast to level 2 with any 200 crystals of the same color, in order to unlock its combat potential.
