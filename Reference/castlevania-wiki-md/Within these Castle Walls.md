  - Within these Castle Walls** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. It is the background theme that plays inside the abandoned ruins of Dracula's Castle as Simon Belmont makes his way toward the basement to burn The Count's body parts and finally put an end to the curse that has befallen the land.
