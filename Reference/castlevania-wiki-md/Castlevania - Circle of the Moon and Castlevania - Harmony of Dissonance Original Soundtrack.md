# Castlevania: Circle of the Moon and Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance Original Soundtrack

- Castlevania: Circle of the Moon and Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance Original Soundtrack** is the soundtrack for *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It was released on June 26, 2002, and a bootleg was released the same year.

The music of *Circle of the Moon* was composed by Sotaro Tojima and Hiroshi Mitsuoka, while the music for *Harmony of Dissonance* was composed by Soshiro Hokkai and Michiru Yamane.

## Additional information
### Composers
  - Circle of the Moon*: Sotaro Tojima (tracks 5, 10, 17), Hiroshi Mitsuoka (tracks 4, 18, 19).
- Concerto of Midnight Sun (*Harmony of Dissonance*): Soshiro Hokkai (tracks 20~41), Michiru Yamane (tracks 42~44).
- Arrange sound composer: Takashi Yoshida (track 45).
- Tracks 01~19: from *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.
- Tracks 20~45: from *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

### Tracks originally from
Tracks composed by Konami Kukeiha Club:
- Tracks 1, 16: from *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* (PCE)
- Tracks 2, 8: from *Castlevania* (N64)
- Tracks 3, 6: from *Castlevania: Bloodlines* (MD)
- Tracks 7, 11, 13, 14: from *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* (FC)
- Tracks 9, 12: from *Super Castlevania IV* (SFC)
- Tracks 15, 22: from *Castlevania* (FC)
- Track 36: from *Castlevania Adventure* / *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge* (GB)
- Track 44: from *Castlevania* / *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* (FC)
