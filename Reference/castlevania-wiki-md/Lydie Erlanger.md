# Lydie Erlanger

is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. She is good friends with Juste Belmont and Maxim Kischine, whom she has known well since childhood.

## Character's history

## Lydie's fate
Lydie helps Juste on his quest in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* and ends up captured by Maxim after he turns evil. If Juste finds every furniture item and puts them all into place, Lydie will lean on his shoulder and cling to his arm during the ending, revealing there may be a romance in the future for the two of them.

## Trivia
- According to IGA, Juste and Maxim received their respective bracelets from Lydie.
- Her name is German, which means: "He/She, who is from Erlangen, Bavaria". This would probably make her an ethnic Transylvanian Saxon or Banat Swabian.
