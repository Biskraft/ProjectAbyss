# Sacred Fist

right|312px

The **Sacred Fist** is a sub-weapon in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. When used, Juste will attack with a flurry of punches to strike enemies repeatedly. When combined with a Spell Book, the sub-weapon can attack with elementally-improved melee attacks, such as an Ice-encrusted rushing punch or an energy ball when used with Wind.

## Notes
- A useful glitch can be performed by equipping the **Sacred Fist** and the Ice Book, allowing the player to reach certain ledges that are intended to be accessed later in the game. To use it, equip both items, jump toward a seemingly unreachable ledge and at the peak of the jump activate the spell (↑ + ATTACK). If performed correctly, the animation will make Juste to slightly hop upward and lunge forward in midair, allowing him to land on the ledge. This can be used to get Vlad's Ring and Logoth's Ring early in the game, as well as the Platinum Tip once the Sylph Feather (double jump) has been acquired.

## Trivia
- When using this sub-weapon, Juste yells: "Oraoraora!!!", a common Japanese battle cry used in anime/manga usually heard when someone is charging toward an enemy. This yell originates from *JoJo's Bizarre Adventure*, which features several vampire antagonists.
