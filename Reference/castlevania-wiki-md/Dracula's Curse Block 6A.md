Block 6A (Route 8) for the game *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* is the **Causeway of Chaos** portion of the **Curse Tower**. It consists of an aqueduct and a crumbling bridge that leads to Dracula's Castle. Trevor's sub-weapons in order include the Dagger, Holy Water (Ice for Sypha which can freeze the water), Stopwatch, another Holy Water, another Dagger, and an Axe.
