# Flame Shot

right|354px
  - Flame Shot**, also known as **Flame Ball**, is a particularly large and fast moving fireball spell. It fires in a horizontal line forward across the screen and can also be fired from the air.

Soma Cruz can obtain this spell in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair* from a Flame Demon (Fire Demon), and it can also be used while in bat form, much like the Hellfire ability could in *Aria of Sorrow*. Charlotte Aulin can capture and learn this spell from a Fire Demon in *Harmony of Despair*.
