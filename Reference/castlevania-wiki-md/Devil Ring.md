# Devil Ring

The **Devil Ring** is an accessory in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a ring that represents the Tarot card "The Devil". It gives an increase to CON, scaling in effectiveness as one takes damage.

There are a couple notes of interest to keep in mind. Firstly, the effectiveness of the Devil Ring, although not hinted at in its description, does not go away once the player restores HP. This, unfortunately, leads to the second note of interest; Shanoa must suffer  around 9,900 points of damage if she wishes to improve the ring. This is enough to kill her outright at least three times, even at the level cap of 255.
