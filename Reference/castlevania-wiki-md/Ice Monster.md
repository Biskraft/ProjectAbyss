# Ice Monster

The **Ice Monster** is an enemy in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*.

## Description
The Ice Monster is an entity that uses gelid water as medium in order to take shape. They are only encountered in the Tower of Sorcery.

These crystal jellies can collapse into puddles of water on the ground in order to avoid attacks, and then rise again, forming a humanoid shape, and spew streams of cold water at the player.

Ice Monsters in *Legacy of Darkness* are slightly more as resilient than those encountered in *Castlevania* (N64).
