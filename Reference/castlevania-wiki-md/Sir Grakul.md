# Sir Grakul

- Sir Grakul** is a boss in *Super Castlevania IV*. He is a powerful knight, skilled in the use of medieval weaponry.

## Description
Once reaching the end of the Unliving Quarters, Simon Belmont comes across a suit of armor on display inside a decorated crystal case. An earthquake suddenly takes place and the armor comes to life, breaking out of its crystal cage and revealing itself as the menacing knight, Sir Grakul.

The knight attacks by throwing his battle axe at the player, which will return back to his hand in a boomerang-like manner, and also by smashing it on the ground, generating two flames that will traverse across the floor, one on each direction. When three fourths of his life bar have been depleted, his battle axe will break apart and he'll then draw out his sword to continue the battle.
