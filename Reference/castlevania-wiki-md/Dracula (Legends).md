# Dracula (Legends)

- Dracula** (real name **Dracula Vlad Ţepeş**) is the main antagonist of *Castlevania Legends*. He is the master of his own demonic castle. He holds great magic power and rules the Dark World as Prince of Darkness.

## Story
In 1431, Dracula made a pact with an evil deity who granted him his dark powers. He then used his newly gained powers to conquer the nations of Europe. His son Alucard did not support these actions and abandoned him.
