# Gold Heal

right


  - Gold Heal** is an Innocent Devil ability in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It can be learned by the Tiramisu evolution of the Fairy-Type. This is the most potent restorative spell in the game, capable of healing Hector to maximum health, which is similar to using an XS Potion. The only consequence is that the player needs to have an expensive amount of 5,000 Gold, along with 6 Hearts to pay the fairy in exchange for the heal.

Since the consequence is high enough, it is advised to use this ability in dire situations and only when necessary. This is especially the case when money is no longer an issue in the later parts of the game and the grinding would be focused on either the Tower of Evermore, or specifically the Boss Rush Mode.
