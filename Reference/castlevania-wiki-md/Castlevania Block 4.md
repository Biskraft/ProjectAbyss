Block 4 is the fourth stage in *Castlevania*.

## Overview
Simon ends up in this stage after a really long fall down an abyss. He has to cross waters using the floating platforms, while Bats and Fish Men try to knock him off. The cave ceiling will obstruct process, which dips low in some areas, requiring to crouch in order not to get knocked off the platform. After ascending from the caves to an outside part of the castle, Eagles dropping Hunchbacks will attack. A Skele-Dragon is the last obstacle before the bosses of the stage, Frankenstein and his hunchback comrade Igor.
