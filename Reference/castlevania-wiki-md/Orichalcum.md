# Orichalcum

- Orichalcum** is a material in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is the ultimate metal, created on a phantom continent. It shines with a red light.

## Availability
- Stolen from Harpies. Hector must allow himself to be grabbed by a Harpy, break free, and immediately jump and steal to get it.
- One can be found in Aiolon Ruins.
