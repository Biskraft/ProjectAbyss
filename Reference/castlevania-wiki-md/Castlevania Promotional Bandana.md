# Castlevania Promotional Bandana

thumb|The red bandana
Castlevania (Lament of Innocence) **bandanas** were given out at E3 conventions, hence became limited gems for collectors.

There are known to exist two bandanas; a red and a yellow one. The red can frequently been seen in collector's sources, whilst the yellow one is considered extremely rare.
Category: Lament of Innocence
