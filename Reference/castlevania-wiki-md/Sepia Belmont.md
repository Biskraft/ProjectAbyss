 is a character in the *Bomberman* spin-off game, *Bombergirl*. She's a descendant of the Belmont Clan. The character was teased in a series of tweets beginning on December 13, 2018, before being ultimately revealed on December 18th. She became playable during the "Evangelist of Love Advent Gacha" event that began on December 27, 2018. She's voiced by Satomi Satō, the same voice actress for Gesshi Hanafuuma.

## Background
Sepia possesses the Vampire Killer in chain whip form and a Cross Chainsaw that she uses on her opponents.

According to the game's official website and a tweet from the official *Bombergirl* account on Twitter, the following is Sepia's profile:


  - Hobby**: "Gardening. You can learn a lot by raising something."

  - Likes**: "Love! Truly, love is the most precious thing in the world!"

  - Dislikes**: "Cockroaches and insects like that, only a little..."

  - Birthday**: June 12

  - Brand**: , a mature brand with a calm impression, full of girls who have a divine (angelic or godly) motif.
