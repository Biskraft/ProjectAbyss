# Dracula Wraith

The **Dracula Wraith** is a being born of Dracula's remains and the evil soul that was created inside of Maxim Kischine after he came in contact with them. He is the real final boss of *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. He is not actually Dracula himself (he indicates that he has never felt the power of a Belmont before).

## Description
Dracula Wraith's initial form is similar to Dracula's and attacks like him as well. He will teleport to a random location, unleash an attack, and then quickly vanish. He has only two offensive options: in the first and most frequent, he will open his cape and release his trusty three-directional fireballs, which Juste can easily leap over and in the process score a headshot; on the second, he will expel seven flaming spheres, one after another, that after release, they will rev up and blast toward Juste.

His second form is a giant amalgamated version of all of Dracula's remains.

## Maxim Mode
In Maxim Mode, the player fights only the second form of the Wraith, though they can battle the first in Boss Rush Mode. Maxim can abuse a serious exploit against the Wraith's second form: by using his somersault attack, Maxim can move through the Wraith without much trouble. As the Wraith was not programmed to turn around in such an occasion, it can be easily defeated, as it does not attack except with its randomly moving tentacle.
