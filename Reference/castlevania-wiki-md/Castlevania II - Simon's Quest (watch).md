A Tiger Electronics' watch version of *Castlevania II: Simon's Quest*. The gameplay barely resembles the original from *Simon's Quest*, so it could be considered a separate game. It was preceded by a more in-depth version in LCD handheld format.

## Story
Simon Belmont must destroy Dracula in order to lift his curse. Before he can do so, he must defeat hordes of Dracula's servant monsters.

## Arsenal
- Whip - The main weapon in the game. It can be struck in front of Simon, or he can turn around quickly and whip behind him. It can also be struck in front or behind while jumping.
- Crystal Ball - This restores all of your life and can be obtained by whipping it.
- Silver Knife - The box mentions that this knife must be obtained in order to defeat the curse. It is not clear how it is obtained or how it is utilized.

## Stages
- Stage 1 - Contains Bats, 5 Skeletons, Fire Ghosts, Monster Fishes and Flying Monsters.
- Stage 2 - Like Stage 1, except that it contains 10 of each enemy.
- Stage 3 - Like Stage 1, except that it contains 15 of each enemy. Skeletons are now equipped with shields and Monster Fishes have been replaced with Monster Dragons.
- Stage 4 - Like Stage 3, except that it contains 20 of each enemy and Dracula appears at the end.
