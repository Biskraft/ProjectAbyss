# Crystal Cavern

right|350px

The **Crystal Cavern** is a stage in *Castlevania Judgment*. Multicolored crystals can be shattered for items. In the background, one can see aqueducts.

## Notes
- This stage does not feature any stage hazard, not even RING OUT despite its appearance to do so.
