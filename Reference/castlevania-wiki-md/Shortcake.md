# Shortcake

70px|right

A **Shortcake** is a food item that recovers health in the *Castlevania* series. Its first appearance was as a food item for Maria in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood* and later in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*, and is a smaller portion of a Birthday Cake.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, it gives you 11 HP, which is the smallest amount for any dessert, giving you one less point than a Tart. It gives you one more HP than a Chinese Bun, which gives the lowest food value for any Chinese bun. It is dropped by a Salem Witch.

The Shortcake is also found in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* (in the Anti-Soul Mysteries Lab) and in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* (dropped by a Wyvern), and is the only kind of pastry found in these games.
