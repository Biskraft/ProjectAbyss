# Bloody Glove

The **Bloody Glove** is a glove in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is a glove soaked with much blood.

It is the only type of glove that can be purchased from the Merchant (2,100 gold). It gives DEF +25, which is the second highest for any glove, being more than the Arm Plates (+21), but less than the Steel Glove (+28).
