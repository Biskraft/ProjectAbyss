# Lords of Shadow Family Tree

The **Lords of Shadow Family Tree** contains all Belmont relatives in the *Lords of Shadow* saga, from the three games.

This family was started with Gabriel Belmont, and, so far, ends with Victor Belmont, who claims to be the last of his bloodline. Other members of the family, if they exist, are currently unknown (Gabriel's parents, Marie's parents, Sypha's parents, and Simon's descendants).

This family tree only contains characters from the same bloodline. Other characters, like Laura, who could be considered as a sort of 'mother' to Gabriel (as he became a vampire because of her), should not be added to this tree.

<small>***Legend***:</small>
- <small>Solid lines denote blood parent-child relationship</small>
- <small>Dashed horizontal lines denote engagement relationships</small>
- <small>Dashed vertical lines denote the rest of the family tree, that contains unknown members of the family</small>
- <small>A cross denotes a deceased character, who died before, during, or at the end of the events of the game(s) they appear in</small>
