The **Necromancer's Abyss** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*. It is the first stage of Chapter 11.

## Zobek's journal
:*And so it begins, the Necromancer’s abyss lies at his feet. He is dark and terrible and the cries of the dead follow him wherever he goes. There is nothing but hate in his heart. Hate for those who have taken his beloved. Who have made him do terrible things. Who have taken his humanity. Now reap the whirlwind, scions of death, for comes the Prince of Darkness.*
