# Gateau Fraise

A strawberry shortcake is called a **Gateau Fraise** in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*. It can be obtained by dropping a $1,000 gold piece into the register at the City of Haze bakery, which is the hardest cake to obtain there, being harder to get than a Tarte au Poire ($500). It can also be found at 13th Street.

Wearing one or two Gold Rings while attempting to drop the coin into the register will increase the odds of spawning a $1,000 money bag.
