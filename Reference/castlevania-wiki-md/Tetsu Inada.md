# Tetsu Inada

is a Japanese voice actor affiliated with Aoni Production.

He voiced Graham Jones in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, Julius Belmont in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and Hammer in the Japanese version of *Dawn of Sorrow*.
