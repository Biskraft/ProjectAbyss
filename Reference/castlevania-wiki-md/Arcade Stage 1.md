# Arcade Stage 1

The first stage of *Akumajō Dracula: The Arcade* consists of the Ruins and leads up to the bridge to Dracula's Castle, which is guarded by Death. The enemies in this stage are mostly Skeleton Soldiers and Skeleton Archers, along with some Bats. Death also attacks by raising Zombies from the ground. After Death is defeated, he exits by disappearing into the ground, allowing the hero to cross the bridge to the Castle. Blood Relations of Heaven and Earth, a remix of Richter's theme, plays during this level. Death's theme is Vampire Killer.
