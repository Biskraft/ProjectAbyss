# Servant Corpse

right|200px

  - Servant Corpses **are undead summoned by Zobek during his battle with Dracula in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. They also appear in two Kleidos missions.

When in the Basilica, Zobek awakens statues that come alive and howl, rising zombies from the ground. Using the Chaos Bombs will destroy them faster. When all statues are gone, the remaining servant corpses on the ground will die. These corpses are fragile, even with the Shadow Whip. However, their common attack is to grab and bite Dracula. At half-health, their lower bodies will be destroyed, however, they can still move and attack.
