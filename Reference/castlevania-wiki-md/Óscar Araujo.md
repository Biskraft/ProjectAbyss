  - Óscar Araujo** is a Spanish composer. He worked on the soundtrack for MercurySteam's reboot of the *Castlevania* franchise, starting with *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.

## Gameography
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2* - (Konami, 2013)
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate* - (Konami, 2013)
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* - (Konami, 2010)
  - Scrapland* (Enlight, 2004)
  - Pro Rally 2002* (Ubisoft, 2002)
  - Severance: Blade of Darkness* (Codemasters, 2001)
