# Boku Dracula-kun Stage 7

Seventh stage of *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun* for the Famicom is the **Mysterious Realm of Galamoth**. It consists of a space elevator that stretches from the ground in Egypt to a space platform, where you first face Galamoth.
