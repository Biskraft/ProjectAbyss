# Iron Plate

The **Iron Plate** (usually called the **Iron Cuirass** or **Iron Plate**) is an breastplate made of iron. It is usually a step up from a Bronze Armor, but a step down from the Steel Armor.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, the Iron Cuirass gives a Defense boost of +4, which is one more than the Bronze Cuirass, but one less than the Steel Cuirass.

In *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, the Iron Plate gives +25 Defense, which is 7 more than the Copper Plate (and 2 more than the Army Jacket) and 6 less than the Steel Plate.

In *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, the Iron Plate gives +15 Defense, which is 6 more than a Copper Plate, but 3 less than a Silver Plate.

In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, the Iron Plate is said to be made of Steel, so there is no separate Steel Plate in this game. It grants +18 Defense, which is 10 more than the Copper Plate and 10 less than the Silver Plate. It can be purchased at Jacob's Shop once Eugen's quest *"Poor Preparation is Costly"* quest is completed, which requires giving him three Iron Ores.
