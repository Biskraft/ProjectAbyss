# Discus

right|150px

  - Discus** (also called **Death Saucer**) is a circular bladed weapon used by Disc Armors. They are usually attached to a string, thrown out swirling, and then they roll on a surface; sometimes they return and sometimes they are released down slopes.

Soma Cruz is able to utilize this weapon when he captures a Disc Armor's soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*. When he releases it, it is thrown out in front and returns back to him in a circular pattern.

Jonathan Morris is able to use the Discus as a sub-weapon in *Portrait of Ruin* and *Harmony of Despair*.
