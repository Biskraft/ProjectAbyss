# Vick13

- Vick13** is a hidden item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

## Background
Main protagonist in Konami's puzzle game *King's Valley II*. He appears as a collectible hidden item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. Found in the DLC level Chapter 7: *Beauty, Desire, Situation Dire*. The player must make their way to the top of the pyramid and jump for Vick13 to appear. Gives "additional points" to score.
