# New International Track &amp; Field

- New International Track & Field*** is a track and field-themed game developed by Sumo Digital and published by Konami for the Nintendo DS handheld video game console. It is the seventh installment in the *Track & Field* series.

With the release of *New International Track & Field* in the middle of 2008, the *Track & Field* series celebrated its 25th anniversary since its first appearance in 1983.

This edition features various Konami mascot characters, including Simon Belmont, Dracula and Kid Dracula from the ''Castlevania series.

## Other famous Konami characters
- Sparkster from the *Rocket Knight* series.
- Pyramid Head from the *Silent Hill* series.
- Evil Rose from the *Rumble Roses* series.
- Frogger from the *Frogger* series.
- Pentaro from the *Parodius* series.
- Solid Snake from the *Metal Gear* series.
- Power Pro-Kun from the *Power Pro* series.
