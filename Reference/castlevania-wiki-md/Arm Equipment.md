# Arm Equipment

Nathan Graves can equip one item onto each of his arms in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. This is similar to the way most games allow the player to equip two accessories.

The concept of equipping two items, one on each arm, also appears in *Castlevania Judgment*, although all accessories in this game are merely for cosmetic purposes and have no in-game effects.
