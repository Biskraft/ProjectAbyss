# Projectile Circle

A **Projectile Circle** is a projectile of some kind that orbits its user in a circle, which deals damage to enemies and projectiles it comes into contact with.  

There are four kinds of Projectile Circles that have appeared in *Castlevania* games:

- Fire Circle
- Ice Circle
- Stone Circle
- Fidelis Medusa
