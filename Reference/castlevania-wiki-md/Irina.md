# Irina

is a supporting character in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. She is one of Wygol's villagers and a distant descendant of the Belmont Clan. She is Anna's and Serge's mother.

## Description
Because of her concern for her kids, Serge and Anna, Irina will give Shanoa three missions in which she must kill off enemies for her. She will not, however, give missions until Shanoa gets the whole family back together, and this also applies to both of her children.
