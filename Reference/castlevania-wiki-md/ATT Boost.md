# ATT Boost

right|73px
  - ATT Boost** is a Stat Boost spell or power that increases Attack. See also STR Boost.

In *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, the **Black Bishop** is a relic, that raises the player attacks temporarily (+15). This found behind the yellow dragon door in House of Sacred Remains, 1F.
