# The Countess

- The Countess** is a character that was intended to appear as the main antagonist in the canceled Sega Dreamcast game *Castlevania: Resurrection*. She opens a portal to resurrect Count Dracula, and both would have surely made a formidable team against Sonia Belmont and her descendant, Victor.

## Notes
- The "Countess" is also referred to as the "Female Dracula" in some artwork.
- Some speculate she is actually Countess Elizabeth Bartley. However, the Countess is described as Dracula's lover instead of his niece.
