# Talon Rod

right|200px**Talon Rod** is a Mage-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It's a level 2 Innocent Devil and can learn Circle Scissors and Freeze. It can be obtained from a Wood Rod Mage, using blue, green or white crystals.
