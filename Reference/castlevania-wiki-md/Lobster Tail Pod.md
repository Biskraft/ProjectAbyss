# Lobster Tail Pod

The **Lobster Tail Pod** (more accurately called a Lobster Tail Pot) is a helmet found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It is created by adding White Steel to a Frog Mouth Helm, increasing its Defense from 12 to 18. It has a greater defense than the Sallet (DEF +14) or the Barrel Helm (DEF +16). However, it cannot be further evolved and will be outclassed when a Sallet continues to evolve.
