# Honey Bee

right|200px**Honey Bee** is the fourth Fairy-Type Innocent Devil evolution on the Fairy-Type evolutionary development chart, but is not always necessarily the fourth evolution. It evolves from the Leaffle for 70 Axe/Knuckle/Special Evo Crystals, and thus, retains all of her abilities. Since the Infant Fairy is always the first evolution, it also retains her abilities, as well as one new ability: Healing Field, which heals for approximately 80-100 HP for about 8-10 seconds, making it more useful than the first level Heal and Time Heal. The move, however, should only be used when one has just cleared the room and is in no danger, for quick and effective healing. It evolves into Tiramisu or Proboscis Fairy.
