# Hideo Kojima

- Hideo Kojima** is a video game designer, writer, director and producer. He is known for being the creator of Konami's *Metal Gear* series. He played an advisory role in the making of *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow* in addition to providing the voice of the Chupacabras for the Japanese version of the game. Kojima left Konami in 2016.
