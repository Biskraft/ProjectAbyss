# Fried Crab Claw

thumb|150px|<p style="text-align: center;">*Harmony of Despair* gamerpic.</p>
Fried Crab Claw is a food item that recovers HP in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It is a two-star item that gives the strongest amount of CON boost (+10), along with its decent amount of HP (+70).

This is better than one-star Canelé, which only gives CON +2 and HP +15.  It gives the same amount of CON as the three star Ramen, which also provides higher HP (+100) and STR +10.
