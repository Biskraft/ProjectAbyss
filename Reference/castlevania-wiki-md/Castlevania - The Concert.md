# Castlevania: The Concert

right|300px

  - Castlevania: The Concert** was a musical event held at the Stockholm Concert Hall on January 19, 2010, performed by the Youth Stockholm Philharmonic Orchestra and conducted by David Westerlung. It also had Michiru Yamane as a guest performer.

## Program
The event was divided into two acts, each lasting about 60 minutes, and a short recess between them. Each section consisted of the following pieces:

### Act 1
#Introduction (*Legacy of Darkness*)
#Dracula's Castle (*Symphony of the Night*)
#Nocturne in the Moonlight (*Symphony of the Night*)
#Theme of Simon Belmont (*Super Castlevania IV*)
#Prayer (*Symphony of the Night*)
#Lost Painting (*Symphony of the Night*)
#Entrance Hall (*Super Castlevania IV*)
#Chandeliers (*Super Castlevania IV*)
#New Messiah (*Belmont's Revenge*)
#Praying Hands (*Belmont's Revenge*)
#The Sinking Old Sanctuary (*Bloodlines*)
#Calling from Heaven (*Bloodlines*)
#Wood Carving Partita (*Symphony of the Night*)
#Old School Medley (*Castlevania I*-*III*)
- Vampire Killer
- Bloody Tears
- Beginning
