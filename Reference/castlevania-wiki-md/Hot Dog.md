# Hot Dog

right|73px

  - Hot Dog** (**Frankfurter** in *Symphony of the Night* and *Encore of the Night*) is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It recovers 19 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is the lowest recovering fast food entree, being one less than a Hamburger, but one more than Cheese.

It is dropped by a Flying Zombie in *Symphony of the Night*. When called a "Hot Dog", it is dropped by hog or dog like enemies, such as the Hell Boar in *Dawn of Sorrow*, Glasya Labolas in *Portrait of Ruin*, and Fenrir in *Curse of Darkness*. It can also be found in the Long Library and Castle Keep in *Symphony of the Night*.

In *Harmony of Despair*, it is a common item that grants +18 HP and +3 CON. This is better than a Canelé or a Hamburger (each +15 HP, +2 CON). It is inferior to Fried Chicken, which grants the same amount of HP, but an additional 2 CON.
