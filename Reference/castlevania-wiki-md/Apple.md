# Apple

- Apple** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It recovers 9 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is the second highest recovering fruit, giving one more than a Pineapple and one less than a Strawberry. It is dropped by a Harpy.

Another kind of apple called the Life Apple can be used by a familiar to restore Alucard to life once all his hit points have been lost.
