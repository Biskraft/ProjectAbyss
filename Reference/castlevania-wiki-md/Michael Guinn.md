# Michael Guinn

- Michael Guinn** (credited as **Michael G.**) was the original English language voice actor for Dracula, the Master Librarian and the Ferryman in the PlayStation version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. He was replaced in his role of Dracula by Douglas Rye, Patrick Seitz and Kyle Hebert in later titles.

Guinn also voiced the lead character Harry Mason in the Konami classic survival horror game *Silent Hill*.

## Background
Michael got his start in voice acting with the 1993 video game *Ridge Racer*, in which he voiced the announcer. He then went on to appear in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, *Time Crisis* and *Cross*. In 1999, Michael performed the voice acting for Harry Mason in *Silent Hill*.

## Trivia
- His name was credited simply as "Michael G.", which for some time caused a lot of mystery and confusion regarding his identity. Some fans theorized Michael G was Michael Gough, but Gough denied it.
