# Diamond Shoes

right|50px

  - Diamond Shoes** are shoes made of Diamond in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

In *Portrait of Ruin*, they are the foot component of Charlotte's Diamond Outfit, which maximizes Charlotte Aulin's Defense (the body armor is a Diamond Corset, the head item is a Diamond Tiara, and the Diamond Brooch is an accessory). Like the other items, it gives more Defense (+6) than any other Charlotte-exclusive item for its equipment slot.
