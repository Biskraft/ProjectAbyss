# Harmony of Despair Stage 6

- Come, Sweet Hour of Death** is the sixth and final original chapter in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It is an amalgamation of a majority of the castle areas from Order of Ecclesia; the Castle Entrance, the Library, and the Final Approach. In addition, the only location outside of Dracula's Castle to featured in this combination was Ecclesia itself. The default boss themes used in the battle against Dracula are Order of the Demon and Dance of Illusions.

### Hidden item
Konami Man makes an appearance as a hidden item in this chapter. At the bottom of the stairs, right before the room leading to Dracula's chamber, jump to the right and get to the roof. Press up and Konami Man will zoom past the screen from the right. Jump to grab him.
