# Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2 Original Game Soundtrack

- Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2 Original Game Soundtrack** is the soundtrack composed by Oscar Araujo and Ferrán Cruixent for the PS3/Xbox 360/PC game, *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2*. It was released on February 25, 2014, along with the game.

On November 16, 2014, the score won the "Best Video Game Soundtrack" award from the Annual Film Soundtrack Center.
