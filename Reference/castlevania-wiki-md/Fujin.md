# Fujin

right

  - Fujin** is an enemy in *Akumajō Special: Boku Dracula-kun*. He is a Japanese god that attacks by hurling wind. He is depicted as an old man, as opposed to the young Raijin.

Fujin hurls wind at the player (while Raijin throws lightning bolts), attempting to knock them off his platform.
