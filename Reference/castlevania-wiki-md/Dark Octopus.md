# Dark Octopus

The **Dark Octopus** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. It is an octopus creature, tormented by black magic.

## Description
Dark Octopi live in damp areas and are often encountered in large swarms. They move rather slowly along the floor and attack by emitting clouds of black ink. The combined clouds emitted by several of these creatures can effectively conceal them from sight, making it difficult to measure at which distance the player is from their tentacles and potentially allowing them to inflict contact damage if not careful.

Dark Octopi can only be found in the depths of the Kalidus Channel. They drop Black Ink, which is one of the items required in order to complete George's "Artists Can Be Selfish" quest, the other being an Eagle Feather.
