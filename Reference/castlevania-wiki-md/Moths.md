# Moths

right|150px

Moth- and butterfly-like creatures have appeared in several *Castlevania* games. They usually hover in the air, sprinkling poisonous dust on the ground below.

Moths first appeared in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*, but not as enemies. Instead, they cluster around lanterns. As these lanterns are destroyed in order to gain their contents (such as Hearts), they move to harass the one who destroyed them or move to the next light source. They are only a nuisance, though, and do not do any actual damage.

The first moth enemy was the Princess of Moss, a mistranslation of "Princess of Moths" or "Moth Princess". She's the boss encountered in *Castlevania: Bloodlines* at the Versailles Palace. She resembles Marie Antoinette, although attacks by Eric Lecarde or John Morris reveal her true and much less pleasant form, a giant moth. When she first transforms, poison dust falls to the ground, but for much of the battle, she just hovers around in the air.

One of the forms of Shadow in *Harmony of Dissonance* is a giant moth or butterfly that will sprinkle poison dust on the player.

A giant moth called King Moth appears in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*, which again, sprinkles poison dust on the player.

## Bitterflies
*Main article: Bitterfly*

Enemies called Bitterflies have a moth-like appearance, but are actually human heads with flapping ears called Chonchons.
