# Power Belt

The **Power Belt** is a belt in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* that improves combat statistics, but emphasizes defense (+3 DEF) over offense (+2 STR). It replaces the Ancient Belt from *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* in this capacity. It is somewhat weaker than its offensive counterpart, the Black Belt (+5 STR, +2 DEF).
