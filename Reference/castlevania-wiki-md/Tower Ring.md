# Tower Ring

The **Tower Ring** is an accessory in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a ring that represents the Tarot card "The Tower". It makes it less likely that Shanoa will be knocked back when hit by an enemy; however, it reduces all stats by 10 because she absorbs more of the impact (STR, DEF, INT, MND, CON and LCK).

It can be obtained as soon as the player acquires the Ordinary Rock at the end of the Skeleton Cave (and with it, the ability to double jump). The ring can be found inside a treasure chest in a room at the top of the central tower of Minera Prison Island, where Aeon is also found.
