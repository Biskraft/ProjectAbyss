 is a party mobile game released for the EZ service on May 1, 2000 in Japan. It was developed and published by Konami Mobile Japan, having an initial monthly fee of ¥300.

It contains images, melodies and minigames from several Konami franchises, including *Castlevania*.

## Overview
In *Konami Characore World*, users can win virtual coins via various minigames featuring characters from popular Konami franchises. These coins in turn allow the user to download wallpapers, ringtones and screensavers for their mobile devices.
