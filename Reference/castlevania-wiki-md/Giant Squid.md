# Giant Squid

thumb|300px|<p style="text-align: center;">Giant Squid from *[[Castlevania Judgment*.</p>]]

In *Castlevania Judgment*, a giant squid's tentacle occasionally harasses combatants on the Ghost Ship level. When it appears, it slams the ground. If a character is hit by this slam attack, they will be sent out flying, or in the worst case, be drawn by the tentacle to the water, causing an instant death, known in-game as RING OUT.
