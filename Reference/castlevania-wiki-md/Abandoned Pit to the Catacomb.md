# Abandoned Pit to the Catacomb

The , known as the **Abandoned Mine** in most English versions, is a location in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.

## Overview
There is very little evidence of this place being a mine (and even less of it being abandoned), save for a few vertical chambers which display doors in the back which may be used to access the deeper and more rewarding regions of the environment. Rocky, mossy, darkened corridors make up the majority of this transition area.

## Geography
The Abandoned Pit to the Catacomb is first reached after destroying a wooden floor in the Underground Caverns using an Ape Skeleton's exploding barrel, which appears after a switch is hit in the room across from the top of the waterfall, which requires the Leap Stone. The pit itself is a very short level which travels downward. It can be exited early to the right to enter a Teleport Room. Continuing downward leads to a room where the boss Cerberus is fought, which appears to be the entrance to Hades. Beyond this area are the Catacombs.

The corresponding level to the Abandoned Pit to the Catacomb in the Reverse Castle is the Cave.
