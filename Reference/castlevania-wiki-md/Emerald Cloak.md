# Emerald Cloak

The **Emerald Cloak** is an Emerald-green cloak in Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance. It gives more Defense than any other cloak that only increases Defense. It gives +5, which is higher than a Velvet Cloak (+3), but less than a Wind Cloak (resistance to Wind) or a Crimson Cloak (+3 Luck Boost), both which have a Defense boost of 12.
