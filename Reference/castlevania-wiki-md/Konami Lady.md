# Konami Lady

is the female counterpart to Konami Man and one of Konami's most renowned mascots. While she has not appeared in any *Castlevania* games to date, she is present in some crossover games and other media that include *Castlevania* characters.

## Background
Konami Lady is an android created by Dr. Cinnamon (from the *TwinBee* series). She appears as a playable character in *Konami Wai Wai World*, where her main form of attack is by kicking. This style of fighting is presumably the basis for Maria Renard's kicks in the Sega Saturn version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.

In Konami's *Wai Wai World* game book, she sets off to Dracula's Castle to rescue Simon Belmont.

Laura, an enemy introduced in *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*, at one point during her battle sheds off the long dress she wears and reveals an apparel underneath that strikingly resembles that of Konami Lady, consisting on a one-piece black suit and long boots, and featuring a pair of cat-like ears that would make for Konami Lady's wing ornaments on her head.

Konami Lady also appears as a professor at St. Gradius Academy (where both Kokoro Belmont and Gesshi Hanafuuma attend to classes) in *Otomedius Excellent*, and fights the students as a boss in the training stage.
