# Paries

right|100px

  - Paries** is a back glyph in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* that allows Shanoa to travel though walls at glowing wall portals. This glyph comes from the boss Wallman, which can be fought in the Library section of Dracula's Castle. Shanoa must start stealing this glyph as soon as the boss uses it, or else he may slip away.

The Glyph does not consume MP over time (only 5 MP for activation), so it's actually fine to activate it most of the time. When near a portal found in specific solid walls, the player must press the **R** button to transform Shanoa into a form similar to Alucard's mist. Shanoa is then free to traverse the wall using the directional pad while looking for an exit portal.
