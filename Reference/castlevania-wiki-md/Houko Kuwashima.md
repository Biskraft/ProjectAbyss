# Houko Kuwashima

is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is currently affiliated with Aoni Production.

Houko Kuwashima is famous for her fans as Yurika Misumaru in the *Martian Successor Nadesico* series, Sango in the *InuYasha* series and Kagura in *Azumanga Daioh*.

Kuwashima voiced Shanoa; the heroine of *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* who appeared in *Castlevania Judgment* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. She also supplied the voice of Carmilla from *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow*.
