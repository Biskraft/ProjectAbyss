# Poison Stinger

right|250px

  - Poison Stinger** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*. It can be obtaining by gaining dominance over a Mushussu's soul. It allows Soma to grow a poisonous tail with which to attack enemies, hitting in conjunction with his current weapon. The damage dealt is higher than that of the normal Stinger and each hit has a slight chance to poison enemies.
