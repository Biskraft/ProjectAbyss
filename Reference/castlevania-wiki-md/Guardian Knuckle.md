# Guardian Knuckle

right|160px
The  is a special attack performed by Maria Renard.

## Games specific information
### *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*
The Guardian Knuckle is a hidden attack available when playing as Maria. It is performed by entering **Up, Down-Right + II** when facing right. The punches are performed by the summoned guardian.

### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
In the Saturn edition, Maria performs the attack herself. It is called Guardians Knuckle.

In *The Dracula X Chronicles* version of *Symphony of the Night*, the attack can be performed when possessing the Doll sub-weapon. It can be item-crashed.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Maria summons a guardian that attacks with a flurry of punches, restoring HP on impact. It can be used against breakable objects for the same effect.

### *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*
The attack is the same as the *Rondo of Blood* version.

### *Castlevania Judgment*
Guard Attack is an unblockable attack, performed by entering **L.Trigger + B**.
