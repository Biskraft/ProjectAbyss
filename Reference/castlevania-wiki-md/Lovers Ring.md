# Lovers Ring

The **Lovers Ring** is the ring of the Tarot persona, The Lovers, in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. While equipped, it doubles the value of Hearts collected by Shanoa, in addition to a token Defense boost of 2. The boost appears to be multiplicative; wearing two Lovers Rings will give Shanoa 4 hearts for a small heart and 20 hearts for a large one. It replaces the Heart Choker that appeared in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, which increased the chances of finding Big Hearts. This same ability could be obtained using a Venus + Manticore DSS Combination in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.
