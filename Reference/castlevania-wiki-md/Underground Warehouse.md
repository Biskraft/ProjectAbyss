# Underground Warehouse

The **Underground Warehouse** is a location in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*. It is a storage area filled with box puzzles. This stage is a basement-like area featuring more Plant and Earth-based enemies. It's also worth mentioning the rest of the enemies' roster is given a major upgrade, introducing more powerful versions of former creatures.

## Overview
This stage consists on a series of ramps and big vertical rooms, as well as a huge library at its center. Death is the boss of this area, and contrary to most games in the series, any close acquaintance between him and Dracula is never mentioned in the game, other than being just his subordinate. Once Nathan defeats him, he may then obtain the Cleansing necklace from the next room.
