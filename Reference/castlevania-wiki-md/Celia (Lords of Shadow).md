# Celia (Lords of Shadow)

- Celia** is the true love of one of the knights of the Brotherhood of Light. Her knight was killed when he couldn't say no to the hypnotic voices echoing through the castle, and awakening the uncontrollable carnal lust within him, fogging his mind, clouding his will, and inviting him to take part in some forbidden game. He asked Celia's forgiveness for not being able to control his actions.
