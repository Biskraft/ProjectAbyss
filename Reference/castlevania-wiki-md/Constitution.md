# Constitution

- Constitution**, generally abbreviated as **CON**, is a statistic found in *Castlevania*. Most often, it is the character's "natural" defense, and their Constitution plus their armor's strength composes the Defense stat proper.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*
Constitution (CON) first made its debut with the RPG mechanics introduced in *Symphony of the Night*, along with Strength, Intelligence, Luck, Defense, and Attack. Ever since, it has been more or less a staple in the metroidvania-style games. It only improves for Alucard, though. As such, Richter Belmont and Maria Renard are more vulnerable in the facet of defense since they do not level up by statistics.

For every 10 points added in Alucard's base CON, he gains +1 DEF. However, for every 2.5 points of CON from equipment on average, he also gains +1 DEF.

CON also governs the duration of the Poison and Curse status effects: as CON increases, the effect time decreases.

### *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*
The stat is unseen in *Circle of the Moon* and *Harmony of Dissonance*, although DEF replaced it for those games.

### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
In *Aria of Sorrow* and the DS games (*Dawn of Sorrow*, *Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia*), for every 2 points of CON earned, the character gains +1 DEF.
