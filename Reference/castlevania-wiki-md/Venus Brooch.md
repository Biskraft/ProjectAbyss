# Venus Brooch

right|50px

The **Venus Brooch** is a brooch blessed by Venus, goddess of love and beauty.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
The Venus Brooch is the most powerful of Charlotte's brooches. In addition to the standard DEF +3 boost (which the Abalone Brooch also has), it also increases other stats by +3, including INT, MND and LCK.
