# Sam Riegel

- Sam Riegel** is an American voice actor and director. He performed the voice of Wallman and Blackmore, as well as the additional voices in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*.

Sam works regularly in several anime and video games as Yuri Lowenthal. His most known roles are probably Shirou Emiya in *Fate/Stay Night*. He also voiced Donatello in *Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles* and Phoenix Wright in both *Ultimate Marvel VS. Capcom 3* and *Phoenix Wright Ace Attorney: Dual Destines*.
