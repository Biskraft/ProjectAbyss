# Masaharu Satō

(born July 1st, 1946) is a Japanese voice actor currently working for Aoni Production. He voiced Death throughout various installments of the *Castlevania* series.

## Career
Satō is best known for the roles of Buffaloman, Sunshine (*Kinnikuman*), Miyamoto Musashi (*Yaiba*), Adah (*Mushiking: King of the Beetles*), and Dora-med III (*The Doraemons*). He is also known for playing numerous roles in the *Kinnikuman*, *Dr. Slump and Arale-chan*, *Fist of the North Star*, and the *Dragon Ball* series.

He gave voice to Death throughout various installments of the *Castlevania* series in Japan, as well as the Master Librarian and Ferryman in *Dracula X: Nocturne in the Moonlight*.
