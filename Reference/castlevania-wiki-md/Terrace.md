# Terrace

The **Terrace** is the twelfth area Simon explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*, and the seventh area Alucard explores in his journey. During Alucard's journey, it is here that the Reaver is encountered. The Reaver first makes a full appearance by crashing down on top of the elevator trying to eat Alucard, after the battle it is shown that a huge hole as been made leading to the clock room as if it dug it's way through area to area. During the battle, Alucard pulls a object on the ceiling that releases a acid-like liquid and injures the Reaver. Before Alucard first came to this area, Simon entered this area through a hole in front of where Alucard comes in. There is no possible explanation for how the hole was quickly repaired/covered up. Eventually, if Alucard successfully taken the elevator, he would have been following Simon's trail.
