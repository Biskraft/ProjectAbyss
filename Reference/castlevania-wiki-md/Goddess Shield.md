# Goddess Shield

The **Goddess Shield** is a shield engraved with a divine image. It has appeared in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, *Encore of the Night* and *Harmony of Despair*, and summons a goddess who provides protection from Dark attacks when the Shield Rod spell is used.
