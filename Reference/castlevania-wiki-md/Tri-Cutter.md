# Tri-Cutter

right|300px
  - Tri-Cutter** is a blade vortex bullet soul ability which Soma Cruz can perform in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be stolen from a Tiny Devil and fires three blades which can change their trajectory slightly to follow enemies. Due to a trajectory bug, targeting upper-left enemies will change the blades' trajectory straight to the left.
