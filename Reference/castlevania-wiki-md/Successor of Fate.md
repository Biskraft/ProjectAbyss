# Successor of Fate

- Successor of Fate** is a music theme composed by Soshiro Hokkai for *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is Juste Belmont's theme and is considered to be the main theme of the game. It plays as the castle Entrance background theme and an arranged version plays during the game's credits.
