# Force Field

right
A **Force Field** surrounds Shaft during his battle with Alucard in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. In *Aria of Sorrow*, Soma can obtain the ability to generate a protective barrier by gaining control of the Giant Ghost's guardian soul and deflect enemy projectiles. In *Encore of the Night*, this is a spell which offers Alucard protection against his opponent's spells and abilities.
