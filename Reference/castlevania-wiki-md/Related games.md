# Related games

Games that are based or related to *Castlevania* which might not actually have any direct references in them.

  - Shaman King: Master of Spirits* and *Shaman King: Master of Spirits 2*- Game adaptation done by Konami for the Shaman King comic. Done in the style of the Game Boy Advance Castlevania games. While the enemies tend to be from Japanese mythology, the skeleton enemies can be recognized from Castlevania games, including a bomber skeleton and an archer skeleton. Some of the music has Castlevania-like melodies in them as well.
  - Nano Breaker* - A PlayStation 2 game created by Castlevania producer IGA. It uses the same engine as *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and was used in part to give the Castlevania team more experience with 3D games before the release of their next 3D project, *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*.
  - Rusty* - A Castlevania-like game that was not produced by Konami that seems to be related to *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*.
  - Bloodstained: Ritual of the Night* - A project started by IGA backed in Kickstarter and estimated to be released on March 2017.
