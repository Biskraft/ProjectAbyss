# Castle Hall (Mirror of Fate)

The **Castle Hall** is a location in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. It is the fifth area Simon Belmont explores, with the Night Watchman acting as the boss.

It was explored previously by Trevor Belmont and appears as the eighth area in his journey. It is here that he first speaks with the Lost Soul and shatters its face.
