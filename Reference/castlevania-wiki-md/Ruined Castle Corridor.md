# Ruined Castle Corridor

- Ruined Castle Corridor**, or simply **Castle Corridor**, is a music theme originally composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It is the background theme for the Castle Corridor, as well as the Clock Tower in Julius Mode. It is Soma Cruz's theme in both *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

Although the track makes no appearance in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, an arranged version named "**The Cross of Fate**" was featured in its official soundtrack because fans requested one for *Aria of Sorrow*, which was released a year later with themes from *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.
