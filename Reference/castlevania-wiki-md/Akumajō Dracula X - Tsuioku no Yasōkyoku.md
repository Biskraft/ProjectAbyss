 (unofficial translation, sometimes translated as *Nocturne of Reminiscence*) is a Japanese-language-only internet radio drama taking place shortly after the events of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.

It was originally a feature on the *Akumajō Dracula: Radio Chronicle* program that was broadcast on Konami Station internet radio. It was released on CD on March of 2010.
