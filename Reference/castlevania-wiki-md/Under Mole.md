The **Under Mole** is an enemy in *Castlevania: The Adventure*. They are little creatures with long arms and no facial or body features, and which barely resemble a mole. Their hands and feet lack fingers and they only end in a big claw-like shape.

## Appearances
### *Castlevania: The Adventure*
Under Moles hide out in several holes in the Caverns' walls and a whole group of them must be faced as a boss fight at the end of that level. There are twenty Under Moles in total which must be defeated to continue to the next level; they leap out from four holes on the wall, do somersaults, and try to overwhelm the player with their vicious claws.

## Trivia
- The Under Mole resembles a Hunchback in the way it curls its body, but attacks by leaping like a Rat.
- Strangely, it is referred as "Poisonous Slime" by Game Boy Nintendo Player's Guide.
