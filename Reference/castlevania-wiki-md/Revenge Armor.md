The **Revenge Armor** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.

## Description
One of the giant armored knights encountered in the game. His characteristic name and the fact that he features the same color scheme as the Living Armor may suggest he is either the same enemy or another knight that was given the assignment of avenging his fallen companion, this time armed with a greatsword.

He is encountered at the bottom of the Luminous Cavern in Castle A. His introduction is short lived, however, because as soon as he enters the screen, an enormous fist will unexpectedly strike him from behind and destroy him, serving only as a prelude to an even greater threat: Talos.

His name will appear if hit during the animation scene before Talos destroys him, but no entry for him will appear in the Encyclopedia.
