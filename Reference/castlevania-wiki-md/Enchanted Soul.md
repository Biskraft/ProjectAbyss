# Enchanted Soul

- Enchanted Souls**, also known as **<span style="color:orange;">Yellow Souls</span>**, are one of the four main types of tactical souls. They allow Soma Cruz to equip them, which will then provide a benefit without MP drain. Only one enchanted soul can be selected at a time, though.
