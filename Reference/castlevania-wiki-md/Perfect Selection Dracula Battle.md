  - Perfect Selection Dracula Battle** is an album produced by Konami in the mid '90s. It features heavy metal renditions of classic *Castlevania* tunes, such as Beginning and Bloody Tears.

Its success was so huge that Konami produced another five "Battle" albums: Perfect Selection Dracula Battle II, Konami Shooting Battle Vol. 1 and 2, Snatcher Battle and Konami Battle.

## Trivia
- Even though the album was arranged by Naoto Shibata and performed by [himself](https://vgmdb.net/artist/787), [Yusuke Takahama](https://vgmdb.net/artist/2993), [Akio Shimizu](https://vgmdb.net/artist/4393) and [Masanori Kusakabe](https://vgmdb.net/artist/7233); the version of Bloody Tears which appears on this album has often been mislabeled as a cover by the black metal band Cradle of Filth on various file sharing networks.
