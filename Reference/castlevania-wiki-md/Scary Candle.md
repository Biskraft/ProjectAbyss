The **Scary Candle**, **Trick Candle** and **Mimic Candle** are enemies in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

## Description
The **Scary Candle**, **Trick Candle** and **Mimic Candle** resemble normal candles (although they are colored differently), but are actually enemies which only take such form when attacked. When struck by a weapon, they will transform into a frail-looking humanoid creature made of wax and drop to the floor, where they'll melt away in a very short time.

Each one of these secret enemies will only appear after a particular boss has been defeated. They're found in rooms that usually have been explored long ago and where the player normally wouldn't have any reason to visit again.

They are similar in function to the Mimics that pose as treasure chests found in other games.

### <span style="color:#52bd84;">Scary Candle</span>
Found in the Iron Golem's boss room in the Machine Tower once the twin Dragon Zombies have been defeated. Has a chance to drop the Uranus card. Due to its quick self-destruction time, it may be difficult to kill.
