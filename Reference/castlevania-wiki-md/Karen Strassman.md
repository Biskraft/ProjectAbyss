# Karen Strassman

- Karen Strassman**, also known as **Kirsty Pape** or **Mia Bradly**, is an American voice actress who has provided English language voices for Japanese anime shows and video games. She provided the voices of Stella Lecarde in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, Tera and Iris in *Castlevania: The Dracula X Chronicles*, Carmilla in *Castlevania Judgment*, and Yoko Belnades in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

Karen's major roles are Kallen Stadtfeld in *Code Geass*, Miyuki in *Lucky Star*, Rider in *Fate/stay Night*, Nina Fortner in *Monster* and Soi Fon in *Bleach*. She is also known as Rosa Joanna Farrell in the NDS version of *Final Fantasy IV*, and Helena Douglas from *Dead or Alive* series.
