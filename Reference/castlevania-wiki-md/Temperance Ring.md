# Temperance Ring

The **Temperance Ring** is the ring representing the Tarot card "Temperance" in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It increases all of Shanoa's statistics by a "temperate" 1 (Defense, Strength, Intelligence, Mind and Luck). It's in contrast to the more dramatic, but dangerous, Death Ring. It can be found by breaking a small suspicious block located at the second room of Giant's Dwelling (the one with a single Ladycat).
