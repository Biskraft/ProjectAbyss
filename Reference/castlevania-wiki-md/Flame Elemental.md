# Flame Elemental

The **Flame Elemental** is an optional boss in *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. He is a knight surrounded by red hot flames.

## Description
The Flame Elemental is a horned, muscular figure with his body completely engulfed in flames and who wields a greatsword. His main method of attack consists in performing two consecutive wide swings with his sword, culminating with a double-handed, overhead slam.

;Attacks
- Impales his sword on the ground, summoning a fire surge around him.
- Performs a double-handed, overhead slam, sending forth flames that travel across the room.
- May use his sword as shield to block attacks.

Once defeated, the player is awarded the Whip of Flames.
