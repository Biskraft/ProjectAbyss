  - Chapel of Dissonance** is a music theme featured in *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*. It is the background theme for the Chapel of Dissonance.

The game's official soundtrack addresses the song as **Chapel in the Air**. The soundtrack also includes an enhanced arrangement of the song composed by Takashi Yoshida.
