# Resist Holy

- Resist Holy** is an Attribute Potion in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* that causes Holy-based attacks to actually heal Alucard.

The item is called **Holy Essence** in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* and raises one's holy affinity, although it doesn't help unlock Holy spells.
