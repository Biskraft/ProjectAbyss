# Nightmare (music)

- Nightmare** is the name of two different music themes in the *Castlevania* series:
  - Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse* - Composed by Konami Kukeiha Club. It appears in the Subterranean Tunnel stage. An arranged version appeared later in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* as the background theme for the Underground Waterway.
  - Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* - Composed by Michiru Yamane. It plays in the "Nightmare" area.
