# Clock Tower (Aria of Sorrow)

The **Clock Tower** is a location in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*.

## Overview
The Clock Tower is first reached from the Floating Garden, and also contains paths to the Castle Corridor and Top Floor (opened from the other side). The clock tower consists of many vertical routes, with gears serving as moving platforms.

The boss of the region is Death. Upon his defeat, Soma will find Skula's soul in the next room, which endows him the Deep Seeker ability, allowing him to submerge and breathe underwater.
