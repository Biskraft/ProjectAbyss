# Castlevania Special Music CD

This CD is a part of Castlevania Limited Edition in Japan. The music contained in is from *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*, *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* and *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, composed by Michiru Yamane. The release date was November 27, 2003.
