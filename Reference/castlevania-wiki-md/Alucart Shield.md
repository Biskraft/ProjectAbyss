# Alucart Shield

The **Alucart Shield** is the shield component of the Alucart series of equipment, the other two being the Alucart Sword and the Alucart Mail. This shield is utterly worthless in the facet of protection, not even bothering to increase Alucard's DEF stat in the majority of its appearances, in addition to the lack of reflecting/blocking capabilities. The only real use for this shield comes from the respective set bonuses in *Symphony of the Night* and *Encore of the Night*, both activating only when the shield is equipped along with the other two pieces of the Alucart equipment.
