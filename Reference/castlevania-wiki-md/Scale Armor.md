# Scale Armor

The **Scale Armor** (also called **Scale Mail**) is armor made up of layered metal scales.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*, it grants Defense +30, which is 2 more than Chain Mail, but 2 less than the Guardian Armor.

In *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*, it gives Defense +20, which is 4 more than Ring Mail and 5 less than Chain Mail.
