# Game Over

- Game Over**, also known as **Player Out**, is a jingle originally composed by Konami Kukeiha Club that plays in almost every *Castlevania* game when the player dies.

In newer games, similar jingles with different names have been used instead, such as Land of Benediction (*Symphony of the Night*), Scarlet Fine (*Curse of Darkness*), or Consummation (*Order of Ecclesia*).
