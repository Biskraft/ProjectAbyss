# Harmony of Despair Stage 8

- The One Who Is Many** is the second DLC stage of *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and the eighth stage overall. The level takes place in an amalgamation of the underground levels of *Symphony of the Night* such as the Abandoned Pit to the Catacomb and the Underground Caverns.

The boss theme in this stage is "Poetic Death", both from *Symphony of the Night*. It was released on January 12, 2011.

### Hidden item
The hidden item of Chapter 8 is Lucky Cat. Japanese Maneki Neko.
