# Special Weapons

- Special Weapons** featured in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness* are weapons that do not belong to the usual medieval-style of weapon categories like Swords, Axes, Spears or Knuckles. Thus, each weapon has its own unique set of combos. Moreover, many consider them as "joke" or weapons made for comedic purposes, since these are based on modern-day equipment. Crafting one of these usually results from a complex recipe of weapons. Killing enemies with a special weapon will cause them to drop White Evo Crystals.
