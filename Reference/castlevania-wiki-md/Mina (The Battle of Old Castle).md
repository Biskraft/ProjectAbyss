# Mina (The Battle of Old Castle)

- Mina** is Simon Belmont's girlfriend, according to the non-canon gamebook *The Devil Castle Dracula: The Battle of Old Castle*. She is later portrayed by actress Lucy Lane for the film adaptation about their adventures.

## Notes
- After all, there is no information on Mina because the movie is canceled.

## Trivia
- Mina shares her name with Mina Harker from the original 1897 novel *Dracula* by Bram Stoker.
