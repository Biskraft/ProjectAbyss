# Dracula/Curse of Darkness

250px|thumb|Dracula artwork from *Curse of Darkness*.

  - Dracula** is the final boss of *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. He has been destroyed by Trevor C. Belmont three years ago (as depicted in the prologue), but he left a horrible curse across Europe. When Isaac has been defeated by Hector, Zead uses his body and revives Lord Dracula from the dead. Dracula pronounces sentence of death upon Hector, but he is defeated.

In the English version of the game, Dracula is voiced by Douglas Rye.
