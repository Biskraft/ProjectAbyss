# Crepes Suzette

- Crepes Suzette** is a food item that recovers health in *Castlevania*. It is a breakfast item that appears in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. The Draculina enemies in Dracula's Castle have a fairly high chance of dropping this item.
