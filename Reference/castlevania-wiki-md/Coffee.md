# Coffee

- Coffee** is a beverage in *Castlevania* which recovers one of the player's meters (either HP, MP or Hearts, depending on the game). It appears in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow*, *Portrait of Ruin* and *Order of Ecclesia*.
