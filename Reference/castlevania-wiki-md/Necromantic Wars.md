# Necromantic Wars

The **Necromantic Wars** were a series of wars fought between the forces of the Lycans and the Aghartians. The mages of Agharta had created giant golems, the Titans in hopes of ending the war. However, they were ultimately defeated in the end, and the Aghartian civilization was destroyed. In the initial stages of the conflict, the Aghartians fought using magical crystals and devised the Titans as weapons of war, but in the end, the Lycans' superior numbers overwhelmed them, and the city was conquered. What few remained fled, were captured or killed, though Claudia's father managed to survive and created a protector for his daughter, the Black Knight Golem.
