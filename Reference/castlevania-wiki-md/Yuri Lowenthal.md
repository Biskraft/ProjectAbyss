# Yuri Lowenthal

- Yuri Lowenthal**, also known as **Jimmy Benedict** or **Stephen Fisher**, is an American voice actor. He voiced Alucard in *The Dracula X Chronicles* version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, replacing Robert Belgrade. Since then, Yuri voiced the character in two more *Castlevania* games: *Judgment* and *Harmony of Despair*, as well as the crossover game *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate*.

Yuri is well known for his fans as the voice of Haseo in *.hack//G.U.* series and Sasuke Uchiha in *Naruto*; as well as Suzaku Kururugi in *Code Geass*, Simon in *Gurren Lagann*, Pip Bernadotte in *Hellsing Ultimate*, Cecil Harvey in the NDS version of *Final Fantasy IV*, Makoto Yuki in *Shin Megami Tensei: Persona 3*, Ben Tennyson in *Ben 10* and The Prince in *Prince of Persia* series.
