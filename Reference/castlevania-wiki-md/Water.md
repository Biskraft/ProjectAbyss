# Water

- Water** is a type of elemental damage. Sometimes Ice-based attacks are considered Water type attacks (such as in *Aria of Sorrow* and *Dawn of Sorrow*). Other times, Water is a separate type of attack than Ice, such as in *Symphony of the Night* (though in this instance, certain enemies are programmed to strength against water damage with no way for the player to incur it). Usually, however, there is no Water element, and only the Ice element exists.
