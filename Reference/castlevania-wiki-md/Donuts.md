# Donuts

thumb|Donuts and [[Crullers]]
  - Donuts** are common desserts in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. They are crisp donuts often embellished with different flavors, offering additional benefits.

The **Plain Donut** recovers 30 MP and HP and increase INT by 2.

The **Strawberry Donut** recovers 35 MP and HP and increase LCK and INT by 2. It can be considered the Luck upgrade to the Plain Donut.

The **Chocolate Donut** recovers 35 MP and HP and increase CON and INT by 2. It can be considered the Constitution upgrade to the Plain Cruller.
