The **Holy Armor** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon*.

## Description
The Holy Armor is among the strongest types of armors. He behaves like any typical armor, moving forward four paces and then attacking by pointing his sword in the direction of the enemy and firing a beam of Light energy, either straight forward or diagonal (can fire it either diagonally up or down).

He is less powerful than the White Armor.
