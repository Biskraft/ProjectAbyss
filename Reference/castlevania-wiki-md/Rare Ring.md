# Rare Ring

right|73px

The **Rare Ring** (called **Ring of Arcana** in the North American version of *Symphony of the Night* and **Thief Ring** in *Portrait of Ruin*, *Order of Ecclesia* and *Harmony of Despair*) is a ring which increases the chances enemies will drop both common and rare items. It appears in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, *Harmony of Dissonance*, *Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow*, *Curse of Darkness*, *Portrait of Ruin*, *Order of Ecclesia* and *Harmony of Despair*.

Depending on the game, it may also provide a LCK boost.

## Notes
