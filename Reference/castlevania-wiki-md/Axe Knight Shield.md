# Axe Knight Shield

right|64px

The **Axe Knight Shield** (also known as the **AxeLord Shield**) is the favourite shield of axe-knights.

In *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, this shield drops off Axe Knights and Skeletons.

In *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair,* this is a random chest drop on chapters 2 and 3 on normal. Axe Knight can also drop these shields as well.
