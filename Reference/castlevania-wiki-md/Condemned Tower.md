The **Condemned Tower** is a location in *Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow*.

## Overview
A tall tower enveloped by clouds and moonlight, whose multiple floors collapse during the fight with Gergoth. The outer sides of the tower have many ramps and platforms for climbing purposes. The top of the tower connects to the Cursed Clock Tower. Additionally, below the tower a hidden secret area awaits: the Mine of Judgement.

## Notes
- One useful strategy after the Gergoth fight is to use the Flying Armor's and Puppet Master's soul in conjunction. The player may throw a puppet at a normally unreachable ledge while floating slowly in midair and be teleported to it.
