# Guest Rooms

right|300px

  - Guest Rooms** are a type of environment in the *Castlevania* series. While their portrayal may change between titles, it generally is an elegantly decorated section of the castle that features long hallways where several rooms destined to host the Count's guests can be found.

Enemies associated with domestic labors, such as housemaids and butlers, can usually be found tending these places.

## Appearances
  - Castlevania Chronicles* — The Floating Corridor
  - Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* — Inner Quarters
  - Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow* — Demon Guest House
  - Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2* (*Revelations* DLC) — Guest House
