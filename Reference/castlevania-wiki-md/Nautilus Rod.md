# Nautilus Rod

right|200px**Nautilus Rod** is a Mage-Type Innocent Devil found in *Castlevania: Curse of Darkness*. It's a level 3 Innocent Devil and can learn Purify and Satellite B. It can be obtained from a Scissor Rod Innocent Devil, using green, yellow or white crystals.
