# Guardian Cagnazzo

right|104px
  - Guardian Cagnazzo** is a Guardian Soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained from Cagnazzo and it summons this demon to continuously punch at enemies.

## Trivia
- The concept behind Guardian Cagnazzo is a reference to *JoJo's Bizarre Adventure's* Jotaro Kujo's Stand, Star Platinum. Both appear behind their users, fire a rapid succession of punches while shouting: "ORA ORA ORA!".
