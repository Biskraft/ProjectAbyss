# Life Apple

right|50px

The **Life Apple** (called the **Wonder Drug of Life** in the Japanese version) is an apple which fully restores life after death in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It cannot be selected from the menu and used; instead, it can only be used by a Faerie Familiar, which will use it if at least one is contained in the player's inventory upon death.
