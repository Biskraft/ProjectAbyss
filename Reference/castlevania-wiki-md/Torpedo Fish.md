# Torpedo Fish

right|250px
  - Torpedo Fish** is a Killer Fish launched at full speed underwater by Soma Cruz utilizing its soul. It is effective underwater but will be discarded and die right away if performed out of the water. Soma is able to use this ability in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*, *Dawn of Sorrow* and *Harmony of Despair*.
