  - Pagoda of the Misty Moon** (otherwise known as **Misty Moon Pagoda of the Demon Realm**) is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence*. It is the background theme of the titular location in Walter's Castle.
