# Clover Tiara

The **Clover Tiara** is the weaker of the two tiaras in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* that gives a Mind Boost (+2) in addition to a standard Defense Boost (+1). It is less powerful than the Midnight Tiara, which gives a Defense Boost of +7 and a Mind Boost of +5.
