# Castlevania: Double Pack

- Castlevania: Double Pack*** is a compilation package for the Game Boy Advance handheld system which contains two previous *Castlevania* titles: *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance* and *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It was released on January 10th, 2006 in North America, and later that year in Europe.

This release is part of Konami's "*Double Pack*" series for the GBA system, along with compilations of other franchises, such as *Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles* and *Yu-Gi-Oh!*.

## Features

Both games are included in their complete form, allowing players to either pick Juste Belmont or Soma Cruz in their very own adventure against the forces of evil.

- Castlevania: Double Pack* was picked as the "IGN Game Boy Game of the Month" for January 2006.

## Version differences
- The Medusa Pendant, the Mirror Pendant and the Gold Headband were removed from the *Double Pack* version of *Castlevania: Harmony of Dissonance*.
