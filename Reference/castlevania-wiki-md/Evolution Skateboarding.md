# Evolution Skateboarding

- Evolution Skateboarding*** is a skateboarding video game released by Konami for the PlayStation 2 and GameCube in 2002. It includes several popular skateboarders such as Rick McCrank, Arto Saari, Kerry Getz and Danny Way.

## Features
- Evolution Skateboarding* features a hidden character named "Vampire Killer" who resembles Simon Belmont. In addition, the game also features a level based on Dracula's Castle, where the ultimate goal is to defeat a vampire lord (probably Dracula).

There are also sets of level-specific songs, such as a metal remix of the *Metal Gear Solid* theme and a couple of *Castlevania* songs obtained after unlocking the Vampire Killer, such as remixes of Vampire Killer. A remix of Dance of Illusions is also played when fighting the Dracula-based vampire.
