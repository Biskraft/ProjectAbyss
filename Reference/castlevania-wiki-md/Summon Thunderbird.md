# Summon Thunderbird

right|300px
Summons a Thunderbird which brings along with it a storm of lightning bolts throughout screen, causing Electric damage to all enemies on the screen. This can be performed in *Castlevania: Circle of the Moon* by utilizing the Uranus + Thunderbird DSS combination.
