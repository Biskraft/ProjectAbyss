# Hercules Ring

The **Hercules Ring** is a ring in that prevents the wearer from being sent into hit recovery after taking damage. It first appeared in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*, and later in *Harmony of Despair*. It was replaced by the Tower Ring in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia* (which in exchange causes the player to take more damage from a hit).
