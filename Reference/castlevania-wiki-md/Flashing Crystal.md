# Flashing Crystal

- The Flashing Crystal** is an item in *Castlevania: The Adventure* and *Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge*. This special item is always found inside a candle at the end of each level and will summon the Primary Evil (boss) of that area to show itself and be subsequently fought. Once the Primary Evil has been defeated, bonus points will be awarded to the player for all remaining time, as well as a determined amount depending on the boss.

In *Castlevania Legends*, the Crystal is also used to summon the Death Dragon and the Executioner at the end of stage 2 and the hidden stage.
