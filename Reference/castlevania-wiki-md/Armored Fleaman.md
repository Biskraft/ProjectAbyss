# Armored Fleaman

The **Armored Fleaman** is an enemy in the *Castlevania* series. They are little Flea Men armed with an axe and shield, and protected by armor.

## Description
An Armored Fleaman is a simple Flea Man encased in armor and equipped with a menacing axe and a shield. After his armor is destroyed, the flea man continues to hop around until he is also destroyed.

It should be noted that Armored Fleamen and regular Flea Men are technically not considered to be the same enemies in the games they appear in; an Armored Fleaman may drop an item once his armor is destroyed, and when the resulting Flea Man is destroyed, it may drop yet another item.

While not in armor, regular Fleamen are shown to prefer wielding twin-axe blades in the 3D games *Castlevania: Lament of Innocence* and *Curse of Darkness*.
