  - Mournful Serenade** (otherwise known as **Serenade of Sympathy** or **Mournful Nocturne**) is an original music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *The Dracula X Chronicles* port of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*.

This instrumental track is used as the ending theme of the game, replacing the original vocal theme "I am the Wind".
