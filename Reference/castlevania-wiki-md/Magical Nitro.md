# Magical Nitro

- Magical Nitro** is a powerful explosive item that can be found in the Castle Center stage of in *Castlevania* (N64) and *Castlevania: Legacy of Darkness*. It can be used to destroy walls that have cracks in them. However, it is a highly volatile substance, and will explode if the person carrying it makes any sudden jerking movements, such as jumping or being hit by an enemy attack. This substance must be carried across a long distance through the Castle Center to a wall near where the Mammoth Bull Demon lies dormant. It will also explode after a while when paired with a Mandragora, giving the hero ample time to take shelter from the explosion.
