The **Flying Flower** is an enemy in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*. It is a demon plant that leaps about in search of water.

This enemy originates from Konami's 1987 platform game *Getsu Fūma Den*, which is referenced in the crossover Chapter 11: The Legend of Fuma.
