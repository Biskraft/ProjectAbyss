The **Burning Town** is a location in *Castlevania: Dracula X*.


## Overview
A reimagining of the first stage from the original *Castlevania: Rondo of Blood*.

Richter Belmont arrives to the outskirts of a burning town in search of his beloved Annet. Unfortunately, the place has been completely destroyed and overrun by monsters who now lurk in every corner. Halfway through the level, a huge Behemoth gives chase. Richter can either attempt to fight the beast or escape from it by making it fall to its death near the end of that section. A Food item will be awarded if he succeeds in defeating the creature by conventional means.

The music switches to a dramatic tune in the next room and the flames in the background change to a phantasmagorical greenish tone as preamble for what's to come. No monsters can be seen around, adding to the tension. Not long after this, the three-headed guardian dog of Hell, Keruberosu, jumps into the scene, commencing the first boss battle of the game.
