# Limestone Cavern ~ Waterfall ~ Submerged City

- Limestone Cavern ~ Waterfall ~ Submerged City** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Super Castlevania IV*. It plays during the third stage of the game. It is a medley of three different tracks (The Waterfalls, The Cave and The Submerged City).

An arrangement of 'The Cave' appears in Perfect Selection Dracula ~New Classic~. The track was again given an arrangement for Castlevania: Lords of Shadow under the name, Waterfalls of Agharta.
