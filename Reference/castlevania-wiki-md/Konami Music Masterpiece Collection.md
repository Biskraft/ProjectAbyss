# Konami Music Masterpiece Collection

is a compilation of 15 albums and songs from many Konami video games, composed primarily by the Konami Kukeiha Club. It was released exclusively in Japan on October 1, 2004.

The whole package includes music from series like *Gradius*, *TwinBee*, *Salamander*, *Parodius*, *Ganbare Goemon*, *Contra*, and *Castlevania*, among many others.
