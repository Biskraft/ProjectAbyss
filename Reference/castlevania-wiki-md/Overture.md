# Overture

- Overture** is a music theme composed by Konami Kukeiha Club for *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*. It is the background theme that plays in the Castle Keep and during Dracula's battle. It was later arranged by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* as The Throne Room's background theme.
