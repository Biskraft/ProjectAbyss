# Ruby Pins

The **Ruby Pins** is an accessory in *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*. It is a hair accessory to lower flame damage.

## Availability
Available for purchase at Jacob's shop after completing Laura's second quest, "A Pleasant Accessory", by bringing her a Ruby, an Emerald and a Sapphire.

## Characteristics
Hair pins adorned with rubies. They reduce the amount of damage taken from Flame-based attacks.

## Usage
The Ruby Pins are probably one of the most useful headgear accessories the player could wear in their attempt of completing the Training Hall. Besides spike-covered surfaces, many of the obstacles and hazards in that place involve fire in one way or another. The Ruby Pins effectively reduce much of this type of damage.
