# Apollonian Ring

- Apollonian Ring** (also called the **Sun Ring**) is a ring that improves status during the day (6am to 6pm based on the system clock). It is the day-time counterpart to the Artemisian Ring (also called Moon Ring), which give the exact same amount of stats, but during the nighttime. It has a similar effect as the Sunstone does from Castlevania: Symphony of the Night and in Castlevania: Harmony of Despair is shown to have a greater effect.

The ring first appeared in Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia, where it is called the Sun Ring, after the tarot card [The Sun](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Moon_(Tarot_card)).

Its second appearance was as a green/purple chest drop in the The One Who Is Many downloadable stage of Castlevania: Harmony of Despair on the Normal difficulty, where it was renamed the Apollonian Ring. It and its counterpart, the Artemisian Ring, are the only accessories from Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia to appear in this game. During the night, it increases defense by 1. During the day, it is a much increased effect, increasing all of the stats by 4 (STR, CON, INT, MND, LCK). (STR raises ATK and CON raises DEF)
