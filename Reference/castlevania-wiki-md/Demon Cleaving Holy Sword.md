# Demon Cleaving Holy Sword

right|274px

  - Demon Cleaving Holy Sword** is a bullet soul in *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*. It can be obtained by gaining dominance over a Valkyrie's soul.

## Characteristics
This soul summons a Valkyrie in front of Soma which unleashes a powerful slash upward with her holy sword, damaging enemies in front and above. Since this spell can be obtained relatively early in the game (Inner Quarters, just after defeating the Headhunter), its base damage is quite powerful, has long reach, and it also deals Holy damage (which most enemies are weak against), it's one of the best souls Soma can get his hands upon and it will remain useful for the rest of the game. While it's costly to cast (50 MP), few enemies can withstand this attack.
