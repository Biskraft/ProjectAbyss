# Muse's Tiara

The **Muse's Tiara** is the only tiara in *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin* that gives an INT boost (+8) in addition to the standard DEF boost (+6). It also gives an INT boost in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.
