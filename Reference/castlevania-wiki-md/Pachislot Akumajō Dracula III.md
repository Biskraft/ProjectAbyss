# Pachislot Akumajō Dracula III

is a pachislot casino game and is a follow-up to *Pachislot Akumajō Dracula*. It features much of the cast of *Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse*, including Trevor, Alucard, Sypha, Succubus, Doppelganger and Dracula (including his second form). Released on February 6, 2012.
