The  is an upside down version of the Alchemy Laboratory in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. Here, everything has taken a more ominous slate tone.

It can first be reached from the Anti Chapel from the right and is connected to the Black Marble Gallery (lower path) and the Reverse Entrance (upper path) to the left.
