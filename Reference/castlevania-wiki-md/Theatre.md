# Theatre

The **Theatre** is the eleventh area Simon explores in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow - Mirror of Fate*. It is here that he first encounters Alucard. In Alucard's journey, this is the sixth area explored, and it is here that a puzzle must be solved in order to save Simon.
