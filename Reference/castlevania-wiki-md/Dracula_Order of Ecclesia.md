# Dracula/Order of Ecclesia

250px|right|thumb|<p style="text-align: center;">Dracula artwork from *Order of Ecclesia*.</p>

  - Dracula** has been destroyed by Alucard once again during 1797. But his remains have left with a form of glyph named Dominus. In *Castlevania: Order of Ecclesia*, Dracula was revived by Barlowe, the founder of the Order of Ecclesia, who sacrificed himself when he failed to use his disciple Shanoa. Dracula faces her in the Final Approach but is defeated.

In the English version of the game, Dracula is voiced by Patrick Seitz.

## Trivia
- This is the only incarnation of Dracula in the Metroidvania titles wherein his second phase in the final boss battle does not involve him transforming into a grotesque monster. In this game, he gains an additional attack consisting of walking towards Shanoa instead of simply teleporting.
