# Grenade

right|246px

A **Grenade** explodes for Fire damage one second after the pin is pulled.

## Game specific information
### *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow*
This weapon is available as a bullet soul obtained from a Zombie Soldier.

### *Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin*
Grenade is one of Jonathan Morris's sub-weapon skills in *Portrait of Ruin*. Its initial appearance is that of a pineapple grenade; once mastered, it changes into a stick grenade.
