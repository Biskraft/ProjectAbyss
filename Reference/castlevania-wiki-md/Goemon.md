# Goemon

|  |

  - Goemon** is a hidden item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair*.

## Background
Goemon is the main protagonist from the *Ganbare Goemon* series and one of Konami's most renowned mascots. He appears as a hidden item in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* and has also appeared in several mascot games which usually feature *Castlevania* characters.

In the special two-issue manga *Ganbare Goemon: Chapter Hell*, he fought and defeated Count Dracula alongside Pierre Belmont, Simon's alleged grandfather who he met in the underworld.

## Trivia
- Goemon is loosely based on semi-legendary Japanese outlaw hero Ishikawa Goemon (1558-1594), who stole gold and valuables to give them to the poor.
- In *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate*, Goemon appears as a Mii Costume for the Mii Swordfighter as part of Wave 3.
