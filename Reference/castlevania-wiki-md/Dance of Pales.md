# Dance of Pales

- Dance of Pales**, also known as **Waltz of Pearls**, is a music theme composed by Michiru Yamane for *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*. It is the background theme for Olrox's Quarters, as well as the main menu theme for *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night*.
