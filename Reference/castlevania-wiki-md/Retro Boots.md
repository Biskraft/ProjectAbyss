# Retro Boots

The **Retro Boots** (called **Legend Boots** in the Japanese version) are 8-bit boots in *Castlevania: Harmony of Despair* that can only be equipped by whip wielders.

They increase speed, but only twice as much as the slowest speed-increasing boots, the Mercury Boots. The latter provide a 20% speed boost, the Retro Boots provide 40%, the Winged Boots and the Fuma's Greaves provide 50%, and the Sonic Boots provide 60%.
