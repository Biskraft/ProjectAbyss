# Explosive Earthquake

- Explosive Earthquake** is an advanced skill in *Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2* that involves Shadow Magic. This combo creates a series of heated explosions under Dracula's enemies' feet that will break shields and armor. The subsequent eruption will release multiple final explosions against all nearby enemies. However, he will be rooted to one spot through the duration of this move. This move is the Chaos Claws' equivalent of Exploding Quake.
