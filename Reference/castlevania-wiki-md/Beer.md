# Beer

is an alcoholic beverage found in *Castlevania* which recovers one of the player's meters. Like the other alcoholic beverages in this game, it was censored in the North American version of *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* and is called **Barley Tea**. It recovers 15 HP in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night*, which is the second lowest HP recovering beverage in the game, being 5 more than Sake (Green Tea), but 5 less than Wine (Grape Juice). This recovers the same amount as Ice Cream.
