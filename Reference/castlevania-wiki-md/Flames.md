# Flames

Flames are spells that involve billowing flames.

The first flame spell, Holy Flame, can be performed by Sypha Belnades and Yoko Belnades. This is a quick burst of flames.

Maria Renard is able to call upon flames by summoning Byakko in the Saturn version of ''Castlevania: Symphony of the Night.

Soma Cruz is able to use a Flame Breath ability which emits a cloud of flames that can be turned on or off at will. He's also able to summon a Spittle Bone, which spins around in front of him, allowing him to trace of path of flames as he walks.

Charlotte Aulin has a particularly long reaching and relatively long lasting flame spell called Raging Fire.
