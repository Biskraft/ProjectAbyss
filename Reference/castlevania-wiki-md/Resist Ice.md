# Resist Ice

- Resist Ice** is an Attribute Potion in *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* that causes Ice-based attacks to actually heal Alucard.

The item is called **Water Essence** in *Castlevania Puzzle: Encore of the Night* and raises one's Water/Ice affinity, although it doesn't help unlock Water/Ice spells.
