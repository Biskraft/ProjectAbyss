A puzzle game where you decipher languages.
The game is "Chants of Sennaar",
and it’s a masterclass in unique puzzle design.
Most puzzle games are about manipulating objects
or navigating environments.
But Chants of Sennaar is about deciphering
completely unknown languages.
The Concept: Logic through Linguistics.
You’re exploring a giant tower,
and you encounter different cultures,
each with their own unique script and grammar.
To progress, you must observe how words are used
in different contexts.
When you think you've figured out a word,
you can write your guess in your notebook.
If you get a set of words correct,
the game confirms your translation,
effectively "learning" the language as you go.
Why it’s Brilliant.
This creates a powerful loop of
observation, hypothesis, and validation.
It makes you pay attention to the world
in a way few other games do.
You’re not just looking for clues;
you’re looking for patterns in how people communicate.
It’s a game that values patient observation
and logical deduction above all else.
It turns the process of learning—
something that can often be dry or academic—
into a thrilling and satisfying core mechanic.
Chants of Sennaar shows that there are still
huge, unexplored territories in puzzle design
if you’re willing to think outside the box.
Thanks for watching.
